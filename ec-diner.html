Our Heritage

A Taste of Greece, A Legacy of Flavor

More than just a diner, EC Diner started with a passion for bringing the vibrant flavors of Mediterranean tradition to Ellicott City. Our commitment to quality shines through in every dish, especially our lean, tender baby lamb chops, meticulously marinated in authentic Greek herbs and drizzled with fresh lemon juice. Served with perfectly oven-roasted potatoes and a crisp Greek salad, it's a dish that embodies our heritage and dedication to exceptional taste.

Every dish tells a story, and ours is one of fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, time-honored family recipes, and a warm, inviting atmosphere where everyone feels like family. We pride ourselves on creating memorable dining experiences, blending classic diner comfort with unexpected gourmet touches.

"The baby lamb chops at EC Diner are an absolute revelation! So tender, perfectly seasoned, and the lemon zest just makes them sing. It's truly a taste of Greece right here in Ellicott City. This place never disappoints!"

- Chef Maria K., Local Food Critic
