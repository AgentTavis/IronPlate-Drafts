OUR STORY

More Than Just a Diner

Welcome to Grounds & Vine, your local spot in Kannapolis, NC, where great food meets exceptional service. What started as a vision for a cozy coffee shop quickly blossomed into something much more—a vibrant diner cherished by the community.

Our guests consistently rave about the inviting atmosphere and the quality of our dishes. From hearty breakfast classics to satisfying lunch specials, every meal is prepared with care. We're proud to offer not just fabulous food, but also a surprisingly extensive selection of wine and beer, making us the perfect place for any time of day.

We believe in creating memorable experiences, whether you're grabbing a quick coffee, enjoying a leisurely lunch, or unwinding with a glass of wine in the evening. Our team is dedicated to providing top-notch service that makes every visit feel like coming home.
