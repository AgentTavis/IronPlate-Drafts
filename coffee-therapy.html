Welcome to Coffee Therapy: More Than Just a Diner

At Coffee Therapy, we believe in crafting experiences that refresh your day. Nestled in the heart of Westlake, NC, we're a cozy diner dedicated to serving up delicious treats and invigorating beverages with a smile.
