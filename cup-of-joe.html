Our Roots

More Than Just Coffee, It's Community.

Continuing the Looz family tradition of excellence, Joe Looz proudly introduced Cup of Joe – a coffee shop created to serve the heart of the King community and surrounding areas. This isn't just about coffee; it's about extending a legacy of quality and warm hospitality.

With heartfelt gratitude to his family, friends, and loyal customers, Joe is delighted to offer freshly brewed coffee, a delightful array of pastries, and more. Every cup and every treat is served with the same unwavering commitment to quality and the community spirit that has always defined the Looz name.
