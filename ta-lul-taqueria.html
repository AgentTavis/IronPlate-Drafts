Our Story

From Our Family Kitchen to Your Table: Authentic Mexican Flavors in Spruce Pine.

At Tía Lulú Taqueria, we bring the vibrant flavors and warm hospitality of Mexico right here to Spruce Pine, NC. More than just a taco truck, we are Altapass Mexican Store – your local hub for authentic Mexican products, ingredients, and a taste of home.

Every dish, from our savory tacos to our traditional aguas frescas, is crafted with passion, using recipes passed down through generations. What started as a dream to share our culture and cuisine has blossomed into a beloved local favorite, known for its fresh ingredients and genuine Mexican spirit. Come experience the heart of Mexico, right at 12486 NC-226.
