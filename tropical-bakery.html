Our Story

From Brazil to Matthews: A Taste of Home and Heart

Tropical Bakery isn't just a diner; it's a culinary journey to Brazil, right here in Matthews, NC. We are a phenomenal bakery specializing in authentic Brazilian desserts, alongside a delightful array of cakes for all occasions. Every bite is crafted with passion and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Our warm and friendly staff is always eager to guide you through our fascinating selection, whether you're craving a rich Black Forest cake reminiscent of European delights or eager to explore the vibrant flavors of Brazil. We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home, no Portuguese required!

Beyond our sweet treats, we also offer savory sandwiches and pastries perfect for breakfast, lunch, or a delightful snack. Come discover your new favorite spot for authentic flavors and friendly faces.

"The Black Forest cake was wonderful, rich, and full of chocolate and cherry flavors. It truly reminded me of cakes I've had in Germany! The Brazilian desserts are so unique, and the staff made sure I understood everything even without speaking Portuguese. Can't wait to try their savory pastries next!"

— A Happy Customer
