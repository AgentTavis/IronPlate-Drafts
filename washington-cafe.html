Our Story

Where Every Cup Tells a Tale of Passion.

At Washington Cafe, we believe the best stories are shared over a great cup of coffee and a delicious meal. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a warm, inviting space where everyone feels like family. We're proud to be a true local diner, a place where you'll quickly get to know us, and we, you.

Our commitment to exceptional flavor is at the heart of everything we do. Take our coffee, for instance. We dedicate 20 hours to cold brewing, a meticulous process that virtually eliminates acidity, leaving you with nothing but a crisp, clean, and pure coffee experience. It’s a flavor profile you won't find anywhere else – perfect on its own, or elevated with a touch of blue raspberry for a sensational twist!

"The owners are so welcoming, and that 20-hour cold brew? Absolutely sensational! It's such a clean, pure coffee flavor."

— A Happy Local
