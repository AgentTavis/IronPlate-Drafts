Our Story

A Passion For Perfect Afternoon Tea

Nestled in the heart of Williamston, The Tea Room On Main was born from a desire to bring a touch of elegance and warmth to our community. We believe in the magic of a perfectly brewed cup of tea and the joy of shared moments over delectable treats.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a cozy haven where friends and family could gather, unwind, and savor the finest seasonal flavors. Every month, our culinary team crafts a brand-new Afternoon Tea menu, ensuring there's always something fresh and exciting to discover.

From the delicate balance of cranberry and orange to the comforting embrace of honey and vanilla, we meticulously select ingredients and blend them with warm baking spices to create a harmonious and unforgettable experience. It's more than just tea; it's a cherished tradition we invite you to be a part of.

Our Monthly Creations

Experience The Art of Afternoon Tea

Indulge in our exquisite Afternoon Tea selections, thoughtfully crafted with seasonal ingredients and a harmonious blend of flavors. A new menu awaits you every month!

Honey-Vanilla Scones

Delicate, freshly baked scones infused with local honey and Madagascar vanilla bean, served with clotted cream and preserves.

Cranberry Orange Petit Fours

Elegant bite-sized pastries featuring the vibrant zest of orange and the tart sweetness of cranberry, a true seasonal delight.

Spiced Apple Tartlets

Flaky pastry filled with tender apple chunks, slow-cooked with warm baking spices for a comforting, aromatic treat.

Orange Blossom Madeleines

Classic shell-shaped French cakes, light and buttery, kissed with the fragrant essence of orange blossom.

Creamy Earl Grey Panna Cotta

Silky smooth Italian dessert, delicately infused with premium Earl Grey tea and balanced with a hint of sweetness.

Signature Tea Selection

A curated assortment of premium loose-leaf black, green, and herbal teas, perfectly complementing our monthly menu.
