Our Heritage

Crafting Authentic Mexican Flavors for Olive Branch

Nestled conveniently right next to a grocery store, Chukis Deli Mexicano is more than just a restaurant – it's a culinary journey. We pride ourselves on creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where you're seated fast and attended to with genuine care, ensuring every visit is a comfortable experience.

Our kitchen is a testament to authentic Mexican comfort food, with a special emphasis on our incredible Birria, served in at least half a dozen mouth-watering ways. From the moment you taste our Birria Tacos, you'll understand why they're a local favorite – a perfect blend of rich flavors and tender meat.

But we're not just about the familiar. Dare to explore unique creations like our Delta Burrito. While it might surprise you with its smoked pork, coleslaw, and baked beans, it's a delicious adventure that beautifully marries traditional techniques with unexpected, delightful twists. At Chukis, we're dedicated to bringing you homemade style meals, now served for Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner.

Come for the food, stay for the family. We're Chukis Deli Mexicano, your home for authentic Mexican deli flavors.
