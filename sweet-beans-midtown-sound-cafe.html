Our Journey

More Than Just a Cafe – A Community Hub

Warm Welcomes & Fresh Flavors Since Day One

Nestled right in the heart of the Bryant Building on the campus of Carteret Community College, Sweet Beans Midtown Sound Cafe is a cherished local spot. We're proud to be locally owned and operated, pouring our hearts into every handcrafted coffee and baking passion into every sweet treat.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a warm, inviting space where students, faculty, and Morehead City residents could gather, relax, and savor delicious, freshly made food and beverages. From the moment you step inside, you'll feel the cozy diner vibe, perfect for a quick breakfast, a study break, or a leisurely lunch.

We believe in honest ingredients, friendly faces, and the power of a perfectly brewed latte to brighten your day. Come experience the Sweet Beans difference!
