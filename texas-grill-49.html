Our Heritage

The Heart of Texas Grill 49

At Texas Grill 49, we bring the vibrant flavors of the grill right to your table, deeply rooted in family tradition. While seafood is our specialty, our heart also beats for the authentic taste of a true Texas parrillada.

Imagine a feast designed for 3-4 people, laden with succulent 1/2 roast chicken, tender brisket, savory sausage BBQ, and fall-off-the-bone pork ribs. Each parrillada is generously served with warm flour or corn tortillas, fluffy rice, hearty chile beans, fresh pico de gallo, and creamy guacamole. It's more than just a meal; it's a celebration of heritage, quality ingredients, and the joy of sharing great food.

"Texas Grill 49 isn't just a restaurant; it's where our family goes for celebrations. The Parrillada is a masterpiece, a true taste of home that brings everyone together. Every dish, from the savory brisket to the tender pork ribs, is crafted with so much care!"

- The Rodriguez Family
