Our Story

Where Every Meal Feels Like Home.

Nestled in the heart of Oxford, NC, Main St Oasis began with a simple vision: to create a place where families and friends could gather, enjoy delicious food, and make lasting memories without breaking the bank. We believe that fine dining doesn't have to come with a hefty price tag, and that every guest deserves an exceptional experience.

Since opening our doors, we've dedicated ourselves to serving up comfort, quality, and community. With a stellar 4.4-star rating from over 189 reviews, our guests consistently praise our inviting atmosphere and incredible value. Our menu is crafted with care, focusing on fresh ingredients and timeless flavors that appeal to all ages. Come discover your new favorite spot!
