Our Journey

Where Every Meal Feels Like Home

Welcome to Main St Oasis, a cherished cornerstone of Oxford, NC's culinary scene. For years, we've dedicated ourselves to creating a unique dining experience that blends the warmth of family-style hospitality with the elevated taste of fine dining, all at a price that won't break the bank.

Nestled at 130 Main St, we pride ourselves on being more than just a Bar & Grill. We're a place where friends gather, families celebrate, and every guest feels like a part of our extended community. Our commitment is simple: exceptional food, inviting atmosphere, and service that makes you want to come back again and again.
