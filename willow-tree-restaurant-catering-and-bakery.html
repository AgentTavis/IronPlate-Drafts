Our Journey

Crafting Unforgettable Moments in the Mountains.

Nestled in the picturesque town of Robbinsville, Willow Tree Restaurant, Catering and Bakery is more than just a place to eat – it's an experience. Born from a passion for exceptional food and warm hospitality, we’ve been serving our community and visitors alike with dishes that delight the senses and create lasting memories.

From the sizzle of a perfectly cooked Filet Mignon to the delicate artistry of our artisan pastries, every item on our menu is prepared with the finest ingredients and a touch of mountain charm. We believe in sourcing locally when possible, ensuring freshness and supporting our neighbors.

Whether you're seeking a romantic fine dining evening, a perfectly crafted cake for your special occasion, or a full-service catering experience, Willow Tree is dedicated to exceeding your expectations. Join us and discover why we're a beloved culinary destination in Western North Carolina.

Our Culinary Creations

Signature Dishes That Define Willow Tree.

Prime Rib

Our succulent Prime Rib, slow-roasted to perfection and seasoned with a house blend of herbs and spices. A true classic.

Filet Mignon

Tender, melt-in-your-mouth Filet Mignon, grilled to your exact preference and served with our signature demi-glace.

Pork Chop

A thick-cut, juicy Pork Chop, seared to a golden finish and complemented by seasonal accompaniments.

Grilled Caesar

A sophisticated take on a classic. Crisp romaine hearts, lightly grilled, with a creamy Caesar dressing, parmesan, and croutons.

Ribeye

Richly marbled and full of flavor, our Ribeye is grilled to perfection, delivering a robust and satisfying experience.

Trout

Locally sourced mountain Trout, pan-seared or grilled to a delicate crisp, served with fresh lemon-herb butter.
