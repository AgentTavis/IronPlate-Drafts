OUR STORY

More Than Just a Meal

At Emmets Social Table, we believe in bringing people together through exceptional food and a warm, inviting atmosphere. Our journey began with a simple passion: to craft house-made small plates using the freshest seasonal ingredients, served right here in Waxhaw.

Whether you're celebrating a cozy family gathering or planning a grand corporate event, our dedicated catering services are designed to make your occasion truly unforgettable. We pride ourselves on a diverse menu that caters to all tastes and dietary needs, ensuring every guest leaves completely satisfied. From exquisite appetizers to delightful desserts, our culinary team pours love and expertise into every dish. Let us handle the food, so you can fully immerse yourself in enjoying the moment with your loved ones or colleagues.

Come experience the heart of our kitchen and the warmth of our hospitality. We're more than a bar and grill; we're a place where memories are made around the table.
