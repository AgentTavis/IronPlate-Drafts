OUR STORY

More Than Just Coffee, It's a Community Hub.

Welcome to Coffee Time Bake Shop, a beloved family-owned and operated bakery and coffee shop nestled in the heart of Salem, NC. For over 30 years, we've had the immense privilege of serving our cherished community, becoming a daily destination for delicious treats and warm conversations.

What started as a simple dream has blossomed into a cherished local institution. Every morning, our bakers craft a delightful array of cakes, pastries, and breakfast items from scratch, using time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Our coffee is brewed with care, complementing our fresh offerings perfectly. We believe in the power of good food, good coffee, and good company to bring people together, making every visit feel like coming home.

"Coffee Time Bake Shop feels like a warm hug every morning. Their commitment to quality and community shines through in every pastry and every smile. A true Salem gem!"
— A Happy Regular
