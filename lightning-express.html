Our Journey

Welcome to Your Home Away From Home in East Bend

Nestled right off NC-67, Lightning Express is more than just a bar and grill; it's a cornerstone of the East Bend community. We believe in the simple pleasure of good food, friendly faces, and a welcoming atmosphere that feels just like coming home. Our doors open bright and early, Monday through Friday, ready to serve you hearty breakfasts and satisfying lunches that fuel your day.

At Lightning Express, we're dedicated to home-style cooking, preparing every dish with fresh ingredients and a touch of local love. Whether you're a regular, a passerby, or new to East Bend, we invite you to experience our casual dining, where every meal is crafted to bring comfort and joy. It's our passion to provide a place where neighbors gather, stories are shared, and delicious memories are made.
