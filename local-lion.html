Our Story

Crafting Moments, One Roast at a Time.

At Local Lion, we believe every cup tells a story. Nestled in the heart of Boone, NC, we're more than just a bar and grill — we're a community hub where quality coffee meets mountain inspiration. Our journey began with a simple commitment: to deliver unparalleled freshness.

That's why our coffee beans are roasted daily, ensuring that rich aroma and robust flavor you've come to love. It’s in these carefully prepared mountain beans that we find, and share, a fresh perspective with every guest. Whether you're fueling up for an adventure or winding down with friends, Local Lion is your home away from home.
