Our Journey

A Taste of Southern Hospitality, Born in Teachey

Welcome to Southern Bliss Grill

Founded with passion and a vision for delicious, homemade cooking by Debra Haithcock and Christy Baggett, Southern Bliss Grill officially opened its doors on July 8, 2024. More than just a bar and grill, we've created a welcoming space where the Teachey community can gather, share hearty meals, and experience genuine Southern hospitality.

We're proud to bring you fresh, flavorful dishes, prepared with that special Southern touch that makes every bite feel like home. From our early morning breakfasts to our satisfying lunches, every meal is crafted with care and a commitment to quality ingredients. Come on down and experience the bliss that only true Southern cooking can provide!
