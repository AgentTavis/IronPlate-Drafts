Our Story

Crafting Exceptional Brews, Building Community.

At Wicked Weed, we've cultivated a reputation for an exceptional beer list. Our passion for quality shines through in a diverse selection of our own finely crafted brews on tap, complemented by a thoughtful array of cans and bottles from other respected brands. We also proudly offer wine and cocktails for those looking for something different.

Many of our guests quickly become regulars, drawn in by standouts like our Appalachian, a session IPA that delivers a truly delicious experience. For a lighter touch, the Day Light is a wonderfully refreshing option, while our Pernicious stands as a most excellent full IPA, a favorite among hop enthusiasts.

While our focus is primarily on the superb libations, we understand the desire for great food. Conveniently, you'll find yourself surrounded by fantastic culinary options right outside our doors, with local burger, sandwich, and fried chicken stalls, including the beloved Shake Shack, from which you are welcome to bring snacks and meals right inside to enjoy with your Wicked Weed beverage.
