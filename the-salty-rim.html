Our Journey

Where Coastal Flavors Meet Laid-Back OBX Vibes

Nestled in the heart of Corolla, The Salty Rim is more than just a bar and grill – it's a coastal escape where delicious food, refreshing drinks, and good times come together. We set out to create a spot where locals and visitors alike could unwind after a day on the beach, grab a bite, and enjoy the true Outer Banks spirit.

Our passion for fresh ingredients shines through in every dish, from our signature flavor-packed tacos to our juicy burgers. We believe in crafting an experience that's as memorable as the Corolla sunsets, served with a friendly smile and a relaxed atmosphere that feels just like home. Come on in, kick back, and let us take care of the rest.
