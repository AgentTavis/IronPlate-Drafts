Our Heritage

A Family's Passion for Authentic Japanese Hibachi

At Chopsticks, we believe that great food brings people together. As a proud family-run establishment in the heart of Lillington, NC, we've poured our hearts into creating a dining experience that feels like home.

Our journey began with a simple desire: to share the joy and excitement of traditional Japanese hibachi with our community. Every dish is crafted with care, using fresh ingredients and time-honored techniques, ensuring that each bite is a celebration of flavor.

We're dedicated to more than just serving meals; we're dedicated to fulfilling all of your hibachi-related desires, from the sizzling grill to the warm, welcoming atmosphere. Come join our family table and taste the difference passion makes.
