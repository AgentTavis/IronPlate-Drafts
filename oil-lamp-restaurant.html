Our Heritage

A Taste of Tradition, Crafted Fresh Daily

Welcome to The Oil Lamp Restaurant! Nestled in the heart of Perry, NC, we're more than just a place to eat – we're a gathering spot where friends and family come together over plates of fresh, flavorful, and distinct food. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a cozy atmosphere where every meal feels like a celebration.

We pride ourselves on using quality ingredients, prepared with passion and served with a smile. From our sizzling grill to our hearty daily specials, every dish is designed to satisfy your appetite and leave you wanting more. We believe good food brings people together, and we're committed to making every visit a memorable one for our cherished community.

Order online for convenience, or pull up a chair and experience the warmth and flavor that only The Oil Lamp can offer. We're excited to share our culinary passion with you!
