Our Story

Crafted with Passion, Served with Pride.

At The Upper Deck, our story is woven into every dish we serve, particularly our famed Upper Deck Signature Plate. It's more than just a meal; it's a testament to our commitment to fresh ingredients and bold flavors, designed to bring people together for an unforgettable experience.

Our signature plate features succulent grilled chicken breast over crisp lettuce, perfectly complemented by smoky bacon, creamy avocado, tangy blue cheese crumbles, a hard-boiled egg, and juicy tomatoes. All these incredible ingredients are meticulously tossed in our rich ranch dressing, creating a loaded lineup that truly brings the flavor from every angle.

We believe in honest food, great company, and a welcoming atmosphere. From our kitchen to your table, every detail is considered to ensure you savor the best bar and grill experience in Greensboro.
