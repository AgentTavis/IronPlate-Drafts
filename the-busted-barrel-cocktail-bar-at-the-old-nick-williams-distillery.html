Our Journey

From Distillery to Destination

Nestled in the heart of Lewisville, The Busted Barrel Cocktail Bar emerged from a passion for exceptional spirits and community. For years, guests have savored the unique tastes of The Old Nick Williams Co. Farm & Distillery.

Thanks to the passing of NC Senate Bill 290 on September 1, 2019, we proudly opened our doors, transforming our beloved distillery into a vibrant gathering spot. Now, alongside our renowned spirits, we craft exquisite cocktails, pour fine wines, and offer a selection of refreshing beers.

We invite you to gather your friends and family, unwind amidst the beautiful farm scenery, and create lasting memories with every sip. It's more than just a drink; it's an experience rooted in local tradition and genuine hospitality.

The Busted Barrel Difference

Beyond the Drink, It's an Experience

Crafted with Our Own Spirits

Every cocktail is a testament to the quality of Old Nick Williams Distillery. Taste our signature ONW Bourbon in a classic Old Fashioned or the unique Zeb's Rye Whiskey in a Sazerac, knowing it's distilled just steps away.

Unique Farm-to-Glass Experience

Enjoy hand-crafted drinks amidst the scenic beauty of our working farm. It's a setting unlike any other, offering a serene escape and a true taste of Lewisville's local charm. Perfect for a Blueberry Mint Julep.

Rated & Loved by Locals

With a stellar 4.9-star rating across 143 reviews, our guests consistently praise our friendly service, inviting atmosphere, and exceptional cocktails like the popular Whiskey-Rita. Come see why we're a community favorite!
