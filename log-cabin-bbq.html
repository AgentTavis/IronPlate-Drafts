Our Story

Generations of Authentic Carolina BBQ Flavor

At Log Cabin BBQ, we're not just serving food; we're upholding a cherished tradition. Nestled in the heart of Albemarle, NC, our cabin has been a cornerstone for locals and visitors alike, dishing out the truest taste of Southern slow-cooked barbecue for decades.

Every bite tells a story of dedication, from the carefully selected Boston butts and pork shoulders to the hours of slow cooking over hickory smoke. We believe real BBQ is an art form, served exactly how you like it – hand chopped, tenderly pulled, or perfectly sliced. It's the taste of home, of community, and of genuine North Carolina hospitality.
