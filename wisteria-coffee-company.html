Our Journey

More Than Just a Coffee Shop, It's a Community Hub.

Wisteria Coffee Company isn't just a place to grab a quick coffee; it's a beloved local spot where neighbors connect, friends gather, and good times are brewed daily. Nestled in the heart of King, NC, we've built a reputation for exceptional coffee, delicious food, and a warm, inviting atmosphere that feels like home.

We believe in the power of a perfect cup and a friendly smile to start your day right or unwind in the evening. From our carefully selected beans to our freshly prepared bar & grill favorites, every detail is crafted with passion and a commitment to serving our community with the very best.
