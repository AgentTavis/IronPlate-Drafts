Our Journey

From Instagram to Indianapolis: A Story of Connection

Our journey began with ‘Muslims of the World,’ an Instagram initiative while our owner studied at Butler University. It quickly became a beacon of hope for Muslims worldwide, showcasing stories of resilience, faith, and humanity. This spirit of community and shared humanity is the very foundation of MOTW Coffee and Pastries.

Here in Indianapolis, NC, we extend that same mission into every cup we brew and every pastry we bake. We're more than just a place to grab a quick bite; we're a vibrant hub where diverse stories converge, where friendships are forged, and where the simple act of sharing good food and drink creates lasting memories. Every visit is an invitation to connect, to belong, and to experience a slice of global inspiration with local warmth.

"MOTW is truly special. It's not just about the incredible coffee, but the warm, inclusive atmosphere. I always leave feeling uplifted and connected. A true community gem!"
