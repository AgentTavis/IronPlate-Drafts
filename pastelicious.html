Our Heritage

A Taste of Authentic Mexico, Baked with Love in Mt Airy

At Pastelicious, we invite you on a culinary journey into the heart of authentic Mexican baking. We understand that traditional Mexican pastries can sometimes be a delightful mystery – a feast for the eyes that challenges preconceived notions of sweetness. Unlike overly sugary treats, our creations honor the genuine flavors of Mexico, offering a true taste of home.

We craft each item with passion, from the tempting visual delights to the humble buns bursting with savory jalapeños and cheese or sweet fruit fillings. Every bite is an authentic experience, reflecting the rich culinary heritage we bring to Mt Airy. Come discover the true essence of Mexican baking, where every item tells a story, and every flavor is a celebration.
