Our Story

Where Every Meal Feels Like Coming Home

Nestled just off the beaten path in Providence, NC, Lillards Family Restaurant is more than just a place to eat – it's a family tradition in the making. Our building might show a little age, but what truly matters is the warmth and flavor inside. We're a small, family-owned business, proudly run by a father and son duo who are passionate about good food and genuine hospitality.

From the moment you walk through our doors, you're greeted like family. We've poured our hearts into creating a menu of comforting American classics that keep our regulars coming back. It’s not uncommon to find a friendly face from the neighborhood enjoying a meal right alongside you.

We believe in simple, honest food made with care. Every dish we serve is crafted to bring you that familiar, satisfying taste of home. We're doing well, thanks to the incredible support of our community, and we invite you to experience the Lillard's difference for yourself.

"This small family-owned spot is a hidden gem! Always greeted by the owner or his father, and they truly make you feel like family. Every dish we've ordered has been delicious and comforting." - A Loyal Customer
