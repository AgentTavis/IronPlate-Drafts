Our Legacy

Where Heartfelt Cooking Meets Hometown Charm

At The Country Barn, we believe in bringing folks together over good food and a warm, inviting atmosphere. Nestled right here in Beulaville, NC, we've poured our hearts into creating a spot where every visit feels like coming home.

Our journey began with a simple passion: crafting delicious, homemade goodness from scratch. Visitors can enjoy delicious homemade donuts, flavorful sandwiches, and a variety of snacks and spreads. With its charming atmosphere and quality products, The Country Barn is more than just a place to eat – it's a community gathering spot.
