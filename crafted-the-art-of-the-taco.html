Our Journey

More Than Just Tacos, It's an Art Form

Welcome to Crafted The Art of the Taco, our passion project born right here in Mebane. While our roots also reach Greensboro with "Crafted The Art of Street Food," our Mebane location is dedicated to perfecting the humble taco, transforming it into a culinary masterpiece.

We believe great food comes from fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and a touch of artistic flair. Every dish is a testament to our commitment to quality and creativity, designed to tantalize your taste buds and offer a memorable dining experience that feels both familiar and exciting.

We're proud to be a part of the vibrant Mebane community, serving up innovative Mexican cuisine with a warm, local touch. Come taste the difference that passion and precision make!

Our Culinary Creations

Taste The Art of the Taco

Carne Asada Taco

Perfectly grilled marinated steak, cilantro, onion, and a hint of lime. A timeless classic, expertly 'crafted'.

Al Pastor Taco

Succulent marinated pork slow-cooked on a vertical spit, topped with pineapple, onion, and cilantro.

Birria De Res Taco

Tender, slow-braised beef, rich consommé for dipping, onions, and cilantro. An authentic, flavorful experience.

Baja Fish Taco

Crispy fried white fish, zesty cabbage slaw, creamy chipotle sauce, and fresh pico de gallo.

Spicy Chorizo Taco

Savory and spicy Mexican chorizo, potato, cotija cheese, and a drizzle of avocado crema.

Veggie Fiesta Taco

A vibrant mix of roasted seasonal vegetables, black beans, corn salsa, and a sprinkle of fresh herbs.

Crafted Quesadilla

A generous portion of melted cheese and your choice of protein, grilled to perfection and served with sides.

Elote (Street Corn)

Grilled corn on the cob, slathered with crema, cotija cheese, chili powder, and lime. The perfect side!
