Our Legacy

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Memory.

Nestled at 110 S Marine Blvd, Capt. Bob Beck’s Marina Cafe has been a cornerstone of Jacksonville, NC, for over two and a half decades. What started as a local diner quickly grew into a beloved institution, known for its warm atmosphere and hearty American cuisine.

We believe in good food, great company, and traditions that last. Our doors have been open for generations, serving up delicious meals that bring families and friends together. We're proud to be Jacksonville's best-kept secret, and we invite you to become a part of our story.
