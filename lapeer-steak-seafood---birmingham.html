Our Story

Crafting Culinary Journeys on the Coast

Lapeer Steak & Seafood is a chef inspired coastal concept. We are dedicated to delivering an unparalleled dining experience right here in Birmingham, NC, blending the freshest local catches with premium cuts to create unforgettable dishes that celebrate the spirit of the coast.

Our passion for exceptional food and warm hospitality shines through in every dish and every interaction. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll feel the inviting ambiance that makes Lapeer Steak & Seafood a true local gem.
