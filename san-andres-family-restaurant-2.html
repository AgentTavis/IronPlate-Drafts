Our Journey

Delicious Meals, Family Values, Unbeatable Prices.

At San Andres Family Restaurant 2, we believe in crafting memorable dining experiences where great food, excellent service, and unbeatable prices come together. From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a customer, you're family.

We pride ourselves on every dish being cooked to order and served hot, from our expertly grilled hotdog buns that stay perfectly crisp to our renowned Philly Cheesesteak, a true customer favorite. Our commitment to quality extends to every crispy fry and every friendly smile. Having recently opened our second location right here in Madison, we're thrilled to bring our beloved flavors and warm hospitality to even more of our wonderful community.
