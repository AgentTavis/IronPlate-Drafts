Our Heritage

Experience the Heartfelt Charm of Cake Bakery & Café

At Cake Bakery & Café, we believe every visit should feel like coming home. Nestled in the heart of historic Mill Street in Downtown Grass Valley, our café is a warm, inviting haven where passion for baking and genuine hospitality intertwine.

We proudly boast an incredibly talented and artistic baker in Debbie Jensen. With her skilled hands, she introduces you to a delightful array of both savory and sweet desserts. From delicate pastries to hearty breakfast and lunch offerings, everything is made fresh in our kitchen daily, ensuring a taste that's simply unforgettable.

More than just delicious food, our dedicated staff strive to make each guest feel welcome and truly part of the Cake Bakery family. We’d love to make you an exquisite espresso drink featuring Wrecking Ball coffee, or perhaps you'd prefer to enjoy a robust dark roast from Taylor Lane while soaking in the vibrant atmosphere of our charming town.

Come and visit us today – we look forward to sharing a moment of sweetness with you.

"Cake Bakery & Café is an absolute gem! Every time I visit, I'm greeted with a smile, and Debbie's pastries are out of this world. The coffee is perfect too – it's truly a part of our community."

— Eleanor Vance, Local Resident
