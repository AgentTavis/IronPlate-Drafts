OUR STORY

From Local Gem to Elevated Experience

Woodstone Tavern began with a simple vision: to create a welcoming space where friends and family could gather. What started as a beloved local spot has gracefully evolved into an elevated dining experience, while still retaining its inviting, neighborhood charm.

Enjoy our beautiful patio dining, nestled amidst a vibrant garden of perennials and hanging plants. Step inside to discover our impressive 40-foot bar, offering a perfect setting for both lively evenings and relaxed conversations. We're dedicated to serving dishes that delight and creating memories that last.
