Our Heritage

Where Every Dish Tells a Family Tale.

At Lupitas, we pour our heart and soul into every dish, bringing the vibrant, authentic flavors of Oaxaca, Mexico, right here to Burgaw, NC. As a family-owned establishment, we believe in fresh ingredients, time-honored recipes, and creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels like family.

From our sizzling carne asada to our freshly made guacamole, each bite is a celebration of our heritage and passion for Mexican cuisine. We're proud to serve our community, offering a taste of tradition prepared with love, whether you choose to dine-in, grab takeout, or visit our food truck.
