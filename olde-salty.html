Our Story

Serving Carolina Beach's Best Seafood, Decades in the Making

Nestled right on the vibrant Carolina Beach Boardwalk, Olde Salty has been a beloved local spot for decades. Recommended by regulars who've enjoyed our fresh catches for over 30 years, we pride ourselves on simple, perfectly prepared seafood.

From plump, subtly seasoned fried shrimp to our popular steam combos, every dish is crafted to bring you the authentic taste of the coast. Come experience our lively, casual atmosphere, perfect for families and groups, and enjoy ocean breezes from our outdoor seating.

"This place truly feels like a local secret, even on the busy boardwalk. You can taste the care in every dish, especially those perfectly fried shrimp!" — A Happy Regular
