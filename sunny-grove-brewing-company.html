Our Roots

Brewing Community, One Pint at a Time

More than just a brewery, we're a gathering place for friends, family, and furry companions.

At Sunny Grove Brewing Company, we believe in the power of fresh, local ingredients and a welcoming atmosphere. Our passion is crafting unique, rotating beer styles right here in East County, offering a taste of Santee's vibrant spirit in every glass.

But a great brew needs great food! That's why we partner with San Diego’s best local food trucks, ensuring every visit tastes a little different — and a lot delicious. From savory to sweet, there's always something new to pair with your favorite Sunny Grove pint.

We've cultivated a laid-back, dog-friendly taproom vibe where everyone feels at home. Come on down, bring your pups, catch up with old friends, or make new ones. Unwind, relax, and be part of the Sunny Grove story.

Our Offerings

Fresh Brews & This Week's Food Truck Schedule

Our Signature Craft Brews

Experience a rotating selection of our finest craft beers, brewed on-site with local ingredients. From crisp IPAs to rich stouts and refreshing lagers, there's always a perfect pint waiting for you. Ask our friendly staff about this week's special taps!

Guest Taps & Local Favorites

Beyond our own creations, we feature a curated selection of guest taps from other esteemed local breweries and cideries. Discover new favorites and support the vibrant craft beverage scene of East County.

Wednesday Food Truck

Kick off your mid-week with delicious eats from a rotating local food truck. Check our social media or call ahead for this week's featured culinary partner!

Thursday Food Truck

Thursday evenings just got tastier! Grab a fresh pint and enjoy a unique menu from one of San Diego's top food trucks. Always something new and exciting.

Friday Food Truck

Celebrate the start of the weekend at Sunny Grove! Our Friday food truck brings incredible flavors to pair perfectly with your favorite brews and the lively taproom atmosphere.

Saturday Food Truck

Weekend vibes are in full swing with our Saturday food truck. Enjoy diverse cuisines, from gourmet burgers to ethnic street food, alongside your favorite Sunny Grove beers.

Food Truck Schedule Disclaimer

Our food truck lineup is dynamic and subject to change. For the most up-to-date schedule and menus, please visit our social media channels or call us directly!
