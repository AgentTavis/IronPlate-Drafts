Our Heritage

Where Southern Charm Meets Hearty Homestyle Cooking.

Located at 605 N McLewean St in the heart of Kinston, NC, Annie Bee's Country Cookin' is more than just a restaurant – it's a culinary journey back to the flavors of home. We pride ourselves on serving up generous portions of classic Southern comfort food, crafted with care and a sprinkle of Kinston's warm hospitality.

Since opening our doors, we've become a beloved local favorite, known for dishes that taste just like grandma used to make. We believe in good food, good company, and creating memorable experiences around the table. Come on down and experience the true spirit of country cookin' right here in Kinston.

Our Offerings

Freshly Prepared, Down-Home Comfort Food.

Our menu features daily specials and classic country staples, prepared fresh for you with the finest local ingredients. While our exact dishes rotate to celebrate seasonal flavors, here's a taste of the hearty fare you can expect when you visit Annie Bee's.

The Classic Fried Chicken

Crispy on the outside, juicy on the inside, our signature fried chicken is seasoned to perfection – a true Southern staple.

Homestyle Mac & Cheese

Rich, creamy, and baked to a golden finish. This cheesy delight is a crowd-pleaser and the ultimate comfort food side.

Southern Style Collards

Slow-cooked with smoky ham hocks, our collard greens are tender, flavorful, and packed with traditional Southern goodness.

Hearty Meatloaf

A family recipe, our succulent meatloaf is seasoned just right and topped with a savory glaze, served with mashed potatoes.

Sweet Potato Pie

A slice of pure bliss! Our homemade sweet potato pie is a velvety, spiced dessert that's the perfect end to any meal.

Daily Vegetable Sides

From fresh green beans to butter beans, our daily changing vegetable sides ensure there's always something fresh and delicious.
