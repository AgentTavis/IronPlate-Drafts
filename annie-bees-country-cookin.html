Our Story

Where good food and good company meet.

Annie Bee's Country Cookin' isn't just a place to eat; it's a taste of home right here in Kinston, NC. Located at 605 N McLewean St, we're a classic bar and grill serving up the kind of hearty, delicious meals that make you feel like you're at grandma's table.

We believe in simple, honest food made with love and served with a smile. From our sizzling platters to our ice-cold drinks, every dish is crafted to bring joy and satisfaction. Come on down and experience the warm, friendly atmosphere that makes us a local favorite.
