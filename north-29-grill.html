Our Story

Serving Kannapolis with Heart & Flavor

North 29 Grill has been a beloved fixture in Kannapolis, NC, bringing families and friends together over delicious, homemade meals. Nestled at 2800 N Cannon Blvd, we pride ourselves on being more than just a bar and grill – we're a community hub where quality food meets warm hospitality.

From our sizzling burgers to our hearty seafood platters, every dish is prepared with passion and the freshest ingredients. We're proud to be ranked among Kannapolis' top restaurants on platforms like Tripadvisor, a testament to our commitment to delivering an exceptional dining experience, every single visit.
