Our Story

Generations of Flavor, Right Here in Kannapolis.

Nestled at 2800 N Cannon Blvd, North 29 Grill isn't just a restaurant; it's a cornerstone of the Kannapolis community. For years, we've been proudly serving up classic American comfort food, crafted with passion and local charm.

From the moment you walk through our doors, you're not just a customer, you're part of our family. We believe in honest, delicious food and a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home. Our commitment to quality and heartwarming meals has made us a beloved local spot, a place where memories are made over mouthwatering burgers and perfectly cooked seafood.

As Tripadvisor noted, we're a highly-rated local choice, and we constantly strive to exceed expectations, one delicious plate at a time.
