OUR STORY

Crafting Laurinburg's Favorite Flavors for Years.

For years, Jesse's Subs has been a cornerstone of the Laurinburg community, dedicated to serving fresh, high-quality subs, pizzas, and appetizers. We pride ourselves on using the finest ingredients to create meals that bring families and friends together.

From our hearty hot and cold subs to our perfectly baked pizzas, every dish is prepared with passion and a commitment to flavor that keeps our customers coming back. We're not just a restaurant; we're a gathering place where great food and good times are always on the menu.
