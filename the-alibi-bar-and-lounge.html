Our Story

Your Local Hub for Food, Drinks & Fun.

Welcome to The Alibi Bar and Lounge, Thomasville's premier destination for good times, great food, and unforgettable entertainment. Nestled right on Salem Street, we've built a reputation as the local favorite where friends gather, laughter echoes, and every night promises something special.

From sizzling burgers and comfort food classics to a full bar offering your favorite brews and handcrafted cocktails, The Alibi is more than just a bar and grill. We’re a community cornerstone, dedicated to providing a vibrant atmosphere whether you're here for a quiet dinner, a lively dance party, or to catch some incredible live music. Escape the ordinary and make The Alibi your go-to spot!
