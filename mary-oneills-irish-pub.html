OUR STORY

Your Home Away From Home

Nestled in the charming town of Waxhaw, NC, Mary O'Neill's Irish Pub offers more than just a meal—it's an authentic experience steeped in Irish hospitality. We pride ourselves on creating an atmosphere that truly feels like home, a welcoming haven for locals and visitors alike.

Our dedicated staff are renowned for being nice, friendly, and always ready to help, ensuring every visit is a pleasant one. We keep our place clean and organized, providing a comfortable environment where you can relax and enjoy.

From the moment you step in, you'll discover a menu boasting a lot of options, including fantastic gluten-free choices, all prepared with a good flavor that captures the essence of hearty Irish cuisine. Come join us in the shadows of the Water Tower for a great pint and an unforgettable taste of Ireland right here in Waxhaw.
