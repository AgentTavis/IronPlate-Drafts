Our Heritage

Serving Shelby's Freshest Seafood for Years

Welcome to Fish House, formerly known as Shelby Oyster and Fish Company. Nestled in the heart of Shelby, NC, we've been dedicated to bringing the freshest seafood to our community for years. Our charming eatery is more than just a restaurant; it's a place where families gather, friends connect, and delicious memories are made.

We specialize in a variety of fresh seafood dishes, meticulously prepared to highlight the natural flavors of the ocean. From succulent oysters to our daily fresh catches, every dish is crafted with passion and served with our signature warm hospitality. We believe in quality ingredients, traditional techniques, and creating an atmosphere where everyone feels at home.
