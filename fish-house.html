Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience

Shelby Oyster and Fish Company is more than just a place to eat; it's a cherished part of the Shelby community. Nestled right here in Shelby, NC, we've dedicated ourselves to bringing you the freshest seafood dishes, prepared with passion and served with a smile.

We're known for our warm atmosphere – a place where families gather, friends laugh, and everyone feels at home. Our commitment to quality means every dish, from our perfectly grilled fish to our savory oysters, is crafted to delight your taste buds. We believe in good food, good company, and creating memories that last.

Come on in and discover why we've become Shelby's go-to destination for an unforgettable seafood experience. We can't wait to welcome you!
