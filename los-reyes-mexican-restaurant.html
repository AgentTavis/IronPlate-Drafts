Our Heritage

A Taste of Mexico, Crafted with Love

At Los Reyes, we bring the vibrant flavors and warm hospitality of Mexico straight to Snow Hill, NC. As a family-owned restaurant, our kitchen is a place where cherished recipes passed down through generations come to life. We believe in using the freshest ingredients, prepared with care and passion, to create dishes that are not just food, but a celebration of culture and community.

Every meal at Los Reyes is an invitation to experience authentic Mexican cuisine – from sizzling fajitas to comforting enchiladas. We're dedicated to providing a clean, welcoming environment where families and friends can gather, share laughter, and enjoy truly flavorful food.

Our Specialties

Savor the Flavors of Mexico

Tacos al Pastor

Marinated pork, grilled with pineapple, served with fresh onion and cilantro on soft corn tortillas.

Enchiladas Verdes

Tender chicken or cheese enchiladas smothered in a vibrant green tomatillo sauce, topped with sour cream.

Carne Asada

Perfectly grilled marinated steak, served with fluffy rice, refried beans, and warm, fresh tortillas.

Fajitas Supremas

Sizzling grilled chicken, steak, or shrimp with sautéed bell peppers and onions, served with all the fixings.

Burrito California

A massive burrito filled with succulent steak, crispy fries, melted cheese, fresh guacamole, and sour cream.

Chiles Rellenos

Fire-roasted poblano peppers stuffed with savory cheese, lightly battered and fried, then topped with our special sauce.
