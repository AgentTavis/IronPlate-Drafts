Our Journey

More Than Just a Pub – It's a Community Hub

Stooges Pub & Grub, located at 13230 Albemarle Rd in Mint Hill, NC, is more than just your average bar and grill. We pride ourselves on being a lively and welcoming pub, a true cornerstone of the Mint Hill community. Since opening our doors, our mission has been simple: to create a comfortable spot where friends and neighbors can gather for great times.

With a wide selection of delicious food, ice-cold drinks, and the best live music scene around, we've crafted an atmosphere that keeps our regulars coming back and welcomes newcomers with open arms. Whether you're here to catch a game, enjoy a meal with family, or dance the night away, Stooges is the place to be.
