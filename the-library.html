Our Legacy

More Than Just Pizza: A Culinary Journey in Every Slice

My daughter and I stopped into The Library as an afterthought, leaving the archery academy back to Jacksonville. To our pleasant surprise, we discovered it was their second grand opening! We were instantly greeted at the door by a lovely and happy server, setting the tone for a wonderful experience.

The restaurant is spacious, thoughtfully built to accommodate large groups while ensuring comfortable spacing for all guests, allowing for both lively gatherings and intimate meals. We ordered, and the food arrived piping hot in record time. The pizza I ordered for us was absolutely delicious, a true highlight after a long day. Every dish reflects our commitment to fresh, local ingredients and global flavors.

Under the expert guidance of award-winning Chef Johannes Klapdohr, we bring a seasonal menu to life, crafting recipes from around the world with an American twist. Come and discover why The Library is becoming a cherished spot in Houston, NC, where every visit feels like coming home.

Our Selections

Crafted Pizzas & American Favorites

The Classic Margherita

San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, basil, and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil. A timeless favorite.

Spicy Pepperoni & Honey

Crispy pepperoni, spicy Calabrian chilies, house-made tomato sauce, and a finishing touch of local hot honey.

Garden Harvest Pizza

Seasonal roasted vegetables, goat cheese, garlic confit, and a light herb pesto base. Vegetarian delight.

The Meat Lover's Feast

Pepperoni, Italian sausage, crispy bacon, and savory ham over our rich tomato sauce and mozzarella blend.

Truffle Mushroom Dream

Assorted wild mushrooms, truffle oil, Fontina cheese, and fresh thyme on a creamy white sauce.

BBQ Chicken & Red Onion

Smoky BBQ chicken, crisp red onions, cilantro, and a blend of cheddar and mozzarella cheeses.
