Our Heritage

Crafting Culinary Memories in Waynesville

Welcome to Fuego, a truly Modern American kitchen nestled in a cozy spot here in Waynesville. Our passion is to serve familiar items with a sophisticated, modern twist, creating an unforgettable dining experience for our guests.

From the moment you step inside, you'll discover a menu curated with care, featuring everything from delightful bao buns to a refreshingly precise tuna poke bowl. Our commitment extends beyond the plate, ensuring good service in a warm and inviting atmosphere. Don't forget to save room for our exquisite chocolate lava cake or a slice of our tangy key lime pie – the perfect end to any meal.
