Our Story

Crafting Unforgettable Seafood Experiences in Greensboro

At Fishbones, we believe in more than just serving food; we believe in creating memories. Our journey began with a simple passion: to bring the highest quality seafood and the freshest local produce to the heart of Greensboro.

It's where the beer flows like wine and the women flock instinctively like the salmon of Capistrano. We meticulously craft each entree, ensuring every dish is a culinary delight. Beyond our exquisite seafood, we proudly feature a fully stocked bar with 8 rotating taps, a great selection of wines, and signature cocktails to complement your meal. We're a local spot where friends, family, and good food come together.
