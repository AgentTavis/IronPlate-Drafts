Our Journey

More Than Just Coffee, It's Our Community.

Welcome to Something's Brewing Coffee Shop, the heart of Raeford, NC! We are incredibly proud to be a Veteran and locally owned and operated business, pouring passion into every cup.

Our mission is simple: to serve you the BEST coffee in Raeford. We achieve this by proudly partnering with Cactus Creek Coffee Roasters, a local gem that provides us with exceptional, freshly roasted beans. It's this commitment to quality and community that makes every visit special.

Whether you're starting your day, taking a break, or catching up with friends, we invite you to experience the warm, friendly atmosphere and delicious offerings that have made us a local favorite.

“Walking into Something's Brewing feels like coming home. The coffee is consistently amazing, and the staff always remembers my order. It's truly the best place to start my day in Raeford.”

— A Happy Regular
