Our Journey

Bringing the Heart of El Salvador to Lenoir

Welcome to Los Izotes, where every dish tells a story of tradition, flavor, and family. Nestled just off Highway 321 in Lenoir, NC, we've poured our hearts into creating a dining experience that transports you straight to El Salvador and Mexico, right here in your neighborhood.

Our kitchen is a vibrant blend of authentic El Salvadorean traditional dishes and Mexican favorites, each prepared with our unique touch. We believe in fresh ingredients, time-honored recipes, and the joy of sharing good food with great company. From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a customer; you're family.

Taste the Tradition

A Glimpse into Our Flavors

Discover a curated selection of our most beloved El Salvadorean traditional dishes and Mexican specialties, all prepared with the freshest ingredients and our authentic touch. (Full menu available in-restaurant.)

Authentic Pupusas

Handmade corn tortillas stuffed with savory fillings like cheese, beans, or chicharrón, grilled to perfection and served with curtido and salsa.

Savory Tacos

Classic Mexican tacos featuring your choice of perfectly seasoned meats, fresh onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime.

Hearty Platters

Generous plates featuring grilled meats, fresh vegetables, rice, and beans, offering a complete and satisfying meal.

Fresh Ceviche

Tangy and refreshing seafood marinated in citrus juices, mixed with crisp vegetables – a taste of coastal freshness.

Signature Burritos

Packed with flavor, our burritos come with your favorite fillings, rice, beans, and fresh toppings, wrapped in a warm tortilla.

Sweet Plantains

Caramelized sweet plantains, a delightful and comforting side dish or dessert, perfectly complementing any meal.
