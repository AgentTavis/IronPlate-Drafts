Our Journey

From Our Kitchen to Your Table

At Los Izotes, we bring the heart of El Salvador and the vibrant spirit of Mexico right here to Lenoir, NC. Our journey began with a passion for sharing authentic flavors and dishes prepared with traditional recipes and our unique touch.

Conveniently located just off Highway 321, we've become a cherished spot for families and friends to gather, share, and enjoy truly memorable meals. Every dish tells a story, blending the rich culinary traditions of two incredible cultures. We invite you to experience the warmth and taste that makes Los Izotes a local gem.
