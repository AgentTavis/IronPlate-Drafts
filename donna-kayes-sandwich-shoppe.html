Our Journey

Handcrafted with Heart, Served with a Smile.

At Donna Kaye's Sandwich Shoppe, we believe a great sandwich starts with great ingredients. That's why we meticulously select premium meats & cheeses, bake our bread fresh daily, and partner with local Elkin growers for the freshest produce.

Every sandwich is a labor of love, made to order with dedication and care. We're told it takes a bit longer than most sandwich shops, but we promise, the rich flavors and quality ingredients are always worth the wait!

Our owner, Donna Kaye, is passionate about customer service and making sure every guest feels like family. It's this commitment to quality and warmth that makes us a beloved spot in the heart of Elkin, NC.
