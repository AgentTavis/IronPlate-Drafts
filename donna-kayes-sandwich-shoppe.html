Our Story

Crafted With Care, Served With a Smile.

At Donna Kaye's Sandwich Shoppe, we believe a great sandwich is worth savoring. Located proudly in the heart of Elkin, we're dedicated to crafting memorable meals using only the freshest, premium ingredients available.

We take immense pride in our selection of quality meats, fine cheeses, crisp local produce, and freshly baked bread. You might find our process takes a touch longer than your average grab-and-go, but that's because every sandwich is made with care, precision, and a warm smile.

Our owner, Donna Kaye, ensures every guest feels like family, providing excellent customer service that keeps our patrons coming back, often just days after their last visit! It’s this dedication to quality and community that makes DK's a beloved local spot.
