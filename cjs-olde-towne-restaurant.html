Our Heritage

Taste the Tradition, Feel the Warmth.

At CJ's Olde Towne Restaurant, we believe in serving more than just food; we serve a piece of Asheboro's heart. For years, we've been the community's go-to spot for comfort food and friendly faces.

Our commitment is simple: well-cooked, large portions, served quickly, and always with a smile. We pride ourselves on offering an experience that feels like home, without breaking the bank. It's why we're consistently rated high and cherished by our local patrons.

Whether you're starting your day with our famous breakfast or enjoying a classic diner meal, you'll find quality and value that brings people back again and again. Come see why CJ's is where Asheboro eats.
