Our Story

More Than Just a Meal: It's an Arcadia Experience.

Welcome to Arcadia Restaurant, where every visit feels like coming home. Nestled in the heart of Lincolnton, NC, we pride ourselves on offering a genuine, no-frills experience.

Step into our cool dive bar atmosphere, meet super friendly people, and discover why locals rave about our amazing burgers and dogs, perfectly paired with the coldest beer in town. We believe in honest food, fair prices, and creating a space where memories are made. It's more than just a meal; it's the Arcadia experience you can't beat.
