Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

At Arcadia Restaurant, we believe in good food, cold beer, and even better company. Nestled right here in Lincolnton, NC, we've cultivated that "cool dive bar vibe" everyone loves, where strangers become friends and every visit feels like coming home.

Matt S. said it best: "Cool dive bar vibes. Super friendly people. Cold beer. Amazing burgers and dogs. Fair prices. Can't beat it." We've built our reputation on these simple truths, serving up delicious comfort food with a smile, day in and day out.

From our sizzling burgers to our perfectly grilled hot dogs, every item on our menu is prepared with care, ensuring a taste that keeps you coming back for more. Come on down, grab a seat, and discover why Arcadia Restaurant is a true Lincolnton gem.

Our Offerings

Taste The Arcadia Difference

The Arcadia Classic Burger

Our signature burger, featuring a juicy patty, fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and our secret sauce on a toasted bun.

Spicy Chili Dog

A perfectly grilled hot dog smothered in our homemade spicy chili, topped with shredded cheddar cheese and diced onions.

Crispy Onion Rings

Golden-fried to perfection, these crispy onion rings are the ideal companion to any meal. Served with a zesty dipping sauce.

Loaded Nachos

Crispy tortilla chips piled high with seasoned ground beef, melted cheese, jalapeños, salsa, sour cream, and guacamole.

Sweet Potato Fries

A healthier, yet equally delicious, alternative to regular fries. Sweet, savory, and served with a side of maple aioli.

Classic BLT Sandwich

A timeless favorite: crispy bacon, fresh lettuce, and ripe tomatoes served on toasted artisan bread with a smear of mayonnaise.
