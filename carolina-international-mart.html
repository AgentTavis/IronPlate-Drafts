OUR STORY

Bringing Farm-Fresh Flavors to Raleigh

At Carolina International Mart, we believe in serving food that's not just delicious, but also rooted in quality and community. While our name reflects our diverse offerings, our heart beats for authentic, fresh ingredients, especially when it comes to our Bar and Grill selections.

We are proud to partner with trusted local farms like Fuster Cluck Farm, a small, family-operated farm near Kenly, North Carolina. They raise their own chicken, pork, and lamb, and partner with other local farms to provide high-quality beef and other carefully sourced produce. This commitment ensures that every dish we prepare carries the unparalleled taste of true farm-to-table freshness.

Come and experience a menu where tradition meets innovation, served with a friendly smile and a warm, inviting atmosphere. We're more than just a Mart; we're a gathering place for friends, family, and fantastic food.
