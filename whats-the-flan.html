Our Heritage

A Warm Welcome Awaits You in Mebane

At What's the Flan, we believe dining should be an experience, not just a meal. Our commitment to creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere is matched only by our passion for exceptional food.

What's the Flan started with a simple idea: bring together the best of classic diner comfort with a touch of unique, curated culinary delight. We're nestled right here in Mebane, proud to serve our community a distinct dining experience.

Our kitchen is a hub of creativity, where traditional recipes meet fresh, high-quality ingredients. We're more than just a place to eat; we're a place to connect, laugh, and savor moments. That's why our seating is intentionally limited – to foster an intimate setting where every guest feels like family.
