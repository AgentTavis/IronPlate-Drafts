OUR STORY

A Flavorful Journey, Redefined

At Luna Rotisserie and Empanadas, we pride ourselves on being that rare breed of establishment that only comes along once in a blue moon. Our passion for culinary exploration has led us to create a menu whose cuisine doesn't fit neatly into any established category, but instead defines a new category of its own.

From our vibrant Durham kitchen, we craft dishes that tell a story, blending traditional rotisserie techniques with the beloved versatility of empanadas, infused with unique flavors inspired by diverse culinary traditions. Every dish is a testament to our commitment to fresh ingredients, bold spices, and a truly unforgettable dining experience.
