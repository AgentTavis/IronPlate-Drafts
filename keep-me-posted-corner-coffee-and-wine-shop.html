OUR STORY

More Than Just a Coffee Shop, It's the Heart of Hayesville.

Nestled in the charming town of Hayesville, Keep Me Posted & Corner Coffee and Wine Shop offers a unique blend of essential services and welcoming community space. We're not your typical big-city coffee shop; instead, we evoke the spirit of a beloved small-town general store, where locals gather, business gets done, and connections are made.

At our core, we're a reliable FedEx and UPS shipper, ensuring your packages get where they need to go. But within the same walls, you'll discover a cozy coffee and wine shop, perfect for catching up with friends, hosting an informal business meeting, or simply enjoying a moment of peace with a quality brew or a glass of local wine.
