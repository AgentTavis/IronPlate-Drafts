Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience

At Laura's Little Burger Joint, we've been serving up Decatur's finest burgers since before we were voted the #1 Burger in 2013! Customers keep coming back, often telling us it's their 3rd, 5th, or even 10th visit – and we're proud to say we never disappoint.

Our award-winning burgers are everything you dream of: fresh ground meat, hand-pattied to a generous 7oz (though many say they feel more like 8-9oz!). Every bite is bursting with flavor, incredibly juicy without ever being greasy. Truly, they are some of the best burgers you will ever eat, whether you choose to dine inside or enjoy our outdoor seating.
