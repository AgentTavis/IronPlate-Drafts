Our Heritage

From Our Family Kitchen to Your Table.

Welcome to Venice Italian, Yanceyville's beloved destination for authentic Italian and delicious pizza. Located conveniently at 1748 NC Hwy 86 N, we've been serving the community with passion and dedication, bringing the rich flavors of Italy right to your neighborhood.

From hearty lunches to exquisite dinners, our menu is crafted with care, featuring classic Italian dishes and mouth-watering pizzas made with the freshest ingredients. We pride ourselves on creating a warm, inviting atmosphere where families and friends can gather to enjoy great food and even better company.

With a 4.4-star rating from over 549 reviews, our commitment to quality and exceptional service shines through. Come experience the true taste of Italy, right here in Yanceyville!
