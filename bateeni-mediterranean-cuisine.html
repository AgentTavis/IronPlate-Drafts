Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Family Tradition.

At Bateeni Mediterranean Cuisine, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a family-owned heart that beats with the spirit of Goldsboro, NC. Since opening our doors, we've poured our passion into crafting authentic Mediterranean flavors, built on generations of recipes and a deep commitment to our community.

Every dish we serve is a taste of home, a celebration of heritage, and an invitation to gather around our table. Experience the warmth, the flavor, and the authentic hospitality that defines Bateeni. We believe in good food, good company, and making every visit feel like coming home.
