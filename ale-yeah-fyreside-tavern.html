Our Journey

More Than Just a Tavern, It's Family.

Welcome to Ale Yeah Fyreside Tavern, your true #hometownhangout in Granite Falls. We set out to create more than just a place to grab a drink and a bite; we envisioned a community hub where friends and family gather, stories are shared, and memories are made.

From the crackling fyreside ambiance to the inviting sounds of live music filling the air every Friday and Saturday, our tavern embodies the warmth and spirit of Granite Falls. We pride ourselves on our rotating selection of 20 craft beer taps, carefully curated to offer something new and exciting with every visit. Paired with our delicious food, it's the perfect recipe for a great night out.

Ale Yeah Fyreside Tavern is where everyone feels like family. Come on in, pull up a chair, and experience the local charm that makes us a beloved spot in the community.
