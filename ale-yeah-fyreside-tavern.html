Our Journey

More Than Just a Tavern, It's Family.

Ale Yeah Fyreside Tavern, nestled in the heart of Granite Falls, began as a vision to create the ultimate local gathering spot. From our beginnings as Fyreside Bottles & Brews, we've evolved into a vibrant tavern where friends become family, and every visit feels like coming home. We're proud to be a cornerstone of our community, offering a warm welcome, incredible craft beers, and delicious food that keeps you coming back.

We're known for fostering a strong sense of community – people truly feel like "family here!❤️". We believe in good times, great company, and the best local entertainment, making us the perfect spot for both a casual weeknight meal and an unforgettable weekend experience.

"Ale Yeah Fyreside Tavern is our absolute favorite place. It truly feels like our #hometownhangout, a place where everyone knows your name and the atmosphere is always buzzing with friendly faces and laughter."

Crispy Chicken Wings

Perfectly fried to a golden crisp, tossed in your choice of classic buffalo, tangy BBQ, or zesty garlic parmesan.

Loaded Nachos
