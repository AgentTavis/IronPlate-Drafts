OUR STORY

A Taste of Tradition, Crafted with Heart

At San Marcos, we're dedicated to bringing the vibrant, authentic flavors of Mexico right here to Mayodan, NC. Our dishes are crafted with passion and traditional recipes, ensuring every bite is a celebration.

We pride ourselves on a warm, welcoming atmosphere where families and friends can gather to enjoy good food and great company. It’s more than just a meal; it’s an experience that transports you straight to the heart of Mexico.
