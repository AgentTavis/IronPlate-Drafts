Our Legacy

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

Ward's Grill is a true step back in time, a classic 50's diner nestled in the quiet, charming town of Saluda, NC. But the history goes even deeper – our home is a beautiful building dating back to the 1890s, giving us a unique blend of vintage Americana and historic Southern charm.

We pride ourselves on serving up comforting breakfast and lunch with typical diner fare, all made with a dedication to quality and a warm, welcoming smile. From the moment you walk in, you're not just a customer; you're part of the Ward's Grill family. It's a place where memories are made, laughter is shared, and every meal feels like a taste of home. We invite you to experience the nostalgia for yourself!

"Ward's Grill perfectly captures that classic diner feel! Their biscuits are incredibly fluffy, and the coffee is always hot. It's our go-to spot every Saturday morning."
