Our Journey

More Than Just a Spot, It's a Community.

The Buzz Beer & Wine Co. is a beloved local spot in Wesley Chapel, NC. More than just a bar and grill, The Buzz is a true community hub where great company, exceptional brews, and delicious food come together.

We pride ourselves on a welcoming atmosphere and a commitment to quality that keeps our regulars coming back. Our passion is creating a space where neighbors can unwind, connect, and enjoy the simple pleasure of good food and drink in a friendly, local setting.

"The Buzz isn't just a place to grab a drink; it's an extension of our community. Always a warm welcome and an unforgettable time!"

– A Happy Regular
