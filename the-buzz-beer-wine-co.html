Our Story

More Than Just a Store, It's a Community Hub.

At The Buzz Beer & Wine Co., we believe in bringing people together over exceptional drinks. Nestled in the heart of Wesley Chapel, NC, we've carefully curated a collection of hundreds of hand-selected bottles from local favorites to rare international finds.

Whether you're looking for that perfect craft beer to unwind with, a sophisticated wine for a special occasion, or simply a friendly spot to hang out and discover something new, we've got you covered. We're passionate about what we do, and we love sharing our knowledge and enthusiasm with every guest.

Our Selection

Discover Your Next Favorite Bottle.

While we pride ourselves on an ever-changing, expansive selection, here are some of the categories you can expect to explore at The Buzz.

Craft Beers

Explore a rotating selection of local, regional, and national craft brews. From hoppy IPAs to smooth stouts, find your perfect pint.

Fine Wines

Journey through vineyards around the globe with our hand-picked collection of red, white, rosé, and sparkling wines.

Local Brews

Support North Carolina's vibrant brewing scene with an array of beers from our talented neighbors. Fresh and flavorful!

Seasonal Picks

Taste the season with special releases and limited-time offerings that capture the essence of spring, summer, fall, and winter.

Imported Delights

Discover unique flavors from renowned breweries and wineries across Europe, Asia, and beyond. Global tastes, locally found.

Ciders & Sours

For those who enjoy something different, our selection of refreshing ciders and intriguing sour beers offers a delightful twist.
