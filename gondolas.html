Our Heritage

Bringing Italy's Heart to the Appalachian Mountains

Nestled in the charming town of Franklin, NC, Gondolas offers a delightful taste of Italy in the heart of the Appalachian Mountains. We believe in creating more than just a meal; we craft an experience.

Our chefs pour their passion into every dish, using authentic recipes passed down through generations and the freshest ingredients. From hearty pastas to crispy pizzas, every bite transports you to the sun-drenched landscapes of Italy. Come, be part of our family tradition.
