Our Heritage

A Taste of Italy in the Heart of Appalachia

Nestled in the charming town of Franklin, NC, Gondolas offers a delightful taste of Italy in the heart of the Appalachian Mountains. For years, we've poured our passion into crafting authentic Italian dishes, using time-honored recipes and the freshest ingredients.

Our journey began with a simple dream: to bring the warmth and richness of true Italian family dining to our community. From classic pastas to crispy, flavorful pizzas, every dish at Gondolas is prepared with love and tradition, ensuring a memorable experience for every guest.

We believe in good food, good company, and creating a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels like family. Whether you're dining in, grabbing takeout, or planning a catered event, Gondolas is your destination for genuine Italian hospitality.
