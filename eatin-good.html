Our Story

More Than Just Good Food, It's a Community Hub

At Eatin' Good, we believe in crafting more than just meals – we create experiences. Nestled in the heart of Oneida, NC, we've quickly become your go-to gourmet sandwich shop, dedicated to serving fresh-cut meats daily and dishes made with passion.

Our doors are open to everyone, offering a clean and welcoming space where delicious food and friendly faces come together. We pride ourselves on a menu that offers variety, including fantastic vegetarian options, ensuring there's something to delight every palate. We're not just a restaurant; we're a part of the Oneida family.
