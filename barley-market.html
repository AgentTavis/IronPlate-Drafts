Our Journey

More Than Just a Bar, It's a Community Hub.

Barley Market isn't just a place to grab a drink; it's a Craft Beer Bottle Shop & Tasting Room in the heart of Lake Norman, Cornelius. We've created a warm, inviting space where friends gather, new connections are made, and great times are shared.

Our passion lies in bringing you an unparalleled selection, featuring 16 rotating taps and over 50 craft beer and wine selections from local gems and celebrated breweries alike. Each visit is an opportunity to discover your new favorite pour, perfectly paired with our delicious grill fare. We're proud to be a cornerstone of the Cornelius community, dedicated to quality, connection, and craft.
