Our Story

A Taste of Tarboro, Made with Love

Nestled within the vibrant Tru Vegas Event Center at 115 Commercial Rd, Tarboro, YumYum Shop is more than just a place to eat – it's a 'lil kitchen with a big heart.' We're proud to serve the Tarboro community, offering a delicious escape with our carefully crafted bar and grill favorites.

From our renowned Meat Lovers dishes to fresh daily specials, every plate is prepared with passion and a commitment to flavor. We believe in good food, good company, and creating memorable experiences, one bite at a time. Come taste why we're a local favorite!
