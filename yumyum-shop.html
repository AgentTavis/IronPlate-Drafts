Our Story

More Than Just a Meal: Our Heartfelt Journey

Nestled within the vibrant Tru Vegas Event Center at 115 Commercial Rd, YumYum Shop is more than just a place to eat; it's a culinary dream brought to life in Tarboro, NC. We're a local bar and grill with a simple mission: to serve up delicious food made with passion, creating a dining experience that feels like home.

Our founder envisioned a "Lil kitchen with the big ❤️"—a place where every dish is prepared with care and every customer is treated like family. We pride ourselves on crafting hearty, satisfying meals that bring smiles to faces, from our daily specials to our beloved meat lovers' favorites. Come taste the difference that genuine love for food and community makes!

From our humble beginnings, we've poured our heart into every dish, creating a true 'Lil kitchen with the big ❤️' for our community.
