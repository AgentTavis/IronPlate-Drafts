Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's Our Story.

Since opening our doors, 44 Mills Kitchen & Tap has become a cherished gathering place for friends and family across Concord, NC. We believe in crafting memorable experiences through great food, refreshing drinks, and a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Every dish is prepared with passion, and every pour is served with a smile. We’re more than just a bar and grill; we’re a cornerstone of the community, where every visit feels like coming home. We're proud to be a beloved local spot, consistently striving to exceed expectations and create lasting memories for everyone who walks through our doors.

Our Selection

Explore Our Craft

While our full menu is always evolving with fresh, seasonal ingredients, here's a taste of the types of delicious items you can expect from our kitchen and tap.

Signature Burgers

Hand-pattied, locally sourced beef, crafted to perfection with your choice of premium toppings.

Crispy Wings

Classic wings tossed in your choice of our house-made sauces, served with ranch or blue cheese.

Loaded Nachos
