Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal: A Community Hub in Concord

44 Mills Kitchen & Tap is more than just a place to eat; it's a beloved local spot in Concord, NC, where friends and family gather. We believe in crafting memorable experiences through delicious food, refreshing drinks, and a welcoming atmosphere that feels like home.

Since opening our doors, we've committed to being a cornerstone of the community, serving up classic bar & grill favorites with a unique twist. Every dish is prepared with care, and every tap is poured with precision, ensuring a consistent quality that keeps our guests coming back.
