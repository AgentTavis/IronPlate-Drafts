Our Story

More Than Just Breakfast, It's a Community.

Welcome to The Scullery, a hidden gem nestled in the heart of downtown Greenville, NC. We're proud to be your go-to breakfast spot, where every dish is prepared with passion and the freshest local ingredients.

Don't be fooled by our cozy quarters; we've designed a comfortable and clean space for you to relax and enjoy. Our dedicated team is known for being quick, attentive, and incredibly friendly, ensuring every visit feels like coming home.

From our amazing breakfast bowls to our delicious lattes and famous sausage cheddar cheese balls, we believe good food brings people together. We invite you to experience the warmth and flavors that make The Scullery a true local favorite.
