Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

At 311 Tavern, we believe that great food and good company are the keys to a perfect evening. From the outside, we might look unassuming, but step inside and prepare to be surprised by some of the best bar & grill fare in Walnut Cove.

We pride ourselves on crafting huge, delicious burgers – like our fan-favorite Pimento Cheese Burger or the classic Bacon Cheeseburger, often hailed as "the best burger he's had in a while" by our regulars. Paired with perfectly crispy fries and our signature ranch, or a side of tasty onion rings, you're in for a treat.

We're dedicated to providing a relaxed, friendly atmosphere where you can unwind. Whether you're enjoying a quiet early dinner or challenging a friend to a game of Connect 4 at your table, you'll feel right at home. All this, for an unbeatable value – it’s a dining experience you won’t soon forget.
