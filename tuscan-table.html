Our Story

From Our Heart to Your Table

Nestled in the heart of Black Mountain, Tuscan Table is more than just a diner – it's a culinary journey. We pride ourselves on creating a cafe-type vibe where every visit feels like coming home. Our passion lies in crafting delightful dishes with fresh ingredients, served with a genuine smile.

We believe that great food brings people together. That's why we pour our heart into every plate, from the comforting richness of our Squash Burrata to the savory depths of our Mushroom Wellington. Our aim is to not just feed you, but to create memorable moments around the table, whether you're inside enjoying our cozy ambiance or soaking up the sun on our welcoming patio.
