Our Heritage

More Than Just A Meal: It's Our Family's Passion.

Welcome to 573 Grill, Stoneville's beloved family-friendly bar and grill. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll feel the warmth of a true hometown restaurant. We believe great food starts with great ingredients and a whole lot of love.

That's why our burgers are always hand-pattied with care, ensuring every bite is juicy and flavorful. Our sauces? Crafted right here in-house, bringing unique tastes you won't find anywhere else. We're proud to serve up classic American favorites with that special 573 Grill touch.

Whether you're craving a legendary Trump burger, a fresh and zesty taco salad, or just a relaxing evening with good company, we've got something for everyone. We might have a bar, but our welcoming atmosphere is designed for families and friends to gather, laugh, and make memories around delicious food.
