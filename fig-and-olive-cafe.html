Our Journey

The Sweet Tale of Fig and Olive

For a while, the aroma of Fig & Olive Cafe has beckoned, and finally, we made it by for dessert – it did not disappoint! Our visit for the Biscoff New York style croissant and a decaf latte was a revelation. The croissant was exquisitely delicious, perfectly flaky with just the right amount of sweet, creamy filling that leaves you wanting more. And the latte? Truly one of the best we've ever had.

Our dedicated employees ensure a warm welcome and efficient service, making every visit a pleasure. We offer comfortable spaces for every group size, from intimate tables to larger gatherings and solo visits. Plus, the seamless connection to Nazareth Bakery next door means even more delightful treats are just a step away. Come discover your new favorite sweet escape at Fig & Olive Cafe.
