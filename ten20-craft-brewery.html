Our Heritage

Brewing Community, Crafting Memories

Welcome to TEN20 Craft Brewery, Louisville's premier destination for exceptional beer, delightful cocktails, and an atmosphere that feels just like home. Since opening our doors, we've been dedicated to creating the perfect gathering spot for every occasion—whether you're celebrating a special event, meeting up with old friends, or simply unwinding after a long day.

Our friendly, professional bartenders are always ready to welcome you in, ensuring a memorable experience with every visit. We believe in the power of good company, great drinks, and a vibrant community spirit. Come discover your new favorite spot!
