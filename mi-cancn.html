Our Culinary Delights

Savor the Flavors of Mexico

Taco Platters

Authentic corn or flour tortillas filled with your choice of savory meats, fresh toppings, and zesty salsas.

Enchilada Specials

Rolled tortillas brimming with delicious fillings, smothered in rich sauces and melted cheese.

Sizzling Fajitas

Grilled strips of steak or chicken, bell peppers, and onions served with warm tortillas and all the fixings.

Burrito Grandes

Large flour tortillas packed with rice, beans, meat, and cheese, then expertly rolled and served.

Quesadillas

Melted cheese and your favorite ingredients grilled to perfection between two crispy tortillas.

Nachos Supreme

Crispy tortilla chips piled high with generous servings of cheese, beans, meat, and fresh garnishes.

Fresh Guacamole

Our homemade guacamole, prepared fresh daily with ripe avocados, onions, cilantro, and a hint of lime.

Specialty Drinks

Quench your thirst with our selection of traditional Mexican beverages and refreshing aguas frescas.
