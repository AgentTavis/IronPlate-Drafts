Our Story

Where Every Bite Tells a Story of Family & Flavor

Welcome to Smoke a 'Lil Smoke, Marion's cherished family-run BBQ joint. We specialize in bringing you authentic Southern flavor, slow-smoked with love and care. Our mission is simple: to offer delicious food and super friendly staff, making every visit feel like coming home.

From our succulent pulled pork to our crispy fried pickles, every dish is crafted with passion. It's more than just a meal; it's a taste of tradition that keeps our customers coming back for more. Come join our family and experience the true taste of Marion BBQ!
