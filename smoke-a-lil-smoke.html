Our Heritage

From Our Family To Your Table: A Taste Of Tradition.

At Smoke a 'Lil Smoke, we're more than just a restaurant – we're a family tradition. Nestled in the heart of Marion, NC, we pride ourselves on serving up authentic, slow-smoked BBQ and other delicious delights, prepared with recipes passed down through generations. Every dish is a labor of love, designed to bring people together around great food.

We believe in friendly service, quality ingredients, and the unmistakable flavor that only comes from true pitmaster expertise. Join our family for a meal, and taste the difference passion makes.
