Our Heritage

Where Craft Meets Community: The Hub of Waynesville

Nestled in the heart of downtown Waynesville, Blue Ridge Beer Hub isn't just a place to grab a drink – it's a destination. Housed in a cool, old Masonic building, our hub offers an atmosphere that's truly one-of-a-kind. We pride ourselves on being the go-to spot for both local brews and those hard-to-find craft beers that excite even the most seasoned connoisseur.

From the moment you step in, you're greeted by a warm, inviting environment where friends gather, stories are shared, and the vibrant sound of live bluegrass often fills the air. Our dedicated team is passionate about ensuring every visit is memorable, whether you're a regular or joining us for the first time with a large group. We're more than a bar and grill; we're a cornerstone of the Waynesville community, a place where good times and great flavors consistently meet.

Our Offerings

Our Curated Selection

While our tap list rotates daily, here's a taste of what you can expect at the Hub. We feature an extensive selection of local craft beers, rare finds, ciders, and a thoughtful menu of delicious bites to pair with your brew.

Craft Beers On Tap

A rotating selection of the finest regional and national craft brews, curated for exceptional flavor and variety. Ask our staff for today's special pour!

Local Brews & Favorites

Showcasing the best of North Carolina's vibrant brewing scene. Support local and discover your new favorite pint right here.

Hard-to-Find Bottled Beers

Take home a unique bottle from our exclusive selection, perfect for connoisseurs seeking something special or for carry-out enjoyment.

Delicious Grill Menu

From savory appetizers to hearty meals, our grill offers satisfying options designed to perfectly complement your beer selection. (Food was amazing! - Julie B.)

Ciders & Sours

A refreshing alternative to traditional beer, our ciders and sours provide a delightful spectrum of crisp, tart, and fruity flavors.

Wine & Non-Alcoholic Options

For those preferring something different, we offer a small but thoughtful selection of wines and various non-alcoholic beverages.

Our full menu and daily tap list are updated regularly in-house! Stop by to see what's new.
