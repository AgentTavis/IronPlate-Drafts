Our Roots

Crafting Community, One Cup at a Time.

At Founders Grounds Coffee Company, we believe in more than just serving great coffee; we're dedicated to cultivating a vibrant community space right here in Speedway, NC. Our journey began with a simple passion: to create a place where friends, family, and neighbors could connect over expertly crafted beverages and delicious diner-style treats.

We're proud to be an independent coffee company, sourcing premium beans and preparing every drink with care. Whether you're grabbing a quick latte on the go or settling in for a leisurely meal, you'll find a warm, inviting atmosphere that feels like home. Come experience the heart of Speedway with us.

Our Creations

Popular Drinks to Fuel Your Day

Checkered Flag

Our signature blend, a robust and flavorful coffee that kicks off your day with a winning start. Perfect for true coffee connoisseurs.

Cafe Vanilla Latte

A classic favorite, expertly crafted with rich espresso, steamed milk, and a touch of sweet vanilla. Smooth, creamy, and comforting.

Espresso Martini

For those looking for a sophisticated kick. A perfectly balanced cocktail combining bold espresso with a hint of sweetness.

Iced Chai

Spicy, sweet, and perfectly chilled. A refreshing blend of black tea, exotic spices, and creamy milk, served over ice.

Matcha Madness

Vibrant green tea latte, whisked to perfection. Earthy, slightly sweet, and packed with energy for a focused boost.

Smores Latte

Indulge your sweet tooth with this delightful treat. Tastes just like the campfire classic, with chocolate, marshmallow, and graham cracker flavors.
