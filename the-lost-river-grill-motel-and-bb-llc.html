Our Heritage

Where Every Meal Feels Like Home

Nestled in the heart of Lost City, The Lost River Grill, Motel and B&B LLC is more than just a restaurant—it's a local institution. For years, we've been serving up heaping plates of comfort and hospitality, becoming a cherished gathering spot for families, friends, and travelers alike.

At Lost River Grill, we believe in the power of good food made with love. Our menu is a tribute to classic American comfort, carefully crafted to satisfy every appetite. We take pride in using quality ingredients, preparing each dish from scratch, just like Grandma used to make.

From the sizzle of our hand-cut steaks to the sweet embrace of our famous cream pies, every bite tells a story of tradition and passion. We're not just serving meals; we're creating memories around the table.

Beyond the grill, our cozy motel and charming B&B offer a peaceful retreat, extending our warm hospitality to those looking to stay a while. Come join us by the river, where delicious food and a welcoming atmosphere await.
