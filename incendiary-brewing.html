Our Journey

More Than Just a Brew, It's Our Community Spirit.

Born from a passion for exceptional craft beer and hearty American fare, Incendiary Brewing has grown to become a cornerstone of the North Carolina community. Our Lewisville location embodies this spirit, offering a welcoming space where friends and family gather to share laughter, delicious food, and unforgettable brews.

We're proud to be a place where memories are made, one pint and one plate at a time. With a commitment to quality ingredients and a vibrant atmosphere, Incendiary Brewing is dedicated to serving not just great food and drink, but also building lasting connections within our beloved community.
