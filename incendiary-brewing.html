Our Story

Brewing Community, One Pint at a Time.

Incendiary Brewing isn't just a bar and grill; it's a cornerstone of the North Carolina community. With four thriving locations, we've dedicated ourselves to crafting exceptional experiences and fostering connections.

Our Lewisville location embodies this spirit, offering a warm and inviting atmosphere where friends, family, and neighbors can gather over expertly brewed craft beers and delicious, hearty food. We believe in quality ingredients, passionate service, and the power of a good brew to bring people together.

From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a customer; you're part of the Incendiary family. We're proud to be a local hub, a place where memories are made, laughter is shared, and every visit feels like coming home.
