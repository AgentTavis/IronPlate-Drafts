OUR STORY

Crafting Experiences, One Pour At A Time.

Novel Taproom in Newton, NC is more than just a place to grab a drink; it's a vibrant and welcoming establishment that offers a truly unique experience. We've cultivated a community hub where beer enthusiasts can explore a diverse, rotating selection of craft brews, and fitness enthusiasts can engage in invigorating activities.

Our vision was to create a space where great drinks, friendly faces, and memorable events come together. Whether you're here to discover your new favorite local beer, challenge yourself with a unique fitness class, or simply unwind with friends after a long day, you'll find your perfect spot at Novel Taproom. We're proud to be Newton's premier destination for entertainment, connection, and good times.
