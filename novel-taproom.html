Our Story

More Than Just a Taproom: Your Local Gathering Spot

Novel Taproom in Newton, NC, isn't just another bar; it's a vibrant and welcoming establishment designed to be the heart of our community. We started with a simple vision: to create a place where everyone feels at home, whether you're a seasoned craft beer aficionado, looking for a great meal, or simply seeking a fun, friendly spot to unwind.

We believe in good drinks, great food, and even better company. Our space is meticulously crafted to offer a unique experience for all, blending the best of a classic bar and grill with a modern, energetic atmosphere. It's where friendships are forged, laughter echoes, and every visit feels like coming home.
