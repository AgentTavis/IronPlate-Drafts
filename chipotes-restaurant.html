Our Journey

Bringing Genuine Mexican Taste to Raleigh

At Chipotes Restaurant, we believe that great food starts with passion and tradition. Our culinary journey began with a simple dream: to share the vibrant, rich flavors of Mexico with our beloved community here in Raleigh, NC. We are a family-owned establishment, dedicated to crafting authentic Mexican dishes using only the freshest ingredients, prepared with recipes passed down through generations.

Every dish tells a story, from our sizzling fajitas to our savory enchiladas, reflecting the warmth and hospitality of Mexican culture. We invite you to join us and experience a taste of Mexico that feels like home.

"Chipotes is more than just a restaurant; it's a culinary experience! The flavors are so authentic, and you can feel the love put into every dish. A true gem in Raleigh."

— A Loyal Customer

Our Flavors

Explore Our Authentic Mexican Menu

Every dish is a celebration of traditional Mexican cuisine, made with care and the freshest ingredients.

Sizzling Fajitas

Your choice of marinated steak, chicken, or shrimp, grilled with peppers and onions. Served with warm tortillas and fresh toppings.

Street Tacos

Authentic corn tortillas filled with your choice of carne asada, al pastor, carnitas, or pollo, topped with cilantro and onion.

Enchiladas Rojas

Corn tortillas filled with cheese, chicken, or beef, smothered in our rich, savory red chili sauce and melted cheese.

Burrito Supreme

A hearty flour tortilla packed with rice, beans, your choice of meat, cheese, sour cream, and salsa.

Quesadillas al Horno

Crispy flour tortilla filled with melted Oaxaca cheese and your favorite filling, served with guacamole and crema.

Nachos Grande

Crispy tortilla chips piled high with refried beans, melted cheese, jalapeños, pico de gallo, sour cream, and your choice of meat.

Chile Relleno

Poblano pepper stuffed with cheese, lightly battered and fried, then covered in a mild red sauce.

Tostadas de Tinga

Crispy fried tortillas topped with shredded chicken in a smoky chipotle sauce, lettuce, crema, and queso fresco.
