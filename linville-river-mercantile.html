Our Heritage

More Than Just a Diner, It's a Newland Tradition.

Nestled in the heart of Newland, Linville River Mercantile is more than just a place to eat—it's a community hub, a cherished stop, and a source of incredible homemade delights. What started as a simple idea for coffee and ice cream quickly blossomed into a local obsession, particularly for our exquisite desserts.

Walk through our doors and be greeted by the amazing aroma of fresh baking. We believe in crafting every dish with love and care, from our signature Tres Leches to our decadent cheesecakes, all made from scratch. It's this dedication to quality and a warm, welcoming atmosphere that keeps our customers coming back, day after day, year after year.

Whether you're stopping by after a tubing adventure or just craving a sweet escape, Linville River Mercantile promises a taste experience that will leave you saying "WOW WOW WOW!" We're proud to be a part of Newland's story, serving up joy one delicious bite at a time.
