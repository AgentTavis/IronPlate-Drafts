The stage in the corner.

Purple lights, real amps, a stool, and a crowd that actually listens. Our stage sits in the corner by the brewery sticker wall — acoustic enough to talk over, loud enough to matter.

Acts rotate: solo acoustic guitarists, singer-songwriters, duos, and the occasional small band. Regional talent, no cover.

The weekly rhythm

What a week looks like.

Some things change weekly. Some things are always the same. Here's the shape of it.

FriWeekly
Friday Nights

Solo acoustic sets

Most Fridays · typically 7 PM onward

A regional guitarist or singer-songwriter takes the corner stage. Grab a glass of wine, find a leather couch, stay for the whole set.

SatWeekly
Saturday Nights

Duos & small bands

Most Saturdays · typically 8 PM onward

Saturday brings fuller sound — duos and small bands. The room fills up. Bring your people.

SunWeekly
Sunday Afternoons

Wine & wind-down

Every Sunday · 12 – 7 PM

Slower pace, mimosas, wine by the glass, the game on the TV. A good way to close out the weekend.
