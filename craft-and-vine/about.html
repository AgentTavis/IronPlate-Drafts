What we are

Warm. Neighborhood. Craft-first.

Oak Ridge Craft & Vine is a craft beer and wine bar with a proper bottle shop built in. Twelve rotating taps. A wine cooler that runs the wall. Premium bourbon in the back. Brewery stickers and tin signs covering the brick behind the bar.

We opened to give Oak Ridge a place that takes craft beer seriously without taking itself too seriously. No pretense. No dress code. Just cold pours, good wine, and people who know your name after a few visits.

&

The space pulls its character from the room itself — polished concrete floors, a wood-topped bar, pendant lights overhead, leather couches in the lounge. A small stage in the corner for Friday and Saturday music. An outdoor patio with blue umbrellas for the warmer half of the year. Dogs welcome. Outside food welcome. That's the vibe.

What makes it work

Things we believe matter.

01

Taproom + bottle shop

Sit down for a pint, or take home a mix & match 6-pack. Most places do one. We do both.

02

Wine gets equal billing

The "&Vine" is literal. The cooler runs the wall. By the glass or the bottle — reds, whites, rosés, sparkling.

03

Live music, not a concert

Fridays and Saturdays, mostly. Acoustic or a duo, in the corner. Quiet enough to talk over.

04

Outside food welcome

We don't have a kitchen. Bring your own, order delivery. No pressure to buy dinner.

05

Dog friendly

Bring 'em in. Water bowl by the door. The regulars' dogs already know the room.

06

Lounge seating

Leather couches, coffee table, brewery tin signs. A second living room that happens to have 12 taps.

07

Outdoor patio

Blue umbrellas, green cushions, a view of Oak Ridge Road going by. Good weather? Sit outside.

08

The sticker wall

Sierra Nevada, Cigar City, Lagunitas, Dogfish Head, Ballast Point, Green Flash — the brewery wall says everything.
