What we are

Warm. Neighborhood. Craft-first.

Oak Ridge Craft & Vine is a craft beer and wine bar with a proper bottle shop built in. Twelve rotating taps. A wine cooler that runs the wall. Premium bourbon in the back. Brewery stickers and tin signs covering the brick behind the bar.

We opened to give Oak Ridge a place that takes craft beer seriously without taking itself too seriously. No pretense. No dress code. Just cold pours, good wine, and people who know your name after a few visits.

&

The space pulls its character from the room itself. Polished concrete floors, a wood-topped bar, pendant lights overhead, leather couches in the lounge. A small stage in the corner for Friday and Saturday music. An outdoor patio with blue umbrellas for the warmer half of the year. Dogs welcome. Outside food welcome. That's the vibe.
