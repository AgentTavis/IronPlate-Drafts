The Cheers of Oak Ridge.

A craft beer bar. A bottle shop. A neighborhood hang. For six years, the spot where the bartenders know your order before you sit down.

4.7 ★ on Google · 699+ reviews · Since 2019
Three bars in one

Taproom. Bottle shop. Neighborhood hang.

We opened in 2019 with a simple idea. Oak Ridge needed a real neighborhood bar. The kind of place where you could grab a craft beer after work, pick up a bottle of wine on the way home, and catch a band on the weekend. All in one building.

Twelve rotating taps keep the beer list changing. A wall of wine covers the back of the bar. A bourbon selection grows every season. And because we don't have a kitchen, you bring your own food from any of the restaurants around us. We'll pour the drinks.

Six years in, the regulars have names and the bartenders know their drinks. That's not marketing. That's just what happens when you build a bar for the town you live in.
