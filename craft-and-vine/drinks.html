02 · Wine

The &Vine is real.

Our wine cooler runs wall-to-wall with reds, whites, rosés, and sparkling — by the glass or the bottle. Curated, approachable, and priced for the neighborhood.

"A taste of heaven in a glass & a journey through different regions, flavors, and aromas. Whether you prefer a bold red or a crisp white — we've got you covered."
R

Reds

Bold Cabernets, earthy Pinots, approachable blends. Ask about the tasting lineup.

W

Whites

Crisp Sauvignon Blancs, buttery Chardonnays, and everything between — chilled and ready.

Ro

Rosé

Pink-hour all day. Dry, fruit-forward, and easy to share across the table.

S

Sparkling

Prosecco, cava, and the occasional proper Champagne — because some nights call for bubbles.

By-the-glass list rotates seasonally. Bottle selection printed at the bar.

Bottle shop inside the bar.

Most taprooms don't do retail. We do. Mix & match your own 6-pack from the cooler wall, or take home a 32 or 64oz growler fill from any of the 12 taps.
