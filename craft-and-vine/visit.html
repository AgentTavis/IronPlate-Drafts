Before you come

A few things to know before you visit.

Parking

Plenty of free parking in the Oak Ridge Commons lot. Look for us between Lowes Foods and the diner.

Food

We don't have a kitchen. Bring your own food from any restaurant nearby, or order delivery straight to the bar.

Welcome on the patio and inside. Water bowls provided.

All ages welcome until 9pm.
