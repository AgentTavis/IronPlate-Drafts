Good to know

A few practical things.

Free parking

Shared lot in front of the shopping center. Easy in, easy out. Overflow parking on the side.

Outside food welcome

We don't serve food. Bring your own or order delivery to the bar. Anything goes.

Dogs welcome

Water bowl inside. Leashed, please. The regulars already know the drill.

Outdoor patio

Blue umbrellas out front. Good weather? Best seat in the house.

No reservations

Walk-in only. If a big group's coming, give us a call so we can set the lounge for you.

To-go sales

Mix & match 6-packs, growler fills, wine bottles. Retail hours = bar hours.
