Three bars in one

Taproom. Bottle shop. Neighborhood hang.

One building. Three reasons to walk in.

01 The Taproom

Twelve rotating taps. Wine by the glass. Bourbon worth lingering over. Pull up a stool, catch the game, stay for the music.

02 The Bottle Shop

A wall of wine. Craft cans. Mix and match 6-packs. 32 and 64oz growlers filled at the bar. Buy a bottle on your way out.

03 The Hang

Bring your own food from anywhere nearby. Dog friendly. Outdoor patio. Wine Wednesdays. Live music most weekends. No cover.
