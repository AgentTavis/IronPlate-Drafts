About us

A taproom and a bottle shop.

Sit down with a cold pour from one of 12 rotating taps, share a bottle of wine, or grab a mix & match 6-pack on your way out. Polished concrete floors, wood-topped bar, brewery tin signs on the wall, leather couches in the lounge — it's the kind of place where you stay a while.

Bring your dog. Bring your own food. Settle in.
