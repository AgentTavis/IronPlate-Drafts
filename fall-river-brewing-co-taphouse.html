Our Legacy

Crafting the Spirit of Fall River Valley

Nestled in the heart of Redding, NC, Fall River Brewing Co. Taphouse brings the untamed beauty and vibrant essence of Northern California's Fall River Valley directly to you. Our passion is to handcraft exceptional beers that tell a story – a story of pristine nature, bold character, and innovative brewing.

We believe beer is more than just a drink; it's a celebration of flavor, a nod to tradition, and a toast to discovery. Our signature barrel-aged sours, for instance, are meticulously crafted to evoke the feeling of sunlight in a glass, bursting with natural tones of apricot, peaches, and plums, matured gracefully in fine wine barrels. It's a testament to our dedication to unique tastes and unforgettable experiences.

Join us to explore a world of flavors, from bright, fruity brews to robust, complex ales, all served in a welcoming atmosphere where good company and great beer flow freely.

"Every visit to Fall River Brewing Co. feels like a mini-escape. Their commitment to unique, flavorful beers is unmatched, and the atmosphere is just perfect for unwinding."
– A Loyal Patron

Taste Our Craft

Our Signature Brews & Bites

Barrel Aged Apricot Sour

Like sunlight in a glass, this barrel-aged sour bursts with bright tones of apricot, offering a crisp, complex, and delightfully tart experience. A true celebration of flavor.

Peach Haze IPA

A hazy IPA infused with the sweet, succulent essence of ripe peaches. Enjoy a smooth, juicy brew with vibrant fruit notes and a perfectly balanced hop character.

Plum Saison

An elegant saison showcasing the rich, subtle sweetness of plums. Aged to perfection, it offers delicate fruity undertones and a refreshing, dry finish.

Classic Taphouse Burger

Our juicy, perfectly grilled patty served on a toasted brioche bun with fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, and a side of crispy fries. A timeless favorite.

Loaded Nachos

Crispy tortilla chips piled high with melted cheddar, jalapeños, black beans, pico de gallo, sour cream, and your choice of seasoned chicken or beef.

Artisan Cheese Board

A curated selection of local and imported cheeses, served with fresh fruit, artisanal crackers, and a drizzle of honey. Perfect for sharing.
