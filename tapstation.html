Our Story

Crafted in Community, Served with Passion.

Welcome to TapStation, a beloved Apex establishment born from the spirit of a repurposed gas station. We've transformed a piece of history into a vibrant gathering spot where good times flow as freely as our 23 beers on tap.

From day one, our mission has been simple: to create a fun, welcoming atmosphere for everyone. Whether you're bringing the kids, your furry friend, or just looking for a relaxed evening out, our unique indoor and outdoor seating options make us the perfect choice.
