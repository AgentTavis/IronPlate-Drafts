Our Story

More Than Just a Coffee Shop, It's a Community Hub.

Welcome to The Grind Coffee House & Creamery, Benson, NC's sweetest and coziest spot! From the rich aroma of our freshly brewed coffee to the delightful chill of our premium ice cream, we craft experiences that warm the soul and brighten the day. We're proud to be locally sourced, bringing you delicious muffins, doughnuts, and pastries baked with love right here in Benson.

More than just a place to eat, The Grind is where friends gather, stories are shared, and every visit feels like coming home. We believe in great taste, warm smiles, and creating a space where everyone feels welcome. Come discover your new favorite spot!

Our Commitment

Why Choose The Grind?

Locally Sourced Goodness

We believe in supporting our community. Our delicious muffins, doughnuts, and pastries are all baked with love, using local ingredients right here in Benson, NC, ensuring peak freshness and flavor.

More Than Just Coffee

While our coffee is legendary, we're a true creamery and diner! Enjoy everything from rich espresso drinks and refreshing loaded teas to premium ice cream scoops, hand-crafted milkshakes, and hearty breakfast sandwiches.

Your Cozy Community Hub

The Grind is designed to be your home away from home. With a warm atmosphere, comfortable seating, and friendly staff, it's the perfect place to relax, catch up with friends, or get some work done.
