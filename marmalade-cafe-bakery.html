OUR STORY

From Beaufort to Morehead City: A Legacy of Flavor and Friendship

Marmalade Cafe has always been about bringing joy to our community through delicious food and a warm atmosphere. We're thrilled to have found our new home at 410 Arendell St in Morehead City, where we've expanded our menu and seating to welcome even more of you!

What started as a beloved spot in Beaufort has blossomed into a more open and inviting space, rich with vibrant decor and the same friendly faces. Our commitment to outstanding food, yummy coffees, and incredible pastries remains unwavering. Come experience the evolution of Marmalade Cafe – a place where every visit feels like coming home.
