Our Story

Crafted with Passion, Served with Warmth.

Step into Old Tom's, your cozy retreat in Manteo, where every visit feels like coming home. We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming atmosphere, just like our fantastic team member, Randy, who's always ready with a smile and a great recommendation.

We're passionate about crafting delicious drinks and offering unique finds, making sure your time with us is relaxing and memorable. Whether you're unwinding after a day exploring Manteo or simply looking for a friendly face, Old Tom's is your spot for quality beer, wine, and wares.
