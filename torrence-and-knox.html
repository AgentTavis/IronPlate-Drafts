Our Journey

From Our Family to Your Table: Passion in Every Dish

Welcome to Torrence and Knox, a cherished bar and restaurant right here in Cornelius, NC. We're Andrew and Danielle Sheridan, and our passion is simple: to offer you great food and drinks in a relaxed, casual atmosphere.

Here, you're invited to kick back, relax, and hang out with friends, catch the big game on one of our dozen televisions, or just unwind after a long day. We believe dining out should be an experience of comfort and joy.

Our menu is a celebration of flavor, featuring signature dishes like our Korean BBQ wings, classic cheeseburgers, and our mouth-watering Brooklyn Chicken and Prime Rib Sandwiches. We've also meticulously curated a diverse wine menu and an extensive selection of beverages to perfectly complement your meal.

Every dish is crafted with care, and every visit is an invitation to become part of our story. Come on in, we can't wait to serve you!
