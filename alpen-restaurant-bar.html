OUR STORY

Where Rustic Charm Meets Modern Comfort

Nestled in the heart of Beech Mountain, Alpen Restaurant & Bar began with a vision to create a truly special dining experience. Jane & Fred took the idea further, enhancing the Inn with a new modern kitchen and a new restaurant addition, the **Mountain View Room.** This thoughtful expansion was strictly guided by the rustic style of the old Inn, ensuring that every corner resonates with the cozy, timeless appeal of the mountains.

From our beautifully appointed dining spaces to the warmth emanating from the original old **Fireplace**, every detail at Alpen is designed to make you feel right at home. We pride ourselves on blending traditional mountain hospitality with a refined culinary approach, creating dishes that are both comforting and exciting. Come discover the unique ambiance that makes us a beloved local gem.
