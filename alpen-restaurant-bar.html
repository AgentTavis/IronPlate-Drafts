Our Story

A Mountain Legacy, Reimagined.

Nestled high in Beech Mountain, Alpen Restaurant & Bar began as a vision by Jane & Fred. They took the charming idea of a mountain inn further, enhancing it with a new modern kitchen and introducing the breathtaking Mountain View Room.

Every design choice was carefully guided by the rustic spirit of the original inn, honoring its heritage and centering around the warmth of the original old Fireplace. It's a place where history meets modern comfort, creating an inviting atmosphere for all who seek a memorable dining experience amidst the beauty of the Blue Ridge Mountains.
