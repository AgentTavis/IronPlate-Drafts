Our Journey

Generations of Flavor, Coastal Charm.

Welcome to Uncle Ike's Sandbar & Grill, a true Outer Banks gem in Corolla, NC. We're a proud family-run establishment, dedicated to serving up delicious, made-from-scratch American and Seafood favorites in a casual, friendly atmosphere. For years, we've been the go-to spot for locals and visitors alike, offering a taste of coastal comfort with every dish.

At Uncle Ike's, you're not just a customer; you're family. We believe in great food, cold drinks, and good times – the perfect recipe for memorable moments by the beach. Come on in, kick back, and let our family treat yours to an unforgettable dining experience.
