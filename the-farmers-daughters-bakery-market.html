Our Story

A Taste of Southern Tradition, Crafted with Love.

Nestled in the heart of Hertford, The Farmer's Daughters Bakery & Market began with a simple dream: to share the warmth and richness of Southern, handcrafted food with our community. We believe in wholesome ingredients, time-honored recipes, and the joy of a truly delicious meal.

From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a customer, you're family. We pour our hearts into every savory dish and sweet treat, ensuring each bite brings a comforting taste of home. Our market also features unique, locally-inspired gifts, perfect for any occasion.

"Every visit feels like coming home. The Omelettes are divine, and I can never leave without a few of their delicious baked goods!"
— Sarah L., Loyal Customer
