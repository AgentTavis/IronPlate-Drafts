OUR STORY

Our Passion for Homemade Goodness in Eden, NC

The Ridge in Eden, NC, is more than just a diner; it's a beloved local spot where community gathers for comforting meals and friendly faces. We're proud to offer a delectable buffet and salad bar, brimming with a variety of dishes prepared with care and quality ingredients.

Located conveniently at 243 W Kings Hwy, Eden, NC 27288, we're praised for our welcoming atmosphere and the consistently high-quality food we serve. From classic comfort dishes like chicken and dumplings to savory corned beef, and our always well-stocked salad bar, there's always something to satisfy every craving. We also feature made-to-order dinner options, including a fresh catch of the day, ensuring a memorable dining experience for both locals and visitors alike.
