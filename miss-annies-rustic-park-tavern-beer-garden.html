Our Heritage

Where Community Gathers, Flavors Bloom.

Step into Miss Annie's, a cornerstone of the St Joseph, NC community for years. We've built our rustic haven on the simple joy of good company, delicious food, and the finest selection of beverages. Our Beer Garden is a beloved spot where laughter echoes, friendships are forged, and memories are made under the open sky. We invite you to experience the warmth and charm that make us a local legend.
