Our Story

Crafting Community, One Brew at a Time

Nestled in the heart of Newton, NC, Brookrise Brewing is more than just a bar and grill – it's a testament to local history and community spirit. Housed in a beautifully transformed former firehouse, we've ignited a new kind of gathering place where friends, family, and neighbors can come together for exceptional food, craft beers, and good company.

Our commitment is to quality ingredients, a welcoming atmosphere, and creating memorable experiences, all while honoring the rich heritage of our unique location. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll feel the warmth of our hospitality and taste the passion in every dish and every pour. We're proud to be a part of Newton's vibrant scene.
