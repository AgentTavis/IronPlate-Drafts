Our Journey

The Heart Behind Living Beans

Crafting Community & Sustainable Sips.

Living Beans is more than just a Bar & Grill; it's a testament to community and sustainable practices right here in Hildebran. We believe in a 'cycle of life' approach, bringing you fresh, delicious experiences in every bite and every cup.

Our commitment extends to our sourcing, including partnerships with eco-friendly restaurant suppliers, some even from the picturesque Black Forest, Colorado. This ensures our savory dishes and 'spring sustainable sipping' with @counterculturecoffee are not only delightful but also kind to our planet.

"Living Beans is an absolute gem! Their commitment to fresh, quality food and amazing coffee makes it our family's favorite spot. Always a warm welcome!"
