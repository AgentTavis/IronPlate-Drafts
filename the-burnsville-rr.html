Our Journey

Where Good Times and Great Food Come Together.

Nestled in the heart of Burnsville, The Burnsville RR is more than just a bar and grill – it's a community gathering spot. We believe in serving up delicious, hearty meals in an atmosphere that feels like coming home. Since opening our doors, we've been dedicated to providing a place where friends and family can connect over fantastic food and refreshing drinks.

Our commitment is simple: great flavors, genuine hospitality, and that signature warm ambiance that makes every visit memorable. We're proud to be a local favorite, and we invite you to pull up a seat, relax, and experience the best of Burnsville's casual dining scene.

Our Offerings

Crafting Comfort & Flavor.

Our full menu is currently being perfected! In the meantime, here's a taste of the kind of delicious, comforting dishes you can expect from your favorite local bar and grill.

Classic Burger

A juicy, perfectly grilled patty, fresh toppings, and our signature sauce on a toasted bun. A timeless favorite.

Crispy Chicken Wings

Tossed in your choice of classic buffalo, tangy BBQ, or a dry rub. Perfect for sharing (or not!).

Loaded Nachos

Piled high with melted cheese, savory beef or chicken, jalapeños, salsa, and sour cream.

Hearty Chili

Our slow-cooked, rich and robust chili, topped with cheddar cheese and a dollop of sour cream.

Fresh Garden Salad

Crisp greens, seasonal vegetables, and your choice of dressing. A lighter, refreshing option.

Seasonal Dessert

Ask your server about our delightful dessert special of the day, a perfect end to your meal.
