Our Heritage

Authentic Italian Flavors, Family Values

At Bobby D's, we believe in bringing the heart of Italy to the High Country. Since opening our doors in 2014, we've been dedicated to serving up delicious, homemade Italian dishes made with fresh ingredients and a whole lot of passion.

From our crispy, New York-style pizzas to our rich, comforting pasta dishes, every item on our menu is prepared to delight. We're proud to be a locally owned and operated establishment, committed to our community and to creating memorable dining experiences for all our guests. Whether you're dining in, taking out, or picking up curbside, you're part of the Bobby D's family.
