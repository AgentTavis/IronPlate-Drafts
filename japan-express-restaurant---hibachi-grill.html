Our Story

Bringing Authentic Japanese Flavors to Batesburg-Leesville

At Japan Express, we believe in the art of delicious food made with passion. Located in the heart of Batesburg-Leesville, NC, we're your local destination for an unforgettable Hibachi and Grill experience. Our skilled chefs craft each dish with care, focusing on the vibrant flavors and fresh ingredients that make Japanese cuisine so special.

From sizzling Hibachi steak and succulent shrimp to perfectly grilled chicken, every meal is prepared to satisfy. We're proud to be a cherished part of the community, offering a warm and welcoming atmosphere where friends and family can gather to enjoy great food and create lasting memories.
