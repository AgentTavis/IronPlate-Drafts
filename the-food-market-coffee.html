OUR STORY

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community Hub.

The Food Market Coffee is more than just a place to eat; it's where Columbia, NC, gathers. We're proud to serve delicious cafe fare, hearty lunches, satisfying dinners, and delightful brunches, complemented by a selection of drinks, wine, and a lively happy hour.

From your morning coffee to your evening meal, we're dedicated to providing a warm, welcoming atmosphere and quality food that feels like home. Our mission has always been simple: to create a space where friends, families, and neighbors can connect over great food and even better company.
