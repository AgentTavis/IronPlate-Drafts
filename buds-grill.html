Our Legacy

Generations of Flavor, Community, and Good Times

Welcome to Bud's Grill, a cornerstone of the Tarboro community where every meal is a celebration of local taste and hospitality. For years, we've poured our passion into crafting classic bar and grill favorites, making us a beloved destination for families, friends, and solo diners alike.

With options for patio dining, a full-service bar featuring local brews and signature cocktails, and a private banquet room capable of accommodating up to 40 guests, Bud's also prides itself on catering for special occasions and creating memorable experiences for every guest. Whether it's a quick lunch, a relaxed dinner, or a private event, we're dedicated to making your visit exceptional.

Come on down and taste the difference. At Bud's, you're not just a customer; you're family.
