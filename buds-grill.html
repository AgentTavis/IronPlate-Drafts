Our Legacy

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Tarboro Tradition.

At Bud's Grill, we believe in hearty food, genuine hospitality, and creating memories that last. Nestled right in the heart of Tarboro, we've been serving up classic American bar and grill fare with a warm smile for years. We're proud to be a community staple, a place where friends gather, families celebrate, and everyone feels right at home.

With options for sunny patio dining, a lively full-service bar perfect for unwinding, and a private banquet room capable of accommodating up to 40 guests, Bud's is designed for every occasion. We also pride ourselves on catering for your special events, bringing the taste of Bud's to your celebration.
