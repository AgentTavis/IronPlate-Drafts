Our Heritage

A Taste of Home, Rooted in Community.

Harbor Cafe, located within the charming Twin Harbor community in Mount Gilead, NC, is more than just a place to eat – it's a local institution. Known for its warm atmosphere and delicious, comforting food, we've proudly served our neighbors and friends for generations.

We believe in simple, honest meals made with care, just like you'd find at home. From our sizzling breakfasts to our hearty lunches, every dish is prepared to bring a smile to your face and warmth to your heart.

Our Offerings

Diner Classics, Made with Love.

Classic Breakfast Platter

Two farm-fresh eggs cooked to your liking, crispy bacon or savory sausage, golden hash browns, and your choice of toast or fluffy biscuits.

Harbor Burger Deluxe

A juicy, hand-formed patty, topped with crisp lettuce, ripe tomato, onion, pickles, and our special sauce, served on a toasted bun with a side of fries.

Southern Fried Chicken

Perfectly seasoned and crispy fried chicken, a true Southern comfort. Served with creamy mashed potatoes and collard greens.

Homestyle Meatloaf

Our comforting meatloaf, slow-baked to perfection and topped with a rich gravy. Accompanied by green beans and sweet corn.

Daily Soup & Sandwich

Ask your server about our freshly prepared soup of the day, paired with a classic deli sandwich. A perfect light lunch.

Pancakes & Waffles

Fluffy buttermilk pancakes or golden waffles, served with butter and warm maple syrup. Add fresh berries or chocolate chips for a sweet treat.
