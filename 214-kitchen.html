Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Pollocksville Tradition.

Welcome to 214 Kitchen, your go-to Bar & Grill on Main Street in Pollocksville, NC. We're more than just a place to eat; we're a community hub where friends and family gather to share good times, incredible food, and awesome vibes.

We believe in simple pleasures done exceptionally well. From sizzling burgers fresh off the grill to our famous Philly Cheesesteak, every dish is crafted with care and a touch of local flavor. Our bar is stocked with your favorite drinks, perfect for unwinding after a long day or celebrating life's moments.

Come on down, kick back, and make yourself at home. We can't wait to serve you and become a part of your favorite Pollocksville memories.

Taste the Tradition

Our Signature Flavors, Fresh Off the Grill.

From comforting classics to local favorites, there's something for everyone to enjoy.

Philly Cheesesteak

Our famously authentic Philly, packed with thinly sliced steak, melted cheese, and grilled onions on a toasted hoagie roll.

Classic 214 Burger

A juicy, hand-pattied burger grilled to perfection, served with fresh toppings and your choice of cheese.

Crispy Chicken Wings

Perfectly seasoned and fried to a golden crisp, tossed in your choice of classic buffalo, BBQ, or our secret house sauce.

Signature Grill Platter

A hearty selection of our grilled favorites, expertly prepared to satisfy any craving.

Fresh House Salad

Crisp mixed greens, fresh vegetables, and your choice of dressing – a perfect lighter option.

Milk Shakes

Hand-spun, creamy indulgence. Choose from classic vanilla, chocolate, strawberry, or a rotating special flavor.

Local Craft Beers

Featuring a rotating selection of the finest craft beers from North Carolina breweries.
