Our Legacy

More Than a Meal — It's a Kinston Tradition.

Don't let the exterior fool you; there's some serious cooking going on inside Byrd's Restaurant. For generations, we've been Kinston's go-to spot for hearty, down-home meals crafted with care. We appreciate folks knowing what they want when it's their turn, as our kitchen is always buzzing with activity, serving up delicious dishes to a line that never slows.

We pride ourselves on friendly service and an atmosphere that reminds you of simpler times, where good food and great company are always on the menu. Come discover why Byrd's has been a beloved part of Kinston's culinary landscape.
