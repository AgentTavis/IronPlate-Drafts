Our Journey

Crafted with Passion, Served with Soul.

At Elevated Grains, we believe in the simple pleasure of good company, great food, and exceptional craft beverages. Founded by local enthusiasts Scott and Mike, our bar and grill is more than just a place to eat – it's a community hub where quality ingredients meet laid-back Raleigh vibes.

We're dedicated to offering an experience that uplifts your spirits, whether you're catching up with friends, enjoying live music, or discovering your new favorite beer from our carefully curated selection. Come as you are, and leave with a smile.

Why Visit Us

Experience the Elevated Grains Difference.

Craft Beer Excellence

Dive into a meticulously curated selection of local and regional craft beers. From hoppy IPAs to rich stouts, our ever-rotating taps ensure there's always something new and exciting to discover for every palate.

Unbeatable Atmosphere

Whether you're looking for an intimate evening, a lively group gathering, or a night out with live music, Elevated Grains offers a versatile and welcoming vibe. Enjoy our cozy indoor space or relax in our vibrant outdoor area.

Local Charm & Owners

As a proud Raleigh establishment run by Scott and Mike, we're deeply rooted in the community. Experience genuine hospitality and a friendly, mom-and-pop feel that makes every visit feel like coming home.
