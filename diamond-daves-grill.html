Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Bladenboro Tradition.

At Diamond Dave's Grill, we're proud to be Bladenboro's beloved local spot, serving up plates of pure comfort and genuine hospitality right here on Main Street. Our patrons rave about our consistently delicious food and the warm, friendly faces that greet them every visit.
