Our Story

More Than Just a Diner

Unwind Bar + Cafe is a beloved local spot in High Point, NC. More than just a diner, we're a vibrant community hub where friends gather, stories unfold, and every visit feels like coming home. We pride ourselves on crafting memorable experiences, from our rich coffee to our lively late-night atmosphere, always served with a smile.

We believe in creating a space where everyone feels welcome, whether you're starting your day with a comforting coffee, enjoying a leisurely afternoon with friends, or unwinding with a craft cocktail after hours.
