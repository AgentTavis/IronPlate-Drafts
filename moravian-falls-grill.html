About Moravian Falls Grill

A Tradition of Exceptional Flavor

Welcome to Moravian Falls Grill, where we honor Carolina's rich culinary heritage with authentic BBQ and expertly grilled cuisine. Since opening our doors in Moravian Falls, NC, we've been committed to smoking meats the old-fashioned way—low and slow, with care and precision.

Every dish is crafted with locally-sourced ingredients and time-tested recipes passed down through generations. Whether you're joining us for a casual lunch or a special celebration, you'll experience the warmth and flavor that defines Southern hospitality.

Our dedicated team takes pride in every plate we serve. From our signature smoked ribs to our famous pit-cooked brisket, quality and tradition are never compromised.
