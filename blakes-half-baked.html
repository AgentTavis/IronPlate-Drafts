The Heart Behind Blake's Half Baked

Step inside Blake's Half Baked, Asheboro's cherished spot for Italian-inspired comfort food. We believe in the simple joy of a perfectly crafted sandwich, especially when paired with the warmth of homemade soup or the crisp freshness of a salad.

Since opening our doors, we've been dedicated to creating a welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels like family. Our commitment to quality ingredients and genuine hospitality is at the core of everything we do. We're more than just a meal; we're a gathering place for friends, families, and anyone looking for a taste of authentic local flavor.
