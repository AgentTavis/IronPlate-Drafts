Our Story

More Than Just a Spot. It's Downtown Greensboro's Heartbeat.

Chandler's isn't just a bar and grill; it's a Greensboro institution. Nestled right in the vibrant downtown core, we've proudly served our community for years, becoming a beloved gathering place for locals and visitors alike. We're where memories are made, good conversations flow, and every meal feels like coming home.

Our commitment is simple: great food, fantastic company, and a welcoming atmosphere that makes you want to linger. We believe in being more than just a place to eat; we're a hub where the community connects, where the coffee is always fresh, and the grill is always hot.
