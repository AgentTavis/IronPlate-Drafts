Our Journey

Where Every Bite Tells a Story of Passion & Flavor.

Welcome to Love and Tacos, a vibrant cornerstone of authentic Mexican cuisine right here in Graham, NC. We believe that great food brings people together, and every dish we create is a testament to tradition, fresh ingredients, and a whole lot of heart.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to share the rich, diverse flavors of Mexico with our community. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll feel the warmth of our atmosphere and the dedication of our team. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a family inviting you to taste the love in every taco, enchilada, and handcrafted salsa.

We source the freshest produce, prepare everything from scratch daily, and pour our passion into every detail. It's this commitment to quality and hospitality that makes Love and Tacos a truly special place – a place where amazing food, wonderful service, and beautiful memories are always on the menu.

Our Specialties

Taste the Heart of Mexico in Every Dish.

Carne Asada Tacos $14.99

Three grilled steak tacos, marinated to perfection, served with onions, cilantro, and a side of our house-made salsa verde.

Chicken Fajitas $16.50

Sizzling skillet of tender chicken, bell peppers, and onions. Served with warm tortillas, rice, beans, sour cream, and guacamole.

Quesabirria $15.99

Flavorful slow-cooked beef birria, melted cheese, and fresh cilantro, folded into crispy tortillas. Served with consome for dipping.

Enchiladas Verdes $13.75

Three corn tortillas filled with your choice of shredded chicken or cheese, smothered in our vibrant green tomatillo sauce.

Al Pastor Burrito $12.99

Large flour tortilla filled with succulent marinated pork, pineapple, rice, beans, and a touch of our special house sauce.

Churros with Ice Cream $7.00

Crispy cinnamon-sugar churros, served warm with a scoop of vanilla bean ice cream and a drizzle of caramel.
