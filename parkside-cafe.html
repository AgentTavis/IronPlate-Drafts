Our Story & Values

Where Southern Charm Meets Home-Cooked Comfort

Nestled in Selma, NC, Parkside Cafe is more than just a diner—it's a warm embrace of Southern hospitality. We’re the kind of place locals recommend, where every visit feels like coming home. Our cozy atmosphere and simple, delicious menu are crafted to bring you comfort and joy, just like a meal at Grandma's house.

We believe in good food, genuine smiles, and a dining experience that's both affordable and unforgettable. From our spotlessly clean dining room to the friendly faces ready to call you "baby" or "hun," we pour our heart into every detail. We're proud to serve up classic dishes alongside those famous free hush puppies that keep everyone coming back for more.
