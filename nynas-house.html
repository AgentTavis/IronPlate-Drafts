Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Memory

Home Breakfast with my favorite guys at our new favorite restaurant, Nynas House in Drexel. We discussed everything from family to Elder ... This is where the magic happens, where every dish tells a story, and every guest leaves feeling like family.
