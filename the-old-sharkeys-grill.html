Our Legacy

The Story Behind "The Old"

When you come here, you may notice that the part of the sign that says "The Old" appears as if it was added on. It was. It seems that back in the day this place was a restaurant and billiard hall with quite a reputation for all the incidents traditionally associated with such establishments. While some tales of the past might make for colorful lore, today, The Old Sharkey's Grill stands as a testament to enduring community spirit and timeless comfort food.

After a transition in ownership, we proudly carry forward the tradition of being a beloved local spot, though with a much friendlier disposition! We've cleaned up the act but kept the soul, focusing on great food, good company, and a welcoming atmosphere for everyone in Burlington. We cherish our casual, divey charm while offering a safe, enjoyable space that's good for kids and even welcomes your furry friends.
