Our Story

The Heart of Marion's Craft Scene

Moved back to NC after six years away and found ourselves living in Marion and were glad to have found this awesome little taproom. We've been here a handful of times. Great use of a brilliant old building on Main Street. The vibe is relaxed and very welcoming.

It's an all craft tap list focusing on NC breweries with cider and wine. The food is "pub food" really tasty and well worth the money. They do a great job of keeping the tap list interesting, usually a good spectrum of styles from NC brew.

"Refinery 13 is our go-to spot! The atmosphere is perfect, the beer selection is always top-notch, and those chicken wings are seriously addictive. A true gem in Marion."

— A Satisfied Regular

Our Delicious Offerings

Explore the best of North Carolina's vibrant brewing scene with our constantly changing tap list.

Our Promise

Why Marion Chooses Refinery 13

Unrivaled NC Craft Beer Focus

We're dedicated to showcasing North Carolina's finest breweries. Our tap list is a curated journey through local flavors, ensuring every visit is a new discovery.

Delicious "Pub Food" Done Right

From our must-try Signature Chicken Wings to other hearty delights, our food is consistently tasty, satisfying, and offers incredible value. Perfect pairing for your brew.

Relaxed Vibe & Welcoming Community

Housed in a brilliant old Main Street building, we offer a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere. It's the perfect spot to unwind, meet friends, or make new ones. Plus, we offer take-out and take reservations for your convenience!
