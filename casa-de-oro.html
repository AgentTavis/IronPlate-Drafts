Our Story

Bringing the Heart of Mexico to Creston

For years, Casa De Oro has been a cornerstone of the Creston community, renowned for its vibrant flavors and a dining experience that feels like home. We believe in the power of great food and warm hospitality to bring people together.

Our kitchen is dedicated to crafting authentic Mexican dishes, prepared with care and the freshest ingredients, just like abuela used to make. From our sizzling fajitas to our refreshing margaritas, every visit is a celebration of taste and tradition.

Our Specialties

Discover Your Next Favorite Dish

Classic Tacos

Your choice of seasoned chicken, savory beef, or succulent carnitas, served on soft corn tortillas with fresh toppings.

Signature Fajitas

Sizzling platters of marinated steak, chicken, or shrimp with peppers and onions, served with warm tortillas and all the fixings.

Loaded Burritos

Packed with your favorite fillings – from hearty beans and cheese to tender carnitas or steak, smothered in our signature sauce.

Enchiladas Rojas / Verdes

Corn tortillas filled with cheese, chicken, or beef, generously covered in our rich red or zesty green sauce and melted cheese.

Queso Fundido

A bubbling skillet of melted cheese, often mixed with chorizo, served with warm tortilla chips for dipping. Pure comfort!

Guacamole & Chips

Our freshly prepared, creamy guacamole made with ripe avocados, cilantro, onion, and lime, served with crispy tortilla chips.
