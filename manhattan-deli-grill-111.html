Our Story

More Than Just Food, It's a Raeford Tradition.

Nestled in the heart of Raeford, Manhattan Deli & Grill 111 has been a beloved local spot, serving up fresh, satisfying meals with a friendly smile. We believe in crafting dishes that bring joy, from our famous cheesesteaks to refreshing salads and delightful desserts. Come discover why we're a true hidden gem for comfort food and good times.

Our commitment to quality ingredients and generous portions makes every visit a memorable one. Whether you're grabbing a quick bite during your lunch break or enjoying a relaxing dinner with family, you'll find a welcoming atmosphere and a menu full of irresistible choices that keep our regulars coming back for more.
