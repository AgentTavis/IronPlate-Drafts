Our Story

Brewing Community, One Pint at a Time.

We are Forty Second Brew Co., a passionate craft brewery nestled in the heart of Kingston, NC. Born from a love for exceptional beer and a desire to connect, we've dedicated ourselves to crafting high-quality brews with a side of fun and engaging experiences.

Our journey is rooted deeply in the South Shore community. We believe in building strong local ties, supporting fellow businesses, and actively contributing to local charities. For us, every pint poured and every meal served is an opportunity to strengthen our community and create lasting relationships.
