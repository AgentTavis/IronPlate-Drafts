Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Tarboro Tradition.

Nestled at the vibrant corner of St James Street and Western Ave, just north of exit 485 off of US-64, Country Sunrise Grill has been a cherished landmark and a convenient stop for locals and travelers alike. We're proud to be a staple in the Tarboro community, offering a warm welcome and delicious, home-style cooking that satisfies every time.

Since opening our doors, we've focused on creating a friendly atmosphere where everyone feels at home. Whether you're passing through or live just around the corner, we invite you to experience the comforting flavors and genuine hospitality that make Country Sunrise Grill a true Tarboro gem.
