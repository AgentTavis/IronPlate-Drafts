Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Tarboro Tradition.

Nestled conveniently at the bustling corner of St James Street and Western Ave, just a stone's throw north of US-64's exit 485, Country Sunrise Grill has been a cherished landmark for both travelers and locals alike. For years, we've prided ourselves on being that welcoming stopping place, a beacon of home-style cooking and genuine Southern hospitality.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a place where comfort food meets community. We believe in serving hearty meals made with love, in an atmosphere where everyone feels like family. Whether you're passing through Tarboro on a long journey or you're a familiar face from down the street, we invite you to pull up a chair and experience the warmth and flavors that make Country Sunrise Grill a true local gem.
