Our Story

A Culinary Journey, Rooted in Passion

At Copper & Salt Northwest Kitchen, we believe in the power of fresh ingredients and heartfelt cooking. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a dining experience where the vibrant spirit of Mexican culinary traditions meets the bountiful produce of the Pacific Northwest. Every dish is a testament to this unique fusion, crafted with care and a deep respect for both heritage and innovation.

We're more than just a restaurant; we're a gathering place for friends, family, and food lovers in Ruston, NC. Our kitchen is where tradition is honored, and creativity thrives, inviting you to discover new favorites and cherish timeless flavors.
