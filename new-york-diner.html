Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community Gathering Spot.

Welcome to New York Diner, Knightdale's cherished local spot where classic diner charm meets a delightful Tex-Mex twist. We're proud to be that "funky spot" locals love, a place where every visit feels like coming home.

From the moment you walk in, you'll feel the friendly energy. Our team loves what they do, and it shows in the warm welcomes, fast service, and delicious food we serve up every day. Whether you're craving our traditional Breakfast Special, savory tacos, or a hearty burger, we've got something to satisfy every taste bud.

We believe in good food, good coffee, and great company. Come on down and see why New York Diner is where memories are made, one delicious bite at a time.
