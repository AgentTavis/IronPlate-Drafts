Welcome Home to Southern Hospitality

Southern Family Restaurant, nestled at 4689 Yadkinville Rd in the heart of Pfafftown, NC, is more than just a place to eat – it's where community gathers. We pride ourselves on offering a warm, welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels like family. From hearty meals prepared with care to our friendly service, we're dedicated to creating memorable dining experiences.

We offer a variety of convenient dining options, including swift takeout for those on the go, and a comfortable dine-in experience perfect for family dinners or a relaxed evening out. Come taste the tradition and become part of our Southern family.
