OUR STORY

Rooted in Tradition, Made with Love

Nestled at 4689 Yadkinville Rd, Southern Family Restaurant has been a beloved cornerstone of Pfafftown, NC, for years. Our doors are open to everyone seeking comfort food cooked with a touch of Southern charm and genuine hospitality.

We pride ourselves on offering a versatile dining experience. Whether you're planning a cozy family dinner, a quick and delicious takeout meal, or a relaxed dine-in experience with friends, we ensure every visit feels like coming home. Our commitment to quality ingredients and heartfelt service is what makes us a true local treasure.
