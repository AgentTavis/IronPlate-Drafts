Our Story

A Local Favorite for Comfort & Flavor

Welcome to Little Fuji Restaurant, a cherished spot in Laurinburg, NC, where the warmth of a classic diner meets the vibrant flavors of Japanese cuisine, sushi, and boba. Right off the highway, we're easy to find and even easier to love.

We pride ourselves on offering an excellent food experience in a casual, welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're craving a hearty hibachi meal, fresh sushi, or a sweet boba tea, we've got something for everyone. Our dedicated team is committed to making every visit a delightful one, feeling just like home.
