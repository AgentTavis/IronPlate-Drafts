Our Story

Experience the Warmth of Our Family Diner

Welcome to Little Fuji Restaurant, a cherished spot in Laurinburg, NC, where the cozy comfort of a diner meets the vibrant flavors of Japan. Nestled conveniently right off the highway, we've become a beloved stop for locals and travelers alike looking for excellent food in a casual, friendly atmosphere.

What started as a simple idea to bring quality Japanese dishes, fresh sushi, and delightful boba to our community has grown into a place where every meal feels like coming home. Our customers rave about the authenticity and deliciousness of our offerings, especially our famous Fuji Combo for 2, a perfect meal to share.

While our ambiance is casual and reminiscent of your favorite neighborhood diner, our commitment to exceptional Japanese cuisine shines through in every dish. Come on in, grab a seat, and let us serve you a meal that's both comforting and exciting.
