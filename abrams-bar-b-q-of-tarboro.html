Our Journey

Where Authentic BBQ Meets Southern Hospitality

Located at 609 W Wilson St, Tarboro, NC, Abrams Bar-B-Q is more than just a restaurant; it's a taste of tradition forged in the heart of Tarboro. We pride ourselves on offering a mouth-watering variety of barbecue and fried chicken dishes, prepared with authentic flavors and a touch of Southern hospitality.

Since opening our doors on that memorable Tuesday, February 3rd, we've been dedicated to serving up comfort food that tastes like home. From our slow-smoked meats to our perfectly crispy fried chicken, every dish is crafted with passion. While some menu items, such as the fried chicken, are absolute fan favorites, every dish at Abrams Bar-B-Q is created to bring smiles and satisfy the heartiest appetites. Come experience the rich flavors and warm atmosphere that make us a beloved local spot.
