Our Journey

Authentic Bar-B-Q Rooted in Tarboro Tradition

Welcome to Abrams Bar-B-Q, Tarboro's cherished spot for mouth-watering barbecue and crispy fried chicken. Located at 609 W Wilson St, we pride ourselves on bringing a variety of classic American comfort foods to your table.

Since our exciting opening on Tuesday, February 3rd at 7:00 am, we've been dedicated to serving up hot, fresh, and delicious meals that capture the heart of Southern cooking. Our commitment to quality ingredients and time-honored recipes ensures every bite is a memorable one.
