Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community Hub

Prism Coffee Co. isn't just a place to grab a bite; it's a cornerstone of the Hickory community. For years, we've been dedicated to serving up delicious, comforting meals and creating a welcoming atmosphere where friends and family can gather. From our carefully crafted burgers to our selection of local brews, every detail is designed to make you feel right at home. We're proud to be your beloved local spot, where good food and great company come together.

Our passion for quality ingredients and warm hospitality shines through in every dish we serve. We believe in fostering a space where laughter echoes, stories are shared, and every guest leaves with a smile. Join us and become part of the Prism Coffee Co. family.

Our Delicious Offerings

Classic Cheeseburger

Our signature burger with a juicy patty, melted cheddar, fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, and our special sauce.

Grilled Chicken Sandwich

Tender grilled chicken breast, crisp bacon, provolone cheese, avocado, and tangy BBQ on a toasted bun.

Loaded Nachos

Crispy tortilla chips piled high with seasoned beef, melted cheese, jalapeños, salsa, sour cream, and guacamole.

Crispy Fish & Chips

Golden-fried cod served with a generous portion of seasoned fries and homemade tartar sauce.

House Salad with Grilled Shrimp

Fresh mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, and succulent grilled shrimp with a lemon vinaigrette.

Spicy Chicken Wings

Perfectly crispy wings tossed in your choice of mild, hot, or fiery habanero sauce, served with ranch or blue cheese.

Craft Beer Selection

An ever-changing selection of local and regional craft beers on tap and in bottles. Ask your server for today's specials!

Artisan Coffee & Espresso

From expertly brewed pour-overs to rich espresso drinks, find your perfect cup from our carefully selected beans.
