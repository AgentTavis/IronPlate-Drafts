Our Story

Where Every Bite Tells a Tale

Nestled in downtown Asheboro, The Black Lantern Tea Room & Bakery is more than just a cafe—it's a quaint little escape filled with lots of tasty treat options and big fun. We appreciate that our space always feels like a celebration, with our menu reflecting the festivities and themes of the day or week. Our unique dark academia decor invites you to linger, to study, to dream, all while savoring handcrafted delights.

We pride ourselves on constantly rotating our baked goods and sandwiches, ensuring there's always a new discovery, a new celebration of flavors. We keep our social media up to date on our latest treats, so you're always in the loop. And if you're wondering about our specialties, it is by far the best cheesecake you'll find in town! Come, step into our story, and let us be a part of yours.
