Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's Family Tradition

Tex's Fish Camp isn't just a restaurant; it's a piece of Connelly Springs history. We're a family owned and operated establishment, committed to serving our customers as if they were one of our own. Since the 1970s, we've upheld a simple promise: great food, warm hospitality, and a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home.

From our fresh seafood and hearty steaks to our classic Southern vegetables and daily specials, every dish is prepared with the care and tradition that have made us a local favorite for generations. Come join our family for a memorable dining experience.
