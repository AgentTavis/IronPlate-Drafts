Our Heritage

A Family Tradition, Served with Love

Where Every Guest Becomes Family

Nestled in Connelly Springs, Tex's Fish Camp has been a beloved family-owned and operated establishment for decades. Our philosophy is simple: treat every customer just like family. This isn't just a tagline; it's the foundation of everything we do, from the moment you walk through our doors.

We pride ourselves on serving up hearty, delicious meals – from fresh seafood to perfectly cooked steaks and an array of home-style vegetables and daily specials. We believe in quality ingredients, generous portions, and an atmosphere where laughter and good food are always in abundance. It's the kind of place where memories are made, just like they were back in the 1970s when our doors first opened.

“Tex's isn't just a restaurant; it's a warm hug and a taste of home. Every visit feels like a family reunion, with food that always hits the spot. A true Connelly Springs treasure!”

— A Loyal Customer, Since 1985
