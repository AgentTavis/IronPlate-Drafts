Our Heritage

A Timeless Tradition in the Heart of Columbia

Nestled in the heart of Columbia State Historic Park, this venerable building was first opened by George Morgan in 1856 as the Morgan Hotel and Restaurant. Today, the City Hotel Restaurant carries on this rich tradition, featuring an elevated menu rooted in traditional American cuisine and showcasing locally sourced produce.
