Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Memory.

Welcome to Mi Rancho, your go-to spot in Batesburg-Leesville for authentic flavors and good times. We've poured our heart into crafting a menu packed with bold, flavorful dishes made from the freshest ingredients. From sizzling fajitas to hearty burgers, every plate tells a story of tradition and passion.

But it's not just about the food. It's about the laughter shared over refreshing cocktails and ice-cold beers that perfectly complement your meal. Whether you’re gathering with friends for game night, celebrating a special family occasion, or simply enjoying a relaxed dinner, we’re here to make every visit memorable.

At Mi Rancho, we believe in creating an inviting atmosphere where everyone feels at home. Our friendly staff is dedicated to ensuring you have a fantastic experience, every single time. Come on in, pull up a chair, and let us serve you a taste of South Carolina's favorite Bar & Grill!
