Our Story

Rooted in the Outdoors, Driven by Community

It is the mission of Saluda Outfitters to be the preferred outdoor destination for Saluda, N.C., and the Western North Carolina area. We specialize in top-quality outdoor gear, footwear, and apparel, ensuring you're ready for any adventure our beautiful region offers.

Beyond the gear, we've cultivated a vibrant community hub. Our trendy tap room and grill offer a cozy spot to unwind with gourmet burgers, savory smoked pork, and a rotating selection of craft beers. Enjoy live music from local talents, making every visit a memorable experience. We're more than just a shop or a restaurant; we're a place where adventure meets comfort, and community thrives.
