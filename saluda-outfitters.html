OUR STORY

More Than Just a Meal. It's an Experience.

At Saluda Outfitters, our mission is to be the preferred outdoor destination for Saluda, N.C., and the entire Western North Carolina area. We've brought together the best of both worlds: top-quality outdoor gear, footwear, and apparel, alongside a vibrant and trendy tap room and grill.

Nestled in the heart of Saluda, we're a place where locals and visitors alike can gather, enjoy delicious food, savor craft beers, and unwind to live music. Whether you're fueling up before an adventure, sharing stories after a hike, or simply looking for a lively spot to spend your evening, Saluda Outfitters offers a unique blend of community, culinary delight, and mountain spirit.
