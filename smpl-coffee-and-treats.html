Welcome to SMPL

First thing on the to do list everyday is drink coffee! At SMPL coffee and treats, we believe in keeping things simple, delicious, and community-focused. We're a local Bar And Grill serving Hampstead, NC, where every cup is brewed with care and every treat is baked with love.
