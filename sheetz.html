Our Heritage

More Than Just a Stop, It's a Dobson Tradition

At Sheetz of Dobson, we're proud to be a familiar face in the heart of our community. While we're part of a larger family, our roots run deep here in Dobson, serving our neighbors with genuine hospitality and convenience that fits your busy life.

We've grown to be a place where you can always find what you need – whether it's a quick, satisfying meal from our grill, a refreshing drink, or those essential everyday items. We strive to offer quality and variety, ensuring there's something for everyone, any time of day or night.
