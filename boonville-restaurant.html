Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Boonville Tradition.

At Boonville Restaurant, we believe in crafting more than just delicious food; we create memorable experiences. Nestled right on Main Street, we've become a cherished hub for locals and visitors alike, a place where good company meets great flavor. Our doors are open early for a hearty breakfast and stay open late enough for a relaxed dinner, all served with a genuine smile.

While we're renowned for our burgers, fries, and cheese sticks, our story is deeply rooted in the community of Boonville. We're proud to be a gathering spot, a place where families celebrate, friends catch up, and new memories are made over every plate.
