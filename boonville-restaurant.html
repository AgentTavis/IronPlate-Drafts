Our Heritage

Taste The Story

Welcome to Boonville Restaurant, a cherished local spot where good food and great company come together. While known as your friendly Bar & Grill, we offer a unique experience that goes beyond the plate. Our commitment to quality extends to every dish, crafted with care to bring you comfort and flavor.

With 16 varieties of estate-grown and bottled wines available year-round, visitors can enjoy tastings, pairing dinners, live music, cabin rentals, and even host events. It's an experience that truly sets us apart, inviting you to relax and create lasting memories in the heart of Boonville, NC.
