OUR STORY

Crafting Memories, One Dish at a Time

Welcome to Blue Agave Mexican Restaurant, a cherished gathering place in Fletcher, NC, where delicious food meets warm hospitality. We've crafted a welcoming space with big tables perfect for large parties, ensuring everyone feels at home.

Our friendly staff is dedicated to making your visit memorable, treating you like family from the moment you step inside. Explore our gigantic menu, boasting a wide array of authentic Mexican dishes and delightful American options, designed to satisfy every palate.

We believe in creating unforgettable dining experiences, serving up passion in every plate – especially our renowned, flavorful salsa. Come and discover why Blue Agave is a beloved local favorite!

OUR MENU

Authentic Flavors, Endless Choices

Dive into our gigantic menu featuring traditional Mexican cuisine and beloved American options. Crafted with fresh ingredients and passion, every dish tells a story.

Chicken Fajitas

Sizzling strips of marinated chicken, grilled with onions and peppers. Served with warm tortillas and all the fixings.

Steak Fajitas

Tender steak strips grilled to perfection with bell peppers and onions, ready for you to build your perfect bite.

Tacos Al Carbon

Authentic street-style tacos with your choice of grilled meats, fresh cilantro, and onions.

Burritos

Large flour tortillas filled with savory meats, rice, beans, and cheese, smothered in our delicious sauces.

Quesadillas

Melted cheese and your favorite fillings grilled between two tortillas, a perfect crispy delight.

Enchiladas

Corn tortillas rolled with your choice of filling, topped with rich sauce and melted cheese.

Salsa & Chips

Our famously amazing and flavorful homemade salsa served with warm, crispy tortilla chips. An essential start!
