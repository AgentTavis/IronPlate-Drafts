Our Story

More Than Just a Meal: It's Home Cooking.

Welcome to Peggy Sue’s Diner, a true Blackstone treasure where every plate comes with a side of friendly, down-home service. Owned and managed by Peggy Sue Moore herself, this diner is more than just a place to eat – it's a reflection of her passion for good food and community.

Peggy Sue, a proud resident of Blackstone, Virginia, brings a unique blend of dedication and warmth to her diner. With a background as a former Socs-C USMC Communication Specialist, she understands the importance of clear communication and heartfelt connection, values she now pours into every interaction at the diner. After studying at Ashworth, Peggy Sue envisioned a place where locals could gather for delicious, unpretentious food.

For the past 8 months, Peggy Sue's Diner has quickly become a beloved spot, renowned for its delicious breakfast and lunch menu. It’s a genuine locals spot, where familiar faces are always greeted with a smile and newcomers feel instantly at home. We're currently open for breakfast and lunch, offering a menu crafted to satisfy your cravings with classic comfort. Come on in and experience the charm and taste that make Peggy Sue’s Diner a cornerstone of Blackstone.
