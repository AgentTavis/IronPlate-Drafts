Explore Our Menu

From hearty breakfast plates to customizable hash browns, there's something for everyone.

Breakfast Plates

Served with Biscuit or Toast and choice of grits, gravy, or hash browns. Start your day the right way with our classic, hearty breakfast options. Please note: No Substitutions Please.

Custom Hash Browns

Crispy, golden hash browns with your choice of delicious toppings. Customize with Peppers, Onions, Mushrooms, Diced Ham, Jalapeno, Diced Tomato, or Cheese.

Sides

Perfect additions to any meal, our selection of sides complements your main dish. Ask your server for today's fresh options. CONSUMER ADVISORY: Consuming raw or undercooked meats, poultry, seafood, shellfish or eggs may increase your risk of foodborne illness.
