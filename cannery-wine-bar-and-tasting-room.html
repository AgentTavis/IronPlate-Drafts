Our Heritage

More Than Just a Bar, It's a Community Hub.

Nestled in the heart of Sun Prairie, Cannery Wine Bar and Tasting Room offers a unique escape where warmth meets sophistication. We believe in creating an atmosphere where every visit feels like coming home. Whether you're a connoisseur or just looking for a cozy spot, our doors are open.

You'll find plenty of seating inside at our rustic wine barrel tables or along our inviting long bar. For those who prefer fresh air, our charming outside patio area in the back is the perfect retreat. We pride ourselves on a very clean space, thoughtfully decorated with unique Wisconsin items that celebrate local charm and history.

While our food menu is concise, focusing on our delicious Lotsa Mozza pizza, we warmly welcome you to carry in from any local restaurant. It's about enjoying your favorite food alongside our exceptional selection of beverages.

"Cannery is truly a great downtown spot for wine, craft beer, and spirits. It’s the perfect place for a spirited conversation, especially when paired with their awesome bar pizza or some grub from a local spot. Always a fantastic time!"

— A Loyal Sun Prairie Local
