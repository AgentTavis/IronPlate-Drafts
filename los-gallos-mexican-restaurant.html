Our Journey

Taste the Heart of Mexico in Monroe

Welcome to Los Gallos! Here in Monroe, NC, we're passionate about bringing you authentic Mexican flavors made with love and tradition. Our family recipes are crafted with fresh ingredients, ensuring every dish tells a story of heritage and passion.

As many of our guests rave, our dishes, like the enchiladas Verdes con Pollo, are truly special. We grill our meats to perfection, infusing them with smoky goodness, and our vibrant verde salsa delivers a delightful, authentic heat that will awaken your palate. We invite you to experience the warmth and taste of Mexico right here with us, creating memorable dining experiences for you and your loved ones.
