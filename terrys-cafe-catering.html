OUR STORY

Rooted in Community, Crafted with Care.

Terry's Cafe & Catering, nestled in the heart of State Road, NC, is more than just a place to eat—it's a cherished local institution. For years, we've been known for our warm atmosphere, friendly faces, and a menu brimming with delicious comfort food that feels like a hug from home.

Our commitment is simple: to provide a welcoming space where friends and family can gather, share laughter, and savor hearty meals made with fresh ingredients and a whole lot of love. From sunrise breakfast to Friday night dinners, we invite you to experience the flavor of genuine hospitality.
