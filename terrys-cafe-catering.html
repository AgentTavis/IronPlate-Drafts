Our Heritage

Serving Generations with Heartfelt Meals

Terry's Cafe & Catering, nestled in the heart of State Road, NC, is more than just a diner; it's a cherished community hub known for its warm atmosphere and delicious, homestyle comfort food.

For years, we've been dedicated to serving up classic diner favorites and heartwarming meals, crafted with the freshest ingredients and a touch of genuine Southern hospitality. We believe in good food, great company, and making every guest feel like family from the moment they walk through our doors.

Come on in and taste the tradition that makes Terry's Cafe a true local gem. We're proud to be a place where memories are made, one delicious bite at a time.

Our Offerings

Taste The Terry's Difference

The Triple Terry Burger

Our legendary signature burger: three juicy patties, crispy bacon, melted cheese, and all the fixings, stacked high for the ultimate diner experience.

Classic Breakfast Platter

Two farm-fresh eggs cooked your way, crispy bacon or sausage, golden home fries, and your choice of toast. The perfect start to any day.

Fluffy Buttermilk Pancakes

A generous stack of light and fluffy pancakes, served with real maple syrup and a dollop of butter. Add fresh berries for a sweet treat.

Hearty Reuben Sandwich

Piled high with tender corned beef, tangy sauerkraut, melted Swiss cheese, and creamy Russian dressing on toasted rye bread.

Daily Blue Plate Special

Ask about today's rotating comfort food special, featuring classic dishes made with seasonal ingredients and our unique homemade touch.

Homemade Apple Pie

A slice of classic Americana. Warm, flaky crust filled with tender, spiced apples. Perfect à la mode with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
