Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience

Stony Mountain Vineyards is deeply rooted in the heart of Albemarle, NC, cherished by locals and visitors alike. We are a beloved local spot where community gathers, stories are shared, and every meal is crafted with care and a touch of regional charm.

Nestled amidst picturesque landscapes, our vineyard offers a unique blend of casual bar and grill dining with the sophisticated backdrop of our very own vineyards. We believe in creating a welcoming atmosphere where you can relax, savor delicious food, and enjoy fine wines straight from our estate.

Delightful Dishes & Vineyard Pairings

Vineyard Burger

Our signature burger, crafted with locally sourced beef, caramelized onions, smoked cheddar, and our special house sauce, served on a brioche bun.

Smoked Ribs Platter

Slow-smoked to perfection, our tender ribs are basted in a tangy BBQ sauce and served with a side of coleslaw and crispy fries.

Caprese Flatbread

A fresh and vibrant flatbread topped with ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, basil, and a balsamic glaze, baked until golden.

Grilled Salmon with Dill

Perfectly grilled Atlantic salmon, seasoned with fresh dill and lemon, accompanied by seasonal roasted vegetables.

Artisan Cheese Plate

A curated selection of local and imported cheeses, served with vineyard preserves, olives, and artisan crackers – perfect for wine pairing.

Truffle Fries

Crispy golden fries tossed with truffle oil, Parmesan cheese, and fresh parsley. An irresistible side.

Chicken Caesar Salad

Crisp romaine lettuce, grilled chicken breast, Parmesan shavings, and homemade croutons, tossed in a classic Caesar dressing.

Seasonal Dessert Crumble

Warm, comforting fruit crumble featuring seasonal berries or apples, topped with a buttery oat crust and a scoop of vanilla bean ice cream.
