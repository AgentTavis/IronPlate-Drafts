Our Heritage

Where Warmth Meets Rustic Charm

Back Alley Barn "Saluda's Tavern" is a beloved local spot nestled in the heart of charming Saluda, NC. We're more than just a bar and grill; we're a community hub where laughter echoes, stories are shared, and good times are always on the menu.

We pride ourselves on offering a genuinely cozy atmosphere, a perfect escape from the everyday. Whether you're a long-time local or just passing through, you'll find a welcoming embrace and a taste of Saluda's true, unpretentious hospitality. Come unwind with us and experience what makes our tavern a cherished part of the town.

"Stepping into Back Alley Barn feels like coming home. The ambiance is perfect, the people are friendly, and it's simply the best spot in Saluda to relax!"

— A Happy Regular
