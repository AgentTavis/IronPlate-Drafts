Our Story

Generations of Southern Charm & Flavor

For years, Papa's Old South has been a cherished local gem in Lincolnton, GA, serving up honest-to-goodness country cooking and a welcoming atmosphere that feels just like home. We believe in tradition, quality ingredients, and the simple joy of sharing a meal with loved ones.

Every dish is crafted with care, and every guest is treated like family. Come experience a taste of the real South, where every visit is a celebration of classic hospitality and delicious food that warms the soul.
