Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Memory.

Nestled in the heart of Lincolnton, GA, Papa's Old South is more than just a bar and grill – it's a cherished family tradition. We've poured our heart and soul into creating an inviting country cafe where genuine Southern hospitality meets truly delicious food.

We're proud to be a local favorite, known for our warm atmosphere, friendly service, and a menu filled with Southern comfort classics that taste just like Grandma used to make. Come experience why our guests consistently rate us 4.7 stars and make us the #4 restaurant in Lincolnton on Tripadvisor.
