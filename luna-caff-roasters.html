OUR STORY

Our Passion for Coffee, Brewed with Heart.

Luna Caffè Roasters isn't just a coffee shop; it's a vibrant cornerstone of downtown Wilmington, NC. We are proudly independent, a true local gem where the aroma of freshly roasted beans fills the air every single day.

Our commitment is simple: to bring you exceptional coffee, roasted right here in-house, served in an atmosphere that's as unique and inspiring as our community. We believe in crafting more than just beverages; we create experiences, foster connections, and celebrate the artistry of coffee.

OUR COMMITMENT

Why Wilmington Chooses Luna Caffè Roasters.

Freshly Roasted In-House

As an independent coffee roaster, we ensure every bean is roasted right here in Wilmington, guaranteeing unparalleled freshness and flavor in every cup, from our brewed coffee to your artisan espresso.

Eclectic, Artsy Atmosphere

More than just a café, Luna Caffè is a hub of creativity. Our unique, artsy environment is perfect for inspiration, relaxation, or meeting friends, offering a distinct experience that sets us apart.

Independent & Community Focused

We are a proud local business, deeply rooted in the Wilmington community. With a 4.7-star rating from hundreds of reviews, we thrive on local support and give back to the vibrant culture that surrounds us.
