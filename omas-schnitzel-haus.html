Our Heritage

A Taste of Home, A Family Tradition in Hope Mills

Welcome to Oma's Schnitzel Haus, where every dish tells a story of tradition, flavor, and heartfelt hospitality. Nestled in the heart of Hope Mills, we've been proudly serving up authentic German-American cuisine in a cozy, welcoming atmosphere perfect for family dinners, gatherings with friends, or a relaxing evening at the bar.

We believe in honest cooking and transparent practices. To continue delivering the best experience while managing operating costs, please note that a 3.00% charge will be applied to credit card transactions. This helps us offset processing fees, ensuring we can maintain the quality and value you've come to expect from Oma's.

Our commitment extends beyond the plate; it's about creating a place where you feel at home. From our crispy schnitzels to our friendly service, we invite you to become a part of the Oma's family and discover why we're a beloved local favorite.
