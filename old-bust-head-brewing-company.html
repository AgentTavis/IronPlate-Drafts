Our Roots

More Than Just a Brew, It's a Community.

Welcome to Old Bust Head Brewing Company, New Baltimore's beloved local spot where good times and great flavors come together. We've built a reputation for our vibrant atmosphere, fantastic craft beers, and a commitment to bringing the freshest, most exciting culinary experiences right to your table – or rather, right to our patio!

Our philosophy is simple: keep things fresh and exciting. That's why we feature a weekly lineup of incredible rotating food trucks. This means every visit offers a new adventure for your taste buds, perfectly paired with our expertly brewed craft beers. From gourmet burgers to international delights, there's always something new to discover.

We believe in creating a space where friends and family can gather, relax, and make lasting memories. Come for the beer, stay for the camaraderie, and always look forward to what's new on the menu from our visiting food partners.
