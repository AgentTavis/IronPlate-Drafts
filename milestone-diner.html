Our Story

Experience the Milestone Difference: A Warm Welcome Off the Interstate.

We believe that a diner is more than just a place to eat; it's a stop on your journey, a moment of comfort, and a place where everyone feels at home. Our commitment to friendly service and classic, satisfying meals is what makes us a local favorite. We love hearing about our visitors' experiences, like this recent traveler:

"We stopped here while waiting to pick up our furniture. The salesperson suggested we go down to the next exit to grab a bit to eat while waiting. My husband didn't want to drive all around so we seen that there was a diner on exit 75. From the minute we walked in the service was very welcoming and warm. The food was diner food, but I was so excited to get gravy on my fries. Something we don't find near Charleston. We travel with my service dog and we were not stuck with a little table in the corner."

That's the Milestone experience we strive for every day – a welcoming atmosphere, delicious food, and a place for everyone.
