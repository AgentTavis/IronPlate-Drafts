Our Story

More Than Just a Meal: A Community Hub in Stoneville

At Ritz Gourmet, we believe in great food, warm hospitality, and giving back to the community that has embraced us. Nestled in the heart of Stoneville, we've become a cherished gathering place where friends, family, and neighbors come together to enjoy delicious bar and grill classics.

We're proud of our roots and our commitment to service. In fact, our dedication to Stoneville was even recognized by Congressman Mark Walker on November 5, 2018. He personally presented Amy Martin a Certificate of Special Congressional Recognition for her impactful service, even joining us in the kitchen to help cook some tenderloin!

This spirit of community and hands-on dedication is infused into every dish we serve. We're not just a restaurant; we're a part of Stoneville's story, and we invite you to be a part of ours.
