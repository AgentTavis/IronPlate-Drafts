Our Story

A New Chapter for a Franklin Legacy.

Nestled in the heart of Franklin, NC, Rock House Lodge proudly continues the cherished tradition of the famous Louise's Rockhouse Restaurant. Under the passionate new ownership of Hoyt, we are dedicated to serving up hearty breakfasts, satisfying lunches, and delicious dinners that bring our community together.

We honor the rich history and flavors of the past while crafting new memories and experiences for every guest. Our commitment is to fresh ingredients, genuine hospitality, and a welcoming atmosphere where every visit feels like coming home. Your trust in our legacy and new vision is our greatest priority.
