Our Story

Crafting Moments, One Sip and Bite at a Time

Welcome to Murphys Wine Bar & Beer Garden, Murphys, NC's newest destination for exquisite tastes and memorable evenings. Our doors opened on December 5th, and we've been delighted to welcome you into our cozy space.

We believe in the power of good company, great conversation, and even better provisions. That's why we're offering a curated experience that brings together Wine, Food, and Sparkling Surprises, all under one roof.

Our wine list is a journey in itself, traveling the globe to bring you the finest selections. So whether you fancy a bold red that speaks of ancient vineyards, a crisp white perfect for any occasion, or Ann Marie’s beloved sparkling wines—she’s our official Bubble Boss and her passion for fizz is infectious—there’s truly something for every palate.

But we're more than just wine! Our kitchen is bustling, serving up fresh, craveable delights. From vibrant salads and mouth-watering flatbreads to artistic cheese & charcuterie boards that are almost too beautiful to eat, and delightful desserts. Every dish is crafted to complement your drink and elevate your experience.

"Murphys is exactly what our town needed! The atmosphere is perfect, the wine selection is incredible, and those Roman-inspired Pinsas are an absolute game-changer."

— A Local Enthusiast
