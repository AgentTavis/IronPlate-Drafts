Our Story

More Than Just Coffee – It's a Rodanthe Tradition.

From a humble beginning, Waves Coffee & Candies has grown into a cherished Rodanthe landmark. We're proud to be locally owned and operated, pouring our heart into every cup of coffee and curating a delightful selection of candies and treats.

Our commitment to quality shines through our locally sourced coffee beans, ensuring every sip is a taste of the Outer Banks. While our acclaimed coffee and sweet treats draw visitors from all over, our charming candy shop offers a nostalgic escape, perfect for finding that special souvenir or indulging your sweet tooth.
