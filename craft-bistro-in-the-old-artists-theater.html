Our Heritage

Experience the Heart of West Jefferson's Culinary Scene

Nestled in the historic Old Artists' Theater, Craft Bistro offers a unique blend of sophisticated dining and mountain charm. We pride ourselves on creating an inviting atmosphere where friends and family can gather to enjoy exceptional food, thoughtfully curated wines, and a selection of local craft beverages.

Since opening our doors, we've become a cherished part of the West Jefferson community, known for our commitment to quality ingredients, impeccable service, and a passion for culinary artistry. Every dish tells a story, and every visit is an experience to remember.
