Our Story

A Taste of Hertford's Heart & Soul

Located at 116 W. Market St in Hertford, Carolina Moon Bakery is more than just a place to eat—it's a community gathering spot. We pour our passion into every creation, from the aroma of fresh breads that greet you at the door to the sweet delight of our cakes and pastries.

Every Wednesday, we transform into Hertford's favorite pizza destination for "Pizza Wednesday," a tradition cherished by locals for our delicious, hot, and fresh-baked pizzas. We're dedicated to bringing joy to your table, one delightful bite at a time. Come experience the warmth and flavors of Carolina Moon Bakery, where tradition meets taste.
