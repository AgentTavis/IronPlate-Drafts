Our Roots

Serving Up Goodness, One Bite at a Time

Welcome to Cups & Cones Caf, your friendly neighborhood spot right here in Andrews, NC! We're more than just a Bar & Grill; we're a community hub where great food meets warm smiles. From the moment we opened our doors, our mission has been simple: to create a cozy, inviting atmosphere where everyone feels at home, enjoying hearty meals and fresh brews.

We believe in honest food, prepared with care, and served with a side of genuine hospitality. Whether you're starting your day with a perfectly brewed coffee, grabbing a quick, delicious lunch, or winding down with friends over a casual dinner, Cups & Cones Caf is the place to be. We're proud to be a local favorite, always striving to bring you exciting new flavors and classic comforts.

Come on in and taste the difference. We can't wait to welcome you to our table!
