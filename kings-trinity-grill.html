Our Story

Where Every Plate Tells a Tale of Trinity's Heart.

Kings Trinity Grill, a cozy eatery nestled in the heart of Trinity, North Carolina, is proudly back in business under new management! We've revived this local gem with a passion for classic American comfort foods and a commitment to our community.

Our diverse menu features everything from "up hearty breakfast plates" that fuel your day to "classic lunch specials" designed to satisfy any craving. We believe in serving "community spirit by the plateful", ensuring every visit is not just a meal, but a warm, welcoming experience for friends and family alike.
