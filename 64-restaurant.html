Taste the Home-Cooked Difference

At 64 Restaurant, we're proud to be a staple of Morganton, NC, serving up hearty "Hillbilly Truck Stop Home Cooked Meals" with a smile. We believe in honest food, generous portions, and a welcoming atmosphere that feels just like home.

Our commitment to quality shines through in every dish. Take our burgers, for example – each patty is hand-crafted right here in our kitchen by the cook, ensuring a flavor and juiciness you won't find anywhere else. Paired with crispy fries or our famous homemade chips, it's a meal that truly satisfies.

From early morning breakfasts to satisfying dinners, our friendly team is dedicated to providing a memorable dining experience. Come hungry, leave happy – that's the 64 Restaurant promise!
