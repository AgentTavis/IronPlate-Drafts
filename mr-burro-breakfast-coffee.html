OUR STORY

Fueling Raleigh's Mornings with Flavor and Heart.

At Mr. Burro, we found the best breakfast burritos in Raleigh, and we're excited to share them with you. These babies are hefty and mouth-watering, packed with scrambled eggs, cheese, black beans, and freshened with pico de gallo.

You can add your choice of meat – whether it's big slices of crispy bacon, flavorful chorizo, or juicy steak. Don't forget to top your burrito with our creamy chipotle sauce for an additional kick. But we're more than just burritos; make sure to try our famous Dulce de Leche or Tres Leche Lattes!

All in all, every visit is a great experience. We believe a great day starts with a great breakfast, served with a smile in the heart of South Raleigh.
