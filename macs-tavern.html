Our Story

More Than Just a Tavern, It's Home.

Since opening our doors in 2004, Mac's Tavern has become a cherished neighborhood watering hole in Cary, NC. We've built a reputation for serving up better-than-average food in a warm, friendly atmosphere that makes everyone feel like family. We pride ourselves on being that special place where, as one regular put it, "everybody knows your name."

From the moment you walk in, our attentive team makes sure you feel welcomed. Whether you're here to catch up with old friends, enjoy a delicious lunch like our famous Ruben sandwiches, or simply unwind after a long day, you'll find Mac's Tavern offers a genuine, local experience that keeps people coming back year after year. It's truly a place where memories are made, one pint and one plate at a time.

Our Food & Drink

Tavern Favorites & Refreshing Brews

The Classic Mac Burger

Juicy, grilled-to-perfection patty, fresh toppings, and our secret sauce. A tavern staple that never disappoints.

Signature Wings

Crispy fried wings tossed in your choice of zesty BBQ, spicy buffalo, or savory garlic parmesan. Perfect for sharing!

Famous Ruben Sandwich

A local legend! Piled high with tender corned beef, sauerkraut, Swiss cheese, and Russian dressing on grilled rye bread.

Crispy Tavern Fries

Golden brown and perfectly seasoned. The ideal companion to any meal, served hot and fresh.

Nachos Grande

A mountain of crispy tortilla chips loaded with melted cheese, jalapeños, salsa, sour cream, and your choice of protein.

Vegetarian Delight Wrap

Fresh seasonal vegetables, hummus, and a savory dressing, all wrapped in a warm tortilla. A delicious, lighter option.
