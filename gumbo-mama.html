Our Story

From Our Family Kitchen to Your Table: The Gumbo Mama Legacy

At Gumbo Mama, we pour our heart into every dish. Mama Tina's passion for authentic seafood shines through, crafting flavors so memorable, they've become legendary in Houston, NC. We believe in perfection – from our Seafood Gumbo, cooked just right with fresh ingredients and abundant, yummy okra, to our perfectly fried shrimp.

We even go the extra mile, ensuring all shrimp are tail-off for your pure enjoyment, and our secret cornmeal batter makes every bite divine. Come experience why Gumbo Mama is a local favorite, serving dishes that are always fresh, never too salty, and cooked with love.
