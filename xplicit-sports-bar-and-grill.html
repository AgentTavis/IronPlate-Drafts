Our Story

More Than Just a Bar, We're a Community Hub

Nestled in the heart of Laurinburg, Xplicit Sports Bar & Grill isn't just a place to grab a bite or a drink – it's where friends gather, families share laughs, and every game becomes an unforgettable experience. We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home.

From the moment we opened our doors, our mission has been simple: to serve up delicious food, ice-cold drinks, and a vibrant setting for sports enthusiasts and casual diners alike. Your trust is our priority, which is why we're committed to transparency and quality in everything we do. We believe in earning your loyalty through exceptional service and a truly Xplicit experience that keeps you coming back.
