Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

Welcome to La Fogata, Wadesboro's beloved Bar & Grill! For over a decade, we've been the heart of our community, serving up delicious meals and crafting memorable experiences. 'La Fogata' means 'The Bonfire,' and true to our name, we aim to be a warm, inviting place where friends and family gather, laughter flows, and good food ignites the spirit.

From sizzling steaks to our signature cocktails, every dish and drink is prepared with passion and a touch of local charm. We believe in quality ingredients, friendly service, and creating an atmosphere where every guest feels at home. Come on in, pull up a chair, and become part of our story!

"The atmosphere at La Fogata is always perfect, and the food never disappoints. It truly feels like a local treasure, a place where everyone is welcome. Our family loves coming here!"

— The Johnson Family, Wadesboro
