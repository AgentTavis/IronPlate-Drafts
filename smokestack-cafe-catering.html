Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Franklin Tradition.

Welcome to Smokestack Cafe & Catering, a cherished local diner in the heart of Franklin, NC. We are proudly located at 61 South Patton Avenue, in the very spot that was once the beloved 4L Market. Our cafe is the dream and dedication of owners Philip Lowe and Roland Mock, who set out to create a warm, inviting space where friends and family can gather for delicious, home-cooked meals.

At Smokestack, we believe in serving up classic comfort food with a fresh twist, made with high-quality ingredients and a whole lot of love. From our hearty breakfasts to our satisfying lunches, every dish is prepared to bring a smile to your face. We're not just a restaurant; we're a part of the community, committed to providing a friendly atmosphere and unforgettable dining experiences.
