Our Journey

Crafting Sweet Memories, One Treat at a Time.

Welcome to Jonny's Bakery, a cherished cornerstone of the Pineville community. What started as a humble dream to share the joy of homemade baking has blossomed into a beloved local spot. We believe in the power of good food to bring people together, and every cake, pastry, and savory bite is crafted with passion and the finest ingredients.

From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a customer; you're family. We pride ourselves on creating a warm, inviting atmosphere where you can escape the everyday, indulge your sweet tooth, and create lasting memories. It's "Cake in progress..." and so much more, right here at 551 N Polk St.
