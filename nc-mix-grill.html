Our Legacy

More Than Just Food, It's a Community Gathering

NC Mix Grill is a beloved local spot in Salisbury, NC. We started with a simple vision: to create a welcoming place where friends and family could gather, enjoy delicious food, and make lasting memories. Every dish we serve is crafted with passion, using fresh ingredients to deliver that authentic bar and grill taste you crave.

From sizzling burgers to crispy fries and perfectly mixed drinks, we've poured our heart into becoming your go-to destination. We believe in good food, great company, and the kind of friendly service that makes you feel right at home. Come on down and taste the difference that genuine local love makes!
