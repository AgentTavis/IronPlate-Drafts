Our Story

From Local Vision to Community Hub

Nestled in the heart of RiverLights, Southern Shores Craft Beer & Wine isn't just a bar – it's a dream brought to life by local owner, Josh. With a passion for premium beverages and a clear vision, Josh has cultivated a space where exceptional taste meets warm, Southern hospitality.

More than just a destination for exquisite wines, craft beers, and select bourbons, Southern Shores is a vibrant gathering place. Here, you'll find friendly faces, captivating live entertainment, and delicious food trucks, all enjoyed in a beautiful, welcoming atmosphere. It's a paradise close to the Cape Fear side, where every patron is treated like family.

"It's paradise close to the Cape Fear Side! And if in doubt? The patrons are all friendly, welcoming, and make you feel like family far from home!"

– A Happy Guest, Wilmington, NC
