Our Story

Generations of Flavor, Community, and Warmth

Nestled in the heart of Washington, NC, Shep's Grill embodies the timeless charm of a true small-town diner. We believe in hearty meals, warm welcomes, and making every guest feel like family. From our sizzling breakfast griddle to our satisfying lunch plates, we've been a beloved gathering spot for locals and visitors alike, serving up comfort and deliciousness seven days a week.

We pride ourselves on using quality ingredients to craft classic diner favorites and fresh takes on beloved dishes. It's more than just food; it's about the laughter, the conversations, and the memories made around our tables. Come experience the authentic small-town diner feel that Shep's Grill is famous for.

Why Choose Us

Experience the Shep's Grill Difference

Breakfast Bliss, Anytime

Craving breakfast for lunch? You got it! We serve our delicious breakfast menu all day Saturday, and a fantastic selection of breakfast and lunch items seven days a week. Don't miss our popular Eggs Benedict or the hearty Full Nelson Breakfast Bowl!

Authentic Small-Town Charm

Step into a truly casual and welcoming atmosphere. Shep's Grill is known for its moderate noise level, making it perfect for good conversation. It's the quintessential small-town diner experience where everyone feels at home, even your furry friends are welcome outside!

Quality Meals, Great Value

From our satisfying Breakfast Burritos to the craveable Brisket Philly Cheesesteak, every dish is prepared with care and quality ingredients. We offer generous portions and flavors that keep you coming back, ensuring a top-notch meal every time.
