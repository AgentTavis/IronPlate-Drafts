Our Roots

Experience Homestyle Comfort at Simply Divina

Welcome to Simply Divina, a charming eatery nestled in the heart of Marion, SC. We believe in crafting dishes that feel like a warm embrace, bringing together delicious homestyle cooking with a cozy, inviting atmosphere that feels just like home.

Our journey began with a simple passion: to create a place where friends and family could gather, share laughter, and savor meals made with love. From our signature "GRAB NA!" to our indulgent Italian Carbonara, every item on our menu is prepared to delight your taste buds and leave you feeling truly satisfied.

Discover Simply Divina's Irresistible Menu

GRAB NA!

Our famously flavorful dish that started it all. A must-try fusion of savory ingredients and a secret Divina twist. You'll understand why everyone's grabbing it!

Italian Carbonara

Creamy, savory, and utterly irresistible. Al dente pasta tossed in a rich, velvety sauce with crispy pancetta and a touch of Parmesan. It's truly calling your name.

Drop 23 Burger

A hearty and juicy burger stacked high with our special sauce, fresh toppings, and premium beef, served on a toasted brioche bun. Pure satisfaction with every bite.

Creamy Tomato Soup

A comforting classic, our homemade creamy tomato soup is rich, flavorful, and perfect for a cozy meal, especially when paired with a grilled cheese.

Savory Steak Tips

Tender steak tips marinated and grilled to perfection, served with a side of your choice. A truly savory experience for the meat lovers.

Irresistible Chocolate Lava Cake

End your meal on a high note with our warm, gooey chocolate lava cake, oozing with rich melted chocolate. A dessert that's truly irresistible.
