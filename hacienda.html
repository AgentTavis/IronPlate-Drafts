Our Heritage

Taste the Tradition, Feel the Family.

Hacienda isn't just a bar and grill; it's a cherished cornerstone of the Goshen, NC community. For years, we've poured our heart and soul into creating a welcoming space where neighbors become friends and every meal feels like a celebration.

We started with a simple idea: serve up delicious, honest food, perfectly paired with ice-cold drinks, all in an atmosphere that feels like home. From our sizzling burgers to our slow-cooked ribs, every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and a whole lot of love. Come on down and experience why Hacienda is truly a beloved local spot.

Our Offerings

Savor Our Flavorful Creations.

Hacienda Burger

Our classic ground beef patty, crisp lettuce, ripe tomato, red onion, pickles, and our special Hacienda sauce on a toasted brioche bun.

Spicy Chicken Sandwich

Grilled chicken breast, melted pepper jack cheese, fresh jalapeños, and zesty chipotle mayo, served on a toasted bun.

Loaded Nachos

Crispy tortilla chips piled high with melted cheddar-jack cheese, seasoned ground beef, fresh pico de gallo, and a dollop of sour cream.

BBQ Ribs

Slow-cooked, fall-off-the-bone pork ribs glazed with our tangy homemade BBQ sauce. A true taste of the South!

Sweet Potato Fries

A delicious alternative to classic fries, served hot and crispy with a side of sweet honey mustard dipping sauce.

Hacienda House Salad

Mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, shredded carrots, and crunchy croutons with your choice of dressing.
