Our Legacy

More Than Just A Bar, It's A Community Staple.

For years, The Brown Derby Pub has been the heart and soul of Jacksonville, NC. We're proud to be your local "dirty derby"—a place where friends gather, stories are shared, and every visit feels like coming home. We've built our reputation on serving up great food, cold drinks, and an unbeatable atmosphere that keeps our regulars coming back for more.
