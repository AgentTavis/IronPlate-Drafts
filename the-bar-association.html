Our Journey

More Than Just a Diner, It's a Community Hub.

The Bar Association in Smithfield isn't just a place to grab a bite; it's a vibrant extension of our passion for great food and community. While our name hints at a bar, we pride ourselves on being a fantastic spot for a delicious lunch or a light, satisfying dinner.

Every dish is crafted with care on-site, a seamless collaboration with our sister space, The Vine at TBA. This dedication ensures fresh, high-quality flavors in every bite. Whether you're unwinding inside one of our two inviting bar areas or enjoying the beautiful North Carolina weather on our spacious patio, you're guaranteed an excellent experience.

You might think our location is off the beaten path, tucked behind the outlet mall and near several hotels, but that's part of our charm. It makes us a hidden gem, easily accessible and worth seeking out for our friendly staff and exceptional food.

Our Flavors

Taste the Tradition, Savor the Freshness.

Classic Diner Burger

Our signature patty, fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and our special sauce on a toasted brioche bun.

Southern Fried Chicken & Waffles

Crispy fried chicken served with golden waffles, a drizzle of maple syrup, and a sprinkle of powdered sugar.

Philly Cheesesteak Hoagie

Thinly sliced steak grilled with onions and peppers, smothered in melted provolone on a crusty hoagie roll.

Fresh House Salad

Crisp mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and croutons with your choice of dressing.

Hand-Spun Milkshakes

Creamy, rich milkshakes made with real ice cream. Choose from vanilla, chocolate, strawberry, or a daily special.

Loaded Chili Cheese Fries

Crispy golden fries piled high with hearty chili, melted cheddar cheese, and a dollop of sour cream.

Smoked Pulled Pork Sandwich

Slow-smoked pulled pork, tangy BBQ sauce, and coleslaw, served on a toasted bun.

The TBA Breakfast Burrito

Scrambled eggs, sausage, peppers, onions, and cheese, wrapped in a warm tortilla with a side of salsa.
