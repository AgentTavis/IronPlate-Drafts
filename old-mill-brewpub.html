OUR STORY

Rooted in Community, Crafted with Care

Old Mill Brewpub isn't just a place to eat and drink; it's a cornerstone of the Lexington, SC community. We've built our reputation on serving up hearty, delicious bar and grill classics alongside a welcoming atmosphere that feels like home. Whether you're looking for a casual dinner, a lively evening with friends, or a perfect spot to host your next event, we've got you covered.

Here at the Brewpub, we proudly offer three distinct rooms available for reservation, each thoughtfully designed to cater to different event sizes and styles. If you are interested in booking a room, check out the options below to find which room best fits your occation. Then, fill out the form below and submit all your information. A manager will then contact you via your provided contact information to confirm the booking and plan food and drink catering. If you have any further questions feel free to contact us.

We believe in good food, great company, and making every visit memorable. Come experience the Old Mill Brewpub difference – where every dish is made with passion, and every guest is treated like family.
