Our Story & Vision

A Hidden Gem Where Family & Flavor Meet

Nestled just off Turnpike Road, across from West Hoke Elementary, Daddy Mac Seafood Restaurant is more than just a place to eat – it’s a culinary home away from home. We're proud to be Raeford's hidden gem, built on a foundation of friendly service, warm atmosphere, and truly great food.

Our passion is bringing delicious, large-portioned meals to your family at prices that make everyone smile. We combine the best of fresh seafood with soulful BBQ and vibrant Jamaican dishes, creating a unique menu that satisfies every craving. Come taste the difference where every meal is made with love.
