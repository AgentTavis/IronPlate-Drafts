Our Story

A Glimpse Into Our Diner Life

I visited Tom's Creek Falls in the Marion area and stopped at the Country Diner for a burger. I had high hopes based on the other reviews. The cheeseburger was the best part of the meal-- it had a very good taste.

The fries were undercooked and meagerly doled out. The Cheerwine did not taste right--like it was diluted or something. There was a mystery smell that kept wafting my way and interfered with my enjoyment.

"Despite a minor hiccup, the cheeseburger flavor really stood out. There's potential here for a great local spot!"

— A Recent Visitor, highlighting a positive aspect
