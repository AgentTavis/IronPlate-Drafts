Our Story

More Than Just a Coffee Shop

At Penland School Coffee Shop, we believe in the simple pleasures: a perfectly brewed cup, a freshly baked pastry, and a moment of calm amidst the creative energy of Bakersville. Known for its warm atmosphere and friendly service, our cafe is a cherished gathering spot for students, instructors, and visitors of the renowned Penland School of Crafts, as well as our wonderful local community.

We've crafted a space where every sip and bite feels like a warm embrace. It's the perfect spot to start your day, take a mid-afternoon break, or simply unwind. Our dedication to quality ingredients and heartfelt service ensures that every visit is a memorable one. Come experience the cozy charm that makes us a true local favorite.

"The Penland School Coffee Shop is truly a gem. After hours in the studio, their delicious pastries and a comforting cup of coffee are exactly what I need. It feels like home!"

— A Penland Student
