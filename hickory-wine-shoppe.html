Our Legacy

More Than Just a Glass: A Hickory Tradition.

Nestled in the heart of Hickory, the Hickory Wine Shoppe offers an unparalleled experience for wine connoisseurs and casual enthusiasts alike. More than just a bar and grill, we are a destination where elegance meets comfort. Enjoy our carefully curated selection of fine wines, craft beers, and gourmet bites in a classy atmosphere.

With live music nights and a welcoming outdoor seating area, we're the perfect spot for groups to gather, unwind, and create lasting memories. We take reservations, offer convenient take-out, and even delivery to bring the Hickory Wine Shoppe experience to you.
