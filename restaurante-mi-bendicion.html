Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Blessing.

Welcome to Restaurante Mi Bendicion, a true blessing in the heart of Lenoir, NC! We’re a cozy Bar & Grill where good food, great company, and heartfelt hospitality come together.

'Mi Bendicion' means 'My Blessing,' and that's exactly what we strive to be for our community – a place where everyone feels at home, enjoys delicious meals, and creates lasting memories. From our family to yours, we pour our heart into every dish and every smile.

We believe in simple, honest food made with passion. Come experience the warmth and flavor that makes us a local favorite.

Our Flavors

Simple. Delicious. Unforgettable.

Mi Bendicion Burger

Our signature Angus beef patty, special sauce, melted cheddar, fresh lettuce, tomato, and onion on a toasted brioche bun.

Crispy Chicken Sandwich

Buttermilk fried chicken breast, pickles, and creamy mayo on a soft roll. A classic done right.

Loaded Nachos

A mountain of crispy tortilla chips topped with seasoned ground beef, melted cheese blend, jalapeños, fresh salsa, and a dollop of sour cream.

Street Tacos (2)

Classic Hot Wings

Golden Fries

Sweet Potato Fries
