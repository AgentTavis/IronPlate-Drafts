OUR STORY

More Than Just Pizza: A Wadesboro Tradition

Crafting Community, One Slice at a Time

At Papa Joe's Pizzeria, we believe great food brings people together. We're that pleasant hole in the wall type of place where you can always expect a warm welcome and, of course, some really good pizza. We've poured our hearts into creating a menu that delights, from our surprisingly delicious thin crust to our hearty sandwiches.

Whether you're passing through Wadesboro on your way to the beach or you're a cherished local, we strive to make every visit special. It's not just about the food; it's about the nice atmosphere and good service that adds the cherry on top. We're proud to be a part of the Wadesboro community and serve up joy, one dish at a time.
