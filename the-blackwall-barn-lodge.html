Our Journey

Experience Comfort and Flavor at The Blackwall Barn & Lodge

Welcome to The Blackwall Barn & Lodge! Nestled in the heart of Gambrills, MD, we are your destination for hearty meals, warm hospitality, and a cozy atmosphere that feels like home. While we are part of the larger Titan Hospitality family, our Gambrills location stands as a unique spot dedicated to serving our local community with exceptional diner-style comfort food and innovative culinary creations.

Our commitment is simple: provide an unforgettable dining experience. Whether you're joining us for a weekday lunch, a vibrant weekend brunch, or a late-night dinner, we strive to make every visit special. We believe in good food, good company, and memories made around the table.
