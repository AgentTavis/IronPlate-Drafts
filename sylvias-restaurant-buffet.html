Our Heritage

Bringing Southern Comfort to Your Table

At Sylvia's Restaurant Buffet in Wilson, NC, we pride ourselves on serving delicious, hearty meals that taste just like home. Sylvia's food is amazing, crafted with love and generations of Southern cooking tradition.

While our Wilson location specializes in convenient take-out, offering generous portions at fair prices, we also welcome guests to enjoy our full dine-in experience at our Statonsburg location. We believe in great food, warm hospitality, and bringing families and friends together over classic Southern comfort. Every dish, from our famous turkey wings to our creamy potato salad, is prepared with passion and a commitment to flavor that keeps our community coming back for more. Come taste the tradition!
