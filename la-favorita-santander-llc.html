Our Story

Taste the Heart of Mexico, Right Here in Troy, NC

At La Favorita Santander, LLC, we believe that great food brings people together. Our kitchen is a hub of vibrant flavors, where traditional Mexican recipes are crafted with passion and fresh ingredients. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a part of the Troy community, dedicated to serving authentic dishes that transport you straight to Mexico.

Your trust is our priority, so businesses can't pay to alter or remove their reviews. Learn more about reviews. This commitment to transparency is just one way we ensure an honest and genuine experience for every guest who walks through our doors.

From sizzling fajitas to savory street tacos, every plate tells a story of heritage and culinary delight. We invite you to experience the warmth of our hospitality and the unforgettable taste of true Mexican cuisine.
