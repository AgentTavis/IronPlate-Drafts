Our Legacy

Crafting Joy, One Healthy Bite at a Time

Welcome to Rose & Rae's Cafe, Burlington's cherished diner where health meets hearty flavor! Born from a passion to provide delicious, worry-free dining experiences, we've built a kitchen that is 100% certified gluten-free. For too long, those with dietary needs have compromised on taste or safety. We believe everyone deserves to enjoy classic diner comforts, freshly baked treats, and satisfying meals without a second thought.

Every dish, from our fluffy pancakes to our savory lunch specials, is prepared with the utmost care, using only the finest gluten-free ingredients. Our dedicated Sugar-Free Bakery is a haven for those with a sweet tooth, offering delightful desserts that are as guilt-free as they are delicious. Rose & Rae's isn't just a cafe; it's a community hub where friends and family gather, knowing they're in safe, flavorful hands. We're proud to be a local favorite, serving up smiles and wholesome goodness since day one.

“Finding a truly dedicated gluten-free kitchen that also offers sugar-free desserts is a dream come true! Rose & Rae's has become my go-to for everything from breakfast to a special treat. Their commitment to quality and taste is unmatched.”
