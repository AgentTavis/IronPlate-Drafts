Our Story

More Than Just a Bar, It's Our Home.

Nestled in the heart of Southport, NC, Southport Tap and Cellar is more than just a bar and grill – it's a vibrant community hub where friends gather, stories are shared, and every meal is a celebration. We pride ourselves on offering a curated selection of craft beers, fine wines, and spirits, perfectly paired with our delicious, elevated bar and grill fare.

From our juicy signature burgers to our fresh local seafood specials, every dish is crafted with passion and the freshest ingredients. Come on in, grab a seat, and discover why we're a local favorite.
