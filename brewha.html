Our Roots

More Than Just a Bar, It's a Community

At Brewha, we're dedicated to being the heart of Wilmington's neighborhood. We started with a simple vision: to create a welcoming space where families, friends, and neighbors could gather, share laughter, and enjoy quality food and drinks in a relaxed atmosphere. While every journey has its challenges, our commitment to providing a family-friendly experience and being a true local hub has never wavered.

We believe in good times, great company, and the simple pleasure of a perfectly poured pint or a delicious meal. Our team works hard to ensure every visit feels like coming home, a place where memories are made and local connections thrive. We're continuously evolving to bring you the best, listening to our community, and striving to be your first choice for a casual, friendly outing.
