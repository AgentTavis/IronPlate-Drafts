Our Story

Taste the Soul of the Caribbean in Every Bite.

At Troy's Caribbean Kitchen, we believe in bringing the vibrant, authentic flavors of the islands directly to Spring Lake. Born from a passion for traditional Caribbean and Soul Food cooking, our kitchen is a place where rich spices, fresh ingredients, and time-honored recipes come together to create an unforgettable dining experience.

We're dedicated to serving dishes that truly reflect the heart and soul of Caribbean culture. Every meal is a journey, prepared with care and seasoned to perfection, ensuring that you taste the genuine essence of our heritage in every plate. Come join us and discover why our community keeps coming back for a taste of paradise!

Our Menu

Savor Our Island-Inspired Creations

Jerk Chicken

Our signature dish! Juicy chicken marinated in authentic jerk spices, slow-cooked to perfection.

Curry Goat

Tender goat meat simmered in a rich, aromatic curry sauce, a true taste of the Caribbean.

Oxtail Stew

Fall-off-the-bone oxtail braised in a savory, hearty gravy with root vegetables.

Rice & Peas

A Caribbean staple: fluffy rice cooked with kidney beans and coconut milk.

Fried Plantains

Sweet ripe plantains, perfectly fried to a golden caramelization.

Mac & Cheese

Creamy, baked macaroni and cheese, a comforting soul food classic.

Why Choose Us

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience

Authentic Island Flavors

We pride ourselves on using traditional Caribbean spices and cooking methods to bring you genuinely authentic dishes, just like you'd find on the islands. No compromises, just pure flavor.

Welcoming & Family-Friendly

Our bar and grill offers a moderate noise level and a warm atmosphere, making it perfect for families, friends, and solo diners alike. Good for kids and good for the soul!

Convenient & Diverse Options

From hassle-free take-out to accepting reservations, we make dining easy. Plus, with many vegetarian options available and all major credit cards accepted, we cater to everyone.
