Our Journey

Our Passion for Flavor, Rooted in Taylorsville.

At Apple City Taphouse, we believe in crafting more than just meals – we create experiences. Nestled right in the heart of Taylorsville, NC, our neighborhood pub & eatery is built on a foundation of quality, community, and an unwavering love for good food and great beer.

We're passionate about bringing you the best: premium smoked meats, crafted with care and perfected over hours. Our dedication extends to every homemade dessert and signature item, ensuring a unique and delicious journey with every visit.

We're proud to be the only Truecue.org certified BBQ joint in the Foothills Region, a testament to our commitment to authentic, mouth-watering BBQ. Paired with our extensive selection of 24 beers on tap, including local craft favorites, Apple City Taphouse is where tradition meets taste, and every guest feels like family.
