Our Roots

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community

At Apple City Taphouse, we believe in bringing people together over exceptional food and the finest local brews. Nestled in the heart of Taylorsville, NC, we've poured our passion into creating a place where premium smoked meats, craft sides, and homemade desserts meet a vibrant selection of 24 rotating beers on tap.

We're proud to be the only Truecue.org certified BBQ joint in the Foothills Region, a testament to our commitment to authentic, quality barbecue. Every dish is crafted with care, every beer is selected to delight, and every guest is treated like family. Come experience the taste of Taylorsville's favorite neighborhood pub & eatery.
