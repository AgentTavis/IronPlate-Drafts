Our Heritage

Our Roots Run Deep in the Crystal Coast.

The Banks Grill is more than just a restaurant; it's a cherished local spot where the vibrant spirit of Morehead City's Crystal Coast comes to life. We pride ourselves on being a fun, laid-back southern coastal diner, serving up some of the finest Breakfast, Brunch, and American comfort food, alongside fresh, delicious Seafood.

From savory morning delights that kickstart your day to hearty lunch favorites that satisfy every craving, every dish is crafted with a touch of southern charm. We invite you to 'run aground' with us, savor a truly memorable meal, and experience the genuine hospitality that makes The Banks Grill a beloved community staple.

Our Promise

Why Guests Keep Coming Back to The Banks Grill

Unforgettable Breakfast & Brunch

Start your day the Crystal Coast way! Our legendary French Toast and hearty Banks Omelette are just a taste of why our breakfast and brunch are local favorites. Fuel your adventures with fresh, delicious morning meals.

Authentic Southern Coastal Seafood

Experience the true flavors of the Carolina coast with our fresh, expertly prepared seafood. From our indulgent Crab Cake Eggs Benedict to the ever-popular Shrimp Burger, we bring the best of the ocean straight to your plate.

A Welcoming & Laid-Back Vibe

More than just great food, The Banks Grill offers a warm, friendly atmosphere where everyone feels at home. "Come run aground" with us and enjoy genuine southern hospitality in a diner setting that feels like a classic coastal escape.
