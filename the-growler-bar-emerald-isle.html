Our Journey

More Than Just a Bar, It's Family.

Welcome to The Growler Bar Emerald Isle, where good times, great drinks, and genuine connections come together. Since opening our doors, we've strived to create a space that feels like a second home for locals and visitors alike. We believe in the simple joy of sharing a perfect pint with friends, discovering a new favorite wine, or raising a glass of sparkling prosecco.

We're proud to offer a rotating selection from our 16 taps of craft beer and wine, ensuring there's always something fresh and exciting to explore. Our atmosphere is intentionally relaxed and welcoming, a place where conversations flow as freely as the beverages. You might even catch a glimpse of our beloved resident dog, RC, enjoying the lively ambiance – a testament to our truly friendly and laid-back vibe.

"The Growler Bar is truly a gem on Emerald Isle! The atmosphere is amazing, the staff feels like family, and there's always a fantastic new beer on tap to try. Plus, their resident dog, RC, is the sweetest greeter!"
