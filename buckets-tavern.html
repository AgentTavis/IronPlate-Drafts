Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community.

At Buckets Tavern, we believe in the simple joys of life: good food, cold drinks, and great company. Nestled in the heart of Eden, NC, we've poured our passion into creating a welcoming space where neighbors become friends and every visit feels like coming home. We're proud to be Eden's favorite local hangout, a place where memories are made over hearty meals and clinking glasses.

From our daily $9.99 Burger Special to our refreshing cold drinks, we're dedicated to serving up quality and comfort. We're not just a bar and grill; we're a hub where the community gathers, whether it's for a quick lunch, a relaxed evening with friends, or to celebrate special occasions. Come experience the warmth and hospitality that makes Buckets Tavern truly special.
