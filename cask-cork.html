Our Story

Crafting Community, One Glass at a Time.

At Cask & Cork, we believe in more than just serving drinks and delicious food. We're dedicated to creating a vibrant community hub right here in Indian Trail, NC. Our vision is simple: to be the go-to spot where friends gather, memories are made, and every visit feels like coming home.

Specializing in private parties and events, we provide the perfect backdrop for your celebrations, big or small. From intimate gatherings to lively festivities, our space is designed to host your most cherished moments. Step into a full bar experience with a meticulously curated selection of wines and an exclusive range of cigars to enjoy in a relaxed setting.
