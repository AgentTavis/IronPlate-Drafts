Our Heritage

Bringing the Heart of Mexico to Lumberton

Mi Casita Restaurante Mexicano, fondly known as La Caseta, is more than just a place to eat; it's a vibrant gathering spot in the heart of Lumberton, NC. We specialize in serving authentic Mexican cuisine in a warm and inviting atmosphere, where every dish tells a story of tradition and passion.

Our journey began with a simple dream: to share the rich, bold flavors of true Mexican street food culture with our community. From our sizzling Carne Asada to our perfectly crafted Quesadillas, each recipe is a tribute to the culinary traditions passed down through generations. We believe in creating a cozy eatery where families and friends can come together, share laughter, and make cherished memories over plates of delicious, handcrafted food.
