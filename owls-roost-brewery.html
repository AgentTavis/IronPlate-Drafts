OUR STORY

More Than Just a Brewpub, It's a Franklinton Tradition.

From a historic movie theater to Franklinton's favorite brewpub, Owl's Roost Brewery is built on a legacy of community and exceptional taste. We pride ourselves on creating a warm, cordial, and attentive atmosphere where every guest feels like family. Our team delivers generous, timely, and kind service, ensuring your visit is nothing short of perfect.

Experience authentic North Carolina barbecued and smoked dishes, alongside our signature craft beers, delicious pizzas, and a selection of ciders, wines, and liquors. Our venue is fun, creative, colorful, and boasts great acoustics, making it the ideal spot for any gathering, from a casual evening out to a lively family celebration.
