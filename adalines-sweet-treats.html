Our Journey

Sweet Beginnings, Family Traditions.

Welcome to Adaline's Sweet Treats, a beloved family-owned bakery nestled in the heart of North Wilkesboro on Main Street. Our passion is creating delectable experiences that bring joy to every customer.

The selection you'll find here is truly excellent — so many beautiful and tempting treats to choose from, it's honestly hard to decide. Every single item is thoughtfully made and visually stunning; often, our cupcakes are almost too pretty to eat!

But the beauty isn't just skin deep. The flavors are just as impressive as the presentation — fresh, perfectly balanced, and clearly made with high-quality, hand-picked ingredients. We believe that every bite should be a moment of pure bliss, crafted with care and a touch of our family's secret recipes. Come in and discover your new favorite treat!

"Adaline's is more than just a bakery; it's a piece of art! Every cupcake is a masterpiece, and the flavors are simply out of this world. You can truly taste the love in every bite!"

— A Happy Customer

Our Delicious Offerings

Indulge in Handcrafted Delights.

Gourmet Cupcakes

An exquisite selection of visually stunning cupcakes, baked fresh daily with unique flavor combinations and decadent frostings. From classic vanilla bean to seasonal specials, each one is a miniature work of art.

Artisan Cookies

Hand-decorated and baked to perfection, our artisan cookies come in a variety of textures and flavors. Perfect for a quick treat or a thoughtful gift.

Decadent Cakes

Celebration-ready cakes, crafted with rich flavors and elegant designs. Ideal for birthdays, anniversaries, or any special occasion needing a touch of sweetness.

Specialty Milkshakes

Creamy, dreamy milkshakes made with premium ice cream and a variety of delicious mix-ins. Don't forget our classic Malt Milkshake!

Fresh Pastries

Start your day sweet with our selection of flaky croissants, Danish pastries, and more, all baked fresh every morning.

Seasonal Pies

Enjoy the taste of the seasons with our homemade pies, featuring fresh fruits and buttery, flaky crusts. Available for a limited time.
