Our Story

From a Lucky Find to Bryson City's Favorite Bite.

What started as a happy accident for one traveler quickly became an obsession – and that's the magic we aim for every day at The Rice Wagon. Nestled on a charming side street in Downtown Bryson City, we're a local food truck serving up incredible island-inspired flavors that keep folks coming back for more.

We believe great food creates great memories, and we pour that passion into every dish, from our perfectly seared Ahi Tuna to our legendary Hurricane Chicken. Come discover your new obsession and taste why we're a beloved gem in Bryson City!
