Our Journey

More Than Just a Bar & Grill

From the moment you step into Tavern 74, you're not just a customer – you're family. What started as a vision to create a lively, welcoming space in Monroe, NC, has blossomed into a local hotspot where great food, amazing drinks, and unforgettable conversations flow freely.

We pride ourselves on being the perfect spot for any occasion, whether it's a casual weeknight dinner, a celebratory evening with friends, or a fun-filled karaoke night. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure every visit is packed with good times and delicious memories.
