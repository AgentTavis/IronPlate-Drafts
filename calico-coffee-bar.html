Our Journey

More Than Just Coffee, It's a Community.

Welcome to Calico Coffee Bar, Wilmington's cherished spot for exceptional coffee and heartfelt community. We believe in crafting delightful experiences, from your morning brew to a sweet afternoon escape. More than just a coffee shop, we're a local hub where good company and quality ingredients come together, always served with a spirit of warmth and welcome.

Our passion lies in creating a space where everyone feels at home, whether you're catching up with friends, diving into a good book, or simply enjoying a moment of peace. We're proud to be a faith-based establishment, dedicated to serving our neighborhood with kindness and delicious offerings.
