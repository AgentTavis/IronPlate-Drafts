More Than Just a Meal: The Hiker's Kitchen Story

Hiker's Kitchen isn't just a bar and grill; it's a local hub where the spirit of the trail meets hearty comfort food right here in Franklin, NC. We take pride in offering a welcoming atmosphere where you can unwind after a long day or gather with friends.

Our highly-regarded food truck often brings our delicious creations directly to local events, and at our Main Street location, you can savor delights like our praised veggie hummus wrap, all while enjoying a refreshing cold pint in our comfortable outdoor seating area.

We believe in good food, good company, and great experiences – the perfect blend for any adventurer or local looking for a culinary escape.
