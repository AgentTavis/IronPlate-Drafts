Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal: The Hiker's Kitchen Story

Welcome to Hiker's Kitchen, your cozy bar and grill nestled in the heart of Franklin, NC. We started with a simple vision: to create a welcoming spot where locals and adventurers alike could unwind, enjoy delicious food, and share stories. What began as a passionate endeavor has grown into a beloved community staple.

Our journey even includes a fantastic Hiker's Kitchen food truck, bringing our unique flavors on the road. We believe in serving food that comforts and delights, like our popular Veggie Hummus Wrap, perfectly enjoyed with a cold pint in our outdoor seating area. Every dish is crafted with care, reflecting our love for good food and great company.

"Hiker's Kitchen is my go-to after hitting the trails. Their food always hits the spot, and the atmosphere is exactly what you need to relax."
