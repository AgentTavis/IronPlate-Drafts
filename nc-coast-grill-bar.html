Our Story

The Heart of Waterfront Dining in Duck, NC

Nestled on the serene waterfront of Duck, NC, NC Coast Grill & Bar is more than just a restaurant; it's an experience. We set out to create a dining destination where the beauty of the Outer Banks meets an elevated culinary journey. Our open kitchen invites you to witness the passion behind every dish, while our upscale decor provides a sophisticated yet comfortable ambiance.

We pride ourselves on a cross-cultural, Chef-inspired menu that tantalizes the taste buds, whether you're joining us for a leisurely lunch or an intimate dinner. From our fresh local seafood to innovative interpretations of classic dishes, every plate is crafted with care and a commitment to flavor. With ample outdoor seating, you can savor your meal with breathtaking views, making every visit to NC Coast Grill & Bar a memorable occasion.
