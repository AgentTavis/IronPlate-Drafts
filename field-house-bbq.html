OUR STORY

Crafting Carolina's Best BBQ, One Plate at a Time

With a menu boasting a delectable array of smoked meats, including tender brisket, juicy pulled pork, and flavorful ribs, Fieldhouse BBQ showcases the artistry of traditional Southern smoking techniques.

Nestled in the heart of Kannapolis, NC, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a community staple built on a passion for authentic, slow-smoked barbecue. Every cut of meat is hand-selected, rubbed with our secret spice blend, and smoked low and slow for hours over hickory and oak. This dedication ensures that every bite delivers a taste of Carolina tradition and a flavor that keeps you coming back for more.

We believe great BBQ brings people together, and our doors are always open for you to share in our delicious journey. Come experience the Field House difference!

OUR FLAVORS

Our Smoked Specialties

Tender Brisket

Slow-smoked to perfection, our brisket boasts a beautiful bark and melt-in-your-mouth tenderness. Hand-rubbed with our signature spice blend and smoked for hours.

Juicy Pulled Pork

Classic Carolina pulled pork, smoked until fall-apart tender, then hand-shredded and tossed in its own flavorful juices. Perfect on a bun or by the plate.

Flavorful Ribs

Our ribs are seasoned generously and slow-smoked for that perfect balance of smoky flavor and juicy meat that effortlessly falls off the bone. A true BBQ staple.
