Our Story

Generations of Flavor, Smoked to Perfection in Kannapolis

With a menu boasting a delectable array of patiently smoked meats, including our renowned tender brisket, juicy pulled pork, and fall-off-the-bone flavorful ribs, Fieldhouse BBQ showcases the artistry and passion behind true Southern barbecue.

Every dish is a testament to time-honored traditions, meticulous preparation, and a commitment to flavor that keeps our community coming back for more. We believe in slow-cooked goodness, rich smoky aromas, and generous portions that satisfy the soul. Come experience the warmth and taste that makes Fieldhouse BBQ a cherished part of Kannapolis.
