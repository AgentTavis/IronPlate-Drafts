Our Story

Generations of Flavor, Right Here in Havelock.

For years, Havelock Cafe has been a cherished local gem, serving up comforting breakfast and lunch dishes that taste just like home. We're proud to be recognized among the best cafes on the North Carolina Coast by Tripadvisor travelers, a testament to our commitment to delicious food, friendly service, and a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Step inside and discover why 299+ locals and visitors have given us a fantastic 4.3-star rating! We believe in hearty portions, honest ingredients, and the kind of welcoming smile that makes you feel like family. Come create new memories with us.
