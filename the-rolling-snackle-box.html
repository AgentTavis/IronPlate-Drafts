Our Journey

From a Humble Shack to Tate's Pizza Haven

We like to try out new places near and around Jasper due to limited options in a small town. The Rolling Snackle Box popped up as new so we went for lunch. It's a compact little "shack" just as the name implies, definitely more designed for takeout than sit-in unless the weather is nice outside and you can use the outdoor seating.

On one particular day, the weather outside was not great, so my wife, son, and I occupied the single table inside for an early lunch. As we went to go order, there were quite a few delicious options to choose from! We believe good food brings people together, and our mission is to deliver authentic, delicious pizza with a friendly, local touch. Even with our cozy setup, the flavor always comes first.

"The Snackle Box might be small, but their pizzas pack a huge punch! Always fresh, always delicious, and the staff makes you feel right at home. A true hidden gem in Tate."

— A Happy Local
