Our Roots

More Than Just Coffee & Ice Cream

Heading toward adventure, Mrs. K. interrupted the tunes on the radio to declare (half playful, half serious) that she wanted a coffee soonish. A quick check of the map showed Nana's Coffee was right on our route, so it became an easy and welcome stop.

The storefront is small and unassuming, tucked into a quiet stretch of road, but inside it opens up into a cozy space that doubles as both ice cream parlor and coffee shop. Here at Nana's, we believe in crafting moments of comfort and joy, whether you're starting your day with a perfectly brewed cup, unwinding with a sweet treat, or catching up with friends. There are plenty of tables, and we pride ourselves on being a community hub where everyone feels perfectly at home.
