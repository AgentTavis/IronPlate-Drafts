Our Story

Your Local Oasis for Amazing Coffee & Fresh Baked Delights

If you're looking for a quick, good quality coffee spot off of I95 in Rocky Mount, Morning Addiction is your perfect stop. After disappointing hotel coffee, many travelers find solace in our cozy local cafe, where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee welcomes you. We pride ourselves on creating a quaint little spot that feels like home, filled with friendly faces and genuine smiles.

At Morning Addiction Coffeehouse, you'll discover an amazing coffee selection paired with delightful fresh baked goods. Our cafe is more than just a coffee shop; it's a community hub where locals gather to enjoy their morning brew, catch up with friends, or simply unwind. Come experience the charm and quality that makes us Rocky Mount's favorite.
