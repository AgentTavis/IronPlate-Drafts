Our Legacy

More Than Just a Pub, It's Home.

Nestled in the heart of Harrisburg, Pub 49 has been a local cornerstone for years, known for its laid-back atmosphere and a menu that hits all the right notes for a true bar and grill experience.

We believe in simple pleasures: hearty food, a wide selection of drinks, and a place where friends and family can gather without pretense. Our vibe is decidedly casual and divey in the best possible way—think comfortable seating, lively chatter, and friendly faces behind the bar.

Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunch, catching the game with friends, or enjoying our popular happy hour specials, Pub 49 is the spot where good times are always on the menu. We’re proud to be a gathering place where everyone feels like family.
