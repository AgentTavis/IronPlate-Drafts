Our Heritage

A Taste of Engelhard's Heart & Soul

Nestled in the charming community of Engelhard, Big Trout Marina isn't just a diner – it's a local institution. We believe in serving up honest, hearty meals, just like Grandma used to make. From the freshest catches of the day to classic comfort sides, every dish is prepared with love and a commitment to flavor that keeps our community coming back.

We pride ourselves on being that friendly, familiar spot where conversations flow as easily as the coffee. Come in and experience the warmth, the genuine hospitality, and the unbeatable taste of true Southern comfort food, right here at Big Trout Marina.
