Our Story

Discover Salisbury's Best Kept Secret

Nestled in the heart of Salisbury on E Innes St, Food Freaks Hidden Gems truly lives up to its name. We set out to create more than just a place to eat; we wanted to craft a genuine small-town, home feel where everyone feels like family from the moment they walk through our doors.

Our welcoming staff are dedicated to making your visit special, serving up a menu that offers enough tantalizing options to satisfy any craving without overwhelming you. Whether you’re a local or just passing through, prepare to find your new favorite spot for comfort food and good times.
