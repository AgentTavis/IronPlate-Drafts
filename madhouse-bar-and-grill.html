Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

Welcome to Madhouse Bar and Grill, your premier destination for great food, ice-cold drinks, and an unbeatable atmosphere in Winston-Salem. Located conveniently on the second floor at 3441 Myer Lee Dr, we've built a reputation for excellent service and a friendly staff that treats you like family.

We pride ourselves on being the perfect spot to catch the game with big TVs everywhere, enjoy a relaxed meal, or unwind with friends. While we're closed on Mondays, Tuesdays, and Wednesdays, we're ready to serve you from 11 AM on Thursdays, Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays. Come on down and see why we're a local favorite!
