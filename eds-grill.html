Our Heritage

A Taste of Small-Town Charm, Served Fresh Daily.

Stopped here several years ago and loved the small town grill atmosphere and of course the food. Took the opportunity to stop the other day and fortunately nothing has changed, except they expanded the seating. At Ed's Grill, we pride ourselves on serving up delicious, classic American fare in a warm, friendly environment. Whether you're craving a juicy cheeseburger, an all-the-way hot dog, or crispy fries, you'll find your favorites prepared with care and served with a smile. Our commitment to friendly, quick service and that authentic small-town charm has made us a local institution for years.

We've grown, but our heart remains true to the simple pleasures: great food, good company, and a welcoming atmosphere that feels just like home. Come on down and experience why Ed's Grill is a beloved Whiteville tradition.
