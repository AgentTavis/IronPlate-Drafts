Our Story

More Than Just a Bar, It's Our Home Away From Home.

Welcome to Folly's Draft & Snack, Kernersville's cherished spot for laid-back evenings and lively gatherings. For us, Folly's isn't just a business; it's a passion, a place where friends and family can unwind, catch up, and create lasting memories.

We pride ourselves on cultivating a wonderfully relaxed environment, perfect for an occasional Saturday night out, or any night you need to escape. You'll find a lively buzz with generally great live bands (and TouchTunes kicking in when they pack up!), the aroma of fresh popcorn, an extensive selection of draft beers, and plenty of TVs to catch the game.

While many bars offer similar amenities, we believe our secret ingredient is our incredible team. Personalities like Tiffany, our beloved bartender on Friday/Saturday nights, make all the difference. She ensures every guest feels genuinely welcomed and cared for, turning a good night into an unforgettable one. It's this unique blend of welcoming atmosphere, quality offerings, and heartfelt service that truly makes Folly's stand out.

"Folly's isn't just another bar; it's our favorite escape. The laid-back vibe, live music, and fresh popcorn are great, but it's the warm welcome from bartenders like Tiffany that truly makes it special. It feels like coming home."

- A Local Regular, Kernersville
