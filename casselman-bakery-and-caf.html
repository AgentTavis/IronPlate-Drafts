Our Heritage

A Taste of Home, Decades in the Making

Casselman Bakery and Café has been a beloved fixture in Oakland, NC, serving up classic diner fare with a touch of homemade goodness. We believe in comfort food that reminds you of simpler times, made with care and a warm smile.

From our hearty breakfast specials to our satisfying lunch options, every dish is prepared to order, ensuring freshness and flavor in every bite. We're more than just a place to eat; we're a gathering spot, a community hub where neighbors become friends over a cup of coffee and a delicious meal.

We're proud to be family-owned and operated, continuing a tradition of hospitality that makes every guest feel right at home. Come on in, pull up a chair, and experience the warm, friendly atmosphere that makes Casselman Bakery and Café truly special.

Our Offerings

Classic Comfort, Freshly Made

Classic Breakfast Plate

Two farm-fresh eggs cooked to your liking, crispy bacon or sausage, golden hash browns, and your choice of toast.

Homestyle Pancakes

Fluffy stack of three pancakes, served with warm maple syrup and butter. Add fresh berries for a sweet touch.

The Casselman Burger

Juicy beef patty, melted cheddar, crisp lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles on a toasted bun. Served with fries.

Reuben Sandwich

Thinly sliced corned beef, sauerkraut, Swiss cheese, and creamy Russian dressing grilled on rye bread.

Daily Soup Special

Ask your server about today's freshly prepared, comforting soup. Perfect for a light lunch or appetizer.

Fresh Baked Pie Slice

A rotating selection of our signature homemade pies, baked fresh daily. The perfect sweet ending to any meal.

Grilled Cheese & Tomato Soup

A timeless classic! Gooey grilled cheese sandwich paired with a warm bowl of creamy tomato soup.

Coffee & Fresh Brew

Start your day right or enjoy a mid-day pick-me-up with our freshly brewed coffee, available all day.
