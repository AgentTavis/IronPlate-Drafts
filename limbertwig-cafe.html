OUR STORY

Morganton's Go-To for Fresh, Flavorful Lunches

Nestled in the heart of Morganton, Limbertwig Cafe has been a local gem, delighting palates with our commitment to quality and freshness. We're proud to be ranked #7 of 85 restaurants in Morganton on Tripadvisor, a testament to the love and care we put into every dish, reflected in our 4.6-star rating from 236 reviews.

Our passion lies in crafting custom gourmet sandwiches and wraps, made to order with the freshest ingredients. From the moment you step in, you'll feel the warm, welcoming atmosphere of a true neighborhood diner. We believe good food brings people together, and our diverse menu, from hearty sandwiches to freshly brewed coffee and tempting baked goods, is designed to offer something special for everyone.

OUR MENU

Crafted Fresh, Just For You

Explore our array of fresh sandwiches, wraps, and delightful beverages, all made to order with care.

The Classic Deli Sandwich

A timeless favorite with oven-roasted turkey, provolone cheese, crisp lettuce, fresh tomato, and a touch of mayo on artisan bread.

The Spicy Southwest Sandwich

Grilled chicken, zesty pepper jack cheese, ripe avocado, and a kick of chipotle mayo, perfectly pressed on a hearty bun.

Veggie Delight Wrap

A fresh and vibrant wrap packed with creamy hummus, crisp cucumber, spinach, bell peppers, and a light vinaigrette in a whole wheat tortilla.

Mediterranean Chicken Wrap

Flavorful grilled chicken, crumbled feta, Kalamata olives, red onion, and a refreshing tzatziki sauce, all in a soft wrap.

Limbertwig Signature Blend Coffee

Our freshly brewed, bold, and smooth house blend, perfect for a midday pick-me-up. Available hot or iced.

Daily Fresh-Baked Cookies

Indulge in our rotating selection of delicious, warm cookies, baked fresh every morning. Ask about today's special!
