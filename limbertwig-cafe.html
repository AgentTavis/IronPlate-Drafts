Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Morganton Tradition.

Nestled in the heart of Morganton, Limbertwig Cafe has been serving up smiles and spectacular food since day one. We believe in crafting every dish with fresh ingredients and a dash of genuine Southern hospitality.

From our humble beginnings, we've grown into a beloved local diner, known for an array of fresh sandwiches, incredible wraps, and a variety of freshly brewed coffee that gets your day started right. We're proud to be a place where the community gathers for great food and good times.

Our Delicious Offerings

Savor Our Signature Sandwiches & More

The Limbertwig Club

A classic triple-decker with smoked turkey, crispy bacon, fresh lettuce, juicy tomato, and mayo on toasted sourdough.

Spicy Southwest Wrap

Grilled chicken, black beans, corn, avocado, pepper jack cheese, and a zesty chipotle ranch, all wrapped in a warm tortilla.

Mediterranean Veggie

Hummus, cucumber, bell peppers, Kalamata olives, feta, spinach, and a lemon-herb vinaigrette in a whole wheat wrap.

The Morganton Melt

Thinly sliced roast beef piled high with caramelized onions, Swiss cheese, and horseradish aioli on grilled rye bread.

Daily Brew Coffee

Signature Brownie
