Our Roots

A High Point Legacy of Flavor & Family

Welcome to Our Family Kitchen!

Becky's & Mary's Restaurant is more than just a diner; it's a cherished High Point institution serving up authentic American cuisine with a soul food twist. From hearty breakfasts to satisfying lunches and dinners, we pride ourselves on offering a warm, welcoming atmosphere where every meal feels like home.

We're known for our commitment to quality, generous portions, and a flavor that keeps our community coming back for more, making us the undisputed best soul food restaurant of 2025.
