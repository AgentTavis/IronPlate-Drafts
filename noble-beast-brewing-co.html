Our Heritage

Crafted Brews & Timeless Tales.

At Noble Beast Brewing Co., we're more than just a bar and grill – we're a living canvas of Cleveland's history. Our story is woven into the very fabric of our establishment, anchored by a truly unique piece of local art.

Step inside and discover our vintage beer ad mural, a hand-painted masterpiece by Old Soul Sign Co. This mural isn't just decoration; it's a heartfelt homage to a now-covered Gold Bond advertisement from the mid-twentieth century, once a familiar sight from the Shoreway. We've adapted this iconic image onto our Bella+Canvas sponge fleece hoodies, bringing a piece of Cleveland's past into the present.

We believe in celebrating heritage, fostering community, and pouring quality into every glass and plate. Come experience the rich history and vibrant atmosphere that defines Noble Beast Brewing Co.

"Every visit feels like stepping into a piece of local history. The mural is stunning, and the vibe is unmatched. A true gem in Cleveland!"

— A Loyal Local
