Our Story

Generations of Southern Hospitality and Flavor

Welcome to Annabellas, a true Southern gem nestled in the heart of Dunn, NC. For decades, we've been serving up hearty meals and warm smiles, becoming a cherished gathering place for families and friends.

Our kitchen is a testament to timeless Southern traditions, where every dish is prepared with passion and the freshest local ingredients. We believe that good food brings people together, and our mission is to create memorable dining experiences that feel just like coming home.

From our classic Country Fried Steak to our rich, homemade Mac & Cheese, Annabellas is where comfort food meets community. We're proud to be a part of Dunn's vibrant spirit, offering a casual, family-friendly atmosphere where everyone is welcome.
