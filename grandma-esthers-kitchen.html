Our Culinary Creations

A Taste of Italy, With a Touch of Esther's Magic

Homemade Sourdough Pizza

Crafted with our signature house-made sourdough crust, topped with fresh, authentic ingredients and baked to perfection. A Shepherd favorite!

Squid Ink Pasta

An exquisite, rich pasta dish featuring homemade squid ink pasta, tossed in a savory sauce with fresh seafood and aromatic herbs.

Colby Jack Pub Burger

This fan favorite makes every bite feel like a celebration. A juicy burger topped with Colby Jack cheese, served with crispy fries. (A delightful surprise for an Italian kitchen!)

Classic Lasagna

Layers of rich pasta, savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheeses baked to a golden perfection. A true comfort classic.

Chicken Parmigiana

Tender chicken breast, breaded and fried, topped with marinara sauce and mozzarella, served with a side of spaghetti.

Tiramisu

Our delightful homemade Tiramisu, a classic Italian dessert with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cheese, and cocoa.
