Our Story

Generations of Island Hospitality

Nestled on the charming shores of Ocracoke, Pony Island Restaurant isn't just a place to eat—it's a tradition. As the island's oldest eatery, we've been serving up heartwarming Southern breakfasts and the freshest local seafood for decades. We believe in keeping things simple and perfect, letting the natural flavors shine through in every dish.

Our kitchen prides itself on quality ingredients, carefully prepared, ensuring your meal is just what you're craving. You'll find our atmosphere genuinely casual and neighborly, a true reflection of island life. We're a place where service is always friendly, efficient, and attentive, making every visit feel like coming home.

"Definitely recommend this joint for breakfast! The service is friendly, efficient, and attentive. Food is very, very good; not overly done. Sometimes cooks try too hard and end up messing up a good thing, but not here and not today. Husband had the taco omelette and I had a sausage, egg and cheese sandwich with a side of cheese grits. Very pleased!" — A Happy Customer
