Our Story

More Than Just Coffee, It's a Community.

At Agapé Coffee + Handcrafted Doughnuts, we believe in crafting joy, one cup and one doughnut at a time. Nestled in the heart of Plymouth, NC, we’re more than just a place to grab a quick bite; we're a community hub where every visit feels like coming home.

We pour our passion into every batch of our unique, handcrafted doughnuts and every perfectly brewed coffee, ensuring a delightful experience that brightens your day. Come taste the difference made with love and dedication, and become a part of our growing family here in Plymouth.
