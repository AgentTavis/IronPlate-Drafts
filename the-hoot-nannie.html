Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Forest City Tradition.

At The Hoot Nannie, we believe in crafting more than just delicious food and drinks; we create an experience. Located in the heart of Forest City, we’ve built a reputation for our inviting atmosphere and the friendly faces you'll meet every time you walk through our doors.

Our kitchen, guided by the talented Steve, is always on point, serving up menu items known for their generous, satisfying portions. And at the bar, Kevin consistently has it stocked with a good offering of top-shelf spirits, ensuring there's something perfect for every palate.

We're proud to be a local gathering spot where friends and family can come together, enjoy great company, and make lasting memories. Come discover why The Hoot Nannie is a beloved part of the Forest City community.
