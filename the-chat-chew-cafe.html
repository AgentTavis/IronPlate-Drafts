Our Story

More Than Just a Cafe, It's Archdale's Living Room

I love Chat and Chew Cafe! This time I tried a few of their seasonal Lotus drinks. The Scrooge was very sweet but delicious. I also loved the Mistletoe Kiss drink, and was happy that the green apple flavor was not too overpowering. I also got an Asiago bagel with their bacon and chive cream cheese. This was absolutely fantastic and I will be getting it again.

The service is always wonderful, but the wait can be a little long if it there are a lot of people. I never mind this because the food and coffee are always worth it. It’s truly a gem in Archdale, offering a welcoming spot to relax, work, or catch up with friends. From our perfectly brewed coffee to our delectable pastries and savory bagels, every visit feels like a treat. We pride ourselves on creating a cozy atmosphere where community thrives, making every moment you spend with us a delightful escape.
