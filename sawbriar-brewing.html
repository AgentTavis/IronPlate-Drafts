Our Journey

More Than Just a Brewery, It's a Community Hub.

Welcome to Sawbriar Brewing, Jamestown's cherished gathering spot where good times are always on tap. We started with a simple passion: to bring exceptional TN craft beer, authentic wood-fired pizza, and a vibrant social scene to our beloved community.

Every week, our calendar is brimming with exciting events. From lively game nights and creative workshops to high-energy live music every Saturday and unforgettable big game Sundays, there's always something happening here. We believe in creating experiences, fostering connections, and making every visit memorable.

Our commitment extends beyond just great food and drinks. It's about crafting an atmosphere where friends become family, and every moment is savored. Come on in, grab a pint, a slice, and let the good times roll!

"Sawbriar Brewing is my go-to! The atmosphere is electric, especially on Saturday nights with live music. And you absolutely HAVE to try their wood-fired pizza – it's perfection!"

— Jessica L., Regular Patron
