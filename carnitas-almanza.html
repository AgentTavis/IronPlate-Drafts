Our Heritage, Your Table

Generations of Flavor, Crafted with Love

At Carnitas Almanza, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a family tradition brought to the heart of Mint Hill, NC. Our journey began with a passion for authentic Carnitas Estilo Michoacán, a culinary art passed down through generations. Every dish tells a story of heritage, dedication, and the vibrant flavors of Mexico.

We believe in simple, honest food made with the freshest ingredients and time-honored techniques. From our tender, slow-cooked carnitas to handmade tortillas, every bite transports you to the bustling streets of Michoacán. We're proud to be a family-owned business, serving our community with warmth and deliciousness, seven days a week.
