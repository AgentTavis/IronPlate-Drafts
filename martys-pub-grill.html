Our Story

More Than Just a Pub, It's a Statesville Tradition.

At Marty's Pub & Grill, we believe in good food, great company, and even better times. We've been a beloved fixture in Statesville, offering a laid-back atmosphere where friends and family can gather. From our sizzling Half-Pound Burgers to our refreshing daily drink specials, every visit is an experience tailored for fun and flavor.

We're proud to be a place where laughter echoes, stories are shared, and every customer feels like a regular. Whether you're catching a game, challenging a friend to a round of pool, or just unwinding after a long day, Marty's is your go-to spot for quality food, fantastic drinks, and memories made right here in the heart of Statesville.
