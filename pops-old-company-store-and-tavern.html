Our Heritage

More Than Just a Tavern, It's a Catawba Tradition

The Heart of Catawba's History

Step into Pop's Old Company Store and Tavern, a truly unique place where history meets modern comfort right here in Catawba. What was once a bustling company store has been lovingly transformed into a warm and welcoming bar and grill, preserving the charm of its past while offering an inviting spot for today's community.

We're passionate about providing a memorable experience, whether you're joining us for a night out or picking up a quick take-out meal. With our carefully selected range of beers, wines, and signature mixed beverages, there's always something special to discover. Come and be a part of the story!
