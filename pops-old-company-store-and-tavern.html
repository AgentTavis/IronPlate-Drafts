Our Story

Where Memories are Made, One Sip at a Time.

Welcome to Pop's Old Company Store and Tavern, a truly unique place to eat and enjoy right here in Catawba, NC. More than just a bar and grill, Pop's is a cherished local spot where tradition meets good times. We believe in providing a comfortable, welcoming atmosphere where friends and family can gather, unwind, and make lasting memories.

Our doors may be open for limited hours, but those are the hours when the magic happens. We've cultivated a special kind of charm – a blend of old-school hospitality and modern enjoyment, ensuring every visit is a memorable one. While parking in front is limited, we have plenty of additional space behind the building for your convenience.

Why Pop's?

More Than Just a Tavern, It's an Experience.

Exceptional Drink Selection

We pride ourselves on offering a wide array of quality beverages. From our carefully curated selection of local and imported beers to a thoughtful list of wines and expertly crafted mixed beverages, there's always something perfect for your taste.

A Unique & Welcoming Vibe
