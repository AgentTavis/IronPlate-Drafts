Our Heritage

Crafting Memories, One Dish at a Time.

Keepsmiling A'Latte Kesson&Co., LLC is more than just a name; it's a promise of warmth, flavor, and genuine hospitality. We are a beloved local spot in Mt Airy, NC, where every visit feels like coming home. From our expertly grilled dishes to our comforting lattes, we pour our heart into everything we serve.

Our commitment to community and quality ingredients has made us a gathering place for friends, families, and anyone looking for a moment of joy. We believe in the power of good food and great company to brighten your day. Come in, relax, and become part of our story.
