Our Journey

The Heart of Gig Harbor's Craft Scene

Welcome to our family-owned specialty taproom, proudly serving the Gig Harbor and Key Peninsula communities! With 12 rotating taps of craft beer, plus a curated selection of wines, ciders, and seltzers, there's a perfect pour for everyone. And now, we're also serving delicious food!

Bring the whole crew to enjoy our space until 6 PM—we're 21+ after that. Please note that to comply with health regulations, no outside food or beverages are permitted. Our comfortable, welcoming space is perfect for unwinding with friends, enjoying a meal with family, or catching up on your favorite game. Settle in and stay a while!
