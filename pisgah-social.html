Our Legacy

From Fish Camp to Feast: The Pisgah Social Journey

Come eat with us at the new Pisgah Social! What started as a vision to transform the beloved old fish camp has become a vibrant reality. Our brick and mortar is currently under construction, but we're already serving up deliciousness twice a week, bringing the community together one plate at a time.

JT, our visionary owner, has masterfully transformed this space to become the proud home of Smash NC, delivering THE best smashburgers you'll ever taste, and Cheef Eats BBQ, offering mouth-watering, authentic barbecue. Pisgah Social is more than just a place to eat; it's a true community effort, bringing incredible flavors and good times to Pisgah Forest.
