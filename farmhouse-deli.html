Our Journey

The Heart & Soul of Farmhouse Deli

At Farmhouse Deli, we believe in the simple joy of a perfectly prepared meal. We craft made-to-order breakfasts, hearty deli sandwiches, and fresh plates using the finest ingredients, ensuring every bite is delicious and satisfying.

Our passion for good food extends to creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where friends and family can gather. Whether you're starting your day with our famous breakfast club or enjoying a leisurely lunch, you'll find quality and care in everything we do.

And for those looking for a little something extra, don't miss our charming small boutique in the back, featuring delightful candles, wax melts, unique candy, and a small clothing selection. It's our way of adding a touch of home to your dining experience.
