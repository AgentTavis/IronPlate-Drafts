OUR HERITAGE

Where Warm Plates & Welcoming Faces Meet.

At Lil's Country Grill, we believe good food brings people together. Nestled in the heart of Kings Mountain, NC, we've been proudly serving our community with classic Southern comfort and a smile for over 15 years.

From our crack-of-dawn breakfast plates featuring those famous homemade biscuits and creamy grits, to a quick, satisfying lunch that never compromises on flavor, every dish is prepared with the warmth and care you'd expect from your own kitchen. We cherish the tradition of good, honest cooking.

We're more than just a place to eat; we're a part of the community, a spot where neighbors gather, and every visitor feels like family. Come on down and taste the tradition—experience the difference that passion and local pride make!
