Our Legacy

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Kings Mountain Tradition.

At Lil's Country Grill, we're more than just a place to eat – we're a cherished part of the Kings Mountain community. For years, we've been serving up hearty, homemade goodness, from our famous fluffy biscuits to our rich, creamy grits, ensuring every guest feels right at home.

We believe in honest food, friendly faces, and a warm atmosphere where everyone is welcome. Whether you're starting your day with a full breakfast – complete with eggs cooked just how you like them and a side of savory brown – or grabbing a quick, satisfying lunch, you'll taste the tradition that keeps locals coming back for more.
