Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal: A Haven for Healing & Connection

Light of the Moon Bakery & Cafe is a unique diner experience nestled in the heart of Waynesville, NC. Inspired by the transformative work of Dr. Anita Johnston, author of 'Eating in the Light of the Moon,' our cafe serves as an inviting extension of an online community dedicated to supporting women navigating food and body issues.

We believe in nourishing not just the body, but also the spirit, providing a warm, judgment-free space where every guest can feel seen, heard, and valued. Come share a meal, a conversation, and find strength in community.
