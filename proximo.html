Our Story

A Taste of Spain, Crafted with Passion

Welcome to Próximo Tapas y Vino, where we bring the vibrant flavors and warm hospitality of traditional Spanish tapas right to the heart of downtown Chapel Hill, North Carolina. Our journey began with a simple desire: to share the authentic culinary heritage of Spain, creating a gathering place where every dish tells a story.

From the bustling streets of Seville to the sun-drenched coasts of Catalonia, our chefs meticulously craft each tapa using time-honored recipes and the freshest ingredients. We believe in the power of good food, good wine, and great company, offering an experience that transcends a meal and becomes a cherished memory.

Join us to explore a world of small plates designed for sharing, exquisite Spanish wines, and a lively atmosphere that transports you straight to Spain.

Our Culinary Journey

Explore Our Tapas Selection

Patatas Bravas

Crispy fried potatoes drizzled with spicy bravas sauce and creamy garlic aioli.

Gambas al Ajillo

Sizzling shrimp cooked in garlic-infused olive oil with a hint of chili.

Croquetas de Jamón

Creamy béchamel fritters filled with savory Spanish cured ham, perfectly golden.

Tortilla Española

Classic Spanish potato and onion omelette, cooked to golden perfection.

Pulpo a la Gallega

Tender octopus served with sliced potatoes, paprika, and olive oil, Galician style.

Pimientos de Padrón

Flash-fried Padron peppers, sprinkled with sea salt (some are hot, most are not!).

Chorizo al Vino

Spicy Spanish chorizo slowly simmered in rich red wine until tender and flavorful.

Tabla de Quesos

A selection of fine Spanish cheeses, served with quince paste and artisan bread.

Pan con Tomate

Toasted bread rubbed with fresh tomato, garlic, and drizzled with olive oil.
