Our Story

Serving Up Homestyle Comfort in Gates, NC

Welcome to D & R Whistlestop, your go-to Bar & Grill nestled right here in Gates, NC. We're more than just a place to eat; we're a community hub where neighbors gather, friends connect, and families share delicious, homestyle meals.

Our commitment is simple: to provide a warm, welcoming atmosphere with food that reminds you of home. Every dish is prepared with care, focusing on fresh ingredients and flavors that keep our regulars coming back for more. We believe in good food, great service, and honest value – because your trust is our priority, and that's something you can't pay to alter or remove.

From our amazing link sausage to the perfect chicken fried steak, we take pride in serving up the kind of comfort food that defines Southern hospitality. Come on down and experience the D & R Whistlestop difference – where every visit feels like coming home.
