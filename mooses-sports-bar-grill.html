Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

Welcome to Moose's Sports Bar & Grill, your go-to spot in Winfield, NC, where every visit feels like coming home. We pride ourselves on creating a fantastic experience, from the moment you walk in to your very last bite.

We're deeply rooted in the local community, known for our warm hospitality and a menu crafted to satisfy every craving. Our team is dedicated to providing service that's as awesome as our food, ensuring you always leave with a smile.

Whether you're stopping by after a day at Timber Rock Lodge or just looking for a great place to unwind, Moose's is where good times and great food come together.
