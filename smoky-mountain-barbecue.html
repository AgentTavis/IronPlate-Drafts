Our Heritage

More Than Just BBQ, It's a West Jefferson Tradition

Tucked just before the main streets of Jefferson, Smoky Mountain BBQ is more than just a popular drive-through-only spot. We're a cornerstone of the community, serving up authentic, slow-smoked goodness that locals have loved for years.

We believe in doing things the right way – with patience, passion, and premium ingredients. From our carefully selected hams to our tender, smoked-meat platters, every dish tells a story of dedication. We pride ourselves on simple, honest food that brings people together, whether you're grabbing a quick lunch or feeding the whole family.

Come experience the taste of West Jefferson. We may be fast-food style, but our commitment to quality and flavor is anything but. Just look for the line – it's evident from the line around 6 PM!
