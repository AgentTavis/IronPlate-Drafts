OUR STORY

A Warm Welcome, Generations of Flavor.

Nestled in the heart of Peachland, Allen's Restaurant has been a beloved fixture for years, offering a dining experience that feels just like home. What started as a chance stop for many has become a cherished tradition, where every meal is prepared with pride and served with a smile.

We believe in big value and excellent food, crafted from fresh ingredients to please every palate. From hearty breakfast options served all day to authentic Italian dishes, juicy steaks, classic burgers, chicken, pork, fresh salads, and even Chicago-style hotdogs and delicious tuna melts – our menu is truly 110% full of options. It's home-style cooking at its best, brought to you by a friendly team dedicated to making your visit memorable.

Come on in and experience the warmth, the flavor, and the friendly service that makes Allen's Restaurant a true Peachland gem.
