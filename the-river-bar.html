Our Heritage

Where Good Times Flow as Freely as the River

Nestled alongside the tranquil Neuse River, The River Bar is more than just a place to grab a drink – it's a vibrant destination where memories are made. With a laid-back atmosphere perfect for unwinding, we offer a diverse selection of refreshing drinks to quench any thirst, from local craft beers to expertly mixed cocktails.

Our kitchen serves up tasty bar bites that perfectly complement your beverage, crafted to satisfy your cravings. Beyond the delicious food and drinks, The River Bar comes alive with regular live music and special events, creating an energetic and welcoming space for friends and family to gather. Come discover your new favorite spot in Goldsboro.
