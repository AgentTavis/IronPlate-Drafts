Our Story

A Taste of Timeless Comfort in Reidsville

Located in the heart of Reidsville, NC, Reid's House Restaurant is a beloved diner that exudes charm and warmth. With its retro decor and cozy booths, this spot has been a local institution for decades, serving up classic American comfort food with a smile.

From hearty breakfasts to delicious lunch specials and comforting dinner platters, every dish is prepared with care, ensuring a memorable dining experience that truly feels like stepping back in time. We pride ourselves on our homemade pies and customer service at its finest, making every visit feel like coming home.
