Our Roots, Our Passion

From Our Family to Your Plate: The La Kitchen de'Lite Story

Every dish tells a story, and ours is one of tradition, community, and unforgettable flavors.

It all began with a simple question from a family member: "When was the last time you had some herring?" That nostalgic query sparked a journey to bring authentic, no-frills, incredibly delicious food to Garysburg. We found our home in a cluster of stores right on 301, a convenient spot for our community to gather and enjoy.

At La Kitchen de'Lite, we believe good food doesn't need fuss. It needs passion, quality ingredients, and recipes passed down through generations. Our aim is to serve meals that remind you of home, like the ones Mom makes – perfect every time.

Our Signature Dishes & Culinary Treasures

While we're known for our delightful pizzas, discover the local favorites that have captivated Garysburg. Here's a glimpse of what makes us truly special.

The Legendary Herring Plate

A beloved local specialty: three pieces of perfectly hard-fried herring, served with two pieces of our soul-satisfying spoon cornbread, two pieces of savory fatback, and a crispy hash brown.

Classic Artisan Pizzas

Hand-tossed crusts, rich tomato sauce, premium cheeses, and your choice of fresh toppings. From classic pepperoni to gourmet vegetarian, there's a pizza for everyone.

Homestyle Comfort Sides
