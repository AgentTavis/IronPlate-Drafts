Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Elkin Tradition.

Tucked away from the main road, The Wild Flour Bistro is Elkin's charming secret, a cozy spot where locals and visitors alike discover exceptional coffee and a menu crafted with care. What started as a seemingly specialized coffee haven has blossomed into a beloved bar and grill, known for its warm atmosphere and delicious, homemade offerings.

We pride ourselves on being that delightful surprise you stumble upon, a cute place where every cup of coffee is brewed to perfection and every dish is prepared with the freshest ingredients. It’s a place where community gathers, stories are shared, and good food nourishes the soul. Come discover why we're Elkin's favorite hidden gem.
