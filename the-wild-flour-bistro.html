OUR STORY

Where Every Bite Feels Like Home

Nestled away from the main road in Elkin, The Wild Flour Bistro is a charming local eatery known for its warm, inviting atmosphere and culinary delights. While celebrated for our exceptional artisanal coffees and a variety of delicious bagels and homemade cream cheeses, we also pride ourselves on gourmet sandwiches and hearty specials like our famous Friday & Saturday Biscuits & Gravy. We believe every visit should feel like a moment of comfort and joy. Come discover your new favorite spot!

"The Wild Flour Bistro is such a cute place! Tucked away, it's a delightful surprise. Their bagels are incredible, and the coffee is always spot on. A true Elkin treasure."

- Sarah L., Loyal Customer
