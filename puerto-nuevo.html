Our Legacy

More Than Just a Meal: It's a Taste of Tradition and Community.

Welcome to Puerto Nuevo, where every dish tells a story and every guest feels like family. Nestled in the heart of Newland, we've been proudly serving the Banner Elk area since 2009. Our passion is bringing the vibrant flavors of authentic Mexican cuisine and the cheer of the best margaritas to your table.

We believe in fresh ingredients, time-honored recipes, and creating an atmosphere where laughter and good food go hand-in-hand. From our sizzling fajitas to our perfectly crafted cocktails, we invite you to experience why we've become a beloved local favorite for over a decade. Come and discover your new go-to for Mexican comfort food and unforgettable nights.
