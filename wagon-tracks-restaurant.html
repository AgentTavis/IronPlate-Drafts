Our Story

Where Every Meal Feels Like Coming Home

Nestled in the heart of Rutherfordton, NC, Wagon Tracks Restaurant has been a cherished local institution, serving up hearty American classics and genuine Southern hospitality. We're more than just a place to eat; we're a community hub where neighbors gather, families make memories, and every meal feels like coming home.

For over a decade, we've poured our passion into crafting delicious, comforting dishes made with love and the freshest local ingredients. From our sizzling breakfasts to our comforting lunch specials, we promise a warm welcome and a plate full of deliciousness. Come on down and experience the Wagon Tracks difference!

"Wagon Tracks isn't just a restaurant; it's a piece of Rutherfordton. The food is consistently good, and the friendly faces make you feel like family. A true local treasure!" - David P., Regular Customer

Our Delicious Offerings

Explore Our Hearty Menu

Classic Breakfast Platter

Two eggs any style, crispy bacon or sausage, grits or home fries, and your choice of toast or biscuit. A perfect start to your day!

Wagon Wheel Burger

Our signature juicy beef patty, melted cheddar, crisp lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles on a toasted bun. Served with golden fries.

Southern Fried Chicken Sandwich

Crispy, tender fried chicken breast with pickles and mayo on a soft brioche bun. A lunchtime favorite, served with fries.

Homemade Chili Bowl

A comforting bowl of our rich, slow-cooked chili, topped with shredded cheddar cheese and diced onions. Perfect on a cool day.

Daily Hot Plate Special

Ask your server about today's chef-inspired special. Always fresh, always delicious, and always a surprise!

Biscuits & Gravy

Fluffy, warm biscuits smothered in our creamy, savory sausage gravy. A true Southern classic, guaranteed to satisfy.
