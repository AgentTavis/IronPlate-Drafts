Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community Hub.

Wagon Tracks Restaurant isn't just a place to eat; it's a cornerstone of the Rutherfordton community. Nestled on Poors Ford Rd, we've built a reputation for serving up hearty meals, warm hospitality, and a welcoming atmosphere that feels just like home.

For years, locals and visitors alike have gathered at Wagon Tracks to share laughs, create memories, and enjoy delicious, unpretentious food. We believe in good old-fashioned quality, friendly service, and being a place where everyone feels like family. Come on down and experience the local charm that makes us Rutherfordton's beloved spot.
