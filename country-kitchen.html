Our Story

Generations of Flavor, Rooted in Timberlake.

For years, Country Kitchen has been the heart of Timberlake, a reliable spot where families and friends gather for a truly satisfying meal. We pride ourselves on offering a wide selection of delicious dishes, always served with a smile by our courteous team in a clean, welcoming atmosphere.

We believe good food shouldn't break the bank, especially for our younger guests, ensuring there's something to delight every palate in your group. From a bustling Sunday lunch after church to a quiet weeknight dinner, we're dedicated to making every visit memorable.
