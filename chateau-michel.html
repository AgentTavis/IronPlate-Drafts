Our Menu

Taste the Chateau Michel Difference

From hearty classics to delightful surprises, our menu is crafted to satisfy every craving. Explore our highlights below!

Michel's Signature Burger

A juicy, perfectly seasoned patty, melted cheddar, crisp lettuce, tomato, onion, and our secret Michel's sauce on a toasted brioche bun.

Loaded Nacho Tower

Crispy tortilla chips piled high with melted queso, jalapeños, black beans, pico de gallo, sour cream, and your choice of grilled chicken or seasoned beef.

Spicy Chicken Sandwich

Grilled or fried chicken breast, tossed in a zesty Nashville hot sauce, with pickles and a cool ranch slaw on a toasted bun.

Truffle Parmesan Fries

Golden, crispy fries drizzled with aromatic truffle oil, sprinkled generously with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh parsley.

BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders

Three mini brioche buns filled with tender, slow-cooked pulled pork, drenched in tangy BBQ sauce and topped with a hint of coleslaw.

Craft Beer Selection

A rotating selection of the finest local and regional craft beers on tap, perfect for pairing with any dish or enjoying on its own.

Seasonal Berry Cobbler

A warm, homemade cobbler bursting with fresh seasonal berries, topped with a buttery oat crumble and served with a scoop of vanilla bean ice cream.

Classic Caesar Salad

Crisp romaine lettuce, crunchy croutons, shaved Parmesan, and a creamy Caesar dressing. Add grilled chicken for a complete meal.
