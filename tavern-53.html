Our Heritage

A Taste of Tradition, Crafted with Passion.

Nestled at the heart of the historic Pickens Golf Club, Tavern 53 has become a cherished gathering place for locals and visitors alike. We believe in good food, great company, and the timeless charm of Southern hospitality.

From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a customer – you're family. We pride ourselves on creating memorable dining experiences, whether you're joining us for a casual lunch, a lively dinner, or our famous Sunday Buffet.

Our commitment to "grilled to perfection" dishes and a welcoming atmosphere is what truly sets us apart. Come, savor the flavor, and become a part of the Tavern 53 story.

"Every visit to Tavern 53 feels like coming home. The Sunday Buffet is a must-try, and their grilled dishes are always spot on. The atmosphere is just what Pickens needed!"

— A Loyal Local, Pickens Resident
