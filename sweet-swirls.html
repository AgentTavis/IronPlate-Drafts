Our Journey

Our Passion, Your Sweet & Savory Experience.

Sweet Swirls recently opened on Main Street in China Grove, quickly becoming a beloved local spot. We believe in bringing joy through freshly made cinnamon rolls that you can customize with endless toppings, making every bite a unique adventure.

But we're not just about sweets! Our commitment to deliciousness extends to our savory menu, where we serve up equally tasty items. From great cupcakes and decadent cheesecakes to savory classics, there's something to satisfy every craving.

We LOVE the pimento cheese, bacon and tomato sandwich for a hearty lunch, and our shepherd's pie is a comforting favorite. Come experience the warmth and flavor that makes Sweet Swirls truly special.
