Our Journey

Bringing the Heart of Oaxaca to Edenton

At La Oaxaqueña, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a culinary bridge to the vibrant flavors and rich traditions of Oaxaca, Mexico, right here in Edenton, NC. Our journey began with a passion for sharing authentic Mexican cuisine, crafted with fresh ingredients and a whole lot of love.

Every dish tells a story, from the sizzling spices of our Chorizo with Scrambled Eggs to the tangy embrace of our Chilaquiles in Green Sauce. We pour our hearts into every meal, ensuring each bite transports you to the bustling markets and comforting kitchens of Oaxaca.

It's a friendly place where locals and visitors alike can gather, share a delicious meal, and create lasting memories. We believe good food brings people together, and we're dedicated to making your experience unforgettable.

Our Offerings

Taste the Tradition

Chorizo with Scrambled Eggs

A classic breakfast favorite! Perfectly spiced chorizo scrambled with farm-fresh eggs, served with warm tortillas for a truly authentic start to your day.

Chilaquiles in Green Sauce

Crispy tortilla chips smothered in our vibrant, spicy, and tangy green sauce, topped with over-medium eggs. A traditional Oaxacan delight you won't forget.

Mexican Coffee

A comforting cup of coffee with a delightful hint of cinnamon, brewed to perfection. The ideal complement to any meal, morning or afternoon.

Sweet Breads

Indulge in our selection of freshly baked sweet breads. Perfect for a dessert or a delightful treat with your Mexican coffee.
