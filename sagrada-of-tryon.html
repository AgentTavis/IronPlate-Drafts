Our Heritage

From Our Family to Your Table: A Culinary Journey

At Sagrada of Tryon, we believe dining should be an experience that delights all your senses. Nestled in the heart of Tryon, NC, we've poured our passion into creating a space where modern culinary artistry meets the warmth of Latin flair. Our journey began with a simple vision: to offer exceptional, from-scratch meals in an atmosphere that's both chic and casually inviting.

It is difficult to find this level of quality meal preparation elsewhere. Every ingredient is carefully selected, and every dish, from our vibrant small plates to our comforting homestyle meals, is crafted with meticulous attention to detail. We pride ourselves on our craft cocktails and an upbeat ambiance that makes every visit memorable.

Come discover why Sagrada of Tryon is quickly becoming a beloved culinary destination. We can't wait to share our table with you.

Our Offerings

Savor Our Modern Faire, Latin Flair

The Sagrada Margarita

Our signature take on a classic, crafted with premium tequila, fresh lime, and a hint of exotic fruit.

Artisan Empanadas

Crispy, golden pastries filled with savory, slow-cooked meats or fresh vegetables, served with a zesty dipping sauce.

Seared Scallops with Chimichurri

Perfectly seared scallops resting on a bed of creamy polenta, drizzled with vibrant homemade chimichurri.

Braised Short Rib Arepas

Tender, succulent short ribs slow-braised to perfection, served atop fluffy corn arepas with a spiced aioli.

Chef's Signature Paella

A comforting homestyle classic with a modern twist, featuring saffron-infused rice, fresh seafood, and savory chorizo.

Seasonal Market Salad

A refreshing blend of locally sourced greens, seasonal fruits, nuts, and artisanal cheese with our house vinaigrette.

Decadent Chocolate Lava Cake

Rich, warm chocolate cake with a molten center, served with a scoop of vanilla bean ice cream and fresh berries.
