Our Roots

Crafting Good Times, One Brew at a Time

Surf City Brewing Company is more than just a bar and grill; it's a beloved local spot in Hampstead, NC, where community, craft, and comfort converge. Since August 2023, we've poured our passion into brewing exceptional beers and serving up delicious, satisfying food in a friendly, laid-back atmosphere that feels like home.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a gathering place where friends could connect, families could enjoy a meal, and everyone could savor the taste of expertly crafted beverages and hearty dishes. We believe in good company, great flavors, and making every visit an experience to remember.

"This place perfectly captures the Hampstead spirit! Fantastic environment to hang out, and their commitment to local vibes truly shines."

- Emily R., A Regular Guest
