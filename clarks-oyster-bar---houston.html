Our Heritage

Bringing Coastal Freshness to the Heart of Houston, NC

Since opening our doors in 2012, Clark's Oyster Bar has been dedicated to delivering an unparalleled dining experience. We pride ourselves on our extensive raw bar, showcasing the freshest oysters sourced from pristine waters, alongside a diverse menu of expertly prepared seafood and beloved American classics. With a commitment to quality and a passion for flavor, we've become a cherished destination for seafood lovers across multiple locations, including our vibrant spot here in Houston, NC.
