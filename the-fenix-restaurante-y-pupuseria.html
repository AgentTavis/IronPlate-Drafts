Our Heritage

Experience the Authentic Flavors of Honduras in Eden, NC.

Restaurante The Fenix is more than just a place to eat; it's a culinary journey to the heart of Honduras, right here in Eden, North Carolina. We pride ourselves on bringing you traditional dishes, lovingly prepared with recipes passed down through generations.

From our renowned pupusas, handcrafted with fresh masa and savory fillings, to a variety of other Honduran and Central American specialties, every dish tells a story. We believe in honest, delicious food, fair prices, and fantastic service that makes you feel right at home.

Our Culinary Delights

Signature Dishes That Keep You Coming Back.

Handcrafted Pupusas

Our famous pupusas, a true Honduran staple. Thick, savory corn tortillas filled with a variety of delicious ingredients like cheese, beans, chicharrón (fried pork), or a mix.

Street Tacos

Authentic street-style tacos, prepared with your choice of seasoned meats, fresh onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime on warm corn tortillas.

Hearty Gorditas

Crispy on the outside, soft on the inside, our gorditas are made with fresh ingredients and generously stuffed with flavorful fillings. A satisfying and delicious choice.

Our Signature Soup

As one customer raved, "the best soup I've ever had." Experience our rich, flavorful, and comforting soup, a true taste of home.

Platos Fuertes (Main Dishes)

Beyond pupusas, explore a selection of traditional Honduran and Central American main dishes, each prepared with care and authentic spices.

Fresh Aguas Frescas

Quench your thirst with our refreshing homemade aguas frescas, a perfect complement to any meal.
