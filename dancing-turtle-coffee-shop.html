Our Story

Experience the Warmth of Hatteras Island

Nestled in the heart of Hatteras, The Dancing Turtle Coffee Shop is more than just a place for your morning brew. It's a community hub, a serene escape, and a true reflection of the island's unique charm.

We pride ourselves on creating an atmosphere where "spring break vibes are HERE" every day. From our early morning open to our friendly smiles, we're dedicated to keeping you caffeinated and smiling.

Just like the celestial light of Hatteras Island, our vibe welcomes you with open arms. Brian, our owner, ensures every visitor feels the genuine warmth and hospitality that makes The Dancing Turtle truly special.
