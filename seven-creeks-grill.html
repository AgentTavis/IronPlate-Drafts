Our Roots

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Tradition.

At Seven Creeks Grill, we believe in hearty Southern flavors and generous portions. Located in the heart of Nakina, NC, we've become a cherished spot for locals and visitors alike seeking an authentic bar and grill experience.

Many of our patrons tell us our delicious seafood is a must-try, but for those who crave a classic, our 10oz Ribeye with Baked Potato for just $15 is legendary. We trim it close, so you truly get every ounce of that savory goodness.

And let's not forget the sweet tea – we brew it just right, so you can actually taste the tea, not just the sugar. It's the little details like these that make Seven Creeks Grill feel like home. Come join our table, where good food and good company are always on the menu.
