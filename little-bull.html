OUR STORY

A Culinary Journey in Durham

Little Bull is more than just a restaurant; it's a celebration of vibrant flavors and artful presentation, nestled near the heart of Downtown Durham. We pride ourselves on offering delicious Mexican-esque food that excites the palate with every carefully crafted bite.

Our passion lies in creating an unforgettable dining experience, where selecting a cocktail, an entree, or one of our delightful small plates becomes an excellent problem to have. We cherish the opportunity to serve our community and look forward to welcoming you back again and again.
