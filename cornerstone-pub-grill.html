Our Story

Your Neighborhood Home

Nestled in the heart of Matthews, Cornerstone Pub & Grill is more than just a place to eat and drink — it's where community gathers. We pride ourselves on being your go-to neighborhood pub, serving up ice-cold beverages and delicious, satisfying food in a warm, friendly atmosphere.

We believe in good times, great company, and even better food. Our menu features classic pub fare with a homemade touch. Whether you're craving a perfectly cooked burger, exploring our diverse drink selection, or joining us for our popular weekend brunch, you're always part of the family here.
