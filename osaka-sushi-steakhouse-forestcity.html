Our Story

A Taste of Tradition, Crafted with Passion

At Osaka Sushi & Steakhouse ForestCity, we believe in crafting more than just meals; we create experiences. Our journey began with a simple vision: to bring the authentic flavors and artistry of Japanese cuisine to the heart of Forest City, NC. We are dedicated to using the freshest ingredients, preparing each dish with precision, and offering a welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels like family.

From the delicate art of hand-rolled sushi to the sizzling spectacle of hibachi cooked right at your table, every visit promises a culinary adventure. We invite you to gather with friends and family, witness culinary artistry unfold before your eyes, and savor moments that linger long after the last bite.
