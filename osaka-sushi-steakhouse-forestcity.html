Our Journey

The Heart & Soul of Forest City's Japanese Cuisine

At Osaka Sushi & Steakhouse, we believe in bringing exceptional dining experiences to the heart of Forest City. What started as a vision to combine the delicate art of sushi with the fiery excitement of hibachi has blossomed into a cherished destination where every dish is a celebration of fresh ingredients and culinary craftsmanship.

We are dedicated to providing a memorable fine dining experience, where tradition meets innovation, and every guest feels like family. We invite you to explore our menu, see what our guests are saying, and discover why we're a must-visit destination for those seeking authentic Japanese flavors and a captivating meal.
