Our Heritage

Serving Heartfelt Meals & Community Spirit

For decades, Etna Diner has been the warm heart of Whiteville, NC, a cherished gathering place where classic American comfort food meets genuine Southern hospitality. We believe in serving up generous portions of honest, delicious meals, crafted with care and tradition. From our sizzling breakfast platters that kickstart your day to our satisfying lunch and dinner specials that feel just like home, every dish is a taste of local heritage.

Etna Diner is more than just a meal; it's a timeless experience, a place where families and friends connect, and new memories are made over every cup of coffee. Come and discover why we're a beloved Whiteville tradition.
