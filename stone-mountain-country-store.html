OUR STORY

More Than Just a Store

Stone Mountain Country Store is a true Traphill institution, a place where community gathers and memories are made. For years, we've been proudly serving our neighbors and visitors, offering a unique blend of a classic country store and a lively bar and grill.

Whether you're an adventurer stopping by for camping supplies, a family looking for a delicious meal and ice cream, or just someone craving a refreshing drink and good company, you'll find a warm welcome here. Our wide variety of options ensures there's something for everyone, making us your convenient one-stop destination for both essentials and indulgence.

Come experience the heart of Traphill with us – where every visit feels like coming home.
