Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Walnut Cove Tradition

Tucked away inside Hicks Pharmacy, Red Rooster Grill is a beloved local secret and a true culinary gem in Walnut Cove, NC. We're proud to be that classic "Drug Store" Diner where everyone knows your name and the food tastes like home.

For years, we've been serving up American comfort food with a smile, crafting a unique dining experience that blends nostalgia with delicious, satisfying meals. Our commitment to quality ingredients and our cozy, welcoming atmosphere has made us a 4.5-star favorite.

Come experience the charm and taste the tradition that has made us a cherished part of the Stokes County community. We're a hidden treasure worth discovering!

"The Red Rooster is an absolute classic! You feel like you've stepped back in time the moment you walk in. Great food, friendly service, and a truly unique setting inside the pharmacy. It's a must-visit in Walnut Cove!"
