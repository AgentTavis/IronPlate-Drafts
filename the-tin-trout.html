Our Roots

More Than Just BBQ, It's an Experience.

Nestled in the heart of Newland, The Tin Trout is a charming, family-owned establishment that's much larger and more inviting than it appears from the outside. We believe in creating a unique dining experience, combining rustic mountain charm with incredible flavors.

Step inside to discover interesting country artifacts adorning every wall, a nod to the rich history of our beautiful region. Choose from casual indoor dining, the lively 'garage room', or our tranquil back porch overlooking a cute little stream. Every corner offers a new delight, making each visit a memorable escape.

"The atmosphere here is absolutely captivating. I felt like I was dining in a cozy museum, and the creek outside added such a peaceful touch. A truly unique gem in Newland!"

- A Happy Creekside Diner

Our Flavors

Our Most Loved Dishes

Smoked Trout Dip

A true mountain delicacy. Our signature dip is rich, creamy, and packed with locally smoked trout, served with fresh, crispy bread for dipping. An absolute must-try starter!

Fried Chicken Sandwiches

Crispy, juicy fried chicken perfected, served on a toasted bun with fresh toppings and our special sauce. A comfort food classic with a Tin Trout twist that keeps guests coming back.

Delightful Desserts

End your meal on a high note with one of our irresistible, homemade desserts. From decadent cakes to seasonal pies, each treat is crafted with love and guarantees a sweet finish.

These are just a taste! Discover even more delicious options on our full menu when you visit.
