Our Heritage

More Than Just Coffee, It's Home.

Some places just serve coffee. Cafe Sol serves people. We believe in creating a space where everyone feels at home, a true extension of your living room in the heart of Franklinton.

Our coffee is robust, intentional, and unapologetically excellent. You come for the caffeine; you stay for the warmth, the atmosphere, and the quiet, steady hum of a community that truly matters. We're not just fueling mornings; we're fueling revival, one perfect cup at a time.
