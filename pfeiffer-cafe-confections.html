OUR STORY

Crafting Delicious Memories Since 1985.

Welcome to Pfeiffer Cafe & Confections, a beloved diner nestled in the heart of Shelby, NC. We're more than just a place to eat; we're a community hub where every dish is made with passion and served with a smile. From our cozy indoor atmosphere to our inviting outdoor patio, we strive to make every visit a memorable one.

Our menu thoughtfully caters to all appetites, offering delightful half-sized portions for lighter cravings and robust full plates for hearty hunger, ensuring there's always something perfect for you. Since 1985, we've been dedicated to serving delicious, comforting food that feels like a hug on a plate. Come on in and experience the warmth and flavors that make us a local gem.
