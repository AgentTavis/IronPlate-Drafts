Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience

Nestled in the heart of Concord, NC, Afton Tavern isn't just a place to eat; it's a vibrant community hub where friends gather, families celebrate, and good times are always on tap. We've been proudly serving our neighbors with a commitment to quality food, refreshing drinks, and an atmosphere that feels like home.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a local spot where everyone feels welcome. From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a customer, you're part of the Afton Tavern family. We believe in genuine hospitality, hearty portions, and a menu crafted to satisfy every craving. Come for the food, stay for the camaraderie, and leave with a smile.
