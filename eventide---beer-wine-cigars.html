Our Story

Crafting Coastal Memories for Every Guest.

EvenTide is more than just a dining establishment; it's a cherished Surf City tradition built on a passion for exceptional coastal cuisine and a vibrant community spirit. We've dedicated ourselves to creating a welcoming space where friends and family can gather, unwind, and savor the finest flavors of the sea.

Beyond our delicious seafood and fresh catches, EvenTide proudly offers a carefully curated selection of craft beers from multiple cooler doors, a diverse wine list to perfectly complement any dish, and an exclusive collection of premium cigars. It’s a place where every detail is considered to enhance your experience, from the first bite to the last puff.
