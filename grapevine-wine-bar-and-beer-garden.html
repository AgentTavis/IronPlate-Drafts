Our Journey

Crafting Experiences, One Sip at a Time

At Grapevine Wine Bar and Beer Garden, we believe in more than just serving exceptional wine and craft beer; we're dedicated to cultivating an experience. Born from a passion to bring a friendly, upscale neighborhood atmosphere to York, NC, Grapevine quickly became a cherished local spot.

It's the warmth, the conversation, and the discovery of your new favorite pour that brings our guests back, time and again. We've poured our hearts into creating a space where every visit feels like coming home, a place where you can relax, connect, and truly savor the moment.
