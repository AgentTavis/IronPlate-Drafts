Our Heritage

Discover Fairfield's Hidden Culinary Gem

Nestled in the heart of Fairfield, Harris Steak & Seafood House is more than just a restaurant—it's an experience. We were pleasantly surprised by this gem in the middle of nowhere, a discovery many of our guests make while traveling through Swan Quarter en route to Ocracoke.

Our commitment is simple: serve generous portions of hot, perfectly seasoned, and prepared food in a friendly, welcoming atmosphere. From our fresh seafood dishes like succulent shrimp and flaky flounder to our signature homemade crab cakes, every plate tells a story of tradition and quality. And don't forget our incredible salad bar—it's huge, fresh, and offers a delightful selection that truly surprises and satisfies.

We pride ourselves on offering a memorable dining experience, where every dish is crafted with care and every guest feels like family. Come and taste why Harris Steak & Seafood House is a beloved local secret.
