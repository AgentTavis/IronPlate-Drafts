Our Heritage

A Taste of Roan Mountain's Heart

Nestled in the heart of Roan Mountain, McKinney Resturant is more than just a place to eat – it's a cherished homestead where country cooking traditions meet the bounty of fresh seafood. For years, we've been proudly serving hearty breakfasts and delicious lunches, creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere for our community and visitors alike.

From classic Southern comfort dishes to our signature seafood plates, every meal is prepared with love, just like Grandma used to make. We believe in honest, flavorful food that brings people together, reflecting the true spirit of Roan Mountain hospitality.

"Bringing the true taste of home to every plate, every day."
