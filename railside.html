Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community Hub.

Welcome to Railside, Tryon's cherished Bar & Grill! We're more than just a place to eat – we're a true gathering spot for friends, family, and the whole community right here in Downtown Tryon.

From delightful lunches served Monday through Saturday, to our exciting special events, we're always cooking up something delicious and creating memorable moments. Discover our passion for great food, warm hospitality, and the unique charm that makes Railside a local favorite.
