Our Journey

The Heart Behind Mr. Taco

Mr. Taco is a vibrant eatery located in Ellenboro, NC, offering a delicious array of authentic Mexican dishes. We started with a simple dream: to bring the true flavors of Mexico to our community, prepared with love and the freshest ingredients.

What began as a small vision has grown into a beloved local spot, known for its friendly atmosphere and a menu that truly speaks to the soul. Every dish we serve is a reflection of generations of family recipes, crafted to transport you straight to the bustling streets and vibrant markets of Mexico.

We believe in good food, good company, and making every meal a celebration. From our kitchen to your table, we pour our heart and soul into every taco, burrito, and enchilada, ensuring an unforgettable culinary experience right here in Ellenboro.
