Our Journey

From Our Family to Your Table

Mr. Taco is more than just a vibrant eatery in Ellenboro, NC—it's a passion project brought to life by a family dedicated to sharing the true taste of Mexico. We believe that authentic flavors shouldn't be a luxury, but an everyday joy.

Every dish, from our savory tacos to our hearty burritos, is prepared with the freshest ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations. We started this journey with a big heart and a simple goal: to serve delicious, authentic Mexican food that brings smiles to our community.
