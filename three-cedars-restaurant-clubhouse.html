Our Story

A Millersburg Legacy of Flavor

Welcome to Three Cedars Restaurant & Clubhouse, a place where culinary passion meets community spirit. We pride ourselves on serving up authentic American cuisine with a special focus on mouth-watering BBQ.

From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a customer; you're family. We offer delicious lunch and dinner options, with comfortable seating, friendly table service, and convenient takeout.

Our commitment is simple: create memorable dining experiences through quality ingredients, bold flavors, and a warm, inviting atmosphere that keeps you coming back for more.
