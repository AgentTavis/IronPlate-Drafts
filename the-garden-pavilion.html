Our Story

A Local Gem, Rooted in Community & Good Times

Welcome to The Garden Pavilion, Jacksonville's favorite local spot where good times and great food come together. We've cultivated a vibrant atmosphere, perfect for a casual evening or a lively gathering.

Step onto our spacious outdoor patio, a true gem ideal for soaking up the sun or enjoying the night air. Inside, you'll find a welcoming, attentive team ready to make your visit memorable. While our decor nods to a classic, relaxed pub style, the energy here is always fresh and fun. We’re more than just a Bar And Grill; we’re a place to connect, laugh, and make lasting memories with friends and family.
