Our Story

More Than Just a Bar – It's Your Pineville Home Away From Home.

Every small town needs a local dive, and Pineville Tavern proudly fills that role for our small but rapidly growing suburb of Pineville, NC. For years, we've been the go-to spot where friends gather, laughter echoes, and good times are always on tap.

We're easy to miss from the outside, keeping a low profile, but step inside and you'll discover a spacious, lively hub. Complete with a stage for incredible live music, plenty of comfortable seating, and even pool tables – we're designed for unwinding and making memories.

Whether you're popping in before a movie, looking for a place to catch up with friends, or ready to dance the night away, Pineville Tavern is where the good times begin. We're more than just a place to eat and drink; we're a community staple, a stage for local talent, and your friendly neighborhood escape.

Our Offerings

Great Food, Cold Drinks & Entertainment

While our full, dynamic menu is best experienced in person, here’s a taste of what makes Pineville Tavern a local favorite. From classic bar bites to satisfying mains, paired with a wide selection of drinks – we've got something for everyone.

The Tavern Burger

Our signature patty grilled to perfection, with fresh toppings and your choice of cheese. A classic done right.

Crispy Chicken Wings

Choose from our selection of savory sauces – Buffalo, BBQ, or Garlic Parmesan. Perfect for sharing (or not!).

Loaded Nachos

A mountain of crispy tortilla chips, melted cheese, jalapeños, pico de gallo, sour cream, and your choice of protein.

Craft Beer Selection

Rotating taps featuring local breweries and national favorites. Ask your server for tonight's specials!

Hand-Cut Fries

Perfectly golden and seasoned, served hot. The ultimate sidekick to any meal.

Signature Cocktails

Expertly mixed drinks, from timeless classics to our own Pineville Tavern creations. Cheers!

Daily Specials

Ask your server about today's fresh culinary creations and drink features. Always something new to discover!

Poolside Pizza Bites

Crispy mini pizzas, perfect for a casual bite while enjoying games or live music.
