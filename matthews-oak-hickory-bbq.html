Our Story

Rooted in Carolina Tradition.

At Matthews Oak & Hickory BBQ, we honor South Carolina's rich barbecue heritage right here in Saluda. Our pitmasters meticulously slow-smoke premium meats over genuine oak and hickory wood, creating flavors that are both timeless and unforgettable.

While the SC barbecue scene is packed with legendary joints and hidden gems, we pride ourselves on offering a uniquely authentic experience, drawing locals and visitors alike to our table. Come taste the tradition that makes us a favorite in Saluda and beyond.
