OUR STORY

A Legacy Smoked to Perfection.

South Carolina’s barbecue scene is indeed packed with legendary joints and hidden gems, and we are proud to be Saluda's authentic contribution to this rich tradition. At Matthews Oak & Hickory BBQ, we believe true BBQ is an art form passed down through generations.

Every morning, our pits are fired with carefully selected oak and hickory woods, slow-smoking our meats to tender, flavorful perfection. It’s more than just food; it’s a commitment to the craft, a passion for genuine Southern flavors, and a dedication to serving our community with a smile.

Whether you're starting your day with a hearty breakfast or joining us for a satisfying lunch, you'll experience the heart and soul we pour into every dish. We invite you to taste the difference that real pit-smoked BBQ makes.
