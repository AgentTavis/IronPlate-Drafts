Our Heritage

Crafting Moments, One Sip at a Time.

Nestled in the heart of Stokesdale, NC, Stonefield Cellars Winery is more than just a destination – it's an experience. We believe in the power of good wine, delicious food, and even better company to create lasting memories. Our journey began with a passion for viticulture and a dream to cultivate a place where community gathers.

We've meticulously nurtured our vineyards, selecting varietals that thrive in our unique North Carolina terroir. Every bottle tells a story of dedication, from the gentle whisper of the vines to the careful hand in the cellar. As a local Bar and Grill, we pair our exceptional wines with a thoughtfully curated selection of dishes, designed to complement and enhance your tasting adventure.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, enjoying a casual evening out, or simply seeking a serene escape, Stonefield Cellars offers a warm, inviting atmosphere where every guest feels like family. Come discover the magic woven into every detail.
