Our Story

Generations of Flavor, Rooted in Cherokee.

Welcome to Paul's Family Restaurant, a beloved local spot nestled at the picturesque base of the mountains in Cherokee, NC. For decades, we've been serving up a unique blend of hearty seafood and comforting mountain-inspired dishes that keep families coming back.

We're proud to be known for our incredible, crispy cheese curds (a TikTok sensation!), our wildly popular fried chicken, and burgers that hit the spot without breaking the bank. While our menu offers something for everyone, it's the warm, welcoming atmosphere and the taste of home that truly define us.

Come gather around our table, enjoy some genuine Southern hospitality, and discover why Paul's is a cherished part of the Cherokee community.

Why Paul's?

Uniquely Cherokee Flavors

Experience a taste of the mountains with our distinctive dishes like the unforgettable Indian Taco, savory Elk Burger, and traditional Frybread Burger. We celebrate local ingredients and heritage in every bite.

From our famous cheese curds and crispy fried chicken to classic burgers and Country Fried Steak, every meal at Paul's is prepared with a generous helping of love. It’s a place where families gather and memories are made.
