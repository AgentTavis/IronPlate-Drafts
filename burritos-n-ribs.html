Our Story: More Than Just a Meal, It's a Fiesta!

Nestled in the heart of Troutman, Burritos n Ribs was founded on a simple premise: bring truly mouth-watering Mexican cuisine with a unique twist to our community. We believe in fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and generous portions. Our specialty, from the sizzling fajita platters bursting with peppers and onions to our famously tender International Ribs, is crafted to transport your taste buds straight to the heart of Mexico.

Every visit is an invitation to gather, share, and enjoy. With over 900 five-star reviews, our loyal customers agree – Burritos n Ribs isn't just a restaurant, it's where Troutman comes to celebrate good food and great company.
