OUR STORY

Rooted in Authentic Flavor

At Burritos n Ribs, we believe in crafting experiences as vibrant as our food. Our signature dishes start with the freshest ingredients, from a sizzling platter bursting with tender peppers and onions to perfectly seasoned meats.

Each meal is a complete fiesta, served with fluffy rice, savory beans, fresh guacamole, and your choice of warm corn or flour tortillas, bringing the authentic taste of Mexico right to your table here in Troutman, NC.

We're passionate about sharing the rich, authentic flavors of Mexican cuisine with our community, creating a welcoming space where every meal feels like a celebration.
