Our Journey

Savor the Tradition, Taste the Smoke.

At Mesquite Grill NC, we believe in the art of slow-smoked BBQ. Located in the heart of Havelock, we've been dedicated to crafting mouth-watering dishes that bring friends and families together.

Our journey began with a simple passion: to serve truly authentic, slow-cooked meats infused with the rich flavor of mesquite wood. Every cut of beef brisket and pork barbecue sandwich is prepared with meticulous care, ensuring a tender, juicy experience that leaves you craving more. And it's not just the meats; our sides, from the spicy macaroni and cheese to the delicious baked beans, are made from scratch daily, perfecting your BBQ feast.

We're more than just a restaurant; we're a place where community gathers, stories are shared, and every meal is a celebration of Southern hospitality. Come on down and taste the difference that passion and tradition make!
