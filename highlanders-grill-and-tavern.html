Our Delicious Offerings

Savor the Taste of the Mountains

Highlanders Classic Burger

A juicy, seasoned patty grilled to perfection, served with fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, and your choice of cheese.

Smoked Brisket Sandwich

Slow-smoked, tender brisket piled high on a toasted bun with our signature BBQ sauce and crispy onions.

Mountain Chili Bowl

Our hearty, homemade chili, slow-simmered with premium beef, beans, and a blend of spices. Topped with cheddar and onions.

Crispy Fried Chicken

Golden-fried chicken, seasoned to perfection, offering a crispy exterior and juicy, tender meat inside.

House Mac & Cheese

Creamy, rich macaroni and cheese, baked with a blend of premium cheeses for the ultimate comfort food.

Grilled Salmon Plate

Flaky grilled salmon, lightly seasoned, served with seasonal vegetables for a healthier, delicious option.

Side Salad with House Dressing

Fresh mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onion, tossed with our tangy house vinaigrette.

Apple Cobbler ala Mode

Warm, spiced apple cobbler, baked until bubbly, served with a scoop of vanilla bean ice cream.
