Our Heritage

Crafting Delicious Moments, One Sub at a Time.

Nestled in the heart of Valdese, NC, Chubby's is more than just a restaurant – it's a local institution built on personal service and a passion for great food. We're proud to be locally owned and operated, bringing you custom-made subs crafted daily on our signature fresh-baked bread.

While we're known for our incredible subs and daily specials including hearty soups, it's the warm, welcoming atmosphere and the taste that keeps our community coming back. Many say they prefer us over the big chains, and we believe it's because we put a little piece of ourselves into every meal. Come on down and experience the Chubby's difference!
