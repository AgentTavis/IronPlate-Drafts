Our Journey

Our Recipe for Community & Authentic Taste

For over two decades, Mike's Pizza has been a cornerstone of the Sonora community, serving up delicious, hand-tossed pizzas made with passion and the freshest ingredients. What started as a small dream to bring authentic flavors to our neighbors has grown into a beloved local tradition.

We believe in good food, great company, and the power of a perfect slice to bring smiles to every face. From our family to yours, we invite you to experience the warmth and taste that makes Mike's Pizza a true Sonora favorite.
