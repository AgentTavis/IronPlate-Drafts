Our Menu

Savor the Flavors of Mexico

Enchiladas Rojas

Corn tortillas filled with savory shredded chicken or cheese, smothered in our rich, traditional red sauce.

Tacos Al Pastor

Marinated pork slow-cooked on a vertical spit, served in soft tortillas with pineapple, onions, and cilantro.

Carne Asada

Grilled marinated skirt steak, thinly sliced and served with grilled onions, rice, beans, and fresh tortillas.

Chile Rellenos

Poblano peppers roasted and filled with cheese, battered and fried, then topped with a mild sauce.

Fajita Combo

A sizzling platter of chicken, steak, and shrimp fajitas with peppers and onions, served with all the fixings.

Burrito Supremo

A large flour tortilla filled with your choice of meat, rice, beans, lettuce, cheese, and sour cream.

Quesadillas Rancheras

Crispy flour tortillas filled with melted cheese, grilled chicken or steak, and a side of fresh pico de gallo.

Sopa de Tortilla

A comforting chicken broth-based soup with crispy tortilla strips, avocado, cheese, and a hint of chili.
