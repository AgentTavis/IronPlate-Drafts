Our Legacy

Where Every Wag is Welcome.

At Boulder's, our love for dogs runs deep... it's in our name, after all. Boulder wasn't just a pet, he was family, and we know many of our guests feel the same way about their four-legged companions. That's why we proudly welcome well-behaved dogs on our outdoor patio.

Whether you're grabbing a drink after a walk or enjoying a sunny afternoon with friends, your pup can be part of the experience. It's our way of making sure Boulder's legacy lives on in every wagging tail and happy bark, making us a true home away from home for families (both two-legged and four!).
