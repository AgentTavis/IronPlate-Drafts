Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience

With its cozy ambiance and friendly staff, Three Fork Cakes provides a welcoming atmosphere for guests to relax and savor their meal. Whether you're in the mood for a hearty meal, a refreshing craft beer from our microbrewery, or a delightful baked treat from our bakery, you'll find it all here.

We're more than just a bar and grill; we're a community gathering spot where good times and great food come together. Our commitment to quality ingredients, unique craft beverages, and a warm, inviting setting ensures every visit is memorable. Come for the food, stay for the company, and leave with a smile.
