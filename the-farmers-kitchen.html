Our Heritage

From Our Farm to Your Table, with Love

Nestled in the heart of Bunn, NC, The Farmer's Kitchen is more than just a restaurant; it's a gathering place where hearty Southern comfort food meets genuine hospitality. We believe in serving up honest, wholesome meals just like Grandma used to make.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a cozy spot where families and friends can come together to enjoy delicious, home-style cooking. We pride ourselves on using fresh, quality ingredients, preparing every dish with care and passion.

From our crispy Country Ham to our savory Hamburger Steak, every bite tells a story of tradition and community. We're a family-friendly establishment, good for groups, and always ready to make you feel right at home. Come on down and experience the warmth of The Farmer's Kitchen!

Our Delicious Offerings

Comfort Food Favorites, Made Fresh Daily

Chicken Salad

Our classic, creamy chicken salad, made with tender all-white meat chicken, crisp celery, and a hint of special seasoning. A light yet satisfying choice.

Country Ham

A true Southern staple. Our thinly sliced, pan-fried country ham delivers a rich, salty flavor that pairs perfectly with any side. A taste of tradition.

Hamburger Steak

A hearty, perfectly seasoned ground beef patty, grilled to your liking and served with a savory brown gravy. Pure comfort on a plate.

Brown Sugar Bacon Wrapped Chicken

Today's Special: Tender chicken breast wrapped in crispy bacon, glazed with sweet brown sugar, served over fluffy rice. Available after 4 PM!

Crispy Chicken Crunchwrap Slider

A fun, slider-sized crunchwrap featuring crispy all-white meat chicken nuggets, fresh lettuce, cheese, and a secret sauce, all wrapped in a crunchy tortilla.

Daily Vegetable Plate

Ask your server for today's selection of fresh, seasonal vegetables. A healthy and delicious way to enjoy the bounty of the season.
