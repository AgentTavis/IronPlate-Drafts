Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Memory.

We believe that many of life’s best memories are made by enjoying good food and drink with the people we love best. That's why we dreamed of creating a space where everyday moments of joy could be cultivated, right here in the heart of Goshen.

We imagined The Table as a welcoming haven within downtown Goshen's vibrant food culture. A place for families, friends, and neighbors to gather in a casual community setting, any night of the week. We pour our passion into every Italian-inspired dish, crafted to bring comfort and excitement to your plate.

So whether you're bringing the kids for a fun dinner, meeting old friends for a catch-up, or inviting your family to experience new flavors, our doors are always open. Come, share a laugh, a story, and a truly delicious meal in our welcoming space.

Our Flavors

Taste the Heart of Italian-Inspired Comfort

From classic pasta to hearty grills, our menu is crafted to delight every palate with fresh ingredients and a touch of Goshen's charm.

Classic Spaghetti & Meatballs

Our timeless recipe with slow-simmered marinara and house-made beef & pork meatballs.

Wood-Fired Margherita Pizza

Simple perfection with San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil, blistered in our oven.

Hearty Lasagna Bolognese

Layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, béchamel, and melted cheese, baked to golden perfection.

Grilled Chicken Parmesan

Juicy grilled chicken breast, topped with marinara and melted provolone, served with linguine.

Creamy Pesto Pasta

Al dente pasta tossed in a vibrant basil pesto cream sauce, finished with cherry tomatoes.

Fresh Caprese Salad

Ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, fragrant basil, drizzled with balsamic glaze.

Gourmet Burger & Fries

A juicy patty, premium toppings, served on a brioche bun with crispy seasoned fries.

Shrimp Scampi with Linguine

Succulent shrimp sautéed in garlic butter, white wine, and lemon, served over linguine.
