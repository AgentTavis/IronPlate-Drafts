Our Story

Where Good Food Meets Great Company.

Dianne's Dairy Center is more than just a restaurant; it's a cherished cornerstone of the Newton, NC community. For years, we've been dedicated to serving up delicious, wholesome bar and grill classics in a friendly, welcoming atmosphere.

We believe in simple pleasures: quality ingredients, a warm smile, and a place where families and friends can gather to create lasting memories. From our sizzling burgers to our satisfying sides, every dish is prepared with care and a touch of homegrown love.
