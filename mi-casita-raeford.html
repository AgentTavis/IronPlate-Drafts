Our Roots

Where Every Dish Tells a Story of Flavor and Family

Mi Casita Raeford isn't just a restaurant; it's a culinary journey to the heart of Mexico, right here in Raeford, NC. As a no-frills chain spot, we pride ourselves on delivering genuine, delicious Mexican cuisine without the fuss.

For years, we've been serving up generously portioned burritos, crispy tacos, and vibrant steak & seafood dishes, alongside a refreshing selection of drinks. Our philosophy is simple: use quality ingredients, cook with passion, and create an atmosphere where everyone feels like family.

Whether you're craving a quick lunch or a relaxed dinner, Mi Casita Raeford is the place where tradition meets taste, making every visit a memorable one. Come and discover why we're a local favorite!
