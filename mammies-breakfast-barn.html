Our Roots

A Taste of Home in Bakersville

Welcome to Mammie's Breakfast Barn, a cherished local diner nestled in the heart of Bakersville, NC. Since opening our doors, we've been dedicated to serving up warm, hearty breakfasts and delicious homemade meals that feel just like grandma used to make.

We're more than just a place to eat; we're a community gathering spot where friends and family come together to start their day right. From the aroma of freshly brewed coffee to the sizzle of our classic burgers, every visit promises a comforting experience.

At Mammie's, we proudly offer a welcoming atmosphere, complete with free off-street parking, comfortable seating, and friendly table service. Whether you're craving a robust breakfast, a satisfying homemade lunch, or just a burger and fries, we accept credit cards and offer convenient takeout options. Come see why we're Bakersville's favorite spot for delicious mornings!
