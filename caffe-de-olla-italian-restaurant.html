Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Taste in Robbinsville

Nestled in the heart of Robbinsville, Caffe De Olla embodies the rich culinary traditions of Italy. We're dedicated to crafting memorable dining experiences, focusing on fresh, quality ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations.

Our commitment is to bring the authentic taste and warmth of Italian hospitality to our community, making every visit a special occasion. From classic pasta dishes to unique regional favorites, we invite you to discover your next beloved meal with us.

Our Menu

Taste the Authentic Flavors of Italy

Ricotta Calzone

A classic Italian pocket of golden dough, generously filled with creamy ricotta cheese and savory spices.

Greek Salad

A refreshing blend of crisp greens, ripe tomatoes, cucumbers, olives, and feta, dressed with a zesty vinaigrette.

Steak Fajitas

A surprising but delicious offering! Sizzling steak strips with bell peppers and onions, seasoned to perfection.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Classic Roman pasta dish with eggs, hard cheese, cured pork (guanciale), and black pepper.

Margherita Pizza

A timeless favorite with fresh mozzarella, vibrant tomato sauce, and fragrant basil on a thin crust.

Chicken Parmesan
