Our Mission

More Than Just Coffee, It's a Message.

At Message Coffee, we believe in crafting moments of joy and connection. Nestled quaintly inside the USPS in Kernersville, we're a beloved local spot where every visit feels like coming home. Christine, our wonderful owner, pours her heart into every cup, ensuring a welcoming and friendly experience.

We're passionate about quality, which is why we proudly use rich Ghirardelli dark chocolate sauce for our heavenly mochas. For tea lovers, our loose-leaf cherry oolong tea is a made-to-order delight, bursting with flavor. And don't forget our freshly baked treats – from moist peanut butter chocolate brownies to flavorful pumpkin muffins with rich, spicy notes, there's always something to sweeten your day.
