Our Heritage

A Taste of Community, Crafted with Care.

Copper Penny Grill isn't just a place to eat; it's a cornerstone of the Forest City community. For years, we've been serving up classic bar & grill favorites with a unique local twist, creating a welcoming atmosphere where friends and family gather, stories are shared, and new memories are made.

From our sizzling burgers to our perfectly poured drinks, every item on our menu is prepared with passion and a commitment to quality. We believe in the power of good food to bring people together, and we're proud to be a beloved local spot where connection is found, not lost.
