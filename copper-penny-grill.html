Our Journey

The Heart of Forest City's Culinary Scene

Copper Penny Grill is more than just a restaurant; it's a beloved local spot in Forest City, NC, where friends and family gather for good times and even better food. Since opening our doors, we've poured our heart into creating a welcoming atmosphere, serving up classic bar and grill favorites with a unique Copper Penny twist. Join us and discover why over two thousand guests have made us their go-to for memorable meals and genuine connections.

We pride ourselves on using quality ingredients, crafting dishes that satisfy, and fostering a community spirit that makes every visit feel like coming home. Whether you're here for a quick lunch, a lively dinner, or just to unwind, we promise an experience that's truly local and authentically delicious.
