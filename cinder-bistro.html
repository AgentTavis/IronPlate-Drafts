OUR STORY

Rooted in Flavor

Welcome to Cinder Bistro, where mouthwatering BBQ and smoked meats are at the heart of everything we do. We're proud to bring Wilkes County a culinary experience that blends tradition with innovative new dishes. Every day, our brisket, chicken, and pork are seasoned and smoked to perfection, a testament to our commitment to flavor and quality.

But Cinder Bistro is more than just exceptional BBQ. We also offer an array of delectable new dishes complemented by a curated selection of craft beer, cocktails, and fine wines – perfect for any occasion. And beyond our restaurant walls, we're your premier catering service, ready to elevate any event with our exquisite cuisine and exceptional beverages. Join us today to discover why we're the top choice in Wilkes County for food and drinks!
