Our Story

Rooted in Tradition, Crafted with Passion

At Cinder Bistro, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a community staple bringing the heart and soul of BBQ to North Wilkesboro. Our journey began with a simple mission: to craft mouthwatering BBQ and smoked meats that taste like home.

Every day, our brisket, chicken, and pork are seasoned and smoked to perfection, slow-cooked with care to achieve that irresistible tender, smoky flavor. Beyond our classic BBQ, we're constantly innovating with an array of delectable new dishes and a curated selection of craft beer, cocktails, and fine wines to perfectly complement your meal.

And the excitement doesn't stop there – we're thrilled to announce that Cinder Bistro will soon be expanding to include our very own brewery! Whether you're dining with us or taking advantage of our premier catering service, we promise an exceptional culinary experience. Join us today and discover why we're the top choice in Wilkes County for food and drinks!
