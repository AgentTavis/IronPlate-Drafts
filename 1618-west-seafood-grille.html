Our Heritage

Our Passion, Our Promise: The 1618 West Story

At 1618 West Seafood Grille, we believe dining should be an experience. Nestled in the heart of Greensboro, NC, we've dedicated ourselves to crafting inventive seafood dishes that tantalize the palate and leave a lasting impression.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to combine the freshest catch with culinary artistry, all served in a sleek, airy setting that feels both refined and welcoming. Every plate is a testament to our commitment to quality, creativity, and the vibrant flavors of the sea.

Beyond the food, we've curated a large wine list, a selection of craft beers, and an array of signature cocktails designed to perfectly complement your meal. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a destination where memories are made, one exquisite dish at a time.
