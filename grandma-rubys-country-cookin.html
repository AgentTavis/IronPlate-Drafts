The Heart of Southern Comfort

At Grandma Ruby's, we believe in hearty meals, friendly faces, and prices that make you smile. Owner Dennis, a true Winston-Salem native, ensures that every plate leaving our kitchen carries the warmth and flavor of authentic country cooking.

Our team, like Jana, are here to make you feel right at home from the moment you step in, whether it's a quick cup of our great coffee or a delicious bacon, egg and cheese biscuit prepared just the way you like it. We're proud to be a local favorite, serving up simple, delicious food just like Grandma used to make.

Our Promise

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

Authentic Home-Style Cookin'

From our mouth-watering Philly Cheese Steak to the perfect bacon, egg and cheese biscuit, every dish is prepared with the love and tradition of Grandma's kitchen. We use classic recipes to bring you genuine Southern comfort.

Unbeatable Value & Quality

Our owner Dennis constantly hears, "how do you keep your prices so low?!" We believe delicious, quality food shouldn't break the bank. Enjoy generous portions and fresh ingredients without compromising your budget.

Warm & Welcoming Service

Walk in and immediately feel like family. Our team, like Jana, greets you with a smile and ensures your visit is comfortable and enjoyable, from your first cup of great coffee to your last bite. It's the local charm you deserve.
