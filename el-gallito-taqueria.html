Our Story

Bringing Authentic Mexican Flavors to Trinity

El Gallito Taqueria brings the vibrant flavors of Mexico right here to Trinity, NC. We specialize in serving authentic and flavorful tacos, burritos, and other traditional Mexican delights, crafted with passion and fresh ingredients. Every dish is a celebration of our heritage, designed to transport you straight to the heart of Mexico. Come taste the tradition!
