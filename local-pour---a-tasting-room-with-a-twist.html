Our Journey

Crafted for Connection. Poured for Pleasure.

At Local Pour, our mission is to provide a fun and friendly tasting room where guests can explore a variety of Michigan-made wine, beer, and spirits under one roof. We've created a welcoming space right here in Sawyer, NC, that feels like home, whether you're a seasoned connoisseur or just beginning your tasting journey.

We value every guest, no matter their tasting experience, and welcome the opportunity to make them feel at home while they learn and enjoy the wonderful products we offer. From complex whites to unique IPAs, every bottle and tap handle tells a story of Michigan's vibrant craft scene. Come, discover your new favorite.

WHITE WINES (White Cab Franc)

Veritas Estate Winery, South Haven, MI

Complex aromas of ripe stone fruit & sweet herbs combine with a great acid structure & underlying minerality.

Dry Traminette

Verterra Winery, Leland, MI

Dry, with a mineral backbone and restrained floral notes.

Dry Traminette

Domaine Berrien, Berrien Springs, MI

Fruity & floral aromas of rose & peach. Bright flavors of tangerine & apricot with a crisp finish.

Gewürztraminer & Riesling Blend

Lake Michigan Vintners, Benton Harbor, MI

Offers lush notes of lychee, peach & citrus blossom.

Pinot Blanc

Boathouse Winery, Leelanau, MI

Great citrus aromas. Creamy texture complemented by high acid & tropical, stone fruits.

Pinot Grigio

Lawton Ridge Winery, Kalamazoo, MI

Classic with balanced acidity. Green apple, citrus zest.

Cody Kresta Blend

Cody Kresta, Mattawan, MI

A blend of Traminette, Vidal Blanc, Riesling & White Cab Franc with flavors of peach & honey. Made in support of the Southwest Michigan Land Co.

Chardonnay

Chateau Fontaine, Lake Leelanau, MI

Our smoothest wine, with very light notes of citrus & spice. A great pairing wine for Polish cuisine & vegetables.

IPA - 7.5% ABV

Watermark Brewery, Stevensville, MI

Unique & fruity with nice balance. Tropical with a hint of pine.
