Our Story

Serving Hearty Southern Comfort by the Plateful.

Welcome to Carolina Girls Seafood and Grill, a true Beulaville original where the warmth of Southern hospitality meets the freshest flavors of the coast. While our space is cozy, our commitment to delicious, homestyle cooking is immense.

We believe in serving up generous portions of perfectly prepared seafood and grill favorites, crafted with care for lunch and dinner. Forget the frills; here, it's all about great food, a friendly face, and a meal that feels like coming home. We're proud to be a local spot where you can unwind, savor classic dishes, and enjoy the simple pleasure of good food.

"Carolina Girls is that unpretentious gem you always hope to find. Great food, friendly service, and a truly local feel. My go-to lunch spot!"

— A Loyal Beulaville Customer
