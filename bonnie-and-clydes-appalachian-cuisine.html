Our Heritage

A Taste of Home, Rooted in the Appalachians.

At Bonnie & Clyde's, we believe in serving more than just a meal; we offer an experience that embodies the heart and soul of Appalachian tradition. Nestled in the scenic beauty of Marshall, NC, our Bar & Grill takes pride in crafting dishes that blend authentic regional flavors with a touch of culinary artistry.

Our journey began with a simple passion: to bring the rich, comforting tastes of the mountains to your table. We source ingredients locally whenever possible, ensuring every bite tells a story of the land and its people. From hearty breakfast classics to satisfying lunch specials, each dish is prepared with care, reflecting a culinary heritage passed down through generations.

Join us for a relaxed atmosphere, friendly faces, and food that warms the soul. Whether you're a long-time local or just passing through, Bonnie & Clyde's is your destination for genuine Appalachian hospitality and unforgettable flavors.

Our Creations

Indulge in Appalachian Comfort & Culinary Delights.

Ham and Cheese Omelette

A classic breakfast favorite, fluffy eggs filled with savory ham and melted cheddar cheese, served with your choice of side.

Seasonal Salad

Fresh, crisp greens sourced from local farms, topped with seasonal vegetables and our house-made vinaigrette. A light and refreshing choice.

Mountain Seafood Platter

A delightful selection of locally inspired seafood, expertly prepared and seasoned to bring out the natural flavors. Ask your server for today's catch!

Classic Appalachian Burger

Juicy, grilled beef patty, fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles, served on a toasted bun with a side of crispy fries. A hearty classic.

Smoked Pulled Pork Sandwich

Slow-smoked pork, hand-pulled and piled high on a brioche bun with our signature BBQ sauce and coleslaw.

Buttermilk Fried Chicken

Crispy, golden-brown fried chicken, marinated in buttermilk and seasoned with our secret blend of spices. A true Southern comfort.

Biscuits & Gravy

Fluffy homemade biscuits smothered in rich, creamy sausage gravy. The perfect start to any Appalachian morning.
