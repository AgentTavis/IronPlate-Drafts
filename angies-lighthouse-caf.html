Our Story

Where Every Meal Feels Like Home

At Angie's Lighthouse Café, we believe in the simple joy of a home-cooked meal. Located in the heart of Cape Carteret, NC, we've been serving up real down-home cooking, just the way grandma made it, for years. Our doors are always open, offering a warm, welcoming environment where friends and family can gather, share stories, and savor dishes made with love.

We're more than just a diner; we're a cornerstone of the community, a place where memories are made over plates piled high with comfort food. From hearty breakfasts to satisfying lunches, every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and a sprinkle of coastal charm.
