Our Story

Taste the Heart of Chadbourn

Nell's Chophouse & Catering is more than just a place to eat; it's a cherished local tradition in Chadbourn, NC. We’ve been serving up hearty meals and warm hospitality, becoming a beloved spot where friends and family gather.

From our sizzling Hibachi specials to classic Bar & Grill favorites, every dish is crafted with care and a touch of Southern comfort. We believe in great food, great service, and creating a memorable experience for every guest who walks through our doors or orders for delivery.
