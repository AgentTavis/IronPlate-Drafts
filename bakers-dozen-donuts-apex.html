Our Story

More Than Just Donuts: A Sweet Apex Tradition Begins

Don't want to drive all the way to Cary to get doughnuts? No sweat! Go to Apex and get your Baker's Dozen fix right here instead. We're thrilled to bring the beloved Baker's Dozen experience directly to our Apex community.

Our service is always nice, and we pride ourselves on being very accommodating. We love seeing our customers leave with a smile! We even have some doughnuts that aren't filled, allowing you to get them filled with whatever your heart desires – fresh and personalized, just for you.

Take our famous Bear Claw, for instance. It's almost too darn cute to eat! We believe you eat with your eyes first, and our treats reflect the time and care we put into every single one. But don't worry, the taste is even better than they look. We've seen countless happy customers, including one who gulped it down within seconds and exclaimed it was incredible, immediately asking for more!

"Absolutely wonderful donuts, and the staff is so friendly! Being able to get them filled fresh is a game-changer. So happy Baker's Dozen is finally in Apex!"

— A Happy Local, Apex Resident
