Our Heritage

Taste the Tradition

Breakfastime Jonesville is a cherished local diner in Jonesville, NC, serving up classic American comfort food. From hearty breakfasts to satisfying lunches and brunches, everything is made from scratch with love and dedication to quality ingredients.

We're proud to offer vegetarian-friendly options and a welcoming atmosphere perfect for families, complete with highchairs and friendly table service. Our mission is to provide a place where everyone feels at home, enjoying delicious, wholesome meals that remind them of simpler times.

Come experience why we've been a local favorite for years, where every plate tells a story and every visit creates a warm memory.

Our Offerings

Made-From-Scratch Favorites.

Classic Pancakes

Fluffy golden pancakes, griddled to perfection and served with warm syrup. A timeless breakfast delight.

Hearty Omelets

Build your own with fresh eggs and a variety of wholesome fillings, cooked just the way you like it.

Southern Biscuits & Gravy

Warm, flaky biscuits smothered in our savory homemade sausage gravy. Pure comfort on a plate.

Breakfast Burritos

Packed with scrambled eggs, cheese, and your choice of meat and veggies, wrapped in a warm tortilla.

Signature Burgers

Juicy, hand-pattied burgers grilled to your liking, served on a toasted bun with fresh toppings.

Fresh Salads

Crisp greens and vibrant vegetables with your choice of protein, dressed with our homemade vinaigrettes.

A comforting classic: gooey melted cheese between buttery toast, paired with a cup of rich tomato soup.
