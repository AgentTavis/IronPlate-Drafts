Our Roots

Where Every Bite Feels Like Home.

Welcome to C&C Cafe, the heart of Germantown, NC's culinary scene. We're more than just a cafe; we're a dream brought to life by a local family with a passion for good food and great company. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a cozy spot where everyone feels like family, enjoying honest, delicious food made with love.

Every morning, the aroma of freshly baked pastries fills our cafe, a testament to our commitment to quality and tradition. From the moment you step in, you're not just a customer, but a cherished guest. We believe that exceptional customer service is just as important as the food on your plate, and we strive to make every visit memorable.

"C&C Cafe is truly a gem! The moment we walked in, we felt like we were visiting old friends. The service was absolutely wonderful, and the warm atmosphere made our morning even better. A true mom and pop experience in every sense!"

— A Happy Local, Germantown Resident

Our Offerings

Cakesicles

Our signature cakesicles are a delightful fusion of cake and popsicle, beautifully decorated and perfect for any occasion. A sweet treat that's as fun to look at as it is to eat!

Lychee Sleeping Bear Cake

A unique and adorable creation, our Lychee Sleeping Bear Cake combines the delicate sweetness of lychee with a light, fluffy cake. A truly whimsical dessert experience.
