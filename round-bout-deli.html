Our Story

Where Good Food & Good Times Meet

Welcome to Round 'Bout Deli, Murphy's beloved diner, serving up classic comfort food with a smile. We believe in hearty meals, friendly faces, and a warm atmosphere where everyone feels like family. From sizzling breakfast platters to satisfying deli sandwiches, every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and a touch of homestyle care.

Nestled right on Tennessee Street, we've been a go-to spot for locals and visitors alike, offering a cozy escape and delicious food that hits just right. Come on in, grab a booth, and let us make your day a little brighter.
