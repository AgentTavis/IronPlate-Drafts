Our Story

Taste the Future of Flavor in Hickory, NC.

Welcome to Plant Based Boss, Hickory's pioneering fully plant-based restaurant! We're not just serving food; we're crafting a new culinary experience, proving that plant-based can be incredibly delicious, satisfying, and familiar.

From savory "Goin Ham" sandwiches to hearty "BBQ Beef" wraps, vibrant "Olmec Bowls," and refreshing smoothies, every dish is prepared with passion and a commitment to flavor. Join the growing community who've rated us 4.9 stars for our innovative menu and warm, local atmosphere.

Find us at 104 2nd Ave NW, open Wednesday to Saturday, ready to serve your new favorite plant-powered meal!
