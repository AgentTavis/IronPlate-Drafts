Our Story

Two Years of Freshness & Community

Nestled in the heart of Nags Head, Dune Street Rawbar & Grill has been a beloved neighborhood staple for two wonderful years. We're open year-round, Tuesday through Saturday, serving up the freshest flavors of the Outer Banks.

Our commitment runs deep – we proudly support local fishermen, ensuring that every dish on your plate is as fresh as it gets. From our raw bar selections to our grilled specialties, you can taste the difference that local, quality ingredients make.

Thank you for celebrating two fantastic years with us! We invite you to join our growing family of loyal customers and experience the warm, friendly atmosphere that makes Dune Street a true local favorite.

"Dune Street has become our go-to spot in Nags Head. The food is consistently amazing, and you can feel the genuine care they put into everything. Truly a gem!"
— A Happy Local
