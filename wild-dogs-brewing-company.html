Our Journey

Crafting Community, One Brew at a Time

Nestled in the heart of Old Town Sanford, Wild Dogs Brewing Company was born from a simple passion: to create exceptional craft beer and a welcoming space where everyone feels at home—including their furry best friends! We believe that good company and great beer go hand-in-paw, which is why our doors are always open for you and your beloved canine companion.

Our journey began with a dream of building a local spot that celebrates both artisanal brewing and the vibrant community spirit of Sanford. Every batch of our beer is crafted with care, using quality ingredients and a commitment to flavor that stands out. We're more than just a brewery and grill; we're a gathering place, a local hang-out, and a testament to the simple joy of sharing good times.

From the moment you walk in, you'll experience our dedication to hospitality and a relaxed atmosphere where you can unwind. We're proud to be a part of Sanford's story, and we invite you to become a part of ours. Come for the beer, stay for the good vibes, and bring your dog for an unforgettable experience!
