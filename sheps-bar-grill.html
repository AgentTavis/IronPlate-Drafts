Our Roots

More Than Just Drinks, It's a Community.

Welcome to Shep's Bar & Grill, your cherished neighborhood spot in Mint Hill, NC. We believe a great bar is more than just a place to grab a drink – it's a hub for friends, family, and good times. For years, we've poured passion into every pint and mixed memories into every cocktail, establishing ourselves as a true local institution.

Step inside and you'll always find a friendly face and a lively atmosphere, whether you're a regular or visiting for the first time. We're proud to offer a wide selection of ice-cold beers on tap, ensuring there's always something fresh and delicious to quench your thirst. From a straightforward, perfectly poured shot of whiskey to a refreshing pint of your favorite brew, our extensive range of classic spirits has your drink of choice waiting.

At Shep's, it's about connecting, unwinding, and enjoying the simple pleasures of good company and great drinks. We're dedicated to being your go-to bar where quality beverages and a warm, inviting atmosphere always come first.
