The Place

Owner-operated, the old way.

Duke's Pub is the kind of bar your dad would have taken you to. Owner-operated, family-run, built on first names. By the second visit, somebody behind the bar already knows what you're drinking.

No kitchen, on purpose. The room stays loose, the conversation stays louder, and Sundays in football season come with free food on the house. Bring food in or order from down the street. Nobody minds.

Nine-plus rotating taps, a wine list worth talking about, a full bar. Bingo on weeknights. Live music on weekends. Football on Sundays. Friends on every stool.

Reminds me of a local Cheers Bar where everyone knows your name. Robbin S. · Yelp Elite
