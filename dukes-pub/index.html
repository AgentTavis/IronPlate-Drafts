What We Are

Three things, done honestly.

No kitchen, no pretense, no corporate playbook. Duke's is a pub in the original sense of the word: a public house where regulars and first-timers sit next to each other and sort the world out over a good pour.

01

A Real Taproom

Nine-plus rotating taps. Hazy IPAs, amber lagers, a fruited wheat, a proper cider. Wine, whiskey, and full liquor too. We update the lineup monthly because that's how you keep it interesting.

Beer · Wine · Cocktails
02

Greensboro's Sports Bar

NFL Sunday is the headline. Multiple TVs, plenty of seating, free food the whole crowd shares. Keno on the side if you feel lucky. Bingo on weeknights when the season takes a breath.

NFL · Keno · Bingo
03

A Community, Not a Concept

Duke and Claire own it, work it, and remember faces. Pet friendly. Smoke friendly. Walk-in welcome. A few reviewers have called it the Cheers of Greensboro and we have not been able to shake the comparison.

Owner Operated
Game Day & Live Events

Sunday's the main event.

From the first kickoff in September through the Super Bowl, Duke's is where Greensboro watches football. Every other night of the week has its own thing, too.

Sundays in Football Season

NFL Sundays. Free food on the house.

Every Sunday during the NFL season we put out free food for the crowd. Get there early for a good seat, pick your team's TV, and settle in for an honest day of football and beer.

Headline Event
