From the September kickoff through the Super Bowl, Sundays at Duke's mean football, free food, and a room full of people who actually care who wins.

The Headline

NFL Sundays. Free food.

Every Sunday during football season we put out free food for the crowd. No catch, no buy-in. Show up early, pick your team's TV, settle in for an honest day of football and beer.

Every Sunday · Football Season

The Sunday rules.

Doors open at noon. Multiple TVs running every game in the slate. Plenty of seating, indoor and on the patio when the weather agrees. Free food laid out for the room. Keno on the side if you feel lucky between drives.
