Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community.

Starving Squirrel is a cozy eatery nestled in Elk Park NC offering a warm and inviting atmosphere. Known for its delicious comfort food, the menu features a delightful array of dishes crafted with care and local ingredients. We believe in creating a space where everyone feels at home, from morning coffee enthusiasts to families enjoying dinner.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to bring people together over great food and genuine hospitality. Every dish on our menu tells a story, a blend of traditional flavors and a touch of mountain charm. Come in, relax, and become part of our growing family. We're proud to be a gathering spot for friends, neighbors, and visitors alike.

The Starving Squirrel Menu

Classic Cheeseburger

Juicy beef patty, melted cheddar, crisp lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles on a toasted bun.

Crispy Chicken Tenders

Golden-fried chicken tenders served with your choice of dipping sauce and a side of fries.

Hearty Chili Bowl

Our signature chili, slow-cooked with beans, beef, and a blend of spices. Topped with cheese and onions.

Southern Fried Catfish

Cornmeal-crusted catfish fried to perfection, served with hushpuppies and creamy coleslaw.

Signature BBQ Ribs

Slow-smoked pork ribs smothered in our tangy BBQ sauce, served with a side of mashed potatoes.

Warm Apple Crumble

Sweet baked apples topped with a buttery oat crumble, served warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
