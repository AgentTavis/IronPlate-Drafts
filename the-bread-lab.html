Our Story

More Than Just Bread, It's a Community Hub.

Welcome to The Bread Lab, formerly Lily's, now proudly nestled within Pioneer Coffee Company. We're passionate about bringing freshly baked goodness and warm hospitality to the heart of Shelby, NC.

Our establishment has recently undergone a beautiful renovation, creating a clean, well-decorated, and inviting space perfect for your morning brew, a delightful pastry, or a leisurely lunch. While it can get lively with more people, our friendly team, like Daniel, is dedicated to serving you kindly and promptly.

We believe in quality, from our excellent coffee to our irresistible pastries, like the must-try cream cheese cinnamon muffin, and our refreshing ice cream. Every visit to The Bread Lab, inside Pioneer Coffee Company, promises a delightful experience. Located conveniently in Uptown Shelby, we're confident you won't be disappointed. We can't wait to welcome you back!
