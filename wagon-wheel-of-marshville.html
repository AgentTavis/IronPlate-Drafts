Our Heritage

Experience Homestyle Comfort at Wagon Wheel

Wagon Wheel Restaurant is more than just a place to eat – it's a cornerstone of the Marshville community, known for its warm hospitality and unbeatable value. We pride ourselves on serving delicious, hearty meals that feel just like home, without breaking the bank.

Since opening our doors, we've been dedicated to offering a dining experience where everyone feels welcome. Our all-you-can-eat lunch buffet is a local legend, featuring a rotating selection of two savory meats, a generous array of fresh vegetables, and a full salad bar, all complemented by a drink and dessert for one incredible price.

We believe great food and friendly faces are the recipe for a truly memorable meal. Come discover why Wagon Wheel is the cherished gathering spot for families and friends across Marshville!
