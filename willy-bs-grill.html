Our Roots

The Story Behind Boone's Best Burgers

Willy B's Grill started as a simple idea: bring Boone, NC, the kind of smash burger that makes you stop and savor every bite. We pour our heart and soul into every patty, every fresh bun, and every perfectly caramelized onion. We believe in quality ingredients, a passion for grilling, and serving up genuine smiles with every order.

What began as a passion project, making a trip up here to see what these burgers were all about, quickly became a local sensation. Our commitment to crafting the "best burgers you ever had" isn't just a tagline, it's our promise. Come taste the difference that dedication makes!
