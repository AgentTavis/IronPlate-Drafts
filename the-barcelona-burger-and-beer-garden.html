OUR STORY

Crafted with Passion, Served with Pride.

At The Barcelona Burger And Beer Garden, we believe that great food and great company go hand-in-hand. Nestled in a vibrant, warehouselike space in the heart of Mooresville, NC, we've created a unique dining experience where the atmosphere is as inviting as the food is delicious.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to elevate the classic bar and grill concept. We combine contemporary culinary techniques with a laid-back garden vibe, serving up handcrafted burgers, succulent rotisserie chicken, and an impressive selection of craft beers. Every dish is prepared with fresh, quality ingredients and a commitment to flavor that keeps our guests coming back for more.

From a casual lunch to a lively evening out, The Barcelona Burger And Beer Garden is more than just a meal – it’s a destination where friends and family gather to create lasting memories. Join us and discover why we're a Mooresville favorite!
