Our Story

Crafting Joy, One Fresh Bake at a Time

At Sugarloaf Island Bakery, we believe in the simple magic of freshly baked goods. Nestled in the heart of downtown Morehead City, our artisan bakery is a true labor of love, dedicated to bringing exquisite flavors and heartwarming treats to our community.

We pour passion into every item, from our delicate croissants to our decadent custom cakes. It’s more than just baking; it’s about creating moments of pure delight, whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply treating yourself to a daily indulgence.

We're proud to be a local establishment, rooted in the rich traditions of coastal North Carolina, and committed to baking the very best for our neighbors and visitors alike.
