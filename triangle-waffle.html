Our Story

From Our Family to Yours, A Taste of Tradition.

Welcome to Triangle Waffle, a beloved local spot serving Dunn, NC, with a unique blend of warm hospitality and vibrant Mexican flavors. For years, we've been crafting dishes that bring joy to our community, from savory breakfast classics to authentic Mexican specialties.

We're more than just a restaurant; we're a place where friends and family gather, make memories, and savor every bite in a casual, welcoming atmosphere. Come experience the taste of tradition and the warmth of home right here in Dunn.

Our Menu

Savor the Vibrant Flavors of Triangle Waffle.

Chilaquiles Rojas

Crispy tortilla chips simmered in a rich red sauce, topped with queso fresco, crema, and a perfectly fried egg.

Huevos Rancheros

Two fried eggs served on warm corn tortillas with our zesty ranchero sauce, refried beans, and fresh avocado.

Breakfast Burrito Supreme

A hearty blend of scrambled eggs, potatoes, melted cheese, and your choice of savory chorizo or crispy bacon, all wrapped in a warm tortilla.

Al Pastor Tacos (3)

Succulent marinated pork, grilled pineapple, fresh cilantro, and diced onions on authentic warm corn tortillas.

Veggie Fajita Bowl

Sizzling medley of colorful bell peppers, tender onions, and earthy mushrooms, served over rice with black beans and creamy guacamole.

Mexican Waffle Delight

Golden waffle infused with cinnamon, generously topped with luscious dulce de leche, fresh berries, and a dollop of whipped cream.
