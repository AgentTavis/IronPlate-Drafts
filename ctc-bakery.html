Our Story

Heartfelt Flavors, Local Roots

CTC Bakery is a beloved local spot in Wake Forest, NC. We're more than just a Bar And Grill; we're a cherished community hub where friends and family gather to enjoy good company, great food, and a vibrant atmosphere. Established 5 years ago, we've dedicated ourselves to providing an unforgettable experience with every visit.

Our passion for delicious, comforting food and a welcoming environment drives everything we do. From our hearty offerings to our friendly service, we strive to make every guest feel right at home. Come join us and discover why we're a favorite gathering place in Wake Forest!

Our Offerings

A Taste of What We Create

While our specific menu items change seasonally to bring you the freshest flavors, here's a glimpse into the types of delicious categories you can expect from our kitchen.

Appetizers & Starters

Perfect for sharing, our appetizers are crafted to ignite your palate and start your meal right. Think savory bites and comforting classics.

Signature Grill Selections

Experience the heart of our Bar And Grill with expertly prepared meats and fresh ingredients, grilled to perfection and served with wholesome sides.

Hearty Sandwiches & Burgers

Generous portions of classic comfort, from juicy burgers to stacked sandwiches, all made with premium ingredients and our special touch.

Fresh Salads & Lighter Bites

For those seeking something lighter yet flavorful, our salads feature crisp greens, vibrant vegetables, and delicious homemade dressings.

Crafted Beverages & Local Brews

Quench your thirst with our selection of refreshing drinks, including local craft beers and expertly mixed non-alcoholic options.

Sweet Endings (Desserts)

Conclude your meal with one of our delightful desserts, baked fresh and designed to satisfy your sweet cravings.
