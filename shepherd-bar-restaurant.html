Our Legacy

A Tradition of Culinary Excellence in Shepherd

At Shepherd Bar & Restaurant, we pride ourselves on delivering an unparalleled fine dining experience rooted in local tradition. Our culinary journey is a celebration of exquisite flavors, none more celebrated than our tender, perfectly seasoned prime rib.

This local favorite, served with all the classic fixings, is just one reason why our friends keep coming back. We're dedicated to crafting memorable moments around the table, combining impeccable service with a warm, inviting atmosphere right here in Shepherd, NC.
