Our Heritage

Crafting Moments, One Macaron at a Time

Nestled in the heart of Birkdale Village, Macaron Espresso Bar is more than just a shop—it's a destination for exquisite tastes and delightful experiences. We believe in the magic of simple pleasures: the perfect crunch of a freshly baked macaron, the rich aroma of expertly brewed espresso, and the warmth of community.

Born from a passion for authentic French-inspired pastries and premium coffee, we handcraft every macaron with precision and care, using only the finest ingredients. Our espresso beans are carefully selected and roasted to perfection, ensuring every cup delivers a rich, satisfying experience.

Come for the sweets, stay for the cozy atmosphere. We’re proud to be a local spot where friends gather, families make memories, and every visit feels like a little escape. Welcome to our family.

Our Offerings

Explore Our Delightful Menu

Classic Macaron Assortment

A timeless selection featuring our most loved flavors: Vanilla Bean, Rich Chocolate, Tart Raspberry, and Nutty Pistachio. Perfect for every palate.

Seasonal Macaron Collection

Experience new sensations with our rotating seasonal flavors, crafted with fresh, inspiring ingredients. Ask about today's unique selections!

Espresso

A concentrated shot of rich, aromatic coffee. Experience the pure essence of our premium beans. Available as single or double.

Latte

Our smooth espresso blended with steamed milk and a delicate layer of foam. Customize with Classic, Vanilla, Caramel, or Hazelnut syrup.

Cappuccino

A perfectly balanced drink with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and a generous cap of frothed milk. A true coffee classic.

Americano

A simple yet elegant coffee made by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to brewed coffee.

Freshly Brewed Coffee

Our daily selection of premium coffee, brewed to perfection. The ideal choice for a comforting and reliable coffee experience.

Butter Croissant

Flaky, buttery, and baked to golden perfection. A quintessential French pastry, perfect on its own or alongside your coffee.

Chocolate Croissant

Our classic butter croissant filled with rich, dark chocolate. A delightful treat for any time of day.

Our Promise

Why Macaron Espresso Bar Stands Out

Artisan Macarons

Every macaron is a tiny work of art, handcrafted daily with premium almond flour, fresh fruits, and gourmet chocolates. Experience a symphony of authentic flavors and delicate textures that transport you to Paris.

Premium Espresso

Our baristas are passionate about coffee, expertly pulling rich espresso shots and crafting lattes with precision. We source quality beans to ensure every cup delivers an unparalleled aromatic experience, from your first sip to the last.

Cozy Birkdale Vibe

Located in the vibrant Birkdale Village, our shop offers a welcoming and cozy atmosphere. It's the perfect spot for a quiet moment, a lively chat with friends, or a delightful break in your day. Enjoy outdoor seating too!
