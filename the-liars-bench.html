Our Heritage

Where Great Food & Good Company Meet

At The Liar's Bench, we believe that great food is worth the wait. Nestled in the heart of Lansing, we've cultivated a reputation for serving up unforgettable bar & grill classics with a personal touch. As one happy customer put it, "We had six people and all had different stuff but everyone loved their sandwiches."

We pride ourselves on a 'no rush, no slap together' philosophy, where every dish, from our hearty sandwiches to our delectable sides, is crafted with care by our dedicated chef. It’s this commitment to quality that makes simple dishes extraordinary – like our BLT, praised for its 'tomato slice every bit thicker than 1/2 inch.'

Come for the food, stay for the genuine hospitality. We might take our time, but we promise, the wait is always well worth it. It's more than just a meal; it's an experience, a place where stories are shared and memories are made, one delicious bite at a time.

"I must say with a group, patience is required as there are only two people working and the chef makes sure it is right, no rush, or slap together sandwiches. If you have to wait, the wait is well worth it." – Kelli, A Valued Customer
