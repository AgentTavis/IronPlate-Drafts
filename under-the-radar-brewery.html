Our Journey

Brewing Community, One Pint at a Time

Under the Radar Brewery began with a simple vision: to create a welcoming space where friends, family, and neighbors could gather over exceptional craft beer and delicious pub fare. Founded by Calvin Woof in 2018, our passion for brewing unique flavors quickly blossomed into a Houston, NC institution.

We believe great beer brings people together. Our dedication to quality ingredients, innovative recipes, and a friendly atmosphere has made us a beloved local spot. Every pour, every plate, and every laugh shared within our walls is a testament to the community we’ve built.

Our Selections

Handcrafted Brews & Delicious Pub Fare

While our full menu updates frequently with seasonal specials, here are some of the classics we're famous for.

The UTR Signature Burger

A juicy, seasoned patty, perfectly grilled, topped with aged cheddar, crispy bacon, fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, and our secret sauce, all on a toasted brioche bun.

Rotating Craft Beer Flight

Experience the best of Under the Radar with a flight of four 5oz pours from our ever-changing tap list. From hoppy IPAs to rich stouts, there's always something new to discover.

Spicy Chicken Tacos

Three soft tortillas filled with grilled, spiced chicken, crisp slaw, pickled red onions, a drizzle of avocado crema, and a sprinkle of fresh cilantro. A flavor explosion!

Brewery Pretzel with Beer Cheese

A giant, warm, soft pretzel, salted and baked to perfection, served with a generous side of our house-made craft beer cheese dip. The ultimate beer companion.

Seasonal Salad Bowl

Fresh, locally sourced greens tossed with seasonal vegetables, nuts, cheeses, and a light vinaigrette. A refreshing option for a lighter meal.

Crispy Fish & Chips

Flaky white fish, beer-battered and fried golden brown, served with a generous portion of seasoned fries and tangy tartar sauce. A British classic done right.
