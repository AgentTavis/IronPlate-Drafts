Our Story

Where Every Pour Tells a Tale of Community and Craft.

Crafty Beer, Wine & Spirits isn't just a bar; it's a vibrant hub where friends, neighbors, and their furry companions gather to unwind. Owners Wendy and Glen envisioned a space that feels like an extension of your own living room – friendly, welcoming, and always ready with a great drink.

From the moment you step in, you're greeted by a friendly crowd and a genuine neighborhood pub vibe. We're proud to offer a diverse selection, focusing on the best national and international craft beers, with a special emphasis on the incredible brews from right here in North Carolina.

“Wendy and Glen are the owners and absolutely delightful. This neighborhood bar has a friendly crowd of locals that welcomed us at the door. We will be back!”

Wendy H. (Business Owner, via customer review)
