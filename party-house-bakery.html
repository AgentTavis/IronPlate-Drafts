Our Story

More Than Just a Restaurant, It's a Home.

At Party House & Bakery, we believe in bringing people together through great food and warm hospitality. For years, we've been Kannapolis' go-to spot for everything from hearty bar & grill classics to exquisitely crafted custom cakes. We're a family-run business with a passion for quality ingredients and a commitment to creating memorable experiences for every guest.

Whether you're stopping by for a casual meal, celebrating a special occasion with one of our famous cakes, or gathering with friends for a good time, you'll find a welcoming atmosphere and a team dedicated to making your visit perfect. We're proud to be a part of the Kannapolis community and look forward to serving you for many more years to come.

Taste the Tradition

Classic Cheeseburger

Homemade Meatloaf

Our comfort classic, slow-baked and smothered in savory gravy, served with mashed potatoes and green beans.

Fried Chicken Platter

Signature BBQ Ribs

Fresh Garden Salad
