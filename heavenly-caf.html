Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Mt Airy Tradition.

At Heavenly Café, we believe that great food starts with passion and a touch of local magic. Nestled right in the heart of Mt Airy, we've quickly become the town's go-to spot for an unforgettable breakfast and lunch experience.

What's our secret? It's simple: quality ingredients, a dedication to flavor, and a desire to bring a unique culinary delight to our community. Many rave about our bagels – water-boiled and perfected right here in Mt Airy. Who would've thought?

Our "pro tip"? You absolutely must try the Everything Bagel loaded with scallion cream cheese, crispy bacon, and fresh tomato. But don't stop there! We're always experimenting, always innovating, always bringing you new flavors of yummyness. Just ask what's new and exciting!
