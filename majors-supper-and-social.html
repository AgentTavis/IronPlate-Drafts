Our Story

Taste the Fusion, Feel the Local Charm

Where Culinary Art Meets Local Heart

Nestled in the heart of Valdese, Major's Supper and Social is more than just a bar and grill — it's a culinary journey. We pride ourselves on serving delicious Asian fusion dishes, meticulously crafted with the freshest local ingredients sourced right here in North Carolina.

Our passion is to create a welcoming atmosphere where friends and family can gather, enjoy exceptional food, and make lasting memories. Every dish tells a story, blending traditional Asian flavors with a modern twist, reflecting our commitment to quality and innovation.

Our Menu

Savor the Flavors: Asian Fusion Delights

Experience our innovative take on Asian cuisine, crafted with fresh, seasonal ingredients. While our menu frequently rotates, below are illustrative examples of the unique dishes you might find.

Spicy Noodle Delight

A vibrant medley of noodles, crisp vegetables, and your choice of protein, tossed in our signature spicy sauce. A true flavor explosion!

Lemongrass Chicken Skewers

Tender chicken marinated in aromatic lemongrass and grilled to perfection. Served with a refreshing dipping sauce.

Crispy Spring Rolls

Hand-rolled to perfection, these crispy spring rolls are filled with fresh vegetables and served with a sweet chili sauce.

Major's Fried Rice

Our special house fried rice, packed with savory flavors, fresh vegetables, and your choice of delicious additions.

Coconut Curry Nirvana

A rich and creamy coconut curry with tender vegetables and your preferred protein, perfectly spiced for a comforting meal.

Ginger Beef Stir-Fry

Succulent slices of beef stir-fried with fresh ginger, garlic, and an array of seasonal vegetables in a savory sauce.
