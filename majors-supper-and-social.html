OUR STORY

Where Valdese Gathers for Unforgettable Flavors

Our Saturday Spotlight this week is Major's Supper Social in Valdese, NC. We're proud to bring delicious Asian fusion to our community, made with fresh local ingredients. Whether you're looking for a memorable night out, a delightful family dinner, or simply unwinding with friends, Major's is the place to be.

At Major's, we believe in crafting experiences as much as we craft exceptional food. Our kitchen is a labor of love, bringing together traditional Asian techniques with the freshest local produce to create dishes that surprise and delight every palate. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a gathering place where great food and good company create lasting memories.

OUR MENU

A Fusion of Flavors, Crafted with Care

Signature Stir-fry

A vibrant mix of fresh seasonal vegetables and your choice of protein, wok-tossed in our house-made savory sauce.

Crispy Spring Rolls

Hand-rolled with tender vegetables and glass noodles, fried until golden brown, served with our sweet chili dipping sauce.

Spicy Noodle Bowl

Authentic noodles in a rich, aromatic spicy broth, topped with tender protein, fresh herbs, and a hint of lime.

Savory Dumplings

Choose from steamed or pan-fried, filled with a delightful blend of seasoned pork and cabbage, served with ginger soy.

Aromatic Curry

Creamy coconut curry slow-simmered with tender chicken or tofu, potatoes, and exotic spices, served with jasmine rice.

Specialty Rice Dish

Fragrant jasmine rice infused with unique Asian flavors, fresh vegetables, and your choice of grilled shrimp or chicken.
