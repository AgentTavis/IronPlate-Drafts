Our Roots, Our Passion

Crafting Belmont's Signature Tastes

At Belmonts Specialty Foods, we're more than just a bar and grill; we're a cornerstone of the Belmont community. For years, we've prided ourselves on bringing together the finest flavors and freshest ingredients, specializing in exquisite deli fine meats, cheeses, and handcrafted sandwiches that satisfy every craving.

Our partnership with Clifton's Deli ensures that every bite is a testament to quality and tradition. From a casual lunch with friends to unwinding after a long day, Belmonts offers a welcoming atmosphere where good food and great company come together.

We're constantly striving to enhance your experience. As we undergo a major site overhaul, we appreciate your patience and invite you to (704) 829-8063 or email luke@shopbelmonts.com for any information. We're building a better way to connect with the Belmonts you love!
