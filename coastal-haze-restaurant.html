Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal: Our Veteran-Owned Story

Coastal Haze Restaurant, nestled in the heart of Cape Carteret, is a testament to the idea that the best experiences often come from unassuming places. What started as a vision for a local spot with incredible food and a welcoming atmosphere has blossomed into a community favorite.

We're proud to be a veteran-owned and family-operated establishment, pouring our hearts into every dish and every interaction. Our team believes in serving up not just fantastic grub, but also genuine hospitality, making sure every guest feels like family from the moment they walk through our doors.

From the quick, friendly service to the "phenomenal" flavors that keep people coming back, Coastal Haze is dedicated to creating memorable dining experiences that truly hit the spot.

Our Dishes

Signature Flavors You Can't Miss

Drip Burger

Our juicy burger, loaded with savory toppings and a secret sauce that drips with flavor. A true crowd-pleaser.

Shrimp Tacos

Fresh, perfectly seasoned shrimp nestled in soft tortillas with crisp slaw and a zesty crema. A taste of the coast!

Pierogis
