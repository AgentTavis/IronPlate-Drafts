Our Heritage

Our Family's Passion, Served Fresh Daily.

At Zoe's Place, we bring you the comfort and joy of Italian cuisine, served with the same warm hospitality that defines Zoës Kitchen – a fast-casual Mediterranean restaurant known for its distinct menu of fresh made Mediterranean dishes. We believe in creating a welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels like family, enjoying dishes crafted with love and tradition right here in Columbus, NC.

We're proud to be a cornerstone of the Columbus community, serving memorable meals and fostering connections around our tables. From classic pasta dishes to flavorful sandwiches, our menu is designed to delight every palate with fresh ingredients and authentic tastes.
