Our Story

Crafting Connections, One Sip at a Time.

At Social Habit, we believe in more than just serving great food and drinks; we're about creating an inviting atmosphere where memories are made and friendships flourish. Nestled in the heart of Kernersville, we've poured our passion into building the town's premier cocktail lounge, a true local gem.

From the moment you step in, you'll feel the warmth of our hospitality. Our skilled bartenders are artists, mixing innovative cocktails like our famous Cinnamon Bourbon Smash, while our kitchen serves up delicious grill favorites designed to satisfy every craving. We're open 7 days a week, because every day is a good day to connect, unwind, and enjoy the Social Habit experience.
