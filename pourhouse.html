Our Legacy

More Than a Meal: A Port Townsend Tradition

Pourhouse is more than just a Bar And Grill; it's a beloved local spot in Port Townsend, NC, where friends gather and good memories are made. We're proud to be a cornerstone of the community, serving up delicious food and refreshing drinks in a welcoming atmosphere. From casual weeknights to lively weekends, Pourhouse is where everyone feels at home.

We believe in good food, great company, and making every visit special. Our team is dedicated to creating an experience that keeps you coming back, whether it's for our flavorful dishes, our extensive drink selection, or simply the warm, friendly vibe that defines us.
