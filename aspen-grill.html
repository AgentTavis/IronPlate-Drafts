Our Story

Bringing Authentic Flavors to Lincolnton

Nestled in the heart of Lincolnton, Aspen Grill has become the go-to destination for those craving delicious, quick, and affordable Tex-Mex. We pride ourselves on serving up excellent tacos, hearty burritos, and so much more, crafted with fresh ingredients and a passion for flavor.

But we're not just about Tex-Mex! Our kitchen has perfected its craft, and our burgers are now better than ever, making us a versatile choice for any craving. We believe in great food, friendly service, and a welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels like family.
