Our Story

Serving Lincolnton's Flavorful Favorites Since Day One.

Welcome to Aspen Grill, your go-to destination in Lincolnton for quick, affordable, and incredibly delicious Tex-Mex and burgers! We started with a simple vision: to create a friendly local spot where families and friends could gather for great food without breaking the bank.

From sizzling tacos and hearty burritos to our famously improved burgers, every dish is prepared with care and packed with flavor. We believe in fresh ingredients, a warm atmosphere, and making every visit a memorable one. Come see why Lincolnton loves Aspen Grill!
