Our Journey

Taste the Heart of Bryson City

Nestled in the heart of Bryson City, Iron Wok is more than just a Bar & Grill – it's a community hub where delicious food, refreshing drinks, and good company come together. We're proud to serve locals and visitors alike, offering a cozy atmosphere and a menu crafted with passion.

Our commitment is to fresh ingredients, expertly prepared dishes, and a welcoming experience that makes you feel right at home. Come on in and discover why we've become a local favorite with over 900 positive reviews!
