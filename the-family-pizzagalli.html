Our Legacy

Serving Bessemer City, One Slice at a Time.

Welcome to The Family Pizzagalli, a true Bessemer City treasure located at 1219 Gastonia Hwy. For years, we've been crafting delicious Italian-American cuisine with a passion that runs deep in our family. We believe in using fresh, quality ingredients to create meals that bring smiles to faces and warmth to hearts.

Our commitment to authentic flavors and exceptional service has made us a beloved local spot. Whether you're craving a classic pizza, a hearty pasta dish, or just a friendly face, you'll find it here. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a part of your community, dedicated to providing a little "pizza" heaven right here in Bessemer City.
