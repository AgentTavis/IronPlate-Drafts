Our Legacy

Over 20 Years of Serving Plymouth's Best

A Local Tradition, Home-Cooked Goodness.

Located in the heart of Plymouth, NC, Carol's Variety Shop #2 & Grill is a cherished local establishment that has been serving our community for over 20 years. While our name hints at variety, we're proudly known as a "ONE-STOP" shop for delicious, hearty American grill favorites.

From sizzling, juicy steaks to mouth-watering cheesesteaks and classic comfort food, we pride ourselves on offering a warm, welcoming atmosphere and quality meals that keep our neighbors coming back. Come experience the taste of a true Plymouth tradition.
