Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

Dire Wolf Sports Bar & Grill isn't just a place to eat; it's Hayesville's ultimate hub for sports, delicious food, and friendly competition. Located right on Hwy 64W, we've built a reputation as the go-to spot where locals and visitors gather to catch the game, shoot some pool, and enjoy a meal that always hits the spot. With our inviting atmosphere and a commitment to great service, we're proud to be a favorite in the community.

We believe in good times, great company, and even better food. Whether you're here to cheer on your favorite team, challenge a friend to a game of billiards, or simply unwind with a cold drink and a satisfying meal, Dire Wolf Sports Bar & Grill offers an experience that feels like home.
