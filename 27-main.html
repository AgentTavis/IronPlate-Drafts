Our Roots

Welcome to the Heart of Old Fort Pizza

At 27 Main, we believe great pizza is more than just food – it's an experience. Nestled in the heart of Old Fort, NC, we've poured our passion into creating a welcoming space where friends, families, and neighbors can gather, share, and enjoy delicious meals together.

Our friendly staff is the backbone of our charming ambiance, always ready with a smile and dedicated to making your visit special. We focus on fresh ingredients and crafting pizzas that bring joy to every bite, ensuring that every time you step through our doors, you feel like part of the 27 Main family.
