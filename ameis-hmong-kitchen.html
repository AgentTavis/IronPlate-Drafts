Our Heritage

A Taste of Hmong Tradition, Right Here in Weaverville.

Amei's Hmong Kitchen is more than just a restaurant; it's a cozy eatery nestled in the heart of Weaverville, CA, offering a unique culinary experience inspired by Hmong culture and tradition.

We are passionate about bringing the rich, vibrant flavors of our heritage to your table. Every dish is a story, carefully prepared with fresh ingredients and authentic techniques passed down through generations. From the warmth of our community to the burst of flavors in our fried rice, we invite you to experience a truly special meal.
