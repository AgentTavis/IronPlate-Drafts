Our Heritage, Your Table

Discover the Heart of Hmong Culinary Tradition

Amei's Hmong Kitchen is more than just a restaurant; it's a culinary journey rooted in the rich traditions of Hmong culture. Nestled in the welcoming community of Weaverville, CA, we've created a cozy eatery where every dish tells a story of heritage and passion.

Our kitchen is a place where time-honored recipes are crafted with fresh, local ingredients, bringing the authentic taste of Hmong home cooking to your table. We believe in the power of food to connect, to share, and to celebrate community. From our family to yours, we invite you to experience a unique dining adventure that's bursting with flavor and made with love.

Our Signature Dishes

Hmong Style Fried Rice

A hearty classic, our fried rice is cooked to perfection with a blend of fresh vegetables, savory meats, and traditional Hmong seasonings. A comforting and flavorful favorite.

Chicken Skewers

Tender chicken marinated in our secret Hmong spice blend, grilled to juicy perfection. Each skewer bursts with fresh, authentic flavors, a true crowd-pleaser.

Traditional Hmong Delight

Experience the essence of Hmong cuisine with a dish inspired by our elders. Rich, aromatic, and deeply satisfying, offering a taste of true tradition.

Flavorful Hmong Stir-Fry

Crisp vegetables and succulent protein wok-tossed with our signature Hmong sauces. A vibrant and healthy option, packed with fresh, invigorating tastes.

Signature Spring Rolls

Hand-rolled with fresh ingredients and served with our tangy dipping sauce. A perfect appetizer to begin your culinary journey with authentic Hmong flavors.

Amei's Special Noodles

Chewy noodles tossed in a savory broth with choice meats and greens. A comforting and deeply flavorful dish, cherished by our community.
