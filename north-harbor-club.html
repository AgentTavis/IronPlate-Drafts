Our Legacy

Davidson's Heart. Your Table.

Welcome to North Harbor Club, where every meal is crafted with passion and served with a smile. We are proud to be a cornerstone of Davidson, NC, offering a vibrant bar and grill experience that brings people together.

Our commitment to amazing food is at the heart of everything we do. As our patrons rave, "The food here is amazing... The breading was crisp and flavorful and the fries were perfect!" We take pride in delivering dishes like our famous chicken strips with parm truffle fries that leave a lasting impression.

While we're known for our delicious cuisine, we also excel at hosting unforgettable gatherings. Whether it's an intimate dinner or a grand private event, North Harbor Club is your ideal setting. Come discover why we're Davidson's go-to for great food and great company.
