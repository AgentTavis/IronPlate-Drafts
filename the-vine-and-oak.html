Our Journey

Crafting Unforgettable Moments, One Sip and Bite at a Time.

At The Vine and Oak, we believe that great food and exceptional drinks are the heart of community. Born from a passion for quality and a love for our hometown of Madison, NC, we set out to create more than just a place to eat; we built a gathering spot where memories are made.

Indulge in our unique craft cocktails, meticulously prepared with the finest spirits and fresh ingredients, promising a symphony of flavors that delight the senses. Paired with our delicious grill menu, every visit is an experience. We pride ourselves on a warm, inviting atmosphere where everyone feels like family, whether you're catching up with friends or celebrating a special occasion.

Our Offerings

Savor Our Signature Tastes & Handcrafted Drinks

Signature Oak Burger

Grilled to perfection, topped with smoked cheddar, crispy bacon, and our special sauce.

Spicy Margarita

Tequila infused with jalapeño, fresh lime juice, and a hint of agave. A true kick!

Truffle Parmesan Fries

Crispy golden fries tossed in truffle oil, fresh Parmesan, and herbs.

Smoked Rib Platter

Slow-smoked baby back ribs, glazed with our house BBQ sauce, served with coleslaw.

Garden Fresh Salad

Whiskey Sour Classic

A timeless blend of bourbon, fresh lemon juice, simple syrup, and an orange twist.

Our Promise

Experience The Vine and Oak Difference

Unrivaled Craft Cocktails

We take pride in our award-winning mixology. Each drink, from the invigorating "Spicy Margarita" to the complex "Smoked Old Fashioned," is a carefully constructed masterpiece using premium spirits and the freshest ingredients.

Delicious Bar & Grill Fare

Beyond the drinks, our kitchen delivers. Savor juicy "Signature Oak Burgers" and tender "Smoked Ribs," all prepared with passion and served with our famous "Truffle Parmesan Fries." It's comfort food elevated.

Warm, Welcoming Atmosphere

More than just a restaurant, we're a community hub. Our cozy, rustic-chic ambiance combined with genuinely friendly service ensures every visit feels like coming home. Perfect for a casual evening or a special celebration.
