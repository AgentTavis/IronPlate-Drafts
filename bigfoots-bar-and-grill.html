Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

Nestled in the heart of Troy, NC, Bigfoot's Bar and Grill has quickly become a beloved landmark at 3079 NC-109. We're not just a place to eat; we're a community hub where friends gather, stories are shared, and good times are guaranteed.

Our commitment is simple: create a lively atmosphere where everyone feels welcome. We pride ourselves on offering a fantastic selection of classic bar and grill favorites, crafted with care to satisfy your cravings. From the moment you step in, you'll feel the energy that makes Bigfoot's unique.

With a solid 4.6-star rating from hundreds of patrons, we're dedicated to delivering the kind of local experience that keeps you coming back for more. Come on down and discover why Bigfoot's is Troy's go-to spot for an unforgettable evening.
