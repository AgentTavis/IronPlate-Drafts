Our Story

Gather, Relax, Savor

At Blue Ridge Beer Garden, we believe in creating more than just a meal – we craft an experience. Nestled in the heart of Hendersonville, NC, our garden is a vibrant escape where good company, delicious food, and wonderful drinks come together.

We pride ourselves on our friendly, helpful staff and an atmosphere that's truly perfect for a summer evening out. What started as a vision for a community gathering spot has blossomed into a beloved local gem, where every visit feels like coming home. The delicious BBQ food truck is a highlight, offering flavors that keep our guests coming back for more.
