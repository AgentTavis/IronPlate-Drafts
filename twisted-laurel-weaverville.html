About Us

The flagship Twisted Laurel is on lovely Main Street in downtown Weaverville, North Carolina surrounded by shops, galleries, and more. You can enjoy our beloved casual, American comfort food with “a Twist” like salads, subs, stone hearth pizzas, and other entreés in a fun, family-friendly atmosphere either inside our beautiful dining rooms or outside on one of our two, enclosed, dog-friendly patios.
