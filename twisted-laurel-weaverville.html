OUR STORY

A Weaverville Welcome, A Taste of Home

Nestled on lovely Main Street in downtown Weaverville, North Carolina, Twisted Laurel Weaverville stands as a beloved local gem. We're proud to be surrounded by the vibrant energy of local shops, galleries, and the beauty of our community.

At our flagship location, we invite you to enjoy our casual, American comfort food with "a Twist." From fresh, crisp salads to hearty subs, and our signature stone hearth pizzas, every dish is crafted to delight. Our entreés promise satisfying flavors in a fun, family-friendly atmosphere that feels just right.

Whether you choose to dine inside our beautiful, inviting rooms or prefer the fresh air on one of our two enclosed, dog-friendly patios, we guarantee a memorable experience for everyone. Come discover your new favorite spot in Weaverville.
