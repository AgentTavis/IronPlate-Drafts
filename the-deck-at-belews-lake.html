Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's the Lake Life.

Nestled on the scenic shores of Belews Lake, The Deck is more than just a Bar & Grill – it's a community gathering spot where good food and great company come together. We're proud to serve fresh, delicious meals for breakfast, lunch, and dinner in a clean, welcoming environment. From our sizzling burgers to our satisfying breakfast plates, every dish is prepared with care, because our people are always our first priority.

Come relax, unwind, and enjoy the views – we're ready to make your day on the lake even better. Whether you're fueling up for a day on the water, grabbing a casual lunch, or enjoying a relaxed dinner, we're here to provide a memorable experience for everyone.

Why The Deck?

Your Perfect Lakeside Escape

Stunning Lake Views

Dine with a picturesque backdrop of Belews Lake. Our deck offers a serene ambiance, perfect for enjoying a meal, a drink, and making lasting memories with family and friends.

Fresh & Delicious Fare

From our much-loved burgers and hot dogs to delightful breakfast and dinner options, every dish is crafted with fresh ingredients, ensuring quality and taste you'll love.
