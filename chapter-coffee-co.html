Our Story

More Than Just Coffee, It's a Community

At Chapter Coffee Co, we believe in crafting experiences as rich as our coffee. Nestled in the heart of Hickory, NC, we're not just your average coffee shop – we're a beloved local hub where stories unfold, connections are made, and every visit feels like coming home.

Beyond our expertly brewed coffees and refreshing teas, we take pride in offering a delightful array of beverages like zesty lemonades and invigorating smoothies. And for those with a sweet or savory craving, our baked goods selection is unparalleled: from fresh bagels and fluffy muffins to delicate danishes and other sweet treats, there's always something to perfectly complement your drink.

In partnership with @mychapterhouse, we invite you to settle in with a good book, connect with friends, or simply enjoy a moment of peace. Come discover why we're Hickory's favorite coffee shop and bookstore.
