Our Heritage

A Taste of Home, Crafted with Care.

Welcome to Winners Circle Restaurant, a cherished, locally owned establishment in the heart of Jefferson, NC. We believe in serving more than just food; we offer a family-friendly experience where quality, comfort, and outstanding value meet.

Since day one, our commitment has been simple: Food, Service, and prices are Outstanding. Our portions are always generous and of excellent quality, ensuring every visit leaves you satisfied. Enjoy our well-stocked and fresh Salad Bar, or dive into a Baked Potato large enough to be a meal in itself. We're proud to be a gathering place for friends and families, creating lasting memories one delicious meal at a time.
