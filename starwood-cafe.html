Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Tradition.

At Starwood Cafe, we believe in bringing people together over delicious, heartwarming meals. What started as a beloved local spot in the DFW area of Texas has grown into a cherished chain, and we're thrilled to be part of the Lewisville, NC community.

We specialize in crafting the perfect breakfast and lunch, using fresh, quality ingredients to ensure every dish tastes like home. Whether you're starting your day with our famous fluffy pancakes or grabbing a satisfying burger for lunch, you'll feel the warmth and care in every bite. We're proud to be a place where memories are made, laughter is shared, and every guest feels like family.
