Our Journey

Brewing Community, One Pint & Plate at a Time

At Tight Lines Pub & Brewing Company, we're proud to be Morehead City's only operating brewery and restaurant. Our story began with a simple vision: to create a welcoming hub where locals and visitors alike could gather, enjoy exceptional craft beer, and savor delicious, fresh food.

Nestled conveniently on Arendell St, we've poured our heart into crafting an experience that goes beyond the ordinary. From our spacious, inviting interior to the friendly faces behind the bar, every detail is designed for your comfort. We believe in good humor, great company, and food that surprises and delights – like our unforgettable Ahi Tuna Wonton Nachos, a dish that often steals the show!

We invite you to stop by, pull up a seat, and discover why Tight Lines is more than just a pub; it's a cornerstone of the Morehead City community, where every visit feels like coming home.

Our Offerings

Taste the Craft: A Sneak Peek at Our Menu

Ahi Tuna Wonton Nachos

Not your ordinary nachos! Crispy fried wontons loaded with fixings and succulent ahi tuna. Get it raw, seared, or however you like it – our perfect sauce ties it all together.

Signature Craft Brews

Experience the freshest pints in Morehead City. From hoppy IPAs to smooth stouts and refreshing lagers, our taps always feature something new and exciting from our own brewery.

Hearty Pub Classics

Enjoy elevated bar & grill favorites. Think gourmet burgers, loaded fries, and other comforting dishes made with a Tight Lines twist, perfect to pair with your brew.

Fresh Catch Specials

Being so close to the coast has its perks! Our kitchen regularly features fresh, locally sourced seafood specials, prepared with creativity and flavor.

Artisan Flatbreads

Crispy crusts topped with inventive combinations of fresh ingredients. A perfect appetizer or a lighter meal to share with friends.

Gourmet Sandwiches & Wraps

Satisfy your hunger with our selection of handcrafted sandwiches and wraps, featuring premium ingredients and bold flavors, served with your choice of side.
