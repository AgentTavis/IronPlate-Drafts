Our Journey

More Than Just Seafood, It's Family.

At The Other Side Of The Moon, we believe every meal is an opportunity to create cherished memories. From our very first location, our mission has been simple: to offer an exceptional seafood dining experience where fresh flavors meet genuine hospitality.

We've proudly grown to 7 vibrant locations across North Carolina, but our heart remains firmly rooted in the local communities we serve. Our tagline, "Come as a stranger, leave as a friend!", isn't just a saying – it's the promise we live by, every single day.

Join us and discover why guests return time and again for our irresistible dishes and the warmth of our welcoming atmosphere. We can't wait to make you feel right at home.
