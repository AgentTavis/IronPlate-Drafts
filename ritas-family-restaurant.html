Our Heritage

A Warm Welcome, Just Like Family

We're not fancy, and we don't pretend to be. At Rita's, you'll find real food, served with genuine warmth, in a down-to-earth setting. Forget the frills – we're all about comfort, community, and classic diner fare.

Expect sturdy plastic plates, honest-to-goodness flavor, and a smile with every meal. That's just how we do things, and our regulars wouldn't have it any other way. We're a family restaurant, and when you step through our doors, you're part of that family too.

Our Dishes

Classic Comfort, Made Fresh Daily

At Rita's, we pride ourselves on serving up hearty, delicious diner staples that hit the spot every time. From our famous coffee to our buttermilk biscuits, every dish is crafted with care to bring you that comforting, homemade taste.

Hearty Breakfast Plates

Classic eggs, crispy bacon or sausage, golden hash browns, and your choice of toast or our renowned buttermilk biscuits.

Homestyle Sandwiches

Piled high with fresh ingredients, served with a side. Perfect for a satisfying lunch any day of the week.

Belmont's Best Coffee

A fresh cup of hot coffee, brewed perfectly to warm you up and get your day started right. A local favorite!

Fluffy Buttermilk Biscuits

Our soft, warm buttermilk biscuits are a must-try. Delicious on their own or with a dollop of butter and jam.
