Our Legacy

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Del Rio Tradition.

Slab Cafe is more than just a place to eat; it's a cornerstone of the Del Rio community. We believe in serving up great food that's budget-friendly, all delivered with a pleasant smile from our dedicated staff.

Our standard luncheonette menu is packed with comfort food favorites for both lunch and dinner. From our satisfying burgers to our classic hot dogs and hearty sandwiches, we've never disappointed our regulars. We're a great little place to stop by and grab a bite and a snack when you're in Del Rio, offering that familiar, welcoming atmosphere you'd expect from a true local diner.
