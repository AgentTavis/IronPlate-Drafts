Our Story

More Than Just Meals, It's Matthews Family Tradition.

For decades, Jonathan's has been a cornerstone of the Matthews community, built on a simple philosophy: serve incredible food with generous portions and warm, inviting hospitality. What started as a dream to create a place where everyone feels at home has blossomed into a beloved local eatery.

From our sizzling breakfast platters served all day to our classic, bubbling pizzas perfect for family dinner, every dish is crafted with care and a commitment to quality. We believe in good food, great prices, and making memories around the table. Come experience the comfort and flavor that makes Jonathan's a true neighborhood gem.

"Jonathan's truly embodies the spirit of Matthews. The food is consistently delicious, and the staff makes you feel like family every time. It’s our go-to for a relaxed meal where you always leave satisfied!"
