Our Story

Welcome to Amalfi's Italian Restaurant, where tradition meets passion. Since opening our doors in Wilkesboro, we've been committed to bringing authentic Italian flavors to our community. Every dish is crafted with care, using time-honored recipes and the finest ingredients.

Our chefs are dedicated to creating an unforgettable dining experience that transports you straight to the picturesque Amalfi Coast. From handmade pasta to classic Italian sauces, we celebrate the rich culinary heritage of Italy in every bite.
