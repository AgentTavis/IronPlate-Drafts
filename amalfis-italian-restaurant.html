About Amalfi's

Welcome to Amalfi's Italian Restaurant, where tradition meets passion. Since opening our doors in Wilkesboro, we've been dedicated to bringing the authentic flavors of Italy to your table.

Our chefs craft each dish with the finest ingredients, honoring time-tested recipes passed down through generations. From our handmade pasta to our signature sauces, every bite is a journey to the heart of Italy.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a casual dinner with family, Amalfi's is your home for unforgettable Italian dining.
