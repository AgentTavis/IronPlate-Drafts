AMALFI'S

Authentic Italian Cuisine in the Heart of Wilkesboro

Taste Italy in Every Bite

At Amalfi's Italian Restaurant, we bring the warmth and flavor of the Amalfi Coast directly to your table. Since opening our doors in Wilkesboro, we've been dedicated to serving authentic Italian cuisine made with the finest ingredients and time-honored recipes.

Our chefs craft each dish with passion, using traditional techniques passed down through generations. From our handmade pasta to our wood-fired pizzas, every meal at Amalfi's is a celebration of Italian culinary excellence and hospitality.

Whether you're joining us for an intimate dinner, a family celebration, or a special occasion, we promise an unforgettable dining experience that transports you to the enchanting Italian coastline.
