Our Journey

Where Passion Meets Plate: The Halal Bites Story

Taste the authentic flavors of the Mediterranean, right here in Asheboro.

At Halal Bites, formerly Mr. Kofta, we invite you to dive into a world where Mediterranean magic meets Halal perfection. Our journey began with a simple passion: to bring the vibrant, rich, and utterly delicious tastes of traditional Halal cuisine to our beloved Asheboro community.

From the moment you step through our doors, you'll savor the bold flavors crafted with care and tradition. We pride ourselves on using only the freshest, highest-quality Halal ingredients, ensuring every dish from our succulent meat over rice platters to our juicy kebabs delivers an unforgettable culinary experience. It's more than just a meal; it's a taste of home, a celebration of heritage, and a commitment to flavor.
