Our Story

More Than Just Coffee, It's Community.

Welcome to Stir It Up Coffee Shop, Emerald Isle's beloved local diner! Nestled in the heart of Emerald Plantation Shopping Mall, we're more than just a coffee shop – we're a community hub where delicious aromas meet friendly faces.

Since opening our doors, we've been dedicated to crafting the perfect cup of coffee, serving up fresh, hearty bagels, and providing a welcoming space for locals and visitors alike. Whether you're starting your day with a morning brew, grabbing a quick lunch, or seeking a quiet spot to work, Stir It Up is your happy place, open daily from 7 AM to 2 PM.
