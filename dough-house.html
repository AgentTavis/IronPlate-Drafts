Our Story

Crafting Happiness, One Doughnut at a Time.

Dough House isn't just a place to eat; it's a Black Mountain tradition built on simple joys and exceptional flavor. From the moment we open our doors, the aroma of freshly brewed coffee and warm, handcrafted doughnuts fills the air.

We believe in serving up smiles, one delicious bite at a time, using time-honored recipes and the freshest ingredients. Come experience the comfort and community that make Dough House a true local favorite. We are proudly dog-friendly!
