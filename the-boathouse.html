Our Story

A Local Gem, Crafted with Passion.

Nestled in the heart of downtown Elizabeth City at 400 East Colonial Avenue, The Boathouse has become a beloved fixture in our community. We believe in creating more than just a meal; we craft an experience – a place where friends and family gather, celebrate, and create lasting memories.

From our lively atmosphere perfect for Sunday Brunch or catching the big game, to evenings filled with karaoke and friendly pool competitions, there's always something happening at The Boathouse. We're proud to serve up American classics made with care, offering a warm welcome to everyone who walks through our doors.
