Our Journey

Crafting Culinary Magic, One Bite at a Time.

It's rare for an experience to be truly 5-star, but Fire and Dough Bakery manages it every time. Even during off-peak hours on a Sunday, our bakery buzzes with happy customers – a testament to the irresistible flavors we create. We invite you to embrace a short wait for an unforgettable meal.

At the heart of Fire and Dough is our commitment to Mediterranean-style cooking. Each dish is a symphony of flavors, meticulously prepared to transport your taste buds. We're renowned for our Chicken Shawarma Pizza, where insane flavors burst with every slice. But don't let the name "bakery" fool you; our Middle Eastern Spiced Cheeseburger, cooked right into the dough, is an absolute revelation – truly the best thing many have tasted in a long while. It's this intriguing fusion that sets us apart.
