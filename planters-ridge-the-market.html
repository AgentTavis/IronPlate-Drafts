Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Hertford Tradition

Nestled in the heart of Hertford, NC, Planters Ridge: The Market is more than just a bar and grill – it's a cherished gathering place where friends and family come together. We've poured our passion into creating a welcoming atmosphere, serving up hearty dishes, and crafting drinks that make every visit a celebration.

We believe in good food, great company, and making memories. From our cozy interiors to our friendly staff, every detail is designed to make you feel right at home. Come experience the warmth and charm that makes us a beloved local spot.

"Planters Ridge is our go-to! The vibe is always lively, the staff feels like family, and every dish is packed with flavor. It’s the true taste of Hertford hospitality."

- A Loyal Local

Our Offerings

Savor the Flavors of Planters Ridge

Hearty Grill Platter

A generous selection of our finest grilled meats, perfectly seasoned and cooked to your liking. A true crowd-pleaser for any appetite.

Crispy Market Wings

Our famous crispy wings, tossed in your choice of signature sauces. Perfect for sharing, or keeping all to yourself!

Signature Market Burger

A juicy, hand-pattied burger with all the fixings, served on a toasted bun. An absolute classic done right.

Fresh Garden Salad

Loaded Market Fries

Local Craft Brews
