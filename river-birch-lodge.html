Our Story

Rooted in Community, Crafted with Care

Inspired by the relaxed spirit of the outdoors, River Birch Lodge offers a cozy escape, perfect for family dinners, date nights, or gathering with friends. Located in the heart of the Triad, on Robinhood Road, our lodge-style restaurant blends wood-fired favorites with fresh, quality ingredients.

Whether you're warming up by our inviting fire or enjoying a refreshing glass of wine on the patio, your comfort is our goal. We’re incredibly proud to be a local favorite for the last 20 years, a place where great food and genuine hospitality consistently meet the expectations of our cherished guests.
