Our Legacy

More Than a Meal, It's a Memory.

Nestled in the heart of McBride, Al's Place isn't just a diner – it's a cornerstone of our community. For over 78 years, our family has poured love into every dish, creating a basic, unpretentious spot where everyone feels at home.

You won't stumble upon us by accident; Al's is a cherished secret, passed down through generations. Step inside, and you'll find a small town restaurant that probably looks just like it did 50 years ago, and that's precisely our charm. We're a family-run establishment, serving up honest-to-goodness diner fair with a side of McBride hospitality.

From the moment you walk through our doors, you're part of our story. We believe in good food, great service, and prices that make delicious meals accessible to everyone. Come see why Al's Place has been a local favorite for decades – the kind of place where familiar faces greet you with a smile, and every meal is made with care.
