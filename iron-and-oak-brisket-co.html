OUR STORY

Where Tradition Meets Taste

At Iron and Oak Brisket Co., we believe true barbecue is an art form, a labor of love passed down through generations. Our commitment to authentic, slow-smoked brisket is at the heart of everything we do. We meticulously select the finest cuts of meat, season them with our signature rub, and smoke them for hours over real oak wood until they reach an unparalleled tenderness and flavor.

Every plate is a testament to our passion, served with the essential accompaniments: two delicious sides, a tangy pickle, fresh onion, and classic Wonder Bread. It's the full, unpretentious BBQ experience, designed to bring people together and satisfy even the most discerning palates. Come taste the tradition that makes us a Woodfin favorite.
