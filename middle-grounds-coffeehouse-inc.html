OUR STORY

More Than Just Coffee: A Kinston Community Hub

At Middle Grounds Coffeehouse Inc., we believe in creating more than just exceptional coffee and delicious food – we're crafting a gathering place, a community hub right here in Kinston, NC. From the aroma of freshly brewed coffee to the friendly smiles that greet you, every detail is designed to make you feel right at home.

We pride ourselves on genuine connections and service that goes the extra mile. Whether you're catching up with friends, settling in for a productive work session, or simply enjoying a moment of peace, Middle Grounds is your space. We're committed to quality, warmth, and making every visit a truly elevated experience.
