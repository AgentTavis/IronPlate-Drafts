About Us

Smoke. Flavor. Tradition.

Welcome to Brushy Mountain Smokehouse & Creamery, where we combine authentic Carolina BBQ traditions with innovative dessert creations. Since opening our doors in Wilkesboro, we've been committed to serving the finest smoked meats and handcrafted ice cream in the region.

Our whole hog barbecue is slow-smoked for 16+ hours over hickory wood, creating that perfect bark and tender, flavorful meat. Every meal is a celebration of mountain heritage and culinary passion.

From classic smoked brisket to our unique bourbon pecan ice cream, every bite tells a story of craftsmanship and quality ingredients sourced from local suppliers whenever possible.
