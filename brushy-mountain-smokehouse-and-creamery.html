Who We Are

Brushy Mountain Smokehouse & Creamery is Wilkesboro's premier destination for authentic Carolina barbecue and handcrafted ice cream. For years, we've been perfecting our craft, smoking meats low and slow over hardwood, and churning fresh creamery flavors that keep our community coming back.

From our signature pulled pork to our seasonal creamery creations, every bite reflects our commitment to quality, tradition, and genuine Southern hospitality.

🔥 Wood-Smoked

Low and slow smoking with premium hardwoods for authentic flavor

🥄 Handcrafted Creamery

👨‍🍳 Family Recipes
