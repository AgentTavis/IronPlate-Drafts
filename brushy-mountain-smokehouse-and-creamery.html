About Us

Smoking Tradition Meets Sweet Indulgence

Since opening our doors in Wilkesboro, Brushy Mountain Smokehouse & Creamery has been the community's favorite destination for slow-smoked BBQ and artisan ice cream. We believe in quality ingredients, time-honored smoking techniques, and the perfect finish—a scoop of our homemade creamery treats.

Our family-owned restaurant combines the best of mountain hospitality with world-class barbecue. Every brisket, ribs, and pulled pork is smoked low and slow over hardwood, and every ice cream flavor is crafted fresh in our kitchen.
