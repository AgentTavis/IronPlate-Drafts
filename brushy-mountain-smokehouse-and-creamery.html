About Us

Welcome to Brushy Mountain Smokehouse and Creamery, where tradition meets innovation in the heart of Wilkesboro, North Carolina.

Our passion is simple: slow-smoked, mouth-watering barbecue paired with our handcrafted ice cream and creamy treats. We use only the finest meats, authentic smoking techniques, and premium ingredients to create memorable dining experiences.

Whether you're craving smoky ribs, tender brisket, or a refreshing scoop of our signature flavors, we've got something for everyone. Family-owned and proudly serving Wilkesboro since day one.
