Our Story

Bringing the Taste of the Coast to Millers Creek.

At Mad Jax, we believe in serving up honest, delicious seafood and comfort food that brings people together. Nestled in the heart of Millers Creek, we're a casual spot where families, friends, and groups can gather for a great meal without the fuss.

From our fresh grilled shrimp to our hearty smothered chicken, every dish is prepared with a touch of Southern hospitality. We're proud to be a local favorite, offering a welcoming atmosphere where good food and good times are always on the menu.
