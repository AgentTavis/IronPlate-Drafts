Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Catawba Tradition.

For years, Taste of Catawba has been the heart of our community, a true local watering hole where friends gather and memories are made. We believe in the simple joy of good food shared with good company, right here by the waterside.

Recently, we've refreshed our space with a vibrant new look and feel, blending classic diner charm with modern comfort. Our commitment remains the same: to serve up delicious, honest diner fare with a warm smile and exceptional service. We're dedicated to being the spot where everyone feels at home.

Come experience the renewed spirit and welcoming atmosphere that make us a celebrated part of Catawba's story. We're more than just a place to eat – we're family. See you @ the Waterside!
