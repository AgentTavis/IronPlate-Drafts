Our Story

A Taste of History, A Heart of Community

White Story Cafe is more than just a diner; it's a cherished part of Downtown Wake Forest's history. Nestled within the beautifully renovated historic Wilkerson Building, we've carefully preserved the cozy atmosphere that makes our space so unique.

We believe in honest, homemade food served with a smile. From the first bite of our freshly baked goods to the comforting warmth of our daily soups, every item on our menu is crafted to bring joy and a sense of home. Come experience a place where rich history meets delicious, modern comfort.

"Stepping into White Story Cafe feels like coming home. The aroma of fresh coffee and pastries, coupled with the historic charm, makes it my favorite escape."

— A Loyal Wake Forest Local
