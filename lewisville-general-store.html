Our Heritage

A Taste of Tradition, Fresh from Lewisville

For generations, the Lewisville General Store has been more than just a place to shop – it's been a gathering spot, a pillar of our community. Today, as Alex's Grill & Ice Cream, we proudly continue that legacy, blending classic Southern charm with the freshest seafood and homemade delights.

We believe in simple pleasures: the crunch of perfectly fried flounder, the rich creaminess of fresh oysters, and the joy of sharing a meal with loved ones. Every dish is crafted with care, sourcing local ingredients when possible, to bring you a truly authentic taste of Lewisville. Come join our family at the table.
