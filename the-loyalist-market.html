OUR STORY

A Taste of Passion, Rooted in Tradition

At The Loyalist Market, we're more than just a place to eat – we're a culinary experience born from a deep love for artisan craftsmanship. Nestled in the heart of Matthews, NC, our journey began with a simple vision: to bring the rich, diverse world of premium cheeses and cured meats to our community.

By day, we craft gourmet sandwiches featuring the freshest ingredients. As evening falls, we transform into an inviting cheese bar, where friends gather to explore over 60 exquisite artisan cheeses and cured meats sourced from the finest producers, both locally and abroad. We also proudly feature artisan food products from the best the South has to offer, celebrating regional flavors and traditions. Come discover your new favorite.
