Our Journey

Bringing the Authentic Flavors of Mexico to Boonville, NC

Mi Tierra Mexican Cuisine, nestled in the heart of Boonville, NC, offers a truly delightful and authentic Mexican dining experience. From the moment you step inside, you're greeted with a warm, inviting atmosphere that feels like a taste of home. We believe in crafting dishes that tell a story – a story of rich culinary traditions, fresh ingredients, and a passion for flavor.

Our journey began with a simple desire: to share the vibrant, heartwarming taste of genuine Mexican food with our community. Every taco, every burrito, every dish is prepared with the same care and love you'd find in the kitchens of Mexico. We are a family-run establishment, proud to serve our neighbors and create a place where good food and great memories are always on the menu.

Taste the Tradition: Our Signature Dishes

Signature Tacos

Hand-pressed tortillas filled with your choice of seasoned meats, fresh salsa, and authentic toppings. A true taste of Mexico!

Hearty Burritos

Generously sized burritos packed with savory fillings, rice, beans, and cheese, all wrapped in a warm flour tortilla.

Flavorful Enchiladas

Corn tortillas rolled with delicious fillings, smothered in rich, homemade sauces and melted cheese.
