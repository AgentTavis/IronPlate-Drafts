Our Story

A Taste of Tradition

Mi Tierra Mexican Cuisine, nestled in the heart of Boonville, NC at 127113 Boonville, NC 27011, offers a truly delightful and authentic Mexican dining experience. We believe in bringing the vibrant flavors and rich culinary heritage of Mexico directly to your table.

From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be welcomed by an atmosphere that feels like a home away from home. Our commitment to authentic ingredients and time-honored recipes ensures every dish tells a story, a story of passion, flavor, and community.
