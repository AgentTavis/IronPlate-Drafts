Our Journey

A Taste of Home, With a Mexican Twist.

At Tina's Country Kitchen, we blend the heart of Southern hospitality with the vibrant flavors of authentic Mexican street food. Nestled in Candor, NC, we're dedicated to serving up generous portions of delicious, freshly prepared dishes that feel like home – whether you're craving a classic burrito, a savory torta, or one of our daily specials.

We believe in simple, good food made with passion and served with a smile. Our kitchen is where tradition meets innovation, creating a unique dining experience that brings comfort and excitement to your plate. Come taste the difference where every meal is made with love.
