Our Story

A Culinary Journey Through Southern Hospitality

Nestled in the heart of Wadesboro, NC, Jones Creek Southern Restaurant is more than just a meal – it's an experience. Born from a passion for authentic Southern flavors and genuine hospitality, our restaurant, previously known as Jones Creek Fish Camp, has become a cherished local gem.

What started as a spontaneous stop for travelers searching for local delights quickly turned into a recommendation worth the wait. We pride ourselves on creating dishes that resonate with warmth and tradition, ensuring every guest feels right at home from the moment they step through our doors.

Our commitment to fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes ensures that staples like our perfectly seasoned salt and pepper catfish, delicious hush puppies, and homemade onion rings are always a highlight. We invite you to join our family and taste the difference that true Southern care makes.
