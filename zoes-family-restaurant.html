Our Story

Serving Comfort, Creating Memories.

Welcome to Zoe's Family Restaurant, a true gem in Winston-Salem. We pour our hearts into every dish, creating a warm, inviting atmosphere where everyone feels like family. It's more than just a meal; it's a moment to connect over delicious, home-cooked food.

Our dedicated team, including the wonderful Trish, Melissa, and Dolly, are the backbone of Zoe's. They take genuine care of every customer, ensuring your visit is always special. From the moment you walk in, you'll experience service with a smile that makes you want to come back again and again.

We're so glad Zoe's moved into this neighborhood – it's exactly what Winston-Salem needed. A place where the food is consistently great, the service is even better, and the community gathers.
