Our Story

Generations of Comfort, Right Here in Lexington.

Old 64 Diner is more than just a restaurant; it's a piece of Lexington's heart. Nestled right in the community, we've been serving up classic American comfort food for decades, becoming a cherished gathering spot for families and friends.

Our philosophy is simple: good food, friendly faces, and a cozy atmosphere where everyone feels at home. We believe in the power of a hearty meal and a warm smile to brighten your day. From our early morning breakfast rush to our bustling dinner service, every dish is prepared with the care and tradition you'd expect from your favorite local diner.

Come on in and taste the difference that genuine hospitality makes. We're proud to be a quaint eatery in Lexington, dedicated to offering a delicious escape from the everyday.

Our Menu

Classic American Comfort, Made Fresh Daily.

While our full menu awaits your visit, here’s a taste of the comforting dishes you can expect:

Hearty Breakfast Plates

Start your day right with classic eggs, crispy bacon or sausage, fluffy pancakes, and golden hash browns. The perfect fuel for your morning.

Diner Lunch Classics

From juicy, hand-pattied burgers to our signature hot sandwiches and fresh, crisp salads. Your midday cravings, satisfied.

Homestyle Dinner Entrees

Savor our comforting dinner dishes, prepared with love. Think meatloaf, fried chicken, and daily specials that warm the soul.

Sides & Appetizers

Perfect additions to any meal or for sharing. Crispy onion rings, loaded fries, and our famous creamy coleslaw.

Sweet Treats & Desserts

Indulge in our selection of classic diner desserts, from homemade pies to rich milkshakes. A sweet end to any meal.

Beverages

Freshly brewed coffee, ice-cold tea, soft drinks, and refreshing juices to complement your meal.
