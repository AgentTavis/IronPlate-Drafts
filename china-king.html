OUR STORY

A Legacy of Flavor, Crafted with Care.

At China King, we believe in the power of good food to bring people together. Located right here in Gatesville, NC, we've dedicated ourselves to serving the community with authentic Chinese cuisine and fresh, delicious seafood. Every dish is prepared with passion, using traditional techniques and the freshest ingredients available.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a welcoming place where families and friends can gather to enjoy extraordinary flavors. We take pride in our culinary heritage, offering a diverse menu that caters to every palate, from classic Chinese takeout favorites to delectable seafood specialties. Come and taste the difference that genuine care and quality ingredients make!
