Our Story

Welcome to Our Cherished Porch

Front Porch Cakery & Deli, proudly owned and operated by Missy and Ben Reed, invites you to experience our charming Cherokee spot. Stepping onto our "front porch" means stepping into a place where good food and warm hospitality are at the heart of everything we do. We're formally known as Sweeties, and we've poured our passion into creating a welcoming storefront business right here in Cherokee, NC.

We're known for our delicious deli sandwiches, crafted with care and fresh ingredients, and our irresistibly sweet cupcakes that are perfect for any occasion. Our cozy, porch-like atmosphere is designed to be your perfect escape, offering a comfortable and cute setting for a delightful meal. Plus, enjoy our lovely outdoor dining space where your furry friends are always welcome to join you!

At Front Porch Cakery & Deli, we believe in kindness and community. Come and discover why we're a local favorite, delivering a unique dining experience that feels just like home. We can't wait to welcome you.

Why Choose Us?

More Than Just a Meal

Authentic Comfort Food

From our signature "Big Joey" and hearty "Bigfoot Club" sandwiches to our soul-warming chili, every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and a touch of home-style love. Don't miss our classic "Fried Bologna" or our rich "Italian Sandwich".

Charming Porchside Experience

Our deli is decorated "much like a porch" and offers a "cute" and cozy ambiance. Enjoy our delightful outdoor dining area where you can relax and even "bring your pup" for a truly welcoming and unique atmosphere in Cherokee.

Sweet Treats & Local Love

Indulge in our "sweet cupcakes" – the perfect finish to any meal or a delightful treat on their own. We're a proud local business, "owned and operated by Missy and Ben Reed", committed to friendly service and being a cherished part of the Cherokee community.
