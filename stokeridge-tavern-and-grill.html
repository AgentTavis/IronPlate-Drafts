Our Story

Nestled between Oak Ridge and Stokesdale, NC, StokeRidge Tavern is a locally owned American Bar and Grill with a fully stocked bar featuring 50 bourbons, 30 tequilas, and our own craft vodka, Stoked Vodka.

Established in 2011, we've built our menu around what our customers love. Each quarter, we feature our Local Flavor Menu where you suggest items that get added on a temporary basis. If they sell well, they join our permanent menu. The result is a dining experience designed solely around you.
