About Us

Welcome to Stokeridge

Stokeridge Tavern and Grill is your destination for exceptional dining and unforgettable gatherings. Located in the heart of Oak Ridge, we combine the warmth of a traditional tavern with the culinary excellence of a full-service grill.

From our signature grilled specialties to our carefully curated selection of beverages, every dish is crafted with pride. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a casual night out, Stokeridge delivers an experience that keeps guests coming back.

Great food. Great company. Great memories.
