OUR STORY

Where Great Food Begins

Welcome to Stokeridge Tavern & Grill, your beloved local spot nestled in the heart of Oak Ridge, NC. We’re passionate about serving up delicious food and creating a warm, inviting atmosphere for friends and family alike.

Since opening our doors, our mission has been simple: to provide a place where the community can gather, enjoy expertly prepared meals, and make lasting memories. We believe in good company, great food, and the kind of hospitality that makes you feel right at home.
