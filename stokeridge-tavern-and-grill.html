Our Story

Nestled between Oak Ridge and Stokesdale, NC, StokeRidge Tavern & Grill is a locally owned American bar and grill with a passion for great food, craft spirits, and community.

Since 2011, we've built our menu around what our customers love. Our fully stocked bar features 50 bourbons, 30 tequilas, and our very own Stoked Vodka. But what makes us truly unique is our commitment to you: each quarter, we feature a Local Flavor Menu where you suggest dishes that temporarily join our offerings. The ones that win your hearts become permanent fixtures.

Come experience a restaurant designed entirely by and for our community—where great food meets genuine hospitality.
