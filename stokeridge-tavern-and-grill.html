Our Story

Stokeridge Tavern and Grill is your go-to spot for casual American fare in Oak Ridge, NC. We specialize in signature dishes that keep guests coming back—from our renowned Moonshiner Sirloin to our beloved STG Shrimp and Grits, not to mention our wildly popular Sumadem Wings.

Beyond our signature items, we offer a diverse range of sandwiches, wraps, and other classics. Whether you're here for a family dinner, a night out with friends, or something special, we've got the food and atmosphere to make it memorable.
