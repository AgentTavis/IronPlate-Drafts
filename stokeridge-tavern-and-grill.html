Our Story

Nestled between Oak Ridge and Stokesdale, NC, StokeRidge Tavern & Grill is a locally owned American Bar and Grill with a fully stocked bar featuring 50 bourbons, 30 tequilas, and their own signature vodka—Stoked Vodka. Established in 2011, we've built our menu around what our customers love.

Each quarter, we feature a Local Flavor Menu where you get to suggest dishes that we test out. If they sell well, they earn a permanent spot on our menu. The result? A menu designed entirely by our community. We don't just serve food—we celebrate the people who make StokeRidge feel like home.
