Our Story

Nestled between Oak Ridge and Stokesdale, NC, StokeRidge Tavern is a locally owned American Bar and Grill with a fully stocked bar featuring 50 bourbons, 30 tequilas, and their own vodka (Stoked Vodka). Established in 2011, STG builds its menu around customer requests.

Each quarter, they offer a Local Flavor Menu where folks suggest items that get added on a temporary basis. If they sell well, they join the permanent menu. The result is a menu designed solely around their customers.
