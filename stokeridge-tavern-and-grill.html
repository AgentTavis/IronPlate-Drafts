Our Story

Nestled between Oak Ridge and Stokesdale, NC, StokeRidge Tavern is a locally owned American Bar and Grill with a fully stocked bar featuring 50 bourbons, 30 tequilas, and our own signature Stoked Vodka.

Established in 2011, we build our menu around what our customers love. Each quarter, we offer a Local Flavor Menu where you suggest items that get added on a temporary basis. If they sell well, they join our permanent menu. The result is a menu designed entirely around our community.

Whether you're here for our signature bourbon-glazed dishes, fresh seafood, or just to unwind with friends, StokeRidge is where great food meets great company.
