OUR STORY

Rooted in Community, Crafted with Care

Welcome to Stokeridge Tavern & Grill! More than just a bar and grill, we're a beloved gathering spot in the heart of Oak Ridge, NC. Since opening our doors, we've been dedicated to serving up delicious food, refreshing drinks, and an atmosphere where friends and family can connect.
