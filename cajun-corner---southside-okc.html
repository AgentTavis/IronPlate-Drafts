Our Roots

Taste the Tradition, Feel the Family

No matter what you're craving, there's something delicious waiting for you at one of our 3 locations across Oklahoma City. At Cajun Corner - Southside OKC, we bring the vibrant spirit and rich flavors of Louisiana right to your neighborhood.

Born from a passion for authentic Cajun cuisine and a desire to create a welcoming space for our community, Cajun Corner has grown into a beloved local favorite. We pride ourselves on using time-honored recipes, fresh ingredients, and a dash of Southern hospitality in every dish we serve.

Explore our menu, place an online ToGo order + more below, and discover why families and friends keep coming back for our hearty portions and unforgettable taste. We're more than just a meal; we're a gathering place where good food and good times are always on the menu.

Our Delicious Creations

Taste the Authentic Cajun Difference

Discover our selection of classic Cajun dishes, prepared with passion and served with a smile. (Note: Specific menu items are illustrative due to data limitations.)

Classic Chicken & Sausage Gumbo

A rich, dark roux-based stew with tender chicken, smoked sausage, holy trinity, and served over steamed rice. A true taste of Louisiana.

Spicy Shrimp & Grits

Creamy, cheesy grits topped with succulent shrimp simmered in a robust, spicy tomato-based sauce. A Southern comfort classic with a kick.

Jambalaya (Chicken & Andouille)

Our flavorful one-pot rice dish packed with tender chicken, spicy Andouille sausage, and a medley of vegetables, seasoned to perfection.

Crawfish Etouffee

A Louisiana staple! Tender crawfish tails smothered in a rich, buttery, spiced roux served over fluffy white rice. Pure indulgence.

Cajun Fried Catfish Platter

Crispy, perfectly seasoned catfish fillets, deep-fried to golden perfection. Served with our signature coleslaw and hushpuppies.

Bayou Burger (Spicy)

A juicy patty seasoned with Cajun spices, topped with pepper jack cheese, fried onions, and our special spicy aioli on a brioche bun.

Shrimp Po'Boy

Crispy fried shrimp piled high on soft French bread with lettuce, tomato, pickles, and a generous drizzle of remoulade sauce.

Dirty Rice

A savory side dish made with rice cooked with ground meat (pork & beef), chicken liver, and aromatic Cajun spices.

Our Commitment

More Than Just Food, It's an Experience

Authentic Cajun Recipes

We honor the traditions of Louisiana cooking, using time-tested recipes passed down through generations. Every dish, from our Gumbo to our Etouffee, is crafted with the genuine flavors of the Bayou.

Vibrant Atmosphere & Live Music

Step into a lively environment reminiscent of the French Quarter. Enjoy friendly service, great company, and often, the soulful sounds of local musicians that make every visit a celebration.

Handcrafted Cocktails & Local Brews

Complement your meal with our expertly mixed cocktails, featuring a Cajun twist, or choose from our wide selection of local craft beers and Southern favorites. The perfect pairing for any dish!
