Our Roots

More Than Just Coffee – It's Community.

Welcome to Catawba Coffee Co., a beloved gathering spot in the heart of Mt Holly, NC. We're more than just a Bar And Grill; we're a place where friends connect, families relax, and local flavors come to life.

From our welcoming dine-in space to our refreshing outdoor seating, we've crafted an environment where every visit feels like coming home. We pride ourselves on creating memorable experiences, one delicious coffee or sweet treat at a time, making us a true cornerstone of the Mt Holly community.

Whether you're grabbing a quick in-store pickup, settling in for a meal, or enjoying the fresh air outside, we invite you to experience the warmth and charm that make Catawba Coffee Co. a local favorite.
