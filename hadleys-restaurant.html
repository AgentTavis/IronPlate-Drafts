Our Heritage

Serving North Wilkesboro's Heart & Soul

Hadley's Restaurant is more than just a diner; it's a cherished local institution in North Wilkesboro, NC. We proudly serve American classics, from hearty breakfasts to satisfying lunches and dinners.

Known for "all your favorites you remember," our doors have welcomed generations seeking delicious, comforting meals and a warm, friendly atmosphere. We believe in good food, good company, and the timeless tradition of a true American diner.

Come experience the taste of North Carolina hospitality that keeps everyone coming back, day after day, year after year.

Homestyle Meatloaf Dinner
