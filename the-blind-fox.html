Our Menu

Crafted Bites & Potent Potions

While our full, ever-evolving menu awaits your discovery in person, here's a glimpse into the unique offerings you can expect. Prices are not available online for our exclusive selections.

The Blind Fox Old Fashioned

A timeless classic, reimagined with our select bourbon, house-made bitters, and a touch of orange zest. Smooth, sophisticated, and perfectly balanced.

Smoked Gouda Mac & Cheese

Our indulgent take on a comfort classic. Creamy, rich smoked gouda cheese sauce with cavatappi pasta, topped with a crispy breadcrumb crust.

Speakeasy Sliders

Three miniature gourmet burgers, each crafted with premium beef, special sauce, aged cheddar, and caramelized onions on toasted brioche buns.

Truffle Parmesan Fries

Crispy golden fries tossed with aromatic truffle oil, fresh grated Parmesan cheese, and a sprinkle of parsley. An irresistible side.

Prohibition Punch

A vibrant and refreshing house cocktail featuring a secret blend of spirits and fresh fruit juices, guaranteed to liven up your evening.

Seasonal Craft Beer Selection

A rotating selection of the finest craft beers, hand-picked from local and regional breweries to complement our menu and atmosphere.
