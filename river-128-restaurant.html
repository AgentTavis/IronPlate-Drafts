OUR STORY

Where Southern Smoke Meets River Charm

Nestled in the vibrant heart of downtown Wilmington, River 128 Restaurant offers a culinary experience unlike any other. Our journey began with a simple passion: to bring authentic, delicious BBQ and inspired Southern comfort food to our beloved community, all while embracing the stunning backdrop of the historic Cape Fear River.

My husband and I truly love what we've created here. From the moment you step into our enclosed patio, you're greeted by the lovely view and the promise of an exceptional meal. Our attentive and knowledgeable servers are dedicated to making your visit memorable, guiding you through a menu crafted with care and the freshest local ingredients. We believe good food, great views, and warm hospitality are the ingredients for a perfect dining experience, making River 128 a true keeper for locals and visitors alike.
