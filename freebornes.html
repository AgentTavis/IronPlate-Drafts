OUR STORY

A Place Where Everyone Belongs

Freeborne's is more than just a bar and grill; it's a cherished local gem in Laurel Springs, NC. Housed in rustic quarters within an unassuming motel, we offer a down-to-earth dining experience complete with a charming veranda.

We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels like family. Whether you're a regular stopping by for your favorite dish or a first-time visitor passing through, you'll find good food, great company, and heartfelt hospitality always on the menu.

Our commitment to classic comfort food and a relaxed setting has made Freeborne's a beloved gathering spot for the community. Come discover your new favorite local hangout.
