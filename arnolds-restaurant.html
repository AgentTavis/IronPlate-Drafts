Our Legacy

The Heart of Lumberton's Grill Scene

Welcome to Arnold's Restaurant, a true Lumberton institution where good food, great company, and unforgettable flavors come together. For years, we've been serving up classic American Bar & Grill dishes with our own unique twist, becoming a beloved spot for families and friends.

From our sizzling burgers to our renowned Alligator Bites, every dish is crafted with passion and local pride. We believe in creating an atmosphere where every visit feels like coming home. Whether you're craving a quick lunch, a hearty dinner, or just want to relax with some delicious wings, Arnold's is your go-to destination.
