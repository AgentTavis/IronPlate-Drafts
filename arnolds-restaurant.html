The Arnold's Story: Heartfelt American Classics in Lumberton

Arnold's Restaurant has been a beloved American dining spot in Lumberton, NC, for years, serving up classic American comfort food with a unique twist. Known for its lively Bar & Grill atmosphere and generous portions, we pride ourselves on being a go-to destination for locals and visitors alike.

From the moment you walk through our doors, you're treated like family. Our commitment to quality ingredients, friendly service, and a welcoming ambiance is what sets us apart. Whether you're craving a juicy burger, fresh salad, or our legendary Alligator Bites, every dish is crafted to deliver a memorable experience that keeps our community coming back for more.
