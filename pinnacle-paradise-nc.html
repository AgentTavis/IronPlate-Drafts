Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Pinnacle's Pizza Paradise

At Pinnacle Paradise NC, we believe in bringing joy to our community through delicious food and a warm, inviting atmosphere. Located right here at 3532 N Old 52 Rd in Pinnacle, we're excited to open our doors and share our passion with you.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a place where everyone feels at home, whether you're swinging by for a quick breakfast bite or gathering with family for a pizza feast. We're proud to be part of the Pinnacle community and look forward to becoming your go-to spot for great food and even better company.

From our signature pizzas to our delightful specialty milkshakes and refreshing shaved ice, every item on our menu is crafted with care. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a place where memories are made, and good times are shared. Come experience the paradise we've created just for you!
