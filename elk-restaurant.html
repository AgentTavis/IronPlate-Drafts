Our Story

A Taste of Monroe, Crafted with Care.

We can't wait to host you! Elk Restaurant is bringing a fresh and inviting Bar And Grill experience to the heart of downtown Monroe, North Carolina. We are passionate about creating a space where friends, family, and neighbors can gather to enjoy great food, refreshing drinks, and memorable times.

Our doors are open Sunday, Monday & Thursday from 11am-8pm, and Friday & Saturday from 11am-10pm. We take pride in our commitment to quality and community, and we're thrilled to become a new cornerstone for dining and socializing in Monroe. Join us soon!
