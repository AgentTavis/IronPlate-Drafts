Our Story

More Than Just Donuts – It's a Durham Tradition.

Nestled in the heart of Downtown Durham, Monuts isn't just a bakery and cafe; it's a bustling hub of community and flavor. We've built our reputation on serving up extraordinary breakfast and lunch fare, from our signature soups and salads to our delightful sandwiches and expertly brewed coffee.

But let's be honest, what truly steals the show are our donuts! Each one is a little piece of art, made with passion and the finest ingredients. Whether you're grabbing a quick bite, enjoying a leisurely brunch, or picking up a catering order for your next event, you'll feel the warmth and care that goes into everything we do.

Come experience why Monuts is a beloved staple for locals and visitors alike. We're proud to be a part of Durham's vibrant food scene and can't wait to share our passion with you.
