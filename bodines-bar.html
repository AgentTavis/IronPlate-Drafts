Our Story

Where Every Visit Feels Like Coming Home.

More Than Just a Bar & Grill.

At Bodine's Bar, we believe in creating a welcoming space where friends become family and every meal is an experience. Nestled in the heart of Clemmons, we're proud to be your local spot for delicious food, refreshing drinks, and unforgettable memories.

Our friendly staff is dedicated to making every guest, whether it's your first time or your hundredth, feel right at home. We're passionate about good food, great company, and serving the community we love.

Our Offerings

Savor The Flavors of Bodine's.

Bodine's Classic Burger

Juicy beef patty, melted cheddar, crisp lettuce, fresh tomato, and our signature special sauce.

Crispy Chicken Wings

Golden fried to perfection. Choose from BBQ, Spicy Buffalo, or Zesty Lemon Pepper.

Loaded Nachos

Tortilla chips piled high with creamy queso, jalapeños, salsa, sour cream, and your choice of seasoned chicken or beef.

Steak Frites

Perfectly grilled steak, served with a generous portion of crispy, seasoned fries and garlic herb butter.

Fish & Chips

Flaky, golden-fried cod fillets with seasoned fries and a side of tangy tartar sauce.

House Salad

Fresh mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and your choice of house-made dressing.
