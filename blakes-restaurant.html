Our Story

Generations of Flavor, Family, and Fun.

At Blake's Restaurant, we believe great food brings people together. Nestled in the heart of Candor, NC, we've been serving up classic diner fare with a warm, family-style touch for years. From the moment you step through our doors, you’re not just a customer, you’re part of our extended family.

We pride ourselves on delicious, home-cooked meals prepared with care, reminiscent of Sunday dinners at Grandma's house. Our ambiance is designed for comfort and connection, a place where conversations flow as freely as our coffee.

Whether you're starting your day with a hearty breakfast or winding down with a comforting dinner, Blake's is the spot where quality ingredients and genuine hospitality create an unforgettable dining experience. Come make some memories with us!
