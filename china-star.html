OUR STORY

From Our Family Kitchen to Your Table.

For years, China Star has been serving Raleigh the finest Chinese takeout, built on a passion for authentic flavors and fresh ingredients. Our secret? Time-honored recipes, a dedication to quality, and a commitment to making every meal a memorable experience.

We're proud to be known for our legendary Mumbo Sauce Chicken Wings – a sweet, spicy, and tangy delight that keeps our customers coming back for more. From perfectly crafted Eggrolls to flavorful stir-fries, we invite you to taste the difference that genuine care makes.
