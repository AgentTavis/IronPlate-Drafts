Our Story

A Taste of Home in Franklin, NC

Welcome to Kountry Kitchen, a truly quaint small town cafe nestled in the heart of Franklin, NC. As an owner-operator, we pour our heart into every dish, serving up the hearty, comforting Southern fare that locals and visitors alike have cherished for years.

From our fluffy eggs cooked just the way you like them, to our famous biscuits and gravy, every meal is prepared with a touch of home and a commitment to flavor. We believe in good food, warm smiles, and creating a space where everyone feels like family. Come on down and taste the difference that genuine Kountry hospitality makes.
