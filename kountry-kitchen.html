Our Heritage

A Taste of Franklin's Heart & Soul

Nestled in the picturesque mountains of Franklin, NC, Kountry Kitchen is more than just a diner; it's a cherished local institution. For years, we've been serving up comforting Southern fare with a side of warm, small-town hospitality. We believe in the power of a good meal to bring people together, creating memories over plates piled high with flavor.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to offer hearty, home-style cooking reminiscent of grandma's kitchen, using fresh, quality ingredients. Every morning, the aroma of brewing coffee and sizzling bacon fills our cozy cafe, inviting you to start your day the Kountry Kitchen way. It's a place where everyone is family, and every dish is prepared with genuine care.

Many of our guests tell us stories about their eggs being cooked just right, with that perfect soft yolk, or how our biscuits remind them of home. We pour our heart into every detail, ensuring that from your first sip of coffee to your last bite of gravy-smothered goodness, your experience is nothing short of excellent.
