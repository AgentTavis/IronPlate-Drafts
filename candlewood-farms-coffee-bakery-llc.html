Our Story

Crafting Comfort, One Loaf at a Time

Candlewood Farms Coffee & Bakery is more than just a place to eat; it's a labor of love dedicated to the art of baking and brewing. Nestled in the heart of Whiteville, NC, we've built our reputation on the simple principle of serving exceptional quality in a warm, welcoming environment.

From our signature artisanal sourdough breads, baked fresh daily, to our carefully selected, aromatic coffee blends, every item on our menu is prepared with passion and precision. We believe in wholesome ingredients, traditional methods, and the joy of sharing good food with our community.

Our Offerings

Baked Goodness & Brewed Perfection

While our daily specials are ever-changing, here's a glimpse into the categories of deliciousness you can expect at Candlewood Farms. We prioritize seasonal ingredients and fresh preparations!

Artisan Sourdough Breads

Our foundational offering. Crafted with natural starters, slow-fermented, and baked to a perfect crust and airy crumb.

Freshly Baked Pastries

A rotating selection of croissants, muffins, scones, and other delightful treats, perfect for any time of day.

Signature Coffee Blends

Carefully sourced beans, roasted to bring out rich, complex flavors. Available as drip coffee or pour-over.

Espresso Creations

Expertly pulled shots, lattes, cappuccinos, and macchiatos crafted with precision and care.

Specialty Teas & Drinks

A curated selection of herbal and black teas, refreshing iced options, and seasonal beverages.

Breakfast & Lunch Sandwiches

Hearty and delicious sandwiches, often featuring our fresh sourdough, with savory fillings.

Our menu items rotate daily based on fresh ingredients and seasonal availability. Please visit us or call to inquire about today's full selections!
