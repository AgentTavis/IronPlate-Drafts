More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience

At Cerro Grande, we believe in bringing the vibrant, rich culinary traditions of Mexico right to your plate here in Conover, NC. From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a customer, you're family. We pour our heart into every dish, creating authentic flavors that transport you straight to the heart of Mexico.

Our commitment to fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes, like our signature Carne Al Carbon, has made us a beloved staple in the community. It's a big thank you to our incredible customers who've made us their local favorite since 2010. We look forward to sharing our passion with you!
