Our Story

More Than Just a Meal: A Taste of Mexico in Every Dish.

From our kitchen to your table, Cerro Grande brings the vibrant tastes of Mexico to Conover, NC. We're proud to be a local favorite, serving up authentic dishes crafted with passion and the freshest ingredients.

Our renowned Carne al Carbon is a standout, a testament to our commitment to traditional flavors and culinary excellence. Every dish is a celebration of Mexican heritage, prepared with love and care.

A big thank you to our incredible community for making us a cherished spot. We wouldn't be here without your continuous support and we strive every day to provide an unforgettable dining experience.
