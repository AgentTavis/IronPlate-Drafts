Our Story

Fresh Ingredients, Local Flavor, Heartfelt Cuisine.

Welcome to Fresh Depot, your culinary home right here on 321 in Granite Falls, NC. We believe that truly great food starts with the best ingredients. That's why we're dedicated to cooking fresh for you, every single day.

Our specialty lies in crafting incredible sandwiches on freshly made breads, accompanied by farm-to-table plates and vibrant vegetables. We take immense pride in supporting our community, sourcing our premium veggies and beef directly from local farmers. This commitment to local, fresh produce ensures every dish we serve is packed with flavor and supports the heartbeat of our region.

From our mouthwatering Wagyu burgers to our hearty spaghetti salad specials and the freshest seafood, each bite tells a story of quality and passion. Come experience the difference that fresh, local, and lovingly prepared food makes.
