Our Journey

Where Freshness Meets Flavor in Granite Falls

Welcome to Fresh Depot, your neighborhood spot right here on 321 in Granite Falls. We're passionate about bringing you delicious, high-quality seafood and a range of other amazing dishes, all cooked fresh daily. Our commitment to freshness starts with our ingredients.

We pride ourselves on our 'farm to table' approach, carefully sourcing our vegetables and premium beef directly from local farmers. This ensures every bite you take is bursting with flavor and supports our community. From our mouthwatering burgers to our hearty spaghetti salad specials, everything is prepared with care and a touch of local love.
