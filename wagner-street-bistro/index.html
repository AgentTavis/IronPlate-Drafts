Welcome to the Family

A Family Kitchen
on Wagner Street

Wagner Street Bistro is a small, family-owned restaurant right here in Troutman. Shane and Shaunna Dooley built this place on a simple idea: serve real food, made from scratch, with the kind of care you only get from a family kitchen.

Every soup is homemade. Every meatball is rolled by hand. Every plate goes out the way they'd serve it to their own family — because most of the time, they kind of are.
