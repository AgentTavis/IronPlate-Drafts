Meet the Dooleys

A Family Kitchen on Wagner Street

Shane and Shaunna Dooley didn't open Wagner Street Bistro to chase a trend. They opened it to serve real food, made from scratch, in a place that feels like home. They cut the ribbon on Wagner Street with a dream, a stack of family recipes, and the kind of work ethic that small-town businesses are built on.

This isn't a chain. It's not a franchise. It's Shane in the kitchen rolling meatballs by hand. It's Shaunna making sure every guest feels welcome the moment they walk through the door. It's a small team in matching black tees and striped aprons, cooking your food the way they'd cook it for their own family.

The menu draws from Italian roots with an American heart. Fresh soups made daily. Sauces that simmer for hours. Paninis pressed to order. Burgers stacked tall on brioche. And every day, there's something new on the specials board — because Shane and Shaunna believe regulars deserve a reason to keep coming back.
