Our Roots

Bringing Comfort & Flavor to Smithfield, Daily.

At Diner, we believe in serving up more than just food; we serve moments. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll experience genuinely great service that makes you feel right at home. Every dish is crafted hot and fresh to order, ensuring quality in every bite.

While many of our regulars rave about our breakfast, our lunch specials are just as legendary. Take, for instance, our Wednesday special: spaghetti with meatballs, often praised alongside savory garlic bread. If you're looking for classic comfort, our simple hamburger is a revelation – hand-pattied, juicy, and free from fillers. Paired with LC's homemade potato chips, it's a meal that truly satisfies. We're proud to be a local favorite, delivering delicious, hearty meals that bring smiles to faces across Smithfield, NC.
