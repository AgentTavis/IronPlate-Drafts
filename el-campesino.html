Our Story

Generations of Flavor, Crafted with Heart.

El Campesino in Columbus, NC, is more than just a restaurant; it's a culinary journey rooted in authentic Mexican tradition. For years, we've built a cherished reputation for our generous portions, lightning-fast service, and an unwavering commitment to genuine flavors. Every dish, from our hearty Burritos to our sizzling Tacos, is meticulously prepared by skilled hands, ensuring an experience that transports you straight to the heart of Mexico.

We pride ourselves on offering a diverse menu that caters to all, including vegetarian-friendly options, with comfortable seating, efficient table service, convenient takeout options, and full wheelchair accessibility. Come, discover the taste of true Mexican hospitality and join our family at El Campesino.

Our Menu

Savor the Flavors of Mexico.

Tacos

Experience the vibrant essence of Mexico with our authentic street-style tacos. Each one is meticulously prepared, bursting with fresh, seasoned fillings and traditional toppings that deliver an explosion of flavor in every bite.

Enchiladas

Indulge in our classic enchiladas, a symphony of rich, savory fillings encased in soft tortillas, smothered in our signature sauces and melted cheese. A comforting and deeply satisfying dish that captures the heart of Mexican cuisine.

Burritos

Our burritos are legendary for their generous portions and incredible taste. Packed with your favorite tender meats, fresh vegetables, rice, and beans, then perfectly wrapped, they're a complete and hearty meal designed to satisfy.
