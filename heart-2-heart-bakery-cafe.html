Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

At Heart 2 Heart Bakery & Cafe, we believe in bringing people together. Nestled in the heart of Cornelius, NC, we've cultivated a warm and inviting space where delicious desserts, soulful jazz, and genuine fellowship create unforgettable moments.

From our handcrafted popular desserts like the decadent Red Velvet and comforting Pies, to our aromatic Coffee Cake, every item is made with a sprinkle of love and a dash of passion. We're proud to be a local favorite, not just for our treats, but for the vibrant community we've built.

Come for a sweet escape, stay for the live jazz, and leave with a heart full of joy. We're Heart 2 Heart, and we're here to share our passion with you.

Our Delights

Popular Desserts & More

Red Velvet

A classic favorite, our rich and moist red velvet cake with luscious cream cheese frosting. A perfect balance of sweetness and tang.

Pies

Hand-baked daily with fresh ingredients, our pies offer a taste of home. From fruit to cream, find your perfect slice of comfort.

Coffee Cake

Perfectly spiced and topped with a buttery crumble, our coffee cake is the ideal companion to your morning brew or afternoon tea.

Freshly Brewed Coffee

Start your day right or enjoy an afternoon pick-me-up with our expertly brewed coffee, a perfect complement to any of our desserts.

Assorted Pastries

Discover a rotating selection of delightful pastries, from flaky croissants to sweet danishes, baked fresh every morning.

Signature Smoothies

Refresh yourself with our creamy, fruit-filled smoothies. A healthy and delicious option to invigorate your day.
