Our Legacy

Our Passion for Flavor, Served Daily.

At Chicken Kitchen, we believe great food brings people together. For years, we've been a beloved local spot in Williamston, NC, serving up classic bar and grill favorites with our unique twist.

We're proud to be the home of the legendary Chop-Chop® Bowl and our secret Mustard'n Curry® Sauce, crafted with love and fresh ingredients. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a community cornerstone, where every meal is prepared with care and served with a smile. Come on in, pull up a chair, and experience the warm, friendly atmosphere that makes us a true local favorite.

Our Delicious Offerings

Feast Your Eyes & Taste Buds

Chop-Chop® Bowl

Our signature creation! Crispy fried chicken chopped and mixed with rice, fresh veggies, and drizzled with our famous Mustard'n Curry® Sauce. A flavor explosion in every bite!

Mustard'n Curry® Fried Chicken Platter

Two pieces of our perfectly seasoned fried chicken, served with a side of golden fries and a generous helping of our unique Mustard'n Curry® Sauce for dipping.

Chicken & Waffles

Crispy fried chicken tenders served atop fluffy, golden waffles with a side of syrup and our special hot sauce. A brunch favorite, any time of day!

Spicy Chicken Sandwich

Juicy fried chicken breast, pickles, and spicy mayo on a toasted brioche bun. Served with a side of crispy onion rings.

Family Feast Bucket

A generous bucket of our classic fried chicken (8 pieces), large fries, and your choice of two signature sauces. Perfect for sharing!

Loaded Fries

Golden crispy fries topped with melted cheddar cheese, chopped bacon, jalapeños, and a drizzle of ranch dressing.
