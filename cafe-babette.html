Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Memory

My wife and I had a running joke for a long time that this wasn't a real restaurant. She'd eaten here many times in the past with her parents, but I'd never been past when they'd looked open. Well, the cars park down the side, and you won't see a line out the door most of the time.

That's because the man at the counter moves things along, and there's plenty of space to sit and enjoy your food. No, the tables aren't fancy, and they may need brushing off, but there's real kindness in this place, making every visit feel like coming home.

"Stepping into Cafe Babette is like stepping back in time. The warmth and genuine smiles are as comforting as their classic breakfast. A true Shelby gem!"

— A Loyal Customer
