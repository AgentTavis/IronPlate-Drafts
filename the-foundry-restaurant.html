OUR STORY

A Legacy Forged in Flavor

At The Foundry, we forge exceptional food and drink every day. Our culinary team takes raw ingredients and crafts beautifully finished, bespoke plates, perfect for a casual lunch or an anniversary dinner. We are passionate about showcasing local and regional house-crafted selections, emphasizing healthy, freshly made, and flavorful fare with a distinct Southern twist.

Each dish is thoughtfully prepared to pair deliciously well with our Club’s signature drink, the refreshing Back Porch Sweet Tea. The entire experience at The Foundry is centered on three tried-and-true principles: fresh ingredients, culinary craftsmanship, and a warm, inviting atmosphere that makes every guest feel at home.
