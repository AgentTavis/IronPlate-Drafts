OUR STORY

Where Craft Meets Comfort

At The Foundry, we forge exceptional food and drink every day. Our culinary team takes raw ingredients and crafts beautifully finished, bespoke plates, perfect for casual lunch or an anniversary dinner. Featuring local and regional house-crafted selections, the restaurant emphasizes healthy, freshly made and flavorful fare with a Southern twist, all pairing deliciously well with the Club’s signature drink, the Back Porch Sweet Tea.

The experience at The Foundry is centered on three tried-and-true principles: fresh ingredients, culinary artistry, and genuine hospitality. We believe in creating memorable moments through exquisite flavors and a welcoming atmosphere, making us Denver, NC's premier dining destination.
