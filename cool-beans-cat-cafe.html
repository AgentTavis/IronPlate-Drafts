OUR STORY

A Community Hub, Brewed with Love.

Cool Beans Cat Cafe is a beloved local spot in Gastonia, NC. More than just a cafe, we're a warm, inviting space where great coffee meets purr-fect companionship. We've poured our hearts into creating a place where you can relax, connect, and enjoy the company of our adorable resident cats, all while savoring delicious diner-style treats.

Our journey began with a simple dream: to create a unique haven where people could unwind, enjoy quality food and drink, and experience the undeniable joy that comes from spending time with rescued felines. Every visit supports our mission and helps provide a loving environment for our cats until they find their forever homes.

"Cool Beans is my happy place! The atmosphere is so cozy, and the cats are just delightful. It’s the perfect spot to unwind and truly disconnect from the hustle and bustle. A gem for Gastonia!"

— A Happy Regular, Gastonia Resident
