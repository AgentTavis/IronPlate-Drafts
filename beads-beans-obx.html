Our Journey

More Than Just Coffee & Gifts: It's a Family Tradition

At Beads & Beans OBX, we're not just a coffee house; we're a cherished part of the Hatteras Island community. As the only family-owned and operated spot of our kind in Rodanthe, we pour our heart into every cup and every unique item in our gift boutique.

We pride ourselves on supporting independent artists and craftspeople, offering a curated selection that captures the spirit of the Outer Banks. Our regulars know us for our laid-back "Island time" vibe – a friendly reminder to relax and savor the moment.

From the aroma of freshly brewed coffee to the delightful treasures awaiting discovery, Beads & Beans OBX is a place where memories are made. We make a limited number of each food item daily, ensuring freshness and quality. Our popular breakfast sandwiches and burritos often sell out early, so for those early birds, a warm biscuit or a hearty burrito awaits!
