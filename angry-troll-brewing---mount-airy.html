Our Story

Where Life Brews Flavor

Hey there. And thanks for stopping by. We’re Mount Airy's local microbrewery and restaurant, nestled in the historic Old Belk's building and Prather Block in vibrant downtown Mount Airy. Our journey began when a resourceful troll, whose bridge was demolished, decided to turn adversity into opportunity. As he wisely pointed out — when life tears down your bridge, you start a brewery.

What emerged from that playful philosophy has grown into a special gathering spot, not just for Elkin, but now deeply rooted in the hearts of Mount Airy residents. We're proud to serve up delicious hearth-fired pizza, flavorful wings, and classic pub fare alongside our unique craft brews, all in a welcoming, community-focused atmosphere.
