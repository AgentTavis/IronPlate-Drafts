Our Roots

A Taste of Home in Cliffside

In the heart of Mooresboro's historic mill town, where community spirit shines bright, Cliffside Diner stands as a unique beacon. We're more than just a place to eat; we're a gathering spot, a local institution, and virtually the only restaurant bringing comfort and flavor to Cliffside.

Step into our welcoming space and discover a delicious serving line offering three mouth-watering meat choices daily, alongside a rotating selection of classic diner favorites. We believe in hearty meals, friendly faces, and a taste of home that keeps our neighbors coming back, day after day.

"Cliffside Diner is the heart of our town. It's not just about the amazing food, but the warm smiles and genuine welcome you get every time you walk through the door. A true local treasure!"
