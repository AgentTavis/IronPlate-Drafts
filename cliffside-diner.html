Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Mooresboro Tradition.

Welcome to Cliffside Diner, a true gem in the heart of Mooresboro, North Carolina. Nestled in our historic town, we pride ourselves on being a cornerstone for the community. The Dutch Broad Cafe is unique as virtually the only restaurant in the crumbling mill town of Cliffside. We continue this legacy, offering a serving line with three delicious meat choices daily, ensuring there's always something fresh and hearty to enjoy.

We're dedicated to providing comforting, homestyle meals made with care. Every dish tells a story, steeped in local flavor and tradition, from our classic diner fare to our evolving specials. Come experience the warmth and taste that makes us a local favorite.
