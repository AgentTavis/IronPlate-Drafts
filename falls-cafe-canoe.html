Our Journey

A Taste of Home, Crafted with Love.

Falls Cafe & Canoe is more than just a diner; it's a culinary experience rooted in the heart of Granite Falls. We are a women-owned establishment taking immense pride in our strict from-scratch approach, sourcing local goods to bring you the freshest breakfast, lunch, and brunch.

Beyond our American, Cafe, Diner, Native American, and Armenian cuisines, we celebrate community with water recreational rentals, unique art, and thoughtful gifts. Every dish tells a story, every visit builds a memory. Come discover your new favorite spot where tradition meets innovation, and every bite feels like home.
