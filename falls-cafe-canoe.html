Our Story

Crafting Freshness, Building Community.

Welcome to Falls Cafe & Canoe, a beloved women-owned diner nestled in the heart of Granite Falls, Minnesota. We're more than just a place to eat; we're a community hub where delicious food meets local adventure.

Our passion lies in a strict from-scratch approach to every dish, from the first crack of dawn for breakfast to our satisfying lunch offerings. We meticulously source local goods, ensuring every bite is fresh, wholesome, and supports our incredible regional farmers and artisans.

But the Falls Cafe & Canoe experience extends beyond the plate. We're proud to offer water recreation rentals, perfect for exploring the beautiful surroundings of Granite Falls. Browse our selection of local art and unique gifts, discovering a piece of Minnesota to take home with you. Whether you're craving a hearty brunch, a classic American diner meal, or simply a warm cup of coffee, we invite you to taste the difference that pride, passion, and local love make.

"Every dish at Falls Cafe & Canoe feels like a homemade meal. The dedication to local ingredients and the cozy atmosphere make it my favorite spot in town!"
