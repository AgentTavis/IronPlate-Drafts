Our Story

Crafting Community, One Sip at a Time.

Welcome to Blackwater Cider LLC

Nestled in the heart of Windsor, NC, Blackwater Cider LLC is more than just a bar and grill – it's a vibrant community hub where friends and family gather. We believe in creating memorable experiences, whether you're joining us for a delicious meal, sampling our unique cider concoctions, or simply enjoying the lively atmosphere.

Our passion lies in bringing people together over great food and exceptional drinks. We're proud to offer a casual, inviting space perfect for groups, with the added bonus of dog-friendly outdoor seating. From the moment you walk in, you're part of the Blackwater family.
