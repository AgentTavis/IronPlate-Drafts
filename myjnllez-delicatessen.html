OUR STORY

The Heart and Soul of Princeville BBQ.

At MyJnlle’Z Delicatessen, we believe in honest, slow-smoked BBQ and a plate full of Southern comfort. Nestled right in the heart of Princeville, NC, our deli is more than just a restaurant – it's a taste of tradition, a gathering place for friends and family, and a proud part of our community's rich heritage.

Every dish is crafted with passion, using time-honored recipes passed down through generations. From our signature tangy BBQ sauce to sides made from scratch, we pour our heart into creating flavors that warm the soul and bring people back for more. Come experience the true essence of Princeville's culinary spirit.
