Our Journey

Experience the Heart of Princeville BBQ

At MyJnlle’Z Delicatessen, we're more than just a place to eat – we're a cornerstone of the Princeville community. Nestled at 306 S. Main Street, we proudly serve up authentic, mouth-watering BBQ and classic deli delights that have become a local legend.

Our commitment is simple: bring fresh, flavorful, and unforgettable meals to your table, prepared with passion and served with a smile. From our tender, slow-cooked pork smothered in tangy BBQ sauce to our perfectly fried whiting fish, every dish tells a story of tradition and quality. Come on in and taste the difference!
