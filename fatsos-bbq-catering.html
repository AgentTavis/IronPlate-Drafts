Our Roots, Our Passion

From Our Pit to Your Plate: A Tradition of Flavor.

Fatsos BBQ & Catering is a beloved local spot where every dish, from our succulent pulled pork to our smoky burnt ends, is crafted with passion and dedication. We believe in fast service without compromising on the deep, rich flavors that define authentic Southern BBQ.

What started as a dream to share genuine, slow-smoked meats with our community has grown into a cornerstone of the Pickens culinary scene. We pour our hearts into every recipe, ensuring each bite is absolutely delicious and truly unforgettable. Come taste the difference that real pitmaster expertise makes!

Why Fatsos BBQ is Pickens' Favorite
