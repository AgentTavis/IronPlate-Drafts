About Us

Welcome to The Farmers Deli – where tradition meets fresh, quality ingredients. Located in the heart of Wilkesboro, NC, we've been serving our community with authentic, farm-sourced sandwiches, salads, and deli favorites since our opening.

Our commitment to supporting local farmers and using the freshest ingredients available sets us apart. Every sandwich is made with care, every salad is crafted with pride, and every customer leaves with a smile.

Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunch or gathering with friends and family, The Farmers Deli is your neighborhood destination for genuine, wholesome food.
