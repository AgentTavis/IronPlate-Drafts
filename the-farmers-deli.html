Our Story

The Farmers Deli was born from a simple belief: great food starts with great ingredients. For over a decade, we've partnered with local farmers and producers throughout the Wilkes County region to bring you the freshest, most flavorful deli experience possible.

Every sandwich, every salad, every side is crafted with intention and care. We believe in supporting our community and celebrating the bounty of local agriculture.
