Our Story

A Family Tradition, Baked with Love

The Farmers Deli, nestled in the heart of North Wilkesboro, NC, isn't just a place to eat; it's a labor of love, brought to you by our family. We believe in the simple pleasure of good food, made with care and served with a warm smile.

Our cozy atmosphere is built around a dedication to fresh, locally sourced ingredients. From the moment you walk through our doors, you'll feel the difference that comes from a commitment to quality and community. We're proud to be a local staple, serving up sandwiches that truly taste like home.
