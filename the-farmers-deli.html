About The Farmers Deli

Since opening our doors in Wilkesboro, The Farmers Deli has been committed to celebrating local farmers and providing the freshest, most authentic farm-to-table experience in the region.

We believe in quality over quantity. Every sandwich, salad, and side dish is crafted with locally sourced ingredients, seasonal produce, and sustainable practices that honor our community and the land.

From our signature heritage bread to hand-selected cured meats and artisan cheeses, every element tells a story of local craftsmanship and agricultural pride.
