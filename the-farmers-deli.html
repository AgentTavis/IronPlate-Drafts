Our Roots

Our Family's Passion for Fresh, Homemade Flavors

Welcome to The Farmers Deli, a place where fresh, locally sourced ingredients and family tradition come together to create unforgettable meals. Nestled in the heart of North Wilkesboro, NC, our deli is more than just a restaurant; it's a labor of love.

We believe that the best food starts with the best ingredients. That's why we meticulously select produce, meats, and cheeses from local farms and trusted suppliers. Every sandwich, salad, and cold cut is prepared with care, ensuring a taste of home in every bite. Our signature difference? The bread, baked fresh in-house every single day, just like grandma used to make.

As a family-owned and operated establishment, we pour our hearts into providing a cozy atmosphere and friendly service that makes every customer feel like part of our extended family. Come experience the warmth, the quality, and the true taste of North Wilkesboro at The Farmers Deli.
