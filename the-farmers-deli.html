About Us

Local Ingredients, Genuine Flavor

The Farmers Deli has been a cornerstone of the Wilkesboro community since 2015, dedicated to bringing the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients to your table.

We partner directly with local farmers and suppliers to ensure every sandwich, salad, and side is made with produce and meats you can trust. From our signature turkey clubs to our seasonal vegetable specials, we believe in supporting our community while delivering exceptional taste.

Visit us today and experience the difference that fresh, local ingredients make in every bite.
