Our Heritage

A Taste of Glade Valley Tradition, Crafted with Love

Nestled in the heart of Glade Valley, NC, Circle L Family Restaurant at 3238 US Highway 21 S has been a cherished local gem for families and friends.

We pride ourselves on offering a warm, inviting dining experience where every meal feels like coming home. From hearty breakfast plates to satisfying dinner entrées, our kitchen is dedicated to serving up classic American comfort food that brings smiles to every face.

We believe in good food, great company, and making memories around the table. Come on in and become part of our story!
