Our Story

Serving Generations, Building Memories.

Circle "L" Family Restaurant in Glade Valley, NC, offers a warm and inviting dining experience for families and friends. For decades, we've been a beloved local hub, serving hearty American Bar & Grill classics with a smile. Our commitment is simple: delicious food, genuine hospitality, and a place where everyone feels at home.

We pride ourselves on being a cornerstone of the Alleghany County community, a place where locals gather and visitors are welcomed like family. Come gather around our table and create your own cherished memories with us – we can't wait to welcome you!
