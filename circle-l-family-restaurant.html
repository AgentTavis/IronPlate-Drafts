OUR STORY

Rooted in Community

Circle "L" Family Restaurant, nestled in the heart of Glade Valley, NC, has been a beloved community hub for generations. We pride ourselves on offering a warm, inviting dining experience where families and friends can gather, share good food, and make lasting memories. Our commitment to classic comfort food and genuine hospitality makes every visit feel like coming home.

From hearty breakfast plates to satisfying lunch and dinner options, our kitchen serves up delicious bar and grill favorites crafted with care. We believe in creating a place where everyone feels welcome, whether you're a local regular or just passing through Alleghany County.
