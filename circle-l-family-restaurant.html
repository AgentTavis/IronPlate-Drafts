Our Heritage

Generations of Flavor, Family, and Fun in Glade Valley

Nestled in the heart of Glade Valley, Circle L Family Restaurant is more than just a place to eat – it's a cornerstone of our community. Since our humble beginnings, we've been dedicated to serving up hearty, homestyle bar and grill favorites in a warm, inviting atmosphere that feels like an extension of your own home.

We believe in good food, great company, and making every guest feel like family. From sizzling burgers and classic sandwiches to comforting entrees, every dish is prepared with care, using fresh ingredients and a whole lot of love. Come as you are, pull up a chair, and let us take care of the rest. We're proud to be a part of your family's traditions and create new memories together.
