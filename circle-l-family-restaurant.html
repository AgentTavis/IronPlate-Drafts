Our Story

Generations of Flavor, Rooted in Glade Valley

Circle "L" Family Restaurant in Glade Valley, NC, offers a warm and inviting dining experience for families and friends alike. Nestled right on US Highway 21 S, we've been a beloved local gathering spot, serving up honest, delicious food with a smile.

Our commitment is to provide a comforting atmosphere where every meal feels like coming home. We believe in classic American comfort, generous portions, and a community spirit that makes every visit special.
