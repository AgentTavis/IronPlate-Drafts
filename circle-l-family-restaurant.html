Our Legacy

More Than Just Food, It's Family.

Rooted in Glade Valley

Circle "L" Family Restaurant in Glade Valley, NC, has been a cherished local establishment, offering a warm and inviting dining experience for families and friends for years. Our doors opened with a simple vision: to create a place where everyone feels at home, where the food is hearty, and the laughter flows freely.

We pride ourselves on being more than just a place to eat; we're a community hub where memories are made, stories are shared, and good times are had by all. From our classic bar and grill favorites to our friendly service, every detail is crafted with our guests in mind.
