Our Story

A Taste of Home, Rooted in Alleghany County.

Circle "L" Family Restaurant, nestled at 3238 US Highway 21 S in Glade Valley, NC, has been a cherished local gathering spot for decades. We offer a warm and inviting dining experience perfect for families, friends, and anyone looking for hearty, delicious food in a comfortable setting.

From our family to yours, we pour our heart into every dish, creating a welcoming atmosphere where good food and great company come together. We're proud to be a part of the Glade Valley community, serving up smiles alongside our classic Bar And Grill favorites.

"Circle L truly feels like home. The atmosphere is always friendly, and you can tell the food is made with care. A true Glade Valley gem!"

— A Loyal Customer, Glade Valley
