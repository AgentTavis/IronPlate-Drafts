Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Family Tradition.

Nestled in the heart of Glade Valley, Circle "L" Family Restaurant has been a beloved community cornerstone. We've built our reputation on serving hearty, delicious bar & grill classics in a warm, inviting atmosphere.

From our family to yours, we believe in fresh ingredients, generous portions, and creating a space where memories are made. Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunch or a relaxed family dinner, you'll always find a friendly face and a plate full of comfort.

Our Delicious Offerings

Classic Bar & Grill Favorites, Cooked to Perfection.

Classic Cheeseburger

A juicy beef patty grilled to perfection, topped with melted cheddar, crisp lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles on a toasted bun.

Crispy Chicken Tenders

Golden-fried chicken tenders served with your choice of dipping sauce. A family favorite!

Hearty BBQ Ribs

Slow-cooked, fall-off-the-bone ribs smothered in our signature tangy BBQ sauce.

Fresh Garden Salad

A vibrant mix of fresh greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and bell peppers with your choice of dressing.

Loaded Nachos

Crispy tortilla chips piled high with melted cheese, seasoned beef, jalapeños, salsa, and sour cream.

Spicy Buffalo Wings

Ten crispy wings tossed in our fiery buffalo sauce, served with cool ranch or blue cheese.

Grilled Salmon

Lightly seasoned and perfectly grilled salmon fillet, served with a side of steamed vegetables.

Homestyle Fries

Perfectly seasoned, golden-brown crispy fries. The ideal side for any meal.
