Our Heritage

Generations of Flavor, Steps from the Sand

Since February 14th, 2022, Ocean View Restaurant has quickly become a beloved fixture in Kure Beach. We may not offer an ocean view from our windows, but step outside and you're just moments from the pristine sands, ready to explore.

What we do offer is a warm, bustling atmosphere, friendly service, and generous portions of classic diner fare with a coastal twist. From our fluffy veggie omelettes packed with fresh spinach, tomatoes, and mushrooms to our crispy chicken and waffles that leave you wanting more (and often taking some home!), every dish is crafted with care and passion.

We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming space where locals and visitors alike can enjoy a hearty meal that feels like home. Join the community who knows that an open spot at Ocean View is a true rarity – a testament to the unforgettable experience we provide, serving up smiles and delicious food seven days a week (except Tuesday!).

— A Happy Diner, Kure Beach
