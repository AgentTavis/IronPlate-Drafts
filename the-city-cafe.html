Our Roots

Welcome to THE City Cafe – Where Good Food & Community Meet!

Nestled in the heart of Williamston, NC, THE City Cafe is more than just a diner; it's a warm, welcoming gathering spot for friends, families, and travelers alike. We're proud to be a barbecue restaurant, serving up classic, soulful dishes made with love and local flair.

As soon as you walk in, you'll feel right at home. We believe in genuine Southern hospitality and food that truly hits the spot, just like one of our guests said: "Felt welcome as soon as me and family walked in! The food was amazing and hit the spot as we adventure back home from the beach."

Our walls are adorned with beautiful local artwork, available for sale, celebrating the vibrant creativity of our community. Every meal here is an experience – a taste of tradition, a friendly face, and a connection to Williamston.

Our Promise

More Than Just a Meal

Authentic BBQ Excellence

As a proud barbecue restaurant, we meticulously slow-smoke our meats, from the Pulled Pork to the Brisket, ensuring every bite is packed with traditional Southern flavor. It's the food that truly "hits the spot" for every guest.

Warm & Welcoming Atmosphere

Step inside and instantly feel at home. Our friendly team and cozy diner ambiance ensure you and your family "felt welcome as soon as we walked in." We're your go-to spot for genuine hospitality.

Local Art & Community Love

Beyond the plate, we celebrate local talent. Our walls feature local artwork for sale, turning your dining experience into a cultural journey. Support local artists while enjoying Williamston's best BBQ!
