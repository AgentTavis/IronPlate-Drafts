Our Story

Bringing Classic American Flavors to Sparta's South Side

Welcome to South Side Caddy Shack, Sparta's newest drive-thru and drive-in restaurant! We're proud to open our doors at 544 South Main Street, bringing a fresh, convenient, and delicious dining experience to our beloved community.

Our vision is simple: serve up classic American comfort food with a smile, fast and fresh. From juicy burgers to loaded hotdogs and satisfying sandwiches, we've crafted a menu designed for everyone to enjoy on the go or right here in our friendly drive-in atmosphere. We believe in good food, great service, and being a welcoming spot for friends and family.
