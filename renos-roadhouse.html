Our Heritage

More Than Just Great Food

Reno’s Roadhouse is not just a place to enjoy delicious meals—it’s a full-service dining destination that focuses on exceptional experiences and genuine hospitality. We believe in creating a welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels like family, whether you're here for a quick bite or a memorable family dinner.

From our hand-cut steaks to our fluffy pancakes, every dish is crafted with care, using fresh ingredients. We're proud to be a cornerstone of the Somerset, NC community, serving up comfort food classics with a modern twist.
