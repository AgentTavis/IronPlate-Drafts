Our Heritage

More Than Just Donuts – A Taste of Kernersville Tradition

Since its humble beginnings, Intown Donutz has been Kernersville's cherished spot for freshly baked donuts and comforting diner fare. We believe in starting your day, or sweetening your afternoon, with the perfect bite. Our commitment to quality ingredients and time-honored recipes ensures every visit is a memorable one.

From our signature Apple Fritters to the rich Maple Bacon Bars, every item is crafted with love and a sprinkle of tradition. We're more than just a donut shop; we're a community gathering place where friendly faces, hot coffee, and warm, delicious treats make every visit special. Come on in, or grab a drive-thru delight, and taste the difference that passion makes.
