Our Heritage

A Taste of Elegance in the Heart of Hudson

Welcome to The Allard Bakery and Bistro, where every dish tells a story of passion, tradition, and local flavor. Nestled in the vibrant heart of downtown Hudson, NC, we bring a unique French-American dining experience to your table. Our commitment lies in crafting exquisite meals using only the finest, simplest, and freshest local ingredients.

We believe in the power of good food to bring people together, creating memorable moments around a table. From the warmth of our bakery to the inviting ambiance of our bistro, we invite you to savor the art of French cuisine, gracefully blended with the comforting essence of American favorites.
