Our Journey

Crafting Whiteville's Sweetest Memories

At Darel's Bakery & Sweets, we believe that every bite tells a story. We started with a simple passion: to bring the finest, freshest baked goods and comforting treats to our beloved Whiteville community. From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a customer; you're family.

We've poured our hearts into creating a place where you can find joy in a perfectly flaky croissant, celebrate milestones with a custom-crafted cake, or simply enjoy a moment of peace with a delicious cupcake. Our dedication to quality ingredients and time-honored recipes shines through in everything we make.
