OUR STORY

Where Lake Views Meet Family Flavors

LakeHouse offers two unique dining experiences for our guests to enjoy. Our upstairs dining area, where we accept reservations starting at 5 PM daily, offers an upscale casual dining experience with phenomenal views of the lake. Imagine enjoying a gourmet meal as the sun sets over the shimmering waters of Lake Lure.

Our downstairs dining area, seated on a first come-first serve basis, provides two distinct experiences: an inside sports bar & grill area and an outside patio seating area. Both downstairs dining options provide a laid-back atmosphere with, again, breathtaking views of Lake Lure and the surrounding Blue Ridge Mountains, perfect for a casual lunch, evening drinks, or catching the game.
