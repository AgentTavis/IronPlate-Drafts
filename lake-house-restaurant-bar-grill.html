Our Legacy

A Taste of Lake Lure, Rooted in Tradition

Lake House Restaurant Bar & Grill offers two unique dining experiences designed for every guest. Our inviting upstairs dining area, where we happily accept reservations starting at 5 PM daily, provides an upscale casual atmosphere complemented by phenomenal, panoramic views of the stunning Lake Lure.

For those seeking a more relaxed vibe, our downstairs dining area operates on a first-come, first-serve basis. Here, you can choose between our lively inside sports bar & grill or our serene outside patio seating area. Both downstairs options promise a laid-back atmosphere, again, with breathtaking views of the lake. Whether you're seeking an elegant evening or a relaxed meal with friends and family, Lake House offers the perfect setting to create lasting memories.
