Our Story

Where Every Visit Becomes a Cherished Memory.

At State of Grace, we believe in crafting more than just a meal – we offer an unforgettable experience. Our journey began with a passion for bringing the freshest coastal delights to the heart of Houston, infused with the warm, inviting spirit of Southern hospitality.

Our kitchen is a testament to quality, where every dish is prepared with meticulous attention to detail. From the perfectly salty oysters that capture the essence of the ocean to our exquisite desserts, including the decadent crème brûlée, each item on our menu is a celebration of flavor.

But it's not just the food that keeps our guests returning. It's the overall ambiance – a harmonious blend of refined elegance and comfortable charm – and the attentive service that ensures your drinks flow as freely as your conversations. We strive for every guest to leave with a full heart, a satisfied palate, and memories so vibrant, you'll forget to take pictures.

— A Happy Guest

Why Choose Us

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

Unforgettable Ambiance

Step into State of Grace and be enveloped by our unique atmosphere. We pride ourselves on creating an ambiance that is both elegant and incredibly welcoming, perfect for intimate dinners or lively gatherings. It’s an experience for all your senses.

Exceptional Service & Libations

Our dedicated team is committed to making your visit seamless and delightful. Enjoy expertly crafted cocktails and a wide selection of beverages, served with warmth and precision. Our attentive staff ensures your glass is never empty and your needs are always met.

Culinary Excellence
