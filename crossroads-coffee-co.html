Our Story

More Than Just Coffee, It's Community.

At Crossroads Coffee Co., we believe in the simple joy of good company, great food, and the perfect cup of coffee. Nestled in the heart of Vass, NC, we're a locally owned and operated diner where every guest is treated like family.

Our passion is to create a welcoming space for our community to gather, relax, and savor delicious, homemade meals. We're proud to be a local favorite, serving up smiles alongside our freshly brewed coffee and classic diner fare. Come on in, pull up a chair, and experience the warm, friendly atmosphere that makes Crossroads Coffee Co. a true gem.
