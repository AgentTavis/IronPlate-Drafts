About Plaza Del Sol

Welcome to Plaza Del Sol, where tradition meets flavor in the heart of Wilkesboro. For years, we've been serving authentic Mexican cuisine crafted with love and the finest ingredients.

Our mission is simple: bring the warmth, hospitality, and bold flavors of Mexico to your table. Every dish is prepared fresh by our dedicated team, ensuring an unforgettable dining experience.

From family recipes passed down through generations to innovative new creations, Plaza Del Sol celebrates the rich culinary heritage of Mexico while embracing the vibrant spirit of our Wilkesboro community.
