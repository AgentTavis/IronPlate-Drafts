About Us

Welcome to Plaza Del Sol, your destination for authentic Mexican cuisine in Wilkesboro, NC. Since opening our doors, we've been committed to bringing the vibrant flavors and warm hospitality of Mexico to our community.

We use only the freshest ingredients, time-honored recipes passed down through generations, and prepare everything with love and care. From our sizzling fajitas to our hand-rolled tortillas, every dish is crafted to transport your taste buds south of the border.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, enjoying a family dinner, or grabbing a quick lunch, Plaza Del Sol is your perfect choice for delicious Mexican food and exceptional service.
