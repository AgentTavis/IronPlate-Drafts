Our Culinary Delights

Explore Our Authentic Flavors

Classic Carnitas Plate

Slow-cooked pork, tender and juicy, served with rice, beans, and warm tortillas. A true Mexican favorite.

Signature Quesadillas

Melted cheese perfection with your choice of savory fillings, grilled to a golden crisp. A satisfying classic.

Loaded Queso & Chips

Our rich, creamy queso dip generously topped and served with a basket of crispy, house-made tortilla chips.

Build Your Own Tacos

Customize your perfect taco experience with fresh ingredients and a variety of authentic fillings.

Enchiladas Verdes

Corn tortillas filled with chicken or cheese, smothered in vibrant green salsa, served with rice and beans.

Chile Relleno

Poblano pepper stuffed with cheese, lightly battered and fried, topped with savory sauce. A traditional delight.
