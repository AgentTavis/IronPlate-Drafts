OUR STORY

Generations of Flavor

The culinary journey at Plaza Del Sol began with a simple passion: to bring the vibrant, authentic tastes of Mexico to Wilkesboro. We believe that great food brings people together, and every dish we serve is a reflection of our heritage and dedication to quality.

Indeed, many of our patrons call us the best Mexican restaurant they've experienced. We’ve heard it time and again – whether it’s their first visit or their third, the food is consistently hot, authentic, and delicious. Our team is committed to providing service that is not just excellent, but genuinely warm, making every visit a memorable culinary journey for you and your loved ones.
