Authentic Mexican Cuisine in Wilkesboro, NC

Welcome to Plaza Del Sol

Since opening our doors, Plaza Del Sol has been committed to bringing the authentic flavors of Mexico to Wilkesboro. We believe in using fresh, high-quality ingredients and traditional cooking methods to create dishes that transport you straight to Mexico.

Our family-run restaurant prides itself on exceptional customer service and a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're joining us for a quick lunch, celebrating a special occasion, or enjoying dinner with family, you'll experience the true taste of Mexico made with love and passion.

From our sizzling fajitas to our handmade tamales, every dish tells a story of tradition and flavor. We invite you to become part of the Plaza Del Sol family.
