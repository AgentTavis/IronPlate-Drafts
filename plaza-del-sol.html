Bienvenido a Plaza Del Sol

Welcome to Plaza Del Sol, where tradition meets flavor. For over a decade, we've been serving the Wilkesboro community with authentic Mexican cuisine prepared with passion and the finest ingredients.

Our family-owned restaurant celebrates the rich culinary heritage of Mexico, bringing you dishes that transport your taste buds south of the border.

  • Authentic family recipes
  • Fresh, quality ingredients
  • Vibrant, welcoming atmosphere
  • Expert preparation by skilled chefs
  • Full bar with premium tequilas
