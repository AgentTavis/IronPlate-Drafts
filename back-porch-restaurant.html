OUR STORY

Where Island Charm Meets Culinary Delight.

Welcome to Back Porch Restaurant, a beloved culinary gem nestled in the heart of Ocracoke, NC. For years, we've invited guests to unwind and savor delectable New American cuisine in our unique open-air setting.

Our signature three-level screened porch dining rooms offer a serene escape, perfect for enjoying a beautiful evening breeze, while our cozy interior provides a climate-controlled haven. We pride ourselves on fresh, locally-inspired dishes and a warm, inviting atmosphere that feels like home. Whether you're a long-time local or visiting our charming island, we look forward to providing an experience that captures the true spirit of Ocracoke.

"Every visit to Back Porch feels like coming home. The ambiance, the friendly service, and the incredible food make it a cornerstone of our Ocracoke trips."

— A Valued Regular
