Nestled in the heart of Hillsborough, Nash Street Tavern has been the go-to spot for locals and visitors alike seeking genuine good times. We believe in the power of live, local music to bring people together, creating an atmosphere buzzing with energy and camaraderie.

Our doors are always open, offering a welcoming space where strangers become friends and every night feels like a celebration. Forget the fuss – here, it's about authentic experiences, friendly faces, and a soundtrack that keeps your feet tapping.

Our Promise

Why Nash Street Tavern is Hillsborough's Top Pick.

Live & Local Music Every Night

We're dedicated to showcasing the incredible talent of local musicians. From acoustic sets to energetic bands, our stage is always alive, providing the perfect soundtrack to your evening. Never a dull moment!

No Cover. Always a Party!

Forget the hidden fees. At Nash Street Tavern, we believe good times should be accessible to everyone. Enjoy our vibrant atmosphere, fantastic music, and friendly crowd without ever paying a cover charge.
