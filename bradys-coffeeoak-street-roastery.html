Our Journey

Crafting Mt Airy's Perfect Brew, From Our Roastery to Your Cup.

The Heart of Mayberry Coffee Culture

At Bradys Coffee—Oak Street Roastery, we're more than just a coffee shop; we're a cornerstone of the Mt Airy community, deeply rooted in the "Mayberry" spirit of North Carolina. Our passion for exceptional coffee starts with carefully selected beans, roasted right here on Oak Street to unlock their fullest flavor and aroma. We believe in crafting an experience that warms the soul, invigorates the mind, and brings people together.

Every cup tells a story of dedication, quality, and local charm. Whether you're starting your day, catching up with friends, or simply savoring a quiet moment, Bradys Coffee provides the perfect setting. We're proud to be part of your daily ritual and a place where memories are brewed.
