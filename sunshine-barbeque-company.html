Our Heritage

Crafting Tradition, One Smoke Ring at a Time.

Sunshine Barbeque Company isn't just a place to eat; it's a Bostic tradition. Nestled right here in the heart of North Carolina, we're proud to be a beloved local spot. Our commitment is simple: bring you the finest, most authentic barbeque experience possible.

We specialize in small batch, 100% wood cooked, slow smoked NC pork and chicken, lovingly prepared over locally sourced hardwoods. Every bite tells a story of tradition, passion, and flavor that's been perfected over years. We believe in quality ingredients, time-honored techniques, and the joy of sharing great food with our community. Come taste why we're Bostic's favorite.

Our Promise

The Sunshine Difference: Taste the Tradition

100% Wood Cooked Perfection

We believe true BBQ flavor comes from the fire. Our meats are slow-smoked for hours over local hardwoods, never gas, delivering that unmistakable, deep smoky taste you crave.

Small Batch, Big Flavor

Quality over quantity is our mantra. Each batch of pork and chicken is prepared with meticulous care, ensuring every plate meets our high standards for tenderness and flavor.

A True Bostic Original

More than just a meal, Sunshine BBQ Co. is a community staple. We're a local family, serving our neighbors with pride, and passionate about bringing authentic NC BBQ to your table.
