Our Heritage

The Heart of Bostic's BBQ Tradition.

Sunshine Barbeque Company isn't just a place to eat; it's a taste of home and a testament to true Southern hospitality. Nestled right here in Bostic, NC, we're a beloved local spot built on a simple philosophy: quality ingredients, time-honored techniques, and a passion for flavor.

Every piece of pork and chicken we serve is 100% wood cooked, slow smoked to perfection over locally sourced wood, ensuring that authentic, smoky taste you can only get from true barbecue. We're proud to be a small-batch operation, meaning every plate gets the attention it deserves. Come on down and experience the difference that dedication makes.
