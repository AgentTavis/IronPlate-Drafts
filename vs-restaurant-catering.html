Our Heritage

Crafting Memories, One Plate at a Time

Nestled in the heart of Garysburg, NC, at 312 US Highway 301, V's Restaurant & Catering is more than just a meal – it's a celebration of local flavor and community. We believe in crafting a delightful dining experience, one person, one plate at a time, utilizing the freshest, locally sourced ingredients available. Our mission is to bring friends and family together over honest, delicious food, prepared with passion and served with genuine Southern hospitality.

For years, we've been a cornerstone of the Garysburg community, growing with every smile shared and every meal enjoyed. Come discover why V's is a cherished local favorite, where every bite tells a story of quality and care.
