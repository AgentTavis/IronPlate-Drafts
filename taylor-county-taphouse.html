Our Roots

Welcome to Our Slice of Texas in Abilene, NC

The Taphouse is a locally owned and operated Abilene restaurant and bar that specializes in what we like to call Texas Comfort Food. Our made-from-scratch menu is flanked by a huge selection of craft beer, craft cocktails, and a carefully chosen wine list.

The end result is a comfortable, casual environment with great food and drink served by a friendly and knowledgable staff. We’re proud of our little slice of Texas, and cordially invite you to come experience it for yourself…we hope to see you soon!

Our Flavors

Taste Our Signature Texas Comfort Food

Smoked Brisket Sandwich

Slow-smoked, tender brisket piled high on a brioche bun with our tangy BBQ sauce.

Jalapeño Popper Burger

A juicy patty topped with cream cheese, crispy jalapeños, and bacon on a toasted bun.

Chicken Fried Steak

Classic Texas comfort: golden-fried steak smothered in creamy gravy, served with sides.

BBQ Ribs Platter

Fall-off-the-bone ribs seasoned with our secret dry rub, slow-cooked to perfection.

Texas Chili Mac

Hearty beef chili, cheddar cheese, and elbow macaroni baked to a golden brown.

Pulled Pork Tacos

Smoky pulled pork, fresh slaw, and a zesty drizzle in warm tortillas.

Fried Catfish Plate

Crispy cornmeal-crusted catfish with hushpuppies and a side of tartar sauce.

Southern Pecan Pie

A sweet, rich pecan filling in a flaky crust, perfect for a Texas-sized finish.
