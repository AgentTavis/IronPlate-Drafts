Our Journey

Bringing Authentic Mexican Flavors to the Blue Ridge.

Nestled in the picturesque town of Banner Elk, Blue Ridge Restaurant & Cantina is more than just a place to eat—it's a vibrant culinary experience. We're proud to bring the rich, diverse flavors of Mexico to the heart of the Blue Ridge Mountains, serving traditional dishes made with passion and fresh ingredients.

Since opening our doors, our mission has been to create a welcoming atmosphere where friends and family can gather, share great food, and make lasting memories. From sizzling fajitas to savory enchiladas, every dish is crafted to transport your taste buds south of the border. Come discover why we're quickly becoming a local favorite!

Our Culinary Delights

Explore Our Vibrant Menu

Sizzling Fajitas

Our signature dish! Your choice of chicken, steak, or shrimp, served with sautéed peppers, onions, warm tortillas, and all the classic toppings.

Street Tacos

Authentic street-style tacos with your choice of carne asada, al pastor, or pollo. Topped with fresh cilantro and onions.

Enchiladas Verdes

Corn tortillas filled with shredded chicken or cheese, smothered in a tangy green tomatillo sauce and melted cheese. Served with rice and beans.

Giant Burritos

A hearty flour tortilla packed with your favorite protein, rice, beans, cheese, and salsa. A meal in itself!

Quesadillas Supremas

Crispy flour tortilla filled with melted cheese and your choice of grilled chicken or steak. Served with guacamole and sour cream.

Chiles Rellenos

Roasted poblano peppers stuffed with cheese, lightly battered, and fried. Topped with a mild ranchero sauce.

Fresh Guacamole

Made fresh daily with ripe avocados, diced tomatoes, onions, cilantro, and a hint of lime. Served with crispy tortilla chips.

Huevos Rancheros

A classic breakfast favorite! Two fried eggs on corn tortillas, topped with ranchero sauce, served with rice and beans. (Available until 2 PM)
