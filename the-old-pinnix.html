Our Legacy

More Than Just a Place to Eat – It's Home.

For years, The Old Pinnix has been a cherished cornerstone of the New Bern community. We're not just a bar and grill; we're a gathering place where friendships are forged, laughter echoes, and good times are always on the menu.

Nestled on Hancock Street, we pride ourselves on offering a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels like family. From hearty meals cooked with passion to your favorite drinks served with a smile, every visit is designed to create lasting memories.

We love bringing people together for fun beyond the plate, too! Keep an eye out for special gatherings like our upcoming Cookie Decorating Class on Sunday, September 22nd, from 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM. It’s a sweet evening of creativity and fun, perfect for all ages. Join us and become part of the Old Pinnix story!
