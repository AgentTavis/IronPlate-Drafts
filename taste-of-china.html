Our Story

Crafting Culinary Delights, One Dish at a Time.

Experience the Tradition of Taste of China.

Taste of China has been a beloved culinary cornerstone in Pilot Mountain, serving up authentic Chinese-American flavors with a warm, welcoming bar and grill atmosphere. Since opening our doors, we've dedicated ourselves to creating memorable dining experiences for our community.

Whether you're joining us for a family dinner, a quick takeout lunch, or a cozy evening out, our commitment to fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes ensures every dish is a celebration of taste. We pride ourselves on offering freshly prepared Chinese-American favorites, from our savory Roasted Pork with Vegetables to our famed General Tso's chicken, all made with passion and care.

Come discover why 156 happy customers have rated us 4.6 stars. We look forward to welcoming you to our table at 630 S Key St!

Our Menu

Explore Our Freshly Prepared Favorites.

General Tso's Chicken

Our most popular dish! Crispy chicken morsels tossed in a sweet and spicy sauce with a hint of tang. A true Chinese-American classic.

Roasted Pork with Vegetables

Succulent roasted pork, thinly sliced and stir-fried with a medley of fresh, crisp seasonal vegetables in a savory brown sauce.

Sweet and Sour Chicken

Golden-fried chicken pieces generously coated in our vibrant, tangy, and sweet sauce with bell peppers, pineapple, and onions.

Beef with Broccoli

Tender slices of beef wok-tossed with fresh broccoli florets in a rich, flavorful garlic brown sauce. A healthy and hearty choice.

Classic Egg Rolls

Hand-rolled to perfection, crispy on the outside with a savory filling of pork and mixed vegetables. The perfect appetizer.

Shrimp Lo Mein

Soft noodles stir-fried with tender shrimp and crisp vegetables in a light, savory sauce. A comforting and satisfying meal.

House Fried Rice

A generous portion of fluffy rice wok-fried with chicken, pork, shrimp, egg, and fresh vegetables. The ultimate side or standalone dish.

Moo Shu Pork

Shredded pork stir-fried with cabbage, mushrooms, and scrambled eggs, served with warm thin pancakes and hoisin sauce.
