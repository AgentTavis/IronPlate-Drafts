OUR STORY

Where Every Dish Tells a Family Tale

Welcome to Wagner Street Bistro, a place where the heart of Italy meets the warmth of Troutman, NC. We are a truly family-owned and operated small Italian-style restaurant, pouring our passion into every plate we serve.

Our journey began with a simple dream: to bring authentic, homemade flavors to our community. From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a customer; you're an extension of our family. We believe in the power of good food to bring people together, create memories, and celebrate life's simple pleasures.

We pride ourselves on using fresh ingredients to craft a delightful array of homemade soups, vibrant salads, satisfying paninis, classic sandwiches, juicy burgers, and hearty dinners. Each recipe carries the touch of generations, perfected with love and served with a genuine smile. We truly can't wait to serve you!
