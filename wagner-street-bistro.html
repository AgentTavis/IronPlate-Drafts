Our Journey

Our Family's Passion, Your Italian Tradition.

At Wagner Street Bistro, we believe in the simple joy of sharing a meal. As a cherished family-owned and operated Italian restaurant in Troutman, NC, we pour our heart into every dish we serve.

From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a customer; you're family. We pride ourselves on crafting authentic, homemade Italian-style comfort food, using recipes passed down through generations. It's a taste of Italy, made with local love.

Whether it's our rich, homemade soups, crisp salads, savory paninis, classic sandwiches, hearty burgers, or comforting dinners, each item is prepared with the freshest ingredients and a personal touch. We truly can't wait to serve you!
