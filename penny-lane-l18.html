Where British Charm Meets Cornelius Hospitality

Welcome to Penny Lane L18, Cornelius's authentic British-style pub where good times, great company, and unforgettable moments are always on tap. Nestled right here in Cornelius, NC, we're more than just a bar and grill – we're a community hub, a haven for live music lovers, and the official home for every Liverpool FC supporter. From perfectly poured cocktails to lively events, we invite you to experience the warmth and energy that makes us a local favorite. Come for the food, stay for the craic!

Our passion lies in creating a space where everyone feels at home, whether you're catching the big game, enjoying a pint with friends, or dancing to live tunes. We believe in good vibes, genuine service, and making every visit a memorable one. Join us and become part of the Penny Lane L18 family.
