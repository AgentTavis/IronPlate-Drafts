Our Story & Commitment

A Taste of Belhaven's Finest Seafood

We're Fish Hooks Cafe, a true Belhaven gem where fresh seafood and warm hospitality come together. Our story is simple: we believe in serving delicious, plentiful meals that make you feel right at home. Even when the weather turns, our doors are open, ready to welcome you with a smile and a table full of our finest.

It was on such a day, during a winter storm, that one diner discovered us – and left couldn't have been happier. They experienced our fresh, plentiful buffet, kept constantly restocked. Our shrimp and fried chicken were highlights, perfectly seasoned and never overly breaded. That's the Fish Hooks promise: quality, comfort, and a meal to remember.
