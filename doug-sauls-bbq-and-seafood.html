Our Heritage

Generations of Flavor, Crafted with Passion

For over 30 years, Doug Sauls' BBQ And Seafood has been a cornerstone of the Nashville, NC community, bringing genuine Eastern NC BBQ and beloved seafood dishes to our loyal customers. What started as a small, family-owned establishment has grown into a local favorite, built on decades of tradition and a commitment to delicious, authentic flavors.

We take immense pride in every plate we serve, using time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Our unique hush puppies, shaped like onion rings, are a testament to our family's distinct touch, and our Brunswick Stew is a delicious, comforting classic that keeps guests coming back. Come experience the warmth of our family restaurant and taste the tradition that makes us special.
