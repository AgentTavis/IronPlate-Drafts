Our Journey

From Seattle's Shores to Lewisville's Heart

By the proprietors of Miako Teriyaki, 206 Luster Grill was founded by Julia Kwon to bring customers a new culinary experience in DFW. Julia started her teriyaki business in 2002 in Seattle, WA. After multiple, successful locations, she decided to bring her culinary skills to DFW in 2016 and founded Miako Teriyaki.

Now with 206 Luster Grill, Julia can continue to share her passion in cooking with all those around her. There is nothing she enjoys more than bringing happiness through her food. Every dish is a testament to years of dedication and a love for authentic flavors, crafted just for you.
