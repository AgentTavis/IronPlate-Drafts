OUR STORY

An Extraordinary Evening Awaits

NanaSteak offers high-energy upscale dining at its finest. From the moment you step through our doors, you're invited to enjoy an atmosphere vibrant with possibility and exceptional flavors.

Gather with friends at our beautiful and well-appointed bar for drinks and appetizers, or settle in for an exquisite meal from a menu full of local North Carolina fare. Our commitment to sourcing fresh, regional ingredients ensures a taste that is both authentic and elevated.

The Wine Spectator Award-winning wine list offers many unique and interesting selections, expertly curated to compliment any meal. With an impeccable attention to detail, NanaSteak provides the perfect backdrop for an extraordinary evening, whether it's a special celebration or a memorable night out.
