Our Journey

More Than Just a Diner: A Community Hub

Nestled in the heart of Moyock, Blue 42's Brewing Barn was born from a simple idea: to create a welcoming space where community thrives. What started as a vision for a cozy spot to enjoy great coffee evolved into a vibrant destination for craft beer, fine wine, and unforgettable live entertainment.

We believe in good company, great drinks, and the magic of shared moments. Our big stage, once quiet, now buzzes with local talent, turning ordinary evenings into extraordinary memories. From the first sip of our rich espresso to the last note of a live band, every visit to Blue 42's is an experience crafted with passion.
