Our Heritage

More Than Just Breakfast, It's a Tradition.

Breakfastime- Cabarrus Ave is a beloved local spot in Concord, NC, known for its hearty breakfasts, friendly service, and comforting diner atmosphere. For years, we've been serving up classic morning favorites, becoming a cherished gathering place where neighbors connect and families create lasting memories over a plate of hotcakes and a freshly brewed cup of coffee.

We believe every morning deserves a great start, and we're here to deliver just that. Our commitment to quality ingredients, generous portions, and a warm, welcoming environment ensures that every visit feels like coming home. Join us and discover why Breakfastime is a true Concord institution.
