Our Story

Easy to Miss, Hard to Forget.

Hayesville Brewing is a small micro brew located just off the side of US-64. Nothing about the exterior stands out as you're driving down the highway; however, if you're looking for quality craft beer at a cheap price, this is the place for you!

We pride ourselves on creating unique, high-quality craft beers and a welcoming atmosphere. Come discover your new favorite brew, like our smooth Possum Drop Porter with subtle hints of chocolate and coffee, perfectly garnished with a chocolate pretzel.

"Absolutely love the vibe here! It's exactly what Hayesville needed – great beer, friendly faces, and a cozy spot to unwind. Their Possum Drop Porter is a masterpiece!"

Our Brews & Bites

Crafted Flavors, Unbeatable Value.

Possum Drop Porter

Our signature dark ale, smooth with subtle hints of chocolate and coffee, garnished with a chocolate pretzel.

Mountain Mist IPA

A hazy, hop-forward IPA bursting with citrus and pine notes, a true mountain experience.

Appalachian Amber Ale

Malty and balanced, this amber ale offers a delightful caramel sweetness and a clean finish.

Hayesville Hazy Pale Ale

Light, refreshing, and incredibly smooth with tropical fruit aromas, perfect for any occasion.

Smoky Mountain Stout

Rich and roasted, a full-bodied stout with notes of dark chocolate and espresso.

Brewmaster's Seasonal Lager

Ask about our crisp and clean rotating lager, crafted to celebrate the season's best ingredients.

Brewpub Nachos

Crispy tortilla chips loaded with cheese, jalapeños, black beans, salsa, sour cream. (Many vegetarian options)

Loaded Tater Tots

Golden tots topped with melted cheddar, crispy bacon bits, and a drizzle of ranch. (Vegetarian option without bacon)

BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders

Tender slow-cooked pulled pork smothered in tangy BBQ sauce on soft slider buns.

Why Hayesville Brew?

More Than Just a Brewery, It's an Experience.

Hidden Gem, Local Vibe

Tucked away just off US-64, we're the cozy spot locals rave about. Experience genuine Hayesville hospitality in our unassuming brew haven. Easy to miss, impossible to forget!

Exceptional Craft Beers

From our signature Possum Drop Porter with its chocolate and coffee notes to our rotating seasonal brews, we promise quality craft beer at prices that make you smile.

Delicious Bites & Options

Pair your favorite brew with our satisfying bar & grill fare. With many vegetarian options and tasty take-out, there's something for everyone to enjoy.
