Our Heritage

From Our Family to Your Plate: Authentic Mexican Flavors

At La Raza, we believe in bringing the true taste of Mexico to the heart of Rockingham, NC. Our journey began with a simple passion: to share authentic, "super legit Mexican style tacos" and other beloved classics with our community. We pride ourselves on using traditional recipes and the freshest ingredients, ensuring every dish that leaves our kitchen is "hot and fresh."

When you dine with us, you're not just enjoying a meal; you're experiencing a piece of our culture. From our freshly made corn tortillas to our "actually pretty spicy" hot salsa, we're dedicated to delivering an unforgettable culinary adventure. Whether you're passing through or a local looking for your next favorite meal, we invite you to discover why La Raza has earned its reputation as a true local gem.
