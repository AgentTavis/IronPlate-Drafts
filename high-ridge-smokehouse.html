Our Legacy, Our Flavor

More Than BBQ, It's a Mars Hill Tradition.

For years, High Ridge Smokehouse has been a beloved local spot in Mars Hill, NC, serving up authentic, slow-smoked BBQ that brings families and friends together. We believe in the power of good food to create lasting memories, and every dish we serve is a testament to our passion for traditional Southern flavors.

Our journey began with a simple commitment: to provide our community with the highest quality smoked meats, crafted with care and a touch of homegrown hospitality. We take pride in our roots, our recipes, and the genuine connections we've built over countless meals.

Our Promise to You

The High Ridge Difference

Authentic Slow-Smoke

We honor the tradition of true BBQ, slow-smoking our meats for up to 14 hours over select hardwoods. This dedication brings out unmatched tenderness and a rich, smoky flavor you can only get with time and care.

Local & Family-Owned

As a beloved local spot in Mars Hill, we're proud to be family-owned and operated. We're part of this community, and we strive to provide a welcoming atmosphere and a meal that feels like home.

Unforgettable Flavor

From our fall-off-the-bone ribs to our melt-in-your-mouth brisket, every dish is crafted to deliver an unforgettable taste experience. We blend classic recipes with our unique touch, ensuring every bite is pure perfection.
