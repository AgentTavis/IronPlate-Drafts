Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's Family.

At Woods Family Diner, we pour our heart into every dish. For years, we've been a beloved cornerstone of the Etowah community, serving up classic American diner fare with a homemade touch.

From sizzling fresh burgers and slow-smoked BBQ to our famous hotdogs and ever-changing daily specials, there's always something delicious cooking. But it's our homemade desserts, especially our tempting array of brownies and other sweet treats, that truly set us apart. Come on in, pull up a chair, and experience the warm, friendly atmosphere that only a family-owned diner can offer.
