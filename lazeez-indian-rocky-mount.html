Our Heritage

A Taste of Tradition, Crafted with Love.

Lazeez Indian Rocky Mount is more than just a restaurant; it's a beloved local spot where the rich culinary traditions of India come alive. Nestled in the heart of Rocky Mount, NC, we pride ourselves on offering an authentic Indian bar and grill experience that delights the senses.

From aromatic curries to sizzling tandoori specialties, every dish is prepared with the freshest ingredients and a passion for flavor. We believe in creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where friends and family can gather to share memorable meals and create lasting memories.

Our Offerings

Explore Our Irresistible Flavors.

Flavorful Curries

Experience the heart of Indian cuisine with our rich and aromatic curries, crafted with traditional spices and fresh ingredients. Perfect with rice or naan.

Sizzling Tandoor Grills

From tender chicken to succulent lamb, our tandoor dishes are marinated to perfection and cooked in a traditional clay oven for an unforgettable smoky flavor.

Aromatic Biryanis

Indulge in our fragrant basmati rice dishes, layered with tender meats or fresh vegetables, slow-cooked to infuse every grain with exquisite taste.

Classic Appetizers

Start your meal with a selection of crispy samosas, savory pakoras, or other delightful bites, perfect for sharing.

Fresh Baked Breads

Complement your meal with our freshly baked naan, roti, or paratha, straight from the tandoor.

Refreshing Beverages

Quench your thirst with a selection of traditional Indian drinks, lassis, or your favorite soft drink from our bar.
