Our Journey

The Heart of Selma's Community

Since welcoming new management in the 2020 holiday season, The Alley Bar has been Selma's go-to spot for incredible value and a friendly atmosphere. We're a comfy, small-town bar, deeply rooted in our community, and proudly veteran-friendly.

While we keep our indoor space smoke-free for everyone's comfort, we offer a charming outdoor patio – perfect for a relaxing drink or conversation with friends. Keep an eye out for our occasional free food offerings, making your visit even more delightful!

Come discover why we're a beloved local establishment, serving up amazing deals like $4 Guinness, $3 Domestics, and $5 Wine. We can't wait to welcome you.
