Our Heritage

A Taste of Southern Tradition in Laurinburg

General McArthur's Restaurant in Laurinburg, NC offers down-home Southern cuisine with a focus on homestyle cooking that brings families together. From our legendary fried chicken to a mouth-watering array of classic Southern sides, every dish is prepared with tradition and a sprinkle of Carolina love.

We're more than just a meal; we're a taste of Laurinburg's heritage, served fresh for take-out and available for catering your special events. Our commitment to authentic flavors and warm hospitality has made us a beloved staple in the community.
