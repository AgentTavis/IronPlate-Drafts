Our Roots in Hayesville

More Than Just a Bar – It's Home.

Welcome to Fat Dog On Main, Hayesville's beloved bar and grill where good food, cold drinks, and great company come together. Nestled right on Main Street, we've been serving up classic American favorites and a vibrant local atmosphere to our community for years. We believe in simple pleasures: a perfectly cooked burger, a refreshing craft beer, and a place where everyone feels like family.

Our passion lies in creating a space where locals can unwind after a long day, friends can gather for a lively evening, and visitors can experience the warm hospitality of Hayesville. With our friendly staff and inviting ambiance, we’re more than just a place to eat and drink—we’re a cornerstone of the community. We're proud to be dog-friendly on our outdoor patio, so your furry friends can join the fun too!
