Our Journey

Bringing Eden's Community Together, One Sip at a Time.

At Sunday Morning Coffee, we believe every day can feel like a relaxed Sunday morning. We're more than just a Bar And Grill; we're a vibrant gathering spot in the heart of Eden, NC, where friends and family connect over quality coffee, delightful drinks, and a warm atmosphere.

Our philosophy is simple: create a space where you can unwind, savor good company, and enjoy handcrafted beverages and bites. Though our name evokes Sunday mornings, we extend that relaxed, welcoming vibe throughout the week, offering a perfect escape from the everyday hustle.
