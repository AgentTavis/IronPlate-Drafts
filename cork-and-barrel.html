Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

Cork and Barrel is more than just a place to eat; it's a beautiful little spot designed to be a true escape in the heart of Lenoir, NC. Our dedicated waitresses and talented bartender are all incredibly kind, ensuring every visit is filled with genuine warmth and attentive service.

From fantastic food to expertly crafted drinks, we pour our heart into every detail. The restaurant itself is adorable and charming, creating a welcoming atmosphere where you can relax, connect, and make lasting memories. We're proud to bring the community of Lenoir a memorable dining and entertainment experience, where good food meets great company and even better music.
