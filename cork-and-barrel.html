Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience

Cork and Barrel is a beautiful little spot nestled in the heart of Lenoir, NC. From the moment you step inside, you'll be greeted by an inviting atmosphere that blends rustic charm with modern comfort. Our dedicated waitstaff and skilled bartenders are the heart of our hospitality, always striving to make your visit memorable.

We pride ourselves on serving fantastic food and drink, crafted with care and passion. Whether you're here for a quick lunch, a lively evening with friends, or a special celebration, our adorable restaurant is the perfect backdrop. We believe in creating a space where great music, delicious flavors, and warm company come together, making Cork and Barrel a true local gem.
