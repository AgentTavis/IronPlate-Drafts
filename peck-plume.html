OUR STORY

A Local Gem, Crafted with Care

Nestled in the heart of downtown Cary, Peck & Plume isn't just a place to eat; it's a place to gather, to connect, and to savor moments. We’ve cultivated an atmosphere where comfort meets modern American dining, creating a truly unique experience.

Find comfort everywhere from our chill study, vibrant bar, chatty dining room, breezy terrace, relaxing lawn, or private event space. Each area offers a distinct ambiance, ensuring every visit, whether for a quick bite or a special celebration, feels just right. We invite you to make our table your own.
