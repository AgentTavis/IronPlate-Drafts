Our Heritage

A Taste of Home in Walnut Cove

At Hillside Market, we're more than just a convenience store and grill – we're a cornerstone of the Walnut Cove community. For years, we've been proudly serving our neighbors with a warm smile, hearty meals, and all the essentials you need, right when you need them.

We believe in good food, great company, and the kind of friendly service that makes you feel like family. From our grill to our shelves, every item is selected with our community in mind, ensuring quality and convenience. Come on down and experience the Hillside difference!
