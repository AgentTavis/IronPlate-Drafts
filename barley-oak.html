Our Heritage

Mandeville's Home for Global Brews & Great Food

The Barley Oak, nestled on the picturesque shores of Lake Pontchartrain in Mandeville, isn't just a bar and grill—it's an experience. We pride ourselves on being a unique old-world draught haus, a true haven for beer enthusiasts. Our journey began with a simple vision: to bring the world's finest beers to our local community.

Boasting an impressive menu of 150 beers from all over the world, our selection is unmatched. From crisp lagers to robust stouts, rare imports to local craft favorites, there's always something new to discover. But great beer deserves great food. That's why we pair our extensive draught list with hearty, delicious bar and grill fare, prepared with quality ingredients and a touch of Mandeville charm.

It's a place where friends gather, stories are shared, and every visit feels like coming home. We invite you to step into our cozy atmosphere and become a part of the Barley Oak tradition.
