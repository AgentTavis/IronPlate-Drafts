Our Story

A Unique Blend: Coffee, Comfort, and Curated Finds.

At Boleka's, we believe in crafting experiences as unique as our name. We've built a space where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee mingles with the thrill of discovery, where hearty meals meet hidden treasures. It's more than just a bar and grill; it's a community hub.

We take pride in offering a welcoming atmosphere for families and friends to gather, share a delicious meal, and perhaps stumble upon that perfect, one-of-a-kind antique or a cherished piece of home decor. We're proud to be Dunn's destination for both culinary delights and an ever-changing inventory of interesting finds.
