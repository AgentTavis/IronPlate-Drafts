Our Story

Crafting Community, One Brew at a Time.

At Baileson Brewing Co., we believe in good beer, good company, and good times. Nestled in the heart of Houston, NC, we've built a space where friends gather, stories are shared, and every visit feels like coming home.

We're not just a bar and grill; we're a local hub, a place to unwind and connect. We pride ourselves on a welcoming atmosphere and a unique approach to dining. Our philosophy is simple: exceptional craft beer paired with ultimate flexibility. Don’t see anything you like from our limited fare? Feel free to bring your own food or have it delivered right to your table. Your perfect evening, your way.

"Baileson Brewing Co. is our family's favorite spot! The beer selection is fantastic, and we love that we can bring our own dinner. It's truly a unique and welcoming place."

— The Miller Family, Loyal Patrons
