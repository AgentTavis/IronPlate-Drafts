Our Heritage

A Southern Legacy, Baked with Love.

At Tonya's Cafe & Bake Shop, every dish tells a story – a story of family, tradition, and Southern hospitality. Tonya Council, a trailblazing food entrepreneur and third-generation Southern baker, carries forward a legacy of culinary excellence.

She's the proud granddaughter of the late Mildred Council, affectionately known as Mama Dip, a true pillar of Southern cooking whose recipes and spirit continue to inspire every plate and pastry we serve. Our kitchen is where heritage meets fresh, local ingredients, crafting meals that warm the soul and bring people together.

"Tonya's Cafe is a true gem! You can taste the love and tradition in every bite, from their incredible biscuits to their comforting daily specials. It's like stepping into Mama Dip's kitchen."

— A Loyal Customer, Chapel Hill
