Our Journey

Where Every Sip Tells a Story, Locally Sourced.

Nestled in the heart of Statesville, The Coffee Lodge began with a simple vision: to create a destination, not just another stop. We believe in the power of local – from our beans to the unique treasures in our country store, everything is chosen with our community in mind.

We're a diner at heart, serving up comforting flavors, but our soul is in the aroma of freshly brewed coffee and the warm smiles of our team. It's a place where friends gather, families connect, and every visitor feels like they've found a home away from home. Come discover what makes us truly special.
