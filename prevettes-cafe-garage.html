Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Prevettes Tradition.

At Prevettes Cafe & Garage, the aroma of sizzling bacon and freshly brewed coffee truly fills the air, welcoming guests to indulge in hearty breakfast favorites or classic diner burgers. For years, we've been the heart of Roaring River, a place where folks gather, share stories, and enjoy good, honest food made with love.

We believe in simple pleasures: a perfectly cooked breakfast, a juicy burger, and friendly service that makes you feel right at home. Come on down and taste the difference that genuine care makes!
