OUR STORY

Where Tradition Meets Taste

The aroma of sizzling bacon and freshly brewed coffee fills the air, welcoming guests to indulge in hearty breakfast favorites or classic diner burgers. At Prevettes Cafe & Garage, we're more than just a diner; we're a beloved local institution, serving up comforting meals and a warm, friendly atmosphere to the Roaring River community. Every dish is prepared with care, ensuring a taste of home in every bite, just as it has been for years.

We pride ourselves on being a place where neighbors become friends, and every meal feels like a gathering. Come experience the genuine hospitality and delicious, no-frills food that makes Prevettes a true Roaring River gem.
