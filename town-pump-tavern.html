Our Story

More Than A Bar, It's A Black Mountain Legacy.

Since 1984, the Town Pump Tavern has been the unwavering heart of Black Mountain, NC. We're more than just a bar and grill; we're where the community gathers, a genuine local institution. We believe in providing a comfortable, no-frills space where hard-working folks can truly unwind, share a laugh, and enjoy simple pleasures.

Whether you're looking to escape the brisk mountain air with a refreshingly cheap draft beer or bringing the family for our much-loved 'helluva kids menu', you'll always find a warm welcome and genuine conversation here. We may keep things classic and uncomplicated, but our commitment to ensuring you have a great time and enjoy good company is always our modern priority. Come, sit, relax, and be part of our story.
