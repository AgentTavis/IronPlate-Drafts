Our Roots

Crafting Community, One Sip at a Time

Nestled in the heart of Hudson's vibrant HUB Station, 3 Drinks Down Beverage Co. is more than just a tap room – it's a community gathering spot. We're passionate about bringing you a meticulously curated selection of craft beers, fine wines, crisp ciders, and refreshing seltzers.

Our mission is to create a welcoming atmosphere where friends, family, and neighbors can unwind, connect, and discover their new favorite pour. Come on down and experience the local flavor of Hudson with every sip.
