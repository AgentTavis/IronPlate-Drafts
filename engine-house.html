Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

At Engine House, we've crafted a unique diner experience that combines classic comfort food with the thrilling ambiance of a fire station. Our doors are always open, welcoming friends, families, and local heroes alike to enjoy a memorable time.

Wednesdays are Firefighter Days, when The Engine House truly comes alive. It feels even more like a proper fire station as local personnel stop by to socialize, share stories, and yes, occasionally hose down patrons who are speaking too heatedly – all in good fun, of course! It's a tradition that's become a cornerstone of our community.

Together, firefighters and their fellow diners can also play tunes on the digital jukebox, ensuring the perfect soundtrack for every visit, or catch a game on the restaurant's large projection screen and copious plasma and flat-screen TVs. Engine House is where good food, good company, and great entertainment converge.

"Engine House isn't just a diner; it's a vibrant hub where every visit feels like a celebration. The atmosphere is electric, especially on Firefighter Days!"

— A Happy Regular, Mt Clemens
