Our Heritage

Where Every Meal Feels Like Home

Welcome to Zeno's, a cornerstone of Italian dining in Troy, NC. What started as a dream to bring authentic Italian flavors and heartwarming hospitality to our community has grown into a local favorite. We believe in the power of good food to bring people together, creating memories around the table.

Our dedicated staff embodies the spirit of genuine Italian welcome, ensuring every visit is more than just a meal—it's an experience. From the moment you step into our fairly large dining room, you're treated like family. We pride ourselves on creating a friendly and outgoing atmosphere where delicious food and great company go hand-in-hand.

While we are located in a convenient strip mall, our passion for creating an inviting Italian ambiance shines through, making Zeno's the perfect spot for family dinners, romantic evenings, or a quick, satisfying lunch.

Our Selections

Taste the Tradition

Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

A Roman classic with eggs, hard cheese, cured pork, and black pepper. Simple, rich, and unforgettable.

Hearty Lasagna al Forno

Layers of pasta, rich Bolognese sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted mozzarella, baked to perfection.

Zeno's Chicken Parmigiana

Tender chicken breast, breaded and fried, topped with marinara and melted provolone, served with spaghetti.

Creamy Fettuccine Alfredo

Fresh fettuccine pasta tossed in a luxurious sauce of butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese.

Margherita Pizza

Our traditional Neapolitan pizza with San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, basil, and olive oil.

Homemade Tiramisu

An exquisite Italian dessert with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cheese, and cocoa.

Garlic Knots (NEW & IMPROVED!)

Soft, warm knots of dough, baked golden and brushed with a fragrant garlic-herb butter. Perfect for sharing!

Four Cheese Ravioli

Delicate pasta pockets filled with a blend of ricotta, Parmesan, mozzarella, and asiago cheeses, served with your choice of sauce.
