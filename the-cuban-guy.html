Our Story

Taste the Tradition. Experience the Flavor.

At The Cuban Guy, we're more than just a diner; we're a culinary journey through the heart of Cuba and Puerto Rico, right here in Waynesville, NC.

Our passion for authentic island flavors runs deep, rooted in recipes perfected over generations. Every dish tells a story, a vibrant narrative of family traditions, fresh ingredients, and the warmth of Caribbean hospitality.

From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a customer – you're family. We believe in serving food that not only satisfies your hunger but also touches your soul, bringing a little piece of our heritage to your plate. Come and discover why The Cuban Guy is a true local treasure.
