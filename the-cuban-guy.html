Our Story

Bringing Generations of Cuban & Puertorrican Flavors to Waynesville

The Cuban Guy isn't just a diner; it's a culinary journey rooted deep in family tradition. We proudly serve the Waynesville, NC area with outstanding Cuban and Puertorrican food, crafted from recipes perfected over generations.

Every dish tells a story, a taste of home and heritage. From the robust flavors of our Cuban Sandwich to the delicate pastries, we pour our heart and soul into creating an authentic dining experience that transports you straight to the Caribbean.

Our Menu

Our Signature Dishes

Classic Cuban Sandwich

Slow-roasted pork, ham, Swiss cheese, pickles, and mustard on toasted Cuban bread. A true classic, just like abuela used to make.

Medianoche Sandwich

Similar to the Cuban, but served on sweet egg dough bread, making it perfect for a late-night (or any-time) craving.

Breakfast Platter

Start your day the Cuban way! Eggs, choice of meat, toast, and our flavorful home fries. A hearty beginning.

Beef Empanada

Flaky pastry filled with seasoned ground beef, perfectly spiced and fried to a golden crisp. A crowd favorite.

Guava & Cheese Empanada

A delightful sweet and savory combination of rich guava paste and creamy cheese, encased in a golden pastry.

Cafe con Leche

A strong Cuban espresso shot perfectly balanced with steamed milk. Your essential daily pick-me-up.
