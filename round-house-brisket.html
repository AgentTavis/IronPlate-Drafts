Our Journey

More Than Just Brisket: A Place for Connection

At Round House Brisket, we believe great food brings people together. Located right here in Morganton, NC, we've built more than just a restaurant; we've created a cozy and fun spot where friends, family, and even new acquaintances can come together over plates piled high with delicious BBQ.

We're known for our fantastic brisket – tender, smoky, and packed with flavor – and we always make sure you get plenty to eat. But it's not just the food that keeps our customers coming back; it's the warm smiles, the friendly faces, and the impeccably clean building that make every visit a comfortable and memorable experience. Come see why so many say they'll be back here many times!

Our Culinary Delights

Taste the Tradition of Southern BBQ

Signature Brisket Plate

Our slow-smoked, perfectly tender brisket, served with your choice of two classic sides. A true taste of Texas in Morganton.

Smoked Pulled Pork Sandwich

Juicy, flavorful pulled pork, smoked for hours and piled high on a toasted bun with a side of coleslaw.

St. Louis Style Ribs (Half Rack)

Fall-off-the-bone ribs, dry-rubbed and smoked to perfection. A BBQ classic you won't forget.

Smoked Chicken Plate

Half a chicken, smoked until golden and juicy, seasoned with our special blend of spices. Served with two sides.

Brisket Chili Mac

A hearty blend of creamy mac & cheese, topped with our famous smoked brisket chili and a sprinkle of cheddar.

Classic BBQ Sides

Choose from our homemade Mac & Cheese, Creamy Coleslaw, Smoky Baked Beans, or Fresh Potato Salad.
