Our Journey

More Than Just Brisket, It's Family.

At Round House Brisket, we believe great food brings people together. Nestled right here in Morganton, NC, our restaurant is built on a simple promise: serve delicious, authentic BBQ with a side of warm Southern hospitality. We're known for our great brisket, always slow-smoked to perfection, and we make sure you get plenty to eat every time you visit.

Our commitment to a nice, clean building and friendly service means every visit is a comfortable and enjoyable experience. Whether you're dining with friends, gathering with family, or simply looking to meet someone new, you'll find a cozy and fun atmosphere that feels just like home. We are incredibly grateful for the support of our community and look forward to welcoming you back many times.
