Our Roots

Serving Up Comfort and Community, One Plate at a Time

Nestled in the heart of Elizabethtown, Corner Cafe has been a cherished local institution for decades, serving up classic diner fare with a side of warm hospitality. We believe in good food, good company, and great value.

From our sizzling breakfast plates that kickstart your day to our comforting nightly specials, every dish at Corner Cafe is crafted to bring a smile to your face. We pride ourselves on creating a friendly, family-like atmosphere where everyone feels welcome. Come on in, grab a booth, and become part of our story!
