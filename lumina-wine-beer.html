Our Story

More Than Just a Drink, It's a Community Hub.

Established in January 2009, Lumina Wine & Beer has been a beloved fixture in downtown Asheboro. What started as a charming shop offering a unique selection of wines and beers has grown into a vibrant gathering place for friends, families, and neighbors.

We pride ourselves on creating an inviting atmosphere where everyone feels welcome. Whether you're a connoisseur seeking a rare vintage or simply looking for a relaxed spot to unwind after a long day, Lumina offers the perfect blend of local charm and quality beverages.

"Lumina truly feels like the heart of Asheboro. Always a warm welcome and the perfect place to catch up with friends. A real community gem!"
