Our Journey

Where Every Meal Feels Like Coming Home.

Welcome to Yadkin Valley Wine Company, proudly hosting Darwin's Cafe in the heart of Hamptonville. We're more than just a place to eat; we're a community gathering spot where the aroma of home-cooked goodness fills the air. Our journey began with a simple passion: to serve delicious, comforting meals that bring smiles to faces.

We believe in honest food, made with care. From our juicy burgers and crispy wings to fresh seafood and hearty home-cooked specials, every dish at Darwin's Cafe is prepared with the warmth and hospitality you'd expect from a true local gem. Come on in, pull up a chair, and experience the flavors that make us a Hamptonville favorite.
