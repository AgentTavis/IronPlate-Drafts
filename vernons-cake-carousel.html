Our Journey

A Taste of Wilkesboro's Heart

Vernon's Cake Carousel was more than just a restaurant; it was a cornerstone of the Wilkesboro community, known for bringing families and friends together over exceptional BBQ and unforgettable cakes.

We specialized in creating moments, from lively family reunions to joyous graduation parties. Our commitment to quality ingredients, especially our outstanding cakes and legendary BBQ rolls, defined every dish.

At Vernon's, we believed in supporting local and providing a warm, welcoming atmosphere where every guest felt like family. While our doors may now be closed, the memories of shared meals and impeccable service continue to resonate through Wilkesboro. We were proud to be a local business, highly recommended by those who cherished our unique flavor and heartfelt hospitality.
