Our Heritage

Taste the Tradition

Gourmet and Company is a beloved local spot in Johnson City, NC, proudly serving our community with passion and dedication. What started as a simple dream has blossomed into a vibrant gathering place where friends and family connect over delicious food and refreshing drinks.

We believe in creating more than just meals; we craft experiences. Every dish, every drink, and every smile from our team is a testament to our commitment to hospitality and quality. We cherish the role we play in Johnson City, a place where memories are made and good times are shared.
