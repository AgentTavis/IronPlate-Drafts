Our Heritage

Our Family's Passion, Served Fresh Daily.

Dine-N-Dash is more than just a restaurant; it’s a little gem in Wilkesboro, North Carolina, built on family values and a passion for great food. We're proud to be family-owned and operated, bringing the community together with delicious, fine-fast-food that feels like home.

From classic hotdogs to savory chicken salad croissants and even hearty bean burgers, we believe in offering something special for everyone. Our commitment to great service and great food is at the heart of everything we do, ensuring every visit is a memorable experience. Come on in and taste the difference that local care makes.
