About Us

Nestled in the heart of North Wilkesboro, NC,Brushy Mountain Smokehouse and Creamery is a full-service family restaurant known for its delectable hickory-smoked dishes. Their menu boasts a variety of mouthwatering options including pulled pork, sliced pork tenderloin, chicken, and St. Louis-style ribs.
