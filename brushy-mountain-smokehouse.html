OUR STORY

Rooted in Tradition, Smoked to Perfection

Nestled in the heart of North Wilkesboro, NC, Brushy Mountain Smokehouse and Creamery is more than just a restaurant—it's a family tradition. We're a full-service family restaurant renowned for our delectable hickory-smoked dishes, prepared with passion and a commitment to authentic flavor.

Our menu boasts a variety of mouthwatering options, from tender pulled pork to perfectly sliced pork tenderloin, savory chicken, and our signature St. Louis-style ribs. Each dish is expertly prepared, infused with that rich, smoky flavor that sets us apart and highlights our dedication to quality and taste.

And we can't forget the sweet finish! Complementing our savory BBQ is our delightful creamery, featuring 18 flavors of homemade ice cream, including a host of fan favorites. It’s the perfect way to cap off your meal or enjoy a treat any time of day.

THE MENU

Our Signature Smoked Delights

Pulled Pork

Slow-smoked to tender perfection, our succulent pulled pork is a timeless classic, infused with rich hickory flavor and ready to melt in your mouth.

Sliced Pork Tenderloin

Delicately smoked and thinly sliced, this lean and flavorful pork tenderloin offers a refined twist on traditional BBQ, packed with smoky goodness.

Smoked Chicken

Our juicy chicken, slow-smoked until golden brown, delivers a delightful smoky aroma and tender texture, a favorite for all ages.

St. Louis-Style Ribs

Fall-off-the-bone tender and coated in our signature rub, these St. Louis-style ribs are a BBQ connoisseur's dream – bursting with flavor in every bite.

Homemade Ice Cream

Choose from 18 delightful homemade ice cream flavors, from classic favorites to unique creations, the perfect sweet ending to any meal.

Classic Sides

Complete your meal with our selection of classic smokehouse sides. Ask your server for today's fresh, delicious offerings.
