Our Story: A Taste of Family in Every Bite

Nestled in the heart of North Wilkesboro, Brushy Mountain Smokehouse & Creamery isn't just a restaurant; it's a family tradition steeped in the rich flavors of true Southern hospitality.

For over two decades, we've perfected the art of hickory-smoked BBQ, slow-cooking our meats to tender, juicy perfection. From our legendary pulled pork to our fall-off-the-bone St. Louis-style ribs, every dish tells a story of passion and commitment to quality.

But the journey doesn't end there! We believe a great meal deserves a sweet finish, which is why we also handcraft 18 delightful flavors of homemade ice cream. It's the perfect ending to a hearty meal, making us the ideal spot for families to create delicious memories.

Come on in, pull up a chair, and let our family feed yours. We can't wait to welcome you.

"There's nothing quite like the smell of our smokers at dawn. It's more than just cooking; it's bringing families together, one delicious meal at a time. And watching the kids light up over our ice cream? That's the real reward."

— The Brushy Mountain Family
