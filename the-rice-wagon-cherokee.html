Our Journey

Authentic Flavors, Local Heart.

Nestled in the heart of Cherokee, The Rice Wagon is more than just a Bar & Grill – it's a culinary journey to the islands. We're a cherished local spot, known for bringing the vibrant, comforting tastes of Hawaiian-inspired cuisine right to your table.

Our commitment to delicious, well-marinated food is what makes us a favorite. Take our Shoyu Chicken, for instance: tender, flavorful, falling off the bone, with rice perfectly coated in its rich sauce. Or our Hawaiian Mac Salad – a delightful twist on a classic that even the pickiest eaters rave about.

Whether you're a long-time resident or just passing through Cherokee, we invite you to experience the warmth, flavor, and community that makes The Rice Wagon a truly special place.
