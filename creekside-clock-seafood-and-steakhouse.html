Our Heritage

Serving Shelby with Passion and Flavor for Decades

For generations, Creekside Clock has been a cherished culinary landmark in Shelby, NC. Nestled conveniently at 1025 Kiser Street, our passion lies in serving heartwarming American, Steakhouse, and Seafood dishes that feel like a delicious home-cooked meal.

We believe in simple, honest food prepared with care, bringing families and friends together around a table of fresh flavors and comforting classics. From our perfectly grilled steaks to our succulent seafood and beloved sandwiches, every dish is crafted to leave you feeling satisfied and wanting more.
