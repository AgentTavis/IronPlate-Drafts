Our Journey

Our Family's Table, Your Culinary Escape.

Nestled in the heart of Shelby, Creekside Clock Seafood and Steakhouse has been a cherished local institution, serving up delicious homestyle cooking for generations. We pride ourselves on creating an inviting atmosphere where families and friends can gather to enjoy hearty American, Steakhouse, Seafood, Grill, and Diner classics.

From the moment you step through our doors at 1025 Kiser Street, you're not just a customer; you're part of our extended family. We're known for our commitment to fresh ingredients, expertly prepared dishes, and a warm, friendly service that makes every meal a special occasion. Whether it's a quick lunch or a relaxed dinner, we promise a taste of home that keeps you coming back.

"Creekside Clock truly feels like home. Their dishes, especially the mains, are always cooked to perfection and their welcoming staff makes every visit a joy. It's our go-to spot in Shelby!"
