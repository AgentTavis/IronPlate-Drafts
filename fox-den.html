Our Roots

A Family Tradition of Flavor and Hospitality

Welcome to Fox Den, a true family-owned gem nestled in the heart of Del Rio, NC. Since day one, our mission has been simple: to create a welcoming place where friends and family can gather over delicious, freshly prepared meals.

We pride ourselves on using the freshest ingredients, carefully selected to ensure every dish bursts with authentic flavor. From hearty breakfast classics to comforting Southern favorites, each plate is crafted with the love and attention only a family business can provide.

But it's not just about the food. It's about the experience. Our staff, many of whom are like family, are dedicated to providing the friendliest service, making sure every visit to Fox Den feels like coming home. We're more than just a diner; we're a part of the Del Rio community, and we can't wait to share our table with you.
