Our Story

A Taste of Home, Crafted with Love in Valdese.

Nestled in the heart of Valdese, Buckle & Brew is more than just a diner – it's a culinary journey rooted in comfort and creativity. We've just finished a delightful remodel, making our space even more inviting for you to enjoy our unique take on classic diner fare.

Our menu is a celebration of flavors, where every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and a whole lot of heart. Guests often rave about our unexpected delights like the Fried Green Tomato Chicken Club sandwich – a true standout. But for those craving familiar comforts, our Buttermilk Chicken sandwich and irresistible Loaded Tots are always a hit.

We believe in good food, good company, and creating memorable experiences. Come on down and discover why Buckle & Brew is quickly becoming Valdese's favorite spot for a meal that feels like home, but tastes even better.
