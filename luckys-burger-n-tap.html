Our Legacy

More Than Just a Meal: It's a Lucky's Experience.

Welcome to Lucky's Burger N Tap, Asheboro's cherished gathering spot! From the moment we opened our doors, our mission has been simple: to serve up unforgettably delicious meals in an atmosphere that feels like home. We believe that great food brings people together, and every dish we craft is made with passion and the freshest ingredients.

We have worked tirelessly to package our meals in a way that lets you bring the quality of our meals into your home. We always love to see you in person, to share a laugh and a cold one, but even when we can't, we ensure that your dining experience, whether takeout or dine-in, is top-notch!
