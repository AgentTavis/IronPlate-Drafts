Our Roots

Where Good Food & Great Company Meet

At Pig's Ear American Bistro, we believe in crafting experiences as much as we craft delicious food. Nestled in the heart of Hurricane, NC, our bistro is more than just a place to eat – it's a community hub where friends and family gather for memorable moments.

Our journey began with a simple passion: to serve a wide variety of American comfort food, elevated with fresh ingredients and a touch of bistro elegance. From sizzling entrées to tantalizing starters, every dish is prepared with dedication. And what's good food without a perfect pairing? Our curated selection of fine wines and cold beers ensures there's always something to complement your meal and uplift your spirits.
