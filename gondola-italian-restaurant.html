Our Story

A Taste of Italy, Right Here in Mt Airy.

Nestled at 101 W Lebanon St, Gondola Italian Restaurant has been a beloved fixture in Mount Airy, NC, bringing authentic Italian flavors to our cherished community. We pride ourselves on offering a delightful selection of traditional pastas, mouth-watering pizzas, and other Italian specialties, crafted with passion and fresh ingredients.

For years, we've been the go-to spot not just for a relaxing family dinner, but also for those "snappy lunches" that keep Mount Airy's workers fueled and happy. Our commitment to quality food and a warm, inviting atmosphere has made us a local favorite, truly a slice of Italy in the heart of North Carolina.
