Our Story

Serving Authentic Mexican Flavors to Our Kenansville Family

At Pearsall Family Restaurant, we believe that great food brings people together. Nestled in the heart of Kenansville, we're proud to be a local, family-owned establishment dedicated to serving the community delicious, authentic Mexican cuisine.

Our passion lies in traditional recipes, fresh ingredients, and a warm, inviting atmosphere. Every dish is prepared with care, reflecting the rich culinary heritage of Mexico, and designed to make you feel right at home. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a place where memories are made around the table.

"The atmosphere is so welcoming, it feels like home. Every dish is made with love and you can taste the family tradition in every bite!"

— A Valued Regular
