Our Journey

The Heartbeat of Onaway Dining

At 211 Bar & Grill, we're more than just a restaurant – we're a cornerstone of the Onaway community. We pride ourselves on serving up hearty, delicious meals made with fresh, quality ingredients, all in a welcoming atmosphere that feels like home. Since opening our doors, our mission has been simple: to create a place where friends and family can gather, share great food, and make lasting memories.

From our legendary in-house roasted Prime Rib, slow-cooked to perfection, to our famous Award-Winning Fish Fry, every dish is crafted with passion and a commitment to flavor that keeps our guests coming back. We believe in classic comfort food with an elevated touch, ensuring there's always something special on the menu. Join us for a meal, a drink, and experience the warm hospitality that defines 211 Bar & Grill.
