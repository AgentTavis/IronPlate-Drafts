About

One building. Four floors. One city.

Downtown Greensboro's only four-story bar — and the only rooftop on Greene Street worth talking about.

The Story

A multi-level experience, not a single room.

Most bars pick one vibe. We built four. The building at 113 N Greene Street lets every floor have its own character — a live-music brewhouse on the ground, a dedicated whiskey bar in the middle, two open-air rooftop bars above.

The rooftop with its string lights and downtown skyline is the draw. The bourbon wall on the second floor is the quiet brag. The glass garage door on the first floor, open to the street on good nights, is the invitation.

4.2 ★ on Google across 246 reviews. 234 unique beers poured on Untappd. Regulars who bring friends.
