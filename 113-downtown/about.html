About

One building. Four floors. One city.

The building at 113 N Greene St is a downtown Greensboro landmark — dark facade, glass garage door, "ONE THIRTEEN" up top, string lights overhead. Inside, every floor is a different night out.

The Story

A multi-level experience, not a single room.

Most bars pick one vibe. We built four. Come downstairs for the stage, climb to the bourbon floor, end the night above the skyline under the string lights. Your crowd, your mood, your floor.

Floor by Floor

Take the tour.

Ground Floor
01

Main Bar + Live Stage

Exposed brick, red stools, custom wood bar top. A large stage hosts bands and DJs, and the glass garage door opens to Greene Street when the weather allows for that true indoor-outdoor feel.

Seating

Second Floor

Bourbon + Whiskey Bar

A dedicated floor for brown spirits, under a wall-sized "WHISKEY" sign. Full bottle collection on metal shelving, TVs for the game, a reservable private room overlooking the first-floor main stage.

What Sets Us Apart

Why people keep coming back.

NC Craft-Forward

A rotating lineup from Foothills, Brown Truck, NoDa, Four Saints, Wise Man, Red Oak, Creature Comforts. Untappd clocks us at 234 unique beers poured.

A Bourbon Floor

Not a bourbon menu — a bourbon floor. The second story is its own destination, with a deep shelf under the "WHISKEY" sign.

The Rooftop

The rooftop is the draw. Pergola, string lights, two bars, and a 360° wrap of downtown Greensboro.

Live Music Venue

Regular bands and DJs booked through Bandsintown. Weekly shows Friday and Saturday, plus pop-ups mid-week.

Private Events

Floor 02 has a reservable private room. Want the rooftop for a birthday? Ask about buyouts.

Downtown Location

Walk from any downtown hotel, restaurant, or show. Greene Street, right in the middle of everything.
