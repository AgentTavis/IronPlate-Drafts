Floor 02

A whole floor for brown spirits.

Climb one flight and the vibe shifts. Exposed brick, a wood bar top, a wall of bottles under the "WHISKEY" sign, and two TVs for the game. The second-floor bourbon and whiskey bar is its own destination — with a private room that overlooks the main stage below.

Bourbon. Rye. Scotch. Tequila. The shelves are deep — Fireball, 1800, George Dickel, and a rotating lineup of pours from across the spirit world. Ask your bartender for a recommendation or build a flight.
