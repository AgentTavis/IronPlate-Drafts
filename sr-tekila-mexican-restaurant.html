Our Journey

Bringing Authentic Mexican Flavors to Kannapolis

Welcome to Sr Tekila Mexican Restaurant, your neighborhood gem located at 515 S Main St, Kannapolis, NC. Our passion is to serve you the most authentic and vibrant Mexican cuisine, prepared with fresh ingredients and a whole lot of love.

From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be treated to a warm, family-friendly atmosphere that makes every meal a celebration. We believe great food brings people together, and we strive to create memorable dining experiences for all our guests. Come taste the tradition, feel the hospitality, and become a part of the Sr Tekila family.

Our Signature Flavors

Explore our wide array of classic Mexican dishes, crafted daily with the freshest ingredients. Full menu available in-restaurant!

Authentic Tacos

Soft corn or flour tortillas filled with your choice of savory meats, fresh salsa, onions, and cilantro.

Sizzling Fajitas

Grilled strips of steak or chicken, bell peppers, and onions, served with warm tortillas and all the fixings.

Hearty Burritos

Large flour tortillas stuffed with seasoned rice, beans, cheese, and your favorite protein.

Enchilada Platters

Corn tortillas rolled with fillings, smothered in our rich red or green sauces and melted cheese.

Fresh Guacamole

Our homemade guacamole, prepared fresh daily with ripe avocados, tomatoes, onions, and cilantro.
