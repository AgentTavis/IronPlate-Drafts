Our Journey

Experience the Heart of Mexico in Kannapolis

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Kannapolis, NC, Sr Tekila Mexican Restaurant has been a beloved culinary destination at 515 S Main St. We invite you to step into a world where authentic Mexican flavors meet a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Our passion is to bring the rich traditions of Mexican cuisine directly to your table, crafted with fresh ingredients and heartfelt recipes.

Whether you're joining us for a lively lunch, a family dinner, or celebrating a special occasion, our commitment to exceptional food and service shines through. We're proud to be a local favorite for delicious, unforgettable dining.
