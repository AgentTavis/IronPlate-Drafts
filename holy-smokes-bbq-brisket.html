Our Heritage

A Tradition of Smoky Perfection in Yanceyville

At Holy Smokes BBQ & Brisket, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a cornerstone of Yanceyville, NC, bringing the community together with the irresistible aroma and taste of authentic, slow-smoked barbecue. Every day, Tuesday through Saturday, from 11 A.M. to 8 P.M., we dedicate ourselves to the craft of traditional BBQ.

Our journey began with a simple passion: to serve the most mouthwatering, tender, and flavorful BBQ and brisket you've ever tasted. We believe that true barbecue is an art, patiently perfected over hours in the smoker. It's this dedication to time-honored techniques and quality ingredients that makes Holy Smokes a beloved local spot.
