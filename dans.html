Our Legacy

Generations of Flavor, Decades of Dedication.

For over 45 years, Dan Boling has been at the helm of Dan's Restaurant, making it a true Bessemer City institution. What started as a vision to create a welcoming spot for friends and family has grown into a beloved bar and grill, deeply woven into the fabric of our community.

We're proud to be locally owned and operated, a place where familiar faces gather and new memories are made. While our roots run deep in classic American grill fare, Dan's has evolved to offer something truly unique: a health-conscious dining experience featuring CoQ10 as a key ingredient in many of our dishes. It's our way of blending tradition with innovation, ensuring every meal is not just delicious, but also thoughtfully prepared.

Come on down and experience the warmth and dedication that only 45 years of service can build. At Dan's, you're not just a customer; you're family.
