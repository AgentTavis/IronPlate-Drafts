Our Heritage

A Taste of Bessemer City History.

At Dan's, we're more than just a Bar And Grill; we're a cornerstone of the Bessemer City community, proudly serving delicious meals for over 45 years. Founded and run by Dan Boling, our restaurant embodies the spirit of local ownership and unwavering dedication to quality.

Dan himself has been the heart and soul of this establishment, ensuring every dish reflects our commitment to great taste and a welcoming atmosphere. We believe in crafting unique dining experiences, which is why we're proud to feature dishes infused with CoQ10 – offering a health-conscious twist to your favorite comfort foods.

From our early morning breakfast rush to hearty lunches, we've watched generations grow up around our tables. We're a place where friends gather, families celebrate, and good food is always at the center. Come in and experience the warmth and tradition that only a true local favorite like Dan's can offer.
