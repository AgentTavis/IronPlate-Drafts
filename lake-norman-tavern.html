OUR STORY

More Than Just a Tavern, It's Home

Established in 2005, Lake Norman Tavern has grown to become the heart of our Mooresville neighborhood. We set out to create more than just a place to eat and drink; we envisioned a welcoming space where friends and family could gather, enjoy exceptional food, and make lasting memories. Our commitment to great food, great beverages, and great service has been unwavering since day one.

From the moment you walk in, you'll feel the warmth of a true local spot. Whether you're catching the big game on one of our 11 giant TVs with the NFL Ticket, challenging a friend to a game of pool, or enjoying some outdoor fun with horseshoes and corn hole, there's always something to do for everyone. We're proud to be family-friendly, ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable experience for all ages.

"Absolutely love the vibe here! The staff is fantastic, and the atmosphere makes you feel right at home. It’s definitely our go-to for a relaxed evening out."

- A Happy Regular

OUR SIGNATURE DISHES

Taste the Tavern Tradition

Crafted with care and bursting with flavor, these are the dishes that make Lake Norman Tavern a local favorite. From decadent comforts to classic grill fare, there's something to satisfy every craving.

Lobster Mac & Cheese

Our renowned, decadent dish that's rich, creamy, and loaded with tender lobster. A true tavern indulgence.

Gourmet Burgers

Juicy, handcrafted burgers, cooked to perfection with your choice of premium toppings. A classic done right.

Signature Wings

Crispy on the outside, tender on the inside, tossed in your favorite sauce. Perfect for sharing... or not!

Loaded Nachos

A mountain of crispy tortilla chips piled high with savory toppings, melted cheese, and fresh garnishes.

Steak Frites

A perfectly grilled steak served with crispy golden fries. Simple, satisfying, and always delicious.

Classic Fish & Chips
