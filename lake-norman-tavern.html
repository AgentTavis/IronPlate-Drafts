Our Legacy

More Than Just a Tavern, It's Home.

Welcome to Lake Norman Tavern, your quintessential neighborhood bar and grill in Mooresville, NC, proudly serving our community since 2005. We're renowned for our exceptional food, from decadent Lobster Mac & Cheese to mouth-watering burgers, wings, and nachos. With 11 giant TVs showing the NFL Ticket, it's the ultimate spot to catch the game.

Beyond the delicious eats and drinks, we're a family-friendly destination featuring pool tables, horseshoes, corn hole, and a variety of activities for everyone to enjoy. Come on down and discover why we're the local favorite – where great food, great beverages, and great service come together in one welcoming spot.
