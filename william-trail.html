Our Specialties

Taste the William Trail Difference

From our hearty classics to fresh, seasonal delights, our menu is crafted to satisfy every craving. No prices needed – just pure, delicious flavor.

William's Signature Burger

Our classic juicy patty, melted cheddar, crisp lettuce, ripe tomato, and secret sauce on a toasted brioche bun.

Smoked Ribeye Steak

Tender, perfectly smoked ribeye, seasoned to perfection and served with your choice of savory sides.

Crispy Chicken Sandwich

Golden fried chicken breast, zesty slaw, pickles, and a touch of spicy mayo on a fresh brioche bun.

Local Catch of the Day

Fresh, responsibly sourced seafood, expertly prepared with seasonal ingredients and a chef's special touch.

Classic Wings

Crispy, flavorful chicken wings tossed in your choice of Buffalo, BBQ, or Lemon Pepper. Perfect to share!

Farmhouse Salad

Fresh mixed greens, seasonal vegetables, local cheese, and our house-made vinaigrette.

Loaded Mac & Cheese

Creamy, rich macaroni and cheese baked to perfection, topped with savory bacon and green onions.

Decadent Chocolate Lava Cake

A warm, rich chocolate cake with a molten center, served with a scoop of vanilla bean ice cream.
