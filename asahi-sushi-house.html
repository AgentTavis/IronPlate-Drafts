Our Journey

Experience the True Taste of Sushi in Brevard.

Welcome to Asahi Sushi House, Brevard's premier destination for authentic Japanese flavors and a truly unforgettable dining experience. We've meticulously remodeled our space at 539 N Broad St, transforming a former fast food site into a sleek, inviting haven where quality sushi takes center stage.

Our commitment is simple: exceptional sushi, pleasant, attentive service, and an all-you-can-eat lunch and dinner experience that delights both seasoned sushi lovers and curious first-timers. We believe great sushi shouldn't be a secret, it should be shared.
