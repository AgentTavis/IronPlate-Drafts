Our Story

Crafted with Passion, Baked with Love in Cary.

Welcome to Paul and Jack's Bakery, Cary's newest destination for artisan and craft desserts. Since opening our doors at 1881 Lake Pine Drive, we've been overwhelmed by the warm reception from our community.

It's not uncommon to find our cafe bustling, especially on a Friday evening, with both our cozy indoor and charming outdoor seating areas at full capacity. We're proud to have quickly become a local favorite, known for our handcrafted pastries, delicious coffee, and vibrant atmosphere.

We invite you to experience the passion and craftsmanship in every bite – whether you're joining us for a quick coffee, a sweet treat, or a relaxing afternoon. Come see why everyone is talking about Paul and Jack's Bakery!

Why Paul and Jack's

More Than Just a Bakery – It's an Experience.

Artisan Quality, Local Charm

Our commitment to "Artisan and craft desserts" shines in every bite, from our delicate Pastries to our rich Slice Carrot Cake. With a stellar 4.9-star rating, we're a beloved fixture in the Cary community.

Daily Fresh Bakes & Variety

Experience the joy of freshly baked goods every morning. Our selection boasts popular items like Bomboloni, Chocolate Almond Croissants, and Filled Donuts, ensuring a fresh delight for every palate.

Community Hub & Family Favorite

We pride ourselves on being a lively gathering spot. With both indoor and outdoor seating often at capacity, and a warm welcome for dogs, Paul and Jack's is where Cary comes to connect and indulge.
