Our Heritage

A Taste of Home, Rooted in Henderson Tradition.

At Hattie's, we believe in the simple pleasure of good food and warm company. For years, our diner has been a cornerstone of the Henderson community, serving up classic comfort food made with care and a sprinkle of nostalgia. We're more than just a place to eat; we're a gathering spot where friendships are forged over a fresh cup of coffee and families create memories around our tables.

Every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and a dedication to the timeless flavors that make a diner truly special. We invite you to step in, relax, and experience the welcoming atmosphere and delicious meals that have made Hattie's a local favorite.

Our Offerings

Diner Classics, Comfort Food, Done Right.

Hearty Breakfast Plates

Start your day the Hattie's way with our generous breakfast platters, featuring eggs cooked to order, crispy bacon, savory sausage, golden hash browns, and fluffy pancakes.

Classic Sandwiches

From towering club sandwiches to grilled cheese melts, our lunch sandwiches are made with fresh ingredients and served with your choice of side, perfect for a midday treat.

Daily Lunch Specials

Ask about our rotating daily specials, crafted with seasonal ingredients and designed to offer you a taste of something new and exciting for lunch.

Freshly Brewed Coffee

Homemade Desserts
