Our Story

From Our Hometown Kitchen, To Your Plate.

At Mug Shotz Cafe & Quick Trigger Brewing Co., we're more than just a diner – we're a beloved part of the Ahoskie community. We pride ourselves on serving up classic Southern comfort food with a smile, ensuring every visit feels like coming home.

Our journey began with a simple passion: to create a place where great food, friendly faces, and fantastic coffee come together. We love seeing our guests enjoy everything from a hearty breakfast to a fresh-made sandwich, always with that touch of genuine Southern hospitality.
