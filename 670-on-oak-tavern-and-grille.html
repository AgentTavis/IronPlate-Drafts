Our Legacy

The Heartbeat of Spruce Pine Dining

From a cherished drive-in diner to a vibrant evening gathering spot, 670 on Oak Tavern and Grille has been a beloved fixture in Spruce Pine for years. We're more than just a place to eat; we're where neighbors gather, friends celebrate, and good times are always on the menu.

Our commitment to quality shines through in every dish, from our famous double cheeseburgers to our perfectly crisp fish and chips. With a full bar offering a wide selection of drinks and live music filling the air on weekends, every visit to 670 on Oak feels like coming home.
