Our Journey

Our Passion, Your Plate: The Samantha's Pupusas Story

Welcome to Samantha's Pupusas, a true hidden gem nestled just outside the historic district of Hillsborough, NC. We are a local favorite, known for serving up authentic Salvadorian dishes and irresistible Mexican delights at prices that will make you smile.

Our kitchen is a hub of flavor, crafting delicious meals that have garnered us a fantastic 4.7-star rating from hundreds of satisfied customers. We take immense pride in every dish we prepare, from our affordable and absolutely delicious pupusas to a wide array of other traditional offerings.

Among our must-try specialties are the garlic and chicharrón pupusas, praised by many for their rich, savory taste. For the adventurous palate, we're proud to offer unique fillings like the delicate loroco flower—a true Salvadorian treat that many experience for the first time with us. And for those seeking something truly unique, explore our huitlacoche options across many of our food offerings.

At Samantha's Pupusas, we believe in bringing the vibrant, heartwarming flavors of El Salvador and Mexico straight to your table. Come visit us and discover your new favorite spot!
