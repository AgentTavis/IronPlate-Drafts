Our Story

A Local Gem Born from a Love for Great Food.

We were looking for breakfast in what seemed like the middle of nowhere and this little coffee shop popped up on our search. It did not disappoint!! It was a Sunday morning so only biscuits were offered for breakfast. We got the sausage, egg and cheese biscuits and coffees. It was one of the best biscuits we ever ate! The sausage was amazing, the coffee was hot and definitely coffee shop quality. We also had to try a piece of one of the beautiful cakes displayed on the counter and OMG! The butter-cream frosting was heavenly, and the cake itself was incredibly moist.

Big Blue 252 Coffee Bar + Plus truly is a hidden gem in Creswell, offering a warm welcome and an unforgettable breakfast experience that keeps locals and travelers coming back for more. Come taste the difference of homemade goodness and friendly service.
