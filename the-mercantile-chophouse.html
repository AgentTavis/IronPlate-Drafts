Our Legacy

Taste the History. Savor the Craft.

Once home to the Boyles Mercantile and Shore Mercantile Company, this community landmark now houses The Mercantile Chophouse, a culinary destination renowned for celebrating the rich heritage of King, NC. We meticulously source local ingredients, transforming them into classic American steakhouse dishes, all served within a historic venue that whispers tales of yesteryear.

Our commitment is to an unparalleled dining experience, blending tradition with refined taste. From our carefully curated menu to our warm, inviting atmosphere, every detail at The Mercantile Chophouse is designed to create lasting memories for our guests.

Our Promise

Why The Mercantile Chophouse Stands Apart

A Taste of Local Heritage

Our commitment to the King, NC community extends to our plate. We pride ourselves on partnering with local farmers and purveyors to bring you the freshest, highest-quality ingredients, celebrating the bounty of our region in every dish.

Dining in History

Step into a meticulously restored piece of King's past. The Mercantile Chophouse retains the character and charm of its Boyles Mercantile and Shore Mercantile Company days, offering an ambiance unlike any other. Every visit is a journey through time, paired with culinary excellence.

Unforgettable Culinary Craftsmanship

With a 4.6-star rating from 116 delighted guests, our passion for fine dining shines through. Our chefs craft classic American steakhouse dishes with precision and flair, ensuring each bite is an exquisite experience that keeps you coming back.
