OUR STORY

A Taste of Tradition, Since 2002

For over two decades, Amalfi's Italian Restaurant & Pizzeria has been a cherished part of the Wilkesboro community. We opened our doors in 2002 with a simple mission: to bring the authentic, traditional flavors of Italy to Wilkes County. From day one, our commitment has been to quality, comfort, and a true family dining experience.

Our passion is evident in every dish. We take pride in making our sauces fresh daily, ensuring that rich, homemade taste that keeps our customers coming back year after year. Our New York style pizza, a true highlight, is crafted with the perfect crust and balance of toppings, making it spot-on every time.

We've built a reputation for consistency and warmth, where every meal is good, and every guest feels like family. Come experience the tradition, the fresh ingredients, and the comforting taste that has made Amalfi's a local favorite since 2002.
