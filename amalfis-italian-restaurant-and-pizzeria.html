OUR STORY

Two Decades of Authentic Italian Tradition

Since 2002, Amalfi's Italian Restaurant & Pizzeria has been a beloved cornerstone of the Wilkesboro community. We are passionate about bringing the true flavors of Italy to your table, crafted with time-honored recipes and a commitment to quality.

Our loyal customers have been with us for years, and for good reason: the food is consistently superb. We believe in fresh, made-from-scratch ingredients, especially our rich, flavorful sauces. And yes, our New York-style pizza? It's spot on—a taste of the Big Apple right here in North Carolina.

"Amalfi's has been our family's go-to for authentic Italian for as long as I can remember. The consistency is incredible, and you can truly taste the freshness in every dish. Their NY style pizza is simply the best!"

— A Loyal Customer from Wilkesboro
