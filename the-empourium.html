Our Journey

More Than Just Drinks: Your Community Hub for Fun

Nestled in the heart of Kernersville, The Empourium stands as a beacon for those seeking a unique late-night experience. We've cultivated a space where quality beverages meet engaging entertainment, making every visit memorable.

From the moment you walk in, you'll discover a world of taste, featuring an expansive selection of over 200 craft beers and meticulously crafted cocktails. Our commitment to quality extends to every detail, from sourcing incredibly fresh local ciders to offering accommodating options like our popular dairy-free White Russian. We believe everyone should find something they love here, and we're constantly evolving to keep up with the trends.

But we're more than just great drinks. The Empourium is where friends gather, where live music fills the air, and where the nostalgic glow of retro arcade games brings out the kid in all of us. Come join our vibrant community and discover your new favorite spot.
