Our Story

Crafting Community, One Brew at a Time.

Welcome to White Elephant Beer Company, where the love for great beer meets delicious food and a warm, inviting atmosphere. Nestled in the heart of Mt Airy, we've built more than just a bar and grill; we've cultivated a community hub where friends gather, stories are shared, and every visit feels like coming home.

From the moment you walk through our doors, you're not just a customer – you're part of the White Elephant family. We're dedicated to serving up an exceptional experience, whether you're here for a quick bite, a leisurely dinner, or to catch up over a perfectly poured pint. Come see why we're a beloved fixture in the Mt Airy scene.

Our Selection

Feast Your Eyes. Satisfy Your Cravings.

Craft Burgers

Juicy, gourmet burgers crafted with premium ingredients and served on toasted brioche buns. Customize with your favorite toppings.

Artisan Flatbreads

Crispy, thin-crust flatbreads topped with fresh, high-quality ingredients. Perfect for sharing or a light meal.

Local & Regional Brews

A rotating selection of the finest craft beers from local Mt Airy breweries and beloved regional favorites. Ask your server for today's tap list!

Signature Cocktails

Expertly mixed cocktails, from timeless classics to our unique house specials, designed to complement your meal or stand alone.

Pub Favorites

Sweet Endings
