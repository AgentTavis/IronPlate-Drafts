Our Legacy

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Kings Mountain Tradition

Doanes Carolina Cafe has been a cornerstone of the Kings Mountain community for decades. Located on S Cansler St, our charming eatery is known for its inviting, casual, and cozy atmosphere—a true taste of home.

We pride ourselves on serving up delicious comfort food and classic Southern dishes, prepared with love and the freshest local ingredients. Every visit to Doanes is an experience, a chance to gather with family and friends, share stories, and create lasting memories over hearty meals.

From our family to yours, we invite you to pull up a chair and discover why Doanes Carolina Cafe isn't just a restaurant; it's a beloved part of Kings Mountain life.

"Doanes feels like stepping back in time to my grandma's kitchen. The fried chicken is out of this world, and the staff always makes you feel like family. A true local treasure!"

- Sarah L., Loyal Customer
