Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Memory.

Doanes Carolina Cafe is a charming eatery nestled on S Cansler St in the heart of Kings Mountain, NC. From the moment you step inside, you're enveloped in an inviting, casual, and cozy atmosphere that feels just like home. We believe in the power of a good meal to bring people together, to comfort the soul, and to create lasting memories.

Our kitchen is a labor of love, dedicated to serving up delicious comfort food and classic Southern dishes passed down through generations. We source local ingredients whenever possible, ensuring every plate that leaves our kitchen is fresh, flavorful, and made with the kind of care you'd expect from your own family. Come gather around our table and experience the true taste of Carolina hospitality.
