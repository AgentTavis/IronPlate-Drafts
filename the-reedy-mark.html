OUR STORY

Where Sophistication Meets Southern Charm

Located in the heart of Roseboro, NC, The Reedy Mark stands out as a sophisticated establishment offering a selection of allocated bourbons, premium spirits, and finely crafted American fare. Our vision was to create a warm, inviting space where friends and family could gather to enjoy exceptional drinks and elevated comfort food.

We pride ourselves on sourcing the best ingredients and curating a beverage list that delights connoisseurs and casual diners alike. From our carefully selected whiskeys to our expertly mixed cocktails, every detail at The Reedy Mark is designed to provide an unforgettable experience that blends small-town hospitality with big-city quality.
