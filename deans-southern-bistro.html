OUR STORY

A Taste of Southern Comfort

Welcome to Deans Southern Bistro, nestled right here in Wilkesboro, NC. We are passionate about serving up some of the most delicious home-cooked style meals you'll find around. Our kitchen is a place where classic Southern flavors meet heartwarming comfort, creating dishes that taste just like Grandma used to make.

From savory wings and juicy burgers to our special chicken entrees, every dish is prepared with care and a generous helping of hospitality. But we're not just about main courses; our homemade cakes and baked goods are legendary, crafted daily to satisfy your sweet tooth. Looking to host an event? Our flexible catering menus are perfect for any occasion, ensuring your guests enjoy the authentic taste of the South.
