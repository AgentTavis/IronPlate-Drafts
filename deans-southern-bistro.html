OUR STORY

A Taste of Home, Rooted in Tradition

At Deans Southern Bistro, we believe good food brings people together. Located in the heart of Wilkesboro, NC, we've been proudly serving our community with the kind of delicious, home-cooked style meals that warm the soul and satisfy the palate.

Our journey began with a simple passion: to offer hearty American classics alongside delightful homemade desserts. From our savory wings, juicy burgers, and tender chicken dishes to our famous homemade cakes and baked goods, every item on our menu is crafted with care and a touch of Southern hospitality. We also offer comprehensive catering menus, making us the perfect choice for any event, big or small.
