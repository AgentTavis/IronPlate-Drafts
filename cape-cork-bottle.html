Our Journey

Your Oasis on the Outer Banks.

This cape is not just for superheroes... it's for everyone seeking a tranquil escape in Avon, NC. At Cape Cork & Bottle, we've poured our passion into creating a welcoming haven where every visitor feels like family from the moment they walk through our doors.

Our owners meticulously search near and far to curate an exceptional selection of the best brews and fine wines. We believe in quality, comfort, and community – offering a perfect blend of sophisticated sips and laid-back island charm. Whether you're unwinding inside our inviting bar or relaxing on our fantastic outdoor patio (shoes optional, good vibes mandatory!), you'll discover why we're a must-stop while visiting the island.
