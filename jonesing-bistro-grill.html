Our Story

Taste the Passion, Savor the Flames.

More than just a meal, Jonesing Bistro Grill is an experience crafted with love, dedication, and the finest ingredients, right here in South Holland, NC.

Welcome to Jonesing Bistro Grill, where every bite is a celebration of flavor! We founded Jonesing with a simple yet profound vision: to create a dining destination where expertly grilled dishes are crafted fresh every single day, just for you. We believe in the magic that happens when food is kissed by the flames, imparting that unbeatable smoky taste that makes every meal memorable.

Our journey began with a craving—a 'jonesing' for something truly exceptional in South Holland. We poured our hearts into perfecting every recipe, sourcing the best ingredients, and creating an atmosphere where friends and family can gather, laugh, and indulge in the pure joy of great food. From our kitchen to your table, we promise a meal that tantalizes your taste buds and leaves you wanting more. Join us and discover your new favorite spot!
