Our Journey

Crafting Sweet Memories, One Treat at a Time

Welcome to Kool Kakes Bakery & Cafe, a cherished spot in the heart of Rockingham, NC. We believe that every celebration, big or small, deserves a memorable treat. Our journey began with a simple passion: to bake the most delicious and beautiful cakes, and to create a warm, inviting space where friends and family can gather over delightful pastries and refreshing drinks.

From custom birthday cakes that bring dreams to life to a diverse menu of everyday delights like boba tea, rolled ice cream, and freshly baked croissants, we pour our heart into everything we make. It's more than just a bakery; it's a place where happiness is baked fresh daily.
