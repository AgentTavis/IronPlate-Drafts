Our Heritage

Taste the Tradition: More Than Just Seafood

Mayflower Seafood Restaurant has been a cherished part of the Roanoke Rapids community for years. Located conveniently at 1330 Julian R Allsbrook Hwy, we've built our reputation on serving delicious, fresh seafood in a welcoming atmosphere that feels just like home.

From the moment you walk through our doors, you're not just a customer; you're family. We pride ourselves on generous portions and reasonable prices, making us the perfect spot for any occasion, whether it's a casual family dinner or a gathering with friends. Experience the taste of the coast, right here in Roanoke Rapids.
