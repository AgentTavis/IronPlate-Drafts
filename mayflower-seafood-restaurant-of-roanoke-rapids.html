Our Heritage

Serving the Freshest Flavors in Roanoke Rapids

Located conveniently at 1330 Julian R Allsbrook Hwy, Mayflower Seafood Restaurant has been a beloved fixture in Roanoke Rapids for years. We pride ourselves on being the top choice for families and groups seeking delicious, high-quality seafood in a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

From our commitment to generous portions to our dedication to keeping prices reasonable, every aspect of the Mayflower experience is designed with our guests in mind. We believe that great seafood should be accessible to everyone, and we work hard to ensure every visit leaves you satisfied and planning your next meal with us.
