Our Story

Your Home Away From Home in Mooresville

At K Craft Beer and Wine, we've created the "man cave" you always wanted but never built. Step into a space adorned with distressed wood, multiple flat screens, and comfy couches – designed for pure relaxation. Our fridges are always stocked with an ever-changing selection of incredible craft beers and fine wines, ensuring there's always something new to discover.

It's more than just a bar; it's a community hub. Our owner, Kurt, often works behind the bar, ensuring a personal touch and a friendly welcome. With live music every week and Sunday grill-outs, it truly feels like hanging out at a friend's house. And if you're on the go, grab your favorite brews or bottles to take with you!

We strive for a friendly, welcoming atmosphere and a chill vibe, making K Craft Beer and Wine Mooresville's go-to spot for good times and great drinks.
