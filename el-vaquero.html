Our Journey

Where Tradition Meets Taste

Our Specialties

Savor the Authentic Taste of Mexico

Tacos al Pastor

Marinated pork, cooked on a vertical spit, served in warm corn tortillas with pineapple, onions, and cilantro.

Enchiladas Suizas

Soft corn tortillas filled with tender chicken, smothered in a rich, creamy green tomatillo sauce and melted cheese.

Carnitas Plate

Slow-cooked, tender, and juicy pork carnitas, served with rice, beans, tortillas, and your choice of salsa.

Chile Relleno

Roasted poblano pepper stuffed with cheese, battered and lightly fried, topped with a savory red sauce.

Quesadillas Fajita

Large flour tortilla filled with grilled steak or chicken fajita, bell peppers, onions, and melted Monterey Jack cheese.

Guacamole Fresco

Freshly mashed avocados, mixed with diced tomatoes, onions, cilantro, and a hint of jalapeño. Served with crispy tortilla chips.
