The Authentic Mexican Experience in Conover

Welcome to El Vaquero, where we bring the vibrant flavors of Mexico to Conover, NC. We pride ourselves on serving the BEST Mexican food around Locust, Midland, Concord, and Mount Pleasant – a claim our loyal customers happily back up!

Every dish at El Vaquero is prepared with passion and the freshest ingredients, cooked to perfection by our great chef. We believe in plentiful, delicious food that reminds you of home, or introduces you to the authentic taste of Mexico for the very first time.

Tacos al Pastor
