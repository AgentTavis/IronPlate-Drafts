Our Heritage

A Taste of Home, Cooked with Passion

Nestled at 349 Rink Dam Rd in Taylorsville, NC, PJ's Bethlehem Seafood & Steak is more than just a restaurant; it's a culinary cornerstone where delicious memories are made. We pride ourselves on offering a delightful dining experience with a menu featuring the freshest seafood and succulent, perfectly cooked steaks.

Our commitment to quality, warm hospitality, and a welcoming atmosphere has made us a beloved local spot. Whether you're craving our hand-breaded fish, wonderful oysters, or a hearty pork chop special, we ensure every dish is crafted with care, bringing that authentic Southern comfort food feel right to your table. Come and discover why our community keeps coming back for more!
