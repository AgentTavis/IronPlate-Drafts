Our Heritage

Generations of Flavor, Crafted with Love.

Nestled in the heart of Taylorsville, NC, PJ's Bethlehem Seafood & Steak has been a beloved family tradition for years. We believe in the simple pleasure of good food, prepared with the freshest ingredients and served with genuine Southern hospitality.

From our hand-breaded seafood caught with care to our perfectly grilled steaks, every dish tells a story of passion and dedication. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a gathering place where memories are made around the table.

Come experience the difference that local, homemade cooking makes. We can't wait to welcome you to our table.
