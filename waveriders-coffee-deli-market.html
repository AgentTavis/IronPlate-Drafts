OUR STORY

More Than Just a Meal: A Nags Head Tradition.

Nestled in the heart of Nags Head, Waveriders Coffee, Deli & Market is where community gathers and flavors come alive. For years, we've been dedicated to serving up comfort and quality, from the first aroma of our freshly brewed coffee to the last bite of a perfectly crafted deli sandwich.

We're a mom-and-pop shop with a big heart, passionate about simple, delicious food made with care. Whether you're a local starting your day, a visitor fueling up for beach adventures, or someone seeking a quiet spot for a quality lunch, you'll find a warm welcome and a taste of Nags Head's finest here.
