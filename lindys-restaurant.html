Our Roots

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Fayetteville Tradition

For over 50 years, Lindy's Restaurant has been a cornerstone of Fayetteville, NC, serving up hearty, handmade meals that feel like home. What started in 1968 has grown into a beloved local institution, where every breakfast tells a story.

We believe in good food, made with care, just like you'd find in your own kitchen. Our menu is packed with options, but no matter what you choose, quality and flavor are always spot on.

As one customer put it: "I have been in Fayetteville for almost 4 years now. My elderly dad and I try new places all the time but 3 1/2 years ago we went to Lindy's for the first time and this is the #1 spot for a good, hardy, handmade meal. Lindy's is our normal Breakfast stop. Breakfast (and other meals) is always spot on. There are several options but regardless what you try it is always good. Now let me quickly swivel to the COFFEE... OMG!! Best coffee ever!!!! They do not use the standard coffee like other breakfast spots. It’s the perfect start to any day, a true taste of home right here in Fayetteville. Come experience the warmth and tradition that has made Lindy's a local legend since 1968."
