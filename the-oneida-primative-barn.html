Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal: A Taste of Oneida Tradition

The Oneida Primitive Barn welcomes all, whether you're dining in for a relaxed meal or need a quick bite on the go with our convenient takeout option. Our courteous and dedicated staff works hard to ensure every visit is a memorable one, serving up hearty Southern classics in a warm, family-friendly atmosphere.

We're not just a place to eat; we're a hub where local history meets delicious food, offering a unique blend of a bar & grill experience alongside an intriguing collection of handcrafted items, antiques, and collectibles. Every corner tells a story, making your visit to The Primitive Barn an experience that delights all the senses.
