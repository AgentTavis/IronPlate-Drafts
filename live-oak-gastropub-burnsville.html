Our Story

More Than Just a Meal: A Burnsville Tradition

Live Oak Gastropub @ Burnsville is a beloved local spot in Burnsville, NC. Nestled in the heart of our vibrant community, we are more than just a restaurant; we are a gathering place where cherished memories are made.

Our passion is to offer a unique dining experience, combining the warm, welcoming atmosphere of a local pub with the refined flavors of a gastropub. We're dedicated to serving up delicious bar and grill favorites, crafted with quality ingredients and a touch of culinary creativity. Come on in, reconnect with friends, and discover why we've earned our reputation as Burnsville's favorite.

Our Menu

Crafting Flavors, One Plate at a Time

Gastropub Burgers

Our signature burgers, crafted with premium beef and gourmet toppings, served on a toasted brioche bun with a side of crispy fries.

Artisanal Sandwiches

From classic reubens to innovative grilled cheeses, our sandwiches are elevated comfort food, made with fresh, local ingredients.

Savory Appetizers

Perfect for sharing, our starters include delicious wings, loaded nachos, and crispy onion rings, ideal for kicking off your meal.

Hearty Entrees

Beyond the grill, discover satisfying main courses, from succulent steaks to fresh seasonal specials that change weekly.

Craft Beer & Cocktails

Explore our extensive selection of local craft beers on tap and expertly mixed cocktails, perfect for any occasion.

Desserts & Sweets

Indulge in our delightful selection of homemade desserts, the perfect sweet ending to your gastropub experience.
