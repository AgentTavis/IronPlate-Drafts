Our Heritage

More Than Just a Tavern, It's Home

Tavern At Tina's Country House is a beloved local spot in Macomb, NC, a true gem where friends gather and memories are made. For years, we've poured our hearts into creating a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels like family.

From the moment you walk through our doors, you're greeted with warmth and genuine hospitality. We're proud to be the heartbeat of Macomb, offering a lively and cozy retreat where laughter fills the air and stories are shared over a good meal or a refreshing drink.

"Tina's isn't just a place to eat, it's a feeling. The warmth, the friendly faces, it's truly the soul of Macomb. I always leave with a smile."

— A Loyal Local, Macomb Resident
