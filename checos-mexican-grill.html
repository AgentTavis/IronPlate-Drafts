Our Roots

A Taste of Mexico, Crafted with Passion.

Welcome to Checos Mexican Grill, where passion for authentic Mexican cuisine comes alive in Raleigh, NC. Founded by Chef Oscar Diaz, our mission is to bring you fresh, vibrant flavors and a dining experience that feels like home.

Every dish is a celebration of traditional recipes passed down through generations, reimagined with a modern touch. We believe in honest food, exceptional service, and creating memorable moments around the table for our community.

Chef Oscar's dedication to quality ingredients and time-honored techniques ensures every bite transports you straight to Mexico. Join us and discover your new favorite local spot!
