Our Philosophy

Creating Unforgettable Moments, One Plate at a Time.

We set out to create a fun, lively, and approachable restaurant and bar in our own backyard — a friendly, bustling neighborhood spot where the very best in food and drink would be served in a welcoming setting.

At Olive + Oak, we are looking at every single detail to make sure that it will be the perfect place to enjoy great wine, craft beer, well-made drinks and thoughtful, ingredient-driven food with friends and family. Our passion for hospitality shines through in every aspect of your visit, ensuring a truly memorable experience.
