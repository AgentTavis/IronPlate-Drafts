OUR STORY

Where Every Bite Tells a Tale of Local Flavor and Passion

The Bakery at Mount Pleasant is proud to be your local, neighborhood bakery, nestled in the heart of Mt Pleasant, NC. We specialize in crafting beautiful, uniquely decorated almond shortbread cookies, stunning wedding and event cakes, and a wide array of festive treats that bring joy to every occasion.

Our passion is creating delicious memories, one sweet bite at a time, using only the finest ingredients and a sprinkle of local charm. We're more than just a bakery; we're a community hub where friends and family gather over exceptional coffee and homemade delights. Come experience the warmth and taste the love that goes into every single item we bake.
