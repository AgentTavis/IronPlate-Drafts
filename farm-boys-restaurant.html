Our Heritage

Taste the Tradition, Feel the Charm: Your Belhaven Home Away From Home

Farm Boy's Restaurant is where timeless taste meets genuine small-town charm. We’re celebrated for our classic American comfort food – think sizzling burgers, delicious hot dogs, and a variety of crispy fried favorites that hit just the right spot. For generations, we've been serving up hearty meals and warm smiles, becoming a true local favorite in Belhaven, NC.

What truly sets us apart is our inviting outdoor seating area. Nestled on a charming deck with rustic wooden picnic tables, it offers a serene escape. On a beautiful day, with the gentle breeze off the Pungo River, it's the perfect spot to enjoy a laid-back meal and soak in the best of Belhaven.

"There's nothing quite like enjoying a classic burger on their outdoor deck with the river breeze. It's truly a Belhaven gem!"

— A Happy Local
