Our Heritage

Where Every Meal Feels Like Home.

Welcome to The Barge, your cherished local diner in Carolina Beach. For years, we've been the heart of the community, serving up generous portions of homemade comfort food with a side of genuine Southern hospitality. Just a stone's throw from the Carolina Beach Boardwalk, we're the perfect spot to start your day with a hearty breakfast, grab a satisfying lunch, or unwind with a classic dinner.

We believe in simple pleasures: fresh ingredients, friendly faces, and the kind of cooking that reminds you of grandma's kitchen. It's more than just a meal here; it's an experience built on tradition and a passion for making our guests feel like family. Come on in, pull up a chair, and discover why The Barge is a Carolina Beach institution.
