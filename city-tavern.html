Our Heritage

Bringing Salisbury Together Since Day One

Welcome to City Tavern, Salisbury's beloved Bar And Grill where good times, great food, and lasting memories are always on the menu. Nestled in the heart of downtown, we pride ourselves on offering a vibrant atmosphere, fast, friendly service, and a truly exceptional dining experience.

From our inviting outdoor seating perfect for a sunny afternoon, to our unparalleled beer selection featuring local crafts and national favorites, City Tavern is more than just a place to eat – it's a community hub. We believe in serving up delicious shareables that bring people closer, like our famous Mozzarella Wedges and spicy Tempura Jalapeno.

Come for the food, stay for the camaraderie. We're dedicated to being your go-to spot for everything from a casual lunch to a lively evening with friends.
