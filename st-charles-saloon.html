Our Heritage

Our Passion for Pizza, Rooted in Columbia.

At St. Charles Saloon, we believe great pizza is more than just food—it's a shared experience. Nestled in the heart of Columbia, NC, our saloon is where time-honored traditions meet modern flavors. We're dedicated to crafting pizzas that bring people together, using only the freshest ingredients and a whole lot of heart.

From our family to yours, we invite you to taste the difference that passion and quality make. Whether it's a casual night out or a special celebration, St. Charles Saloon is your friendly neighborhood spot for delicious food, cold beer, and good times.
