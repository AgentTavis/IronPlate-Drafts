Our Story

Taste the Comfort, Feel the Warmth of Lydia's Kitchen.

At Lydia's, we believe in the power of a truly satisfying meal. It's more than just food; it's about warmth, comfort, and generous portions that remind you of home. From the moment you step in, you're part of our family.

My better half opted for the breakfast burrito covered with sausage gravy and accompanied by hash browns (off the posted specials board) and I went with a skillet (can't recall the name) stacked with hash browns, open-faced biscuit, sausage patties, gravy and two over easy eggs.
