Our Heritage

A Passion for Fresh Seafood

At Taste and See, we believe in bringing the vibrant flavors of the ocean directly to your plate in Asheboro, NC. Our journey began with a simple vision: to offer high-quality, delicious seafood in a welcoming atmosphere. We carefully select the freshest ingredients, preparing each dish with dedication and a touch of coastal inspiration.

From our humble beginnings participating in local events, we've grown into a beloved community spot, thanks to the unwavering support of our guests. We're constantly striving to elevate your dining experience, ensuring every visit is memorable, filled with exquisite tastes and warm hospitality.

Our Culinary Creations

Explore Our Fresh Catches

Grilled Salmon

Succulent Atlantic salmon, perfectly grilled, served with seasonal vegetables and lemon-dill sauce.

Shrimp Scampi

Jumbo shrimp sautéed in garlic butter, white wine, and fresh herbs, tossed with linguine.

Fish Tacos

Crispy or grilled white fish, fresh slaw, pickled onions, cilantro, and a zesty Baja sauce in warm tortillas.

Cajun Crab Boil

A generous feast of snow crab legs, shrimp, sausage, corn, and potatoes seasoned with our signature Cajun spice blend.

Seafood Platter

A delectable assortment of fried shrimp, scallops, and catch of the day, with crispy fries and tartar sauce.

New England Clam Chowder

Rich and creamy, packed with tender clams, potatoes, and savory bacon.

Lobster Roll

Sweet, tender lobster meat lightly dressed and piled high on a buttered, toasted brioche bun.

Calamari Fries

Lightly breaded and fried calamari strips, served with spicy marinara and lemon aioli.
