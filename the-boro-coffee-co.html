Our Story

Brewed with Love, Served with Passion in Roxboro

At The Boro Coffee Co, we believe that every cup tells a story. As a proud family-owned coffee shop, we've poured our hearts into creating a warm, inviting space right here in Roxboro, NC, where community gathers and flavors flourish.

Our journey began with a simple passion: to perfect the art of coffee. But it didn't stop there. We quickly realized that great coffee deserves great company—and delicious food to match! That's why we meticulously craft every dish, sweet, and dessert right in front of you, ensuring freshness and quality in every bite.

From our family to yours, we invite you to experience the difference. Come for the coffee, stay for the comfort, and leave with a smile. It's more than just a meal; it's a moment, made fresh, just for you.
