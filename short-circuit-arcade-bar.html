Our Story

More Than Just a Bar, It's a Community

Short Circuit Arcade Bar is a beloved local spot in Lexington, NC. Step into a world where classic arcade games meet modern pub fare and craft brews. We're more than just a bar; we're a community hub where friends gather, high scores are chased, and good times are always on tap.

From the nostalgic glow of retro cabinets to the sizzle of our grill, every visit promises a memorable experience for gamers and foodies alike. We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming atmosphere where everyone can unwind, compete, and enjoy delicious food and drinks.

"Short Circuit is my favorite spot in Lexington. The atmosphere is electric, the games are a blast, and the food always hits the spot. It truly feels like home!"

— A Loyal Customer
