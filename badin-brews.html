Our Journey

Experience the Heart of Albemarle at Badin Brews

Nestled in the vibrant heart of downtown Albemarle, Badin Brews is more than just a restaurant – it's a destination. We believe in crafting memorable experiences, from the first sip of a locally sourced craft beer to the last bite of our signature dishes.

Our journey began with a passion for bringing people together over exceptional food, fine wines, and the best live music the region has to offer. We're proud to be a local gathering spot, a place where good vibes are always on tap, and every visit feels like coming home.

Don't miss our famous O-M-Cheese Burger, now with more cheese for just $11.99! It's a crowd-pleaser that embodies our commitment to quality and flavor, making Badin Brews a true fine dining experience with a welcoming, down-to-earth spirit.

"Badin Brews consistently delivers! The atmosphere is fantastic, the craft beer selection is top-notch, and the O-M-Cheese Burger is an absolute must-try. Albemarle is lucky to have this gem."
