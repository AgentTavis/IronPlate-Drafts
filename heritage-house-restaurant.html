Our Story

Taste the Soul of Southern Hospitality, Crafted with Love for Generations.

At Heritage House Restaurant, we believe in the comforting power of a home-cooked meal. Nestled in the heart of Windsor, NC, we've been serving up authentic Southern flavors with a side of genuine hospitality since 1995. Our kitchen is a place where tradition meets taste, where every dish is prepared with the care and warmth you'd find at your grandma's table.

From our overflowing buffet featuring classic Southern staples to our famously homemade cakes and refreshing sweet tea, we're dedicated to bringing you a true taste of the South. Whether you're dining in, grabbing a meal to go, or letting us cater your special event, we promise an experience that feels like coming home. Join our family at Heritage House and create your own delicious memories.
