Our Heritage

Where Community Meets Craft

At Slate Farm Brewery, we believe life's best moments are forged over shared meals and exceptional drinks. Born from a passion for bringing people together, we've cultivated a space where GOOD FRIENDS gather, making every visit a chance for GOOD TIMES to unfold.

Our kitchen is dedicated to serving truly GOOD FOOD, crafted with fresh, quality ingredients that satisfy and delight. And, of course, our name wouldn't be 'Brewery' without an unwavering commitment to GREAT BEER – from refreshing lagers to bold IPAs, all brewed with care and precision right here in Whiteford, NC.

We're more than just a bar and grill; we're a cornerstone of the Whiteford community, inviting you to create lasting memories with us.
