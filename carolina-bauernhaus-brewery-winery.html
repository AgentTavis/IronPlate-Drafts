Our Journey

From Local Passion to Community Hub.

At Carolina Bauernhaus, we believe in the art of fermentation and the joy of shared experiences. Nestled in the heart of Anderson, NC, we craft exceptional small-batch beers and unique wines, complemented by a menu of delicious, scratch-made bar and grill favorites.

Our passion is to create a welcoming space where friends, family, and community can gather, unwind, and savor truly authentic flavors. We're more than just a brewery and winery; we're a place where stories are shared, and every visit feels like coming home.

"Carolina Bauernhaus is truly a gem in Anderson. Their commitment to unique flavors and a welcoming atmosphere is evident in every detail. It's our go-to spot for great food and even better company!"
