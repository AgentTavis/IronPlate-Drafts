Our Heritage

A Culinary Journey Rooted in Conway

At Rivertown Bistro, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a part of Conway's vibrant community. Our passion for exceptional seafood and Southern hospitality shines through in every dish we prepare. We believe in creating memorable dining experiences that blend local charm with sophisticated flavors.

We are incredibly proud to join our community in congratulating our Chef/Owner, Darren Smith, for his recent appointment as a 2025 SC CHEF AMBASSADOR by Governor Henry McMaster! This esteemed recognition highlights Chef Smith's dedication to culinary excellence and showcases the best of South Carolina's rich gastronomic traditions right here at Rivertown Bistro.

Join us to celebrate and savor the flavors that make our bistro a true local gem, continuously striving to bring innovative and delicious dishes to your table.

Our Promise

Why Rivertown Bistro is Your Best Choice

Award-Winning Culinary Excellence

Led by 2025 SC Chef Ambassador, Darren Smith, our kitchen crafts innovative and delicious dishes that truly showcase the best of coastal South Carolina flavors. Experience the difference an award-winning chef makes.

Unmatched Seafood Freshness

From our Oysters on the ½ Shell to our Cajun Shrimp and Fried Calamari & Creek Shrimp, we prioritize the freshest, locally sourced seafood available, ensuring every bite is a taste of the Atlantic's bounty.

Unique Bistro Creations

Beyond traditional seafood, discover unique Rivertown Bistro originals like our Lowcountry Spring Rolls, Daily Fondue, and specialty sushi rolls such as the General Tso Roll. There's always something new to savor.
