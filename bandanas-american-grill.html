Our Roots

From Downtown Burgaw to Your Table

A New Chapter for Bandanas Grill

Bandanas American Grill has a long-standing history in Burgaw, beloved by locals for its comforting flavors and welcoming atmosphere. While locations may change, our commitment to serving hearty, delicious American classics remains at the core of who we are. We're always striving to improve, listen to our community, and bring you the best possible dining experience right here in Burgaw.

Come on down and taste the difference. We're committed to bringing back the quality and value you remember and love, making new memories with every dish served.
