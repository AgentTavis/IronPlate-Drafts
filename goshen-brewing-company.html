Our Journey

Crafting Flavor, Building Community

Welcome to Goshen Brewing Company, where every dish and every pour tells a story of passion, tradition, and innovation. Nestled in the heart of Goshen, Indiana, we're more than just a brewpub – we're a gathering place for families, friends, and food enthusiasts.

Our culinary philosophy is rooted in Southern-style smoked traditions, bringing you mouth-watering BBQ that’s been slow-cooked to perfection. But we don't stop there. We infuse our dishes with a unique Asian influence, creating a vibrant fusion that delights the palate and offers a truly distinct dining experience you won't find anywhere else.

Beyond the plate, our commitment extends to our craft. With a wide selection of meticulously brewed beers, from crisp lagers to hoppy IPAs, there’s a perfect pint for every taste. We believe in farm-to-table freshness, sourcing local and seasonal ingredients to ensure quality and support our community. Come on in, pull up a chair, and become part of our story.

Our Promise

Experience The Goshen Difference

Unique BBQ Fusion

Dive into our one-of-a-kind menu that expertly blends Southern-style smoked BBQ with exciting Asian influences, offering flavors you won't find anywhere else in Goshen. Think smoked brisket with a hint of ginger, or pulled pork with a spicy gochujang glaze!

Handcrafted Local Brews

With over 15 craft beers on tap, all brewed in-house using local ingredients when possible, we promise a perfect pairing for every dish. From refreshing lagers to bold IPAs, our selection caters to every palate.

Family-Friendly & Fresh

We're proud to be a community hub for families, offering a welcoming atmosphere and a menu that prioritizes seasonal, farm-to-table ingredients. Enjoy delicious, wholesome food made with care, perfect for all ages.
