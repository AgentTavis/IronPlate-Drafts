Our Story

A Final Toast, A Lasting Legacy in Raleigh

For years, Touchdown Sports Bar has been a beloved spot for sports fans, friends, and family in Raleigh. We've seen countless games, celebrated victories, and shared memorable moments together. Today marks our final day, and it's with a heavy heart that we announce our permanent closure.

We want to extend our heartfelt gratitude to every single person who walked through our doors, shared a laugh, and cheered on their favorite teams with us. It's been an incredible journey, and while we're closing our doors, the memories made here will last a lifetime.

Join us one last time today, and let's make some final memories. As a special thank you for your unwavering support, we're offering our famous wings for just 75 cents all day long! Come by, say goodbye, and enjoy the best wings in town one last time.
