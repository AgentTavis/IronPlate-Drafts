Our Heritage

More Than Just Desserts: A Passion for European Delights

At Paul & Jack, we bring the exquisite tastes of European patisseries and vibrant New York City bakeries right here to Wake Forest, NC. Our mission is to craft wonderful selections of pastries and sweets that transport you with every bite.

Imagine the flaky layers of a perfect napoleon, the rich creaminess of a Basque cheesecake, or the comforting warmth of an authentic egg custard, reminiscent of a recent trip to Portugal. We believe in tradition, quality ingredients, and the simple joy a truly artisan dessert can bring.

Since opening our doors, we've dedicated ourselves to baking fresh, exceptional treats daily, becoming a beloved part of the community across our locations. We invite you to experience the passion and craftsmanship in every confection.
