Our Story

More Than Just A Bottleship. It's A Community.

At Grapes & Hops, we set out to create more than just a bottleshop – we built a true community hub right here in Cameron, NC. Our vision, cultivated from a passion for unique and high-quality beverages, was to bring a curated selection of fine wines, exceptional craft beers, and local meads and ciders to our neighbors.

We pride ourselves on offering a welcoming atmosphere where every visit feels "like being back home." From the casual drinker exploring new tastes to the seasoned connoisseur, Grapes & Hops is your friendly neighborhood destination for discovery and connection.

"This place feels like home! The selection of craft beers and wines is unmatched in the area, and the atmosphere is always so welcoming."
