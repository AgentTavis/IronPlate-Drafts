Our Heritage

More Than Just a Coffee Shop, It's Home.

Welcome to The Parlor Coffee Bar and Eatery, a beloved fixture in Madison, NC. Nestled in the historic old Wandering Sun location at 104 S Market Street, we're dedicated to serving our community fresh, delicious coffee and comforting diner meals.

Our warm and friendly team strives to create a welcoming atmosphere where every visit feels like coming home. Join us for a moment of quiet reflection, a lively chat, or a hearty meal – your neighborhood spot for good food and great company.
