Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Memory.

At Flying Pig Food & Spirits, we're not just serving meals; we're dishing out a piece of Asheboro's heart. We are proud winners of Eat Drink Restaurant Week, recognized for our outstanding casual dining experience. Our commitment to delicious food, handcrafted with care, and a welcoming atmosphere has made us a beloved local favorite, standing tall alongside esteemed establishments.

Every dish, from our famous stone cooked pizzas to our mouth-watering Philly cheese sandwiches, is prepared with fresh ingredients and a passion for flavor. Come on down and taste why generations of Asheboro residents and visitors keep coming back for more!
