Our Heritage

From Asheboro's Heart, Crafted with Passion

Welcome to Flying Pig Food & Spirits, a true Asheboro gem and a proud winner of Eat Drink Restaurant Week! We're not just another diner; we're a casual dining experience where honest flavors and good times come together. Our story is rooted in a passion for creating delicious, hearty meals that bring people back, time and again.

We're known for our vibrant atmosphere, commitment to quality, and a menu that delights both the classic diner enthusiast and those looking for a unique culinary twist. We love our community, and we believe great food, paired with friendly service, is the best way to show it.
