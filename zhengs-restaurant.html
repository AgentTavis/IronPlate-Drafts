Our Journey

Crafting Healthful & Delicious Moments

At Zheng's Restaurant, we believe that great food starts with great ingredients. Our passion lies in providing healthy and natural dishes that truly satisfy the modern palate. We understand the desire for meals that are not only delicious but also contribute to a vibrant lifestyle.

Every single dish leaving our kitchen is a testament to our commitment. We meticulously select only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients, ensuring that each bite bursts with authentic flavors and is presented beautifully. From farm to table, we prioritize sustainable practices and support local producers whenever possible, bringing you the best Riverdale has to offer.

Come experience the difference that fresh, natural ingredients make. Whether you're a long-time regular or a first-time guest, we invite you to taste the care and dedication in every plate.

Our Culinary Delights

Taste the Freshness

Grilled Salmon with Lemon-Dill

Perfectly grilled salmon, flaky and tender, served with a vibrant lemon-dill sauce and seasonal vegetables. A healthy favorite!

BBQ Ribs Platter

Slow-cooked, fall-off-the-bone BBQ ribs, smothered in our homemade smoky sauce. Served with a side of coleslaw and sweet potato fries.

Classic Cheeseburger

Juicy, 100% grass-fed beef patty, melted cheddar, fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles on a toasted brioche bun. Pure comfort.

Spicy Chicken Wings

Crispy, perfectly seasoned wings tossed in your choice of mild, medium, or fire-hot sauce. Served with blue cheese dip and celery.

Garden Fresh Salad

A medley of organic greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell peppers, and avocado with your choice of house-made vinaigrette.

Sweet Potato Fries

Crispy on the outside, soft on the inside, a healthier and delicious alternative to classic fries. Served with a side of spicy aioli.

Chocolate Lava Cake

A decadent individual chocolate cake with a molten chocolate center, served warm with a scoop of vanilla bean ice cream.

House Craft Beer Selection

Explore our rotating selection of local and regional craft beers on tap, perfect for pairing with any of our grill favorites.
