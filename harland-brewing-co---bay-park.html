Our Heritage

The Spirit of Craft & Community

Harland Brewing Company was created in the image of its owners and by people that inspire others to live their lives doing what they love. Whether it’s building motorcycles in your garage to fly fishing a secret stream, Harland embodies the spirit of rolling up your sleeves to get the job done.

We believe in the artistry of brewing and the honest work of sourcing the finest seafood. Our commitment extends beyond the plate and into the heart of our community, fostering a space where passion, craft, and camaraderie come together. Every dish and every pour tells a story of dedication, quality, and a love for the San Diego way of life.

"Harland Brewing Co. isn't just a place to eat and drink; it's an experience. The atmosphere truly reflects the passion of its creators, and you can taste the dedication in every bite and sip."

— A Dedicated Local Patron
