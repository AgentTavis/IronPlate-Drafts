Our Heritage

More Than Just a Bar, It's a Community.

Nestled in the heart of Goldsboro, Cooper's Tavern has been newly remodeled to bring you a fresh, vibrant bar experience rooted in classic tavern hospitality. We're proud to be a dedicated biker and military dive bar, but our doors are wide open to everyone 21+ who appreciates good company and good times.

Our philosophy is simple: create a welcoming space with lovely staff and patrons, where the beer is cold and inexpensive, and the music sets the perfect mood. Come for a drink, stay for the camaraderie, and become part of the Cooper's Tavern family.
