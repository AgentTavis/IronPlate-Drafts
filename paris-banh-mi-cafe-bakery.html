Our Heritage

A Taste of Paris, Crafted with Heart in Jacksonville.

At Paris Banh Mi Cafe & Bakery, we bring the vibrant, authentic flavors of Vietnam and the comforting aroma of a classic bakery to the heart of Jacksonville, NC. Our journey began with a simple passion: to share the rich culinary traditions of Vietnamese banh mi sandwiches and freshly baked goods with our community.

Every banh mi is a carefully crafted masterpiece, featuring crisp baguettes, savory meats, fresh herbs, and homemade pickled vegetables. Our bakery delights, from flaky croissants to sweet pastries, are baked fresh daily, promising a moment of joy with every bite. We're more than just a diner; we're a place where friends and family gather, where every meal is made with love, and every visit feels like coming home.
