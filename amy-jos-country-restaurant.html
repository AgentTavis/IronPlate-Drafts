Our Heritage

Savoring Generations of Comfort in Wilson

At Amy Jo's Country Restaurant, we believe in the simple joys of good food and warm company. Nestled in the heart of Wilson, NC, we've been a cherished local diner for decades, serving up classic American and Southern comfort dishes that feel like a hug from grandma.

Every morning, our kitchen comes alive with the aroma of freshly brewed coffee, sizzling bacon, and fluffy pancakes. We pride ourselves on using quality ingredients, prepared with love and time-honored recipes that have delighted families across generations. From our hearty breakfasts to our satisfying lunches, we craft each meal to bring a smile to your face.

More than just a place to eat, Amy Jo's is a community hub where friendships are forged over a hot meal, and memories are made. Come on down, pull up a chair, and experience the genuine warmth and delicious flavors that make us a true Wilson favorite.
