Our Roots

Where Every Sip Tells a Story, Brewed with Passion in Monroe.

What an amazing addition to downtown Monroe NC! Local Logic, brought to you by the visionary owners of Southern Range Brewery, the original downtown brewery, has branched out to create a truly luxurious libation experience for our community.

Open Wednesdays through Saturdays, we offer a diverse and expertly curated selection of alcoholic beverages. Indulge in our unique smoked whisky options – a true signature of our craft. Whether you're looking for the perfect starting point for your evening or a sophisticated nightcap, Local Logic provides an ambiance and selection unmatched in Monroe.

We're particularly proud of our monthly drink specials. Each month, our talented bar staff crafts 2-3 non-menu drinks that are always extra fabulous and sure to delight your palate. It’s our way of keeping things fresh and giving you new reasons to explore.

"Local Logic is my absolute favorite spot in Monroe. The smoked whisky options are a game-changer, and their monthly specials never disappoint. Truly a hidden gem!"

— A Happy Regular, Monroe NC
