Our Roots

More Than Just Coffee: A Lincolnton Gathering Place

At Fausto Coffee, we believe in bringing an abundance of flavors and warmth to our community in Lincolnton. From the moment you walk in, you're greeted by our extremely friendly staff, ready to guide you through an exciting journey of taste.

We know that with so many coffee flavor combinations to choose from, it can be overwhelming – that's why we proudly display our whiteboard with suggested pairings, some daring, others comforting, but all designed to inspire you to try something new!

"If coffee wasn't enough, this shop has various sweets to try, original stickers, succulents, and various other cute items to choose from!"
— A Happy Customer

Fausto Coffee is more than just a stop for your daily caffeine fix; it's a destination where you can discover unique treats, find the perfect gift, or simply relax. We're proud to be a vibrant part of Lincolnton, serving up smiles, delicious sips, and a little bit of magic every day.
