Our Story

Generations of Flavor, Crafted with Care.

At Alex's Grill and Ice Cream, we believe in serving more than just food; we serve memories. Since opening our doors in Lewisville, we've been dedicated to bringing you the best in American (Traditional) cuisine, from our expertly grilled sandwiches to our refreshing ice cream.

Our kitchen is a place of passion, where every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and a touch of homegrown love. It’s no secret that our grilled chicken sandwich has a loyal following, and our fries? They’re legendary. We pour our hearts into every meal, ensuring that from the first bite to the last, Alex's never disappoints.
