Our Journey

More Than Just Food, It's a Community

We are excited to share our dream of opening our community cafe in our beloved small town! Our vision is to create a warm and inviting space where people can gather, connect, and enjoy delicious food and drinks. We believe that a cafe is more than just a place to grab a cup of coffee; it’s a hub for community engagement, creativity, and local culture.

Hangry Howies is built on the simple idea that good food, friendly faces, and a welcoming atmosphere can bring people together. Every dish is prepared with care, using fresh ingredients to give you that classic diner taste you love. Come on in, pull up a chair, and become part of our growing family here in Alta.

Hearty Breakfast Platter

Start your day right with a classic spread of eggs, bacon, sausage, hash browns, and your choice of toast or pancakes.

Signature Howie Burger

Our juicy, perfectly seasoned beef patty, fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, and our special sauce, all on a toasted brioche bun.

Classic Grilled Cheese

Melted American cheese grilled to golden perfection on your choice of white, wheat, or rye bread. A timeless comfort.

Chicken Club Sandwich
