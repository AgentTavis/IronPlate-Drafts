Our Legacy

Where Great Food & Good Times Meet

Tumbleweeds Steak and Tavern has been a beloved fixture in Lenoir, NC, serving up classic Bar and Grill favorites with a Southwestern-American twist. We believe in crafting dishes that feel like home, using quality ingredients to bring you flavors you'll crave.

More than just a restaurant, we're a community hub where friends and family gather, share laughs, and make lasting memories. Our warm and welcoming atmosphere, combined with our commitment to exceptional service, makes every visit to Tumbleweeds an experience to remember.
