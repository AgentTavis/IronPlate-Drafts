Our Story

Where Good Company & Great Food Meet

At Tumbleweeds Steak & Tavern, we’re more than just a place to eat – we're a cornerstone of the Lenoir community. For years, we’ve been serving up hearty Southwestern-American flavors in a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

From our sizzling steaks to our comforting Tavern Pasta, every dish is crafted with care, ensuring a memorable experience for friends, families, and solo diners alike. Come on down and taste why we're a local favorite, recognized for our genuine hospitality and delicious food.
