Our Journey

More Than Just a Bar, It's a Community Hub

Located in the vibrant Shops at Lakeside in Flower Mound, CLINK Wine Bar + Bites is more than just a destination; it's a haven for food and wine lovers alike. We envisioned a place where friends could gather, families could connect, and every visit felt like coming home.

Our passion for exceptional wines and delightful culinary pairings drives everything we do. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll feel the warmth of our welcoming atmosphere, designed to encourage relaxation and memorable conversations. We believe in crafting experiences, one sip and one bite at a time.

"Clink is my go-to spot in Flower Mound. The ambiance is perfect for unwinding, and their selection always has something new and exciting to discover!"

— Sarah J., Local Enthusiast
