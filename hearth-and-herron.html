Our Journey

Crafting Culinary Excellence in Hendersonville

Welcome to Hearth and Herron, the culmination of Chef Bradley Herron's lifelong passion for exceptional food and heartfelt hospitality. Located on 7th Ave E in charming Hendersonville, our restaurant is a celebration of rustic technique refined for the modern palate.

Chef Herron meticulously sources the finest ingredients, transforming them into dishes that tell a story of tradition, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to flavor. Every plate is a reflection of his dedication to creating a truly memorable fine dining experience for our cherished guests.
