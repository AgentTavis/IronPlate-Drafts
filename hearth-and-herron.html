Our Heritage

Crafting Culinary Memories in the Heart of Hendersonville

Welcome to Hearth and Herron, a culinary dream brought to life by Chef Bradley Herron. Nestled in the vibrant heart of Hendersonville, our restaurant is a celebration of the rustic traditions he grew up with, meticulously refined through years of classical training and a passion for modern gastronomy.

Every dish served here tells a story – a narrative of the rich Appalachian landscape, the freshest seasonal ingredients, and the dedication of a chef who believes dining should be an immersive art. From the crackling hearth to the meticulously plated creations, Hearth and Herron is more than just a meal; it's an invitation to experience fine dining that feels both elevated and warmly familiar.

Join us at 337 7th Ave E for an evening where exquisite flavors meet heartfelt hospitality. We're proud to be Hendersonville's newest culinary gem, and we look forward to sharing our passion with you.
