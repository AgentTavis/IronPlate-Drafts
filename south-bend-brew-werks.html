Our Legacy

From Historic Walls to Heartfelt Flavors.

Located caddy-corner from Four Winds Field, we’re Downtown South Bend’s only family-owned and operated brewpub. Since our humble beginnings, we’ve proudly served up house-brewed beer, handcrafted food, and a deep commitment to giving back to the community that has welcomed us.

In 2021, we embarked on an exciting new chapter, moving into the historic Hibberd Building. This move allowed us to create an even more expansive and inviting space, where locals and visitors alike can gather, connect, and enjoy great flavors in a setting rich with history and comfort.

Every pint, every plate, and every smiling face here tells a story of passion, dedication, and the unique spirit of South Bend. We're more than just a place to eat and drink; we're a hub where memories are made and community thrives.
