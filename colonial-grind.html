Our Heritage

Crafting Community, One Cup at a Time.

Colonial Grind isn't just a coffee shop; it's a home away from home for the Thomasville community. From our humble beginnings, we set out to create a place where exceptional quality meets genuine hospitality. We believe in the power of a perfect cup of coffee, a refreshing smoothie, or a delightful scoop of ice cream to brighten your day.

Our passion for flavor led us to source the finest ingredients and develop a huge selection of custom coffee creations and non-coffee drinks. But what truly sets us apart is our incredible team. Misty and all our employees embody the gift of hospitality, making every visitor feel like family. We're proud to be a locally owned business, deeply rooted in serving Thomasville with a smile.
