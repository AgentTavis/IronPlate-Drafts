Our Heritage

More Than Just a Tavern, It's Home.

Graff's Tavern, nestled in the heart of Franklin, NC, beckons with its warm and welcoming ambiance. Step into a haven where Chef-inspired delicacies await, crafted with passion and served with a smile. For generations, we've been the gathering place for families, friends, and sports enthusiasts alike, building a legacy rooted in community, delicious food, and unforgettable memories.

We pride ourselves on being Franklin's go-to spot for an incredible meal and a lively atmosphere. Whether you're catching the big game, enjoying a family dinner, or unwinding after a long day, Graff's Tavern offers the perfect blend of comfort, quality, and hospitality that keeps our guests coming back for more.
