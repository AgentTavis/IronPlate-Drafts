Our Journey

More Than Just a Tavern, It's a Community Hub.

Graff's Tavern, nestled in the heart of beautiful Franklin, NC, isn't just a place to eat; it's a warm, welcoming extension of your own home. We've crafted a haven where every visit feels like coming back to family, whether you're catching the big game or sharing a meal with loved ones.

Our passion lies in serving up Chef-inspired delicacies that elevate classic bar & grill fare. From our legendary wings to our juicy steakburgers, every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and a whole lot of love. We believe in good food, great company, and making memories around the table.
