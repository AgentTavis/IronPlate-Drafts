Our Story

Serving Madison's Best Pizza for Over 18 Years

At Tianos, we believe in simple, honest, and incredibly delicious pizza. Nestled in the heart of Madison, NC, we've been a local cornerstone for families and friends gathering over our fresh, hand-tossed pies since 2005.

Our commitment is to quality ingredients and time-honored recipes that bring out the true taste of authentic pizza. Whether you're craving a classic pepperoni, a hearty combo, or a simple cheese slice, every bite is crafted with passion.

We're proud to offer unbeatable value, like our popular deals: Two slices of pizza with one topping and a drink for just $7.50 + Tax, or a refreshing One slice of cheese pizza, a fresh salad & a drink for $9.00 + Tax. We're more than just a pizza place; we're a community hub where great food and good times come together.
