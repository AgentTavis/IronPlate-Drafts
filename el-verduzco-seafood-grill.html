Our Journey

Taste the Tradition, Feel the Family Warmth.

When you step into El Verduzco | Seafood & Grill, you're not just a customer; you're family. We bring the vibrant, authentic flavors of Jalisco straight to Dixon, CA. What started as a local discovery for many has quickly become a cherished spot for delicious, homemade meals that remind you of home.

Our commitment is simple: excellent, delicious, and always homemade food. From our rich enchiladas to our sizzling grills, every dish is prepared with passion and a dedication to traditional Mexican and seafood recipes. We love seeing our regulars and welcoming new faces, eager to share a taste of our heritage.

"I was first hesitant, but after calling and seeing the crowd, I knew this place was special. The food is excellent and delicious and Homemade! The enchiladas were the best I've ever had. I'm definitely going back with my wife!"
