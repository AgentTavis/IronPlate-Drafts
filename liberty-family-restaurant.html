Our Story

Where Every Meal Feels Like Home

Liberty Family Restaurant has been a beloved cornerstone of Lewisville for years, bustling with locals who crave genuine, old-school comfort food. We pride ourselves on serving up hearty, home-cooked meals that bring back cherished memories.

From our famous Spring specials like Home Cooked Ham and Lasagna to everyday favorites like our delicious Country Ham and Chili Dogs, every dish is prepared with care and a touch of Southern hospitality. Come experience why we're Lewisville's top spot for a comforting meal.
