Our Story

Where Gastonia Gathers for Authentic Flavors & Good Times

Nestled in the heart of Gastonia, Double Barrel Saloon isn't just another bar—it's a vibrant gathering place built on a passion for good food, great drinks, and genuine hospitality. We set out to create a truly unique experience, a welcoming haven where friends and family can unwind, share laughter, and make lasting memories.

Our journey began with a simple idea: combine the laid-back, friendly atmosphere of a classic saloon with the bold, exciting flavors of authentic Mexican cuisine. From our hand-shaken margaritas to our savory street tacos, every item on our menu is crafted with care, using fresh ingredients to deliver an unforgettable taste of Mexico right here in North Carolina.
