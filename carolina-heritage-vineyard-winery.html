Our Heritage

Crafting Memories, One Sip at a Time.

We can’t wait to welcome you to Carolina Heritage Vineyard & Winery. Our passion began with a simple dream: to cultivate exceptional organic wines and create a welcoming haven for all.

Whether you’re sipping a glass of our organic wine or enjoying a local craft beer, you’ll find the perfect place to relax—whether inside our solar-powered tasting room, rocking on the porch, or settling into a scenic spot across the vineyard with views of the Blue Ridge Mountains.

Taste Our Offerings

Discover Your Next Favorite Sip

Our culinary and beverage selections are crafted to perfectly complement your visit. While we don't have a fixed online menu, we invite you to explore our seasonal offerings:

Organic Estate Wines

Savor our award-winning wines, meticulously grown and crafted right here in Elkin. From crisp whites to robust reds, each bottle tells a story of our unique terroir.

Local Craft Beers

Beyond our wines, we proudly feature a rotating selection of the finest craft beers from local North Carolina breweries. A refreshing choice for every palate.

Artisanal Cheese Boards

Perfectly paired with our wines, enjoy a selection of gourmet cheeses, cured meats, fresh fruits, and artisanal crackers. Ideal for sharing.

Seasonal Small Plates

Our kitchen offers a curated selection of seasonal small plates, designed to enhance your tasting experience with fresh, local ingredients.

Sweet Delights

Indulge in our selection of homemade desserts and sweet treats, the perfect finish to an afternoon spent with us.

Non-Alcoholic Refreshments

For those seeking alternatives, we offer a variety of refreshing non-alcoholic beverages, including sparkling ciders and artisan sodas.
