Our Story

Crafting Delicious Moments, One Order at a Time

At Eddie's Carryout, we've been proudly serving the Fort Washington community for years, building a reputation for delicious, no-fuss meals made with care. We believe in good, honest food – from our classic pizzas piled high with fresh toppings to our savory Steak and Cheese subs, and even our refreshing milkshakes. We’re your local spot for a quick bite, a satisfying dinner, or that essential late-night craving.
