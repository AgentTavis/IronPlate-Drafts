Our Roots

Taste the Soul of Edenton: Freshness Rooted in Tradition

At Downtown Roots, we believe in bringing you more than just a meal – we offer a taste of the Edentonian lifestyle. Nestled in the heart of Edenton, we've become a beloved local spot renowned for our hearty soups and incredible sandwiches. Our regulars often find themselves coming back for more, drawn in by the comfort of our Crab and Potato soups, and the unforgettable flavor of our fan-favorite Goat Cheese Grilled Cheese.

We craft each dish with the freshest ingredients, packed full of flavor, ensuring every visit is a delightful experience. While our focus is on exceptional food that takes time to prepare with care, we ensure a warm and welcoming atmosphere where you can relax and savor every bite. Call ahead or order at the counter – good food is worth the wait!
