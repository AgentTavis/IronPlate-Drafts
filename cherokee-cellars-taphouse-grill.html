Our Journey

Where Good Times & Great Flavors Meet

Cherokee Cellars Taphouse & Grill is more than just a place to eat; it's a vibrant hub in Murphy, NC. We're proud to be a fun, welcoming venue for live music, bringing energy and entertainment to our community.

Our friendly team, led by owner Jim, ensures every visit feels like coming home. We believe in great service, good company, and a menu that genuinely stands out from the typical bar fare.

From the moment we opened our doors, our mission has been to create a space where memories are made, friends gather, and every dish is crafted with care.
