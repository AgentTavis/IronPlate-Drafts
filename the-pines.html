Our Roots

Crafted with Care, Quality, and Purpose.

At The Pines, we believe good food starts with good intentions. Born from a passion for bringing people together, our community-rooted café has become a cherished spot in Greensboro, NC. We're dedicated to serving fresh breakfast, lunch, and convenient digital ordering, all made with the highest quality ingredients.

Every dish at The Pines is a testament to our commitment to flavor and community. We carefully source our ingredients, support local producers whenever possible, and put heart into every meal. We're more than just a place to eat; we're a place to connect, to relax, and to enjoy food crafted with genuine care.
