Our Story

The Heart of Lumberton's Culinary Scene

Located in the heart of Lumberton, NC, The Potato Barn is more than just a restaurant – it's a quintessential American experience exuding warmth and charm. We believe in good food, great company, and creating memories around the table.

For years, we've been serving up hearty meals and a welcoming atmosphere, becoming a beloved spot for locals and visitors alike. Our commitment to quality ingredients and genuine hospitality is what makes every visit to The Potato Barn a special one.
