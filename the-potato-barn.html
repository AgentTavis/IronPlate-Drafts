Our Roots

Taste the Tradition, Feel the Charm

Located in the heart of Lumberton, NC, The Potato Barn is a quintessential American restaurant that exudes warmth and charm. We believe in good food, great company, and the kind of hospitality that makes you feel right at home.

Since opening our doors, we've been dedicated to serving up classic bar and grill fare with a unique twist, always using fresh ingredients and a whole lot of heart. Our mission is simple: create memorable dining experiences that keep our community coming back for more.

From our signature "Bomb Potato" that's become a local legend, to our hearty grill classics, every dish is prepared with passion. Come on down, pull up a chair, and discover why The Potato Barn is more than just a restaurant – it's a piece of Lumberton's soul.

Our Culinary Delights

From Our Grill to Your Table

The Bomb Potato

Our legendary baked potato loaded with your choice of savory meats, melted cheese, sour cream, and chives. A must-try!

Classic Barn Burger

Juicy, grilled-to-perfection beef patty with crisp lettuce, ripe tomato, onion, and pickles on a toasted bun.

Southern Fried Chicken

Crispy, golden-fried chicken, seasoned with our secret blend of spices. A true taste of Southern comfort.

Smoked BBQ Rib Plate

Slow-smoked, fall-off-the-bone ribs smothered in our tangy, homemade BBQ sauce. Served with classic sides.

Crispy Onion Rings

Hand-battered and fried to golden perfection. The ideal crunchy accompaniment to any meal.

Homestyle Mac & Cheese

Creamy, cheesy, and utterly delicious. A comforting side dish that tastes just like grandma used to make.
