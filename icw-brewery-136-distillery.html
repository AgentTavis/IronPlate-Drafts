Our Journey

A One-Man Passion for Craft Excellence

At ICW Brewery & 136 Distillery, it’s truly a one-man show, and that man is Bryan Bennet. With unwavering dedication, Bryan personally brews every beer, distills every spirit, manages the taproom, and ensures every corner of the establishment is clean and inviting. This hands-on approach guarantees a consistent quality and a personal touch you won't find anywhere else.

From the moment you step in, you're handed a plastic cup and a debit card, inviting you to explore. Our innovative self-pour taps on the wall allow you to try a little of everything, keeping track of your bill as you go. It’s an experience designed for discovery, where every pour is an adventure.
