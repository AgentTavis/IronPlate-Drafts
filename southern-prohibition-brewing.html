Our Heritage

Brewing Community Since Day One

Southern Prohibition Brewing is more than just a bar and grill; it's a beloved local spot in Hattiesburg, NC, where friends gather and memories are made. Our journey began with a simple passion: to craft exceptional brews and pair them with delicious, hearty food that truly satisfies.

From our humble beginnings, we've grown into a community cornerstone, known for our welcoming atmosphere, our commitment to quality, and our dedication to sourcing the best local ingredients. Every dish, every pour, tells a story of craftsmanship and a love for good times shared.

We believe in the power of a great meal and a perfectly brewed beer to bring people together. Come experience the Southern Prohibition difference – where every visit feels like coming home.

Our Flavor

Taste The Prohibition Difference

The Prohibition Burger

Our signature blend patty, aged cheddar, crispy bacon, house sauce, and fresh veggies on a brioche bun. A true classic!

Craft Brewed Tacos

Choose from slow-cooked pulled pork, spicy chicken, or black bean, topped with fresh pico and our special crema.

Crispy Nashville Hot Chicken

Buttermilk fried chicken, Nashville hot spice, pickles, and creamy slaw served on Texas toast. Handle with care!

Loaded Chili Cheese Fries

Golden crispy fries piled high with homemade chili, melted cheese, jalapeños, and a dollop of sour cream.

Artisan Flatbreads

Freshly baked flatbread with various toppings, from classic Margherita to our unique BBQ chicken.

Garden Fresh Salad Bowls
