Our Story

Taste the Comfort of Home-Cooked Meals

At Wake Up & Eat, we believe that great food doesn't need to be complicated. It just needs to be delicious, fresh, and served with a smile. Nestled in the heart of Harrisburg, NC, we've built a reputation for serving hearty breakfast and lunch classics that taste just like grandma used to make.

We're proud to be a local diner where everyone feels welcome. Whether it's a bustling Sunday morning or a relaxed weekday lunch, our commitment to quality ingredients, friendly service, and unbeatable value shines through. We're the perfect spot for families, friends, and solo diners looking for that comforting, familiar flavor.

"I ordered to-go breakfast from this place on a rainy Sunday and I was pleased with how good the food was. Everything was wrapped and still warm by the time I made it home and it was all under $40. Overall, the food tastes like a home-cooked breakfast – not a lot of razzle dazzle to it, but the affordability and good service makes this place a true local gem."
