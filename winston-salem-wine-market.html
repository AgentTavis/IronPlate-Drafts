Our Heritage

Where Every Sip Tells a Story

Winston-Salem Wine Market is more than just a place to grab a drink; it's a beloved local spot in Winston-Salem, NC, where community gathers. We pride ourselves on offering an exquisite selection of wines, perfectly paired with our crafted bar and grill bites.

Our cozy atmosphere and friendly staff create an inviting space for every visit, whether you're here for a casual evening out, a special celebration, or simply to unwind after a long day. Experience the true spirit of local hospitality and discover your new favorite vintage.
