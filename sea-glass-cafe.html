Our Story

A Taste of Home, A New Bern Tradition.

For over 17 years, Sea Glass Cafe has been the heart of New Bern for locals and visitors alike. Established in 2007, we pride ourselves on serving up hearty, delicious breakfast, brunch, and lunch classics made with fresh, quality ingredients.

From our signature Benedicts to our fluffy pancakes and savory breakfast tacos, every dish is crafted with love and a commitment to flavor. We're more than just a diner; we're a place where friends and family gather, where the coffee is always hot, and the welcome is always warm. Come experience the comfort and charm that has made us a New Bern staple for nearly two decades.

"Sea Glass Cafe is truly a gem! The atmosphere is cozy, the food is consistently amazing, and the staff make you feel like family. It's our go-to spot every weekend for breakfast."

— A Loyal Customer, New Bern Resident
