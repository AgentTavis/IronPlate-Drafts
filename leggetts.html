Our Heritage

Generations of Flavor, Rooted in Windsor.

For generations, Leggett's has been the warm heart of Windsor, NC, serving up classic American diner fare with a smile. From our hearty breakfasts to our satisfying lunches, we're dedicated to bringing you delicious, homemade meals that remind you of simpler times.

With a stellar 4.9-star average from our wonderful community, we pride ourselves on fresh ingredients, friendly service, and a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels like family. Come on down and taste why we're Windsor's favorite spot for comfort food!
