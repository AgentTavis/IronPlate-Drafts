Our Journey

Celebrating Generations of Italian Heritage in Monroe, NC

Napoli Italian Restaurant first opened its doors at the Monroe Mall location over three decades ago, bringing the vibrant flavors and heartfelt hospitality of Italy to our beloved community.

Today, 31 years later, we are still going strong, a testament to the unwavering support of our cherished customers and our commitment to authentic Italian cuisine. From fresh pasta made daily to time-honored family recipes passed down through generations, every dish at Napoli tells a story of tradition, passion, and flavor.

We pride ourselves on being more than just a restaurant; we're a place where families gather, friends connect, and memories are made around a table filled with delicious food and warm laughter. Thank you for being a part of our incredible journey.

Our Signature Italian Dishes

Spaghetti Carbonara

A Roman classic with creamy egg, Pecorino Romano, crispy pancetta, and black pepper. Simple perfection.

Lasagna al Forno

Layers of rich Bolognese, béchamel sauce, fresh pasta, and mozzarella baked to golden perfection.

Pizza Margherita

Our traditional Neapolitan pizza with San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, basil, and olive oil.

Fettuccine Alfredo

Rich, creamy Alfredo sauce made with butter, Parmesan cheese, and fresh fettuccine pasta.

Chicken Parmigiana

Breaded chicken breast topped with melted mozzarella and our homemade marinara sauce, served with pasta.

Tiramisu

Classic Italian dessert with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cream, and cocoa powder.
