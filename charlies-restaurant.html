Our Heritage

Generations of Flavor, Rooted in Bayboro

Welcome to Charlie's Restaurant, a true Bayboro gem where Southern hospitality meets the freshest flavors of Pamlico County. For years, we've been dedicated to serving our community hearty, homemade meals that remind you of grandma's kitchen.

Our journey began with a simple passion for good food and gathering folks around a table. From our succulent Fried Flounder to our juicy Shrimp Burgers, every dish is prepared with care, using local ingredients whenever possible to bring you an authentic taste of the coast.

We believe dining out should be an experience – a chance to connect, share laughs, and create memories. That's why we've cultivated a warm, inviting atmosphere perfect for families, friends, and solo diners alike. Come on in and become a part of our story.
