Our Heritage

Savoring Tradition: Dove's, A Bladenboro Staple.

At Dove's Barbecue & Grill, we believe in good food, friendly faces, and the rich tradition of Southern hospitality. Tucked away on North Main Street, we've proudly served the Bladenboro community as a beloved drive-thru spot, making it easy to grab a taste of home, even on your busiest days.

What started as a simple dream has grown into a local favorite, known for our dedication to quality and our welcoming staff. We pour our heart into every dish, ensuring that each visit brings you a comforting, delicious experience. It's more than just a meal; it's a part of Bladenboro.

Our Flavors

The Taste of Authentic Southern BBQ.

Slow-Smoked Perfection

Experience barbecue cooked low and slow to achieve maximum tenderness and a rich, smoky flavor that defines true Southern BBQ.

Signature Sauces

Our unique sauces are crafted to complement our meats perfectly, ranging from tangy vinegar-based to sweet and smoky. A flavor for every palate!

Hearty Sides

No BBQ meal is complete without classic Southern sides. From creamy coleslaw to baked beans, our sides are the perfect accompaniment.

Freshly Prepared

Every dish is prepared with care, using quality ingredients to bring you the freshest and most authentic flavors possible.

Family Recipes

We pride ourselves on using time-honored family recipes passed down through generations, ensuring a taste of tradition in every bite.

Sweet Tea & More

Cool down with our refreshing sweet tea or choose from other cold beverages that pair perfectly with our savory BBQ.
