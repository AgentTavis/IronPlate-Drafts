Our Story

A Taste of the Coast, Crafted for Community.

At Brewery, we believe great food and exceptional beer are best enjoyed with friends and family. Nestled in the heart of Salem, NC, our journey began with a simple passion: to bring the freshest seafood flavors to the Piedmont Triad, perfectly paired with a rotating selection of the finest craft beers.

We're more than just a restaurant; we're a friendly taproom, a vibrant live music venue, and a gathering spot for the North Carolina community. From Winston-Salem to Greensboro, we've poured our heart into creating an atmosphere where every visit feels like coming home. Join us to experience our commitment to quality, community, and unforgettable moments.

Our Offerings

Our Culinary Craftsmanship

Fresh Catches & Local Brews – A symphony of flavors awaiting your discovery.

Classic Fried Shrimp Basket

Crispy, golden-fried shrimp served with our house-made cocktail sauce and a side of seasoned fries.

Pan-Seared Scallops

Delicate sea scallops perfectly seared, finished with lemon-herb butter and a hint of garlic.

Crispy Fish Tacos

Flaky white fish, lightly battered and fried, nestled in warm tortillas with fresh slaw and avocado crema.

Oysters on the Half Shell

A rotating selection of fresh, briny oysters, served chilled with mignonette and hot sauce.

Grilled Salmon with Asparagus

Perfectly grilled Atlantic salmon, seasoned to perfection, accompanied by tender roasted asparagus.

Seafood Gumbo (Chef's Special)

A rich, hearty gumbo brimming with shrimp, crab, andouille sausage, and robust Cajun spices.

Lobster Roll (Weekend Feature)

Sweet lobster meat lightly dressed in mayo, served in a buttered, toasted brioche bun. Available weekends!

New England Clam Chowder

A creamy, comforting classic packed with tender clams, potatoes, and a touch of smoky bacon.

Craft Beer Cheese Dip

Warm, gooey cheese dip infused with local craft beer, served with soft pretzel bites for dipping.

Why Brewery is Your Next Favorite Spot

Unbeatable Craft Beer Selection

Discover your new favorite brew from our constantly rotating tap list, featuring the best of North Carolina's craft beer scene and beyond. Our friendly taproom always has something unique to sip on.

Fresh, Flavorful Seafood

Indulge in an exquisite menu of fresh seafood, prepared with culinary precision. From succulent scallops to crispy fish tacos, every dish celebrates the ocean's bounty with vibrant flavors.
