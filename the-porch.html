Our Heritage

Crafting Community, One Meal at a Time.

Nestled in the heart of Morehead City, The Porch is more than just a bar and grill – it's a community gathering spot. We pride ourselves on serving up classic comfort food with a fresh twist, crafted with quality ingredients and a whole lot of heart.

Whether you're stopping by for a hearty breakfast, a satisfying lunch, or just to unwind with friends, you'll find a warm welcome and flavors that feel like home. Come experience why we're a local favorite!

Menu Highlights

Crispy Chicken Sandwich

Fried to perfection, served with pickles and spicy mayo on a soft roll.

Loaded Nachos

Tortilla chips piled high with seasoned beef, melted cheese, jalapeños, sour cream, and salsa.

Southern Fried Shrimp

Our Promise

Experience The Porch Difference.

Local Flavor, Fresh Ingredients

We source the best local produce and meats to craft dishes like our Classic Porch Burger, ensuring every bite is bursting with freshness and supporting our community.

Unbeatable Breakfast & Lunch

From our Hearty Breakfast Platter to our satisfying Crispy Chicken Sandwich, we're serving up delicious meals from 6:30 AM to 2:00 PM, perfect for any craving.

Warm, Welcoming Atmosphere

Our porch is always open for friends and family. Enjoy a relaxed meal, a refreshing Craft Beer, and genuine Southern hospitality that keeps you coming back.
