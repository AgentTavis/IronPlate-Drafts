Our Heritage

A Taste of Home, Crafted with Love

Welcome to Granny's Kitchen, where every dish is made with the warmth and care of a family recipe. We're a little family-owned bar and grill nestled right here in Asheville, a place where locals and visitors alike feel right at home. We believe in good food, good company, and the kind of hospitality that makes you feel like part of our family.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a space where folks could enjoy hearty meals and friendly faces. Owner Ronnie Turner is often found chatting with guests, ensuring everyone leaves with a full belly and a happy heart. It's this personal touch, combined with our dedication to quality, that makes Granny's Kitchen a cherished spot in the community.

"I selected this place after seeing a video review on TikTok. Since visiting we had a lot of great food, and while we'd tried a lot in the same vein, we decided to try something different for our last night here. It was not far to get to from downtown, and it was so worth it. Truly a wonderful family-owned spot."

— Angie M., Loyal Customer
