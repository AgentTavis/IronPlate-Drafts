OUR STORY

Where Good Times Flow

Welcome to Shoals Bar and Grill, a beloved Mooresville outpost where friends and families gather for exceptional food, refreshing drinks, and memorable entertainment. For years, we've been serving up a popular selection of fresh seafood, classic American grub, and an impressive array of draft brews and handcrafted cocktails.

But we're more than just a place to eat; we're a community hub. Our commitment to regular live music creates a vibrant, lively atmosphere that keeps guests coming back week after week. It's the perfect spot to unwind, enjoy great company, and discover your next favorite dish or drink.
