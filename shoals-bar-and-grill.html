OUR STORY

More Than Just a Meal

Shoals Bar and Grill is Mooresville's beloved outpost, renowned for its vibrant atmosphere and commitment to serving delicious food. We're proud to offer a diverse menu featuring fresh, popular seafood, classic American grub, an impressive selection of draft brews, and expertly crafted cocktails.

But it's not just about the food and drinks – our regular live music events ensure every visit is an unforgettable experience, making us a true local favorite for families, friends, and anyone looking for a memorable time.
