Our Heritage

Wadesboro's Home for Community & Craft

Nestled in the heart of Wadesboro, in the historic Old Firestation, Native State Beer Garden is more than just a bar and grill. It’s a cherished gathering spot where friends, families, and neighbors come together over great food, refreshing drinks, and an unbeatable atmosphere. We believe in creating a welcoming space where every visit feels like coming home.

Our commitment to quality, community, and genuine hospitality is the cornerstone of everything we do. From our carefully curated craft beer selection to our delicious grill favorites, we strive to offer an experience that keeps you coming back. Your trust is our priority, and we're proud to be a truly local business, earning every one of our 5-star reviews.

Why We Stand Out

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience

Wadesboro's Best Brews

As a true Beer Garden, we pride ourselves on offering an unparalleled selection of craft beers. From local favorites to rare finds, our taps are always flowing with quality. Our 5-star rating isn't just for food, it's for our incredible beverage selection too!

The Old Firestation Charm

Housed in the historic Old Firestation, our unique ambiance blends rustic charm with modern comfort. It's the perfect backdrop for casual hangouts, special occasions, or just unwinding after a long day. Experience history with every sip and bite.

Community & Connection

We're deeply rooted in the Wadesboro community. Native State Beer Garden is a place where friendships blossom, and memories are made. Our team is dedicated to providing warm, friendly service that makes you feel like family, contributing to our consistent 5-star guest satisfaction.
