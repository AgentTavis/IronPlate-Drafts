Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience

Welcome to Kimmy at the Depot, Etowah's newest gathering spot for delicious food, refreshing drinks, and good times! Nestled conveniently at 97 Etowah Park Rd, we're firing up the grills and chilling the taps for our Grand Opening on September 13th, from 7 PM to 9 PM.

Join us and be among the first to experience the vibrant atmosphere and discover your new local favorite. We're proud to bring a fresh, local Bar & Grill experience to the heart of Etowah. Follow the excitement leading up to the big night and connect with us on social media: @z.carmean.

Our Offerings

Delicious & Fresh. Menu Coming Soon!

While we perfect our initial selection of culinary delights, get ready for a taste of what's to come! Our full menu, featuring Bar & Grill classics and unique twists, will be unveiled shortly after our Grand Opening. Here’s a sneak peek at the categories you can expect:

The Depot Burger

Crafted with premium beef, fresh toppings, and our signature sauce on a toasted bun. A true bar & grill classic, made just for you.

Crispy Wings

Perfectly seasoned, incredibly crispy wings available with a selection of mouth-watering sauces. Ideal for sharing... or not!

Kimmy's Signature Salad

Fresh, vibrant greens topped with seasonal ingredients and a homemade dressing. A lighter option that doesn't compromise on flavor.

Loaded Nachos

A mountain of crispy tortilla chips piled high with savory meats, melted cheese, fresh salsa, and all your favorite fixings.

Craft Beer Selection

A rotating selection of local and regional craft beers on tap, carefully chosen to complement our delicious food. Ask about our current pours!

Specialty Cocktails

Expertly mixed cocktails, from timeless classics to Kimmy's unique creations. The perfect companion for any evening at the Depot.
