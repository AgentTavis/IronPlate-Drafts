Our Journey

The Heart Behind Kimmy at the Depot

Welcome to Your New Favorite Spot!

At Kimmy at the Depot, we believe in good food, good company, and great times. Born from a passion for serving our community, we're thrilled to be Etowah's newest gathering place. Our grand opening on September 13th marked the beginning of a delicious journey, bringing fresh, hearty bar and grill favorites to our neighbors.

Located right here at 97 Etowah Park Rd, we've poured our hearts into creating a warm, inviting atmosphere where everyone feels like family. Whether you're stopping by for a quick bite, a relaxed dinner, or just to unwind, we promise a friendly face and a plate full of flavor. Come on down and experience the genuine hospitality that makes Kimmy at the Depot special.

Our Delicious Offerings

Classic Depot Burger

A juicy, seasoned patty, fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, and our special sauce on a toasted bun.

Crispy Chicken Sandwich

Golden fried chicken breast, pickles, and mayo, served on a soft brioche bun.

Loaded Nachos

Crispy tortilla chips piled high with melted cheese, jalapeños, salsa, sour cream, and your choice of protein.

Spicy Buffalo Wings

Tender wings tossed in our signature buffalo sauce, served with ranch or blue cheese.

Grilled Steak Salad

Fresh greens topped with perfectly grilled steak, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and a zesty vinaigrette.

Fried Pickles

Crispy breaded dill pickle slices, deep-fried to golden perfection, served with a tangy dipping sauce.

Pulled Pork Plate

Slow-cooked, tender pulled pork with a smoky BBQ glaze, served with coleslaw and cornbread.

Fish & Chips

Crispy battered cod served with a generous portion of seasoned fries and tartar sauce.
