OUR STORY

Step Back in Time, Savor the Quality.

Welcome to Sweet Waters Family Restaurant & Oasis Lounge, a place where time slows down, and quality reigns supreme. We've crafted an experience that harkens back to an era of elegance and comfort, with cushy padded chairs, inviting booths, and charming retro decoration. Forget plastic benches – here, every detail is designed for your relaxation and enjoyment.

Our commitment to excellence shines through in every dish. Everything we serve is of good quality and prepared with care. We're renowned for "The Best Bacon in the West!" and a menu filled with heartwarming classics that keep our regulars coming back for more. Our servers are always efficient and cheerful, creating an atmosphere where every customer leaves well-fed and happier. And if you're lucky, you might even be served by "The Coffee Queen!"

"Sweet Waters truly lives up to its name – an oasis of comfort food and friendly faces. The retro vibe is charming, and their Bacon is truly legendary!"

– A Happy Customer, Albany
