Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a La Grange Tradition.

At 2nd Street Cafe-LaGrange, we believe in bringing people together over delicious, heartwarming diner classics. Nestled right in the heart of La Grange, beside Rileys, we've been serving up smiles and satisfaction at 405 E Washington St. for years.

Our passion is simple: to create a space where every meal feels like coming home. We're proud to be a local fixture, cherished by our community for our friendly service and plates piled high with comfort food. From breakfast to dinner, we're here to make your day a little brighter, a little tastier, and a lot more comfortable.
