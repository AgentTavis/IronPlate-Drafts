Our Journey

Crafting Sweet Memories, Without Compromise.

At Sumosa's Cakery, we believe everyone deserves to enjoy a delicious treat, regardless of dietary needs. Nestled in the heart of Conover, NC, we're not just a bakery—we're a community staple known for our warm hospitality and truly accommodating creations.

Our owner, Rachel, is renowned for her friendly approach and commitment to crafting desserts that are as safe as they are delightful. From gluten-free masterpieces to sugar-free delights, vegan treats, and dye-free options, we pour our heart into every custom order. We're proud to be one of the best bakeries in town, ensuring every bite is a moment of pure joy.
