Our Journey

From Concept to Culinary Delight: The Munchies on Main Story

What started with a vision for the perfect sandwich spot in Pilot Mountain has now blossomed into Munchies on Main. We poured our hearts into renovating our space at 115 W Main St, carefully selecting every piece of equipment, all with one goal: to bring you fresh, delicious, all-to-go sandwiches and more.

The excitement has been building, and we couldn't wait to share our creations with the community. We are thrilled to be open and serving up the flavors that have already garnered us a loyal following.
