Our Passion

Crafting Delicious Moments, One Sandwich at a Time

Welcome to Munchies on Main, Pilot Mountain's newest destination for incredible bar and grill flavors! We're thrilled to bring you a fresh take on classic comfort food, focusing on high-quality ingredients and irresistible combinations.

Born from a love for great food and community, Munchies on Main is more than just a sandwich spot – it's where friends gather, families share meals, and every bite is a little celebration. We believe that simple, fresh ingredients make all the difference, and our dedication to flavor shines through in every dish we serve.

We've poured our heart into renovating our space and equipping our kitchen to bring you the best. While our journey is just beginning, we're already hearing fantastic feedback about our mouth-watering Reuben and Mama Mia sandwiches. Come discover your new favorite meal!

The Turkey Burger
