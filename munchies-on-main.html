Our Journey

Crafted with Passion, Served with a Smile.

At Munchies on Main, we're more than just a sandwich spot – we're a dream coming to life right here in Pilot Mountain. We believe that great food brings people together, and our mission is to serve up delicious, fresh, and satisfying meals that make your day a little brighter.

Our kitchen is bustling with excitement, perfecting every recipe and sourcing the freshest ingredients. From hearty sandwiches packed with flavor to classic grill favorites, every dish is made with care and a sprinkle of our local charm. We're building a place where good food and good company are always on the menu, and we can't wait to welcome you.

Our Delicious Offerings

Handcrafted Sandwiches & Grill Favorites

Turkey Burger

Juicy turkey patty, fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, and our special sauce on a toasted bun.

Divine Chicken Sandwich

Grilled or crispy chicken, provolone, bacon, lettuce, tomato, and creamy ranch.

Classic Reuben

Tender corned beef, Swiss cheese, sauerkraut, and Russian dressing on grilled rye bread.

Mama Mia Sandwich

A taste of Italy with pepperoni, salami, ham, provolone, lettuce, tomato, and Italian dressing.

Cheese Sticks

Crispy Onion Rings

Sweet onion rings lightly battered and fried to a perfect golden crisp.

Philly Cheesesteak

Loaded Fries
