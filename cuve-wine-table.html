Our Philosophy

Crafting Unforgettable Moments, One Sip and Plate at a Time

At Cuvée Wine Table, we believe in the art of gathering. Nestled in the heart of Louisville, NC, we've created a warm and inviting space where friends, family, and colleagues can connect over exceptional food and drink.

Our passion for discovery is reflected in our curated selection of 55 wines by the glass, ensuring there's always a new favorite to uncover. Beyond our extensive wine list, we pride ourselves on offering a diverse range of craft beers and a full bar, catering to every palate.

We're more than just a place to eat and drink; we're a community hub. Reserve our Private Dining Room or Tasting Table for your corporate event or private party. We also host engaging wine tastings and seminars, perfect for connoisseurs and curious newcomers alike. Experience the Cuvée difference, where every visit is a celebration.

Taste Our Creativity

Elegant Small Plates & Curated Beverages

Our culinary team crafts a rotating selection of elegant small plates designed to perfectly complement our extensive wine and beverage offerings. Please call or visit for our current seasonal menu.

Artisan Cheese Board

A rotating selection of local and imported cheeses, served with seasonal fruits, preserves, and toasted baguette.

Crispy Prosciutto Wrapped Dates

Sweet Medjool dates stuffed with goat cheese, wrapped in crispy prosciutto, drizzled with balsamic glaze.

Truffle Fries with Aioli

Golden crispy fries tossed with truffle oil and parmesan, served with house-made garlic aioli.

Seared Scallops with Lemon Butter

Perfectly seared sea scallops, served with a delicate lemon-garlic butter sauce and fresh herbs.

Mini Crab Cakes with Remoulade

Lump crab meat cakes, pan-fried to golden perfection, served with a zesty Cajun remoulade.

Spinach & Artichoke Dip

Creamy blend of spinach, artichokes, and three cheeses, baked until bubbly, served with warm pita bread.
