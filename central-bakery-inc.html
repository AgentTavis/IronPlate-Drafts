Our Heritage

Crafting Joy, One Bite at a Time.

For years, Central Bakery Inc. has been a beloved fixture in Asheboro, NC, a place where traditional recipes meet heartfelt passion. We believe in the magic of scratch-made goods, meticulously preparing every cake, cookie, pie, pastry, and brownie with the finest ingredients and a touch of home.

Our commitment shines through in every item, from the light, melt-in-your-mouth Cheese Bites, so addicting you'll need more than a dozen, to our layered Cinnamon Crisp, perfect with a warm cup of coffee. We're more than just a bakery; we're a part of Asheboro's story, dedicated to bringing delicious moments to your table.
