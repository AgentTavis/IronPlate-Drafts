Our Story

More Than Just a Diner, It's Our Community Hub.

Main Street Social is more than just a coffee shop; it's a cherished spot in Stanley, NC where community thrives. By day, our chill ambiance provides the perfect low-key haven for remote workers, students, or anyone seeking a quiet moment. It's the ideal place to sit, relax, and get some work done on your computer.

As the sun sets, our outdoor space comes alive, often hosting local food trucks that draw a vibrant crowd, creating a lively social scene. While our fresh roasted coffee gets the job done perfectly, many come for our fantastic refreshers. And for those looking to celebrate, our delicious mimosas – especially the strawberry lemonade and caramel apple flavors – are an absolute must-try! We're proud to be a place where both relaxation and connection flourish, welcoming everyone, including kids and their furry friends on our outdoor patio.

"Main Street Social has truly become the heartbeat of Stanley. It's that rare place where you can find peace or party, depending on the time of day. A testament to genuine community spirit."

— A Valued Local Customer
