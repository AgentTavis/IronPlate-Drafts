More Than Just a Meal, It's Nanny's Cabin

Nanny's Cabin isn't just a bar and grill; it's a piece of Mayodan's heart. For years, we've been the beloved local spot where friends gather, families connect, and good times are always on the menu. We believe in serving up honest, hearty food in an atmosphere that feels like home.

Our commitment is simple: great food, warm hospitality, and a place where everyone feels like family. From sizzling burgers to crisp, cold drinks, every item is crafted with care, ensuring a memorable experience each time you walk through our doors.
