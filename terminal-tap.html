Our Story

More Than Just A Bar – It’s Your Neighborhood Hangout.

Terminal Tap is a locally owned and operated establishment, built by two passionate individuals who wanted to create a vibrant space for the Greensboro community. We’re more than just a bar and grill; we’re a gathering place where great memories are made.

We pride ourselves on offering a dynamic experience with a full schedule of engaging events. From the competitive fun of music bingo and challenging trivia nights to exciting tap takeovers featuring local and regional brews, we always have something happening. You’ll also find delicious rotating food trucks and electrifying live music throughout the week, ensuring every visit is unique and enjoyable.
