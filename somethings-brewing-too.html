OUR STORY

More Than Just a Coffee Shop, It's Home.

Welcome to Something's Brewing Too, where every cup tells a story and every visit feels like coming home. We're proud to be the heart of Raeford's coffee scene, a place where friends gather, ideas brew, and delicious moments are savored.

As a Veteran and locally owned and operated establishment, community is at the core of everything we do. We believe in supporting local, which is why we proudly serve premium, locally roasted Cactus Creek Coffee Roasters beans. This commitment ensures you get the freshest, most flavorful coffee experience every single time.

From our rich, aromatic coffees to our comforting diner delights, we've carefully crafted a menu that caters to every craving. Join us and discover why our customers consistently rate us as the BEST coffee shop in Raeford!
