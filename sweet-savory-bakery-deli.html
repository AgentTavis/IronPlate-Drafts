Our Story

Crafting Comfort, One Bite at a Time.

Nestled in the heart of West Jefferson, Sweet & Savory Bakery & Deli is more than just a place to eat – it's a cozy haven where heartwarming soups, artisan sandwiches, and delectable desserts come to life. Born from a passion for fresh ingredients and a desire to create a welcoming community space, we invite you to unwind and savor moments that truly matter.

We pride ourselves on offering a diverse menu that perfectly balances classic deli favorites with delightful bakery treats, all made with care and a touch of homegrown charm. From our daily baked goods to our savory lunch specials, every item reflects our commitment to quality and our love for bringing joy through food.

Our Offerings

A Taste for Every Craving.

Hearty Tomato Basil Soup

A comforting classic, slow-simmered with ripe tomatoes and fresh basil. Perfect for a cozy afternoon.

Classic Turkey Club Sandwich

Layers of premium sliced turkey, crispy bacon, fresh lettuce, and tomato on toasted artisan bread.

Artisan Grilled Cheese

A gourmet blend of cheeses melted to perfection on rustic sourdough bread. Pure comfort.

Fresh Garden Salad

Crisp mixed greens, seasonal vegetables, and your choice of dressing. A light and healthy option.

Signature Lemon Bar

Our famously tart and sweet lemon bar, with a buttery shortbread crust and a dusting of powdered sugar.

Rich Chocolate Chip Cookies

Warm, gooey, and packed with chocolate chips. A timeless treat baked fresh daily.

Blueberry Scones

Flaky, tender scones bursting with blueberries, perfect with your morning coffee or afternoon tea.

Daily Quiche Special

Ask about our rotating quiche of the day, featuring fresh, seasonal ingredients.
