Our Story

Generations of Flavor, Right Here in Mocksville.

Established in the heart of Mocksville, The Feed Bag has been a beloved local institution, serving up hearty American (Traditional) breakfast and lunch for years. Nestled at 2339 N Carolina Cir, #801, we pride ourselves on creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where friends and family gather for comforting meals and good times. From our crispy fried fish to our classic breakfast plates, every dish is prepared with the care and tradition you'd expect from your favorite neighborhood diner.

We're more than just a place to eat; we're a cornerstone of the Mocksville community, committed to serving delicious food and creating lasting memories. Come on in and experience the charm and taste that keeps our customers coming back!
