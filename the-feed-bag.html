Our Story

The Heart of Mocksville's Comfort Food Scene

For years, The Feed Bag has been more than just a restaurant; it's a beloved cornerstone in Mocksville, NC, a place where memories are made and appetites are always satisfied. Located conveniently at 2339 NC-801, we pride ourselves on serving up classic American comfort food with a warm, local touch.

From our hearty breakfasts that kickstart your day to our satisfying lunches, we believe in simple, delicious meals prepared with care. We've built a reputation for our welcoming atmosphere, making us the perfect spot for friends, families, and solo diners alike.
