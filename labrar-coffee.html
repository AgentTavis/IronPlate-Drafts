Our Heritage

Crafting Goldsboro's Best Coffee & Community

Welcome to Labrar Coffee, a cozy diner where every cup tells a story. We believe in creating more than just coffee; we're crafting experiences and fostering community in the heart of Goldsboro. From our incredible selection of coffee and espresso, prepared with various meticulous methods, to our delicious sandwiches and small plates, there’s always something to delight your senses.

Our friendly staff is passionate about coffee and dedicated to making your visit exceptional. Whether you're catching up with friends, diving into your work, or simply enjoying a moment of peace, Labrar Coffee offers the perfect ambiance. Step out back and discover our amazing patio – an open and bright oasis, surrounded by historic Goldsboro brick walls. It’s the perfect escape. Come see why our regulars return tomorrow, and you will too!
