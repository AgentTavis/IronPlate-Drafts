Our Story

More Than Just Coffee – A Norwood Gathering Spot.

At Reservoir Coffee, we believe in the simple joys of life: a perfectly brewed cup of coffee, a hearty meal, and the warm embrace of community. Nestled right on N Main St in charming Norwood, NC, our diner has become a beloved stop for locals and visitors alike.

We're proud to be a place where conversations flow as freely as our excellent house coffee. Whether you're starting your day with our delightful breakfast, enjoying a satisfying lunch, or simply grabbing a yummy iced latte to go, you're part of the Reservoir Coffee family.
