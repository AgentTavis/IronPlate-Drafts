Our Story

Where Lake Views & Local Flavors Meet

At The Boat House, we've created more than just a restaurant; we've cultivated a lakeside experience. Nestled on the serene shores of Lake Tillery, we invite you to unwind and indulge. Our philosophy centers on offering a relaxed, fine dining atmosphere where every meal is complemented by breathtaking views and the gentle lapping of the lake.

While our menu features a thoughtful selection of lakeside favorites and classic American fare, one dish stands out as a local legend and a must-try for first-timers: the famous O-M-Cheese Burger, now with even more cheese, available for just $11.99! We pride ourselves on using quality ingredients to craft every dish, ensuring a memorable experience with every bite.

Beyond the food, The Boat House is a community hub. We offer gas for your boat, ensuring your day on the lake is uninterrupted, and host vibrant live music events on weekends. It's the perfect spot to gather with friends and family for delicious food, refreshing drinks, and an unforgettable atmosphere by the water.
