Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

Welcome to Slim's Bar & Grill, a cherished landmark in the heart of Forest City, NC. We believe in crafting memorable moments, one plate at a time. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a welcoming space where friends and family could gather, enjoy solid, delicious food, and exceptional service.

We're proud of our deep roots in the community and the unique historical decor that surrounds you. Every visit to Slim's is a step back into a cozy, friendly atmosphere where every guest feels like family. From our attentive staff to our carefully prepared dishes, we strive to make your experience over the top good.
