Anticipate Our Culinary Delights

While our full, brand-new menu is being meticulously crafted for our Summer 2025 opening, here's a sneak peek at the authentic Italian flavors and gourmet offerings you can expect.

Handmade Pastas

Experience rich, al dente pasta creations, from creamy carbonara to robust ragu, crafted with traditional Italian techniques.

Neapolitan Pizzas

Savor authentic wood-fired pizzas with a perfect crispy crust, fresh mozzarella, and vibrant, locally sourced toppings.

Signature Entrees

Craft Cocktails

Fine Wines

Explore our curated selection of Italian and international wines, chosen to perfectly pair with our culinary masterpieces.
