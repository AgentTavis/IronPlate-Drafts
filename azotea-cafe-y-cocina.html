Our Story

A Family's Dream, A Community's Table.

Welcome to Azotea Cafe y Cocina, a true labor of love born from the passion of the Cantoran family. Led by Benito Cantoran, our diner brings the vibrant, authentic tastes of Latin America right here to Laurinburg, NC.

Every dish is crafted with care, tradition, and a hearty touch of home. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a place where friends and family gather, where good food meets great company, and where every meal feels like a celebration.

We are 100% family owned and operated, pouring our hearts into every plate we serve and striving to create a welcoming atmosphere for our wonderful community.
