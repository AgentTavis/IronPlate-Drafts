Our Journey

A Family Dream, Flavors for Everyone.

Welcome to Azotea Cafe y Cocina, a true family endeavor right here in the heart of Laurinburg, NC. This isn't just a restaurant; it's a dream brought to life by Benito Cantoran, 100% family-owned and operated. We've poured our hearts into creating a warm, inviting diner where delicious Latin-inspired flavors meet classic comfort food, perfect for every family member.

From our specialty grilled cheese that kids adore to authentic Latin dishes that transport you, every meal is prepared with passion and a commitment to quality. Azotea Cafe y Cocina is more than just a place to eat; it's a place to connect, celebrate, and enjoy homemade goodness that feels like home. Come experience the tradition in every bite.

Our Delicious Offerings

Benito's Special Grilled Cheese

Our signature family favorite. Perfectly toasted bread with a blend of cheeses, made with love.

Huevos Rancheros

Classic Azotea Burger

Taco Tuesday Plate

Two delicious tacos, your choice of protein, with fresh salsa, crema, and a side of rice and beans.

Fluffy pancakes infused with sweet plantain, a perfect Latin twist to a diner classic.
