Our Heritage

A Family Legacy of Flavor

For over six decades, Kelly's Seafood has been a cornerstone of the Shelby community, proudly serving up "fried seafood done right" since 1960. We're a family-owned establishment, steeped in tradition and dedicated to bringing you the freshest flavors of the Carolina coast, right here in the heart of North Carolina.

Our unique, efficient ordering system ensures that our loyal locals get their favorite dishes quickly, a testament to our decades of perfected service. For first-timers, our friendly staff are always on hand to guide you through our classic menu board, ensuring you find exactly what you're craving. We believe in big portions and small prices, making delicious, honest seafood accessible to everyone.

Every dish is prepared with care, using time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Come on in and taste the tradition that has made us a Shelby institution.
