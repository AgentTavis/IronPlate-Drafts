Our Story

A Culinary Journey in Selma: Where Fusion Meets Flavor.

Selma Hibachi & Coffee is more than just a restaurant; it's a vibrant blend of culinary traditions right here in Selma, NC. Born from a passion for delicious, unique flavors, we specialize in bringing a captivating Japanese-Mexican fusion experience to your table. We pride ourselves on creating memorable meals and a welcoming atmosphere that keeps our community coming back for more.

Every dish is crafted with fresh ingredients and a dedication to authentic taste, from sizzling hibachi to our unique fusion creations. We believe in good food, great service, and being a cherished part of the Selma community.
