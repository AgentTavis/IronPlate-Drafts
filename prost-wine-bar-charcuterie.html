Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal: An Experience Awaits

Welcome to Prost Wine Bar & Charcuterie, a buzzing contemporary spot nestled in the charming towns of Bay City and Frankenmuth, MI. Our vision was to create a space where discerning palates could explore an exquisite selection of imported and locally sourced charcuterie, artisanal cheeses, and culinary delights.

From classic handcrafted sandwiches to elevated seasonal entrées and creative small plates, every item on our menu is thoughtfully prepared with the freshest ingredients. We believe in pairing exceptional food with exceptional drinks, offering a curated wine list, timeless and inventive cocktails, and premium liquors.

Whether you're planning an intimate date night, a lively gathering with friends, or simply seeking a sophisticated escape, Prost promises an unforgettable experience. Come, raise a glass, and savor the moment with us!
