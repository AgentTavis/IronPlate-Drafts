Our Heritage

From Our Family Pit to Your Plate.

At TW's Pulled Pork BBQ & More, located right here at 1100 NC Highway 37 N in Gates, NC, we're dedicated to serving up authentic, mouth-watering North Carolina BBQ. We believe great food brings people together, and every dish is prepared with passion and a commitment to flavor that tastes just like home. While we're still growing our reputation, our small but mighty team is proud to offer a taste of tradition to our community.

We take pride in our slow-cooked pulled pork and tender pulled turkey, seasoned to perfection and smoked for hours. And for those who love a classic, our chicken tenders are always freshly hand-breaded and fried crispy. Come by and taste the difference that genuine care and authentic recipes make, right here in Gates.
