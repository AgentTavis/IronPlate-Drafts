Our Legacy

From Our Family Table to Yours, Generations of Flavor.

For years, Marks Seafood n' Steak House has been a cherished culinary landmark in Whiteville, NC. Nestled in the heart of our vibrant community, we've dedicated ourselves to crafting unforgettable dining experiences, serving up the freshest catches from the sea and perfectly seared, juicy steaks from the land.

Our commitment goes beyond just great food. It's about a warm welcome, friendly service, and creating a space where families and friends can gather, share laughter, and make lasting memories. We believe in quality ingredients, a passion for cooking, and the kind of hospitality that feels like coming home. That's the Marks promise, and it's what keeps our community coming back, making us more than just a restaurant – we're a part of your family's traditions.
