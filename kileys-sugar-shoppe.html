Our Journey

Taste the Heart of Taylorsville

Welcome to Kiley's Sugar Shoppe, a true local gem in Taylorsville, NC. More than just a Bar And Grill, we're a place where friends and family gather, laughter flows, and every meal is made with a sprinkle of local love. Founded with a passion for good food and community spirit, we've dedicated ourselves to creating a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home.

From our sizzling grills to our refreshing drinks, we believe in quality ingredients and a friendly smile. We're proud to be a part of Taylorsville's vibrant community and cherish every customer who walks through our doors. Come on in, pull up a chair, and let us share our 'sugar' with you!
