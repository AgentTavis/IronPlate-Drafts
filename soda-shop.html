Our Story

More Than Just Drinks, It's a Vibe.

Welcome to Soda shop, Garner's newest destination for delightful drinks and good times! We're thrilled to announce that @sodabox.love has opened a new location right here in Garner, conveniently located off Timber and Hwy 50, next to the Food Lion.

Step into our cool & funky space—it's incredibly inviting, designed for fun, and perfect for a quick stop or a relaxed hang-out. Plus, we feature a super convenient drive-up window for those on the go!

Our menu is extensive and crafted with love. We specialize in Dirty Sodas – "your favorite soda with a twist" – alongside our Refreshers – "sparkling & fresh water drinks" bursting with fruit choices. Looking for a boost? Try our Energy Drinks, which are "organic, healthy, delicious." And for the little ones, there's Lil' Love, complete with a fun toy!

We're constantly innovating, with smoothies coming soon, and always offer sugar-free & diet options, plus a couple of tasty snacks. Stop in anytime to grab yourself an escape in a cup!

— A Happy Local Customer
