Our Heritage

Bringing Authentic Mexican Flavor to Fuquay-Varina

At Mi Cancún Fuquay-Varina, our passion is to share the vibrant and rich culinary traditions of Mexico with our community. We believe in fresh ingredients, time-honored recipes, and a lively atmosphere that transports you straight to the heart of Cancún.

From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be greeted with the aroma of sizzling fajitas and the festive clinking of glasses. Whether you're joining us for a family dinner, a quick lunch, or celebrating with friends, we promise an experience filled with bold flavors and genuine hospitality.

We are proud to offer a diverse menu including vegetarian-friendly and vegan options, ensuring there's something delicious for everyone. Come discover why we're quickly becoming Fuquay-Varina's go-to spot for real Mexican cuisine.

Explore Our Flavors

Our Signature Dishes & Epic Margaritas

Fresh Tacos

Hand-pressed tortillas filled with your choice of savory meats or vibrant vegetarian options, topped with fresh cilantro and onion.

Sizzling Fajitas

Grilled steak, chicken, or shrimp served on a hot skillet with onions and peppers, accompanied by warm tortillas and all the fixings.

Epic Margaritas

Our renowned margaritas, crafted with premium tequila and fresh ingredients. Available on the rocks or frozen, in a variety of fruit flavors.

Burritos Supremos

A generous flour tortilla packed with rice, beans, your choice of meat, cheese, and smothered in our signature sauce.

Enchiladas Verdes

Corn tortillas rolled with shredded chicken or cheese, bathed in a tangy green tomatillo sauce and melted cheese.

Guacamole Fresco

Made fresh to order with ripe avocados, diced onions, cilantro, jalapeños, and a squeeze of lime. Served with crispy tortilla chips.

Quesadillas Rancheras

Grilled flour tortillas filled with Monterey Jack cheese and your choice of protein, served with sour cream and pico de gallo.

Chiles Rellenos

Poblano peppers stuffed with cheese, lightly battered and fried, then covered in a mild ranchera sauce.

Vegan Fiesta Bowl

A vibrant bowl with seasoned plant-based protein, black beans, corn salsa, avocado, and a zesty lime vinaigrette.
