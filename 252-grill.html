Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Hertford Tradition

At 252 Grill, we believe great food starts with fresh, quality ingredients and a whole lot of heart. Our legendary hand-formed hamburger patties are crafted with care, and topped with garden-fresh tomatoes that capture the true taste of Hertford. This isn't just a meal; it's a taste of home, perfected over years of serving our beloved community in the 252 area code.

We're passionate about bringing you "SO many choices," from our sizzling steaks to our comforting classics. Every dish is prepared with dedication, ensuring that unique flavor that keeps you coming back. We're proud to be a local favorite, a place where families and friends gather to create lasting memories over fantastic food.
