Our Heritage

A Taste of Manteo's Heart & Soul

Nestled in the charming streets of Historic Downtown Manteo, Mia's Bake Shop & Tea Room is more than just a diner – it's a cherished local institution. We’re proud to offer a warm, inviting atmosphere where every guest feels like family, whether you're starting your day with a delightful breakfast or gathering friends for a memorable tea party.

Our passion is simple: creating moments of joy through the art of baking and the comfort of a perfectly brewed cup of tea. Featuring Necy's famous baked goods, crafted with love and tradition, our selection promises a taste of homemade goodness that keeps our community coming back for more. Come experience the cozy charm and delicious flavors that make Mia's truly special.
