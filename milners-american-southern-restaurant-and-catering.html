Our Heritage

A Taste of Southern Tradition, Reimagined.

Milner’s American Southern Restaurant is more than just a place to eat; it's a culinary journey deeply rooted in family, tradition, and a passion for exceptional Southern American cuisine.

As a proud family-owned establishment in the heart of Winston-Salem, NC, we cherish the rich heritage of Southern cooking. Our menus are a vibrant tapestry of classic flavors, each dish meticulously re-imagined with contemporary culinary techniques and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Chefs John and Buddy lead our kitchen with a dedication to sourcing the freshest local and regional ingredients. From the farm to your table, every plate is crafted to delight your senses and create a memorable dining experience that celebrates the true essence of the South.
