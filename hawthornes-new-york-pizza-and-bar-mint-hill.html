Our Story

A Taste of Authentic New York

At Hawthorne's New York Pizza and Bar in Mint Hill, we're dedicated to bringing you the genuine flavors of a classic New York pizzeria. We believe in great food, warm service, and a welcoming atmosphere that feels just like home.

Our commitment to quality shines through in every dish, from our piping hot pepperoni pizzas to our crispy wings and satisfying mozzarella sticks. We were thrilled to hear from one first-time customer who said:

"First time trying Hawthorne's and we were very satisfied. Pepperoni pizza, wings, mozzarella sticks and salad was awesome. It arrived about 10 minutes earlier than expected on a Friday night so that was a major plus. We will be trying them out again."
