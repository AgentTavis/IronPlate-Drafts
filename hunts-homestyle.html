Our Legacy

Generations of Homestyle Flavor, Right Here in Elkin.

At Hunt's Homestyle, we believe in bringing the community together over delicious, comforting food. Matty's proudly presents a place where every meal feels like coming home. We're dedicated to serving up wholesome, hearty dishes that capture the essence of a true diner experience, keeping you fueled for your day.

But there's more to Hunt's Homestyle! We're also proud to offer another passion: craft pizzas. These delicious pizzas are made with fresh ingredients, cooked to perfection on your choice of original or thin crust, and with All Toppings No Extra Charge® – because we believe in giving you exactly what you crave without compromise.
