Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Taste of Home.

At Hunt's Homestyle, we believe in bringing the community together around delicious, honest food. Born from Matty's vision right here in Elkin, we've dedicated ourselves to serving up comfort and joy on every plate.

While we're known for our hearty breakfast and lunch specials, we also pride ourselves on our incredible pizzas. These delicious pizzas are made with fresh ingredients, cooked to perfection on your choice of original or thin crust, and with our unique All Toppings No Extra Charge® policy. It's our way of ensuring you always get exactly what you crave, crafted with care.

“Absolutely love the homestyle breakfast here! The atmosphere is so welcoming, and the food always hits the spot. It's our go-to for a perfect start to the day.”
