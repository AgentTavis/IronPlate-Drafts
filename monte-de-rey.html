Our Journey

Authentic Flavors, Family Traditions

Monte De-Rey isn't just a restaurant; it's a celebration of vibrant Mexican culture and cuisine right here in Walkertown. For years, we've poured our heart and soul into crafting dishes that transport you straight to the bustling markets and warm kitchens of Mexico. Our journey began with a simple passion: to share the rich, diverse flavors of our heritage with our beloved community.

Every dish is prepared with the freshest ingredients, time-honored recipes passed down through generations, and a touch of our unique Monte De-Rey flair. From the sizzling fajitas to the perfectly spiced enchiladas, we promise an experience that's both authentic and unforgettable. We believe in good food, good company, and creating memories around the table.

Popular Dishes You'll Love

Burrito California

$9.99

A hearty flour tortilla filled with savory grilled steak, crispy french fries, sour cream, and fresh pico de gallo. A flavor explosion!

Botana De Carnitas

$12.99

Tender, slow-cooked pork carnitas, fried until perfectly crisp, served with warm tortillas, fresh guacamole, and salsa verde.

La Monterrey

A house special featuring a rich blend of grilled chicken, steak, and shrimp, smothered in a creamy cheese sauce, served with rice and beans.

Barbacoa Tacos

Slow-braised, seasoned beef barbacoa served in soft corn tortillas, topped with fresh cilantro and diced onions, with a side of lime.

Beef Taco

Classic crispy or soft taco shell filled with seasoned ground beef, crisp lettuce, fresh tomatoes, and shredded cheese.
