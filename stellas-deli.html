Our Story

From Our Family to Your Table, Every Bite a Memory

Stop by Stella's Deli for a delicious homemade sandwich that truly satisfies. At Stella's Deli, conveniently located inside Jack B' Quick in Valdese, we believe that great food shouldn't be a compromise.

Come experience the difference of a truly homemade meal, served with a friendly smile.
