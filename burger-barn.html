OUR STORY

Rooted in Union Grove Tradition

At Burger Barn in Union Grove, NC, we've dedicated ourselves to crafting an unforgettable dining experience. We specialize in a mouth-watering variety of burgers, from the timeless comfort of our classic cheeseburger to bold, specialty choices like our signature BBQ burger.

Our commitment is simple: serve hearty, home-cooked meals that bring families and friends together. Every dish is prepared with care, ensuring that you taste the passion in every single bite.
