Our Story

More Than Just Burgers, It's a Union Grove Tradition.

At Burger Barn, we believe in the simple joy of a perfectly crafted meal. Nestled in the heart of Union Grove, NC, we've been serving up a legacy of flavor that brings families and friends together.

Specializing in a variety of hearty burgers, from the timeless classic cheeseburger to our smoky specialty BBQ options, every dish is prepared with dedication. We're not just cooking food; we're dishing out home-cooked goodness and memorable experiences, one delicious bite at a time.
