Our Journey

From Vision to Culinary Landmark

In August 2015, Chef Varanese embarked on an exciting new venture, bringing his unparalleled culinary vision to the vibrant community of Louisville. River House Restaurant and Raw Bar, his second acclaimed establishment, was conceived as part of a sprawling 27,000 square foot development located at the picturesque 3015 River Road.

Our restaurant was built on a foundation of passion for the freshest seafood, a commitment to exceptional service, and a desire to create a warm, inviting atmosphere where every meal feels like a special occasion. We believe in sourcing the finest ingredients, crafting each dish with meticulous care, and delivering an unforgettable dining experience that celebrates the bounty of the sea.

From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a customer; you're family. We pride ourselves on being a beloved local spot where memories are made over delectable seafood and heartfelt conversations.

Our Promise

Unrivaled Freshness
