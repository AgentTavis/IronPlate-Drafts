Welcome to Don's

Don's Seafood & Steak House is a beloved culinary cornerstone nestled in the heart of Wilkesboro, North Carolina. For years, we've been serving authentic southern cuisine with hearty portions and genuine hospitality.

From ocean delights to comfort food staples, every dish is crafted with care using the finest ingredients. Whether you're enjoying our famous Calabash Shrimp, perfectly broiled flounder, or a savory steak, you'll taste the difference quality makes.

  • Fresh Seafood Daily
  • Premium Aged Steaks
  • Homemade Sides & Hush Puppies
  • Warm, Welcoming Atmosphere
  • Generous Portions & Fair Prices
  • Attentive, Friendly Service
