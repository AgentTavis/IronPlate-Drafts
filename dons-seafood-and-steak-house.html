Welcome to Don's

Don's Seafood & Steak House is a beloved dining destination in Wilkesboro, NC, known for its authentic southern cuisine and warm hospitality. Since opening, we've been the go-to spot for locals and visitors seeking hearty portions of fresh seafood and premium steaks.

From our signature Calabash Shrimp to our perfectly broiled Flounder Fillet, every dish is crafted with care and served with genuine southern charm. We pride ourselves on generous portions, fair prices, and an atmosphere that feels like home.

Whether you're enjoying our popular salad bar, savoring hush puppies as an appetizer, or indulging in our famous Surf & Turf, you'll experience quality flavors and attentive service that keeps guests coming back.
