Our Heritage

Where Every Meal Feels Like Home.

At Lillie/Ann Home Cooking, we believe in the power of a good meal. Nestled in the heart of Albemarle, NC, we've been serving up classic American comfort food with a smile, crafting dishes that taste just like they came from your grandmother's kitchen.

From the sizzle of our grill to the rich aromas wafting from our kitchen, every detail is designed to bring you warmth and satisfaction. Our bar and grill offers a relaxed, friendly atmosphere where families and friends can gather, enjoy hearty portions, and create lasting memories.

We're more than just a restaurant; we're a part of the Albemarle community, dedicated to providing delicious food and exceptional service. Come on in and taste the love that goes into every plate.
