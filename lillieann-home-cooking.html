Our Heritage

Generations of Flavor, Served with Love

Lillie/Ann Home Cooking isn't just a bar and grill; it's a cherished culinary landmark where classic American comfort food meets genuine Southern hospitality. Nestled in the heart of Albemarle, NC, we've been serving up hearty lunches and dinners that taste just like home for generations. Our commitment to authentic flavors and generous portions has made us a beloved destination for families, friends, and anyone craving a truly satisfying meal.

With a stellar 4.6-star rating from over 360 happy customers, we pride ourselves on using quality ingredients, time-honored recipes, and a welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels like family. From our famous Meatloaf to perfectly grilled Flounder, each dish is crafted with care and tradition. Experience a true taste of Albemarle – where great food and warm memories are always on the menu.
