Our Story

The Craft Behind Every Cup and Plate

At Noir Bakery & Coffee Co, we believe that great experiences are born from rich, bold flavors and a passion for perfection. Our journey began with a simple idea: to bring expertly roasted beans and meticulously crafted food to North East, NC. Every cup of coffee and every freshly baked item tells a story of precision and dedication.

We source the finest ingredients, ensuring that each offering is not just a meal or a drink, but an experience designed to delight. Our commitment extends beyond the kitchen and bar to fostering a vibrant community hub where connections are built daily, one delicious moment at a time.

"The passion is palpable at Noir. Every detail, from the aroma of the coffee to the warmth of the service, speaks volumes. It’s more than just a place to eat; it’s a place to connect."

— A Loyal Local
