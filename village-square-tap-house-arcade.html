Our Roots

More Than Just a Bar, It's Your Community Hangout

Village Square Tap House isn't just a bar; it's a fantastic spot to kick back with friends and enjoy the best of Clemmons. Every weekend, we light up the stage with incredible live bands, making for unforgettable Friday and Saturday nights filled with energy and great tunes. From rock to country, there's always something new to groove to.

Recently, we expanded our space, adding a whole new area brimming with classic and modern arcade games. It's the perfect place for some friendly competition, especially after you've enjoyed a few of our specialty drinks! With two fully stocked bars, you'll always find your favorite pour and a lively, welcoming atmosphere. Come see why we're a beloved local favorite!
