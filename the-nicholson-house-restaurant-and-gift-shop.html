Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

Welcome to The Nicholson House, where history meets hospitality in the heart of Belvidere, NC. Tucked away just off Hwy 37, our beautifully restored Victorian home has been meticulously maintained, offering a unique setting for both dining and shopping.

Step inside and you'll immediately feel the warmth of our local, homegrown culture. Our wait staff greets every customer with genuine happiness and great hospitality, creating an atmosphere where everyone feels like family. It's a place where friends and neighbors gather, happily enjoying delicious meals and the company of others.

Beyond the amazing food, The Nicholson House also features a charming gift shop, brimming with unique finds. Whether you're here for a delightful meal or to discover a special treasure, we promise an experience that will leave you smiling.
