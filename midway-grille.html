Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community Hub.

Nestled conveniently in Bolivia, NC, Midway Grille has quickly become a beloved spot for locals and visitors alike. We know, being next to a grocery store might seem unexpected, but step inside, and you'll immediately feel the difference.

Our goal was simple: create a cozy, quaint space with an undeniably welcoming vibe, where everyone feels like family. And by the countless smiles and steady stream of regulars, we think we've hit the mark!

From the moment you walk through our doors, our fantastic servers ensure you have a delightful experience. It’s an obvious local favorite, a place where good food and good company naturally come together. We pride ourselves on crafting homemade comfort food, like our incredibly popular, mouth-watering homemade meatloaf – it's truly awesome!

While we focus on fresh, delicious main courses, we believe in keeping things simple and honest. Come discover why Midway Grille is a must-visit when you're in the area. We can't wait to welcome you.
