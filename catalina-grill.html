Our Roots

Bessemer City's Authentic Bar & Grill

Welcome to Catalina Grill, a beloved local spot in the heart of Bessemer City, NC. We're proud to serve classic American comfort food that brings smiles to faces and warmth to hearts. From our juicy burgers to our friendly atmosphere, we've dedicated ourselves to being a place where everyone feels at home.

Known for great food and unbeatable value, Catalina Grill has quickly become a local favorite, recognized as one of THE 10 BEST Restaurants in Bessemer City. We believe in simple, honest cooking and a welcoming experience that keeps our community coming back for more. Come on in, grab a seat, and taste the difference that passion makes.
