Our Story

Good Food, Not Fast Food: That's the Quirky Birds Promise.

Lovely small diner whose slogan on the menu is they offer good food, not fast food. From our cozy spot in the heart of Dillsboro, we pour our passion into every dish, crafting creative, flavorful experiences that keep our community coming back. It’s a place where quality ingredients meet heartfelt service, where every meal is made with care, and never rushed.

We believe in honest, delicious food, prepared with a touch of quirkiness and a lot of heart. We've been a beloved local gem, serving up dishes from our famous chicken salad sandwich to our hearty black bean options, always ensuring a memorable meal.
