Our Story

More Than Just Coffee: A Local Tradition in Rocky Mount

River Walk Coffee Co. is more than just a place to grab a drink; it's a cornerstone of the Rocky Mount community. Nestled conveniently at 1151 Falls Rd, we've dedicated ourselves to crafting the perfect cup and creating a welcoming space where everyone feels at home.

From the first sip of your morning hot coffee to the delightful burst of a refreshing smoothie, we believe in quality, warmth, and connection. Whether you're catching up with friends, finding a quiet moment alone, or powering through your workday, we invite you to experience the difference at River Walk Coffee Co.
