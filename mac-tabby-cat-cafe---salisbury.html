Our Journey

More Than Just Coffee – A Home for Felines and Friends

At Mac Tabby Cat Cafe in Salisbury, we've created a purr-fect sanctuary where the love of coffee meets the joy of feline companionship. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide a warm, inviting space for local coffee lovers to relax, and for adoptable cats to find their forever homes.

Every visit supports our mission, as all the adorable cats you interact with are available for adoption. We partner with local shelters to give these wonderful felines a loving, comfortable environment to socialize and showcase their personalities. It's an experience that nourishes the soul, one cup of coffee and one purr at a time. Come for the coffee, stay for the cats – and maybe even take one home!

"Mac Tabby is a slice of heaven! The atmosphere is so calming, the coffee is delicious, and getting to spend time with the cats is incredibly therapeutic. It's heartwarming to know every visit helps these furballs find homes."
