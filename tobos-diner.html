Our Heritage

Where Every Meal Feels Like Coming Home.

Tobos Diner, nestled in the heart of Cleveland, NC, beckons visitors with its warm ambiance and mouthwatering aromas that waft through the air. For years, we've been more than just a place to eat; we're a gathering spot, a true community cornerstone where friends and family come together to enjoy classic comfort food and genuine fellowship.

Every dish is prepared with love, from our famous fluffy pancakes to our hearty daily specials, ensuring a taste of home with every bite. We believe in good food, good company, and creating lasting memories right here in Rowan County. Come experience the Tobo's Diner difference!
