Our Story

From Humble Hops to Heartfelt Brews.

Nestled in the picturesque hills, Red Shedman Farm Brewery and Hop Yard began with a simple passion: crafting exceptional, farm-fresh beverages for our community. We pride ourselves on a spacious and inviting atmosphere where friends and family can gather, relax, and create lasting memories.

Our journey started with a focus on quality ales, lagers, and refreshing hard ciders, all brewed with care and local ingredients. We love to share our dedication to local flavors and the art of brewing with every visitor. While our in-house food options are simple, we often host incredible local food trucks, ensuring there's always something delicious to pair with your favorite brew.
