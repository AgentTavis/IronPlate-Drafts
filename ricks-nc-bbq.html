Our Heritage, Our Passion

The Secret to North Carolina's Best BBQ

At Rick's NC BBQ, we believe true barbecue is an art form. It's about patience, premium ingredients, and a deep understanding of flavor. I've had BBQ in Memphis, Kansas City, Texas, St. Louis, and right here in Carolina, and I confidently say Rick's stands among the very best.

I, Rick, am a master at achieving just the right amount of smokiness, juiciness, and tenderness in every single dish, especially our legendary ribs and pulled pork. We are meticulous about the quality of meat selected and obsessive about the seasoning of our smoker. Our ribs are so delicious, they're super incredible even without any sauces – a testament to our craft.
