Our Story

A Taste of the Blue Ridge Mountains

Tartan Restaurant, nestled in the charming town of Linville, NC, offers a delightful American dining experience amidst the picturesque Blue Ridge Mountains. For years, we've been a beloved local spot, where residents and visitors alike come to enjoy hearty meals and a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

We pride ourselves on serving up classic American comfort food, prepared with care and a touch of Linville's unique hospitality. Whether you're starting your day with a delicious breakfast or grabbing a satisfying lunch, Tartan Restaurant is where good food and good company come together.
