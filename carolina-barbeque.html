Our Heritage

Taste the Tradition, Feel the Family.

At Carolina Barbeque, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a cornerstone of the Newland community. For years, we've been dedicated to crafting authentic American barbecue, slow-smoked to perfection and bursting with flavor. Our roots run deep in the heart of the mountains, bringing a genuine taste of Carolina to every plate.

Whether you're joining us for a hearty lunch or a relaxing dinner, our kitchen is always buzzing with passion. We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming atmosphere where families can gather, friends can connect, and everyone can enjoy a truly memorable meal. Expect highchairs for the little ones, ample parking, and even live music to enhance your dining experience.

Our Promise

Why Locals & Visitors Choose Carolina Barbeque

Authentic Carolina Flavor

From our signature slow-smoked brisket to our tangy pulled pork, we bring you the true taste of Carolina BBQ. Our pitmasters dedicate hours to perfecting each dish, ensuring every bite is a mouthwatering experience.

Family-Friendly Atmosphere

We've created a welcoming space for everyone. With highchairs available, ample parking, and often live music, Carolina Barbeque is the perfect spot for family dinners, gatherings, or a casual lunch with friends.

Community Roots in Newland

As a proud part of Newland, NC, we cherish our local connections. We're known for our friendly service and dedication to serving our community, making us a true local favorite for authentic American BBQ.
