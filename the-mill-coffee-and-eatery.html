Our Story

Where Community Gathers Over Great Food.

At The Mill Coffee and Eatery, we believe in the simple joy of good food and great company. Nestled in the heart of Roanoke Rapids, our cozy bar and grill has been a local fixture since 2018, serving up classic comfort dishes and a warm, inviting atmosphere that feels like home.

We're more than just a place to eat; we're a gathering spot where neighbors become friends and every meal is made with care. From our robust coffee to our hearty grilled favorites, we pour our passion into every plate, ensuring a delicious experience that keeps you coming back.
