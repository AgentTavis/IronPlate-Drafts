Our Roots

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community Hub

Nestled in the heart of Roanoke Rapids, The Mill Coffee and Eatery is more than just a place to grab a bite – it's a community gathering spot. We pride ourselves on serving up hearty, homestyle meals in a warm and welcoming atmosphere. From our comforting chili and chicken wild rice soup to our classic breakfast plates, every dish is prepared with care and a touch of Southern hospitality. Come on in, pull up a chair, and experience why we're a local favorite.
