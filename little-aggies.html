Our Journey

Crafting Flavors, Building Community.

At Little Aggies, we believe great food brings people together. Nestled in the heart of Kinston, NC, our mission is simple: to serve incredibly delicious meals made with the freshest ingredients, all within a warm and welcoming atmosphere.

From the moment we opened our doors, we've been committed to offering a diverse menu that truly has something for everyone. Whether you're craving a classic, cheesy pizza, a hearty handcrafted sub, a juicy gourmet burger, or the vibrant flavors of authentic Thai cuisine, every dish is prepared with passion and a commitment to quality. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a place where friends and family can gather, share laughter, and enjoy unforgettable meals. Come taste the Little Aggies difference!
