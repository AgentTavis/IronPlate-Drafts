Our Journey

More Than Just Food: A Taste of Kinston's Best

With a genuine commitment to using only the freshest ingredients, Little Aggies in Kinston, NC, has become a beloved local spot. We craft a delightful array of signature pizzas, hearty handcrafted subs, juicy burgers, and authentic flavorful Thai dishes.

Our kitchen is a place where culinary traditions meet innovation, ensuring a unique and satisfying experience for every palate. From family dinners to quick take-out lunches, we're dedicated to bringing delicious, high-quality fusion cuisine right to your table, made with passion and a touch of local charm.
