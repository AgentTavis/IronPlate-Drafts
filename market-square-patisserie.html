Our Journey

The Heart of Franklin's Culinary Scene

Market Square Patisserie is more than just a place to eat; it's a beloved local spot in Franklin, NC, where community, comfort, and culinary craftsmanship come together. We've dedicated ourselves to creating a warm, inviting atmosphere where every guest feels like family.

From the aroma of freshly baked goods to the sizzling sounds from our grill, we pour our passion into every detail. Our commitment to quality ingredients and exceptional service has made us a cherished gathering place for friends and neighbors alike. Come experience the taste of tradition and innovation, crafted with love, right here in Market Square.

Taste Our Craft

Handcrafted Delights, Savory & Sweet

While our full menu awaits your visit, here’s a glimpse into the exquisite flavors and expertly crafted dishes we’re proud to serve daily. We specialize in combining classic patisserie artistry with a modern bar and grill flair.

Artisan Croque Monsieur

Toasted sourdough, smoked ham, gruyere, béchamel sauce, lightly grilled.

Market Salad with Grilled Chicken

Seasonal greens, fresh vegetables, house vinaigrette, perfectly grilled chicken breast.

Signature Quiche of the Day

Flaky crust filled with savory custard, fresh ingredients changing daily.

Classic Butter Croissant

Light, flaky, and golden-brown, a true French patisserie staple.

Seasonal Fruit Tart

Sweet pastry crust, vanilla cream, topped with a medley of fresh seasonal fruits.

Decadent Chocolate Eclair

Choux pastry filled with rich chocolate cream, glazed with dark ganache.

Espresso & Specialty Coffee

Carefully selected beans, expertly prepared for your perfect cup.

Assorted Macarons

A colorful selection of delicate French meringue cookies with various fillings.
