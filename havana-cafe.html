Our Journey

Bringing the Heart of Cuba to the High Country

Havana Cafe specializes in authentic Cuban food, offering a vibrant variety of dishes that reflect traditional Cuban flavors. Located in the picturesque High Country of West Jefferson, we've brought the warmth and spice of Havana to our mountain community.

Our passion is to share the rich tapestry of Cuban cuisine, meticulously prepared with fresh ingredients and time-honored family recipes. From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a customer; you're family, invited to savor a culinary experience that transports you straight to the bustling streets of Cuba.

"At Havana Cafe, we believe food is more than sustenance; it's a celebration of culture, family, and joy. We pour our hearts into every dish, so you can taste the authentic spirit of Cuba."

— The Havana Cafe Family

Our Dishes

Taste the Flavors of Havana

Ropa Vieja

Shredded beef stewed in a rich tomato-based sauce with peppers and onions. A Cuban classic.

Cuban Sandwich

Roasted pork, ham, Swiss cheese, pickles, and mustard, pressed on Cuban bread.

Lechon Asado

Slow-roasted marinated pork, incredibly tender and flavorful, a traditional celebratory dish.

Arroz con Pollo

Saffron rice cooked with tender chicken, peas, and pimientos, a hearty one-pot meal.

Platanos Maduros

Sweet fried plantains, caramelized to perfection, a delicious side dish.

Yuca con Mojo

Boiled cassava root topped with a savory garlic-citrus mojo sauce.

Cafe Cubano

Strong, sweet espresso, a potent and essential part of Cuban daily life.

Flan

Classic caramel custard, rich and creamy, the perfect Cuban dessert.
