Our Journey

Taste the Passion in Every Dish.

From the heart of Elizabeth City, Bistro Tropical began with a simple passion: to bring the freshest seafood and vibrant Central American flavors to our beloved community. We believe in culinary excellence, using only the best ingredients to craft dishes that delight and inspire.

Every plate, from our signature Coconut Shrimp Platter to our savory Shrimp Empanadas, is prepared with meticulous care and attention to detail. Our commitment is to provide not just a meal, but a memorable experience, filled with authentic tastes and a warm, inviting atmosphere.

It's this dedication to quality and flavor that keeps our guests coming back, making Bistro Tropical a true local gem.
