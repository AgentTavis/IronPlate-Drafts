OUR STORY

More Than a Meal, It's a Goldsboro Tradition.

For years, Lantern Inn has been more than just a place to eat; it's been the heartbeat of Goldsboro's community. We believe in good food, cold beer, and even better company. Our doors open early for a hearty breakfast and stay open late for lively evenings with friends, family, and football.

We're a mom-and-pop establishment where every guest is remembered, every favorite drink is known, and every meal is made with that special touch of home. It’s this dedication to service and quality that makes us a true local treasure.

OUR FLAVORS

Classic Bar & Grill Favorites, Made Fresh Daily.

Classic Lantern Burger

A juicy beef patty, melted cheddar, crisp lettuce, fresh tomato, onion, and pickles, all on a toasted brioche bun. A timeless favorite!

Crispy Chicken Tenders

Golden-fried, tender chicken strips served with your choice of our signature dipping sauces. Perfect for sharing or as a hearty meal.

Goldsboro Fish & Chips

Flaky, beer-battered white fish, fried to perfection and served with a generous portion of our crispy seasoned fries and tartar sauce.

Steakhouse Salad

Grilled to your liking, tender steak slices atop a bed of mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and a zesty balsamic vinaigrette.

Loaded Nachos

A mountain of crispy tortilla chips smothered in melted cheese, fresh jalapeños, salsa, and a dollop of cool sour cream. Add chili or chicken!

Brewer's Bratwurst

A perfectly grilled bratwurst sausage nestled in a toasted bun, topped with tangy sauerkraut and a drizzle of spicy mustard.
