Our Heritage

More Than Just a Bar & Grill – It's Home.

At Wagon Wheel, we're proud to be Roanoke Rapids' go-to destination for fantastic food, a vibrant atmosphere, and unbeatable hospitality. We're known for our carefully curated selection of refreshing beer and fine wine, perfectly paired with our legendary bar food.

From sizzling wings and classic pizzas to hearty subs and much more, every dish is crafted with flavor in mind. We're a local favorite, not just for our delicious offerings but for the convenience we provide, including car pickup for those nights you just want to grab and go.

Come for the food, stay for the friendly faces and lively environment. Wagon Wheel is where memories are made, one delicious bite and refreshing sip at a time.
