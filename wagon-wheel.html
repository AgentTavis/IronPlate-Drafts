Our Heritage

Where Good Times & Great Food Meet

At Wagon Wheel, we're more than just a bar and grill; we're a Roanoke Rapids institution. For years, we've been known as the go-to spot for locals seeking a relaxed atmosphere, a fantastic selection of beer and wine, and bar food that truly hits the spot.

Our commitment is simple: serve up delicious wings, satisfying pizzas, hearty subs, and more, all crafted with care. Whether you're catching a game, grabbing a quick bite, or enjoying a late-night meal, Wagon Wheel offers the convenience of curbside pickup and a welcoming vibe for everyone.
