Our Story

Crafted for Community, Poured for You.

At Coasters Downtown Draught House, we believe in bringing people together. Nestled in the heart of Elizabeth City, NC, we're your go-to spot for an incredible selection of craft beers, featuring 29 taps rotating the finest local, national, and seasonal brews.

But we're more than just great beer – we're a hub where friends gather, stories are shared, and good times roll. Our casual and welcoming atmosphere makes everyone feel right at home, whether you're catching up with old friends on our outdoor patio or making new ones over a delicious meal.

We're proud to be a part of Elizabeth City's vibrant community, serving up delicious eats and unforgettable nights until 2 AM, every single day of the week. Come discover your new favorite pint and the friendly vibe that makes Coasters a true local staple.
