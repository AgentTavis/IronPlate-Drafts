Our Story

Generations of Flavor, Served with Love.

At Tarheel Bar-B-Q, we're more than just a restaurant – we're a cherished family tradition. For years, we've been serving up authentic, homemade North Carolina barbecue right here in the heart of Eure. Step inside and you'll feel like you've come home, greeted by friendly faces and the irresistible aroma of slow-cooked perfection.

We believe in honest food, genuine Southern hospitality, and creating lasting memories around the table. It's the kind of place where every meal feels like a special gathering, and every customer leaves feeling like part of the family. Plus, you might even find a unique treat like our local sorghum syrup in our little old country store area!
