Our Journey

Crafting Community, One Brew at a Time

At Pike 51 Brewery, we believe great food and exceptional beer are best enjoyed with great company. Nestled in the heart of Hudsonville, NC, we've built more than just a bar and grill – we've created a vibrant gathering place where friends and family can relax, unwind, and make lasting memories.

Our passion lies in sourcing the finest ingredients and brewing innovative, delicious beers that perfectly complement our hearty grill menu. From our taps to our kitchen, quality and care are poured into every detail. See our wine list, what’s on tap and the latest food menu. It’s all here.
