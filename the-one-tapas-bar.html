OUR STORY

Experience Global Flavors, Local Heart

At The One Tapas & Bar, we believe dining is an experience to be savored and shared. Our concept, born in Matthews, NC, is deeply inspired by the vibrant Spanish tradition of tapas – small, flavorful plates designed for communal enjoyment and lively conversation.

Our culinary journey extends beyond Spain, weaving in rich flavors and aromatic spices from across the globe. We pride ourselves on a menu that constantly evolves, incorporating daily specials and seasonal ingredients to showcase the freshest bounty of each harvest and catch. Every dish is a testament to our passion for quality and innovation.

As a locally owned and operated establishment, we are committed to being a cornerstone of the Matthews community. Beyond our delectable foods, The One Tapas offers a thoughtfully curated selection of signature crafted cocktails and fine wines, chosen to perfectly complement your meal and spark delightful conversations. Come share a moment, a meal, and a memory with us.
