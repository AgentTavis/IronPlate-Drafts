Our Legacy

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community Hub

Davie Tavern isn't just a place to eat; it's the heartbeat of Advance, NC. Since 2005, we've proudly served our community, creating a welcoming space where friends and family gather, laughter fills the air, and every visit feels like coming home. We believe in good food, great company, and genuine hospitality.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a local spot where everyone feels like family. From our scratch kitchen to our friendly staff, every detail is crafted with care to ensure you have an unforgettable experience. Come on down and taste the tradition, feel the warmth, and become a part of the Davie Tavern story.

"Davie Tavern has been our go-to for years! The atmosphere is always lively, the staff are like old friends, and their food never disappoints. Truly a beloved local gem!"

— A Happy Regular from Advance
