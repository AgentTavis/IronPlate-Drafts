Our Story

Your Neighborhood Spot
for 15 Years

Nestled between Oak Ridge and Stokesdale, StokeRidge Tavern is the kind of place where everybody knows your name. For 15 years, we've been the neighborhood spot where locals come to unwind, catch a game, share a meal, and enjoy live music on the patio.


Our menu is freshly prepped from scratch every day. From our signature jumbo wings to hand-cut ribeyes and bourbon-glazed salmon, every plate is made with care — no shortcuts, no frozen patties, no excuses.


We're proud to be family owned, proud to support our local schools and youth organizations, and proudest of the regulars who make this place what it is. Come hungry, stay a while, and leave feeling like part of the family.

A Few Things We're Proud Of

Family Owned Since 2011

Locally owned and operated for 15 years. Every decision gets made with our neighbors in mind.

Scratch-Made Everything

Soups, sauces, chips, dressings — made in our kitchen, not shipped in a bag. Hand-cut steaks every day.
