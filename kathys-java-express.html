OUR STORY

Serving Sanford's Heart, One Plate at a Time.

Since 1958, Kathy's Java Express has been a cherished cornerstone of the Sanford community. We started with a simple vision: to offer delicious, homestyle diner food and exceptional coffee in a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Over sixty years later, that commitment remains at the heart of everything we do.

We pride ourselves on a menu that offers something for everyone. From comforting classics to delightful new dishes, our variety ensures that even the pickiest eaters leave happy. We love seeing families gather, friends catch up, and new faces become regulars, all while enjoying our freshly prepared meals.

It's more than just food; it's about creating memories, fostering connections, and continuing a beloved Sanford tradition that spans generations. Come on in and experience the charm and flavor that make Kathy's Java Express a local favorite.
