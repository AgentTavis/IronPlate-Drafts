Our Journey

Bringing Authentic Hibachi to Kernersville

Ikura Hibachi Express isn't just a restaurant; it's a culinary destination in Kernersville, NC. Located at 428 N Main St, Ste F, we're proud to bring the vibrant flavors and exciting experience of hibachi right to your neighborhood. Our passion is crafting delicious meals that bring smiles to faces, from sizzling lunch specials to delightful adult beverages.

We believe in providing an authentic, memorable dine-in experience, open six days a week from 11 AM to 9 PM, ensuring your hibachi fix is always within reach. Every dish is prepared with care, using fresh ingredients to deliver the quality you deserve. Come on in and taste the difference that has earned us a stellar 4.8-star rating from our wonderful community!
