OUR STORY

From Our Family Kitchen to Your Table.

Nestled in the heart of Ahoskie, The Table Spoon is more than just a restaurant – it's a culinary haven where every dish tells a story of passion and fresh ingredients. We believe in crafting memorable meals, from our hearty breakfast and lunch options to our delectable, fresh-baked breads and hand-cut donuts.

Our cozy, quaint interior and welcoming atmosphere make it the perfect stop for a fresh, home-cooked taste on your journey. Experience the warmth of genuine hospitality and the joy of truly fresh, delicious food, just like our guests rave about our Turkey Melt, sweet Pasta Salad, and unique Strawberry Lemonade Cheesecake Muffin. We’re proud to be a local favorite, serving Ahoskie with love, one spoonful at a time.
