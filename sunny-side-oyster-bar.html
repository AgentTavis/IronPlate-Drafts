Our Heritage

A Timeless Tradition of Coastal Delights

Nestled in the heart of Williamston, NC, Sunny Side Oyster Bar isn't just a restaurant; it's a cherished piece of local history. Our beloved building, proudly listed on the National Register of Historic Places, has been the backdrop for countless good times and memorable meals for generations.

We specialize in serving the freshest steamed seafood, caught daily from our pristine North Carolina waters. Combined with a vibrant, casual bar atmosphere, Sunny Side Oyster Bar is where great people gather for great food and even better company. Come as you are, and leave with a smile and a full belly!

"Sunny Side Oyster Bar is more than just a meal; it's an institution. The atmosphere is always lively, the seafood is unbelievably fresh, and you always leave feeling like family. A true Williamston gem!"
