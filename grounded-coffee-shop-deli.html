Our Story

More Than Just a Diner: Your Daily Escape in Marion.

Though not originally from Marion, we quickly fell in love with this community and decided to open Grounded Coffee Shop & Deli. We envisioned a place where locals could find delicious, reasonably priced food and great coffee in a welcoming, clean environment.

Since opening, we've dedicated ourselves to delivering on that promise. Our staff is friendly and attentive, our kitchen is spotless, and we pride ourselves on using quality ingredients like premium Boar's Head meats for our deli creations.

Whether you're grabbing a quick breakfast, a satisfying lunch, or just a lovely drink to start your week, Grounded is here to be your new favorite spot. Come experience the difference for yourself!
