Our Story

More Than Just a Diner, It's a Community Hub.

When you walk into Muddy's, the first thing you feel is that quaint, genuine mom-and-pop shop vibe. It's a place where every face is a friendly one and every meal feels like it was made just for you. We're proud to be a local favorite in Jacksonville, NC, serving up classic diner comfort with a personal touch.

What truly sets us apart? We're passionate about coffee, and it starts right here. We roast our own green coffee beans, ensuring every cup is fresh, aromatic, and full of flavor. But we're not just about coffee; our menu features a delicious array of breakfast and lunch foods. While our kitchen might be cozy, everything that comes out is fresh, tasty, and made with care.

We believe in quality, from our ingredients to our cleanliness. That's why we're incredibly proud to have scored a 100% from the health department. We invite you to experience Muddy's for yourself – where good food, great coffee, and a welcoming atmosphere come together.
