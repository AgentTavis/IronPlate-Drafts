Our Story

The Roots of Grounded.
Your Daily Dose of Delicious.

At Grounded. Coffee Shop & Food Factory, we believe in starting your day right, or making any moment special. While our partners at Great Commission Coffee House meticulously import and roast all their coffee beans on premise for the freshest coffee available, we bring that same dedication to your cup and plate here in Cherokee.

Our full-service coffee cafe offers a delightful array of Lattes, Cappuccinos, Mochas, and more, crafted with expertise. But we're more than just coffee; we're a Food Factory, bustling with delicious diner-style meals and treats designed to satisfy every craving. We're a local hub where quality ingredients meet heartfelt service, creating a welcoming space for everyone.
