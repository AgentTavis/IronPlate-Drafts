Our Roots

The Heart and Soul of Rocky Mount BBQ

At Smoking Backyard BBQ & Grill Catering, it's more than just food; it's a passion for authentic American BBQ. Nestled right here in Rocky Mount, NC, we've been crafting mouth-watering smoked meats and grilled delights that have become a local legend.

From the moment you step in, you'll experience our dedication to flavor. We take pride in our slow-smoking techniques, ensuring every bite, from our tender Pulled Pork to our unique Blackberry Moonshine BBQ, is packed with a rich, smoky taste. Join us for lunch, or let us cater your next event – we promise an unforgettable culinary experience.

We believe in simple, honest food made with love and the best ingredients. It's the kind of BBQ that brings people together, creates memories, and keeps you coming back for more. Come taste the tradition that makes us Rocky Mount's favorite backyard grill.
