Our Roots

Crafting Community, One Brew at a Time

At Third Hill Brewing Co, we believe that great flavors are born from passion and precision. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a space where friends and family can gather, enjoy exceptional food, and discover unique, handcrafted beers.

We pour our heart into every dish and every pint. Take our signature "Paper, Rock, Scissors?" for example—a Belgian Tripel that tempts with fruity aromas like banana, apple, and pear, with wisps of clove. Its rich, complex flavor, sweet bready maltiness, and subtle bitterness make it a delightfully drinkable experience. It's this dedication to quality and distinctive taste that defines us.

From the sizzle of our BBQ to the bubbling of our fermentation tanks, we're committed to bringing Silver Spring, NC, a culinary and brewing experience unlike any other. Come on down and taste the difference that genuine craft makes.

"Third Hill isn't just a brewery; it's a home away from home. Their commitment to unique flavors, especially their Belgian Tripel, is absolutely unmatched. A true gem in Silver Spring!"
