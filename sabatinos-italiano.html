Our Heritage

A Taste of Italy, Crafted with Love in Apex

At Sabatino's Italiano, we believe that every meal is a celebration. Our journey began with a simple passion: to bring the true, unadulterated flavors of Italy to the heart of Apex, NC. We meticulously craft each dish using time-honored recipes passed down through generations, ensuring an authentic experience from the first bite to the last.

Join us at Sabatino's, and discover why we're more than just a restaurant – we're a gathering place where families and friends come together to create cherished memories over exceptional food. From our rich, slow-simmered sauces to our perfectly al dente pasta, every ingredient tells a story of tradition and dedication.

Our Delizioso Dishes

Sabatino's Signature Selections

Spaghetti Carbonara

Classic Roman pasta dish with eggs, hard cheese, cured pork (guanciale), and black pepper.

Lasagna Bolognese

Layers of fresh pasta, rich beef ragù, béchamel sauce, and Parmesan cheese, baked to perfection.

Fettuccine Alfredo

Creamy, rich, and irresistible fettuccine tossed in a butter and Parmesan cheese sauce.

Chicken Parmigiana

Breaded chicken breast, pan-fried and topped with marinara sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan, served with pasta.

Margherita Pizza

A timeless classic with San Marzano tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, basil, and a drizzle of olive oil.

Ravioli di Ricotta e Spinaci

Homemade ricotta and spinach filled ravioli, served with your choice of marinara or sage butter sauce.

Panna Cotta

Silky smooth Italian dessert made with sweetened cream and vanilla, often served with berry compote.

Tiramisu

Classic coffee-flavored Italian dessert with ladyfingers dipped in coffee, layered with mascarpone cheese and cocoa.
