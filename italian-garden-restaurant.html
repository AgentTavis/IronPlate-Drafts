Our Story

Bringing Authentic Flavors & Family Warmth to Shelby

Italian Garden is a beloved Italian restaurant located at 105 N Main St, Shelby, North Carolina 28152. Known for its warm, family-friendly atmosphere, we pride ourselves on serving authentic Italian, classic Pizza, and delightful Greek dishes.

From a quick lunch to a relaxing dinner, our menu caters to all, including vegetarian-friendly options. We also offer convenient delivery, highchairs for the little ones, comfortable seating, and attentive table service, ensuring every visit is a memorable one. Come and experience why we are Shelby's go-to for delicious, comforting meals.
