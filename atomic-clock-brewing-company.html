Our Story

Crafting Exceptional Brews & Culinary Delights

At Atomic Clock Brewing Company, we're passionate about more than just great beer. We're dedicated to a unique experience where exceptional craft brews meet chef-driven cuisine. Unlike many breweries that rely on food trucks, we take pride in having our own talented chef in-house, creating outstanding dishes that perfectly complement our diverse tap list.

Our service model is designed for a relaxed, taproom atmosphere. You'll find our friendly bartenders ready to guide you through our beer selection, and a dedicated food runner ensures your meal arrives promptly, without any buzzers or loud speakers. This allows us to maintain a clean, comfortable environment where the focus is on quality and enjoyment.

It's what we've found works best for our business, allowing us to serve both our beer enthusiasts and food lovers with the attention they deserve. Come discover the Atomic Clock difference – where every visit is a journey for your taste buds.
