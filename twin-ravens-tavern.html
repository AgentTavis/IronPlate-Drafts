Welcome, traveler, to Twin Ravens Tavern – Jacksonville, North Carolina's authentic Nordic Viking Bar! More than just a place to eat and drink, we've crafted an immersive experience where every visit feels like a journey to the heart of Valhalla. From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a customer; you're part of our clan.
