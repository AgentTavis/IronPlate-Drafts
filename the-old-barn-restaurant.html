Our Culinary Delights

Simple Ingredients, Extraordinary Flavor

Our menu celebrates classic American comfort food with a focus on perfectly cooked steaks and fresh seafood. We believe in quality over complexity, delivering dishes that bring a smile to your face every time.

The Old Barn Signature Ribeye

Our most celebrated dish. A perfectly marbled, hand-cut ribeye, seasoned and grilled to your exact preference. So good, it rivals Mom's cooking.

Classic Fried Shrimp Platter

Generous portion of succulent shrimp, lightly battered and fried to golden perfection. Served with our zesty cocktail sauce.

Fresh Catch of the Day

Ask your server about today's fresh selection, expertly grilled or blackened to highlight its natural flavors. A true taste of the coast.

Homestyle Smothered Chicken

Tender chicken breast, pan-seared and smothered in rich, savory gravy with onions and peppers. Comfort food at its finest.

Creamy Garlic Mashed Potatoes

A classic side, made with real butter, fresh garlic, and whipped to a creamy perfection. The ideal accompaniment to any main.

Decadent Chocolate Fudge Cake

Rich, moist chocolate cake layered with creamy fudge frosting. The perfect sweet ending to your meal.
