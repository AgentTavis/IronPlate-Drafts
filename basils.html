Our Heritage

Crafting authentic Italian flavors, right here in Boone.

At Basil's, we believe in the simple joy of exceptional Italian food, made with passion and served with warmth. As a locally owned establishment in the heart of Boone, NC, we're dedicated to bringing you an experience that feels both authentic and intimately familiar.

We're more than just a restaurant; we're a gathering place, a community hub, and a testament to the timeless appeal of classic Italian cuisine.
