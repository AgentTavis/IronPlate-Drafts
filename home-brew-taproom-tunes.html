Our Story

Crafting Community, One Brew and Tune at a Time.

Home Brew Taproom & Tunes isn't just a place to eat and drink; it's a vibrant cornerstone of downtown Monroe. We're proud to be a small music venue, bringing local talent and good vibes to our community every week. Our passion is creating a welcoming space where friends gather, stories are shared, and memories are made.

We believe in quality, from the perfectly crafted pizza Napoletana that melts in your mouth, to our curated selection of craft beers, signature cocktails, and refreshing cold brew coffee. Every item on our menu is chosen to enhance your experience, whether you're here for a lively night of music or a relaxing afternoon on our outdoor patio.

Join us and discover why Home Brew Taproom & Tunes is more than just a taproom – it’s where good food, great music, and even better company come together.
