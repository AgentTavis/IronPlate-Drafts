Our Legacy

Generations of Flavor, Right Here in Thomasville.

S & S Grill isn't just a bar and grill; it's a Thomasville institution, a place where good folks gather, laughter echoes, and the food always hits the spot. For years, we've been serving up our cherished recipes, becoming a true cornerstone of the community. From our legendary Chicken Salad to the friendly "Say Hey Mama" welcome, every visit feels like coming home.

We pride ourselves on being ranked among the top restaurants in Thomasville, celebrated for our consistent quality and the unmistakable charm that defines us. Our commitment to fresh ingredients, a welcoming atmosphere, and genuine hospitality is what makes us truly "best in class."
