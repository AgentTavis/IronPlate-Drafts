Our Story

Crafting Sweet Memories, One Scoop at a Time.

Nestled in the heart of historic Waxhaw, Waxhaw Creamery has been a beloved local institution, serving up smiles and irresistible treats for years. We believe in the simple joy of exceptional ice cream, crafted with care and a sprinkle of community spirit.

Our cozy spot might be small, but our passion is huge! We're dedicated to providing not just the best sundaes, floats, and even delicious vegan options, but also a warm, family-friendly atmosphere where everyone feels welcome. Our friendly, knowledgeable staff are always ready to help you find your perfect scoop.
