Our Roots

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

Nestled within the scenic Belmont Lake Preserve, Tavern 19 continues a proud tradition of exceptional dining in Rocky Mount, NC. Formerly known as The Main Course, we've reinvigorated our space to bring you a modern Bar & Grill experience while retaining the warmth and hospitality our patrons have always cherished.

At Tavern 19, we believe great food starts with great ingredients. That's why we're committed to sourcing only the freshest, highest quality components for every dish, with a special emphasis on our premium meats. Whether you're unwinding after a round of golf, enjoying a family meal, or gathering with friends, our relaxed atmosphere and attentive service make every visit memorable.

We're proud to be a local favorite, a place where community gathers and delicious memories are made. Come discover the Tavern 19 difference – where tradition meets taste in the heart of Rocky Mount.
