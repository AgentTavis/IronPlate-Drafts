Our Story

More Than Just a Bar, It's Your Neighborhood Spot

Welcome to Ramblers Beer & Wine, proudly under new ownership and committed to being Durham's favorite local haunt. We're not just a place to grab a drink; we're a community hub where friends gather, stories are shared, and good times roll.

Our passion lies in offering a diverse selection of beverages, from craft beers and fine wines to unique THC drinks and refreshing seltzers. With free WiFi and a pet-friendly atmosphere, we've created a casual, intimate, and dog-friendly space perfect for unwinding after a long day or celebrating with a group.

Come experience the difference of a truly welcoming neighborhood bar and beverage store. We can't wait to raise a glass with you!
