Our Story

Crafting Carolina Beach's Favorite Donuts, Daily.

At Wake N Bake Donuts in Carolina Beach, we believe in starting your day right – with a freshly baked donut and a perfect cup of coffee. Born from a passion for sweet treats and genuine hospitality, we've become a beloved local spot where every visit feels like coming home.

Our commitment is simple: create incredible flavors, use quality ingredients, and serve with a smile. From classic glazed to our wilder signature creations, each donut is a little piece of joy, made with care right here in Carolina Beach.
