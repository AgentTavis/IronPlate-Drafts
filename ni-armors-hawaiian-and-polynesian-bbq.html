Our Journey

A Taste of the Islands, Right Here in NC

Welcome to Ni Armors, a little slice of paradise nestled right here in Pittsboro, NC. For years, we dreamt of bringing the authentic, vibrant flavors of the Pacific to our new home. After moving from California, we realized how much we missed the comforting taste of true Hawaiian and Polynesian cuisine. We knew others must too!

So, we poured our hearts into creating a place where you could savor those unique dishes, just like we remember them. While our unassuming location (yes, next to a car wash!) might surprise you, step inside and you'll find a warm, welcoming atmosphere and food that transports you straight to the islands. We're proud to share our family's recipes, from the crispy fried shrimp to our customer-favorite BBQ plates, ensuring every bite is a pleasant surprise.
