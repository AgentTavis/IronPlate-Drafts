Our Story

Serving Ferguson with Heart & Hometown Flavor

Nestled in the heart of Ferguson, NC, Grocery Basket & Grill is more than just a place to eat – it's a community gathering spot. We're proud to be a local favorite, serving up classic comfort food with a friendly, down-home hospitality that feels like family. From hearty breakfasts to satisfying lunches, every dish is prepared with care, ensuring a delicious experience that keeps our neighbors coming back for more.

We believe in simple, honest food made with love. Come on in, pull up a chair, and taste the difference of a meal cooked just for you. Whether you're grabbing a quick bite or settling in for a leisurely lunch, you'll always find a warm welcome and a plate full of your favorites here at Grocery Basket & Grill.
