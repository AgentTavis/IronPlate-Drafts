Our Roots

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

Nestled in the heart of Washington, NC, The Stubborn Mule Oyster Bar & Grill offers a vibrant atmosphere that feels both classic and refreshingly modern. We've brought new life to our space, ensuring a bright and welcoming environment where laughter flows as freely as our curated drinks.

Our kitchen is a testament to flavor, serving up hearty American grill classics with a special focus on fresh, succulent oysters. From the moment you walk in, you're not just a customer; you're family. We pride ourselves on creating unforgettable memories, whether you're enjoying our signature burgers or exploring our vast selection of spirits.

With Alisa, our phenomenal bartender, at the helm of our bar, you're guaranteed expert service and a drink tailored to your taste. Her dedication, alongside our commitment to quality food and a stellar atmosphere, makes The Stubborn Mule a true gem in Washington, NC. Come for the food, stay for the company, and leave with a smile.
