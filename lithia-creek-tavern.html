Our Roots

Welcome to Your Home Away From Home, Lithia Creek Tavern

Nestled in the heart of Lincolnton, Lithia Creek Tavern is more than just a bar and grill – it's your local retreat. Since opening our doors, we've been dedicated to serving up hearty meals, refreshing drinks, and an atmosphere where friends and family can unwind. We believe in good food, great company, and making every visit feel like coming home.

With a vibrant community active across 145 social posts, we're proud to be a cherished spot where good times and great food come together. We're committed to being a cornerstone of Lincolnton, a place where memories are made and every guest leaves with a smile. Come experience why we're Lincolnton's favorite gathering place, open daily from 10 AM to 8 PM.
