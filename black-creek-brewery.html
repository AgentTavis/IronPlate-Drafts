Our Story

Brewing Passion, Serving Community

At Black Creek Brewery, we're more than just a place for great beer and food; we're a cornerstone of the Roxboro community. Nestled in the heart of historic Uptown, our independent brewery was founded on a simple premise: to create exceptional craft beers and a welcoming space for everyone.

We believe in honest ingredients, meticulous brewing processes, and a menu that complements every pour. From our crisp lagers to our hop-forward IPAs, each brew is a testament to our dedication. We're proud to be local owners, pouring our passion into every glass and plate, ensuring a memorable experience for locals and visitors alike.

Taste Our Craft

Our Crafted Brews & Grill Favorites

While our full menu offers daily specials and seasonal delights, here are a few year-round favorites to get your taste buds ready. Crafted with passion, served with pride.

Black Creek IPA

A bold, hop-forward IPA featuring notes of citrus and pine, brewed with a balanced malt backbone for a smooth finish. Perfect for IPA lovers!

Roxboro Lager

Crisp, clean, and refreshing, our signature lager is a true crowd-pleaser. Light-bodied with a subtle malt sweetness, ideal for any occasion.

Smoked Brisket Sandwich

Slow-smoked brisket piled high on a toasted bun, with tangy BBQ sauce and coleslaw. A hearty favorite from our grill.

Crispy Chicken Wings

Juicy, crispy wings tossed in your choice of classic buffalo, BBQ, or a spicy dry rub. Served with ranch or blue cheese.

Artisan Flatbread

Chef's daily selection of fresh toppings on a crispy, oven-baked flatbread. Ask your server for today's special creation!

Black Creek Burger

Our classic beef patty, grilled to perfection, with lettuce, tomato, onion, and your choice of cheese. Served with seasoned fries.
