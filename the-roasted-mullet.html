Our Story

More Than Just a Meal: It's Our Home, It's Your Community.

Welcome to The Roasted Mullet, your cherished neighborhood diner and coffee shop nestled in the heart of Bailey, NC. We're proud to serve delicious, fresh-baked goods, expertly brewed coffees and teas, alongside hearty, classic diner fare.

From our kitchen to your table, every dish is crafted with care, and every cup brewed with passion. We are happy to see you, and happier to serve you, creating a warm, inviting space where friends and family gather. Come experience the true taste of Bailey and become a part of our story!
