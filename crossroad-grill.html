Our Story

A Legacy of Flavor, Right Here in Waxhaw.

Taste the Tradition, Feel the Family.

Crossroad Grill isn't just a place to eat; it's a cornerstone of the Waxhaw community. As a beloved Mom and Pop establishment, we've poured our heart into serving up old-fashioned food with old-fashioned prices. We believe in honest, delicious meals that bring people together, just like they used to.

While Tripadvisor highlights the diverse dining scene with 1699 traveler reviews across 58 Waxhaw restaurants, we're proud to be one that locals consistently choose, renowned for our welcoming atmosphere and, of course, the best hot dogs in town! Every dish is a taste of home, prepared with care and tradition, making every visit a memorable one.
