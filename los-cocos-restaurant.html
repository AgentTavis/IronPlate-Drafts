Our Journey

Experience the Authentic Flavors of Mexico

At Los Cocos Restaurant, we bring the vibrant spirit and rich culinary traditions of Mexico directly to Houston, NC. We believe in crafting dishes that tell a story, using fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Our commitment is to provide an authentic Mexican experience, not just a meal.

From the moment you step inside, you'll find a clean, calm, and welcoming environment, perfect for family dinners, a quick lunch, or a lively happy hour. Our friendly team is dedicated to ensuring every visit is memorable, making you feel right at home with genuine hospitality.
