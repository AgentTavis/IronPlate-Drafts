Our Story

A Taste of Family

Welcome to Little Kitchen, a cherished family-run bar and grill nestled in the heart of Union Grove, NC. For years, we've poured our passion into crafting delicious, home-style meals that bring people together. We believe in the simple joy of good food, generous portions, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Every dish is prepared with care, from our tender chopped meat barbeque sandwich to all the grill classics our customers rave about. Our commitment to quality and community shines through, making Little Kitchen more than just a restaurant – it's a place where memories are made around the table.

Our friendly team, often including the owner's daughter as your server, ensures every visit feels like coming home. We’re proud to be a local favorite, serving up hearty eats and good times to our wonderful Union Grove neighbors and visitors alike.
