Our Story

Generations of Flavor, Family, and Community.

Welcome to Little Kitchen, a true Union Grove gem where every meal is a family affair. As a cherished small, family-run business, we pride ourselves on serving up generous portions of delicious, home-style fare.

You'll often find the owner's daughter ensuring every guest feels like part of the family, providing warm, attentive service that makes your dining experience truly special. It’s more than just a meal; it’s a taste of our heritage and a warm welcome into our community.
