Our Legacy

The Pappas Bros. Promise: Where Every Detail Matters

Join us for an extraordinary evening of delicious Piedmont at Pappas Bros. Steakhouse Houston Galleria, with legendary Barolo estate Elio Grasso. Guests will experience nine exceptional wines, including Elio Grasso's distinctive Chardonnay and three vintages of Barolo drawn from iconic vineyard sites.

Our chef team has designed an intimate four-course dinner to enhance the wines while owner/winemaker, Gianluca Grasso, will guide guests through each wine. This dedication extends to every aspect of your dining experience, from our meticulously sourced ingredients to our attentive service. It's not just a meal; it's a celebration of culinary artistry and passionate hospitality, crafted over years of tradition.
