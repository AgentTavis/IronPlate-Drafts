Our Journey

More Than Just a Bottle Shop, It's a Community.

At B Squared, we believe in the power of good beer, fine wine, and great company. Nestled in the heart of Huntersville, NC, we've crafted a space where enthusiasts and newcomers alike can explore an unparalleled selection of craft beverages. We're not just selling drinks; we're fostering connections, one pour at a time.

Our passion began with a simple idea: bring the best craft beer and wine to our local community, creating a relaxed and inviting atmosphere where everyone feels at home. From our carefully curated taps to our extensive bottle selection, every item tells a story, and we're here to help you discover yours.
