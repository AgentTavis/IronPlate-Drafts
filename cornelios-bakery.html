Our Heritage

A Taste of Tradition, Crafted with Passion

At Cornelio's Bakery & Taqueria, we believe in the power of authentic Mexican flavors to bring people together. Nestled in the heart of Hendersonville, our family-owned establishment is a labor of love, dedicated to crafting traditional dishes and freshly baked goods that transport you straight to Mexico.

From the rich, savory Tlayudas to our delightful Reposteria, every item is made with the freshest ingredients and a deep commitment to culinary heritage. We pour our hearts into every creation, ensuring a taste experience that feels both familiar and exciting. Your trust in our passion for genuine Mexican cuisine is what drives us every day, and we're proud to serve our community with delicious, honest food.
