OUR STORY

A Little Slice of Heaven

Escape to a little slice of heaven today. There is nothing quite like the crunch of our homemade gorditas de pollo—made with love and served fresh, just the way abuela taught us. At Little Heaven Cafe, we believe in bringing you authentic Mexican-American dishes that taste like home.

Located on the tranquil property of our Hamptonville bed & breakfast, our café is more than just a place to eat; it's an experience. We pour our hearts into every handcrafted coffee and every exquisite dish, ensuring that each guest feels welcomed and leaves with a full belly and a happy heart. From the moment you step in, you're part of our family.
