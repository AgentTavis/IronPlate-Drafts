Our Journey

Where Every Sip Feels Like Home: The B & K Journey

Nestled at 129 E Granville St, # B, B & K Coffee Roasters is more than just a coffee shop – it's a dream come true for Brooke and Ken, and a vibrant new hub for the Windsor community. Since opening our doors, we've poured our hearts into creating a warm, welcoming space where neighbors can gather, relax, and savor exceptional beverages.

Our journey began with a simple passion: to bring the finest coffee, the freshest smoothies, and the most delightful frappes to our beloved town. Every bean is carefully selected, every fruit blend perfected, and every drink crafted with precision and care. We're proud to be a local business, built on community spirit and a commitment to quality that you can taste in every single sip.

Come on in and experience the B & K difference for yourself. We're here to make your day a little brighter, one delicious drink at a time. Thank you for making us a part of your daily routine!
