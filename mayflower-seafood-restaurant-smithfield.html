Our Story

A Legacy of Freshness and Flavor

At Mayflower Seafood Restaurant Smithfield, our journey began decades ago with a simple mission: to bring the freshest, most delicious seafood to the heart of North Carolina. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a beloved community cornerstone where families gather, friends share laughter, and every meal is crafted with passion.

We pride ourselves on sourcing quality ingredients, preparing each dish with time-honored recipes, and offering a warm, inviting atmosphere that makes everyone feel right at home. From our crispy Fried Platters to our savory Broiled Combinations, every plate tells a story of dedication to taste and tradition.

"Mayflower Seafood isn't just a meal; it's an experience. The flavors transport you straight to the coast, and the friendly service makes you feel like family. A true Smithfield gem!"
