Our Story

Crafted with Passion, Served with Heart

Welcome to Barley and Burger, where every meal is more than just food – it's an experience. Nestled in the heart of Rocky Mount, NC, our kitchen and bar are a carefully crafted haven designed to nurture the human spirit. We believe that a great burger, a perfectly paired beer, and a welcoming atmosphere can truly make a difference in your day.

We opened our doors with a simple mission: to create a bold yet refined dining destination. From the moment you step inside, you're not just a customer; you're family. We invite you to explore our creative menu with open arms, knowing that each dish is prepared with fresh, high-quality ingredients and a dash of genuine passion.

Our reputation for adventurous dining extends far beyond Rocky Mount, attracting food lovers from Roanoke Rapids, Wilson, and the surrounding areas. Whether you're a local seeking your next favorite meal or a visitor looking for a memorable taste of North Carolina, Barley and Burger is where your cravings meet their match.
