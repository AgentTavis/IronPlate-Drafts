Our Passion

Crafting Delicious Moments, Daily.

Experience the Warmth and Taste of Meli Bakehouse.

At Meli Bakehouse, we believe every meal should be an experience. Nestled in the heart of Mt Airy, NC, we're more than just a Bar & Grill; we're a community hub where fresh flavors meet friendly faces.

We are committed to excellence in everything we do — whether our service, ingredients, or our fresh pastries. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll feel the dedication to quality and a warm, inviting atmosphere that makes you feel right at home. We meticulously select the finest ingredients to ensure every dish, from our hearty grill options to our delicate, fresh-baked pastries, is a testament to our passion.

Come for a quick pickup, linger over a delicious meal, or simply grab a freshly baked treat. We're here to make your day a little sweeter and your plate a little fuller.
