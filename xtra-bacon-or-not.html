Our Story

Serving Up Smiles, One Plate at a Time.

Welcome to Xtra Bacon or Not, a charming mom & pop diner nestled in the heart of Saluda, SC. We're proud to be your local go-to spot for delicious food and a relaxed, casual atmosphere.

What started as a simple idea to serve great coffee, hearty breakfasts, and satisfying lunches has blossomed into a beloved community staple. We believe in big portions, fresh ingredients, and the kind of friendly service that makes you feel right at home.
