Our Legacy

More Than Just a Pub, It's Home.

For over six years, Chauncey's Pub has been a cornerstone of the Hillman community, a place where friends gather, stories are shared, and good times are always on the menu. We pride ourselves on creating an inviting atmosphere where everyone feels welcome, from regulars grabbing their usual to newcomers discovering their new favorite spot.

Our commitment is simple: great food, served quickly, in a comfortable setting. We believe a pub should be an extension of your living room – relaxed, friendly, and always ready with a delicious meal and a cold drink.
