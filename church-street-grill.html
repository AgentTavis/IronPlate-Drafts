OUR STORY

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Nashville Tradition.

At Church Street Grill, we believe in the simple pleasure of good food, great company, and a welcoming atmosphere. Nestled in the heart of Nashville, NC, we've been serving up classic American comfort food with a smile for years. Our kitchen is where fresh ingredients meet time-honored recipes, creating dishes that taste like home.

Whether you're stopping by for a hearty breakfast, a satisfying lunch, or a laid-back dinner, you'll find a spot at our table. We’re more than just a place to eat; we’re a cornerstone of the community, a gathering spot where friendships are forged and memories are made. Come as you are, and leave feeling like family.
