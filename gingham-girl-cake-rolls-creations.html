OUR STORY

Taste the Love, One Roll at a Time.

At Gingham Girl Cake Rolls & Creations, we believe in the simple, heartwarming joy of homemade. Every single cake roll is lovingly crafted from scratch, right here in Monroe, NC. We pour passion and the finest ingredients into each creation, bringing you a delightful twist on classic flavors and exciting new combinations.

Our cozy shop is a grab-and-go haven, where you can quickly pick up your favorite treats. The lady behind the counter? Super helpful and sweet, always ready to guide you through our impressive selection of over 20 different types of delicious cake rolls. We’re small, but our flavors are mighty and our welcome is always warm!
