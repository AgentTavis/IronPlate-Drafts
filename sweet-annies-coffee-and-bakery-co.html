Our Roots

More Than Just a Coffee Shop, It's Home.

At Sweet Annie's Coffee & Bakery Co., we believe in crafting more than just delicious food and drinks; we create a warm, inviting space where everyone feels like family. Nestled in the heart of Bayboro, NC, our diner is a community hub where friendly faces and the aroma of fresh coffee and baked goods fill the air.

Our journey began with a simple passion: to serve up amazing coffees and delectable pastries that brighten your day. From our unique coffee ice cubes that keep your cold brew perfectly strong to our welcoming atmosphere, every detail is designed to make you feel right at home. We're proud to be a local favorite, constantly striving to exceed expectations and create memorable moments for our cherished customers.
