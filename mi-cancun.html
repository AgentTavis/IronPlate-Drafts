Our Story

Experience the Warmth of Authentic Mexican Flavors in Pittsboro.

At Mi Cancun, we believe great food should be accompanied by a warm, inviting atmosphere and genuine hospitality. Our Pittsboro location offers a convenient escape where you can relax, enjoy our comfortable outside dining, and be served by our friendly and prompt staff.

We're proud to start every meal with our signature good tortilla chips, freshly made salsa, and a refreshing selection of cold drinks – the perfect beginning to your authentic Mexican culinary journey. Come taste the tradition and become part of our family!
