Our Story

Your Cozy Corner in Historic Apex

Welcome to Common Grounds Coffee House & Desserts, a charming blend of coffee shop, general store, and cozy retreat right in the heart of Historic Downtown Apex. We envisioned a place where community gathers, where every cup tells a story, and every dessert brings a smile.

Nestled among other wonderful shops, we're proud to be a local gem where convenience meets comfort. Whether you're grabbing a quick, delicious ham and cheese croissant on your way to work, catching up with friends over a warm latte, or settling in to get some tasks done, our doors are open seven days a week for you.

We believe in quality ingredients, friendly service, and creating a space that feels like home. From our wide selection of coffees and teas to our delightful pastries and creamy gelato, everything is crafted with care. We even love when you bring your reusable mug!
