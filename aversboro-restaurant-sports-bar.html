Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community Hub.

Nestled in the heart of Garner, Aversboro Restaurant & Sports Bar has been a beloved local staple for years. We're where friends gather, families celebrate, and sports fans cheer. With a legacy built on great food, friendly service, and an unbeatable atmosphere, we pride ourselves on being your go-to spot for any occasion.

From hearty breakfasts to satisfying lunches and lively dinners, our American, Bar, and Pub cuisine promises something for everyone. Come experience the warmth and energy that make Aversboro a true Garner tradition.
