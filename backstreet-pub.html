Our Roots

More Than Just a Pub, It's Home.

Nestled on Middle Lane in charming Beaufort, Backstreet Pub has been a local gem for years, a true 'bar and grill' where everyone feels like family. We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming atmosphere, whether you're a long-time Beaufort resident or just passing through.

Our spacious outdoor courtyard is truly where the magic happens. Lit by enchanting string lights every evening, it's the perfect spot to unwind, enjoy a cold drink, and soak in the Beaufort night. We're proud to be dog-friendly, always ready with a bowl of water for your furry companions – because good company extends to all members of the family.

Come for the cold beer and great company, stay for the live music and community spirit. We open earlier than most, ensuring there's always a welcoming spot waiting for you. Backstreet Pub is where memories are made, friendships are forged, and good times are always on tap.
