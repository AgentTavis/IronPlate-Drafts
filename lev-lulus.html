Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience

At Lev & Lulu's, we believe that good food, inspiring art, and a warm atmosphere are the ingredients for a perfect day. Nestled in the heart of downtown Weaverville, our diner is a cherished spot where creativity meets comfort food.

We're passionate about bringing joy to our community, whether it's through the newest art displayed on our walls or the delicious breakfast and lunch options served fresh daily. Our space is a vibrant canvas, constantly evolving with new artistic expressions to spark delight and conversation.

And yes, you absolutely can't leave without grabbing our incredibly "deelish GF scones" to go! They've become a beloved staple, embodying our commitment to quality and deliciousness for everyone.
