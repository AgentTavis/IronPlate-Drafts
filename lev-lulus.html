Our Journey

A Taste of Home in Weaverville

Welcome to Lev & Lulu's, a cozy diner nestled in the heart of downtown Weaverville, NC. More than just a place to eat, we're a space where art and comfort food come together. Inspired by the joy of creation, our walls often feature new art, providing a unique ambiance for your breakfast or lunch.

Our passion is to serve delightful meals that feel like home. We believe in good food, warm hospitality, and creating a community hub where everyone feels welcome. Don't forget to grab one of our delicious GF scones to go!
