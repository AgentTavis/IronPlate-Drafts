Our Story

Where Coastal Charm Meets Culinary Excellence.

Nestled on the picturesque intercoastal waterway of Sunset Beach, Oak & Palm offers more than just a meal – it's an experience. The views are simply stunning, with boats gently passing as the sun dips below the horizon, painting the sky with vibrant hues. Our mission is to provide an unforgettable dining experience that delights all your senses.

Our dedicated staff are attentive, friendly, and consistently go above and beyond, making every guest feel exceptionally well-treated. We believe in genuine hospitality that makes you feel like family, from the moment you step through our doors until your final delicious bite.

The food, of course, is nothing short of fabulous. Highlights include our fresh, succulent oysters, the ever-changing chef's specialty, and perfectly cooked steaks that cater to every palate. Our curated wine list complements each dish beautifully, and the complementary hush puppies with cinnamon and honey butter are a delightful, unexpected surprise that our guests adore. What truly sets Oak & Palm apart is its convenient waterfront location, offering an unparalleled dining experience that truly connects you with the beauty of Sunset Beach.

Our Menu

Taste The Coastal Difference.

Coconut Shrimp

Crispy, golden-fried shrimp coated in coconut flakes, served with a sweet and tangy dipping sauce. A tropical delight!

Sunset Style Blackened Grouper

Fresh grouper fillet perfectly seasoned and seared to a delicious crust, capturing the essence of the coast.

Pork Belly Scallops

Tender, pan-seared scallops paired with crispy, savory pork belly. A harmonious blend of land and sea flavors.

Oysters

Freshly shucked oysters, served on the half shell with your choice of classic mignonette or our signature cocktail sauce.

Chef's Specialty

Our chef's daily inspiration, featuring the freshest seasonal ingredients and innovative culinary techniques. Ask your server for today's creation.

Seared White Fish

Delicately seared white fish, light and flaky, served with seasonal vegetables and a lemon-herb butter sauce.
