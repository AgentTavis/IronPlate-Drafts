Our Roots

More Than Just a Brewery, It's a Beaufort Tradition.

At Mill Whistle Brewing, we're not just serving beer; we're serving up a piece of Beaufort's history and community spirit. Nestled in Carteret County, we proudly stand as the oldest microbrewery around, a true testament to our passion for craft and connection.

From the moment you step through our doors, you'll feel that welcoming, back-in-the-days vibe—where good neighbors gather, and local friends and family make memories. And yes, you might even hear our iconic factory whistle blow around 5 PM, a quirky tradition we'd love to tell you more about when you visit!

We're small in size but mighty in heart, offering not just "crazy good beers" but also delicious gourmet popcorn. Whether you prefer the cozy indoor atmosphere or the relaxed outdoor seating, Mill Whistle Brewing is truly a place where everyone feels at home.

The Mill Whistle Difference

Experience Beaufort's Favorite Local Gathering Spot

Our Unique Vibe

Step into a place that feels like home. Enjoy the nostalgic charm, friendly faces, and perhaps even the iconic 5 PM factory whistle that's unique to us. It's more than a bar; it's a community hub.

Dog-Friendly Patio & Outdoor Seating

Bring your furry friends along! Our spacious outdoor seating area welcomes dogs, making it the perfect spot to relax with your pet and enjoy Beaufort's beautiful weather and our amazing brews.

Beyond Beer: Vegetarian Options & Take-Out

While our craft beers shine, we cater to all! We offer many vegetarian options and the convenience of take-out and even delivery for those nights you want Mill Whistle at home. Reservations are also accepted!
