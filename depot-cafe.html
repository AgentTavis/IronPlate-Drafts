Our Heritage

Serving Lattimore with Heart & Home Cooking

Welcome to Depot Cafe, a charming eatery nestled right here at 202 Price St, Lattimore, NC. We're not just a restaurant; we're a gathering place, a slice of comfort, and a staple in our community. For years, we've poured our heart into every dish, creating homemade-style meals that bring folks back to the table, just like family.

Renowned for our tender cube steak and savory meatloaf, every plate we serve is a testament to generous portions and honest, reasonable prices. We believe good food doesn't have to break the bank. Our dedicated staff ensures swift service with a smile, making sure every patron enjoys a warm atmosphere, reminiscent of a cherished family gathering. Whether you're savoring a hearty Sunday lunch or a delicious weekday dinner, you're always welcome at Depot Cafe.
