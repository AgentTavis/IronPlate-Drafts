Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Memory.

Welcome to Depot Cafe, a charming eatery nestled at 202 Price St, Lattimore, NC. For years, we've been renowned for our comforting, homemade-style dishes that taste just like grandma used to make. This hidden gem has garnered rave reviews from locals and visitors alike, a testament to our dedication to honest, delicious food.

Diners can indulge in classics like our tender cube steak and savory meatloaf, all highlighted by generous portions and reasonable prices that won't break the bank. With a dedicated staff ensuring swift service, patrons enjoy a warm, friendly atmosphere reminiscent of a family gathering.

Whether you're savoring a hearty Sunday lunch or a delicious weekday treat, Depot Cafe promises a taste of home and a welcoming smile. We believe good food brings people together, and our kitchen works hard every day to create those unforgettable moments, one plate of comfort at a time.
