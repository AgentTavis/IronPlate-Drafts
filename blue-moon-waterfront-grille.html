More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience

Blue Moon Waterfront Grille is a beloved local spot in Nashville, NC, where delicious food meets a warm, welcoming atmosphere. We've poured our hearts into creating a place where friends and family can gather, share laughter, and enjoy expertly crafted dishes made with passion.

From our humble beginnings, we dreamed of a restaurant that truly felt like home, a place where every guest feels like family. We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality ingredients, friendly service, and a vibrant community spirit that makes every visit memorable.
