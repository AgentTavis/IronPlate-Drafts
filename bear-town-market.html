Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

At Bear Town Market, we believe in good times, great food, and even better company. Nestled in the heart of historic New Bern, our doors open to a lively atmosphere where the jukebox is always playing your favorite tunes and the air is filled with laughter and good cheer.

We're more than just a bar and grill; we're a community hub where friends gather, families celebrate, and memories are made, one delicious bite and one unforgettable night at a time. Come on down and discover why we're a local favorite.
