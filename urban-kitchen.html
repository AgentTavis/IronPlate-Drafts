Our Story

A Culinary Journey Rooted in Corolla

At Urban Kitchen, we believe in celebrating the rich bounty of the Outer Banks. Our journey began with a passion for fresh, local seafood and a desire to create a dining experience that feels both sophisticated and intimately familiar. Every dish is a tribute to the coastal flavors of North Carolina, crafted with care and the highest quality ingredients sourced from our community.

We're a small, family-run restaurant deeply connected to Corolla, striving to offer a cozy atmosphere where every guest feels welcomed and leaves with cherished memories. Our kitchen is where tradition meets innovation, turning the freshest catches into unforgettable culinary moments.
