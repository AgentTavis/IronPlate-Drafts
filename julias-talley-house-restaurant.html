Our Heritage

Where Every Meal Feels Like Coming Home.

For decades, Julia's Talley House Restaurant has been more than just a place to eat in Troutman, NC—it's been a cornerstone of our community. What started as a small, family-run eatery has grown into a beloved bar and grill, known for its warm hospitality, classic American comfort food, and vibrant atmosphere.

We believe in simple pleasures done exceptionally well. That means using fresh, quality ingredients in every dish, serving generous portions, and always greeting you with a friendly face. Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or catching up with friends over a drink, you'll find a welcoming spot at Julia's.

We're proud to be a local institution, creating memories and serving delicious food to generations of Troutman residents and visitors alike. Come on in and experience the taste of true local flavor and tradition.

Crispy Chicken Sandwich

Golden fried chicken breast, fresh slaw, and a tangy secret sauce on a toasted bun.

Loaded Nachos

Crispy tortilla chips piled high with seasoned beef, melted cheese, jalapeños, salsa, and sour cream.

Spicy BBQ Wings

Tender chicken wings tossed in our signature smoky and spicy BBQ sauce.

House Salad with Grilled Chicken

Fresh mixed greens, cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, and grilled chicken with your choice of dressing.

Fish and Chips

Crispy battered fish fillets served with golden fries and a side of tangy tartar sauce.

Sweet Potato Fries
