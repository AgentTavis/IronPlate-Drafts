Our Story

More Than Just Coffee – It's a Warm Welcome.

Step into French Express Coffee House and feel instantly at home. Our spacious and warm interior, designed with your comfort in mind, features five cozy tables perfect for gatherings of 3-4, alongside plush couches and armchairs by a charming fireplace.

We know you love to linger, so we've made sure to include plenty of convenient USB outlets throughout. Even with just two incredible staff members, we pride ourselves on welcoming every guest like an old friend and keeping our popular drive-thru flowing seamlessly all day long. Come for the coffee, stay for the comfort!
