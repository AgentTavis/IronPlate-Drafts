Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community Tradition.

Welcome to Circle II Restaurant, a beloved cornerstone of Elizabeth City where generations have gathered for heartwarming food and genuine hospitality. For years, we've prided ourselves on serving up 'all your favorite eats' – from classic American comfort food to delicious Southern staples and even a few flavorful Mexican-inspired delights. Our mission is simple: to provide a friendly, welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels like family.

We believe great food shouldn't break the bank. That's why we're committed to everyday small-town prices without compromising on quality or taste. Each dish is prepared with care, ensuring a satisfying experience that keeps locals and visitors coming back time and again. Come discover why Circle II is the natural choice for breakfast, lunch, and dinner in Elizabeth City.
