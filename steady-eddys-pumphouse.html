Our Story

More Than Just a Bar. It's a Community.

Nestled in the heart of Matthews, Steady Eddy's Pumphouse has quickly become the local favorite for those seeking the perfect blend of delicious cuisine, refreshing drinks, and an unparalleled atmosphere. We're proud to be more than just a watering hole; we're a community hub where great food and even better people come together.

Whether you're unwinding in our cozy basement lounge, catching the game on our big screen TVs, or enjoying the fresh air on our spacious outdoor patio, we've crafted a unique experience for everyone. Don't just take our word for it—come see why Steady Eddy's is the pulse of Matthews.
