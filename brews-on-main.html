Our Heritage

Our Passion for Craft Beer & Community

We believe in the heart and soul of our local downtowns. That's why we've poured our passion into creating not just one, but three distinct BREWS locations across South Carolina. Each one is designed to showcase the unique BREWS personality, our unwavering commitment to a special customer experience, and our deep love for craft beer.

Every family-friendly taproom offers its own unique, fun, and upbeat atmosphere. And no matter which BREWS you visit, you can always expect an incredible variety of fresh, rotating craft beers on tap. Here at BREWS on Main in Anderson, you'll discover an unparalleled selection of over 200 craft beers, perfect for enjoying right here or taking home to savor later.
