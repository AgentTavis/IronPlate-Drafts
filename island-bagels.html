Our Legacy

More Than Just Bagels, It's a Taste of Carolina Beach.

Island Bagels is a beloved local spot in Carolina Beach, NC, a community staple where the aroma of freshly baked bagels fills the air every morning. We believe in simple pleasures done exceptionally well, starting with our dedication to crafting the perfect bagel, boiled and baked to a golden perfection.

Our journey began with a passion for bringing authentic, New York-style bagels with an island twist to our vibrant coastal town. We're more than just a place to grab breakfast; we're a gathering spot where neighbors connect, families start their day, and visitors discover a true taste of Carolina Beach hospitality.

From classic plain bagels to savory breakfast sandwiches and locally roasted coffee, every item on our menu is prepared with care and served with a smile. We’re proud to be a part of what makes Carolina Beach special, and we invite you to experience the warmth and flavor that only Island Bagels can offer.
