Welcome to Almost on the Lake

Nestled in the picturesque town of Wilkesboro, Almost on the Lake brings the charm and serenity of waterside dining right to your table. Our restaurant celebrates the natural beauty of the region with carefully curated dishes made from the freshest local ingredients.

Whether you're gathering with family, enjoying a romantic dinner, or celebrating a special occasion, our warm atmosphere and exceptional service create memorable experiences that last long after your last bite.

Inspired by the tranquil waters nearby, our culinary team crafts innovative dishes that honor traditional flavors while embracing modern creativity. Every visit is an invitation to slow down, savor the moment, and connect with those who matter most.
