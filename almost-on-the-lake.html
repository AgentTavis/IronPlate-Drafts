Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Wilkesboro Tradition

Located just a stone's throw from the serene waters, Almost On the Lake is nestled at 3600 W NC Hwy 268. Whether you're a local seeking your go-to spot or a visitor discovering Wilkesboro's charm, our welcoming vibe is designed to make you feel right at home.

We believe in good food, good drinks, and even better company. Our doors are open to everyone looking for a relaxed atmosphere, where the laughter is as free-flowing as our cold beers, and every dish is prepared with a touch of down-home comfort.

Come on in, pull up a chair, and experience why we're a beloved gathering place for friends and family alike. At Almost on The Lake, every visit is a chance to make a new memory.
