OUR STORY

Where Every Bite Feels Like Home

Located just a stone's throw from the lake, Almost On the Lake is nestled at 3600 W NC Hwy 268 in Wilkesboro, NC. For years, we've been more than just a restaurant; we're a gathering place where memories are made over plates of delicious American comfort food.

Whether you're a local seeking your go-to spot or a visitor discovering the charm of Wilkesboro, our welcoming vibe and hearty American fare make it the perfect place to unwind and enjoy good company. We take pride in being a community staple, serving up signature comfort food and a fantastic beer selection that keeps folks coming back season after season.

We believe in simple pleasures done right – great food, good drinks, and even better company. Come on down and experience the warmth and flavors that make us Almost On the Lake.
