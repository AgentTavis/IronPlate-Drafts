Welcome to Almost on the Lake

Nestled in the charming town of Wilkesboro, North Carolina, Almost on the Lake offers a unique culinary experience that celebrates both local flavors and timeless cuisine.

Our commitment to excellence begins with sourcing the finest ingredients from local farms and regional suppliers. Each dish is carefully crafted by our talented chefs, who bring passion and creativity to every plate.

Whether you're planning an intimate dinner for two, a family celebration, or a special corporate event, our warm atmosphere and attentive service ensure an unforgettable experience. We pride ourselves on creating moments that matter.

Come discover why Almost on the Lake is Wilkesboro's premier destination for fine dining.
