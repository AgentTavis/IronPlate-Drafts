About Us

Experience Waterfront Dining Like Never Before

Almost on the Lake brings you the finest culinary experience with breathtaking views of our pristine waterfront. Our chef-curated menu celebrates fresh, locally-sourced ingredients combined with innovative cooking techniques.

Nestled in the heart of Wilkesboro, we pride ourselves on exceptional service, an inviting atmosphere, and dishes that tell a story. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a casual evening with loved ones, Almost on the Lake is your perfect destination.

Join us for an unforgettable experience where land meets water, and culinary art meets natural beauty.
