About Us

Welcome to Almost on the Lake, Wilkesboro's premier waterfront dining destination. Nestled in the heart of North Carolina, our restaurant offers an exceptional culinary experience with breathtaking views and warm hospitality.

We pride ourselves on serving fresh, locally-sourced ingredients prepared with care and creativity. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a casual meal with friends and family, Almost on the Lake provides the perfect ambiance and cuisine for your gathering.

Our team is dedicated to delivering outstanding service and memorable dining experiences that keep our guests coming back for more.
