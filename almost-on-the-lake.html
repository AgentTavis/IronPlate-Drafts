Our Story

Welcome to Almost on the Lake, where scenic beauty meets culinary excellence. Nestled in the heart of Wilkesboro, North Carolina, our restaurant offers an unforgettable dining experience with breathtaking views and exceptional cuisine.

Founded with a passion for bringing communities together over remarkable food, we pride ourselves on using locally-sourced ingredients, sustainable practices, and authentic recipes that celebrate the rich flavors of the region.

Whether you're planning an intimate dinner, a family gathering, or a special celebration, Almost on the Lake is your destination for memorable moments and exceptional hospitality.
