Our Journey

Crafting Unforgettable Moments, One Sip at a Time.

At 8th Wonder, we believe in creating more than just beverages – we craft experiences. Nestled in the heart of Houston, NC, our brewery and cannabis bar is a vibrant hub where community thrives and innovation flows. We started with a passion for exceptional craft brewing, perfecting unique flavors that captivate the palate.

Our evolution into a cannabis-inclusive space reflects our commitment to offering a diverse array of carefully curated options for relaxation and enjoyment. From our refreshing Wonder Water THC Seltzers to our delightful Lil Bit Gummies, every product is designed to elevate your moments with quality and care. We're a place where good company meets great taste, welcoming all who seek a distinctive escape.
