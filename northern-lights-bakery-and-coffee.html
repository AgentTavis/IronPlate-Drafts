Our Heritage

Crafting Joy, One Bite at a Time

Nestled in the heart of Corolla, Northern Lights Bakery And Coffee is more than just a bakeshop—it's a family tradition, a warm embrace, and a beacon for fresh, homemade delights. We've poured our hearts into creating a cozy haven where every visit feels like coming home.

From the moment the sun rises, our ovens are working tirelessly, baking up batches of our renowned homemade donuts and flaky artisan pastries. Paired with our gourmet coffee, it's the perfect start to any Corolla day. But our passion doesn't stop at breakfast.

We also craft fresh, delicious sandwiches for lunch and specialize in custom cakes that turn every special occasion into an unforgettable celebration. At Northern Lights, quality ingredients, time-honored recipes, and a dash of family love are in everything we make.
