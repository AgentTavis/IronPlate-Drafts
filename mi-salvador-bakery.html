Our Story

Bringing a Taste of El Salvador to Salisbury

At Mi Salvador Bakery, we believe in the magic of traditional flavors and the warmth of a freshly baked treat. Born from a passion for authentic Salvadoran cuisine, our bakery and grill is a family dream come true, right here in Salisbury, NC.

Every loaf of bread, every sweet pastry, and every savory dish is crafted with love, using time-honored recipes and the freshest ingredients. We're more than just a place to eat; we're a gathering spot where friends and family can share smiles over delicious food, experiencing a piece of Salvadoran culture with every bite.

Our Delights

Baked Fresh, Served with Love

From sweet Salvadoran classics to savory everyday favorites, our menu offers a taste of home, prepared with authentic recipes and the finest ingredients.

Tres Leches Cake

Our signature dessert! A heavenly sponge cake soaked in three kinds of milk, topped with a delicate whipped cream. Simply irresistible.

Freshly Baked Breads

Discover our daily selection of artisan breads, from classic bolillos to sweet Salvadoran pan dulce. Perfect for any meal or on its own.

Cheesecakes

Rich, creamy, and decadent cheesecakes in a variety of rotating flavors. A perfect indulgence for any sweet tooth.

Traditional Pupusas

Handmade corn tortillas filled with a savory blend of cheese, beans, or chicharrón. Served with curtido and salsa for an authentic experience.

Sweet & Savory Empanadas

Flaky pastries filled with sweet plantains, creamy milk, or savory meats. A delightful snack any time of day.

Artisan Coffee & Drinks

Pair your pastry or meal with our freshly brewed coffee or a refreshing agua fresca. The perfect complement to our flavors.

Please visit us in-store for our daily specials and full menu offerings!
