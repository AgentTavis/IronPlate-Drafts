Our Journey

More Than Just Coffee – It's a Community Hub.

At The Coffee Scene Prince Charles, we're proud to be a family-owned and operated café nestled in the heart of Fayetteville, NC. Our passion is crafting exceptional coffee and serving delightful gelato and diner classics that bring people together.

Every cup of coffee is brewed with meticulously sourced beans, and our gelato is made fresh with real ingredients, ensuring a taste that transports you. We believe in the power of local connection, providing a welcoming space where friends can gather, creatives can work, and families can make memories.

We invite you to experience the warmth of our hospitality, the rich aroma of our coffee, and the sweet indulgence of our gelato. Shop local, support your community, and #drinkTCS!
