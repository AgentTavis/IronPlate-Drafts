Our Heritage

Our Family. Our Tradition. Your Table.

At El Vaquero Food Restaurant, we bring the heart and soul of authentic Mexican cuisine to Newton, NC. Located at 1901 Northwest Blvd A, we are dedicated to offering a delightful dining experience with a focus on the rich, traditional flavors of Hidalgo.

Every dish is crafted with passion, from our signature barbacoa and consomé to our handcrafted tacos and quesadillas. We believe in family, community, and the power of great food to bring people together. Come and experience a taste of Mexico, right here in Newton.
