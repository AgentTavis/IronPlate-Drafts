Our Roots

Taste the Tradition, Feel the Family.

At El Vaquero Food Restaurant, we believe food is more than just sustenance—it's a celebration of heritage, flavor, and community. Nestled in Newton, NC, we bring the vibrant tastes of authentic Mexican cuisine right to your table.

Our journey began with a simple passion: to share the rich culinary traditions of Hidalgo, Mexico. From our slow-cooked barbacoa to our comforting consomé, every dish is prepared with the same love and authentic recipes passed down through generations. We're proud to serve our community across two convenient locations, making it easier for you to experience a truly delightful dining experience.
