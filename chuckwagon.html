Our Legacy

The Heart of Rocky Mount's Comfort Food Scene

Welcome to Chuckwagon, where every meal is a journey back to classic diner comfort. We pride ourselves on creating dishes that warm the soul and bring a smile to your face. From the moment you step through our double doors, you'll feel the inviting atmosphere that makes us a true local favorite.

There's ample parking right out front, making your visit easy and stress-free. While you wait for your food – because all items are made to order here, and it's always worth the wait – you can relax in one of our cozy spots, perhaps even our cool old school rocking chair. We believe in fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes, ensuring every bite is packed with killer flavor.

For breakfast, you absolutely have to try our famous Wagon Train Sandwich: double bacon, a perfectly fried egg, and our secret pimento spread, all nestled in a fresh Kaiser roll. It’s a flavor combo you won't soon forget. And don't even think about leaving without a side order of our crispy, golden hash browns – they're legendary!
