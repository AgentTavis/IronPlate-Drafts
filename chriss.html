Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community Tradition.

Chris's is a charming eatery located in Graham, NC, known for its warm atmosphere and friendly service. More than just a restaurant, we're a community gathering place where every meal feels like a homecoming.

Our menu features a delightful mix of down-home comfort food and timeless classics, all crafted with love and a commitment to quality ingredients. From our famous cheeseburgers, now a finalist for Best Burger in the Times News Readers Choice Awards 2025, to our scrumptious shrimp plates and delightful desserts, we've got something to satisfy every craving.

For over a decade, we've been dedicated to providing a friendly and inviting dining experience, making Chris's a true haven in the heart of Graham. Come visit us and become part of our story.
