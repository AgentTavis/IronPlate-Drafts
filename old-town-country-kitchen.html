Our Heritage

A Taste of Home in Historic Bath, NC

At Old Town Country Kitchen, we believe in serving up more than just a meal – we serve a slice of Bath, NC life. Our kitchen is a place where delicious food meets genuine community spirit. Visitors often rave about our quick service, generously portioned meals, and the lively, friendly atmosphere where locals and travelers alike connect over a good meal.

From our perfectly crafted omelets to hearty country breakfasts, every dish is made with care, ensuring you get a taste that feels like home, every time. We're proud to be a staple in Bath, bringing comfort food and good company to your table.
