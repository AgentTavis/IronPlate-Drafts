Our Heritage

Bringing Delicious Flavors & Community Together in Statesville.

At King Buffet, we believe in crafting more than just meals; we craft experiences. For years, we've been a beloved local spot in Statesville, offering a vibrant atmosphere where families and friends can gather to enjoy an incredible variety of delicious food.

Our passion lies in serving up a diverse selection that caters to every palate, from classic American grill favorites to a tantalizing array of Asian-inspired dishes. We’re committed to fresh ingredients, generous portions, and creating a welcoming environment for everyone who walks through our doors.

We're proud to be a part of the Statesville community and strive every day to be your go-to destination for great food and even better memories.
