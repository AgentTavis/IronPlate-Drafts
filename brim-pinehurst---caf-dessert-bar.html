Our Journey

Pinehurst's Living Room for Every Season

BRIM Pinehurst is more than just a café and dessert bar; it's Pinehurst's living room. We envisioned a cozy, community-centered gathering spot where friends and family could connect over exceptional food and drinks.

We pride ourselves on offering artisan coffee crafted with passion, an array of delectable desserts baked with love, and hearty, scratch-made meals that feel like a warm hug. Whether you're starting your day with us, enjoying a lunch break, or unwinding in the evening, we aim to make you feel right at home.

We're here for you Monday through Thursday, 8am-5pm, and late on Friday and Saturday until 8pm, with Sunday hours from 8am-3pm. Keep an eye out for our 'She's Back!' specials and delicious Spring Drink selections! Though off-season hours are here, our commitment to quality and community never changes.
