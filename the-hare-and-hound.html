OUR STORY

A Taste of Landrum's Charm

Welcome to The Hare and Hound, Landrum's beloved Bar & Grill. We're a place where community gathers, and every meal feels like a celebration. Our passion is crafting delicious food in a friendly, welcoming atmosphere that makes you feel right at home.

We're incredibly proud of what we've built, a spot locals and visitors alike rave about. From our great, huge salads to our perfectly grilled favorites, every dish is made with care. We loved this place so much and what we had (great salads-huge!) so much we came back twice. Friendly staff, delicious food, & locals with super-well behaved & snuggly pups—we just loved it here!

Glad for friends & fellow Yelpers with good advice to try us (also, we're the only place hopping on Easter besides the Bunny, who also stopped by for a photo op if we had wanted one!) Sweet!
