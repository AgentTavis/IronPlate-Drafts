OUR STORY

More Than Just a Meal

At The Hare and Hound, we believe in creating more than just a meal – we craft experiences. Nestled in the heart of Landrum, NC, we've become a beloved local gem, known for our genuinely friendly staff, mouth-watering dishes, and the warm, welcoming atmosphere that makes everyone feel right at home.

It's a place where families gather, friends laugh, and even our furry, four-legged regulars are welcome. Come discover why The Hare and Hound is Landrum's go-to for delicious food and unforgettable moments. We're proud to be a part of this community, serving up comfort and cheer, one plate at a time.
