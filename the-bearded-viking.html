Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community

The Bearded Viking isn't just a bar and grill in King, NC—it's a vibrant gathering place built on the spirit of good company, hearty food, and memorable nights. Nestled at 643 E King St, we've become a cherished local hotspot where the laughter flows as freely as our cold brews.

From our welcoming atmosphere to our commitment to serving up delicious meals and fantastic drinks, we strive to make every visit an experience worth sharing. We're proud to be a local favorite, known for our friendly staff, lively ambiance, and being the perfect spot to unwind until the early hours.
