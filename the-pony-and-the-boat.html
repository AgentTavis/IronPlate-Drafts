Our Journey

The Heart & Soul of Comfort Food in Kitty Hawk

The Pony and the Boat Comfort Kitchen elevates comfort food by focusing on high-quality ingredients and scratch-made preparation with no shortcuts. We pour our heart into every dish, bringing creative flair to soul-nourishing comfort food favorites like our legendary pot pie, crispy fried chicken, creamy mac & cheese, tender pot roast, and flavorful shrimp & grits. Every item on our menu, including our daily specials, is a testament to our dedication.

Our unique entrée and two sides concept ensures that our crispy Brussels sprouts, twice-baked potatoes, warm cornbread, and fresh cole slaw are given as much prominence and care as our main dishes. It's comfort food, elevated, with no shortcuts – just honest, delicious cooking made with love for our community in Kitty Hawk.
