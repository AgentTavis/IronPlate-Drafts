Our Journey

Crafting Unique Flavors, One Sip at a Time in Rockport.

Welcome to Winery on the Bay – your destination for exquisite wines with a twist of artisanal craftsmanship. Nestled in the heart of historic Rockport, Texas, our boutique winery prides itself on creating unique blends that capture the essence of different vineyards from around the globe.

But that’s not all – our recent venture into Dog'Gone Brewery introduces a whole new level of innovation, where we turn luscious fruit juices into exceptional wines that are bound to tantalize your taste buds. We believe in tradition meeting innovation, resulting in unforgettable experiences for every palate.

Our passion for quality and creativity drives us to explore new horizons in winemaking and brewing, ensuring that every bottle and pint tells a story of dedication, flavor, and the vibrant spirit of Rockport.
