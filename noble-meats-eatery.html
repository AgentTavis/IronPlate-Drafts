Our Story

Generations of Flavor, Smoked with Passion

At Noble Meats & Eatery, we’re more than just a restaurant; we’re a Spring Lake tradition, proudly serving our community since 1987. What began as a custom butcher shop grew into a beloved barbecue destination, driven by a passion for quality meats and the time-honored art of slow smoking.

Every cut, every rub, every hour spent in the smoker is a testament to our commitment to authentic flavor. From tender brisket to succulent pulled pork, we believe in crafting dishes that bring people together, creating memories around the table.

Come experience the legacy of Noble Meats & Eatery, where tradition meets taste, and every meal is prepared with decades of expertise and a whole lot of heart.

Why Noble Meats?

The Taste of True Southern BBQ

Crafted with Care Since 1987

For over three decades, Noble Meats has been Spring Lake's trusted name for quality. Our legacy is built on custom butchery and the time-honored tradition of authentic barbecue, ensuring every plate tells a story.

Premium Cuts, Perfect Smoke

As a custom butcher shop, we start with the finest meats. Our pitmasters then slow-smoke them to perfection, delivering rich, tender, and undeniably flavorful BBQ. Taste the difference in our Brisket and Pulled Pork!

A Community Favorite
