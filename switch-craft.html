Our Journey

Experience the Craft. Taste the Passion.

Welcome to Switch Craft, your local Bar and Grill in the heart of Holly Springs. We believe that great food and exceptional drinks are best enjoyed in a welcoming atmosphere, shared with friends and family.

From our carefully selected wines to our creative cocktails, and a menu crafted to satisfy every palate, we make every effort to cater to everyone as well as we can. We're constantly evolving our offerings, recently adding more gluten-free and non-alcoholic options, ensuring there’s something special for every guest.

We value our customers and make every effort to accommodate any special requests so you can truly enjoy your visit. Come discover why we're a local favorite!

Our Offerings

Sip, Savor, & Share the Switch Craft Experience.

Discover a curated selection of crafted beverages and delicious bites at Switch Craft. While we continuously update our full menu, here's a glimpse into the diverse flavors we're passionate about.

Craft Cocktails

Our bar boasts an ever-evolving list of handcrafted cocktails, from classic favorites to innovative concoctions. Expertly mixed for your enjoyment.

Fine Wines & Beers

Explore our selection of premium wines, carefully chosen to complement any dish, alongside a rotating tap list of local and regional craft beers.

Savory Bar Bites

Beyond drinks, our kitchen serves up elevated bar and grill fare – perfect for sharing, or as a satisfying meal on its own. Quality ingredients, unforgettable flavors.

Gluten-Free Options

We're dedicated to catering to all dietary needs. Ask your server about our delicious gluten-free selections, always expanding as new options become available.

Nonalcoholic Delights

Enjoy our thoughtful array of nonalcoholic beverages, from refreshing mocktails to specialty sodas, ensuring everyone can toast to a great time.

Seasonal Specials

Our menu features exciting seasonal specials, showcasing the freshest ingredients and creative culinary twists. Always something new to discover!

Our Promise

Why Switch Craft is Your New Local Favorite.

Exceptional Craftsmanship

From our meticulously crafted cocktails and diverse wine list to the savory bar bites, every item reflects our dedication to quality and flavor. As a claimed "Wine Bar" and "Cocktail Bar", we pour passion into every glass.

We believe everyone should enjoy a great meal out. Our recent additions of gluten-free and nonalcoholic options, as highlighted by our customers, ensure a welcoming experience for all dietary preferences and choices.
