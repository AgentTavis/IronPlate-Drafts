Our Story

Serving Hearty Meals, Building Community.

Pinto's Grill is more than just a place to eat; it's a cornerstone of the Lawsonville community. For years, we've been dedicated to serving up delicious, comforting barbecue and grill favorites in a warm, friendly atmosphere. We believe in good food, great company, and the kind of hospitality that makes everyone feel like family.

From our family to yours, we put passion into every dish, using quality ingredients and time-honored recipes that bring people back again and again. Come on down and experience the true taste of local flavor and genuine Southern charm.
