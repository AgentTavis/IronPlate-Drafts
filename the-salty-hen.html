Our Story

From a Community Vision to Your Table

The Salty Hen brings wholesome comfort food and unparalleled service to Somerset, NC. Nestled in what was once the beloved Old Falcon Ridge General Store building, we've poured our hearts into creating a truly special dining experience.

As the latest addition to the area's culinary scene, we're proud to be a mom-and-pop establishment where every guest feels like family. Our focus is on serving great food, crafted with care, and ensuring amazing service that makes every visit memorable.
