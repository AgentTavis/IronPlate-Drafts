OUR STORY

Rooted in Tradition, Crafted with Love

Nestled in the heart of Cody Creek, The Depot Restaurant has been a beloved cornerstone of Southern hospitality for over 25 years. Co-owner Kathy Snow, alongside her husband Freddy, passionately oversees the restaurant, creating a relaxed and inviting atmosphere where every guest feels like family.

Our menu is a celebration of Southern staples, featuring hearty ribs, tender steaks, succulent chicken, and fresh seafood, complemented by crisp salads. Sundays are a special occasion, offering an authentic home-style spread with comforting pot roast, classic meatloaf, crispy fried chicken, warm cornbread, and a vibrant array of seasonal vegetables. And don't forget to save room for dessert – Kathy's talented daughter, Kayla, bakes delicious creations daily that are the perfect sweet ending to any meal.

The unique charm of The Depot is woven into its very fabric, literally. The restaurant proudly bears its name from the Sanford Depot, where Freddy himself meticulously sourced many of the historical parts and pieces that beautifully adorn our dining space, making every visit a journey through cherished traditions.
