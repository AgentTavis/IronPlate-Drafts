OUR STORY

Discover Burlington's Best Kept Secret

Del Sol Cafe is truly a hidden gem nestled right here in Burlington, NC. Our mission is to bring you an extraordinary coffee and dining experience that brightens your day.

We pride ourselves on our delicious coffee, offering a range of customizable options, including our renowned homemade Horchata latte. You'll also find other delightful seasonal coffee offerings, all made with care and available with or without dairy, including our rare and sought-after cold foam.

Beyond our exquisite beverages, we offer a diverse selection of breakfast and takeout options. Every dish is crafted with high-quality ingredients, ensuring a taste that keeps our customers coming back. We're often bustling with calls for orders, a testament to the community's love for our fresh, flavorful food. Come see why Del Sol Cafe is a local favorite!
