Our Journey

Our Commitment: A New Chapter in Cana

At The Copper Pot, we're deeply rooted in the Cana community, building on a legacy and striving to create a dining experience that truly shines. We believe in transparency and continuous improvement, and we value every piece of feedback we receive.

As one guest recently shared their experience, echoing thoughts about our past as 'The Hungry Farmer': "We ordered beef tips and received an over cooked, tough as nails, cut up steak with steak seasoning on it. There was no rice, no sauce. You could see grill marks; it was not slow cooked. We asked our waitress if this was maybe not what we ordered. She said it was and walked away while I had an empty sweet tea. We drank my husband's and then he had to go to the wait station to ask for refills. Sweet tea was great! Unfortunately, this place is not even close to what it was as the Hungry Farmer."

We're committed to learning from every interaction and evolving to meet and exceed your expectations. Our kitchen is constantly working to perfect our dishes, ensuring quality, flavor, and the hearty satisfaction you expect from a local bar and grill.
