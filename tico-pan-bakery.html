Our Journey

More Than Just Food, It's a Taste of Home.

At Tico Pan Bakery & Grill, we're dedicated to bringing the freshest, most delicious baked goods and hearty grill favorites to Lincolnton, NC, and beyond. What started as a passion for traditional recipes and the joy of sharing good food has grown into a beloved community hub.

Every loaf of bread, every pastry, and every grill item is crafted with love and the highest quality ingredients. We believe in honest flavors, warm welcomes, and creating moments that bring people together, whether you're dining with us or enjoying our nationwide shipping.
