Our Story

Crafting Unforgettable Moments, One Board at a Time.

At The Silver Corkscrew, we believe in the simple joy of good company, fine wine, and exceptional food. Nestled in the heart of Burlington, NC, we've created a cozy retreat where friends and family can gather to unwind and indulge.

Our passion lies in serving amazing Charcuterie and Cheese boards, thoughtfully curated with the finest selections to tantalize your palate. Complement your experience with Fresh Crisp Salads, our daily Artisan Breads, and a vast array of French, Italian, and California wine selections. Every visit is an invitation to discover new favorites and create lasting memories.
