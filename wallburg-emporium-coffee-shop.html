OUR STORY

Our Passion, Your Perfect Cup.

Welcome to Wallburg Emporium & Coffee Shop, a cozy haven nestled inside the fabulous Evenbrooke Marketplace in Lexington, NC. We believe in crafting delightful experiences, one cup at a time. Our philosophy is simple: use quality ingredients to create beverages that warm your soul and brighten your day.

From the moment you step in, you'll feel the inviting atmosphere we've cultivated – a perfect spot for a quick coffee run, a relaxed chat, or a moment of peace. We take pride in our unique concoctions and the joy they bring to our wonderful community. We’re more than just a coffee shop; we’re a community hub where delicious drinks and friendly faces come together.

OUR MENU

Signature Drinks to Delight Your Senses.

While we offer a rotating selection of fresh brews and treats, these two customer favorites are always a hit. Come discover what makes them so special!

Iced Hand Warmer

A delightful paradox! Our signature blend of rich chocolate, warm cinnamon, and sweet brown sugar, perfectly balanced with our exquisite coffee, served over ice. It's a cozy feeling, even when it's cold – a perfect balance of syrup to coffee.

Iced Turtle Latte

Indulge in this decadent creation featuring a harmonious fusion of luscious caramel and rich chocolate with our premium coffee, served chilled. A truly blissful experience for your taste buds, with just the right amount of coffee flavor.

WHY CHOOSE US
