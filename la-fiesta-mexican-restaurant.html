Our Culinary Delights

Taste the Tradition: Our Signature Mexican Dishes.

Tacos al Pastor

Marinated pork with pineapple, onions, and cilantro on soft corn tortillas.

Enchiladas Suizas

Chicken enchiladas smothered in a creamy green tomatillo sauce and melted cheese.

Carnitas Plate

Slow-cooked, tender pork served with rice, beans, and warm tortillas.

Chile Relleno

Roasted poblano pepper stuffed with cheese, lightly battered and fried, topped with sauce.
