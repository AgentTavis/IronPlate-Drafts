Our Journey

Taste the Aloha Spirit in Havelock, NC

Welcome to BokaNinas Pacific Island Fusion, a culinary haven in Havelock, NC, founded by Rosette Cruz Singleton. More than just a restaurant, BokaNinas is a passion project born from a deep love for hospitality and food. As a family and veteran-owned establishment, we pour our heart into every dish, bringing the vibrant flavors and warm spirit of the Pacific Islands right to your plate.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to share authentic, delicious Pacific Island cuisine blended with a classic American bar and grill comfort. From our humble beginnings as a food truck to our inviting restaurant space, we've grown alongside the Havelock community, always prioritizing fresh ingredients, friendly service, and a truly unforgettable dining experience. We believe food is love, and we invite you to taste that love in every bite.
