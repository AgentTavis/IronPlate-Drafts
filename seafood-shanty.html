Our Heritage

Generations of Seafood Passion

At Seafood Shanty, we believe delicious seafood should always be an easy and satisfying choice. When you're craving fresh, quality fare without the fuss, our fried fish carryout is your perfect destination, offering a delightful escape from the usual fast-food options.

We pride ourselves on a curated assortment of seafood, including exquisitely prepared shrimp – always minimal breading, meticulously deveined – and succulent fried oysters that are a true customer favorite. While we focus on our specialty items that bring you the best value and flavor, every dish is crafted with care to ensure a memorable meal.
