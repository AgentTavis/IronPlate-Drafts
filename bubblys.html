Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

Bubblys is a charming eatery located in Hickory, NC, known for its warm atmosphere and delicious menu. Offering a variety of flavorful dishes made from the freshest ingredients, we blend traditional Asian flavors with classic bar and grill comfort.

Our passion is to create a welcoming space where friends and family can gather, share great food, and make lasting memories. Come discover why Bubblys is a true Hickory gem and experience a culinary journey that tantalizes your taste buds.

Our Menu

Our Flavorful Creations

Bibimbap Bowl

$13.50

A vibrant and hearty rice bowl topped with seasoned vegetables, a choice of protein (beef, chicken, or tofu), and a fried egg, mixed with our signature gochujang sauce.

Teriyaki Chicken Bowl

$12.00

Grilled chicken glazed with our savory-sweet house-made teriyaki sauce, served over steamed rice with fresh, crisp vegetables.

Creamy Chicken

$16.00

Tender chicken breast in a rich, velvety cream sauce, infused with delicate herbs and spices, served with a side of steamed rice or noodles.

Banh Mi (Sandwich)

$10.00

A classic Vietnamese sandwich featuring savory marinated pork, crisp pickled vegetables, fresh cilantro, and jalapeños, all nestled in a crusty baguette.

Pork Tonkotsu

$16.50

Authentic Japanese ramen with a rich, milky pork bone broth, tender slices of chashu pork, a soft-boiled egg, nori, and fresh scallions.

Bubble Tea

$7.75

Refresh yourself with our popular, customizable bubble tea. Choose from a variety of flavors and toppings for your perfect sweet treat.
