Our Roots

From Our Family Kitchen to Your Table

At Gunter's in the Grove, we believe in the simple joy of a truly home-cooked meal. Our kitchen is an extension of our family's table, offering everything from hearty individual dishes to a bountiful buffet experience.

Patrons rave about our delicious buffet, especially the perfectly seasoned, home-cooked chicken – a true taste of Southern comfort right here in Harmony, NC. We're dedicated to serving meals that are not just delicious, but also bring people together, creating lasting memories around good food.
