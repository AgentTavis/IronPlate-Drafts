Our Legacy, Our Flavor

From Our Pit to Your Plate: The Fat Jack's Journey

At Fat Jack's Pit Cooked BBQ, we believe that real BBQ isn't just food; it's a tradition, a passion, and a labor of love. We've just opened our doors here in Kannapolis, bringing a lifetime of pit-smoking expertise and a dedication to authentic, mouth-watering flavor to our community.

Every cut of meat is slow-cooked over hickory and oak, tenderly smoked to perfection until it falls off the bone. We use only the freshest local ingredients, transforming them into classic BBQ dishes that tell a story with every bite. From our savory pulled pork to our smoky ribs, each dish is a testament to the true art of barbecue.
