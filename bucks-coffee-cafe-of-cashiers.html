Our Journey

More Than Just Coffee, It's a Community

Nestled in the heart of Cashiers, NC, Bucks Coffee Cafe is where locals and visitors alike gather for their daily dose of warmth and flavor. We're proud to serve up more than just delicious drinks and hearty meals; we're a bustling hub where every cup is crafted with care and every guest feels welcome.

From our meticulously handcrafted espresso drinks to our fabulous fair trade, organic coffee and teas, we believe in quality that you can taste. Our menu features beloved breakfast sandwiches and, of course, the world's BEST oatmeal – a claim our regulars happily stand by! We ensure plenty of comfortable seating, creating an inviting space for quick grabs or relaxed visits.

We're constantly striving to offer a clear and delightful experience for everyone, including options like dairy-free choices for those with specific preferences. While we thrive on efficiency to serve our popular offerings swiftly, our commitment is always to friendly service and a memorable start to your day in Cashiers.
