Our Roots

A Taste of Home in Every Bite.

Andrew's Family Restaurant is more than just a place to eat; it's where community gathers and memories are made. For years, we've poured our heart into serving Wingate, NC, with meals that resonate with comfort, quality, and a genuine family touch. Every dish is crafted with care, inviting you to sit back, relax, and savor the moments.

Nestled right here in Wingate, we believe in the simple joy of a good meal shared amongst loved ones. Our commitment extends to using the freshest ingredients and preparing everything with passion. From sizzling steaks to our celebrated IN HOUSE MADE DUMPLINGS, we ensure every plate served is a testament to our dedication to flavor and satisfaction.

It's the kind of place where the aroma of delicious food fills the air, and the laughter of families becomes part of the ambiance. We invite you to become part of our story, to experience the warmth, and to taste the difference that genuine family cooking makes.

Come on in and let us treat you like family.
