Our Story

Serving Wilkesboro with Heart and Homestyle Goodness

Since its inception, Harold's Restaurant has been a beloved cornerstone of the Wilkesboro community, cherished for its unwavering commitment to quality and comfort. What started as a simple dream to serve delicious, wholesome meals has grown into a local institution.

At Harold's, we believe in the power of a good meal to bring people together. Our kitchen is where traditional American diner fare meets heartfelt hospitality. From the moment you step through our doors, you’re treated like family, experiencing service that’s not just efficient but genuinely friendly.

We're passionate about serving breakfast late, ensuring everyone gets a taste of our homemade goodness, no matter the hour. Every dish is prepared with care, using recipes passed down through generations, making our food not just reasonably priced but truly delicious and memorable.
