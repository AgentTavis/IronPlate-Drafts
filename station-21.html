About Station 21

Welcome to Station 21, where tradition meets flavor in the heart of Wilkesboro. For years, we've been committed to serving authentic, slow-smoked BBQ and wood-fired grilled specialties that keep our community coming back.

Our team takes pride in using premium cuts of meat, time-honored smoking techniques, and house-made sauces that capture the true essence of Southern barbecue. Every meal is prepared with passion and attention to detail.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a casual dinner with family, Station 21 offers the perfect atmosphere and unforgettable flavors.
