About Station 21

Welcome to Station 21, Wilkesboro's premier destination for authentic BBQ and comfort food. Nestled in the heart of our community, we've been crafting memorable dining experiences since day one.

Our passion is simple: bring people together through exceptional food made with the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques. From slow-smoked meats to classic comfort dishes, every plate tells a story of dedication and flavor.

Whether you're a local or just passing through, Station 21 is your gathering place for good food, great company, and unforgettable moments.
