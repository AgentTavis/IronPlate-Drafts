Our Roots

A Taste of Southern Tradition in Every Dish.

Jay's Seafood Restaurant, nestled in the heart of Albemarle, NC, is more than just a place to eat—it's a culinary landmark. We've proudly carried forward a rich history, serving generations of families who come back for our authentic Southern hospitality and, of course, the freshest seafood.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a welcoming, family-oriented fish camp atmosphere where everyone feels at home. From our humble beginnings, we've grown, but our commitment to quality ingredients, time-honored recipes, and a genuine smile has never wavered. We believe good food brings people together, and at Jay's, every meal is a celebration of community and tradition.

"Jay's is a true Albemarle treasure. The atmosphere is just like the old fish camps I remember, and the fried shrimp is always perfect. My family has been coming here for decades!"

- Sarah L., Local Patron

Our Dishes

Savor the Flavors of the Sea.

Endless Fried Fish Buffet

Our famous all-you-can-eat golden-fried fish, served with classic Southern sides. A true Jay's tradition!

Classic Fried Shrimp Plate

Perfectly seasoned and fried succulent shrimp, crispy on the outside, tender on the inside, served with fries.

Grilled Flounder Dinner

Lightly seasoned and perfectly grilled fresh flounder fillet, a healthier seafood option with steamed vegetables.

Homemade Crab Cakes

Our secret recipe jumbo lump crab cakes, pan-seared to perfection, bursting with flavor, served with a tangy remoulade.

Seafood Platter

A generous combination of fried fish, shrimp, oysters, and scallops – the ultimate feast for seafood lovers!

Creekstone Oysters

Freshly shucked oysters, available raw on the half shell or lightly fried to a golden crisp. Seasonal availability.
