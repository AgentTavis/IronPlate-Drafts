Our Story

Crafting Good Times, One Drink at a Time.

Welcome to Beaufort Bar!

Beaufort Bar is Beaufort, NC's premier destination for exceptional drinks, lively entertainment, and a truly welcoming atmosphere. As a local Bar & Grill, we're dedicated to creating memorable evenings for our guests, whether you're joining us for a handcrafted cocktail, a classic brew, or to enjoy our incredible live music.

Nestled in the heart of Beaufort, we pride ourselves on a warm, personal touch that makes everyone feel right at home. Come discover why we're quickly becoming the go-to spot for locals and visitors alike.
