OUR STORY

A Taste of Ireland in Reidsville

More than just traditional Irish pub fare, The Celtic Fringe brings you 100% Certified Angus Beef steaks, burgers and sandwiches, along with a variety of poultry, seafood, traditional Celtic cuisine, and local southern favorites.

Every dish is crafted with care, reflecting our passion for bringing authentic flavors and quality ingredients to Reidsville. We believe in creating a comfortable, casual bistro environment where good food, great company, and outstanding drinks come together.
