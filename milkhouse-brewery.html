Our Heritage

From Our Farm to Your Glass: A Taste of Local Tradition

At Milkhouse Brewery, we believe in the power of local. Nestled in the heart of Mt Airy, NC, our brewery isn't just a place to enjoy exceptional craft beer; it's a celebration of community and fresh, local produce.

Adjacent to our vibrant taproom and brewhouse, you'll discover The Mercantile. This unique shop is a treasure trove of seasonal local produce, high-quality local meats, farm-fresh eggs, artisanal cheeses, craft cider, and delectable baked goods. It's a convenient spot to pick up dinner, find the perfect gift, or simply explore the rich bounty our local farmers and artisans have to offer.

Every pint and every purchase supports our mission to bring the best of Mt Airy's agriculture and craftsmanship directly to you. Come, gather, and experience the true flavor of our community.
