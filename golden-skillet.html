Our Legacy

More Than Just BBQ, It's Plymouth's Heart.

For generations, Golden Skillet has been a cherished cornerstone of Plymouth, NC. What began as a humble family eatery decades ago has blossomed into a beloved community staple, a place where familiar faces gather, laughter fills the air, and cherished memories are made.

We pride ourselves on serving up classic Southern comfort food with authentic flavors that bring folks back time and again. Our commitment to delicious, satisfying meals and a warm, welcoming atmosphere ensures that every visit feels like coming home. It's more than just a meal; it’s a taste of Plymouth tradition.

"Coming to Golden Skillet feels like stepping back in time to my grandma's kitchen. Their fried chicken is simply unmatched, and the atmosphere is pure small-town charm."

— Eleanor Vance, Plymouth Resident
