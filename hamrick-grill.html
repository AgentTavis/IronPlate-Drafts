Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Hamrick Tradition.

Hamrick Grill is a cozy restaurant nestled in the heart of Shelby, NC, dedicated to serving up classic American comfort food that tastes just like home. From hearty breakfasts to satisfying lunches and dinners, we believe in simple, quality ingredients and a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Since opening our doors, we've become a beloved spot for locals and visitors alike. We're passionate about creating dishes that bring people together, whether it's for a quick bite or a relaxed family meal. Come experience the comfort and flavor that makes Hamrick Grill a true Shelby gem.
