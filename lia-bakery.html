Our Journey

From Our Oven to Your Heart: The Lia Bakery Story

Welcome to Lia Bakery, Kannapolis's newest destination for delightful treats and comforting diner classics. We believe in crafting every item with passion and serving our community with a smile. From our freshly baked pastries to savory bites, we're dedicated to bringing you an unforgettable experience.

Our philosophy is simple: quality and freshness, always. Even when you arrive at the 'tail end of service times,' as our wonderful customer Becky D. discovered, our commitment to scrumptious quality never wavers. Every day, we strive to make Lia Bakery a place where every visit feels like coming home.
