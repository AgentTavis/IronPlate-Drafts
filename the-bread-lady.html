Our Heritage

Our Passion, Baked into Every Plate.

The Bread Lady isn't just a name; it's a promise of warmth, comfort, and culinary excellence. For years, we've been a beloved local spot in Greenville, NC, a place where friends and family gather, and every meal feels like coming home.

Our journey began with a simple love for good food and the belief that the best dishes start with the freshest ingredients and a whole lot of heart. While our name highlights our dedication to exceptional baked goods, our bar and grill menu offers a diverse array of handcrafted meals designed to delight every palate.

We're proud to be a cornerstone of the Greenville community, committed to serving up smiles, one delicious dish at a time. Come experience the taste of tradition and innovation, all under one roof.

The Baker's Burger

Juicy grilled patty, crisp lettuce, tomato, special sauce, all nestled in our artisan-baked bun.

Signature Grilled Cheese

A gourmet blend of melted cheeses, perfectly toasted on thick-cut, house-made sourdough bread.

Hearty Chicken Sandwich

Tender grilled chicken breast with fresh greens and veggies, served on our freshly baked bread.
