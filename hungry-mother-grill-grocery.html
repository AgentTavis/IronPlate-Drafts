Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community Experience.

Nestled in the heart of Marion, VA, Hungry Mother Grill & Grocery is more than just a place to eat – it's a community gathering spot. We're proud to serve up delicious, homestyle comfort food and refreshing drinks, all with a warm, welcoming smile. While you might know us for our unique connection to exciting outdoor activities like zipline tours through our 'Outpost' offerings, our heart beats for great food and friendly service.

Come on in, grab a bite, stock up on essentials at our grocery, and experience the local flavor that makes Marion special. We believe in good food, good company, and creating memories that last.
