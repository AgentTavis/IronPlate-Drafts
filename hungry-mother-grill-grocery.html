Our Roots

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

Nestled at 2382 Old Lake Rd, Hungry Mother Grill & Grocery is a beloved Marion, VA institution. We're not just a place to grab a bite; we're a community hub where delicious food meets genuine hospitality, all within easy reach of thrilling outdoor adventures.

Our grill serves up satisfying bar and grill classics, prepared with care and perfect after a day of exploring. And our attached grocery ensures you have access to essential provisions, making us your convenient stop for both sustenance and supplies.
