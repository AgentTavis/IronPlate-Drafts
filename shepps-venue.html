Our Menu

Our Culinary Creations

Experience the vibrant flavors of Mexico, crafted with care and passion.

Carne Asada Plate

Flavorful grilled steak, marinated to perfection, served with fluffy rice, savory refried beans, and warm tortillas.

Chicken Enchiladas

Tender, seasoned chicken rolled in corn tortillas, smothered in our rich, homemade red sauce and melted cheese.

Tacos al Pastor

Succulent marinated pork, slow-cooked on a vertical spit, served with pineapple, onion, cilantro, and fresh tortillas.

Quesabirria Tacos

Crispy, cheesy tacos filled with tender, savory birria beef, served with a side of rich consome for dipping.

Veggie Fajitas

A sizzling medley of fresh bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, and zucchini, served with all the classic fajita accompaniments.

Chiles Rellenos

Two roasted poblano peppers generously stuffed with melting cheese, lightly battered, fried, and topped with our signature mild sauce.
