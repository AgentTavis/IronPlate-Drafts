Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience

Glenwood's is more than just a bar and grill; it's a true community cornerstone in Owings Mills, NC. For years, we've poured passion into every dish and drink, creating a welcoming atmosphere where friends and family gather, laughter flows, and good times are always on the menu. We're proud to be your local favorite, dedicated to serving up quality, comfort, and unforgettable experiences.

We believe in the power of good food and great company to bring people together. Our doors are open to everyone, whether you're catching up with old friends, celebrating a special occasion, or simply looking for a relaxing evening out. Come discover why Glenwood's has become a cherished part of the Owings Mills community.
