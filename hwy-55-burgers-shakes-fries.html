Our Story

More Than Just Meals, It's a Memory.

Step back in time at Hwy 55 Burgers, Shakes & Fries in Dunn, NC. We're a retro-themed chain designed to whisk you away to a classic 1950s soda-fountain era, right here in your neighborhood.

Since opening our doors, we've been proudly dishing out familiar fast-food selections with a commitment to quality and a welcoming atmosphere. It's more than just a meal; it's an experience built on nostalgia, friendly service, and delicious food that keeps families coming back for generations.

We believe in creating a place where good food and great memories are made. Come join us and discover why we're a beloved spot in Dunn!
