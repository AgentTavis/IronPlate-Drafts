Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Newton Tradition.

Located at 204 N College Ave, The Hen & Egg is more than just a restaurant; it's a vibrant part of the Newton community. We offer a relaxed atmosphere with moderate noise, making us a great choice for those seeking a pleasant dining experience without the fuss. Our doors open bright and early, serving up comfort and quality from our delicious all-day breakfast to our satisfying lunch options.

We pride ourselves on being a beloved local spot where neighbors and visitors alike can gather. Whether you're starting your day with a freshly brewed coffee from our dedicated coffee bar, catching up with friends over a hearty lunch, or simply enjoying a quiet moment, The Hen & Egg is committed to making every visit special. Come taste the tradition!
