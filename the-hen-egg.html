OUR STORY

Our Table, Your Home: A Newton Tradition

Located right in the heart of Newton at 204 N College Ave, The Hen & Egg isn't just a place to eat – it's a cherished local gathering spot. We've crafted a charming, relaxed atmosphere with just the right amount of friendly buzz, perfect for catching up with friends, enjoying a quiet morning coffee, or a hearty lunch. From our delicious all-day breakfast options to satisfying lunch specials, we're dedicated to serving up comfort and quality with a smile.

Our commitment to community and great food has made us a favorite among locals. We believe in good, honest cooking that brings people together, offering a delightful experience from the moment you walk through our doors. It’s truly a great choice for anyone seeking a pleasant and memorable dining experience in Newton.
