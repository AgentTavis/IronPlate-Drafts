Our Story

Bringing Hearty Flavors to Windsor

Welcome to Lasca's, Windsor's newest gathering spot for good food and great times! Born from a passion for classic American bar and grill fare, we set out to create a cozy, welcoming atmosphere where friends and family can enjoy delicious, freshly prepared meals.

We're thrilled to be part of the Windsor community, serving up juicy steaks, mouth-watering subs, and all your favorite comfort foods. Since our soft opening, we've focused on perfecting our recipes and ensuring every dish leaves our kitchen with that special touch. We believe in honest food, friendly service, and a place where everyone feels at home.
