Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Columbia Tradition.

At Columbia Frosty, we believe in crafting moments as much as we craft delicious food. Nestled in the heart of Columbia, NC, we're dedicated to serving up fresh, satisfying meals that bring families and friends together. Every burger, every fry, every shake is made with a sprinkle of joy and a whole lot of local love.

From our sizzling BBQ Bacon Cheeseburger to our crispy Onion Rings and our kiddo-approved Chicken Tenders, we pour our heart into every dish. We're proud to be a local institution, a place where memories are made and cravings are always satisfied. Come on down and experience the Columbia Frosty difference!
