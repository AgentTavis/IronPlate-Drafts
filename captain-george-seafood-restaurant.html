Our Journey

A Taste of the Ocean, Rooted in Rockingham.

Nestled in the heart of Rockingham, Captain George Seafood Restaurant has been a beloved local institution for years, serving an authentic taste of the coast. We pride ourselves on offering a delectable array of fresh seafood dishes that are sure to tantalize your taste buds.

From succulent fried shrimp to mouthwatering crab cakes, and perfectly seasoned platters, every dish is prepared with passion and the freshest ingredients, ensuring a memorable dining experience for you and your family. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a part of the community, committed to bringing the best of the sea to your table.
