Our Legacy

Crafting Unforgettable Seafood Moments in Rockingham

Welcome to Captain George Seafood Restaurant, a cherished institution in the heart of Rockingham, NC. For decades, we've been dedicated to bringing the freshest flavors of the sea directly to your plate. Our journey began with a simple passion: to serve delectable, high-quality seafood in a warm, family-friendly atmosphere.

We pride ourselves on a delectable array of fresh seafood dishes that are sure to tantalize your taste buds. From succulent shrimp scampi to mouthwatering crab, every dish is prepared with care, using time-honored recipes and the finest ingredients. It's more than just a meal; it's an experience rooted in tradition and community.
