Our Journey

Crafting Community, One Cup at a Time.

At Reed's Coffee + Art + Framing, we believe in simplicity, quality, and community. Nestled in the heart of Morehead City, our two beloved locations, The Webb (812 Evans Street) and The Porch (4466 Arendell Street), are more than just coffee shops – they're places where good vibes flow as freely as our organic coffee.

Enjoy our expertly crafted organic coffees, from rich lattes and cappuccinos to classic drip, along with a refreshing selection of teas and smoothies. Pair your perfect drink with our delightful, freshly baked muffins, warm cinnamon rolls, or comforting hot oatmeal. Our counter-service model ensures quick and friendly attention, allowing you to grab your favorites and enjoy the unique ambiance without delay. It’s all about great taste, a welcoming atmosphere, and convenience, made just for you.

Beyond the brew, we celebrate local art and offer professional framing services, making Reed's a true hub for creativity and connection in our coastal town.
