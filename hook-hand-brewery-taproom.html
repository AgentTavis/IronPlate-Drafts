Our Story

More Than Just a Brewery, It's an Experience

We have been all over the country seeing breweries, and this by far is the most eccentric and perfectly cool place. Hook Hand Brewery Taproom & Art Gallery isn't just a spot for great BBQ and craft brews; it's a destination where art, atmosphere, and community converge.

Bright colors and unique posters have you feeling like you just smoked a mushroom. Maybe? Idk but we had a great time. Our owner is dedicated to creating a vibrant, welcoming space that stands out from the ordinary, offering a brewery experience like none you've ever had.
