Our Roots

More Than Just a Coffee Shop, It's Home.

Welcome to The Coffee Shop Bakery, a cozy haven nestled in Galt, NC. Our story began with a simple idea: to create a place where everyone feels like family, surrounded by the warmth of good company and incredible food. We pour our hearts into every cup of coffee, every espresso shot, and every freshly baked good that leaves our kitchen.

From the moment you step inside, you'll be charmed by our vintage decorations and the irresistibly homey atmosphere. It's a truly adorable spot, perfect for catching up with friends, getting some work done, or simply enjoying a quiet moment with a delicious treat.

We're proud to be a local gem, serving up not just fantastic coffee and tea, but also baked goods that have earned a reputation far and wide. We believe in quality, tradition, and a little bit of magic in every bite.

"I found this little gem of a place while driving through San Joaquin County. It has a lot of vintage decorations and a homey feel—it's incredibly adorable. I ordered the English toffee latte and it was delicious. I also had a slice of the coffee cake that they are known for. I am a foodie and a competitive baker. I can tell you this is one of the best coffee cakes I've ever had. It was truly outstanding."
— A Delighted Foodie & Competitive Baker

Our Offerings

Coffee Cake

Chocolate Chip Muffins

Classic, comforting, and baked fresh daily with generous chocolate chips. A timeless favorite.

Apple Turnovers

Flaky pastry filled with sweet, tender apples and a hint of cinnamon. A delightful treat for any time of day.

Lemon Bars
