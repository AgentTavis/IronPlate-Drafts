Our Roots

The Heart of South Bend BBQ

At Crooked Ewe Brewery & Ale House, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a South Bend tradition rooted in the art of authentic BBQ. For years, we've dedicated ourselves to the slow, meticulous process of smoking meats to perfection, bringing out flavors that tell a story with every bite.

Whether you're looking for a casual meal, a place to gather with friends, or planning a big celebration, our commitment to quality shines through. Throwing a party? You can pre-order whole BBQ briskets and shoulders and wow your guests with minimal effort and maximum flavor. We handle the hard work, so you can enjoy your event.

Come experience the difference that passion, tradition, and community make. We look forward to welcoming you to the Crooked Ewe family.
