Our Story

Where Every Meal is a Smooth Takeoff.

Welcome to The Updraft Kitchen, the great new restaurant bringing delicious flavors to the Mt. Airy Airport! We're dedicated to serving up hearty breakfast and satisfying lunch, Tuesday through Saturday until 4 PM. We believe a great meal should start your day right or power you through it, and we're proud to be a local favorite for fresh, comforting dishes in a unique setting.
