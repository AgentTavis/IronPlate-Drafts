Our Journey

A Warm Welcome to The Updraft Kitchen

Welcome to The Updraft Kitchen, a charming new diner nestled right at the Mt. Airy Airport! We're proud to serve delicious breakfast and lunch fare, crafted with love and local flavor. Our mission is simple: to offer a cozy atmosphere where everyone feels like family, whether you're a local regular or just passing through.

Since opening our doors, we've focused on creating memorable dining experiences with fresh ingredients and friendly service. We're open Tuesday through Sunday, ready to fuel your day with hearty meals from 8 AM to 4 PM. Come discover your new favorite spot for a great meal in Mt. Airy!
