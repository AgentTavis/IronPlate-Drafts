Our Story

Where Community Meets The Coast.

At Saltwater Grill, we embrace the vibrant lifestyle of River Bend Marina. Our waterfront restaurant offers a blend of casual elegance and community spirit. It's more than just a meal; it's an experience where the calming waters of New Bern provide the perfect backdrop for unforgettable moments.

With easy dock access and a friendly atmosphere, it’s the perfect destination to unwind, savor delicious seafood, and spend quality time with friends and family. We're proud to be a cherished part of the New Bern community, serving up smiles and fresh flavors every day.
