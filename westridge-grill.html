Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Westridge Experience.

Welcome to Westridge Grill, where community gathers and flavors come alive right here in Rocky Mount, NC. We believe in crafting more than just delicious food; we’re dedicated to creating memorable experiences with every visit.

Our commitment to local quality shines through our "ever-changing" selection of North Carolina craft beers and fine NC wines. We take pride in showcasing the best our state has to offer, ensuring there's always a new sip to discover. But that's just the beginning...

From our juicy, hand-pattied burgers to our celebrated homemade fried green tomatoes, every dish is prepared with care and a touch of Southern hospitality. We've poured our heart into making Westridge Grill your go-to spot for an exceptional meal, whether you're dining in or picking up.
