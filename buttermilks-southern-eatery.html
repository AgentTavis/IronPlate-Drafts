Our Heritage

The Heart of Southern Comfort in Whiteville

Buttermilks Southern Eatery brings the warmth and comfort of homemade Southern cooking right here to Whiteville, NC. From sun-up with our all-day breakfast delights to hearty lunch plates featuring the freshest seafood, we craft every dish with love and tradition. It's more than just a meal; it's a taste of home, served with genuine Southern hospitality.

We pride ourselves on using quality ingredients and time-honored recipes that evoke the true spirit of the South. Whether you're craving a classic breakfast, a fresh fish basket, or our signature shrimp, you'll find something to satisfy your soul at Buttermilks.
