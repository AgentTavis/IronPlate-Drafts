Our Heritage

More Than Just a Bakery: A Mars Hill Tradition.

At High Ridge Bakery, we pride ourselves on being the heart of Mars Hill's culinary scene. Inspired by the comforting, vast selections you'd find at a classic American grill and dessert bar, we've cultivated an atmosphere where every visit feels like coming home.

From our earliest days, we've been committed to offering a diverse array of fresh, delicious food and desserts. Our philosophy is simple: provide exceptional quality, quick service, and a friendly face every time. It's what keeps our community coming back, day after day.
