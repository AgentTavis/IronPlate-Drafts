Our Heritage

Where Every Meal Feels Like Home

At Roy's Country Corral, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a cornerstone of Lumberton, serving up genuine Southern hospitality and delicious BBQ since day one. We believe in keeping things simple, honest, and utterly delicious.

We pride ourselves on our warm, friendly service. As one guest shared, "Our waiter was an angel. She was very sweet, answered all our questions about the menus, and waitered while helping in the kitchen." That's the Roy's difference – a dedicated team that goes the extra mile to make your dining experience memorable. We pour our heart into every dish, from our crispy catfish to our flavorful BBQ plates, ensuring that "Our food was pretty good" becomes "Our food was absolutely amazing!" for every visitor.

Come on in, settle down, and taste the tradition that makes Roy's Country Corral a true Lumberton gem. We're here to serve you hearty meals and create lasting memories.
