Our Story

Crafting Memories, One Plate at a Time

Nestled in the heart of Historic Downtown Wilson, The Corner isn't just a bar and grill – it's a vibrant community hub where friends gather and flavors come alive. We've dedicated ourselves to creating an exceptional atmosphere, where every visit feels like coming home.

From our welcoming ambiance to our commitment to fresh, quality ingredients, we believe in providing an experience that’s truly "huge" – in both taste and spirit. Join us for an evening that delights all your senses.
