Our Story

More Than Just a Tavern, It's a Vibe.

At Lagerheads Tavern, we've carefully cultivated a space where "Beach dive vibes" meet "New-school fun." We're not just serving cold drinks; we're crafting an experience that locals cherish and tourists eagerly discover.

From the moment you step in, you'll feel the revamped atmosphere — a perfect blend of laid-back charm and lively energy. It's the kind of place where every visit feels like coming home, whether you're catching up with old friends or making new ones.

Our commitment to a chill crowd and fantastic times has made us a cornerstone of Wrightsville Beach. We believe in good company, great drinks, and a memorable experience for everyone.
