Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community Hub.

In the heart of Weldon, NC, Sunshine Grill isn't just a restaurant—it's a cherished local institution. For years, we've been serving up classic American comfort food with a smile, becoming the go-to spot for families, friends, and hungry travelers alike.

Known for our delectable array of hot dogs, juicy hamburgers, and an extensive sandwich menu, we pride ourselves on using fresh ingredients and preparing every dish with passion. Whether you're starting your day with a hearty breakfast or stopping by for a satisfying lunch, you'll always find a warm welcome and a meal that hits the spot.
