Our Heritage

From Our Grill to Your Table: A Weldon Tradition

Nestled in the heart of Weldon, NC, Sunshine Grill is more than just a restaurant – it's a cherished community gathering spot. For over a decade, we've been proudly serving up classic American comfort food, made with a whole lot of love and the freshest local ingredients.

What started as a simple idea to bring delicious, no-fuss meals to our neighbors has blossomed into a beloved local tradition. From our sizzling hot dogs and juicy hamburgers to our extensive sandwich menu, every item is crafted to bring a smile to your face and satisfy your cravings. We believe in good food, great company, and the warm, welcoming atmosphere that makes everyone feel right at home.
