OUR STORY

From Our Soil to Your Glass: A Family Legacy

Welcome to 4Js Farm & Brewery, where every sip tells a story of dedication to the land and a passion for exceptional craft. Nestled in the heart of Warrenton, NC, our farm is more than just a place; it's a living testament to sustainable practices and time-honored brewing traditions.

Our journey began with a simple dream: to share the magic of farm life and the rich flavors born from our own soil. Our beers, meticulously crafted from homegrown ingredients and traditional brewing methods, genuinely tell the tale of our love for the land and unwavering passion for brewing.

We believe in quality, community, and the unique connection between farm and glass. Below, we invite you to discover our three featured brews, each a unique flavor profile waiting to become your new favorite. Or, dive deeper into our full spectrum of artisanal brews on our dedicated beer page.

"You can truly taste the love for the land in every beer. The atmosphere is pure North Carolina charm, and the brews are simply outstanding. A must-visit!"

- A Local Fan

OUR CRAFTED BREWS

Taste the Spirit of the Farm

Harvest Ale

A seasonal favorite, crisp and refreshing with subtle notes of local honey and autumn spices. Brewed with our own farm-grown barley.

Pasture Porter

Rich and robust, this dark porter boasts deep chocolate and coffee undertones, a smooth finish perfect for any evening.

Sun-Kissed Lager

Light and bright, our lager is a golden classic with a clean, crisp profile and a hint of citrus from hand-picked hops.
