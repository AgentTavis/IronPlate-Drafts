Our Heritage

Serving Thomasville with Authentic Flavors Since Day One.

At Skipper's Seafood Restaurant, we've been a beloved local staple in Thomasville, NC, for years. We pride ourselves on offering a genuine, no-frills seafood experience, focusing on the classic preparations our community loves most. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll feel the casual, family-friendly atmosphere that makes us a favorite.

Our commitment is simple: fresh, flavorful seafood prepared just the way you like it – often fried, boiled, or broiled to perfection. While our menu is focused, it's packed with the traditional dishes that keep our regulars coming back. We believe in good food, good company, and making every visit a memorable one. Join us for a taste of Thomasville's seafood tradition!

Our Promise

Why Skipper's is Thomasville's Top Choice for Seafood

Authentic Seafood

We honor the traditional methods of preparing seafood – fried, boiled, and broiled – ensuring a classic taste that's been loved for generations. Expect delicious, unfussy flavors every time.

Family & Group Friendly

With a casual atmosphere, moderate noise levels, and ample space for groups, Skipper's is the perfect spot for family dinners or gatherings with friends. Kids (and even dogs in outdoor seating!) are welcome!

Relaxing Outdoor Seating

Enjoy your meal al fresco on our outdoor patio. It's the ideal setting to unwind, catch up, and enjoy the Thomasville weather while savoring your favorite seafood dishes.
