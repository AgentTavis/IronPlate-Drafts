Our Journey

More Than Just a Brewery, We're Your Community Hub

The Blue Ridge Brewing Company is a beloved local spot in Greer, NC. We started with a simple vision: to create a place where friends and family could gather, enjoy exceptional craft beers, and savor delicious, comforting food. From the moment you walk through our doors, you're not just a customer; you're part of our extended family.

Our passion for quality ingredients, innovative brewing, and a warm, inviting atmosphere sets us apart. We believe in supporting local, fostering connections, and making every visit a memorable experience. Come for the food, stay for the company, and leave with a smile.
