Our Heritage

The Heart of Mt. Holly's Coffee Culture

More Than Just Coffee – It's an Experience.

Located in the charming town of Mount Holly, NC, Coffee Origin is more than just a cozy eatery; it's a destination where the rich traditions of Yemenese coffee culture meet local hospitality. We specialize in high-quality coffee, thoughtfully prepared, alongside a selection of delicious light bites designed to complement your drink.

Our journey began with a chance discovery, a passion for authentic flavors, and a desire to bring a unique coffee experience to our community. From the moment you step inside, you'll feel the warmth of our welcoming atmosphere, designed to be your perfect escape.
