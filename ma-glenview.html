Our Story

A Taste of California's Mediterranean Sun in Glenview

At Ēma Glenview, we believe in bringing vibrant, fresh flavors to your table. We are a Mediterranean restaurant that proudly showcases Chef CJ Jacobson’s lighter, California style of cooking, focusing on wholesome ingredients and innovative preparations.

Our philosophy is simple: create dishes that are not only delicious but also bright and healthful, perfect for sharing and savoring. From our creamy hummuses to our vibrant salads, every plate tells a story of culinary passion and the sun-drenched coasts of the Mediterranean, reinterpreted with a West Coast touch.
