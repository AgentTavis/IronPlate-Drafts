Our Roots

More Than Just Coffee & Ice Cream, It's Family.

Nestled in the heart of Lillington, Beans & Cream is more than just a diner – it's a community gathering spot where every visit feels like coming home. We've built our reputation on a simple promise: exceptional quality, a warm smile, and that unique 'small town friendly' atmosphere that makes Lillington special.

From the first sip of our freshly brewed coffee to the last scoop of our rich, creamy ice cream, we're dedicated to crafting moments of pure joy. It's a fantastic little ice cream shop with a wide selection of both coffee and ice cream, as many of our customers have happily shared. We believe in serving up simple pleasures that bring people together, one cup and one cone at a time.

"Beans & Cream isn't just a place to eat; it's where memories are made. The warmth and genuine hospitality are unmatched."

— A Grateful Regular
