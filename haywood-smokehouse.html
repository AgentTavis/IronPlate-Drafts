Our Story

Rooted in Tradition

At Haywood Smokehouse, we believe true BBQ is an art passed down through generations. Our journey began with a simple desire: to bring authentic, old-school barbecue to Waynesville. Every dish we serve is a testament to this commitment, crafted with passion and time-honored techniques.

We pride ourselves on our premium specialty meats, carefully selected and slow-smoked to perfection. From our fresh, house-made sausages to our rich, house-made sauces, every element is prepared with care. Our fall-off-the-bone ribs and melt-in-your-mouth smoked brisket aren't just meals; they're an experience that will have you coming back again and again.

Whether you crave our tender smoked pork or succulent turkey breast, you'll find a plate that satisfies. Haywood Smokehouse is more than just a restaurant; it's a family-friendly place where memories are made over unforgettable flavors. Join us for daily lunch and dinner specials that are guaranteed to knock your socks off!
