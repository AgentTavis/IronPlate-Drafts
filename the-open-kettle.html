Our Roots

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community.

The Open Kettle is a beloved local spot in Roxboro, NC. For years, we've been the heart of the community, serving up delicious bar and grill classics in a warm, welcoming atmosphere. We believe in good food, great company, and making every visitor feel like family.

Born from a passion for bringing people together, The Open Kettle has grown into more than just a restaurant; it's a gathering place where memories are made, friendships are forged, and every meal is a celebration of local spirit.
