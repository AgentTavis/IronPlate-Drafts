Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Hays Tradition

Welcome to Mountain View Cafe, a local diner nestled on Rock Creek Rd, where comfort food meets breathtaking Blue Ridge scenery. For years, we've been the heart of Hays, NC, serving up delicious dishes and warm smiles. Our cafe boasts a menu brimming with a diverse array of dishes, from hearty breakfast options to savory lunch specials, promising something to satisfy every craving, all served with a smile.

We pride ourselves on being a place where neighbors gather, families make memories, and every guest feels like part of our extended family. It's the friendly faces, the stunning mountain backdrop, and the genuine taste of home that keeps our community coming back.
