Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Memory.

At Mikey's Diner, we believe in the simple joy of good food shared amongst good folks. Located right here in Wilson, NC, we've poured our hearts into creating a space where every plate tells a story of tradition, quality, and genuine Southern hospitality.

Our menu boasts a variety of traditional diner favorites, all made with quality ingredients and served with a side of warmth that makes you feel right at home. It's the kind of place where you come for the food, but stay for the company and the good times.
