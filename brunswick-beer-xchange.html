Our Journey

More Than Just a Bar, We're a Community Hub

Welcome to Brunswick Beer XChange, where great beer meets delicious grill fare in the heart of Leland, NC. We started with a simple vision: to create a warm, inviting space where friends and family could gather, unwind, and enjoy a fantastic selection of craft beers alongside mouth-watering, freshly prepared food.

Since opening our doors, we've poured our passion into crafting an experience that celebrates local flavors, genuine hospitality, and the joy of good company. Every pint pulled and every dish served is a testament to our commitment to quality and community. We believe in providing a place where every visit feels like coming home.
