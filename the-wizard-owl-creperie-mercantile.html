Our Story

Where Community Gathers and Flavors Bloom

At The Wizard Owl, we've created a unique space where the comforting aromas of a classic bar and grill meet the charm of an old-fashioned mercantile. It's half restaurant, half curated marketplace, offering everything from savory crepes and hearty comfort food to specialty foods, fresh local produce, and unique goods for camping & hiking enthusiasts.

Our journey began with a vision to bring a vibrant community hub to Pickens, NC. We meticulously source products, from luxury soaps to local coffees and teas, ensuring every item tells a story of quality and local craftsmanship. It's more than just a meal or a shopping trip; it's an experience designed to delight your senses and connect you with the heart of our community.
