Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community.

Welcome to Your Valdese Home Away From Home.

At Mill Town Social House, we believe good food and good company go hand-in-hand. Nestled in the heart of Valdese, NC, we’ve created a vibrant bar and grill where everyone feels like family. From the moment you step through our doors, you’ll experience friendly and fast service, delivered with a genuine smile.

Our kitchen prides itself on serving delicious food choices designed to satisfy every palate. Don't miss our highly-regarded full healthy salad bar option, perfect for a lighter, fresh meal. Beyond the plate, we are a true social hub, dedicated to bringing people together through engaging experiences.

Join us weekly for exciting live music performances, discover new talent during our popular Open Mic nights, or challenge your friends at our lively trivia competitions. There's always something happening at Mill Town, making every visit a memorable one.
