Our Story

Your Downtown Madison Home for Great Times & Good Company

Nestled in the heart of Downtown Madison, River Club is more than just a bar and lounge—it’s a vibrant hub where friends gather, memories are made, and good times flow. We believe in creating a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home.

Open 7 days a week, we’re dedicated to being your go-to spot for relaxation and entertainment. Whether you're looking to unwind after a long day, enjoy lively conversations, or catch some incredible live music, you'll find your rhythm here.

From our exciting Thursday Game Nights to our weekly specials and the freshest flavors from curbside food trucks, there's always something new to discover at River Club. Come experience the warmth and energy that makes us a true Madison gem.
