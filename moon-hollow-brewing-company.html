Our Journey

Our Roots in Blacksburg

Moon Hollow Brewing Company is more than just a place to eat and drink; it's a beloved local spot in Blacksburg, NC, built on passion and community spirit. From humble beginnings, our vision was clear: to create a welcoming haven where friends, family, and neighbors could gather, share stories, and enjoy exceptional craft brews paired with delicious grill fare.

We're proud to serve our community, focusing on quality ingredients, friendly service, and a relaxed atmosphere that feels like home. Every pint poured and every dish served carries a piece of our dedication to making Moon Hollow a true Blacksburg institution.

"Moon Hollow is our go-to spot! The vibe is always great, and it truly feels like a community hub. Definitely recommend for anyone in Blacksburg!"

— A Loyal Local Patron

Classic Burger

Craft Beer Selection

A rotating selection of local and regional craft beers on tap, from hoppy IPAs to smooth Stouts.

Crispy Fries

Perfectly seasoned golden fries, served hot and crispy with your choice of dipping sauce.
