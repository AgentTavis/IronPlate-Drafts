Our Heritage

Generations of Comfort on Your Plate

At Lady Bug Family Restaurant, we're proud to be a cornerstone of Wadesboro, NC, serving up classic Southern comfort with a family touch. Our famous hot bar, available Tuesday through Saturday from 11 AM to 7:30 PM and Sundays from 11 AM to 5:00 PM, is a beloved tradition.

We believe in hearty portions, warm smiles, and flavors that remind you of home. From our widely praised collard greens to our daily rotating specials, we invite you to gather around our table and experience the Lady Bug difference. We aim to be your home away from home, where every meal is made with care and served with hospitality.
