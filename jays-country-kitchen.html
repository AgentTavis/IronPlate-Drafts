Our Story

A Taste of Southern Comfort, Right Here in Lumberton.

At Jay's Country Kitchen, we believe in the simple joy of good food and warm hospitality. Rooted deeply in the heart of Lumberton, we've been serving up hearty meals and welcoming smiles for years. Our kitchen is where tradition meets deliciousness, crafting classic pizzas alongside beloved Southern comfort dishes that taste just like home.

We're more than just a restaurant; we're a gathering place for friends, families, and neighbors. Every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and a touch of genuine care, ensuring that each visit leaves you satisfied and eager to return. From our crispy fries to our signature steak and cheese subs, we're dedicated to bringing you the flavors you love in an atmosphere where everyone feels like family.
