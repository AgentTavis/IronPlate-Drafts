Our Journey

Fifteen Years of Family Passion & Craft

From humble beginnings as a small winery fifteen years ago, Cedar Creek has blossomed into a unique destination. Still proudly family-owned and operated, we've expanded our passion for crafting exceptional beverages and delicious food.

Today, the Cedar Creek name encompasses a vibrant brewery, a distinctive distillery, and a welcoming eatery, all united by the same dedication to quality, community, and the timeless joy of sharing good company. We invite you to experience the heart of our family in every sip and every bite.

"Cedar Creek truly embodies the spirit of Martinsville. It's incredible to see a family grow such a diverse and high-quality experience all under one roof. Every visit feels like coming home."

— A Loyal Patron

Our Offerings

A Taste of Cedar Creek

Explore our curated selection of bar & grill classics, artisanal plates, and handcrafted beverages from our winery, brewery, and distillery.

Cedar Creek Signature Burger

Our classic beef patty, topped with smoked cheddar, crispy bacon, caramelized onions, and our secret sauce on a brioche bun.

Artisan Wood-Fired Pizza

Hand-stretched dough, rich tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and your choice of premium toppings, fired to perfection.

Local Cheese & Charcuterie Board

A selection of regional cheeses, cured meats, house-made preserves, and crusty bread, perfect for sharing.

Craft Beer Tasting Flight

Sample four of our rotating, award-winning craft beers, brewed right here on-site. Ask your server for today's selection.

Distillery Spirits Sampler

Experience the art of our distillery with a flight featuring our finest whiskies, gins, or vodkas. A true connoisseur's delight.

Seasonal Farm-to-Table Salad

Fresh, vibrant greens and seasonal produce sourced from local farms, with our house vinaigrette and optional protein.

Smoked Brisket Sandwich

Slow-smoked, tender brisket piled high on a toasted bun with tangy BBQ sauce and coleslaw, served with house fries.
