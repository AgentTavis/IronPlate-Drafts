Our Legacy

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Camp Tradition.

Nestled in the heart of Camp Raven Knob, the Harry R. Vaughn Sr. Dining Hall has been the vibrant culinary hub for generations of campers and staff. We believe that good food is essential for good adventures, and our dedicated team is committed to providing nourishing, delicious, and memorable meals.

We pride ourselves on a welcoming atmosphere where every camper feels at home. Our goal is to fuel your day's activities and bring everyone together over hearty plates, fostering community and creating lasting camp memories around every table.

If you have any questions about dietary restrictions or special needs, the dining hall staff is happy to help. The special dietary restrictions form is available here. Send that form to dininghall@ravenknob.com along with any questions you may have and they'll get back to you shortly.
