The Story Behind Big Al's Parkway Pub

Nestled in the heart of Newland, NC, Big Al's Parkway Pub is more than just a bar and grill – it's a community hub built on good food, great company, and genuine hospitality. Founded by the Johnson bros., our pub has become a beloved spot for locals and visitors alike to unwind and enjoy the simple pleasures of life.

We pride ourselves on crafting a welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels like family. From our meticulously prepared dishes to our handcrafted beverages, including the famous Watermelon Mojito, every detail is considered to ensure a memorable experience. We believe in serving up joy, one plate and one drink at a time.
