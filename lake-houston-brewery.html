Our Journey

Crafting Community, One Brew at a Time

Lake Houston Brewery is more than just a place to eat and drink; it's a cornerstone of the Huffman community. As a standout choice on Restaurantji—a convenient, all-in-one local dining guide with reviews, menus, and photos—we pride ourselves on offering a welcoming atmosphere where friends and family can gather.

Our passion for great food and exceptional craft beverages shines through in every dish and every pour. We believe in creating a space where quality ingredients meet friendly service, making every visit a memorable experience. Come see why we're a beloved local favorite!

Our Flavors

Simple Pleasures, Expertly Crafted

Classic Cheeseburger

A timeless favorite with a juicy patty, melted cheddar, crisp lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles on a toasted brioche bun.

Crispy Chicken Sandwich

Golden fried chicken breast, secret sauce, dill pickle chips, and shredded lettuce on a soft bun.

House-Smoked Brisket Plate

Slow-smoked brisket, tender and flavorful, served with your choice of classic sides.

Local Craft Beer Selection

Explore our rotating taps featuring the finest craft beers from local and regional breweries. Ask your server for today's specials!

Signature Loaded Fries

Crispy fries piled high with melted cheese, bacon bits, green onions, and a drizzle of our special sauce.

Garden Fresh Salad

A vibrant mix of seasonal greens, fresh vegetables, and your choice of dressing. Add grilled chicken or brisket for a hearty meal.
