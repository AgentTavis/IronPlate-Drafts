Our Story

A Taste of Home in Oriental, NC

For years, M & M's Cafe has been the heart of Oriental, NC, a place where friends gather, families share meals, and every plate tells a story of comfort and quality. We're proud to be your local diner, serving up hearty classics and daily specials made with fresh ingredients and a whole lot of love.

From Dave's famous daily soup to our freshly cut Certified Angus Beef, we believe good food brings people together. Come on in, pull up a chair, and experience the warmth and taste that makes M & M's Cafe a true Oriental favorite.
