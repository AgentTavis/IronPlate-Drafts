Our Roots

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Lincolnton Tradition

Welcome to Chuckwagon Grill, Lincolnton's beloved hidden gem! Tucked away on NC-27, we've been serving up hearty, homestyle bar and grill favorites that keep locals coming back for more. Step inside and you'll find a charming interior adorned with older signs, reminiscent of classic Southern diners. It’s a place where good food and great company come together.

We pride ourselves on offering a warm, inviting atmosphere that feels like a home away from home. From our incredible Hamburger Steak with onions—a dish truly deserving of a recommendation—to our perfectly fried pickles and okra, every plate is crafted with a touch of Southern hospitality. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a community staple, where every visit feels like catching up with old friends.

Just like the beloved BBQ King down the road, we focus on delivering delicious, satisfying meals that hit all the right notes. Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunch special or a relaxed family dinner, Chuckwagon Grill is dedicated to making your experience memorable. Come discover your new favorite spot!
