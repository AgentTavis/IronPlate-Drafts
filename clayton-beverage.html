Our Journey

Crafting Community, One Drink & Dish At A Time

Born from a passion for good food, great company, and the vibrant spirit of Clayton, Clayton Beverage is your newest local hub. We're proud to bring a fresh, inviting atmosphere to downtown, where every visit feels like coming home.

As a brand new establishment, Clayton Beverage is more than just a bar and grill; it's a place where neighbors gather, stories are shared, and new memories are made. We've poured our hearts into creating a welcoming space where you can unwind after a long day, celebrate with friends, or simply enjoy a delicious meal.

We're deeply rooted in the Clayton community and committed to offering an experience that reflects our local pride. From our carefully selected beers, wines, and spirits to our soon-to-be-famous grill items, we focus on quality and a friendly vibe that keeps you coming back for more. Come discover your new favorite spot!

Our Offerings

Taste The Best Of Clayton

While our full menu is ever-evolving with exciting new creations, here are some highlights to get your taste buds ready for Clayton Beverage!

The Clayton Burger

Our signature burger, piled high with fresh toppings and served on a toasted brioche bun. A local favorite in the making!

Loaded Chili-Cheese Fries

Crispy golden fries smothered in savory chili and melted cheese. The perfect appetizer or sidekick.

Craft Beer Selection

A rotating selection of the finest local and regional craft brews, perfect for every palate.

Spicy Chicken Sandwich

Grilled or crispy chicken, tossed in a tangy hot sauce with cool ranch and fresh lettuce.

Signature Cocktails

Expertly mixed drinks from classic concoctions to unique creations by our talented bartenders.

BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders

Tender, slow-cooked pulled pork in a sweet and smoky BBQ sauce on mini buns.
