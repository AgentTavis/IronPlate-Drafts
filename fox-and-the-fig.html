Our Story

Where Every Dish Tells a Tale of Home and Hospitality

Welcome to Fox and the Fig, a true gem nestled in the quaint, charming town of Spruce Pine, NC. We’ve poured our heart and soul into creating the coziest of spaces, a warm sanctuary where friends and family gather, and every visitor feels like a part of our extended family.

From the moment you step through our doors, you’ll be greeted by the aroma of freshly brewed coffee and the promise of a delightful meal. We pride ourselves on serving a range of invigorating coffee drinks, light bites perfect for any time of day, and an entire menu of house-made items prepared with love and the freshest local ingredients. It's not just a meal; it's an experience crafted just for you.

"Fox and the Fig is more than just a diner; it's the heart of Spruce Pine. Their hospitality is unmatched, and everything on the menu, especially the coffee, tastes like home. A true treasure!"
