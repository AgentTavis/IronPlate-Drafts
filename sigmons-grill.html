Our Heritage

Generations of Flavor, Rooted in Conover.

They know what you want on it. That's the heart of Sigmon's Grill. Nestled all the way at the end of Springs Road down in Conover, we're more than just a bar and grill – we're a beloved local institution. For years, we've been serving up hearty meals and warm hospitality in a great little country place where everyone feels like family.

Come on down and experience the genuine charm and delicious flavors that make Sigmon's a true Conover gem. We believe in good food, great company, and making every visit feel like coming home. Our commitment to quality and community has made us a cornerstone of Conover's culinary scene.

The Sigmon Burger

Crispy Chicken Sandwich
