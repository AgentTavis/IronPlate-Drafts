Our Heritage

A Taste of Conover, Deeply Rooted.

Welcome to Sigmon's Grill, a beloved institution nestled at the very end of Springs Road in charming Conover, NC. For years, we've been serving up more than just great food; we've been serving up a slice of genuine country hospitality.

When our regulars walk through the door, we often already know "what you want on it." It's that personal touch, that familiar comfort, and the consistent quality that makes us truly special. We're proud to be that "great little place," a local haven where everyone feels welcome.
