Our Roots

More Than Just a Meal, It's Family.

At All in the Family Grill and Mini mart, we believe in bringing people together over great food and a friendly atmosphere. We're a cherished spot in Milton, NC, where every visit feels like coming home.

Whether you're gathering with groups, bringing the family for a hearty meal, or just looking to enjoy a local craft beer, our doors are always open. Our grill is famous for slow-roasted comfort meals and homemade favorites made fresh daily.

Beyond our delicious eats, you'll also find a convenient mini mart stocked with essential pantry items. We're proud to be a local hub, supporting our community with fresh ingredients and a welcoming space for everyone.
