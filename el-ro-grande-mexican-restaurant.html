Our Story

A Taste of Tradition, Crafted with Heart.

Nestled within the vibrant Center Stage Shopping Center, El Río Grande Mexican Restaurant has become a beloved fixture in Lumberton, NC. We are more than just a place to eat; we're a destination where authentic Mexican flavors meet warm, genuine hospitality.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to bring the rich culinary heritage of Mexico to our community, using fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes. Every dish is prepared with passion, from our sizzling Texas Fajitas to our famous Pollo Loco, ensuring a memorable experience for every guest.

Our Signature Flavors

Experience Authentic Mexican Cuisine.

Pollo Loco

Our signature dish! Tender, marinated grilled chicken breast, topped with a creamy cheese sauce, served with rice, beans, and warm tortillas. A flavorful fiesta in every bite.

Texas Fajitas

Sizzling steak, chicken, and shrimp grilled with onions, bell peppers, and tomatoes. Served with rice, beans, fresh guacamole, sour cream, and your choice of tortillas. A true crowd-pleaser!
