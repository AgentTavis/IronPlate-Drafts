Our Legacy

Your Riverside Escape for Great Times & Good Food

Nestled just 15 miles south of Omaha, right on the beautiful Platte river, Heron Bay has been Springfield, NC's cherished Bar & Grill for over two decades. We’re more than just a place to eat; we’re a community hub where laughter echoes, music fills the air, and memories are made.

Our commitment is simple: provide a welcoming atmosphere, serve up delicious food, and pour the best adult beverages. We pride ourselves on our incredible team, especially our bartenders who consistently go above and beyond to make every visit special. Whether you're here for our vibrant live music nights, a casual meal with family, or to catch up with friends over a cold drink, you'll find Heron Bay to be your perfect riverside retreat.

From the moment you step in, you're part of the Heron Bay family. We're dedicated to delivering an experience that embodies the true spirit of a local favorite – a decent bar, an awesome crew, and good times waiting to happen.
