Our Roots

Crafting Culinary Experiences, One Creation at a Time.

Welcome to TRU, where fresh ingredients meet endless possibilities. Nestled in the heart of Chapel Hill, we're more than just a bar and grill — we're a creative culinary hub where you design your perfect meal. Our philosophy is simple: provide the highest quality ingredients and let your imagination be the chef.

From the moment we opened our doors, our goal has been to offer a laid-back, welcoming space where friends and family can gather. Whether you're craving a hearty, custom-built sandwich, a vibrant, fresh salad, or just a place to unwind with a drink, TRU has you covered. Enjoy our relaxed indoor ambiance or soak up the Chapel Hill charm on our spacious outdoor patio.

We're proud to be a local favorite, known for our late hours and commitment to quality. Every dish is a reflection of your desires, made with care by our dedicated team. Come experience the TRU difference – your flavor, your way.
