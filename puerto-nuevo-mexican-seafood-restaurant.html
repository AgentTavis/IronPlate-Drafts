Our Heritage

Savor the Spirit of Mexico in Spruce Pine.

Since 2009, Puerto Nuevo Mexican & Seafood Restaurant has been a cornerstone of authentic flavor, bringing the vibrant tastes of Mexico and the fresh catch of the sea to our community. What started as a dream to share our family's traditional recipes has grown into a beloved dining destination, celebrated for "The Best Mexican Food and Best Margaritas in the Town."

While our reputation echoes from Banner Elk to Spruce Pine, our heart is here, serving up genuine hospitality and unforgettable meals. We believe in fresh ingredients, time-honored techniques, and creating an atmosphere where every guest feels like family. Join us and discover why we've been a local favorite for over a decade.
