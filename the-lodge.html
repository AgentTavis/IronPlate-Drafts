Our Journey

More Than Just a Cafeteria

Taste the Tradition, Feel the Warmth

The Lodge cafeteria, nestled in the scenic Beech Mountain, NC, is more than just a place for snacks – it's your go-to for hearty meals conveniently located by the playyard. We pride ourselves on offering 'Playyard Provisions,' featuring Claudias Authentic Mexican Food.

Known for our warm, family-friendly atmosphere and year-round events, we're dedicated to serving delicious, authentic Mexican cuisine to our local community and visitors alike. Come experience the flavors and fun at The Lodge!
