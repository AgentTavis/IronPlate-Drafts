Welcome to 50's Snack Bar

Step back in time at Wilkesboro's beloved 50's Snack Bar, where vintage charm meets modern comfort. For decades, we've been serving up authentic American diner classics that transport you straight to the golden age of diners.

Our commitment is simple: fresh ingredients, generous portions, and a warm atmosphere that feels like coming home. Whether you're craving a juicy burger, a crispy milkshake, or a slice of homemade pie, we've got exactly what your taste buds are looking for.

Family-owned and community-focused, we pride ourselves on creating memories over every meal. Every visitor becomes part of the 50's Snack Bar family.
