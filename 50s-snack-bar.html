Welcome to 50's Snack Bar

Step back in time at 50's Snack Bar, Wilkesboro's premier destination for authentic retro diner dining. For decades, we've been serving up classic American comfort food with a nostalgic twist and a whole lot of charm.

From our hand-crafted milkshakes to our flame-grilled burgers, every item on our menu is prepared with love and the finest ingredients. Whether you're a local or just passing through, we invite you to experience the warmth, hospitality, and delicious flavors that make 50's Snack Bar a true community gem.
