About Us

Welcome to 50's Snack Bar, where timeless American diner classics meet authentic North Carolina hospitality. Since opening our doors in Wilkesboro, we've been committed to serving our community with delicious food, friendly service, and a nostalgic atmosphere that takes you back to the golden era of diners.

From handcrafted burgers and crispy fries to milkshakes that'll make you smile, every item on our menu is prepared with care and quality ingredients. Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunch or settling in for a leisurely meal, you'll find the same warm welcome at 50's Snack Bar.

We pride ourselves on being more than just a place to eat—we're a gathering place for the Wilkesboro community, where memories are made and traditions are honored.
