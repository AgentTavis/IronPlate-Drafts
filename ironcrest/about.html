The Company

Heavy equipment. Hands on the wheel.

IronCrest Land Management is a family owned and operated land work company based in Stokesdale, North Carolina, serving North Carolina, South Carolina, and Virginia. The Spatafora family runs the company together — ownership, operations, and time on the equipment. The standard is set by the people who answer the phone.

We handle the full sequence of land work in-house. Excavation, grading, hauling, site preparation, mulching, and demolition. A unified team, one chain of accountability, one company on the invoice. No subbing the difficult parts.

The fleet is real. Full-size excavators, dozers, and skid steers. Branded trucks, branded equipment, and the kind of presence on a jobsite that tells you the company takes itself seriously. Because we do.
