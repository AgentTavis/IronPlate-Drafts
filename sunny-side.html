Our Story

More Than Just Breakfast, It's a Tradition.

Welcome to Sunny Side, your beloved breakfast haven nestled right here in Hickory, NC. We started with a simple vision: to create a place where every morning feels a little brighter, a little cozier, and a lot more delicious. For years, we've been serving up classic breakfast dishes, made with fresh ingredients and a whole lot of love.

Our commitment to quality and community has made us a local favorite. From sizzling bacon to fluffy pancakes, every plate tells a story of comfort and tradition. We're more than just a place to eat; we're a gathering spot, a starting point for your day, and a reminder of simple pleasures.
