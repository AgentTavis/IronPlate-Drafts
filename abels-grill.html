Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Memory.

Experience the warmth and welcome that makes Abels Grill a beloved Kernersville institution.

At Abels Grill, we believe great food should remind you of home, wherever that may be. Our establishment has been a vibrant part of Kernersville for over a decade, a place where all walks of life gather to eat, laugh, and mingle. It’s a refreshing sight in the South, creating an atmosphere that’s truly unique.

The dedication of our owners shines through in every detail, from the hardworking kitchen staff to the always pleasant waitstaff. We're a true family spot, where you might even see the owner's son playing with dinosaurs at a table – a testament to the genuine, welcoming family feel we cultivate every day.

We're proud to serve up classic American diner fare, breakfast, brunch, and lunch, with an extensive menu built on homestyle cooking principles. Come on in and discover why our regulars say Abel's Grill reminds them of the best spots back in Philadelphia, right here in North Carolina.
