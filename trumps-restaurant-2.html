Our Story

A Plymouth Tradition of Flavor & Family

For years, Trumps Restaurant 2 has been a cherished part of the Plymouth community. Located right beside Dollar Tree on US-64 E, we've built our reputation on serving delicious, homestyle comfort food in a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

We believe in good food, good company, and great value. Our daily buffet offers a fantastic spread of fresh, rotating dishes, ensuring there's always something new and delicious to discover. With 4.5 stars and over 140 happy customers, we're proud to be ranked among Plymouth's top restaurants on Tripadvisor, creating a beloved dining destination for families and friends alike.
