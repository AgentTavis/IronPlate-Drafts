Our Story

More Than Just Coffee – It's a Community

Keeping Ashe County Caffeinated One Shot at a Time, Bohemia Coffee House has been a beloved cornerstone of West Jefferson for years. We believe in crafting exceptional coffee, creating a welcoming space, and celebrating the wonderful people who make it all happen.

Every cup tells a story, and every visit feels like coming home. We're proud to be a gathering place where friends meet, ideas brew, and the mountain air mixes perfectly with the aroma of fresh coffee. Come experience the Bohemia difference.
