Our Journey

Crafting Community, One Cup at a Time

At Palace Coffee | Bakery, our story begins far away in the vibrant coffee farms of El Salvador. We've built direct relationships with farmers who share our passion for quality and sustainability, ensuring every bean tells a story of ethical sourcing and exceptional taste.

Right here in Eugene, we bring those carefully selected beans to life, roasting them in-house to perfection. This commitment to local roasting allows us to deliver unparalleled freshness and to craft unique flavor profiles that truly stand out.

More than just a Bar & Grill, we are a community hub. Every cup brewed and every pastry baked is a testament to our dedication to supporting both people and the planet. We invite you to experience the difference that genuine care, from farm to cup, makes.
