Our Heritage

A Timeless Mountain Dining Experience

Nestled in the heart of Bryson City, The Fryemont Inn offers more than just a meal—it's a journey back to the classic mountain hospitality. For generations, we've prided ourselves on providing a truly unique dining experience where every dinner is a celebration.

All our dinners include a delightful soup of the day, a fresh mixed green salad, your chosen entree, hearty family-style side dishes, and a sweet dessert. We believe in abundance and flavor, ensuring you leave satisfied and eager to return.

Our menu boasts a choice of five different fresh, local mountain trout entrees, alongside beloved classics like lamb shanks, prime rib, NY strip, sirloin, various chicken dishes, Virginia ham, and country ham. Even the little ones have their own special children's menu.

Our commitment to an authentic dining atmosphere hasn't gone unnoticed. The Fryemont Inn's dining room was even proudly featured in this Southern Living article, a testament to our enduring charm and culinary excellence.

"Dining at the Fryemont Inn is like stepping into a cherished memory. The service is impeccable, and the family-style sides bring everyone together. A true Bryson City gem!"

— A Satisfied Guest
