Our Journey

Crafting Unforgettable Moments, One Brew at a Time.

Griffon & Sphynx is more than just a taproom – it's a vibrant hub in the heart of Brevard, NC, where great beer, good company, and live music converge. We pride ourselves on offering a diverse selection of craft beers, ensuring there's always something new and exciting for every palate.

Founded on a passion for quality brewing and community spirit, our friendly team, possibly even the owners themselves, are always on hand to help you navigate our extensive tap list. Whether you're a local or just visiting, we invite you to pull up a seat, sample a flight, and discover your new favorite brew. Plus, we love when you bring your own food to pair with our amazing drinks!
