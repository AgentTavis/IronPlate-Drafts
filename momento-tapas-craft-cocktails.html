Our Story

Passion on Every Plate, Perfection in Every Sip.

At Momento, we believe in creating more than just a meal; we craft an experience. Nestled in the heart of Goldsboro, we've dedicated ourselves to bringing an "upper scale" culinary adventure to our community.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to combine the vibrant flavors of Latin-inspired small plates with an expertly curated selection of craft cocktails. Every dish is a testament to top-of-the-line cuisine, prepared with fresh ingredients and a passion for taste that truly elevates your dining experience.

From the moment you step through our doors, you're invited to "Eat. Sip. Listen." – to savor exceptional food, enjoy delicious drinks, and immerse yourself in an atmosphere designed for unforgettable moments. We're proud to be Goldsboro's go-to for exquisite dining, and an Editor's Pick for Best Tapas in North Carolina.

Our Offerings

Savor the Flavor: Latin-Inspired Small Plates & Craft Cocktails

Patatas Bravas

Crispy fried potatoes tossed in a spicy tomato sauce with a dollop of creamy aioli.

Gambas al Ajillo

Succulent shrimp sizzling in garlic-infused olive oil, chili flakes, and a hint of white wine.

Chorizo a la Sidra

Spanish chorizo slow-cooked in traditional Asturian cider until tender and flavorful.

Tostones con Guacamole

Crispy twice-fried plantain slices served with fresh, house-made guacamole.

Arepas con Queso

Grilled corn cakes filled with gooey melted cheese, a classic Latin comfort food.

Spiced Rum Mojito

Our signature twist on a classic, featuring spiced rum, fresh mint, lime, and a splash of soda.

Smoky Mezcal Old Fashioned

A sophisticated blend of smoky mezcal, agave nectar, bitters, and an orange peel garnish.

Hibiscus Paloma

Tequila, fresh grapefruit juice, and a vibrant homemade hibiscus syrup, topped with soda.
