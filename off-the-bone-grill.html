Our Heritage

More Than Just Food, It's Family.

Established with a simple dream and a big heart, Off The Bone Grill has been a proud cornerstone of the Semora community for years. As a small, family-owned restaurant, we believe in the power of good food, friendly faces, and a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels like family.

Every dish we serve is crafted with passion and local pride, a testament to our commitment to quality and the flavors that bring people together. From our grill to your table, we pour our love into creating memorable dining experiences, one plate at a time.

Our Grill Highlights

While our full menu offers a wider selection, here are a few favorites that keep our community coming back for more. We believe in quality ingredients and hearty portions.

Smoked BBQ Ribs

Slow-smoked to perfection, falling off the bone, served with your choice of savory BBQ sauce.

Classic Cheeseburger

Juicy beef patty, melted cheddar, fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, and our special sauce on a toasted bun.

Crispy Onion Rings

Golden-fried onion rings, perfectly crispy and seasoned, served with a zesty dipping sauce.

Pulled Pork Sandwich

Tender pulled pork, slow-cooked and piled high, drizzled with our tangy BBQ sauce.

Hearty Chili Bowl

Our homemade chili, packed with flavor and warmth, topped with cheese and onions.

Grilled Chicken Salad

Fresh greens, grilled chicken breast, seasonal veggies, and your choice of dressing.
