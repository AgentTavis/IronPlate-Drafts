Our Roots

A Taste of Home, Crafted with Love in Sunbury

Welcome to 32 North Coffeehouse, where every visit feels like coming home. We are a proud family-owned Coffeehouse & Eatery, dedicated to serving the Sunbury community with delicious breakfast and lunch options.

From the first sip of our freshly brewed coffee to the last bite of a delightful pastry, we pour our heart into every item. We believe in simple, quality ingredients and warm, friendly service. Join us Thursday through Saturday, from 6:30 AM to 2:00 PM, for a meal that will brighten your day.
