The Heart Behind Unwine'd

Meet Rosanne & Kevin

Rosanne and Kevin have poured their passion into creating Unwine'd Wine and Spirits – a truly remarkable and unique wine bar nestled in the heart of downtown Richmond, just steps from the scenic Brazos River. More than just a place to grab a drink, Unwine'd is a reflection of their commitment to community, fine beverages, and unforgettable experiences.

They envisioned a welcoming space where friends could gather, families could celebrate, and memories could be made. From carefully curated wine selections to handcrafted cocktails and delicious small plates, every detail at Unwine'd is thoughtfully designed to offer a premium yet approachable atmosphere for locals and visitors alike.
