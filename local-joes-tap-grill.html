Our Story

More Than Just Food, It's Family.

At Local Joes Tap & Grill, we believe great food tastes even better with great company. We're not just serving meals; we're creating memories. Our dedicated servers quickly get to know our regulars, often greeting you by name and remembering your favorite drink—even if it's been months!

The food here is simply amazing. It's that simple. From our hearty wings to our mouth-watering pizzas, every dish is crafted to satisfy. While a single order of wings or a pizza is enough to fill you up, many of our guests, like the reviewer who loved the Rio Rancho and Joe's pizzas with hot/garlic wings, can't resist getting both because it's just *that* good. Come experience the Local Joes difference!

"This place is the absolute best! The servers get to know you quickly and even if we don't go for 6 months, they will greet us by name and drink! The food is amazing. It's that simple."

— A Loyal Regular
