Our Story

Crafting Culinary Experiences, One Plate at a Time.

At Factory 29, we believe that dining is more than just a meal—it's an experience built with intention and passion. Nestled in the heart of Marion, NC, we've dedicated ourselves to assembling rich flavors and crafting exquisite cocktails in an atmosphere designed for comfort and connection.

Inspired by the fresh tastes of the sea and the hearty traditions of Southern American cuisine, our kitchen meticulously prepares each dish. From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a guest; you're part of our story, built one perfect plate at a time.
