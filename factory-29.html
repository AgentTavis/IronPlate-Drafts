Our Story

Crafting Culinary Excellence in Marion

At Factory 29, we believe in building more than just great meals – we're crafting an unforgettable experience. Nestled in the heart of Marion, NC, we pride ourselves on serving the freshest seafood, perfectly cooked, alongside Southern American classics and handcrafted cocktails.

From our savory crab cakes to our perfectly grilled fish, every dish is a testament to our passion for flavor and quality. We source the best ingredients to ensure each plate is a masterpiece, designed to delight your senses. Come discover why Factory 29 is Marion's favorite spot to gather, relax, and enjoy truly well-built food.
