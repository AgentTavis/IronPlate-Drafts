Our Journey

From Our Kitchen to Your Table

Central Cafe has been a cherished part of the Dobson community for decades, serving up classic Southern fare with a genuine touch that feels just like home. While our address may have changed, our heart remains the same.

As of today, we've settled into our wonderful new home at 304 N. Main Street in Dobson. You can expect the same great food, the same friendly service, and the same warm atmosphere you've always loved. We're excited to continue creating delicious memories with you and your family in our renewed space.

Our Promise
