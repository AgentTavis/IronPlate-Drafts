Our Story

Where Every Meal Feels Like Home

Welcome to 133 west, Kings Mountain's cherished local diner. For years, we've been dedicated to serving up delicious meals in a warm, friendly atmosphere. We're proud to be known as our community's favorite place for a nice meal, a reputation built on consistent quality and heartfelt service.

Our commitment extends to catering to all tastes, with fresh vegetarian options always available. We believe in the personal touch, and you'll often find our owner right here, ensuring every plate that leaves our kitchen and every guest experience is nothing short of excellent. Our menu is ever-evolving, constantly featuring new and delicious specials that keep our regulars coming back for more.
