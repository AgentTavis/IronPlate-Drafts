OUR STORY

A Taste of Home, Rooted in Community

At 133 west, we're more than just a diner – we're a cherished gathering place for the Kings Mountain community. Locals consistently tell us it's their favorite spot for a nice meal, often praising our exceptional Sunday brunch, which many consider the best they've ever had. We pride ourselves on offering diverse options, with delicious vegetarian choices always available.

Our dedicated staff and the owner, who is almost always on-site, work tirelessly to ensure every guest enjoys great food and unparalleled service. With a menu that's always changing to feature new and delicious specials, there's always something fresh to discover at 133 west.
