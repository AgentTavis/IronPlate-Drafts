Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

Welcome to The Clubhouse Grill, a hidden gem nestled in Hillman, NC. While our name suggests a traditional grill, we offer an experience that transports you far beyond. Picture yourself embarking on a horse-drawn sleigh ride through picturesque woods, a warm drink in hand, as our knowledgeable staff share fascinating insights about local wildlife and the history of our resort.

Once you arrive at our cozy cabin, you're not just dining; you're wined and dined. Chef Spencer and our dedicated team are committed to making you feel right at home, ensuring you have an unforgettable culinary experience that perfectly complements the rustic charm of your journey.

"The entire experience was magical! From the serene sleigh ride with Jack, learning about the local elk, to Chef Spencer's incredible dinner at the cabin, every detail was perfect. The Clubhouse Grill truly delivers an unforgettable evening."

- A Delighted Guest
