Our Heritage

Experience the Authentic Flavors of Jalisco

Historically, Tlaquepaque is a city and the surrounding municipality in the Mexican state of Jalisco. The name Tlaquepaque derives from Nahuatl and means "Place above clay land".

At Tlaquepaque Mexican Grille & Restaurant, we honor this rich heritage by bringing the vibrant, authentic flavors of Jalisco to Dobson, NC. Our dishes are crafted with passion and traditional recipes, ensuring every meal is a journey to the heart of Mexico.

We pride ourselves on creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where families and friends can gather to enjoy delicious food and create lasting memories. From our family to yours, we invite you to savor the true taste of Mexico.
