Our Story

Where Millersville Gathers for Great Beer & Good Times.

Raney Cellars Brewing Company is more than just a brewery; it's a beloved local spot in Millersville, NC, where friends and family gather to create lasting memories. We're passionate about crafting exceptional beers and fostering a vibrant community spirit.

Our commitment to quality, local partnerships, and unforgettable experiences has made us a cornerstone of Millersville. From our signature Raney Pilsner to exciting food truck events, there's always something new to discover and enjoy.
