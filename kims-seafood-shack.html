Our Journey

Our Delicious Offerings

Savor the Flavors of the Sea

Blackened Catfish

Our famous, perfectly seasoned catfish fillet, seared to a delicious crisp on the outside while staying tender and flaky within. A true house specialty you won't want to miss!

Fried Shrimp Basket

Succulent shrimp, lightly breaded and fried to golden perfection. Served with our signature tangy cocktail sauce and crispy fries.

Grilled Salmon

A fresh fillet of Atlantic salmon, seasoned and grilled to your liking. A healthy and flavorful option, served with your choice of side.

Fish & Chips

Classic battered white fish, fried until golden brown, served alongside a generous portion of our crispy hand-cut fries and zesty tartar sauce.

Crab Cakes

Homemade crab cakes, packed with lump crab meat and our special blend of seasonings, pan-seared to a golden finish. A delightful appetizer or main course.

Clam Chowder

A rich and creamy New England style clam chowder, loaded with tender clams, potatoes, and savory herbs. Perfect for a cozy start to your meal.
