Our Story

A Taste of the Coast, Right Here in Morganton.

Kim's Seafood Shack is more than just a restaurant; it's a cozy haven in Morganton, NC, where the love for fresh seafood takes center stage. We believe in serving up well-prepared dishes in a casual, welcoming setting, making every visit feel like a mini-vacation to the coast.

Our commitment is simple: bring you the finest, freshest flavors from the sea, crafted with care and served with a smile. From our perfectly seasoned blackened catfish to our daily specials, we ensure every plate delivers an unforgettable dining experience.
