Our Story

More Than Just a Bar, It's Your Local Hub.

Welcome to Backstage Pub, Cary's beloved local spot where good times and great company are always on tap. We're not just a bar; we're a community gathering place where friendly faces and top-notch bartenders create an atmosphere you won't find anywhere else.

Step inside and discover endless entertainment: we've got a full-size pool table, an exhilarating shuffleboard table, four league-quality dart boards, and cornhole out back. With 10 TVs playing all the big games, you won't miss a single moment of the action. And while we don't have a full kitchen, we've got you covered with tasty snacks and frozen pizzas to curb any munchies. Plus, we're DoorDash-friendly, so you can order your favorite meal right to your seat!

Come for the games, stay for the company. Our regulars are like family, and new friends are always welcome. Backstage Pub is where memories are made, one drink and one game at a time. Can't find better!
