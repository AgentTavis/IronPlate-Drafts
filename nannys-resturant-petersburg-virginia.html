Our Heritage

A Legacy of Flavor, Family, and Southern Hospitality.

Nanny's Restaurant began with a simple philosophy: serve honest, homemade food with generous portions and genuine care. From the moment you step in, you're not just a customer; you're family.

Our journey started with our legendary fried chicken – perfectly crunchy on the outside, incredibly moist and seasoned to perfection on the inside. It was a true delight that quickly became a local sensation. But it didn't stop there.

Our mashed potatoes are the genuine article, never cutting corners with mixes, just real, comforting goodness. And the greens? Seasoned wonderfully with a hint of smoke and rich porky flavor, they're truly satisfying. Even our pork, which some might dread, is tender and pulls right off the bone, a testament to our slow-cooked tradition.

"Nanny's is more than a meal; it's a taste of home. The fried chicken is a masterpiece, and those greens? Absolutely unforgettable. You can taste the love in every bite!"

— A Loyal Customer, Petersburg, VA
