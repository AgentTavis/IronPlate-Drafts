Our Heritage

Serving Fayetteville's Favorite Seafood Since 1992

Hudson Bay Seafood isn't just a restaurant; it's a Fayetteville tradition. We've been a proud family-owned and operated establishment since 1992, serving our community from the very same location on Ramsey Street for over three decades.

Our commitment to fresh, delicious seafood and a warm, inviting atmosphere has always been at the heart of what we do. We believe in honest cooking and generous portions, bringing you the flavors of the sea with a comforting, home-style touch.
