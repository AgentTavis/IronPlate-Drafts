Our Heritage

More Than Just a Brew, It's a Community

At Sorella Coffee, we believe in the simple joy of coming together. Nestled in the heart of Monroe, NC, our Bar & Grill has been a beloved local spot where friends, family, and neighbors connect over great food and exceptional coffee. We're not just serving meals; we're crafting experiences.

From the moment you step through our doors, you'll feel the warmth of our welcoming atmosphere, designed to be your comfortable escape from the everyday. While our name highlights our passion for coffee, our menu embraces the hearty, satisfying spirit of a classic Bar & Grill, with dishes crafted to delight every palate.

And for those seeking unique treasures, explore our curated collection of goods right here in our space. Whether you're savoring a meal, enjoying a perfect cup, or discovering a special gift, Sorella Coffee is your destination. Quick shop for unique finds here!

Our Offerings

Savor the Flavors of Sorella

While our name is Sorella Coffee, our kitchen masterfully prepares a range of delightful Bar & Grill fare and exquisite coffee creations. Explore a taste of what awaits you.

Artisan Coffees

From rich espresso drinks to creamy lattes and robust pour-overs, handcrafted with the finest beans to start your day right or for an afternoon pick-me-up.

Gourmet Sandwiches

Freshly prepared sandwiches and wraps featuring premium meats, cheeses, and crisp vegetables, served on artisan breads for the perfect lunch or light dinner.

Hearty Salads

Vibrant salads made with the freshest seasonal ingredients, offering a healthy yet satisfying option for any meal, with homemade dressings.

Signature Burgers

Juicy, flavorful burgers made with high-quality beef or vegetarian patties, customizable with a variety of toppings and served with your choice of side.

Comfort Plates

Classic Bar & Grill comfort food dishes, expertly prepared to evoke warmth and nostalgia with every bite, perfect for a fulfilling meal.

Sweet Treats

Indulge in our selection of freshly baked pastries, cakes, and desserts, the perfect accompaniment to your coffee or a delightful end to your meal.
