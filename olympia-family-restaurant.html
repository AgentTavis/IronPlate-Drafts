Our Heritage

A Taste of Home, Rooted in Tradition

Olympia Family Restaurant brings the rich tradition of Greek diners to the heart of Mt Airy, NC. Just like the beloved Greek diners in Florida offer a fresh seafood selection, we proudly serve a carefully curated selection of local cuisine alongside diner classics. From savory Malts to hearty Corn Beef sandwiches, every dish is prepared with the warmth and care of a home-cooked meal.

We believe in honest food, generous portions, and a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels like family. Our commitment is to quality ingredients and time-honored recipes, ensuring every visit is a memorable experience. Come join us and discover why Olympia has been a cherished part of the Mt Airy community for generations.
