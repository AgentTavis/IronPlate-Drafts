Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Mt Airy Tradition

Welcome to Olympia Family Restaurant, a beloved Mt Airy institution with a heart deeply rooted in Greek tradition and a menu crafted for local tastes. Just like Greek diners in bustling Florida cities adapt their offerings to include fresh seafood, we've brought a unique blend of classic Greek hospitality and comforting Southern flavors to North Carolina.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a place where everyone feels like family, enjoying hearty, home-cooked meals made with care. From our savory Country Style Steak on Mondays to our comforting Chicken and Dumplings on Tuesdays, we take pride in serving the dishes that Mt Airy loves, alongside timeless diner classics like malts and robust corn beef sandwiches.

It's this blend of heritage and local appreciation that makes Olympia truly special. We invite you to experience the warmth, the flavor, and the community that defines us. Come by, pull up a chair, and let us serve you a taste of home.
