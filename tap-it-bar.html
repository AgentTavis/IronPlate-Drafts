Our Journey

More Than Just a Bar, We're Your Local Experience.

Tap It Bar in Smithfield, NC is a popular local spot known for its great beer selection, handcrafted cocktails, and fast, friendly service. With a casual and cozy atmosphere, it's the perfect place to unwind after a long day or catch up with friends.

We're proud to be a vibrant part of the Smithfield community, bringing you exceptional live music, delicious drinks, and memorable nights. Every visit is an opportunity to create new memories and enjoy the best of local hospitality.

Explore Our Unbeatable Selection of Drinks.

Craft Beer Selection

Dive into our curated collection of local brews and national favorites. From crisp lagers to hoppy IPAs and rich stouts, our taps are always flowing with something new to discover.

Signature Cocktails

Experience expertly mixed cocktails crafted with premium spirits and fresh ingredients. Our bartenders are ready to whip up your classic favorites or surprise you with a unique house special.

Classic Bar Bites

Perfectly complementing your drink, our selection of delicious and easy-to-share bar bites are ideal for fueling your night of music and good times.

Weekend Specials

Always something new! Ask about our rotating drink and food specials, featuring seasonal ingredients and innovative concoctions designed to excite your palate.

Premium Spirits

For the connoisseur, our back bar boasts an impressive array of premium whiskies, vodkas, gins, and rums from around the world.

Non-Alcoholic Options

Enjoy refreshing mocktails, specialty sodas, and juices, ensuring everyone has a fantastic beverage choice, spirits or not.
