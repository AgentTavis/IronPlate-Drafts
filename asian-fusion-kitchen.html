Our Journey

Authentic Flavors, Family Roots.

At Asian Fusion Kitchen, we believe in bringing the vibrant, authentic tastes of Southeast Asia right here to Morganton. Our journey began with a passion for traditional recipes and a desire to share the rich culinary heritage of our homelands. Every dish on our menu is a testament to fresh ingredients, meticulous preparation, and a touch of heartfelt tradition.

We're more than just a restaurant; we're a community hub where friends and families gather to explore new flavors and enjoy timeless favorites. With over 541 positive reviews praising our authentic cuisine and warm atmosphere, and being rated 4.6 stars, we're proud to be a local favorite, consistently ranked among the best dining experiences in Morganton.

Our Menu

Culinary Delights from Southeast Asia

Lettuce Wrap

Fresh, crisp lettuce cups filled with savory seasoned meat, fresh herbs, and a tangy dipping sauce. A light and flavorful start to your meal.

Steak Salad

Tender grilled steak thinly sliced and tossed with fresh greens, cucumbers, tomatoes, and a vibrant, spicy dressing. A refreshing and hearty choice.

Tom Yum Goong Soup

The iconic hot and sour soup, brimming with succulent shrimp, mushrooms, lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves. A symphony of aromatic flavors.

Pad Thai Noodles

Classic stir-fried rice noodles with your choice of protein, eggs, tofu, bean sprouts, and crushed peanuts in our signature sweet and savory tamarind sauce.

Thai Fried Rice

Fragrant jasmine rice stir-fried with your choice of protein, eggs, onions, and crisp vegetables, seasoned to perfection with traditional Thai spices.

Crispy Egg Rolls

Hand-rolled to perfection, these golden fried egg rolls are filled with savory minced pork and vegetables, served with our sweet chili dipping sauce.
