Our Story

More Than Just Coffee & Ice Cream – It's a Community.

At Higher Grounds, we're more than just your local coffee shop; we're a beloved fixture in Kill Devil Hills, a place where the community of Colington and all our wonderful OBX visitors feel right at home. We believe in crafting exceptional coffee and serving up scoops of happiness, all with a warm, welcoming smile.

Our regulars rave about our unique creations. As one review put it, “Their coffee is so good but you have to try a RipTide, or The Overboard or an Iced Latte, a sundae or a waffle cone filled with deliciousness.” We pour our passion into every cup and cone, making sure each visit is a memorable one.

From the moment you step in, you'll feel the inviting vibe. Whether you're grabbing a quick morning boost or lingering with an afternoon treat on our dog-friendly patio, Higher Grounds is your perfect escape.
