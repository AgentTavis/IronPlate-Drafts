Our Story & Values

Your Coastal Stop for Fresh Coffee & Delicious Comfort Food.

Nestled in a convenient spot in Morehead City, Noontide Coffee + Kitchen might be in a nondescript strip mall, but step inside and you're welcomed into a very bright, clean, and inviting space. We believe that great food and coffee are best served with a smile, and our friendly, helpful staff are always ready to make your day a little brighter.

From the perfect drip coffee to a robust double shot of espresso, we craft every drink with care. But it's our food that truly shines – especially our FANTASTIC bacon, egg, and cheese sandwiches, renowned for their perfectly peppery bacon. We're more than just a coffee shop; we're a community kitchen where quality ingredients and warm service come together to create unforgettable moments. Come on in and experience the Noontide difference!
