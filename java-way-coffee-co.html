Our Story

More Than Just Coffee, It's Community.

Welcome to Java Way Coffee Co, your friendly neighborhood diner in the heart of Murfreesboro, NC. We believe in crafting more than just delicious beverages and bites; we're passionate about creating a warm, welcoming space where connections are brewed and memories are made.

We're proud to exclusively feature Counter Culture Coffee Beans, an amazing NC roastery known for their ethically sourced and incredibly flavorful beans. Every cup is a testament to our commitment to quality, expertly prepared by our incredibly friendly and knowledgeable staff.

From the moment you walk in, you're family. Our baristas thrive on getting to know you, often greeting repeat customers by name and knowing exactly what they'd like before they even say it. We strive to make your coffee exactly how you want it, ensuring every visit is a perfect one. It's truly a wonderful small town coffee shop, and we think you'll find it just as fabulous as we do.
