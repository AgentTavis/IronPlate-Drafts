Our Journey

Crafting Good Times, One Mugg at a Time.

Welcome to Mugg Shots, the heart of Southern Granville County's social scene. More than just a bar and grill, we're a cherished local hub where friends gather, stories are shared, and every visit feels like coming home.

Established with a passion for good company and great drinks, we pride ourselves on offering an expansive selection of fine wines, an impressive array of draft and bottled beers, and expertly brewed coffee. Our space is designed for comfort, camaraderie, and unforgettable moments.
