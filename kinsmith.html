Our Heritage

Crafting Unforgettable BBQ Memories in Montgomery, NC

Welcome to Kinsmith, where every dish tells a story of passion, tradition, and community. Nestled in the heart of Montgomery, NC, we've dedicated ourselves to perfecting the art of BBQ, blending classic Southern flavors with a refined culinary touch. Our commitment is simple: to create memorable dining experiences that bring people together.

We believe great food starts with exceptional ingredients and a team that genuinely cares. From our slow-smoked meats to our handcrafted sides, every detail is thoughtfully prepared to deliver a taste that keeps you coming back for more. Come join our family at Kinsmith – where delicious food and warm hospitality are always on the menu.
