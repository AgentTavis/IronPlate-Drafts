Our Story

The Heart of Jacksonville's Coffee Scene

At Jolie's Frothy Coffee, we believe that great coffee is more than just a drink—it's an experience. Nestled in the heart of Jacksonville, NC, we've poured our passion into crafting the perfect cup, combining the finest beans with a touch of frothy artistry.

From classic lattes to unique Italian-inspired concoctions and refreshing Horchata, every beverage is made with love and a commitment to bring a smile to your face. We cherish our community and strive to be a welcoming hub where friends gather, ideas brew, and everyday moments become special. Come join our family and discover why we're a local favorite.
