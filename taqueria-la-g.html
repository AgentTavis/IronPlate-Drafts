Our Story

A Taste of Tradition, Crafted with Passion.

Taqueria La G brings the heart of Mexico to Danville, NC. We're passionate about serving traditional Mexican food, crafted with authentic recipes and the freshest ingredients. Our modern ambiance, paired with vibrant live music, creates an exceptional dining experience that earned us 'Favorite Mexican Restaurant' in 2022 and 'Best Of Best' in 2023.

As a proud sister restaurant to Taqueria La Guadalupana, we uphold a legacy of culinary excellence and warm hospitality, inviting you to discover new favorite flavors and create lasting memories with family and friends. Come experience the difference that passion makes.
