Our Roots

A Journey of Passion, Flavor, and Resilience.

Roots Wine Bar has embarked on an exciting new chapter since the beginning of 2024, now under the passionate ownership of Suzani Asmlash Grant. An accomplished entrepreneur, Suzani's story is one of incredible resilience and vision. Born in Ethiopia, she navigated the tumultuous years of her country's civil war, ultimately finding a new home in the United States in 1999, initially settling in Las Vegas.

Her journey led her to Houston in 2008, where she met her husband Gary Grant. With a deep love for hospitality and culinary arts, Suzani successfully launched Lucy Ethiopian Restaurant in 2012, a beloved establishment she expertly managed for over a decade. Now, at Roots, she channels her extensive experience and entrepreneurial spirit into crafting an extraordinary dining experience.

Roots Wine Bar is more than just a restaurant; it's a testament to Suzani's enduring passion for exquisite flavors and inviting atmospheres. We blend refined seafood dishes, delectable small plates, and a carefully curated wine selection, inviting you to be part of a story where every dish has a soul, and every visit feels like coming home.

"Suzani's dedication to quality and hospitality shines through at Roots. Every detail, from the ambiance to the flavors, is thoughtfully executed." — A Satisfied Diner
