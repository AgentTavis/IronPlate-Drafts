Our Journey

Taste the Tradition, Experience the Passion.

At Milano's, we believe in the power of a truly great meal. Tucked away on North Main Street, we've been delighting guests with our passion for authentic Italian flavors. Our secret? It's all in the sauce – that rich, slow-simmered red sauce that transforms every dish, from our famous hand-tossed pizzas to our irresistible mozzarella sticks.

We're proud to be a local favorite in Warrenton, a place where every guest feels like family and every meal is crafted with care. Come discover the flavors that surprise and satisfy, right here at Milano's.

Our Creations

Savor the Flavors of Milano's

Hand-Tossed Pepperoni Pizza

Classic perfection: crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, premium pepperoni, and melted mozzarella.

Classic Lasagna

Layers of tender pasta, savory ground beef, creamy ricotta, and our signature marinara sauce, baked to perfection.

Spaghetti with Marinara

Timeless comfort: al dente spaghetti tossed in our beloved, slow-simmered red sauce.

Chicken Parmigiana

Breaded chicken breast, lightly fried, topped with marinara and melted mozzarella, served with pasta.

Mozzarella Sticks

Golden fried mozzarella, served hot and gooey with a side of our famous, flavorful marinara dipping sauce.

Fresh Garden Salad

Crisp mixed greens, fresh vegetables, and your choice of dressing for a refreshing start.

Garlic Knots

Warm, soft knots of dough baked with garlic, olive oil, and fresh parsley, perfect for dipping.

Tiramisu

A classic Italian dessert with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cheese, and cocoa.
