Our Heritage

Where Every Meal Tells a Story of Southern Hospitality.

At Mary's Country Kitchen, we believe in honest food, prepared with heart. Located at 812 Springfield Rd, our humble diner might not be fancy, but the flavors speak for themselves. Mary herself crafts a powerful good hamburger, hand-pattied from ground round and cooked to perfection on a griddle that's seasoned with years of flavor. Bet it goes half a pound!

We're proud to serve up meals that fill your belly and warm your soul. Whether you're stopping by for a hearty breakfast, a satisfying lunch, or a comforting dinner, you'll find quality food and agreeable service. Our home fries are the perfect accompaniment to our famous burgers, creating a meal so substantial, you'll come in hungry and leave satisfied.
