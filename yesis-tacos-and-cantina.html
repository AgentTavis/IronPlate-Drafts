Our Heritage

From Our Family Kitchen to Your Table

At Yesi's Tacos and Cantina, we believe in bringing the vibrant flavors and warm hospitality of Mexico straight to Morganton. Opened on October 2nd, our restaurant is a dream come true, built on a passion for authentic Mexican cuisine and a desire to create a welcoming space for our community.

Every dish at Yesi's is crafted with love, using traditional recipes and fresh ingredients to ensure an unforgettable dining experience. Whether you're craving classic Huevos Rancheros to start your day or the zesty crunch of our Baja Tacos, you'll taste the dedication in every bite.

Our Dishes

Savor the Authentic Flavors of Mexico

Huevos Rancheros

A classic Mexican breakfast featuring fried eggs served on lightly fried corn tortillas, topped with a flavorful ranchera sauce, refried beans, and a sprinkle of fresh cheese.

Baja Tacos

Crispy fried fish or shrimp, nestled in warm tortillas, topped with fresh cabbage, creamy chipotle sauce, and a squeeze of lime for that perfect coastal zest.
