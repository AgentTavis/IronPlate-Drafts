Our Journey

Where Great Tastes & Good Times Meet

Taps on Main is more than just a bar; it's a vibrant hub located right in the heart of Tarboro, NC. We've cultivated a cozy and inviting atmosphere where locals and visitors alike can unwind, share stories, and create lasting memories.

Our passion lies in offering a diverse selection of craft and domestic beers, fine wines, and expertly mixed cocktails, ensuring there's always something new and exciting to discover. From a quiet evening out to lively Saturday nights filled with local music, Taps on Main is designed to be your favorite neighborhood spot.

Come experience the warmth, the flavor, and the community spirit that makes Taps on Main a true Tarboro gem. We're dedicated to providing an exceptional experience with every pour and every smile.
