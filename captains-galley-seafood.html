Our Journey

A Taste of Tradition, Crafted with Love.

Experience the Heart of Granite Falls' Culinary Scene.

Captain's Galley Seafood is a cherished local gem in Granite Falls, NC, offering a warm and welcoming dining experience for lunch and dinner. We've built our reputation on serving delicious American and Southern-style seafood, alongside a variety of other beloved dishes that promise to satisfy every palate.

We pride ourselves on being a family-friendly establishment, complete with highchairs and accessible seating to ensure comfort for all our guests. Whether you're dining in, enjoying our attentive table service, or grabbing a convenient takeout meal, you'll find quality food and a friendly atmosphere awaiting you. Come and discover why we're a true Granite Falls favorite!
