Our Heritage

Bringing Generations of Flavor to Granite Falls

At Captain's Galley, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a cherished Granite Falls tradition. For over two decades, we've been dedicated to serving our community the freshest seafood and heartwarming American dishes, prepared with the same care and authentic recipes that have stood the test of time.

From our family to yours, we believe in hearty portions, exceptional taste, and a welcoming atmosphere where every meal feels like a special occasion. We're proud to be a gathering place where memories are made over plates of delicious, home-style cooking.
