Our Story

A Sweet Escape in the Heart of Matthews.

Taste the Charm of Europe, Right Here in Matthews.

Innescas Sweets of Europe is a delightful cafe in Matthews, NC, a light-filled haven brimming with the aroma of freshly baked goods. We pride ourselves on offering a wide array of beautiful, in-house baked pastries and a killer cappuccino, all served in a bright and clean atmosphere perfect for a sweet escape.

Our cozy space features a few tables, inviting you to relax and savor a taste of Europe right here in Charlotte, North Carolina. We are incredibly thankful to be a beloved local coffee shop, committed to bringing joy with every treat.
