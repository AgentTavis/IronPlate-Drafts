Our Story

Bringing Authentic Italian Flavors to Burlington

Welcome to Brick City Kitchen, your local destination for delicious Italian cuisine in Burlington, NC. We're proud to serve our community with freshly prepared dishes and a warm, inviting atmosphere.

You can find us conveniently located at 1250 Plaza Drive, inside the parking lot of J&R Liquidation BMOC. We're open Tuesday through Saturday, serving lunch and dinner from 12 PM to 7 PM, ensuring you can always find a moment to savor our menu. We are closed on Sundays and Mondays to ensure our team is rested and ready to provide the best experience.
