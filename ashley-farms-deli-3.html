Our Story

More Than Just a Deli, It's Hays' Comfort Spot

Ashley Farm's Deli 3 isn't just a place to grab a bite; it's a staple in the Hays community. Open daily from 5:00 AM to 8:00 PM, we've built our reputation on serving up delicious, dependable meals with a warm, welcoming smile.

We cater mostly to the lunch crowd and solo diners, providing an accommodating space for a laid-back meal. Whether you're starting your day with a hot cup of coffee, stopping by for a hearty lunch, or grabbing a quick, satisfying dinner, you'll find quality food and friendly faces here. We're proud to be your neighborhood spot where good food and good company come together.
