Our Story

Rooted in Community

Ashley Farm’s Deli 3, nestled in the heart of Hays, NC, is more than just a diner – it's a daily gathering spot. Open from 5:00 AM to 8:00 PM, we proudly serve our community with delicious meals and a welcoming atmosphere.

We cater mostly to the lunch crowd and solo diners, providing an accommodating space for a laid-back meal. Whether you’re stopping by for a quick bite or enjoying a leisurely lunch, you’ll find friendly faces and satisfying food that feels like home.

We're proud to be Ashley Farms #3 Food Mart, bringing Hays the quality and convenience you expect, including our famous Hunt Brothers® Pizza, ready to order ahead or online!
