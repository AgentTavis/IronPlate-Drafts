Our Story

A Taste of Tradition, Crafted with Love.

New China Inn, nestled in the heart of Siler City, NC, is more than just a restaurant – it's a culinary journey to the heart of authentic Chinese cuisine. We've dedicated ourselves to crafting dishes that delight the palate and transport you with every bite.
