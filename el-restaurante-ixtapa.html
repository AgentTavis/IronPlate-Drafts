Our Heritage

Taste the Heart of Mexico, Crafted by the Muñoz Family.

At El Restaurante Ixtapa, the Muñoz family welcomes you to a culinary journey rooted in authentic Mexican tradition. We pour our heart into every dish, creating flavors that transport you straight to Ixtapa, Mexico, right here in Hillsborough, NC.

Always ask about our daily specials for fresh, seasonal delights. On weekends, our seafood offerings expand, bringing you the freshest catch. We're proud to say locals rave about our fish tacos, shrimp tacos, and chicken flautas – often called the best in the area!

Don't miss our mixed beef, chicken, and shrimp fajitas – a truly yummy feast that comfortably feeds two hungry people, making it an excellent take-out choice. And for those in the know, our sopa de camaron (shrimp soup) is a world-class dish, praised for its incredible broth alone!

"The Sopa de Camaron is truly world-class; that broth is something else! A must-try every time."

— A Regular Ixtapa Diner
