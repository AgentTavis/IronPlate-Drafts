Our Journey

From Iron Wok's Legacy to Otoro's Sizzle

If you remember the old Iron Wok, you'll be thrilled to know this spot has taken over and is now Havelock's new destination for incredible dining. At OTORO HIBACHI HAVELOCK, we proudly continue a tradition of excellence, bringing the vibrant flavors and engaging experience of authentic Japanese cuisine to our community.

We specialize in dynamic Hibachi, where our skilled chefs prepare your meal right before your eyes – featuring sizzling steak, succulent shrimp, and tender scallops, all cooked to perfection. Beyond the grill, our dedicated Sushi Bar offers a tempting array of fresh, expertly crafted sushi rolls, ensuring there's something to delight every palate.

We're passionate about great food, warm hospitality, and creating memorable experiences for all our guests. Come create new memories with us.
