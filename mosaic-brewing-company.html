Our Journey

The Heart & Soul of Cleveland's Culinary Scene

Mosaic Brewing Company is more than just a restaurant; it's a beloved local spot in Cleveland, NC, where community gathers and flavors come alive. We've dedicated ourselves to creating an inviting atmosphere where every guest feels like family, enjoying exceptional seafood and thoughtfully crafted dishes.

Our passion for quality drives everything we do, from sourcing the freshest ingredients to preparing each meal with meticulous care. We believe in celebrating the bounty of the coast and bringing that authentic experience right to your table, blended with the warmth of Southern hospitality.
