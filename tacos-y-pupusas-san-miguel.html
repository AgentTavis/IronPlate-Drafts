Our Signature Dishes

Irresistible Flavors You'll Love

Pupusas Revueltas

A Salvadoran classic! Thick corn tortillas stuffed with a savory blend of cheese, pork, and refried beans, grilled to golden perfection.

Tacos Al Pastor

Flavorful marinated pork, slowly cooked on a vertical spit, served in warm tortillas with pineapple, onion, and cilantro.

Quesabirrias

Our renowned birria-style beef tacos with melted cheese, served with a side of rich consommé for dipping. A unique flavor experience!

Panes con Pollo

A hearty Salvadoran sandwich featuring seasoned chicken, fresh vegetables, and savory sauce, all nestled in a soft bun.

Yuca con Chicharrón

Crispy fried yuca root served alongside tender, flavorful fried pork rinds, a delightful combination of textures and tastes.

Horchata Fresca

A refreshing traditional beverage made from rice, milk, cinnamon, and a hint of vanilla. The perfect complement to any meal.
