Our Heritage

A Taste of Coastal Tradition, Crafted with Passion.

Welcome to The Kill Devil Grill, a beloved local spot where delicious food and warm hospitality have been our hallmarks for years. We pride ourselves on crafting memorable dining experiences, whether you're joining us for a casual lunch or a special dinner. From our legendary Jumbo Lump Crab Cakes and perfectly Fried Shrimp to innovative specials like our famous Cheese Steak Egg Rolls, every dish is prepared with passion and the freshest ingredients.

Our menu offers a delightful array of American classics and fresh seafood, ensuring there's something to tempt every palate. We're also known for our exceptional service – our team, including stars like Lisette, goes above and beyond to make every visit special, ensuring your drinks arrive quickly and your meal is nothing short of perfect. Don't forget to ask about our incredible, light and airy onion rings, a secret menu favorite! Come discover why The Kill Devil Grill is consistently rated 4.8 stars by thousands of happy guests.
