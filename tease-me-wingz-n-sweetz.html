Our Journey

Yanceyville's Flavor Destination

Welcome to Tease Me Wingz N' Sweetz, Yanceyville's vibrant spot for unforgettable flavors! Located at 1088 NC Highway 86 N, we're a casual dining establishment dedicated to tantalizing your taste buds. We proudly offer an incredible selection of 35 uniquely delicious and seductively good flavored wingz, alongside a decadent array of deep-fried desserts.

From crispy, saucy wings to our signature Stuffed Cones and Classic Funnel Cakes, every visit is a celebration of flavor. Come discover your new favorite craving in a friendly, local atmosphere where good food and good times are always on the menu. We're more than just a bar and grill; we're a community hub where delicious food and sweet memories are made.
