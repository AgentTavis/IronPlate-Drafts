OUR STORY

A Taste of Local Tradition

Melanie's on Howard isn't just a diner; it's a culinary cornerstone in downtown Boone. For years, we've welcomed locals and visitors alike to our cozy indoor and charming outdoor patio spaces, serving up unforgettable breakfast and lunch experiences.

Our eclectic menu is a testament to fresh, local ingredients and homemade comfort. From decadent Benedicts and fluffy Fruit Waffles to hearty Reubens and juicy Burgers, every dish is crafted with passion. We believe good food should be for everyone, which is why we proudly offer a variety of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options.

Complement your meal with a rich mug of freshly brewed Hatchet Coffee, a velvety cappuccino, a glass of vibrant fresh-squeezed juice, or a selection from our full bar. At Melanie's, every visit is an invitation to slow down, savor, and create cherished memories around the table.
