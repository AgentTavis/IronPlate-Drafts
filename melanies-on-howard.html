OUR STORY

Where Comfort Meets Community

Nestled in the heart of downtown Boone, Melanie's on Howard has been a beloved diner for locals and visitors alike. We pride ourselves on offering a full-service dining experience, whether you prefer the cozy ambiance of our indoor seating or the vibrant energy of our outdoor patio.

Our eclectic menu is a testament to fresh, local, and homemade comfort food, carefully crafted to delight every palate. From classic Benedicts and sweet Fruit Waffles to hearty Reubens and juicy Burgers, we believe every meal should be an experience. We're also proud to offer a wide array of options for our vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free guests, ensuring everyone finds something delicious to savor.

Complement your meal with a rich mug of freshly brewed Hatchet Coffee, a velvety cappuccino, a glass of invigorating fresh-squeezed juice, or choose from our full bar selection for the perfect pairing. Come in and discover why Melanie's is more than just a diner – it's a cornerstone of the Boone community.
