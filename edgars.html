Our Legacy

More Than Just a Bar, It's Edgar's.

Nestled in the heart of Hendersonville, Edgar's has carved out a unique spot as the go-to Bar & Grill for those seeking good times, great music, and even better company. We're more than just a place to grab a drink; we're a community hub where memories are made.

As one of the few full-service restaurants open late in the area, we pride ourselves on being there for you when hunger strikes, even after 10 PM. Our full bar, carefully curated for both classic and contemporary tastes, complements our lively atmosphere and unforgettable live music nights. We're a 21+ establishment, ensuring an adult-friendly environment for relaxation and revelry.

When you step into Edgar's, you're not just a customer; you're family. We believe in creating an experience that keeps you coming back, night after night.

Why Edgar's?

Your Ultimate Night Out in Hendersonville.

The Only Late Night Spot

When other places close, Edgar's keeps the good times rolling. We're proud to be Hendersonville's only full-service restaurant open late, serving food and drinks well past 10 PM on operating nights.

Unforgettable Live Music

Feel the energy of live bands and talented local artists. Our stage hosts incredible performances, making every visit a unique auditory experience. Check our schedule for upcoming acts!

Exclusive 21+ Atmosphere

Edgar's offers a sophisticated and relaxed environment exclusively for guests 21 and over. Enjoy a mature, vibrant setting for drinks, dining, and live entertainment, both inside and on our patio.
