Our Heritage

More Than Just Coffee: A Goldsboro Gathering Place.

Welcome to Café Le Doux, Goldsboro's beloved coffee shop and artisan bakery, where every visit feels like coming home. Since opening our doors, we've poured our passion into crafting the finest beverages and baking the most delectable treats, all while fostering a warm, friendly atmosphere.

We believe in quality from bean to cup. That's why we roast our coffee beans right here in-house, ensuring an unparalleled freshness and flavor in every sip of our espresso and brewed coffee. Our friendly baristas are always happy to guide you through our menu and help you discover your new favorite.

But it's not just about the coffee. Our bakery prides itself on small-batch artisan pastries, from flaky croissants to our incredibly popular fruit tarts, which have become a local legend. We're constantly experimenting with new flavors and traditional techniques to bring you baked goods that taste like they came straight from a European patisserie. We truly are your absolute favorite coffee shop in town!

Our Offerings

Taste the Difference at Café Le Doux.

House-Roasted Espresso

Our signature, small-batch, house-roasted espresso. Rich, complex, and perfect on its own or as the base for your favorite coffee creations.

Artisan Fruit Tarts

A true Café Le Doux specialty! Handcrafted fruit tarts with flaky crusts and seasonal fresh fruits, a delightful treat for any time of day.

Freshly Brewed Coffee

Experience the aroma and flavor of our expertly brewed coffee, made from beans roasted in-house for peak freshness and taste.

Daily Pastry Selection

From flaky croissants to sweet danishes, our daily selection of artisan pastries is baked fresh each morning. Ask about today's favorites!

Gourmet Breakfast Sandwiches

Start your day right with one of our hearty and delicious breakfast sandwiches, made with quality ingredients and served on fresh-baked bread.

Signature Lattes & Cappuccinos

Hand-crafted lattes and cappuccinos made with our finest house-roasted espresso and perfectly steamed milk. Customize with your favorite flavors.

Artisan Breads

From rustic loaves to specialty rolls, our small-batch artisan breads are baked daily with traditional methods and high-quality ingredients.

Note: Our menu is updated seasonally. Please visit us in-store for daily specials and full offerings.
