Our Story

Handcrafted Warmth in the Heart of Marshall

At Noodle Hole, we believe in the art of a perfect bowl of soup. Nestled in the scenic mountains of Marshall, NC, we offer a unique culinary experience where every noodle is created right before your eyes, a spectacle of tradition and freshness.

Our philosophy is simple: serve fresh, amazing food in a cozy, welcoming spot. Choose between our rich beef or flavorful vegan soy-based broth, customize with your favorite additions, and finish your meal with a complimentary hot, fresh donut. It’s more than just a meal; it's an amazing, much-needed evening and a taste of the authentic mountain spirit.
