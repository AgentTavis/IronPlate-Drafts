Our Story

Crafting unforgettable nights and award-winning libations in Fuquay-Varina.

At Moonlight Liquor Lounge, we're more than just a bar and grill; we're a cornerstone of community, taste, and good times in Fuquay-Varina. Nestled right on E Broad St, we pride ourselves on creating an inviting atmosphere where every visit feels special.

Our passion lies in the art of the cocktail, a dedication that has earned us the prestigious MAIN & BROAD MAGAZINE MAGGY AWARD for Best Cocktails two years running. We believe in innovation and constantly push the boundaries of flavor.
