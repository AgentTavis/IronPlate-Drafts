OUR STORY

More Than Just a Shop: A Gathering Place for Good Taste.

Nestled in the heart of Mt Holly, My Brother's Bottle at The Picket Fence is your local haven for exceptional fine wines, diverse craft beers, and unique specialty goods. We've cultivated a warm, inviting atmosphere where neighbors become friends over a shared love for quality. Our mission is simple: to bring the world's best to your bottle, and create moments worth savoring right here in our community.

Located conveniently in the Hawkins Real Estate building on W Central Ave, we are more than just a place to buy; we are a destination to explore, taste, and connect. Come discover your next favorite sip, snack, or gift, and experience the passion behind every selection.
