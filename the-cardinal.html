OUR STORY

Rooted in Appalachian Flavor

At The Cardinal, we believe in honoring the rich traditions of American road food while elevating it with the freshest ingredients available right here in Boone, NC. Our commitment to local sourcing isn't just a philosophy; it's a promise to our community and to your plate.

Our kitchen hums with creativity, crafting an eclectic mix of hand-prepared dishes and innovative cocktails. Every item, from our signature American classics to our unique beverage creations, is made in-house with meticulous attention to both taste and presentation. We're more than just a place to eat; we're a destination where every visit is an experience.

Our dedicated staff works tirelessly to ensure your time with us is not only pleasurable but genuinely fun. Whether you're a long-time local or just passing through, we invite you to pull up a chair, relax, and savor the warm, welcoming atmosphere and delicious food that define The Cardinal.
