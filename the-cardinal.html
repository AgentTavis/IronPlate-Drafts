Our Journey

Crafting Memories, One Drink and Dish at a Time

At The Cardinal, we believe in more than just serving great food and drinks – we believe in creating a vibrant community hub where friends and family can connect, relax, and make lasting memories. Nestled in the heart of Wilson, NC, we've poured our passion into building a bar & grill that feels like home, but with an unforgettable lineup of spirits and flavors.

From the moment you walk through our doors, you're part of The Cardinal family. We pride ourselves on a welcoming atmosphere, friendly faces, and a commitment to quality that shines through every glass and plate. Our dedication to a genuine experience is why our patrons keep coming back, week after week.

Just like our commitment to trust in our community, we aim to provide an honest, delightful experience every time you visit. We're here for you, Wilson!
