OUR STORY

A Taste of Boone's Heart

At The Cardinal, we are committed to providing classic American road food with the freshest ingredients available. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a welcoming spot in Boone where friends and family could gather over exceptional food and drinks, crafted with passion and care.

Both our menu and cocktail list feature an eclectic mix of hand-crafted food and beverages; all prepared in-house with an attention to taste and presentation. Our staff works hard to make sure that not only is your experience pleasurable, but that you have fun while you are with us. We believe in community, quality, and a good time, every time you walk through our doors.
