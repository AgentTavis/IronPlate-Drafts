Our Story

From Our Family to Your Table, A Taste of Home.

Nestled in the heart of Murphy, NC, Yogi's Neighborhood Grill is more than just a restaurant – it's a daily tradition for many. We believe in the simple joy of a perfectly cooked meal, shared in a friendly, welcoming atmosphere.

Every morning, we're here to greet you with fresh, comforting breakfast & lunch options, prepared with care and served with warm smiles. From our classic Chicken Parmigiana to our delightful Butter Pecan Cake, we strive to create a place where great food meets great company, making Yogi's a true home for our community. Come experience the warmth and flavors that make us a local favorite.
