Discover The Heart of Duck, NC Dining

Nestled right on Duck Road, Osprey Landing Sound Front Shops offers stunning sound views and an unforgettable dining experience in Duck, NC. We're proud to be a local favorite, known for our welcoming atmosphere and delicious menu that keeps guests coming back.
