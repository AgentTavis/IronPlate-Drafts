Our Heritage, Our Passion

A Family Legacy of Authentic Italian Flavor

The brothers: Miguel, Roel, and Raul, passionate about bringing authentic Italian flavors to Lilburn, NC, combine their decades of experience with a deep love for family and food. After the roaring success of Taqueria Los Hermanos, they've returned to their roots, honoring the incredible women in their family—Le Sorelle—who inspired their culinary journey. Le Sorelle, which translates to "the sisters", honors the amazing and powerful women in their family.

During the 80s and 90s, they honed their craft in renowned Italian restaurants across California, learning under master chefs and developing a distinctive style that blends tradition with innovative flair. Every dish at Le Sorelle is a testament to their dedication, offering a taste of Italy crafted with passion and heritage.
