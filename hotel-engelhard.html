Our Heritage

A Taste of Engelhard, Passed Down Through Generations.

From Our Family to Yours

Welcome to Hotel Engelhard, where tradition meets fresh beginnings! Under new ownership, we're proud to carry forward the legacy of true southern cooking that has made this little mom-and-pop gem a beloved stop in Engelhard. While our exterior might be unassuming, we invite you to step inside and discover a warmth that extends from our friendly service to every plate we serve.

For years, locals and travelers alike have cherished our authentic flavors and welcoming atmosphere. We believe in honest, hearty meals that remind you of home. Whether you choose to order from the window for a quick bite or settle into our cozy dining area, prepare for a taste of Eastern North Carolina BBQ that's simply unforgettable. It might not be your typical fare, but we promise, it's worth the stop.
