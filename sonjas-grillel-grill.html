Our Story

Honest Food, Homemade with Heart.

Welcome to Sonja's Grill / El Grill, a beloved local spot in Supply, NC. For years, we've prided ourselves on simple, honest food – just like our motto says, "When we don't have nothing hide!" We believe in real ingredients: our burgers are made with fresh, non-frozen meat, hand-pattied right here.

Our chili is a homemade family recipe, and we load up every dish with the freshest toppings. Come experience our casual, friendly atmosphere and taste the difference of a meal made with genuine care. We're not just serving food; we're serving up a piece of Supply's heart, a local favorite for American and Seafood dishes.
