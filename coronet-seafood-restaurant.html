Our Heritage

More Than 40 Years of Family Tradition

At Coronet Seafood Restaurant, we're more than just a place to eat; we're a beloved local institution rooted in family values and a passion for exceptional seafood. For over four decades, our family has been proudly serving the Rural Hall community.

Our philosophy is simple, yet profound, built on three unwavering commitments:

  1. High Quality Seafood Prepared Simply: We believe the best seafood speaks for itself. That's why we meticulously source premium ingredients and prepare them with a focus on fresh, natural flavors.
  2. Value-Priced Product: Delicious, high-quality seafood shouldn't break the bank. We are committed to offering generous portions and incredible taste at prices that make dining with us a true value.
  3. Personal Service: As a family-owned business, we treat every guest like an extension of our own. Expect a warm welcome, attentive care, and a dining experience that feels like coming home.

Come and taste the tradition that has made Coronet Seafood a cherished part of Rural Hall for generations.
