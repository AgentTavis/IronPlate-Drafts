Our Journey

The Heart of Waynesville's Coffee Culture

At Smoky Mountain Coffee Roasters, we believe coffee is more than just a drink – it's an experience. We're dedicated to bringing you the freshest, most aromatic coffee, roasted right here in Waynesville.

Our service is rooted in genuine hospitality. From helping you pick the perfect pastry to guiding your kiddo to their new favorite drink, our team is here to make your visit special. But the true magic happens when you encounter the coffee roaster himself.

We invite you to witness the journey from raw, greenish beans to rich, roasted perfection. See the vat, compare the untreated with the transformed, and if you're lucky, you'll be here just as freshly roasted beans emerge! Who needs other entertainment when you can learn about where your coffee truly comes from?
