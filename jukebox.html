OUR STORY

More Than Just A Pub, It's Home.

Nestled in the heart of Indian Trail, NC, JukeBox is your quintessential local dive bar and grill. We pride ourselves on being a welcoming haven where friends gather, stories are shared, and good times are always on tap. From the moment you step through our doors, you're part of the JukeBox family.

We're dedicated to serving up classic pub fare with a smile, ensuring every visit is filled with delicious food, refreshing drinks, and the best live music in town. It's the kind of place where everyone knows your name, and if they don't, they soon will. Come experience the true spirit of Indian Trail hospitality at JukeBox!

"JukeBox truly feels like home. The atmosphere is always lively, the cheesesteaks are incredible, and you can always count on a good laugh and great company. A genuine local gem!"

— A Happy Local Patron

OUR MENU

Local Favorites & Pub Classics

From our legendary cheesesteaks to perfectly crafted pub fare, our menu is packed with comfort food that hits the spot every time. Come hungry!
(Note: Prices not available online, please visit us for current menu pricing.)

JukeBox Signature Cheesesteak

The talk of Indian Trail! Sliced ribeye, melted provolone, and sautéed onions on a fresh hoagie roll. A true classic.

Classic Pub Burger

A juicy beef patty, fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, and your choice of cheese on a toasted brioche bun. Build it your way!

Spicy Buffalo Wings

Crispy, saucy, and perfectly spiced. Served with celery and your choice of ranch or blue cheese. Available in various heat levels.

Loaded Nachos

Crispy tortilla chips piled high with melted cheddar, jalapeños, black olives, pico de gallo, sour cream, and your choice of chicken or beef.

Crispy Onion Rings

Thick-cut, perfectly battered, and golden fried. The ultimate side dish for any meal. Served with a tangy dipping sauce.

Craft Beer Selection

A rotating selection of local and national craft beers on tap, plus all your favorite domestics. Perfect for pairing with any dish.
