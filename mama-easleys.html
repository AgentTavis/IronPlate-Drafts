Our Story

Generations of Flavor, Served with a Smile.

At Mama Easley's, we believe good food and good times should be simple, plentiful, and served with a smile. Nestled in the heart of Corolla, we're the go-to spot for families and groups looking for a delicious, no-fuss meal without the wait.

Our friendly staff whips up your favorites fast, ensuring everyone leaves happy and full. From our famously crispy Fried Green Tomatoes to our tender Calamari, we focus on fresh flavors and generous portions. Come as you are, bring the whole family (pets included!), and experience the casual, welcoming vibe that makes Mama Easley's a true Corolla gem.
