Our Heritage

Discover the Story Behind Springfield's Hidden Gem

Wow! What an unexpected gem. Located right here in Springfield, NC, 1636 North began with a simple desire: to offer an extraordinary dining experience.

Our journey often starts by welcoming curious guests who spot our menu. A warm greeting and an offer of seating, whether inside our cozy space or at our charming outside cafe tables, sets the tone. We pride ourselves on guiding you through a culinary adventure, perhaps from succulent shrimp to our renowned herb-crusted lamb chops, always with a personal touch.

Every guest is treated to a complimentary start: warm, buttery, savory biscuits alongside refreshing water and Perrier. It's these small gestures, combined with our passion for fresh, delicious seafood and expertly prepared dishes, that define the 1636 North experience. We're more than a restaurant; we're a moment, an unexpected delight in the heart of the city.
