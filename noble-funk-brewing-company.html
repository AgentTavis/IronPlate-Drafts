Our Journey

Brewing Memories, One Pint at a Time.

At Noble Funk Brewing Company, we believe in savoring the little luxuries in life – especially making memories with family and friends while enjoying a perfectly crafted beer. We've poured our hearts into creating a space where tradition meets modern energy, a true reflection of our roots.

As a family-owned brewery, we are proud to be invested in the growing SoBro neighborhood in our hometown of Louisville, KY. Our city combines a history rich in tradition with a vision and energy for continued development. This spirit inspires every brew we create and every dish we serve.

From the moment you walk through our doors, you're not just a customer – you're part of the Noble Funk family. Come experience the warmth, the flavor, and the community that defines us.

Our Offerings

Savor Our Craft & Culinary Delights.

The Noble Funk Burger

Our signature patty, aged cheddar, caramelized onions, smoked bacon, and special funk sauce on a brioche bun. Pure satisfaction!

Brewery Wings

Crispy fried wings tossed in your choice of Bourbon BBQ, Zesty Buffalo, or Noble Funk Dry Rub. Perfect for sharing (or not!).

Artisan Flatbread Pizza

Thin crust, fresh mozzarella, San Marzano tomatoes, and basil, baked to perfection. Add pepperoni or roasted veggies.

Loaded Funk Fries

Crispy golden fries smothered in cheese sauce, jalapeños, bacon bits, and a drizzle of spicy aioli.

Seasonal Salad w/ Grilled Chicken

Fresh mixed greens, seasonal fruit, goat cheese, candied pecans, and a light vinaigrette. Add grilled chicken for a hearty meal.

Craft Beer Flight

A curated selection of four 5oz pours from our rotating taps. Discover your new favorite Noble Funk brew!
