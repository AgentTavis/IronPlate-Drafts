OUR STORY

A Sweet Tradition, Baked with Love in Wake Forest.

Welcome to Heritage Donuts, your friendly neighborhood mom & pop donut shop nestled right here in Wake Forest, NC. Since day one, our mission has been simple: to bring joy to your mornings and sweeten your day with the freshest, most delicious donuts you've ever tasted.

We specialize in a delightful variety of yeast-raised, cake, and old-fashioned donuts, all crafted by hand with care and passion. You won't find shortcuts here – we believe in time-honored techniques and quality ingredients. In fact, for our beloved cream and jelly-filled donuts, we fill them right by the counter, ensuring every bite is bursting with fresh flavor!

Come experience the warm, inviting atmosphere and the irresistible aroma of freshly baked goods. We're proud to be a part of the Wake Forest community and look forward to sharing our heritage of deliciousness with you.
