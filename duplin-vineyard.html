Our Story

A Taste of Duplin, A Heart of Carolina

Nestled within the charm of Duplin Winery, The Bistro is more than just a restaurant; it's a culinary experience celebrating the rich bounty of North Carolina. We believe in fresh, local ingredients, expertly prepared to create dishes that perfectly complement Duplin's renowned wines.

Step through our welcoming gift shop and discover a warm, inviting atmosphere where every meal is an occasion. Our dedicated team is passionate about providing not just great food, but exceptional service, making every visit memorable.

Our Menu

Local Flavors, Expertly Crafted

Duplin Signature Burger

A juicy patty crafted with local North Carolina beef, topped with your choice of artisanal cheeses and fresh fixings. A true Bistro classic.

Classic NC BBQ Sandwich

Slow-cooked North Carolina barbecue, pulled and seasoned to perfection, served on a toasted bun with our signature slaw. A taste of tradition.

Chef's Seasonal Soup

Our kitchen's daily creation, featuring the freshest seasonal ingredients from local farms. Ask your server for today's delightful offering.

Grilled Bistro Steak

Tender cuts of quality steak, grilled to your preference and served with seasonal vegetables and your choice of a classic side.

Vineyard Salad with Grilled Chicken

Crisp mixed greens, fresh vegetables, and succulent grilled chicken, dressed with a light, house-made vinaigrette. Perfect for a lighter lunch.

Homemade Mac & Cheese

Creamy, rich, and utterly comforting. Our homemade macaroni and cheese is a beloved side or a hearty meal in itself.
