Our Roots

Hand-Crafted Colombian Delights, Made Fresh Daily

At Carolina Empanada Company, we believe in the simple joy of fresh, authentic food. Our journey began with a passion for traditional Colombian empanadas, perfected through generations of family recipes. We're a proud food truck serving the beautiful Western North Carolina region from our home in Burnsville.

Every empanada is made to order, ensuring you get the freshest, most delicious experience possible. From the perfectly crispy crust to the savory, plentiful fillings, we pour our heart into every bite. It's more than just food; it's a taste of Colombian tradition right here in the mountains.
