Our Heritage

From Woodland to Waterfront Oasis

Nestled in the heart of Hertford, Crawfish Shack / Hidden Gem began as a dream to transform a simple wooded area into a unique dining and event experience. Over the years, we've carefully cultivated this picturesque setting, ensuring every visit feels like an escape.

Our commitment is to offer not just exceptional seafood, but an unforgettable atmosphere. We pride ourselves on the serene beach and tranquil lakeside vistas that provide a stunning backdrop for any occasion, from casual dinners to grand celebrations.

Our Offerings

Today's Fresh Catches & Specialties

Fresh Catch of the Day

Our daily selection of the freshest local seafood, expertly prepared with seasonal ingredients. Ask your server for today's special.

Steamed Blue Crabs

A classic favorite! Sweet, succulent blue crabs, steamed to perfection and seasoned with our secret blend of spices. Market Price.

Local Oysters on the Half Shell

Briny, fresh oysters sourced from nearby waters, served with cocktail sauce, mignonette, and lemon.

Shrimp Boil Platter

Generous portions of succulent shrimp, corn on the cob, red potatoes, and savory sausage, all boiled in our signature seasoning.

Signature Seafood Gumbo

A rich, hearty gumbo brimming with shrimp, crab, andouille sausage, and okra, served with fluffy rice.

Lakeside Lobster Tail

Perfectly grilled or steamed lobster tail, served with drawn butter and your choice of two sides. A true indulgence.

Please note: Our menu items are based on seasonal availability and the freshest ingredients. For the most up-to-date offerings and special event menus, please call us directly.
