OUR STORY

A Taste of Community

Welcome to Lulu Bang Bang, your cherished BBQ spot in Durham, NC. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a gathering place where great food brings people together.

Our passion is crafting dishes that delight, from savory BBQ eel to refreshing desserts, all served with a side of warm hospitality. We believe every meal should be an experience, a chance to create memories with friends and family.

It's this dedication to flavor and fellowship that makes our customers return again and again. Come discover why Lulu Bang Bang is quickly becoming a beloved local tradition.
