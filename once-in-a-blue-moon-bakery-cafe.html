Our Heritage

Where Every Bite Tells a Story

At Once In A Blue Moon Bakery & Cafe, we believe in the magic of handcrafted desserts. For years, we've been dedicated to the art of baking, pouring our heart into every cake, cookie, and pastry. We're a cherished diner in Cary, NC, known for our warm atmosphere and, of course, our stunning desserts that taste just as incredible as they look.

Our journey began with a simple passion: to create moments of joy through exceptional flavors and beautiful presentation. From the classic comfort of a Boston Cream pie to the rich indulgence of a Chocolate Encounter, each item on our menu is a testament to our commitment to quality ingredients and time-honored techniques. We're more than just a bakery; we're a community hub where friends and family gather, celebrate, and savor life's sweet moments.
