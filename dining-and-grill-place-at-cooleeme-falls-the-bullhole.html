Our Legacy

Experience the Heart of Cooleemee Falls

For over 43 years, visitors have cherished the Riverpark (A.K.A. Bullhole, Cooleemee Falls, Cooleemee Beach) as an AWESOME place for outdoor adventure. Kids love to slip, slide, float, fish, and picnic here, making memories that last a lifetime.

While the park is free and allows outside food and beverages, we at The Bullhole Grill (formerly known as the Diggy Dog Stand) are proud to enhance your visit. We offer delicious Hot Dogs, refreshing sodas, cold water, and a variety of snacks, making your day convenient and even more enjoyable. It's the perfect fuel for your riverside escapades!
