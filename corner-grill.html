Our Heritage

Our Kitchen, Your Home

Corner Grill is more than just a restaurant; it's Angier's beloved diner, a place where friends and family gather for comforting American classics. With every dish, we aim to make your stomach happy, just like the good old days. For years, we've poured our heart into serving up genuine home cooking that keeps our community coming back for more.

From our sizzling breakfast plates that kickstart your day to hearty lunch specials that satisfy, we believe in fresh ingredients, timeless recipes, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Come on down and experience the taste of true Southern hospitality and the best little grill in the area!
