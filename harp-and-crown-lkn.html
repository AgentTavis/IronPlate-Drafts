Our Story

Where Community Gathers

Harp and Crown Kitchen and Pub has been the premier English and Irish Pub for Cornelius and the surrounding Lake Norman communities since 2017. We welcome you to join us for a football match, a late-night pint on our patio, or a cozy dinner in our dining room.

Since opening our doors, we've poured countless pints and shared even more laughs, becoming a true local favorite. Our mission is to provide a warm, inviting atmosphere where everyone feels like family, whether you're a regular or visiting for the first time.
