OUR STORY

Baking Sweet Moments, One Custom Order at a Time.

Welcome to Caked!, your dedicated in-home based bake shop nestled in the heart of Hillsborough, NC. We're passionate about crafting delicious, bespoke desserts that make every occasion a little sweeter. Our journey began with a simple belief: everyone deserves a perfect cake, made with love and tailored to their unique celebration.

We're thrilled to announce our new Specialty Cakes are now available! Looking for an egg-free option? Yes, you read that right! Each of these incredible cakes were baked without a single egg, offering a delectable solution for those with dietary needs without compromising on taste or texture. From elegant designs for weddings to whimsical creations for birthdays, we pour our heart into every single order.

At Caked!, we pride ourselves on fulfilling custom requests, transforming your vision into a delicious reality. Place your order now and experience the difference of a truly personalized, handcrafted dessert. We can't wait to bake something special just for you!
