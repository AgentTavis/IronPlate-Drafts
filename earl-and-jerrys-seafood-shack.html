Our Heritage

Our Passion for the Freshest Catch and Southern Comfort.

Nestled in the heart of Roseboro, Earl & Jerry's Seafood Shack is more than just a restaurant – it's a family tradition built on the love of fresh seafood and genuine Southern hospitality. For years, we've been proudly serving our community, ensuring every dish, from our succulent fried shrimp to our hearty crab legs, is prepared with the finest ingredients and a touch of homegrown care.

We believe in generous portions, unforgettable flavors, and a welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels like family. Come on down and taste the difference that passion makes!

Our Offerings

Crab Legs Feast
