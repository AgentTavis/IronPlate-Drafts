Our Journey

Where Culinary Passion Meets Local Charm

At Wine Sellars Bistro, we believe that great food and exquisite wine create unforgettable memories. Our journey began with a simple passion: to bring the freshest seafood and the finest wines to the heart of Lexington, NC.

Every dish is crafted with care, from our savory Applewood Bacon Wrapped Medjool Dates, a perfect start to any meal, to our simply fabulous Lump Crab Cakes and Jumbo Shrimp. Our Seared Ahi Tuna, served with fresh ginger and a sublime wasabi sauce, is a testament to our commitment to fresh, vibrant flavors.

Our walls, lined with bottles of the finest elixir, hint at the carefully curated wine selection waiting to perfectly complement your dining experience. It's more than just a meal; it's a celebration of taste, shared in a cozy and inviting atmosphere.
