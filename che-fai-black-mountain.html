Our Heritage

A Taste of Italy, Crafted with Love in Black Mountain

Nestled in the charming heart of Black Mountain, NC, Che Fai brings authentic, scratch-made Italian cuisine to your table. Our passion is crafting delicious, heartwarming dishes that transport you straight to the sun-drenched landscapes of Italy with every bite.

From our kitchen to your plate, every meal is prepared with fresh, quality ingredients and a touch of genuine Italian tradition. We believe in the simple joy of good food shared with loved ones, making every visit to Che Fai a memorable experience.

We're proud to be a local favorite, serving our community with food made with love, right here at 110 Black Mountain Ave. Come taste the difference that dedication and true Italian spirit make!
