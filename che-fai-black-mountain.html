Our Heritage

Crafting Authentic Italian Flavors in Black Mountain

Nestled at 110 Black Mountain Ave, Che Fai Black Mountain is more than just a restaurant; it's a labor of love, bringing the heart and soul of traditional Italian cooking to our cherished community. We believe in the power of simple, high-quality ingredients, transformed into unforgettable dishes right here in our kitchen.

Every pasta, every sauce, every dish is prepared from scratch, daily. We take pride in our authentic approach, ensuring that each meal served embodies the warmth, comfort, and rich flavors of Italy. From our family to yours, we invite you to experience the true taste of hand-crafted Italian cuisine, right here in the heart of Black Mountain.
