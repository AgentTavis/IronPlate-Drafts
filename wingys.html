Our Journey

Taste the Heart of Selma at Wingy's

From Our Family to Yours

Born from a passion for honest, delicious food and a deep love for our Selma community, Wingy's started with a simple vision: to serve up the freshest seafood and grill classics with a smile. What began as a humble idea has grown into a beloved local spot where families gather, friends laugh, and every bite feels like home.

We believe in sourcing the best ingredients, crafting each dish with care, and making every customer feel like part of our extended Wingy's family. Every dish is cooked to order, ensuring maximum flavor and freshness. Come on in and taste the difference that dedication and heart make!
