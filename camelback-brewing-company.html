Our Heritage

Crafting Exceptional Brews & Unforgettable Moments

Our Passion for Perfection in Every Pint

At Camelback we are always striving to craft the best beer we possibly can. We go to great lengths to produce the most flavorful combinations of specialty grains and unique hop varieties for our patrons to savor with each sip of our beer.

Beyond the beer, we've cultivated a warm and inviting atmosphere where friends and family can gather, share stories, and create new memories. More than just a microbrewery, we are a proud brewpub, deeply rooted in the Sanford community. We believe in good company, great food, and even better beer – a true local gem where everyone feels at home.
