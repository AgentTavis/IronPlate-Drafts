Crafted With Care, Served With Heart

Sips Soda is more than just a place to eat; it's a cherished local spot where the community of Marion, NC, comes together. We pride ourselves on creating a warm, inviting atmosphere where every guest feels like family, enjoying delicious food and unique beverages crafted with care.
