Taste the Sips Soda Difference

Classic Cheeseburger

Our signature patty, perfectly grilled, topped with melted cheddar, crisp lettuce, juicy tomato, and our special sauce.

Spicy Chicken Sandwich

Crispy fried chicken breast with a kick, served on a toasted bun with pickles and a cool ranch drizzle.

Hand-Cut Fries

Golden brown and perfectly seasoned, these crispy fries are the ideal side for any meal.

Crispy Onion Rings

Sweet onions, lightly battered and fried to a golden crisp, served with our zesty dipping sauce.

House Salad with Grilled Chicken

Fresh mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and grilled chicken breast, tossed in a light vinaigrette.
