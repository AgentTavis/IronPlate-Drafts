Our Roots

More Than Just a Bar & Grill: A Community Hub

Prepare to be twisted at The Twisted Squirrel, the charming establishment in Jonesville that blends the artistry of ice cream with the warmth of coffee and the charm of local finds. We're not just serving food and drinks; we're crafting experiences.

Delight in small-batch, locally crafted ice cream cradled in house-made waffle cones and sip on gourmet coffee, creating a symphony of flavors that will awaken your senses. Every visit is an invitation to unwind, connect, and discover something wonderful right here in Jonesville.
