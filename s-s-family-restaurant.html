Our Story

A Taste of Home, Built on Taylorsville Tradition.

For many years, S & S Family Restaurant has been a cornerstone of the Taylorsville community, a place where friends and family gather for hearty meals and warm memories. We believe in serving great food that feels just like home, without breaking the bank.

From our classic juicy burgers and crispy fries to the comforting country style steak and our famous fried chicken, every dish is prepared with the simple goodness you've come to love. We're more than just a bar and grill; we're a part of your family's dining tradition.

"S&S is our go-to for comfort food. The atmosphere is always welcoming, and you just can't beat the taste and value. It truly feels like dining with family."

- A Loyal Customer, Taylorsville
