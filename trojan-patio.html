OUR STORY

Where Comfort Meets Community

At Trojan Patio, we believe in good food, great company, and the kind of welcoming atmosphere that makes you feel right at home. Open several days a week from 7 AM to 3 PM, we're a beloved local diner serving up comfort food with a smile.

Our philosophy is simple: serve delicious, reasonably priced meals that bring people together. Locals rave about our breakfast, which we proudly serve all day! Imagine starting your morning—or afternoon—with a perfectly cooked waffle, fresh eggs, and crispy bacon, just like a recent visitor enjoyed with her daughter.
