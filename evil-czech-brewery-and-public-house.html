Our StoryCrafted for Speed & Flavor

Welcome to Evil Czech Brewery and Public House, where every dish is an adventure and every visit is a celebration. We believe in crafting unforgettable experiences, blending bold flavors with a vibrant atmosphere that's truly unique.

At the heart of our weekday offerings is the Lightnin' Lunch. It isn't just a meal; it's an all-you-can-eat experience built for both flavor and speed. Every weekday our chefs roll out brewery favorites and fresh daily creations in perfect small-plate form, so you can sample it all and go back for more.

Whether you're sneaking out from the office, grabbing a quick bite between errands, or just want a fun midday escape, Lightnin' Lunch makes it easy to fill up, try something new, and still be back on schedule. Fast. Flavorful. All you can eat. It's our commitment to quality, speed, and genuine hospitality that defines us.
