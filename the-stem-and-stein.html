Our Journey

Crafting Moments, One Sip and Slice at a Time.

The Stem and Stein is more than just a restaurant – it's a neighborhood cornerstone for exceptional food, fine wines, and craft beers. Nestled on the corner of County Line Road and Palmer Road in Madison, Alabama, we've poured our heart into creating a welcoming space where friends gather and memories are made.

With over 25 wines by-the-glass and 17 rotating taps of draft beer, we invite you to explore an extensive selection, perfect for pairing with our delicious crostinis, paninis, pizzas, salads, and other fun shareable options. Don't miss our lively Thursday night wine tastings and competitive trivia every Wednesday!

Our Culinary Delights

Taste the Passion in Every Bite

From our signature pizzas to delightful small plates, every item on our menu is crafted with fresh ingredients and a love for flavor. Explore our selection, perfect for any occasion.

Pepperoni Passion Pizza

A classic favorite, loaded with premium pepperoni and rich mozzarella on our crispy, artisanal crust.

Margherita Masterpiece

Simple perfection with fresh basil, vine-ripened tomatoes, and creamy mozzarella on our hand-tossed dough.

Smoked Salmon Crostini

Toasted baguette slices topped with savory smoked salmon, dill cream cheese, and a hint of lemon.

Caprese Panini

Fresh mozzarella, ripe tomatoes, and basil pesto grilled to perfection on artisan bread.

Tuscan Chicken Salad

Grilled chicken, sun-dried tomatoes, artichoke hearts, and mixed greens with a light balsamic vinaigrette.

Artisan Cheese Board

A curated selection of fine cheeses, fresh fruit, nuts, and crackers, ideal for sharing.

Mediterranean Veggie Pizza

A vibrant medley of olives, bell peppers, red onion, feta, and spinach on a light, flavorful crust.

Prosciutto & Fig Flatbread

Sweet fig jam, salty prosciutto, mozzarella, and arugula on a crisp, thin flatbread.
