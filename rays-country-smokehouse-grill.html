Our Heritage

Generations of Flavor, Smoked with Love.

Nestled in the heart of Gastonia, NC at 219 S Broad St, Ray's Country Smokehouse-Grill isn't just a restaurant; it's a labor of love. We're an independent, family-run establishment dedicated to serving up genuinely good quality food at prices that make sense. For over 19 years, we've poured our passion into every slow-smoked cut, crafting a dining experience that feels like home.

From our pit to your plate, we believe in honest BBQ, done right. We stand by our commitment to fresh ingredients, meticulous smoking techniques, and a friendly atmosphere where everyone feels welcome. Come taste the tradition that Gastonia has come to love.
