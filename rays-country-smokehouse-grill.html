Our Heritage

Generations of Smoke & Flavor, Right Here in Gastonia.

At Ray's Country Smokehouse-Grill, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a Gastonia tradition. Located at 219 S Broad St, we're a small, independent establishment driven by a simple passion: serving good quality food for a reasonable price. We've been in business for decades, honing our craft and bringing the authentic taste of slow-smoked BBQ to our beloved community.

Every dish is prepared with care, from our tender brisket and fall-off-the-bone ribs to our unique smoked salads. We believe in honest food, friendly service, and creating a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels like family. Come taste the difference that passion and tradition make.

Our Signature Smoked Delights

Brisket Plate

Our slow-smoked, tender beef brisket, seasoned to perfection and cooked until it melts in your mouth. A true BBQ classic.

Smoked Ribs

Fall-off-the-bone pork ribs, dry-rubbed and smoked low-and-slow for hours, delivering an irresistible smoky flavor.

Signature Smoked Wings

Crispy, juicy chicken wings, perfectly smoked then finished with your choice of our homemade BBQ sauces.

Pulled Pork Salad

Fresh mixed greens topped with our famous smoked pulled pork, crisp veggies, and your choice of dressing. A delicious, lighter option.

Italian Grilled Chicken Salad

Grilled chicken breast marinated in Italian herbs, served over fresh greens with tomatoes, onions, and your favorite dressing.

Pulled Pork Sandwich

Our succulent, slow-smoked pulled pork piled high on a toasted bun, served with a side of our signature BBQ sauce.
