Our Heritage

Generations of Flavor. Unforgettable Taste.

Nestled in the heart of Cleveland, NC, Keaton's Barbecue, Inc. has been a cornerstone of authentic Southern flavor for decades. Our commitment to slow-smoked perfection and time-honored recipes has earned us a reputation far beyond local borders.

Recognized by esteemed publications such as The Splendid Table and Roadfood, Keaton's Barbecue has earned outstanding reviews for its exceptional quality. We believe in crafting barbecue that tells a story—a story of tradition, dedication, and the finest ingredients.

Every bite is a testament to our passion, from our perfectly tender meats to our signature sauces. Come taste the legacy that makes us Cleveland's beloved barbecue destination.
