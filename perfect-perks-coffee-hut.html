Our Story

Experience the Taste of Southern Charm & Speed!

At Perfect Perks Coffee Hut and Micki's Munchies, we've perfected the art of delicious and convenient. Nestled right here in Washington, NC, we're proud to offer a unique drive-through experience that puts your perfect cup and treat in your hands within seconds.

You just pull up on either side, tell us what you want, and you have it within a few seconds. It's that simple. We believe great coffee shouldn't come with a wait. That's why we focus on speed without compromising on quality, ensuring every visit is a delight.

You are in for a delicious coffee and the best coffee you have had in the South. Our dedication to quality ingredients and friendly service has made us a local favorite, where every sip and every bite brings a smile.
