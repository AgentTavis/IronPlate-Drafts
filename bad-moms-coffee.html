Our Story

More Than Just Coffee, It's a Community.

At Bad Moms Coffee, we believe in crafting more than just a perfect cup; we're brewing a consistent experience. Nestled in the heart of Belhaven, we've quickly become a local favorite for our cozy vibes, delicious snacks, and a welcoming dog-friendly patio.

Our dedicated baristas strive for perfection, ensuring that every visit, whether for your daily iced café mocha or a delightful seasonal creation, is spot on, every single time. It's the kind of place where your day truly can start (and end) with a smile and a perfectly made drink.

"I was in Belhaven for a few days on a work trip, and I ended up at Bad Moms Coffee every single day--and for good reason. Their iced café mocha (with less chocolate) was absolute perfection each time. What really impressed me was the consistency--despite having two different baristas during my stay, the drink was spot on every single time."

— A Satisfied Traveler
