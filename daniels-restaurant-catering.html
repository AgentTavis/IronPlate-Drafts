Our Heritage

Crafting unforgettable dining experiences since 1996.

For over two decades, Daniel's Restaurant & Catering has been a cherished culinary cornerstone in Apex, NC. We believe in crafting memorable dining experiences, one fresh dish at a time. From our freshly sauteed pasta dishes to our expertly hand-stretched pizzas, every plate is prepared with passion and precision.

We're proud to offer a full bar, accommodating private dining, and a warm, non-smoking atmosphere where everyone feels welcome. We accept credit cards, provide ample parking, and offer highchairs, ensuring a comfortable visit for all. Join us and discover why Daniel's has been a local favorite for over two decades.
