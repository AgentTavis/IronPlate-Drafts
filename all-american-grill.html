Our Story

Grilling Up Happiness Since Day One.

Welcome to All American Grill, a true family-owned gem nestled on White Lake Drive in Elizabethtown, NC. We started with a simple passion: to bring the unbeatable taste of fresh, hot-off-the-grill comfort food to our community. You know that feeling of a perfectly cooked burger or a hearty wrap from your favorite food truck? We've bottled that vibe and brought it to our kitchen.

Every dish we serve is made with love, using quality ingredients and our signature griddle touch. From morning until evening, we're here to dish out those delicious favorites that make you feel right at home. Come on down and experience the warmth, the flavor, and the friendly faces that make us All American Grill.

Our Delicious Offerings

Griddle Favorites & Comfort Classics

The Classic Griddle Burger

A juicy beef patty, fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, and our special sauce, griddled to perfection.

Spicy Chicken Wrap

Grilled chicken, crisp greens, zesty sauce, and a hint of spice, all wrapped up fresh.

Philly Cheesesteak Sandwich

Thinly sliced steak, melted provolone, and sautéed onions and peppers on a toasted hoagie roll.

Homestyle Grilled Cheese

Classic comfort: perfectly melted cheese between two slices of golden-grilled bread.

Crispy Chicken Sandwich

Golden-fried chicken breast, pickles, and mayo on a toasted bun – simple, yet irresistible.

Breakfast Burrito

Eggs, cheese, sausage (or bacon), and fresh salsa wrapped in a warm tortilla. (Served during breakfast hours)

Veggie Delight Wrap

A fresh mix of seasonal vegetables, hummus, and a light vinaigrette, ideal for a lighter meal.

All American Hot Dog

A classic all-beef hot dog, grilled and served on a fresh bun with your choice of toppings.
