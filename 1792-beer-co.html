Our Story

More Than Just a Bottle Shop – Your Community Hangout.

At 1792 Beer Co., we're passionate about bringing people together over exceptional craft beverages. Nestled in the heart of Uptown Roxboro, we've created a warm, inviting space where friends and family can relax, discover new favorites, and simply enjoy good company.

We pride ourselves on an excellent selection of craft beer in bottles, cans, and on draft, all offered at great prices. And if beer isn't your thing, you'll find a fantastic array of ciders, seltzers, and wines to satisfy every palate. Our owners are truly great people, dedicated to making 1792 Beer Co. the best place to hang out in Roxboro!
