Our Legacy

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience

Nestled in the heart of downtown Mayodan, Downtown Jr's 23 Sports Grill has quickly become a beloved spot for locals and visitors alike. We pride ourselves on serving up good burgers and a nice selection of American classics in a beautifully decorated setting.

Whether you're looking for a casual dinner, a lively sports bar atmosphere to catch the game, or a welcoming place to bring visiting guests, we've got you covered. Our background music, often country and western, sets a relaxed and fun tone, making every visit a memorable one.

We're more than just a restaurant; we're a community hub where great food and good times come together. Come on down and see why we're Mayodan's go-to spot!
