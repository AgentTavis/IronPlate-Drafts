Our Story

More Than a Tavern, It's Home.

Nestled in the heart of the picturesque Tusquittee Valley, Tusquittee Tavern is more than just a place to eat and drink—it's a community cornerstone. As a cherished private club in Hayesville, NC, we've cultivated a warm and inviting atmosphere where friends and family gather.

We pride ourselves on offering a wide range of entertainment options for everyone. From exhilarating live concerts that get your feet tapping to the thrill of major league sports on our screens, there's always something happening here. But what truly sets us apart is the friendly faces, the shared laughter, and the undeniable local charm that makes every visit feel like coming home.

Our Menu

Savor Our Signature Flavors.

While our full menu offers a variety of delicious options, we are especially known for our mouthwatering pizzas, crafted with fresh ingredients and local love.

Classic Pepperoni Pizza

Our timeless favorite, loaded with rich tomato sauce, premium mozzarella, and generous slices of savory pepperoni. A true crowd-pleaser.

Supreme Valley Pizza

Experience a burst of flavors with pepperoni, sausage, bell peppers, onions, and mushrooms, all atop our signature crust.

Garden Veggie Pizza

A fresh and vibrant option with seasonal vegetables, mozzarella, and our zesty tomato sauce on a perfectly baked crust.

BBQ Chicken Pizza

Tangy BBQ sauce base, grilled chicken, red onions, and a blend of cheeses for a smoky, sweet, and savory delight.

Buffalo Chicken Pizza

Spicy buffalo sauce, tender chicken, and mozzarella, drizzled with ranch or blue cheese for a kick that hits the spot.

Cheese Lover's Pizza

A simple yet exquisite blend of four premium cheeses, melted to perfection on our crispy, chewy crust.
