Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's Family.

Jimmy's Munch Box is Mars Hill's newest family restaurant, offering a diverse menu of homemade dishes made with the freshest ingredients sourced from local farms and suppliers whenever possible. We believe in good, honest food, prepared with care.

We're a family owned and operated business, located right here in Mars Hill at 759 Carl Eller Road. At Jimmy's, we're not about fast food; we're about fresh, made-to-order meals that bring people together. Every dish is crafted with a touch of home, inviting you to relax and savor the flavors.
