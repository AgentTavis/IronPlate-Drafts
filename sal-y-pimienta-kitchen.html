Our Story

A Taste of Uruguay, Right Here in Houston.

At Sal y Pimienta Kitchen, we've been passionately bringing the vibrant flavors and warm hospitality of South America to Houston since 2014. Our journey began with a simple yet profound desire: to share authentic Uruguayan cuisine, where good quality meats are cooked to absolute perfection.

We pride ourselves on our traditional tableside asador experience, a culinary art form that ensures every cut is succulent and flavorful. While our meats are the heart of our kitchen, we also craft excellent empanadas and decadent desserts that truly complete your feast. Our dedicated team is committed to providing knowledgeable and friendly service, ensuring every dinner is unhurried and memorable.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply seeking a taste of paradise, Sal y Pimienta Kitchen offers an unforgettable dining experience where every guest feels like family.
