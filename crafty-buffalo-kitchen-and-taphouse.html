Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Salisbury Tradition.

Crafty Buffalo Kitchen and Taphouse is a beloved local spot in Salisbury, NC. For years, we've been the go-to destination for friends and families to gather, share laughs, and enjoy incredible food and drinks in a welcoming atmosphere.

Our passion is simple: to create a memorable experience with every visit. From our carefully crafted dishes to our selection of local brews, we pour our heart into every detail, making sure you feel right at home the moment you step through our doors.

Our Offerings

A Taste of Crafty Buffalo

Crafty Buffalo Wings

Our legendary wings, tossed in your choice of classic buffalo, BBQ, or spicy honey glaze.

Signature Crafty Burger

Juicy beef patty, smoked cheddar, crispy bacon, lettuce, tomato, and our secret sauce on a brioche bun.

Loaded Buffalo Nachos

Crispy tortilla chips piled high with seasoned beef, melted cheese, jalapenos, and a drizzle of buffalo ranch.

Slow-Smoked Ribs

Tender, fall-off-the-bone ribs, slow-smoked to perfection and glazed with our tangy BBQ sauce.

Crispy Chicken Sandwich

Buttermilk fried chicken, pickles, and spicy mayo on a toasted potato bun. A true classic.

Artisan Street Tacos

Three soft tortillas with your choice of grilled chicken or seasoned pork, fresh salsa, and avocado crema.

Buffalo Mac & Cheese

Creamy macaroni and cheese baked with a spicy buffalo kick, topped with crunchy breadcrumbs.

Fresh Garden Salad

Mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and your choice of dressing.

Craft Beer Selection

A rotating selection of the finest local and regional craft beers on tap, always fresh.
