Our Story

Where Every Meal Feels Like Home

For generations, Grapevine Cafe has been Woodland's cherished gathering spot, a place where the aroma of home-cooked meals fills the air and every guest is treated like family. Our doors are open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, offering beloved classics and daily specials that keep our community coming back.

We take immense pride in our friendly staff and the fresh, great food we serve. From our highly recommended grilled seafood on Friday nights to our legendary Sunday buffet, every dish is crafted with care. It's an unbelievable spread of Southern cooking, featuring an array of meats, fresh salads, vegetables, and a dessert table that's truly "oh my!"

We’ve been delighted to serve our community for years, building traditions and sharing smiles. We can't wait to welcome you to our table.
