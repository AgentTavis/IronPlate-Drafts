Our Roots

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Homecoming

At Southern Delights, we believe good food is best shared with good friends. Nestled in the heart of Bennettsville, our bar and grill is a place where authentic Southern cuisine meets warm, friendly service. We're not just serving meals; we're dishing out a taste of tradition, a side of comfort, and a generous helping of hospitality.

From the moment you walk through our doors, you're family. Our kitchen hums with the legacy of generations, preparing hearty favorites like our famously crispy fried chicken, tender collard greens, and melt-in-your-mouth homemade biscuits. Each dish is crafted with love, using time-honored recipes that evoke the true spirit of the South.

Taste the Heart of Southern Cooking

Fried Chicken

Crispy, juicy, and seasoned to perfection – our legendary fried chicken is a Southern staple you won't forget.

Collard Greens

Slow-cooked with savory smoked ham hocks, these tender greens are packed with authentic Southern flavor.

Homemade Biscuits

Fluffy, buttery, and made from scratch daily. Perfect for soaking up every last drop of goodness.

BBQ Pulled Pork

Tender, slow-smoked pork, hand-pulled and smothered in our signature tangy BBQ sauce.

Mac & Cheese

Sweet Tea
