Our Roots

Experience the True Taste of Pantego.

Nestled in the heart of Pantego, NC, MJ’s Country Store + Grill is more than just a place to eat – it's a community hub. We're proud to offer a taste of local charm, combining the rustic appeal of a country store with the savory delights of a classic grill.

From hearty breakfast plates to satisfying lunch specials, and a unique selection of handmade goods, we're dedicated to bringing good food and good company together. Come experience the warmth and flavor that makes MJ’s a local favorite, where every visit feels like coming home.
