Our Heritage

Where Good Times Brew and Memories Are Made

Nestled in the heart of Floyd, NC, Buffalo Mountain Brewery And McDaniel's Tavern is more than just a place to eat and drink—it's a community gathering spot. We’ve poured our passion into creating a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels like family, whether you're a long-time local or just passing through our beautiful mountain town.

Step in and settle in for some Fun, Family-Friendly BINGO! Beyond the games, our tavern is a place to unwind, share stories, and enjoy handcrafted brews and delicious pub fare. We believe in good company, great flavors, and making every visit a memorable one. Join us and discover your new favorite spot.

"This place feels like home! The atmosphere is so welcoming, and the team always makes you feel like family. We love our regular bingo nights here!"

— A Happy Local
