Our Journey

More Than Just a Spot, It's an Experience.

Nestled on the picturesque Front Street, Bake Bottle & Brew is more than just a place to eat and drink—it's a cherished Swansboro tradition. We've crafted a cute, inviting haven where families gather, friends unwind, and memories are made, all against the stunning backdrop of the bay.

From a refreshing scoop of ice cream for the kids to a perfectly chilled craft beer or a fine glass of wine for the adults, we offer something for everyone. Our spacious deck provides that quintessential summer sensation, letting you feel the breezy wind as you gaze out at the water. Step inside and discover a treasure trove of unique finds, including handmade bags, local seasonings, and an expertly curated selection of craft beers and wines. Come for the view, stay for the warmth, and leave with a smile.
