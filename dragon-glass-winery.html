Our Heritage

Crafting Experiences, One Sip at a Time

Here at Dragon Glass Winery, we proudly offer locally sourced, top-quality wines for you and yours to enjoy when you visit our property in beautiful Madison, North Carolina. We are more than just a winery; we're a gathering place, a community hub where cherished memories are made.

Open year-round, we always have something for our guests to enjoy – from relaxing wine tastings and delicious offerings from local food trucks, to vibrant live music and engaging entertainment. Our passion lies in bringing people together over exceptional wines and a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Come discover why Dragon Glass Winery is a beloved destination for both locals and visitors seeking a unique and delightful escape.
