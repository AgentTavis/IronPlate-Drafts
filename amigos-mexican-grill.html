Our Story

A Taste of Home, Decades in the Making.

Honestly, Amigos Mexican Grill isn't much on the eyes from the outside, but step inside and you'll find a place that reminds you of those little hole-in-the-wall Mexican restaurants your dad always insisted we visit as a kid. From what we could tell, it was pretty clean – a real mom-and-pop gem.

It's cozy, and you instantly feel at home. We pride ourselves on serving homemade authentic Mexican cuisine, made with recipes passed down through generations. While things can get pretty busy, especially during peak hours, we never let the quality of our food or the warmth of our welcome falter.

We're more than just a restaurant; we're a community staple where every meal feels like a family gathering. Come on in and experience the heart of Mexico, right here in King, NC.
