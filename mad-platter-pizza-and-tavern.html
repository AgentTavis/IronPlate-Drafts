Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

Welcome to Mad Platter Pizza and Tavern, a proud cornerstone of the Spruce Pine community. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a gathering place where great food, local craft beverages, and lively entertainment come together.

Our journey began with a simple passion: to serve up pizza with a unique twist, paired with an unparalleled selection of local craft beers. The Mad Hatter Tavern, our lively attached space, boasts an incredible 32 taps of local craft beers, ciders & mead, making it the perfect spot to unwind and discover your next favorite brew.

We believe in supporting local, from the musicians who grace our porch to the regional breweries we feature on tap. Every visit is an invitation to connect, enjoy, and make lasting memories in a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Come join our story!
