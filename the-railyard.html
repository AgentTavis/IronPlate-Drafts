OUR STORY

Where Community Gathers

The RailYard is more than just a Bar And Grill; it's the vibrant pulse of Elkin, NC. We've cultivated a welcoming space where community thrives, offering an exciting culinary journey with our daily rotating food trucks. Picture yourself unwinding in our comfy outdoor seating, enjoying great company, refreshing drinks, and the best of local flavors.

Born from a love for good food and even better company, The RailYard stands as a testament to Elkin's lively spirit. We believe in creating memorable experiences, one delicious bite and friendly conversation at a time. Come for the food, stay for the community, and leave with a smile.
