OUR STORY

Serving Gastonia's Heart & Soul, One Plate at a Time.

At Smith's Soul Food Bistro, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a cornerstone of the Gastonia community. Inspired by the spirit of championship and local pride—just like bringing the 'SHIP TO THE GASHOUSE'—we pour our heart into every dish.

From savory breakfast and brunch to classic soul food and the freshest seafood, we promise a taste of home and hospitality that keeps you coming back. We're proud to fuel our community, including our local teams, with delicious, handcrafted meals made with love and tradition.
