Our Heritage

Bringing Authentic Mexican Flavors to Elizabeth City.

Welcome to The Corner Mexican Restaurant, where every dish tells a story of tradition, fresh ingredients, and a passion for vibrant flavors. We believe in creating a welcoming atmosphere where friends and family can gather to enjoy hearty meals prepared with love. Our chefs meticulously craft each plate, from sizzling fajitas to savory enchiladas, ensuring an authentic taste of Mexico right here in Elizabeth City.

We pride ourselves on using locally sourced produce whenever possible, combined with time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Come experience the warmth of Mexican hospitality and discover your new favorite dish at The Corner.

Our Dishes

Taste the Tradition

Classic Taco Plate

Three perfectly seasoned tacos with your choice of meat, fresh cilantro, onions, and a side of rice and beans. A timeless favorite!

Enchiladas Verdes

Corn tortillas filled with savory shredded chicken, smothered in our tangy green tomatillo sauce and melted cheese. Served with rice.

Sizzling Fajita Bowl

Grilled steak or chicken, bell peppers, and onions served over a bed of rice, topped with avocado slices and a hint of lime.

Quesadilla Supreme

A large flour tortilla filled with melted cheese, your choice of meat, served with sour cream and guacamole.

Chile Relleno

Roasted poblano pepper stuffed with cheese, lightly battered and fried, then covered in a mild tomato sauce. A true delicacy.

Burrito Grande

A massive burrito packed with rice, beans, your favorite meat, cheese, and salsa. Enough for two (or one very hungry person)!
