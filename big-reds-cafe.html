Our Story

More Than Just a Meal: A Kings Mountain Tradition.

At Big Red's Cafe, we're not just serving food; we're serving up comfort, community, and the flavors that make Kings Mountain feel like home. For years, we've been the go-to spot for hearty breakfasts, satisfying lunches, and the kind of friendly service that brightens your day.

Our kitchen is where classic diner meets a touch of creative flair. Take our famous Buffalo Chicken Spread for example: a delightful mix of grilled chicken, crispy bacon, sharp cheddar, and fresh tomato, all grilled on flatbread and topped with our unique House Blend – a secret family mix of olive oil, Italian seasonings, garlic, and a splash of vinegar that makes every bite unforgettable.

We believe in good food, good company, and good times. Come on in and become a part of our story.
