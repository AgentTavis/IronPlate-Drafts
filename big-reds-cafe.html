OUR STORY

Serving Comfort & Community, One Dish at a Time.

At Big Red's Cafe, we believe in the simple joy of a hearty meal and the warmth of a welcoming atmosphere. Nestled in the heart of Kings Mountain, we've been a beloved diner for years, known for our dedication to fresh ingredients and classic flavors.

Our journey began with a passion for bringing people together over delicious food, and that passion continues to drive us every single day. We pride ourselves on dishes like our famous Buffalo Chicken Spread, Grilled Chicken, Bacon, Cheddar Cheese and Tomato Grilled on Flatbread & Topped with our House Blend. Our unique House Blend is a mix of olive oil, Italian seasonings, garlic and a splash of vinegar, ensuring every bite is packed with flavor.

Come experience the difference that genuine care and quality ingredients make. We're more than just a cafe; we're a part of the Kings Mountain community, and we can't wait to share our table with you.
