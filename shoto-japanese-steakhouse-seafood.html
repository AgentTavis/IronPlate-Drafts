Our Heritage

A Family Tradition of Flavor & Warmth

At Shoto Japanese Steakhouse & Seafood, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a family's passion brought to life. Located right here at 17 N Main St, Lexington, NC, we've been serving our community with traditional Asian entrees, exquisite steaks, and fresh seafood in a relaxed, welcoming environment.

Every dish is crafted with care, bringing the rich flavors of Asia to your table. We pride ourselves on creating memorable dining experiences for individuals and families alike. We also ensure everyone can enjoy a meal with us, offering a variety of vegetarian options.
