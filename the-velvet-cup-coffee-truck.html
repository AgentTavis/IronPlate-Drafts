Our Journey

More Than Just Coffee: It's a Brevard Experience

At The Velvet Cup Coffee Truck, we believe great coffee isn't just a drink – it's an experience. Born out of a passion for perfectly brewed beverages and a love for the vibrant community of Brevard, NC, we set out to create a mobile gourmet coffee truck that brings exceptional taste directly to you.

We're more than just a beloved local spot; we're a part of your daily routine, your special events, and your cherished memories. Every cup is crafted with care, using high-quality beans and a commitment to flavor that stands out. From our classic brews to custom espresso creations, we ensure every sip brings joy.

Whether you're catching us at a local market, enjoying a catered event, or simply grabbing your morning pick-me-up, The Velvet Cup is here to serve Brevard with a smile and a truly remarkable coffee experience. We're proud to be a mobile hub for community, connection, and the finest coffee on wheels.
