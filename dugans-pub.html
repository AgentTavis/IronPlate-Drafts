Our Heritage

More Than Just a Pub, It's a Pinehurst Tradition.

Nestled in the quaint heart of Pinehurst, Dugan's Pub has been a beloved cornerstone of the community for years. We pride ourselves on serving great tasting food in an atmosphere that feels like a warm embrace from across the Atlantic.

Our bar, rich with golf memorabilia, tells tales of legendary rounds and friendly wagers, creating a vibe where everyone feels like a local. Whether you're craving a delicious burger, crispy french fries, or a perfectly poured pint, you'll find it here, served with genuine hospitality.

Dugan's is where good food meets good company, a place where memories are made, and every visit feels like coming home.
