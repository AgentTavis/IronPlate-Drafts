Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Victory.

Welcome to Victory Kitchen, a place where hearty meals and warm hospitality come together. We're a small, family-owned and operated restaurant nestled in a picturesque farmhouse, right near the entrance to Rumbling Bald on Lake Lure.

Every dish we serve is crafted with love, bringing you the authentic taste of "down home country cooking." From our famous "tasty breakfast" that starts your day right to our beloved daily specials, we believe in using fresh, locally sourced ingredients whenever possible.

Come on in, make yourself at home, and experience the charm and flavor that makes Victory Kitchen a true Lake Lure favorite.
