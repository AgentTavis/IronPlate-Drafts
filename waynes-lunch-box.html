Our Story

Taste the Heart of Spindale: A Local Tradition

At Waynes Lunch Box, we believe that great food brings people together. Nestled in the heart of Spindale, NC, our cozy eatery offers a unique twist on traditional lunch options, providing a diverse menu that caters to all taste buds.

We're more than just a place to eat; we're a community gathering spot where fresh ingredients, classic flavors, and friendly faces come together. From sizzling hamburgers to comforting daily specials, every dish is prepared with care and a touch of local charm. Come on in and become a part of our story!
