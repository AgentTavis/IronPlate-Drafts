OUR STORY

Where Freshness Meets Family

At Backfins Crabhouse, our philosophy is simple: serve nothing less than perfection. Nestled in the heart of Wake Forest, we've dedicated ourselves to bringing you the freshest, most delicious seafood experience possible. It’s more than just a meal; it’s a commitment to quality that runs deep.

From the moment you step through our doors, you’ll discover a menu crafted with passion. Imagine indulging in our signature combo platter, brimming with succulent snow crab legs and perfectly pan-seared scallops, accompanied by new potatoes and crisp green beans. Or perhaps our beloved Crab Cake Sandwich, served with a generous helping of fresh-cut fries, catches your eye. Every dish is prepared with pride, designed to be eaten with joy.

We believe in the power of good food to bring people together. That’s why we’re constantly striving to get better and better, ensuring each visit is a memorable one. Backfins Crabhouse isn't just a restaurant; it's a local cornerstone, a small business proud to serve its community. Come support local folks and taste the Backfins difference – where there are truly no compromises.
