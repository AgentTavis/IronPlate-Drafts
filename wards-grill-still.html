Our Legacy

More Than Just Food, It's a Whiteville Tradition.

Ward's Grill isn't just a place to eat in Whiteville; it's an institution. Since 1947, we've been serving up the kind of delicious, comforting meals that bring people back, year after year, generation after generation. You truly cannot come to Whiteville without experiencing Ward's!

People come from all over the city, county, and state just to get a taste of what makes us special. Our reputation even reached the highest office, with former President Bill Clinton himself chowing down on one of our legendary offerings when he was in town. That's the kind of place Ward's Grill is – truly an outstanding establishment!

We open very early because our customers want their burgers and hotdogs, ASAP! And they're willing to stand in long lines, rain or shine, because they know it's always worth it. The unmistakable, mouth-watering smell of Ward's permeates throughout the main street, a delicious invitation that's impossible to resist.
