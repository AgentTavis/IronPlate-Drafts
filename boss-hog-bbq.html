Our Heritage

Crafting Unforgettable BBQ, One Plate at a Time.

Welcome to Boss Hog BBQ, your local barbecue haven in Sherrills Ford, NC! With a 4.5-star rating from over 769 reviews, we're proud to be a community favorite, known for delivering an authentic, mouth-watering barbecue experience that keeps folks coming back for more.

Born from a passion for slow-smoked perfection and time-honored recipes, Boss Hog BBQ brings the true taste of Carolina barbecue to your plate. Every cut of meat is lovingly prepared, seasoned with our signature rubs, and smoked low and slow to achieve that irresistible tenderness and smoky flavor. It's more than just food; it's a celebration of tradition, family, and the joy of good eating.
