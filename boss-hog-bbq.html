Our Journey

More Than Just BBQ, It's a Tradition

Nestled in the heart of Sherrills Ford, Boss Hog BBQ isn't just a restaurant; it's a testament to the timeless art of Southern barbecue. For years, we've honed our craft, slow-smoking premium meats over select hardwoods until they reach a peak of tenderness and flavor that keeps our community coming back.

Our commitment goes beyond the plate. We believe in serving up genuine hospitality alongside our delicious barbecue, creating an experience that feels like home. Every dish is prepared with passion, using recipes passed down through generations, ensuring an authentic taste that reflects our deep roots in North Carolina.
