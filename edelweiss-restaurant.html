Our Heritage

Taste the Tradition, Feel the Family

Here, in the heart of the Blue Ridge Mountains, lies a small piece of Karlsruhe, Germany. Our journey began generations ago, and we’re still making schnitzel and pretzels with recipes our family has used for decades. Every dish tells a story, a legacy passed down through time, ensuring an authentic taste of Germany with every bite.

That’s the beauty of Edelweiss -- every night is a family dinner for us, and you're always invited. We even offer our visiting friends a handpicked selection of German beers, served in our unique collection of traditional glasses and mugs. Between our German hometown decor and our occasional lively polka bands, walking into our Virginia restaurant always feels like embarking on an authentic German experience.

"Edelweiss isn't just a restaurant; it's a journey back to the warmth of family and genuine German hospitality. Every detail, from the decor to the dishes, feels like home."

— A Loyal Patron

Our Specialties

Handcrafted Dishes, Authentic Flavors

Classic Schnitzel

Crispy pork or chicken, hand-pounded and golden-fried to perfection, a true German staple.

Bavarian Pretzels

Warm, soft, and salty, served with house-made sweet mustard and creamy obatzda cheese spread.

Currywurst

Grilled German sausage smothered in a tangy curry ketchup, a beloved street food classic.

Edelweiss Special Pizza

Our signature pizza featuring select German sausages, sauerkraut, and mozzarella on a crispy crust.

Margherita Pizza

A timeless classic with fresh mozzarella, vibrant basil, and rich San Marzano tomato sauce.

Black Forest Cake

Decadent layers of chocolate sponge, whipped cream, and tart cherries, a traditional delight.

Apple Strudel

Flaky, golden-baked pastry filled with spiced apples, raisins, and a hint of cinnamon.

German Beer Selection

A rotating list of authentic German lagers, pilsners, and wheat beers, served in traditional mugs.
