Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community Hub.

For over five cherished years, The Grill At Quarterfield Station has been a beloved cornerstone of the Glen Burnie community. Nestled right across the street, we've had the privilege of serving generations, becoming a true advocate for outstanding food and memorable experiences.

Our commitment is simple: deliver "absolutely fantabulous" food every single time, from classic bar and grill favorites to delightful new creations. We believe in the power of a great meal shared with loved ones.

Beyond the kitchen, our wait staff stands as a testament to dedication and meticulous training. Being ex-servers ourselves, we know what it takes to provide incredibly attentive service, anticipating your needs and ensuring your visit is nothing short of perfect. Come experience the warmth and quality that makes us a true local favorite.
