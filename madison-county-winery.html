Our Heritage

Crafting Unique Experiences, One Sip at a Time

Just a short drive from the Capitol, Madison County Winery offers a delicious variety of wines, beers, and treats. Our outdoor patio provides a relaxed atmosphere, while our pergola seating will leave you with a perfect view. Cozy up in our tasting room and learn more about our winery from our friendly and helpful staff while you enjoy a wine tasting.

We believe in creating a welcoming space where friends and family can gather, unwind, and make lasting memories. Every visit to Madison County Winery is an invitation to discover new flavors and enjoy the beauty of St Charles, NC.

Our Offerings

Taste the Tradition: Wines, Brews & Bites

House Wine Flight

Explore our curated selection with a tasting flight of three unique wines, from crisp whites to robust reds.

Local Craft Beer Selection

A rotating tap featuring the best local breweries, ensuring a fresh and exciting selection with every visit.

Gourmet Charcuterie Board

A perfectly paired assortment of artisanal cheeses, cured meats, olives, and preserves, ideal for sharing.

Crispy Chicken Sliders

Miniature crispy chicken sandwiches with a tangy slaw and brioche buns, a delightful snack.

Artisan Flatbread Pizzas

Freshly baked flatbreads with gourmet toppings, a light yet satisfying meal perfect with a glass of wine.

Decadent Chocolate Lava Cake

Warm, rich chocolate cake with a molten center, served with a scoop of vanilla bean ice cream.
