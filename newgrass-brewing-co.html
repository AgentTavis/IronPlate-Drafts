OUR STORY

More Than Just a Brewery, It's a Community.

Newgrass Brewing Co. is a cherished local brewery nestled in the heart of Shelby, NC. We're more than just a place to grab a drink; we're a hub where friends, family, and neighbors gather to enjoy exceptional craft beer and delicious farm-to-table food.

Our passion began with a simple idea: to create hand-crafted beers that reflect the rich heritage and vibrant spirit of our region. Every brew is a testament to quality, tradition, and innovation, available both on tap in our welcoming tasting room and in kegs for distribution across NC, SC, and GA.

But great beer needs great food. That's why we've meticulously crafted a food menu that complements our brews, featuring a mix of American classics with a fresh, farm-to-table twist. We believe in supporting local farmers and bringing the freshest ingredients from our fields to your plate.

"Newgrass is our go-to spot! The atmosphere is always lively, the beer selection is fantastic, and their Farmhouse Salad is surprisingly delicious for a brewery!"
