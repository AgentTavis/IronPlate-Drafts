Our Journey

Crafting Community, One Pint & Plate at a Time

Welcome to Newgrass Brewing Co., the heart of Shelby's craft beverage and culinary scene. Born from a passion for exceptional beer and a deep respect for local ingredients, we've grown into a beloved gathering spot where friends and family connect over unique flavors and genuine hospitality.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to brew outstanding craft beers that reflect the rich agricultural heritage of the Carolinas. That vision expanded to include a farm-to-table food menu, ensuring every dish pairs perfectly with our brews, using the freshest ingredients sourced right from our community.

More than just a bar and grill, Newgrass is a testament to quality, craftsmanship, and community spirit. We invite you to experience the difference that dedication to local producers and a love for good food and beer makes.

Our Offerings

Taste the Craft: Beers & Bites

Discover our rotating selection of handcrafted beers on tap, perfectly paired with our delicious American farm-to-table food.
(Below are examples of our typical menu items; actual selection may vary seasonally.)

The Shelby Lager

Crisp, refreshing, and perfectly balanced. Our flagship lager, brewed for easy drinking. A true taste of Shelby.

Piedmont Pale Ale

Hoppy and bright with citrus notes, yet exceptionally smooth. A Southern classic that's always a hit.

Carolina IPA

For the serious hop-head. Bold, aromatic, and bursting with tropical fruit flavors. Our most robust IPA.

The Newgrass Burger

Juicy, farm-fresh patty with local cheddar, crispy bacon, and our signature sauce on a brioche bun.

Smoked Brisket Sandwich

Slow-smoked, tender brisket piled high on a toasted roll with tangy BBQ sauce and house-made slaw.

Seasonal Farm Salad

Fresh greens, seasonal vegetables, local cheeses, and a homemade vinaigrette. Light, fresh, and flavorful.

Hand-Cut Truffle Fries

Our perfectly golden, crispy fries, tossed with truffle oil and Parmesan. An irresistible side.

Daily Chef's Special
