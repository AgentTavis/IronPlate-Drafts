Our Journey

More Than Just a Bakery: Your Sweet Escape & Local Hub

Welcome to Homespun Hobbies, where passion for baking meets community charm. Nestled in the heart of Andrews, NC, we've created a cozy spot where you can satisfy your sweet tooth with our wonderful selection of fresh-baked goodies.

We believe in keeping things exciting, which is why we change our menu weekly. There's always something new and delicious waiting for you, from classic comforts to innovative treats. Beyond the delightful aromas, our mercantile offers a curated selection of local treasures, making every visit a discovery.
