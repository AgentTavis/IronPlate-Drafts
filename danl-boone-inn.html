OUR STORY

A Taste of Family

For over 50 years, Dan'l Boone Inn has been a cherished culinary landmark in Boone and the high country. We pride ourselves on serving delicious, homecooked meals that instantly transport you back to grandma's kitchen.

Our unique family-style dining experience begins with a refreshing salad in the summer or a comforting soup in the winter. Each meal features three hearty meats and five seasonal vegetables, served alongside warm, flaky biscuits, sweet preserves, a delightful dessert, and your choice of beverage. Weekends are a special treat, with a full family-style breakfast served on both Saturdays and Sundays.

We believe in good food, good company, and making memories around the table. Come discover why generations have made Dan'l Boone Inn their favorite gathering place.
