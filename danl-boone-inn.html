Our Legacy

A Taste of Family, Rooted in Tradition.

For over 50 years, Dan'l Boone Inn has been a cherished culinary landmark in Boone and the high country. We've proudly served delicious homecooked meals, just like the ones you remember from grandma's house, creating warm memories for generations.

Our unique family-style dining experience starts with a fresh salad in the summer or a hearty soup in the winter, followed by a bountiful spread of three meats, five garden-fresh vegetables, warm biscuits, homemade preserves, a delightful dessert, and your choice of beverage. It's more than a meal; it's a gathering.

Weekends bring our full family-style breakfast, offering a perfect start to your Saturday or Sunday, ensuring everyone leaves satisfied and ready for their day in the beautiful Blue Ridge Mountains.
