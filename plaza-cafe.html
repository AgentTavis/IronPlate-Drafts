Our Story

A Taste of Home, With a Twist of India.

At Plaza Cafe, we believe in serving up honest, delicious food that makes you feel good. Located right here in Selma, NC, we're a local diner committed to fresh ingredients and friendly service.

While our menu features classic diner favorites like juicy burgers, satisfying wraps, and refreshing drinks, we're particularly known for a delightful surprise: our incredible Indian specials every Wednesday!

It's where Selma comes for a hearty meal, whether it's a quick lunch or a relaxed dinner. Come on in and discover your new favorite dish!
