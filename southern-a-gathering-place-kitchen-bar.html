Our Story

Where Southern Hospitality Meets Culinary Excellence.

At Southern, we believe in creating more than just a meal; we craft an experience. Nestled in the heart of Southport, NC, our kitchen and bar is a true 'gathering place' where incredible service, delectable food, and a warm, inviting ambiance come together.

From the moment you step through our doors, our friendly team is dedicated to making you feel right at home, whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying a delightful evening out. We're proud to offer a menu that showcases the best of Southern flavors, from our perfectly combined fried green tomatoes and pimento cheese to our incredibly tasty grouper and delicious barbecue chicken. Every dish is prepared with passion, served with a smile, and designed to make your visit unforgettable.
