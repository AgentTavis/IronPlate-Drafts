Our Authentic Italian Offerings

While our specific daily menu changes with the freshest ingredients, we always celebrate the rich traditions of Italian cuisine. Here are some of the categories you can expect:

Antipasti

Begin your meal with a selection of classic Italian appetizers. From fresh bruschetta to artisanal cured meats and cheeses, perfect for sharing.

Primi Piatti

Indulge in our exquisite pasta dishes, handcrafted with the finest ingredients. Experience the rich flavors of traditional Italian sauces and fresh, al dente pasta.

Secondi

Our main courses feature succulent meats and fresh seafood, prepared with authentic Italian techniques and served with vibrant seasonal accompaniments.

Contorni

Complement your meal with our selection of fresh, seasonal vegetable sides, prepared simply to highlight their natural flavors.

Dolci

Conclude your dining experience with our delightful homemade desserts. Classic tiramisu, panna cotta, and other sweet treats await.

Vini & Bevande

Pair your meal with our thoughtfully curated selection of Italian wines, beers, and non-alcoholic beverages, chosen to enhance your dining pleasure.
