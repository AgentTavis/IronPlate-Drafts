Our Heritage

Brewed with Passion, Rooted in Tradition

Welcome to Old Barn Brewery, Anne Arundel County's first farm brewery, nestled right here on our beloved family farm on the Broadneck Peninsula in Arnold, NC. Our dream was to create a space where the community could gather, enjoy exceptional craft beers, and experience the charm of farm life.

We're proud to offer a beautiful setting, where the fresh air and scenic views complement every sip. Our beers are crafted with care, reflecting the dedication and tradition that runs through generations of our family. While we're currently open on select Saturdays, we pour our heart into every brew and every moment shared with our guests.

Visitors often comment on the great taste of our beers and the unique farm atmosphere. Don't forget to check out our produce stand during your visit for fresh, local goods straight from our fields!

Our Offerings

Farmhouse Ale

Broadneck IPA

Our Promise

Experience the Old Barn Difference

Authentic Farm Experience

Step onto our family farm and immerse yourself in a truly unique brewery setting. From the rustic charm of our barn to the fresh air of the Broadneck Peninsula, it's an experience unlike any other.

Our dedication to quality shines in every glass. Enjoy a rotating selection of meticulously crafted beers, from our crisp Farmhouse Ale to the bold Broadneck IPA, all brewed with passion and local ingredients.
