Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience

Welcome to Harbor One, High Point's favorite local Bar & Grill. Since opening our doors, we've been dedicated to creating a welcoming space where friends and family can gather, enjoy great food, and make lasting memories. We believe in the simple pleasure of good company and delicious, unpretentious fare.

Our commitment is to fresh ingredients, expertly crafted dishes, and a vibrant atmosphere that feels like home. Whether you're looking for a quick bite after work, a casual dinner, or a lively spot to catch up, Harbor One is your destination. We're proud to be a cornerstone of the High Point community, serving up smiles and satisfaction with every plate.
