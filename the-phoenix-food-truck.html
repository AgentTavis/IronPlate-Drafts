Our Roots

Taste the Passion, Savor the Craft

Welcome to The Phoenix Food Truck! We're proud to bring authentic and innovative Mexican flavors to Robbinsville, NC 28771. Known for our incredible cuisine and a vibrant community presence with over 254 posts sharing our journey, we might be a food truck, but our passion for gourmet Mexican dishes shines through.

Our Culinary Delights

Discover Our Flavorful Menu

Steak Tips

Tender, perfectly seasoned steak tips, a fan favorite that brings a gourmet touch to our truck.

Raspberry Doughnut Cheesecake

A unique and indulgent dessert combining the sweetness of raspberries, creamy cheesecake, and a delightful doughnut base.

Filet Mignon w/ Mashed Potatoes

Elevate your food truck experience with succulent filet mignon served over creamy mashed potatoes, complete with savory mushrooms.

Signature Tacos al Pastor

Marinated pork slow-cooked to perfection, served in warm tortillas with fresh pineapple, onion, and cilantro.

Loaded Nachos Supreme

Crispy tortilla chips piled high with your choice of meat, melted cheese, fresh jalapeños, sour cream, and house-made salsa.

Elote (Mexican Street Corn)

Grilled corn on the cob slathered with creamy sauce, chili powder, cotija cheese, and a squeeze of lime.
