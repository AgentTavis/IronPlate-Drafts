Our Legacy

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Tradition.

Marylou's is a beloved local spot in Fort Washington, NC. For years, we've been the heart of the community, a place where friends gather, families celebrate, and good times are always on the menu. We pour our passion into every dish and every pour, creating an atmosphere that feels like coming home.

Our commitment to local ingredients, exceptional service, and that unmistakable bar & grill buzz is what makes Marylou's truly special. From sizzling burgers to perfectly crafted drinks, we're dedicated to delivering an experience that keeps you coming back for more.
