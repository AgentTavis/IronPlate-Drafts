Our Journey

Our Family, Your Table: A Franklinton Tradition.

At Brazil's Munchies, we believe every meal should feel like a celebration. We’re thrilled to be here on this beautiful Saturday, ready to welcome you to our cozy diner. Our mission is simple: to offer Franklinton a place where you can always get a great meal at an affordable price, wrapped in a warm, friendly atmosphere.

Since opening our doors at 427 E Green Street, we've poured our heart and soul into creating dishes that satisfy and service that makes you feel right at home. It's more than just food; it’s about community, comfort, and the joy of sharing good times around the table. Come on down and taste the difference that passion makes!
