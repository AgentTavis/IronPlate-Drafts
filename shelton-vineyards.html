Our Heritage

A Legacy Steeped in Southern Charm and Exceptional Wine.

Welcome to Shelton Vineyards, an award-winning North Carolina vineyard in the heart of Yadkin Valley wine country. We believe that great wine is born from passion, tradition, and the fertile soil of our region. Our commitment to excellence in every bottle and every guest experience defines who we are.

From estate-grown wines and chef-inspired dining to seasonal events and Southern hospitality, every visit is an invitation to slow down, connect, and savor the experience. We invite you to discover the warmth of our vineyard, the richness of our wines, and the unforgettable moments waiting for you. Your glass is waiting.
