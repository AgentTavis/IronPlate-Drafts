Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Memory.

The Tuck and Table isn't just a restaurant; it's a cherished part of Bryson City. We're a beloved local spot where neighbors gather, travelers find comfort, and everyone leaves feeling a little more at home.

Nestled right on Everett St., we've built a reputation for genuine hospitality and hearty, satisfying food. We believe in simple pleasures done exceptionally well – from our welcoming atmosphere to every plate served.

Come on in, pull up a chair, and experience the warmth and flavors that make The Tuck and Table a true community favorite. We're excited to have you join our story.
