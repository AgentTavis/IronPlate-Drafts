OUR STORY

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community Hub

The LOCAL is more than just a bar and grill; it's the heart of Nottingham, NC. Since 2008, we've been proudly serving up delicious food, ice-cold drinks, and a warm, inviting atmosphere that feels like home. Our passion is creating a space where friends and family can gather, share laughter, and make lasting memories.

We believe in good company, great food, and genuine hospitality. Every dish is crafted with care, using fresh, quality ingredients, and every customer is treated like family. Come on down and experience the true spirit of local camaraderie that makes The LOCAL a beloved Nottingham tradition.
