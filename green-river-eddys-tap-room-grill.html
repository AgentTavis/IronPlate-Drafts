OUR STORY

Rooted in Community, Fueled by Adventure

Green River Eddy's Tap Room & Grill is a proud part of Saluda Outfitters, embodying the spirit of Western North Carolina's outdoor lifestyle. Our mission is to be a preferred destination in Saluda, specializing not only in top-quality outdoor gear and apparel but also providing a vibrant tap room and grill experience.

We believe in serving delicious food that complements an active lifestyle, from gourmet burgers to perfectly smoked pork. Paired with a rotating selection of craft beers and regular live music, we offer a welcoming space where locals and visitors alike can relax, refuel, and reconnect with nature and community.
