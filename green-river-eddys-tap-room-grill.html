Our Story

More Than Just a Meal

At Green River Eddy's Tap Room & Grill, we're proud to be an integral part of Saluda Outfitters, fulfilling the mission to be the preferred outdoor destination for Saluda, N.C., and the entire Western North Carolina area. We specialize in top-quality gear, footwear, and apparel, but that's just one part of our story.

Our passion extends to providing a trendy tap room and grill experience, coupled with an vibrant live music venue. We believe in crafting unforgettable moments, whether you're fueling up before an adventure, unwinding after a day outdoors, or simply enjoying great food and company. We're where community gathers, stories are shared, and every visit feels like coming home.
