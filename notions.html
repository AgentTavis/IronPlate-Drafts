Our Culinary Delights

A Taste of Notions: Tapas & Shared Plates

Starters

Kick off your culinary adventure with our vibrant Cobb Salad, a fresh medley of crisp greens, grilled chicken, smoked bacon, ripe avocado, farm-fresh egg, and a tangy blue cheese crumble, all tossed in our house vinaigrette. Perfect for sharing or a refreshing start.

Entrees

Dive into our perfectly Seared Ahi Tuna, a masterpiece of culinary art. Served rare with a sesame crust, nestled on a bed of ginger-infused sticky rice and vibrant stir-fried vegetables, then drizzled with a sweet chili glaze. A light yet satisfying entree.

Pasta al Pomodoro

Experience the timeless simplicity of Pasta al Pomodoro. Al dente spaghetti tossed in rich olive oil, ripe cherry tomatoes bursting with flavor, and fresh basil, finished with a sprinkle of Parmesan. A comforting classic, reimagined.

Dessert

Indulge your sweet tooth with our creamy Vanilla Cheesecake. A velvety smooth vanilla bean cheesecake atop a buttery graham cracker crust, finished with a seasonal berry compote. The perfect end to your meal.
