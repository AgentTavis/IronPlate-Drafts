Our Story

Crafting Memories, One Evening at a Time.

Welcome to CSB! Nestled in the heart of Clemmons, we’ve created more than just a bar; it's a social sanctuary. Our vision was to blend sophistication with genuine comfort, offering a retreat where every visit feels like a special occasion. From our carefully curated cocktail list to our fantastic liquor selection, every detail is designed to elevate your experience.

We believe in the power of good company, great music, and exceptional taste. Join us for an upscale, relaxed atmosphere, where live music and weekly events add a vibrant rhythm to your evenings. Clemmons Social Bar is where connections are made, laughter is shared, and unforgettable memories are forged.
