Our Legacy

Experience the Heart of Deep Run Cuisine

This is a great roadside restaurant with an outdoor walk up window. One side is dedicated to serving up delicious, hand-scooped ice cream, perfect for a treat on a warm day. The other side is where we craft our mouth-watering burgers and classic American food.

At The Farmhouse, your order gets written down on a pad, a charming tradition that ensures every meal is prepared with personal care and attention. We're proud to be a local favorite, known for our cozy atmosphere and straightforward, honest cooking that has satisfied generations in Deep Run.
