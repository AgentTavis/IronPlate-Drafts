Our Story

A Deep Run Tradition: Simple Food, Honest Service.

This is a great roadside restaurant with an outdoor walk-up window. One side is dedicated to serving up delightful ice cream cones and sundaes, perfect for a refreshing treat. The other side is where you'll find our mouth-watering burgers, subs, sandwiches, hotdogs, and fresh salads.

We believe in keeping things simple and authentic – your order gets written down on a pad, ensuring every meal is crafted with personal care. It's the cozy, local favorite where good food and friendly faces meet, a true Deep Run tradition for generations.
