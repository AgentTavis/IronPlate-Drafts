Our Journey

Crafting Community, One Mead at a Time

Brewlihan Mead Company isn't just a place to grab a drink; it's a beloved local spot in Oakland Park, NC, built on passion and community spirit. From our humble beginnings, our mission has been to create a warm, inviting space where friends and neighbors can gather, share stories, and savor exceptional mead alongside delicious grill classics.

We believe in the power of good company and even better beverages. Every batch of mead is crafted with care, and every dish is prepared to bring joy. Come experience the heart of Oakland Park with us.
