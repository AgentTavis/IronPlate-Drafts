Our Heritage

Crafting Edenton's Favorite Pub Experience.

At Governor's Pub, we believe in serving up more than just great food and drinks – we're dishing out a true community experience. Since opening our doors, our mission has been simple: to be Edenton's go-to spot for mouth-watering pizza, crispy wings, juicy burgers, and all your favorite traditional pub fare.

We're proud to be a place where friends gather, families make memories, and sports fans cheer on their teams on our big screen HDTVs. Our commitment to quality ingredients, friendly service, and a vibrant atmosphere is at the heart of everything we do. Come on in and taste why Governor's Pub is the spot Edenton deserves!
