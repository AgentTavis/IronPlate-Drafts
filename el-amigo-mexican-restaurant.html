Our Heritage

A Taste of Tradition, Crafted with Heart.

Welcome to El Amigo, Your Home for Authentic Mexican Cuisine!

At El Amigo, we believe every meal is a celebration. Nestled in the heart of Kannapolis, NC, our vibrant Mexican eatery has been serving up joy, one dish at a time, for years. We pride ourselves on creating a warm, inviting atmosphere where families and friends can gather to enjoy expansive flavors and unforgettable moments.

From the sizzle of our legendary fajitas to the rich, comforting embrace of our burritos, every item on our menu is prepared with fresh ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations. And of course, no visit is complete without one of our handcrafted margaritas!

Come on in and taste the passion, the tradition, and the friendship that make El Amigo more than just a restaurant – it's an experience.

Our Culinary Journey

Savor the Flavors of Mexico, Crafted for You.

Sizzling Fajitas

Your choice of marinated chicken, steak, or a combo, served with fresh peppers, onions, and all the fixings.

Classic Carnitas Platter

Slow-cooked, tender pork, traditionally seasoned and served with rice, beans, and warm tortillas.

Burrito Grande

A generously sized flour tortilla filled with your choice of savory meats, rice, beans, and topped with our signature sauce.

Enchiladas Supremas

Three delicious enchiladas filled with various fillings, covered in our rich red sauce and melted cheese.

Street Style Tacos

Authentic small corn tortillas with your choice of al pastor, carne asada, or pollo, topped with fresh cilantro and onion.

Quesadillas Rancheras

Grilled flour tortilla filled with melted cheese and your choice of protein, served with sour cream and guacamole.

Chile Relleno Fiesta

Two poblano peppers filled with cheese, lightly battered and fried, then smothered in our special sauce.

Hand-Shaken Margaritas

Our famous blend of premium tequila, fresh lime juice, and a touch of agave – the perfect companion to any meal.
