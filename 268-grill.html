Our Story

Authentic Comfort, Served with a Smile

268 Grill, nestled in the heart of Elkin, NC, is more than just a place to eat – it's a community hub where delicious comfort food meets a warm, inviting atmosphere. We've been dedicated to serving hearty meals that feel like home, made with care and a passion for good food.

Since our doors first opened, we've cherished the support of our incredible customers, especially through challenging times. Every dish we serve is a testament to our commitment to quality and the simple joy of sharing a great meal.
