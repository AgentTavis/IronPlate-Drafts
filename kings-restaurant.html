Our Story

A Legacy of Flavor, Smoked to Perfection Since 1936.

For over 85 years, King's Restaurant has been a cornerstone of the Kinston, NC community, serving up our world-famous chicken and barbecue with a commitment to quality and tradition that spans generations. What started as a humble family endeavor in 1936 has grown into a beloved local institution, cherished by families near and far.

We believe in the power of good food to bring people together. Every recipe, from our slow-smoked barbecue to our perfectly crispy fried chicken, is crafted with the same dedication and secret family techniques passed down through the years. When you visit King's, you're not just getting a meal – you're experiencing a piece of Kinston's heart and a taste of Southern hospitality that makes everyone feel like family.

"King's isn't just a restaurant; it's a family tradition. My grandparents brought my parents here, my parents brought me, and now I bring my kids. The barbecue taste is timeless!"

- A Kinston Local, 3rd Generation Patron
