Our Legacy

Generations of Kinston's Finest BBQ.

For over 80 years, Kings BBQ has been a cherished institution in Kinston, North Carolina. Established in 1936, our family-owned restaurant has perfected the art of authentic Southern barbecue, serving up recipes passed down through generations. We're more than just a place to eat; we're a gathering spot where families create memories, friends share laughs, and everyone enjoys truly world-class food.

Bring the family to Kings BBQ and experience the taste of tradition. From our slow-smoked barbecue to our renowned fried chicken, every dish is crafted with passion and dedication. We invite you to visit us at our Kinston location today and become part of the Kings BBQ story!
