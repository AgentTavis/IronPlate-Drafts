Our Legacy

More Than Just BBQ, It's a Kinston Tradition

For over 88 years, Kings Restaurant has been the heart and soul of Kinston, NC's culinary scene. What started as a small, family-run eatery in 1936 has grown into a beloved landmark, famous for its authentic, slow-smoked barbecue and crispy, flavorful chicken.

We believe in good food, warm hospitality, and the kind of meal that brings families together. Every dish is prepared with the same dedication to quality and flavor that our founders, Mr. and Mrs. King, instilled decades ago. When you dine with us, you're not just eating a meal; you're becoming part of the Kings family tradition.
