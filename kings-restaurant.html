Our Heritage

Generations of Flavor, Rooted in Kinston.

Welcome to King's Restaurant, a true Kinston institution! Since 1936, we've been serving up authentic Carolina BBQ and our famous fried chicken, crafted with recipes passed down through generations. What started as a small, humble eatery has grown into a beloved local landmark, cherished by families and food lovers alike.

Every dish tells a story of tradition, passion, and the commitment to quality that defines Southern hospitality. We invite you to bring your family and experience the warmth, the flavors, and the history that make King's BBQ more than just a meal – it's a memory in the making. Come visit us and taste why we're a Kinston favorite!
