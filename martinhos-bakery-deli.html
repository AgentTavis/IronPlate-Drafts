Our Story

Generations of Taste, Rooted in Mebane.

Nestled in the heart of downtown Mebane, Martinho's Bakery & Deli has been a cherished culinary landmark for decades. Tucked away from the main tourist buzz, we've quietly served our community with homemade goodness, becoming a true local gem.

Surrounded by the unique charm of antique and specialty shops, our eatery offers a comforting escape. We believe in the simple pleasure of fresh, quality ingredients and time-honored recipes, creating an experience that feels like home.
