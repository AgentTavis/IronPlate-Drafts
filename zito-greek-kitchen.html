Our Heritage

A Taste of Greece, Rooted in Mt Airy

At Zito Greek Kitchen, we believe in bringing authentic flavors and warmth to our community. We're more than just a Bar & Grill; we're a place where friends and family gather, where every meal is prepared with passion and served with a smile. Our journey is one of tradition, taste, and a deep appreciation for the culinary heritage of Greece.

We're especially proud to partner with The Bread Basket, ensuring every dish is served alongside the freshest organic sourdough loaves, baguettes, and other handcrafted sourdough treats. This collaboration allows us to bring you an unparalleled dining experience, combining our rich Greek recipes with the finest local, artisanal bread. It's a commitment to quality and community that defines who we are.
