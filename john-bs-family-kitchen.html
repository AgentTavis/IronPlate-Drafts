Our Journey

A Taste of Home, Right Here in Kingsport

Welcome to John B's Family Kitchen, where every dish tells a story of comfort, tradition, and authentic flavor. Born from a passion for bringing people together over incredible food, our kitchen is a true reflection of Kingsport's warm, welcoming spirit.

We believe in full-service dining with a focus on homestyle favorites, crafted with care and the freshest ingredients. From our savory seafood and rich pastas to our hearty sandwiches, we've poured our heart into creating a menu that feels like a hug on a plate.

Your trust is our priority, so businesses can't pay to alter or remove their reviews. We're committed to transparency and letting our food speak for itself. Come on down and become part of our family tradition!
