Our Journey

Where Every Bean Tells a Story

At Majik Beanz Espresso, we believe in crafting more than just coffee – we craft experiences. Nestled in the heart of Carolina Beach, we've been proudly serving our community with exceptional brews, delicious treats, and a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're starting your day with a jolt of espresso, catching up with friends over a creamy latte, or simply finding a peaceful spot to work, our little coffee shop is your everyday escape.

Our passion for coffee runs deep, from carefully selected beans to the art of every pour. We're dedicated to bringing that "majik" to your cup, ensuring that every visit leaves you refreshed and ready for your day. Come on in, where the vibes and the coffee are always good!

Our Creations

Taste the Majik in Every Sip

Espresso Shots

Rich, bold, and perfectly extracted. The pure essence of coffee to kickstart your day.

Classic Latte

Smooth espresso mixed with steamed milk, topped with a delicate layer of foam. Pure comfort.

Rich Cappuccino

A perfectly balanced blend of espresso, steamed milk, and a generous cap of frothed milk.

Bold Americano

A simple yet potent brew, combining hot water with our intense espresso shots.

Smooth Cold Brew

Steeped for hours for a low-acid, naturally sweet, and incredibly refreshing coffee experience.

Signature Milkshakes
