Our Story

More Than Just a Bottle Shop, It's an Experience.

Nestled in the heart of Wilmington's historic City Market downtown district, Market House Beer & Wine offers a fresh take on your local bottle shop. We’ve created a welcoming space where you can explore a curated selection of fine wines, craft beers, and unique THC products.

Whether you're looking for the perfect bottle to take home for a special occasion, or you want to relax and enjoy a glass of wine or a cold beer right here in our cozy bar, we have you covered. Our small prepackaged food items are also available to complement your drink choice, making us the ideal spot for a quick stop or a leisurely afternoon.
