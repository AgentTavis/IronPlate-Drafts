OUR STORY

From Our Nest to Your Table: Baking with Heart in Swannanoa

Welcome to Ovenbird Kitchen & Bakery, a warm and inviting diner nestled right in the vibrant heart of Swannanoa, NC. We're proud to be your local haven for all things delicious, focusing on small-batch baking that brings joy with every bite.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a place where everyone feels welcome, a true community "nest." We pour passion into crafting fresh bread and sweet treats daily, ensuring that whether you're starting your day with a pastry and coffee or enjoying a hearty lunch, you experience the genuine taste of homemade goodness. Every Birdy Welcome is not just a tagline; it's our promise to you.
