Our Heritage

Bringing the Flavors of the Mediterranean to Columbia, NC

At Xenia Greek Kouzina, we invite you on a culinary journey to the sun-drenched shores of Greece. Our modern Greek restaurant blends tradition with contemporary flair, creating an elegant dining destination.

Nestled in the heart of Columbia, North Carolina, Xenia is more than just a meal; it's an experience. We celebrate the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean, with a special emphasis on the freshest seafood, sourced with care and prepared with passion. Our commitment extends beyond the plate to provide unparalleled service and genuine hospitality – the true meaning of 'Xenia'.

"Xenia is about more than just food; it's about sharing a part of our culture, our warmth, and our love for exquisite flavors with every guest who walks through our doors."

— The Xenia Family
