OUR STORY

A Journey of Passion and Tradition

From a day at the vineyard to an exceptional dining or wine-paired experience, JOLO offers unforgettable memories that will last a lifetime. Our journey began with a vision to create a space where exquisite flavors, family traditions, and world-class hospitality converge.

Immerse yourself in a journey of exquisite flavors, family traditions, and world-class hospitality, set against breathtaking views of Pilot Mountain. We’re excited to welcome you and share our award-winning wines, one-of-a-kind vineyard experiences, and rich winemaking heritage.
