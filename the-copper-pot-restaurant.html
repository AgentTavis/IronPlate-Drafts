Our Heritage

Serving Mt Airy with Heart & Soul Since Day One

The Copper Pot isn't just a restaurant; it's a cornerstone of the Mt Airy community. We believe in crafting more than just delicious meals—we're creating memories, fostering connections, and giving back to the town we love.

From the moment you step through our doors, you'll feel the warmth of true American Southern hospitality. Our kitchen is dedicated to serving absolutely fantastic meals, just like Gracie's legendary catfish, that have travelers discovering us as a delightful find on their road trips and locals returning week after week.

We're passionate about good food, good company, and supporting local schools through initiatives like Cedar Ridge Spirit Night. When you dine with us, you're not just eating—you're doing good, strengthening our community, and savoring the authentic flavors of home.
