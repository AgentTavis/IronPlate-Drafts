OUR STORY

The Heart of Carolina Beach Seafood & Fun.

At Buzz's Crab Shack & Seafood, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a Carolina Beach institution. For years, we've been serving up the freshest oceanfront catches, combining them with lively karaoke nights and expertly crafted cocktails. Our commitment to genuine reviews and an unforgettable experience is at the core of who we are.

Your trust is our priority, and you can be confident that our 4.2-star rating from 957 reviews is a testament to the real experiences of our valued guests. We believe in transparency and quality, ensuring every visit is a true taste of coastal hospitality and late-night entertainment.
