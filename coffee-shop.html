Our Heritage

More Than Just Coffee, It's Community.

At Coffee Shop, we're proud to be a cornerstone of the Raeford community. We believe in crafting more than just delicious beverages and hearty meals; we cultivate connections and create a welcoming space for everyone. We're a true local diner, a place where neighbors gather, friends connect, and families share memorable moments.

From our freshly brewed coffee to our classic diner fare, every item on our menu is prepared with care and a commitment to quality. We cherish our role as a beloved spot where "Something's Brewing" every day – be it a perfect cup of coffee or a friendly conversation.

Our Offerings

Diner Classics & Coffee Perfection.

The Raeford Breakfast Burrito

A hearty blend of eggs, sausage, cheese, and hash browns, wrapped in a warm tortilla. The perfect start to your day!

Signature House Blend Coffee

Our unique house blend, carefully brewed for a rich, smooth flavor. The perfect companion for any meal.

Cactus Creek Cold Brew

Slow-steeped for 24 hours using locally roasted Cactus Creek beans. Bold, smooth, and incredibly refreshing.

Classic Diner Cheeseburger

Juicy beef patty, melted cheese, fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles on a toasted bun. A timeless favorite.

Fresh Baked Blueberry Scones

Made fresh daily with plump blueberries and a hint of lemon zest. Perfect with your morning coffee.

Homemade Chicken Salad Sandwich

Our secret recipe chicken salad, fresh lettuce, and tomato on your choice of bread. Light and satisfying.

Kids' Pancake Plate

Fluffy pancakes served with crispy bacon or sausage, just for our younger guests.

Espresso & Latte Bar

Craft your perfect drink with our expert baristas. From classic espresso to custom lattes, we've got you covered.
