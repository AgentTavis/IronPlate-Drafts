Our Heritage

A Taste of Home, Decades in the Making.

Nestled in the charming town of Mount Airy, NC, Ocie's Restaurant stands as a true American classic. We've been serving up heartwarming, home-cooked comfort food to our community and visitors for over two decades.

At Ocie's, we believe in delicious meals, generous portions, and treating every guest like family. It's more than just a meal; it's an experience built on tradition, quality ingredients, and a genuine smile.

From our snappy lunches that keep Mount Airy's workers fueled, to our affordable Southern-style breakfasts that kickstart your day, we're dedicated to providing a comfortable, friendly atmosphere where memories are made around the table.

— A Loyal Customer
