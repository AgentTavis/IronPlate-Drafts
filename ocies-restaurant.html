Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's Family.

Located in the charming town of Mount Airy, NC, Ocie's Restaurant is a quintessential American eatery that captures the essence of home-cooked comfort food. For decades, we've been a beloved local spot, renowned for our generous portions, friendly staff, and dishes that taste just like grandma used to make.

We pride ourselves on serving delicious, affordable Southern-style breakfast and snappy lunches that keep the workers of Mount Airy coming back daily. At Ocie's, every guest is treated like family, ensuring a dining experience that's as warm and inviting as our meals. Come on down and become part of our story.
