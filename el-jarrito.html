Our Story

Bringing Authentic Mexican Flavors to Yadkinville

El Jarrito is truly a hidden gem, tucked away in the charming town of Yadkinville, North Carolina. We're proud to be a local Bar and Grill that consistently punches above its weight, delivering quality dishes and a warm, inviting atmosphere to our community.

Our passion lies in crafting authentic Mexican cuisine with fresh ingredients and traditional recipes. We believe great food brings people together, and every dish we serve is made with love and attention to detail, aiming to create unforgettable moments for you and your loved ones.
