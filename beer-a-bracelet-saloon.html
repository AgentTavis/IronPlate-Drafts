Our Story

More Than Just a Bar: A Community Hub in Brevard.

Welcome to Beer & A Bracelet Saloon, where charm meets craft in the heart of Brevard, NC. We're a unique destination that beautifully merges two passions: celebrating local artistry and serving up exceptional drinks in an unforgettable setting. Step inside and discover a treasure trove of delightful items from talented local artists – a true "Hunters & Gatherers" experience.

But that's just one half of our story. Beyond the curated artisan goods, you'll find what signs proudly declare is the smallest bar in the US. Nestled cozy, with just three intimate chairs for those over 21, our bar offers a distinctive experience. Here, you can unwind with a selection of craft beers, finely mixed cocktails, and expertly brewed coffee-type drinks. We believe in friendly faces, a warm atmosphere, and making every visit feel special. Come for the art, stay for the unique ambiance and a chat with our friendly staff!

Signature Cocktails

Whether you need a morning pick-me-up or an afternoon treat, enjoy our selection of rich coffees and espressos.
