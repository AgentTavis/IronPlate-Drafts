Our Journey

Crafting Sweet Memories, One Bite at a Time.

Welcome to Batter & Crumbs, where every treat is baked with love and a sprinkle of magic! Nestled in the heart of Lenoir, NC, our bakery is dedicated to helping you celebrate life's sweetest moments, big or small.

From whimsical custom cakes to delightful allergen-friendly options, we pour our heart into every creation. We believe that everyone deserves to enjoy delicious baked goods, which is why we meticulously craft treats to suit various dietary needs without compromising on flavor or joy.

It's more than just baking; it's about being a part of your celebrations, your smiles, and your cherished memories. We're proud to be a local favorite, known for our unique designs and the warm, personal touch that goes into everything we make.

Our Offerings

Our Sweetest Creations, Baked Fresh Daily.

While our custom orders are boundless, here are a few fan favorites and examples of our unique approach.

Jellycat-Inspired Cinnamon Roll

A truly unique creation, soft and fluffy, infused with warm cinnamon and sweet glaze. A playful nod to a beloved character, crafted with exquisite detail and flavor that will transport you back to childhood joy.

Whimsical Mushroom Cake

Our famous mushroom cake, a delightful centerpiece for any celebration. Moist, flavorful cake adorned with intricate, edible mushroom details, showcasing our artistry and passion for whimsical design.

Custom Celebration Cakes

Tailor-made for your special occasions. From birthdays to anniversaries, we design and bake the cake of your dreams, reflecting your unique style and taste. Contact us for a personalized consultation!

Gourmet Allergen-Friendly Cookies

Delicious cookies crafted with care for various dietary needs. Enjoy our gluten-free, dairy-free, or nut-free options, all bursting with flavor and made with the finest ingredients. Always check for specific allergens.

Artisan Cupcake Collection

A rotating selection of beautifully decorated cupcakes, perfect for individual treats or party platters. Each cupcake is a miniature work of art, featuring unique flavor combinations and exquisite frostings.

Seasonal Fruit Tarts

Fresh, vibrant fruit tarts made with a buttery shortbread crust and a rich, creamy pastry filling, topped with the finest seasonal fruits. A light and refreshing dessert that's as beautiful as it is delicious.
