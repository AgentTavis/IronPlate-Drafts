Our Story

Savor the Taste of Authentic Italian in Pinehurst

Nestled in the charming Village of Pinehurst, The Fairwoods On Seven brings the heart and soul of Italy to your table. Under the expert guidance of Chef Meg, our kitchen crafts exceptional Italian cuisine with a focus on fresh ingredients and timeless techniques.

We believe dining should be an experience – a moment to connect, celebrate, and indulge. Our casual yet elegant atmosphere provides the perfect backdrop for memorable meals, whether it's a romantic dinner or a gathering with friends.
