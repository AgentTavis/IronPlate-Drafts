Our Story & Experience

Bringing Authentic Southern Soul to Asheboro

At CHIVES, we celebrate the rich, vibrant flavors of Southern, Soul, and Creole cuisine, right here in the heart of Asheboro, NC. Our kitchen is a testament to time-honored recipes, passed down through generations, reimagined with a touch of modern flair and a whole lot of love.

We believe food is more than just sustenance; it's a journey, a memory, and a gathering. Every dish we serve is crafted to evoke that comforting, home-cooked feeling, transporting you to the spirited streets of New Orleans or a warm Southern Sunday dinner. From our slow-cooked specialties to our lively grill selections, we invite you to taste the tradition, feel the soul, and make lasting memories with us.
