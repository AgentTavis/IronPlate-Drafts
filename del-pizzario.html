Our Story

Crafting Culinary Delights in Del Rio

Nestled in the heart of Del Rio, Del PizzaRio has become a beloved local gem, renowned for its commitment to exceptional flavors and a warm, inviting atmosphere. We believe in the power of great food to bring people together, and every dish we serve is a testament to our passion.

Since opening our doors, we've delighted patrons with creative toppings, perfectly baked crusts, and an unwavering dedication to quality ingredients. Our reputation for exceptional pizza, mouth-watering calzones, and crispy wings has made us a community cornerstone, earning us a stellar 4.5 out of 5 rating on Tripadvisor and the #1 spot among Del Rio restaurants.
