Our Legacy

Generations of Flavor, Decades of Memories.

The Jolly Roger isn't just a restaurant; it's a Kill Devil Hills institution. As the oldest Italian restaurant on the Outer Banks, we've been serving up hearty meals and unforgettable experiences for generations.

From our fresh, local seafood dishes that capture the taste of the Atlantic, to our classic Italian pastas and robust steaks, every plate tells a story of tradition and passion.

When the sun goes down, our stage lights up with the beach's only NIGHTLY, award-winning karaoke, turning every dinner into a party and every evening into a cherished memory. Join us for a meal, stay for the fun, and become part of our story.

Why Dine With Kill Devil Hills' Original?

Outer Banks Original

As the oldest Italian restaurant in Kill Devil Hills, we're deeply rooted in the community, offering a rich history and a dining experience built on decades of tradition and quality. Not just a meal, but a piece of local history.

Unforgettable Entertainment
