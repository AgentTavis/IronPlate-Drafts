Our Heritage

Our Family. Your Table. Unforgettable Seafood.

Welcome to Chase & Charli, a true family-owned and operated gem nestled in the heart of Mount Airy, North Carolina. For years, our passion for delicious food and warm hospitality has driven us to create a dining experience where every guest feels like an extension of our family.

We're proud to serve our community, bringing you a diverse menu from classic American comfort food to the freshest seafood, all crafted with care and homemade goodness. Our commitment to quality and a welcoming atmosphere has earned us a 4.5-star rating from over 700 delighted customers, making us a beloved local favorite.

Join us and discover why Chase & Charli isn't just a restaurant, but a place where memories are made, one delicious meal at a time.
