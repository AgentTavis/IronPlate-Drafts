Our Heritage

A Taste of Family, From Our Kitchen to Your Table

At Ralph's NY Italian, we believe that great food brings people together. From the moment you hit the door, we treat you like friends, not just customers. Our passion for authentic New York Italian cuisine is poured into every dish, creating flavors that transport you straight to the heart of Italy. We're proud to serve Edenton with dishes made with love and tradition.

While our cozy space seats 25, we also boast a very robust take-out business, greeting everyone with appreciation. Whether you're dining in or taking home, expect nothing less than genuine hospitality and unforgettable Italian flavors.

Our Offerings

Handcrafted Italian Classics & New York Favorites

Large Cheese Pizza

Our classic New York style pizza, generously topped with mozzarella and rich tomato sauce. Perfect for sharing (or not!).

Special Pizzas

Explore our rotating selection of unique and inventive pizza creations, featuring fresh, seasonal ingredients and bold flavors.

Strombolis

A true New York specialty! Our Strombolis are stuffed sandwiches made with the same great pizza dough, packed with savory fillings.

Convenient and delicious, our cold pastas are prepared fresh daily, ready for you to heat and enjoy at home. Perfect for a quick, authentic Italian meal.
