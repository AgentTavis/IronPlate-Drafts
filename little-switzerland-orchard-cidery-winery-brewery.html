Our Journey

From a Whispered Secret to a Mountain Destination.

Nestled in the heart of Spruce Pine, NC, Little Switzerland Orchard, Cidery, Winery & Brewery began as a local secret, whispered among those who discovered our unique mountain haven. Our journey is one of passion, dedicated to crafting exceptional ciders, wines, and brews. Visitors often rave, much like the server who called our cider "the best she's ever had," about our vibrant, eclectic atmosphere.

Here, every sip tells a story, from our legendary Wango da Mango DIABLO to our rotating seasonal delights. Beyond the taproom, discover awesome live music, a beautiful shaded outdoor space, and even full hook-up & rustic campsites, making us a true destination for flavor, fun, and relaxation. We're proud to be a mom & pop establishment, inviting you to experience the magic of Little Switzerland.
