Our Journey

Serving Heartfelt Meals & Memories for Decades

Sunshine Family Restaurant is more than just a place to eat; it's a cornerstone of the Kannapolis community. For over two decades, we've been dedicated to providing a warm, welcoming atmosphere where families and friends can gather to enjoy delicious, homemade meals.

Our commitment to classic comfort food and genuine Southern hospitality has made us a beloved local spot. We believe in the power of a good meal to bring people together, and every dish is prepared with care, using fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes.

From early morning breakfasts to hearty lunches and comforting dinners, we strive to make every visit feel like coming home. Thank you for making Sunshine Family Restaurant your favorite local gathering place!
