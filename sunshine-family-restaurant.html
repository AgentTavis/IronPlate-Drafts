Our Roots

Serving Smiles & Sizzle in Kannapolis Since Day One.

Sunshine Family Restaurant is a beloved local spot in Kannapolis, NC. For years, we've been serving up classic American comfort food with a smile, creating a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels like family. Our commitment to fresh ingredients and friendly service makes us more than just a place to eat – it's a community hub where neighbors gather, stories are shared, and delicious memories are made.

We believe in simple, honest food done right. Every dish is prepared with care, ensuring a consistent quality that our regulars have come to love. From our hearty breakfasts to our satisfying lunch and dinner options, we're dedicated to providing a taste of home in every bite.
