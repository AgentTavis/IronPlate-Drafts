OUR STORY

A Taste of Italy, Crafted with Passion.

Arturo’s Trattoria is the diners’ choice for authentic Italian cuisine in the Baltimore and BWI Airport area, offering fresh-daily seafood, prime cuts of meat, homemade pasta, and seasonal local produce.

Our cozy, yet elegant, atmosphere is ideal for romantic occasions, business meetings, and private events, making Arturo’s Trattoria a true culinary oasis near BWI. We believe in honoring traditional Italian recipes while infusing them with the freshest local ingredients, creating a dining experience that transports you straight to the heart of Italy.
