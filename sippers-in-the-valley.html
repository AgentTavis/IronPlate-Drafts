Our Story & Commitment

More Than Just A Coffee Shop – It's Home.

At Sippers In The Valley, we've poured our heart and soul into creating more than just a place to eat and drink; we've built a community hub. We're proud to be Maggie Valley's pivotal Mom and Pop coffee shop, reminiscent of "Cheers," where everyone truly knows your name.

From our amazing coffee to our delightful bar & grill offerings, every item is crafted with care and a commitment to quality. We believe in genuine hospitality and a welcoming atmosphere that makes you feel right at home, whether you're a local or just passing through.

And because we're so confident in our brew, we offer a $ Back guarantee on every cup ☕. Come experience the warmth, the flavors, and the friendly faces that make Sippers In The Valley a truly special place.
