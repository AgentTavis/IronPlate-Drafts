Our Legacy

Four Decades of Southern Flavor in Rockingham

Established in 1980, Hudson Brothers Deli has been a cornerstone of Rockingham, North Carolina, for over 40 years. We've dedicated ourselves to serving a diverse menu that truly embodies the warmth and richness of homestyle Southern cooking.

From our humble beginnings, we've grown into a beloved local spot where families gather, friends connect, and everyone feels at home. Our commitment to quality ingredients, time-honored recipes, and a friendly atmosphere is what keeps our community coming back, generation after generation.

"Hudson Brothers isn't just a restaurant; it's a Rockingham institution. Their commitment to classic Southern comfort food and that welcoming diner feel is truly unmatched. You can taste the history in every bite!"

Pulled Pork BBQ Sub

Loaded Baked Spud

Our Promise

More Than Just a Meal: The Hudson Brothers Difference

Authentic Southern Flavors

From our homestyle fried chicken to our creamy mac & cheese, every dish is crafted with generations-old recipes, bringing you the true taste of the South right here in Rockingham.

Family-Owned Since 1980

For over 40 years, Hudson Brothers Deli has been a family passion. We pour our heart and soul into every meal, ensuring a welcoming atmosphere and consistent quality that only decades of dedication can provide.

Your Go-To for Subs & Spuds

While our Southern cooking is legendary, we're equally famous for our loaded subs and incredible baked spuds. Whether it's a Pulled Pork BBQ Sub or a fully Loaded Baked Spud, we have your craving covered.
