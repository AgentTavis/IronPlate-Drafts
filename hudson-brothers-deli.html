Our Legacy

Four Decades of Homestyle Flavor & Community Spirit

Established in 1980, Hudson Brothers Deli has been a cornerstone of the Rockingham, North Carolina community for over 40 years. We pride ourselves on serving a diverse menu that caters to every palate, with a special emphasis on the comforting, hearty flavors of homestyle Southern cooking.

More than just a restaurant, we're a place where friends and family gather, where new memories are made, and where every meal feels like coming home. Our dedication to quality ingredients and genuine hospitality has made us a beloved local favorite.
