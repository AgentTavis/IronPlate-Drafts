Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's an Experience.

Welcome to Railroad Cafe, a true gem in the heart of Eden, NC. Since opening our doors, we've been serving up classic diner fare with a side of genuine hospitality. We're proud to offer really good food at very reasonable prices, all within an inviting atmosphere that harks back to simpler times.

Our friendly staff greets every customer like family, ensuring your visit is always a pleasant one. Step into our "old timey soda shop" feel, where comfort food reigns supreme and memories are made. While we cherish our traditional "cash-only" approach, we promise every stop here is definitely worth it!
