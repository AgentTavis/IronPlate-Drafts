Our Journey

Taste the Tradition, Feel the Warmth

Welcome to TEX-MEX RIVERSIDE, where every dish tells a story of authentic flavors and a passion for Mexican cuisine. Nestled in the heart of Sparta, NC, we're proud to be a beloved local spot offering delicious Mexican fare in a truly casual and inviting riverside setting.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to bring the vibrant tastes of Mexico to our community, prepared with fresh ingredients and served with a smile. We believe in generous portions, ensuring every guest leaves feeling satisfied and eager to return.

From sizzling burritos to cheesy quesadillas and unique Cuban-inspired dishes, our menu is crafted to delight every palate. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a gathering place where friends and family can share laughter, good food, and unforgettable memories.

"At Tex-Mex Riverside, we pour our heart into every recipe. It's about more than just food; it's about sharing a piece of our culture, creating joy, and building a community around the table. That's the secret ingredient."

— The TEX-MEX RIVERSIDE Family
