Our Legacy

More Than Just a Pub, It's a Community Hub.

Nestled in the heart of downtown Winston-Salem at 620 N Trade St, Finnigan's Wake has long been a beloved cornerstone of the community. We're proud to be your go-to Irish Pub & Kitchen, a place where friends gather, stories are shared, and every meal feels like a homecoming.

From the moment you step through our doors, you'll feel the authentic warmth of a true Irish pub combined with the vibrant energy of downtown. We believe in good food, great drinks, and even better company – a tradition we've upheld for years, earning us a stellar 4.6-star rating across over 1700 reviews.
