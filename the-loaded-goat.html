Our Heritage

Serving Mt. Airy with Passion and Flavor

Nestled in the heart of Mt. Airy, The Loaded Goat has become a beloved local institution. We believe in the simple joy of good food, great drinks, and genuine hospitality. Our doors are always open, welcoming friends, families, and newcomers alike to experience a taste of our dedication.

For years, we've been committed to crafting memorable dining experiences, offering a diverse menu that satisfies every craving and a beverage selection that's second to none. More than just a bar and grill, The Loaded Goat is a community hub where laughter is shared, stories are told, and every guest feels like family.
