Our Journey

The Story Behind Your Favorite Fries.

From a "Fry Snob" to a Spud King!

At Spud Kings Foodtruck, our passion is simple: crafting the most delicious, perfectly cooked fries you've ever tasted. It all started with a love for fries and a keen eye for perfection – a self-proclaimed "fry snob" on a mission to bring superior spuds to Apex, NC.

We believe fries aren't just a side; they're an experience. That's why we're dedicated to fresh-cut excellence, generous portions, and flavors that balance perfectly. Beyond our signature fries, we extend that same dedication to every item on our menu, from juicy wings to savory hoagies. We're a local, mom-and-pop operation committed to serving up joy, one perfectly seasoned fry at a time.

"I love fries and I'm a bit of a "fry snob." I've tried the bacon ranch fries from Spud Kings twice and they are sooo delicious. The flavor is balanced and the fries are cooked perfectly. The platter is worth the price because it's a heaping portion and is enough to share. If you see them around, then you should definitely check them out. You will not be disappointed!!!"

- A Happy Fry Snob

Our Offerings

Unforgettable Flavors, Heaping Portions.

Fresh Cut Fries

Our classic, perfectly golden, crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside, hand-cut fries. The foundation of the Spud Kings experience.

Loaded Fries

Dive into a world of flavor with our generously topped loaded fries. Featuring crowd favorites like Bacon Ranch and Philly Cheesesteak!

Wings

Juicy, tender chicken wings tossed in your choice of delicious sauces. A perfect companion to our fries or a meal on its own.

Chicken Tenders

Crispy, golden-brown chicken tenders, seasoned to perfection and served with your favorite dipping sauce.

Hot Dogs

Classic American hot dogs, grilled to perfection and served on a soft bun with your preferred toppings.

Hoagies

Hearty and satisfying hoagies, packed with fresh ingredients and bursting with flavor. A meal designed to fill you up!
