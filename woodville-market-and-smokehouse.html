Our Heritage

Taste the Tradition, Feel the Family

At Woodville Market and Smokehouse, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a cornerstone of the Lewiston Woodville community. Located proudly at 102 Oak Dr, we've dedicated ourselves to bringing authentic, slow-smoked BBQ and a warm, welcoming experience to our neighbors.

Our passion lies in making every meal special, whether it's our famous daily specials or making your holiday feast easy and unforgettable. We believe in good food, good company, and the joy of sharing a meal made with care. We're open Wednesday through Sunday, ready to serve you the best.

Come on in and taste the difference that genuine passion makes. From our family to yours, we're here to serve up delicious memories, one plate at a time.
