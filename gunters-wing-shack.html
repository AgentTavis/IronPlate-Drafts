Our Heritage

Harmony's Home for Hearty Comfort Food

Located in the heart of Harmony, NC, Gunter's Wing Shack isn't just a restaurant—it's a community hub where good food and great times come together. We've been serving up classic bar and grill favorites with a local touch, crafting every dish to bring a smile to your face.

From our famously diverse selection of wings to our satisfyingly hearty salads and comforting pizzas, we believe in generous portions and unbeatable value. We're more than just a place to eat; we're where neighbors become friends over a cold drink and the best barbecue sandwich in town.
