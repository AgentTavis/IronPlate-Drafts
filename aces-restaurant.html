Our Journey

A New Chapter for Yadkinville's Beloved Comfort Food Spot.

Sadly, one of the long-running food destinations in Yadkin County recently closed its doors. Although it was a true hole-in-the-wall, they had some of the best home cooking around, serving up delicious comfort food that captured the heart of our community.

Now, Ace's Restaurant is proud to continue that cherished legacy, bringing back the heartwarming, familiar flavors you remember, with a fresh new spirit right here at 225 E Main St. We're more than just a place to grab a meal; we're a gathering spot for friends, family, and the delicious tastes of home. Join us as we build new memories while honoring the traditions that make Yadkinville special.
