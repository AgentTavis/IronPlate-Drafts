Our Journey

Continuing a Legacy of Comfort in Yadkinville

Sadly, one of the long-running food destinations in Yadkin County recently closed its doors. Although it was a true "hole-in-the-wall," it was cherished for some of the best home-cooked meals around.

Now, Ace's Restaurant proudly carries on that legacy, bringing you delicious, made-from-scratch breakfast comfort food right here in Yadkinville. We believe in good coffee, great company, and even better food to start your day right. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a place where friends and family gather, where new memories are made, and where the aroma of a hearty breakfast fills the air every morning.

"Ace's truly honors the spirit of Yadkinville's classic eateries. Their dedication to genuine comfort food and friendly service makes every visit feel like coming home. A fantastic addition to our community!"

— A Loyal Local

Our Morning Delights

Delicious Breakfast Comfort Food

Ace's Classic Breakfast Plate

Two eggs cooked your way, crispy bacon or savory sausage, golden hash browns, and a choice of toast or biscuits.

Fluffy Buttermilk Pancakes

A stack of three light and fluffy pancakes, served with warm maple syrup and butter.

Hearty Breakfast Burrito

Scrambled eggs, cheese, your choice of bacon or sausage, and a hint of salsa, all wrapped in a warm tortilla.

Southern Biscuits & Gravy
