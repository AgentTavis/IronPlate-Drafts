Our Story

A Taste of OBX Tradition, Served with a Smile.

For generations, Art's Place has been a beloved fixture on the Outer Banks, serving up hearty diner classics with a side of true Southern hospitality. Located right on the Beach Road at MP2.5 in Kitty Hawk, we're the spot where locals gather and vacationers discover their new favorite breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

From our legendary French Toast to our famous Pimento Burger, every dish is made with fresh ingredients and a whole lot of love. Come experience the warmth and flavors that make Art's Place a true OBX tradition.

Popular Dishes & Diner Classics

French Toast

Fluffy, golden perfection, dusted with powdered sugar. The perfect start to your OBX day.

Pimento Burger

Bacon Cheeseburger

A timeless classic: savory bacon, melted cheese, and a perfectly cooked patty.

Breakfast Sandwich

Eggs, cheese, and your choice of bacon, sausage, or ham on a toasted roll.

Crab Cake Sandwich
