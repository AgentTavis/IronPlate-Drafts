Our Story

Two Decades of Sweet Traditions

At Marcella's Sweetery, we believe every day deserves a little sweetness. For over twenty years, our home-based custom bake shop has been lovingly crafting delightful treats for the King, NC community. What started as a passion for baking has grown into a beloved local favorite, known for our dedication to quality ingredients, time-honored recipes, and a personal touch in every single creation.

From our classic Artisan Cinnamon Rolls to bespoke celebration cakes, we pour our heart into bringing joy to your palate. Come experience the warmth and charm of Marcella's – where every bite feels like home.
