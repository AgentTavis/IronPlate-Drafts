Our Roots

More Than Just a Bar, It's Apex's Heartbeat.

Welcome to HighCraft Downtown Apex, a true local watering hole nestled in the vibrant heart of Apex, NC. Since opening our doors, our mission has been simple: to create a welcoming, lively space where friends, neighbors, and visitors can gather to enjoy exceptional craft beverages and unwind.

We're proud to be the establishment in the city that's open the latest, offering a perfect spot to extend your evening. While our food options are designed for delicious snacking, our true passion lies in our myriad beer and cider selections. With constantly rotating taps and a thoughtfully curated wine list, there’s always something new and exciting to discover.

Beyond the brews, HighCraft is a community hub. We regularly host exciting events and popular food trucks, transforming our space into a lively gathering point. Whether you're a local seeking your new favorite pint or just passing through Apex looking for a quality place to relax, HighCraft offers a genuine, unpretentious experience that truly celebrates local spirit.
