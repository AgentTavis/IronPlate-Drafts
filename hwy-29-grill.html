Our Heritage

Where Flavor Meets Family in Kings Mountain.

Discover Hwy 29 Grill, a cherished local spot in Kings Mountain, NC, where delicious American cuisine is served with a side of genuine Southern hospitality. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a gathering place for friends and family to enjoy hearty meals and make lasting memories.

Since opening our doors, we've been committed to crafting dishes that satisfy every craving, from our legendary "good old fashioned burgers" to a delightful selection from our grill and seafood offerings. We believe in great food, warm service, and a welcoming atmosphere that feels just like home.
