Our Story

More Than Just a Meal: It's a Goldsboro Gathering Place.

At Three65 Bar & Grill, we believe in great food, good company, and creating memories that last all year long. Our journey began with a simple passion: crafting the most delicious smash burgers Goldsboro has ever tasted. We're a local spot where everyone feels like family, whether you're catching up with friends or celebrating a special occasion.

We've poured our hearts into making Three65 a place where the food is always delicious, the atmosphere is always welcoming, and "the ole' man is finally feeling better" thanks to the joy of serving our community. From casual weeknight dinners to milestone birthdays – like celebrating Casey's 30th right here with us – we're honored to be a part of your moments.
