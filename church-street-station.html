Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Memory.

At Church Street Station, we believe in the simple joy of a good meal, prepared with care and served with a smile. Nestled in the heart of Eden, NC, we've been a local cornerstone, offering a menu brimming with home-cooked favorites.

Every dish, from our hearty breakfast specials to our daily prepared desserts, is crafted to bring you comfort and satisfaction. We pride ourselves on friendly, attentive staff and food that's always hot and tastes just right. Come on down and experience the warmth of a true neighborhood diner.
