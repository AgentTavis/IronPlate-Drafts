Our Journey

Crafting Joy, One Sweet Bite at a Time

At Sweet Tooth Bakery, we believe in the simple magic of fresh ingredients and heartfelt baking. Born from a passion for bringing smiles to Roxboro, our bakery has quickly become a beloved local gem. We're more than just a place to grab a treat; we're a hub where community gathers, and every item is made with the warmth and care of a home kitchen.

From our legendary doughnuts that draw lines before dawn to our custom-crafted cakes for life's special moments, we pour our love into every recipe. We're proud to be a family-owned business, dedicated to quality, flavor, and creating memories for you and your loved ones. Come experience the taste of true craftsmanship and become part of the Sweet Tooth family!
