Our Story

Our Passion, Baked Just For You.

At Piggypies Bakeshop, we believe in the magic of small batches and the warmth of a freshly baked treat. Nestled in the heart of Whiteville, NC, we handcraft exquisite cookies, decadent brownies, delicate mini cheesecakes, vibrant french macarons, and a variety of other delightful pastries.

Every item is made with love, the finest ingredients, and a dedication to bringing joy to your day. It's more than just a bakery; it's a taste of home, a place where every bite tells a story of passion and tradition. We're proud to be a local favorite, serving our community with deliciousness one treat at a time.

"Piggypies isn't just a bakery, it's a staple! The quality and care in every treat really shines through. A true Whiteville gem."

— A Loyal Customer
