OUR STORY

More Than Just a Meal: A Wilson Transformation

The Cavalier isn't just a bar and grill; it's a piece of Wilson's history reimagined. What once served as an Exxon station on Raleigh Road Pkwy N has been lovingly transformed into a vibrant community hub.

We're proud to bring new life to this iconic location, perfectly situated to capture the energy from the new ballpark. We believe in crafting delicious food, pouring refreshing drinks, and creating a welcoming atmosphere where friends and family can gather, make memories, and celebrate the spirit of Wilson.

"The Cavalier has truly become a cornerstone of our community. The atmosphere is fantastic, and it's amazing to see an old landmark brought back to life with such style!"

- A Satisfied Local
