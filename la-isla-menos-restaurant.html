OUR STORY

Taste the Heart of Puerto Rico, Right Here in Fayetteville.

At La Isla Menos Restaurant, we're more than just a place to eat; we're a vibrant slice of Puerto Rico brought to the heart of Fayetteville, NC. Since opening our doors, our mission has been to transport you to the 'Isla del Encanto' with every single dish.

We invite you to step in, unwind, and savor the authentic flavors and warm hospitality that define our beloved island. Come and enjoy a true taste of home, far from home, where every meal is prepared with passion and served with a smile. ¡Buen provecho!
