Our Heritage

The Story Behind Tarboro's Favorite Smoke

At Myrick's BBQ, our story is slow-smoked, just like our barbecue. Born from a passion for authentic Eastern NC 'cue, we've been serving the Tarboro community and beyond for years, bringing together families and friends over plates piled high with tender, smoky goodness.

Nestled right here at 1415 N Main St, Tarboro, NC 27886, we're more than just a restaurant – we're a tradition. Our journey began with family recipes, perfected over generations, using only the freshest local ingredients and a dedication to the craft of barbecue that you can taste in every bite.

We believe in honest food, genuine hospitality, and the kind of flavor that makes you close your eyes and savor the moment. Come experience the warmth and taste that makes Myrick's BBQ a true Tarboro staple.

Why Myrick's?

Our roots run deep in Eastern North Carolina barbecue tradition. We use time-honored smoking techniques and secret family recipes passed down through generations to deliver that unmistakable, tangy, and smoky taste you crave.
