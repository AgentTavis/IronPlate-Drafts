Our Heritage

More Than Just BBQ, It's a Tarboro Tradition.

Nestled right here at 1415 N Main St in Tarboro, Myrick's BBQ isn't just a place to eat; it's a cornerstone of our community. We believe in the timeless art of slow-smoked barbecue, crafted with passion and served with a smile. From the moment you step up to our pit, you're not just a customer, you're family.

We've poured our hearts into perfecting every rub, every sauce, and every tender cut of meat. Our commitment is to bring you the authentic taste of North Carolina BBQ, a flavor that speaks of tradition, good times, and honest, hardworking hands. Come experience the taste that keeps folks coming back week after week.
