Our Heritage

From Our Family to Your Table

Thompson Seafood, nestled in the heart of Deep Gap, NC, isn't just a restaurant; it's a culinary tradition. We've dedicated ourselves to offering a wide selection of fresh, delectable seafood that truly satisfies. With a focus on quality ingredients and a variety that caters to all tastes, we've become a beloved local favorite, known for our generous portions and a delightful mix of Southern comfort food classics alongside our specialty: authentic Texas wild-caught shrimp.

Every dish we serve is prepared with passion, bringing you the authentic flavors of the coast combined with the warmth of country cooking. We believe in wholesome meals that bring families and friends together, creating lasting memories around delicious food.
