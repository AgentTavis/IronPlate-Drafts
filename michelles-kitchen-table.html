Our Roots

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Memory.

At Michelle's Kitchen & Table, we believe in the simple joy of good food, great company, and a welcoming atmosphere. Nestled in the heart of Burlington, we've been serving up classic American Bar & Grill fare with a local touch for over a decade. Our passion is crafting dishes that satisfy your cravings and create moments worth savoring.

From sizzling burgers made with premium local beef to our crispy, flavor-packed wings and refreshing craft beers, every item on our menu is prepared with care and quality ingredients. Michelle's is more than just a restaurant; it’s a community hub where friends catch up, families celebrate, and everyone feels right at home.

"Michelle's is my go-to spot in Burlington. The food is consistently amazing, and the vibe is always so friendly and inviting. You truly feel like part of the family here!"

Our Offerings

Taste the Craft Behind Every Dish.

Burlington BBQ Burger

Juicy beef patty, smoked bacon, cheddar, crispy onions, and our signature tangy BBQ sauce.

Crispy Buffalo Wings

Perfectly fried wings tossed in your choice of mild, medium, or hot buffalo sauce, served with ranch or blue cheese.

Signature Mac & Cheese

Creamy, rich macaroni and cheese baked to perfection with a golden, crunchy topping.

Grilled Chicken Sandwich

Marinated grilled chicken breast, fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, and a zesty aioli on a toasted brioche bun.

House Salad with Grilled Steak

Mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and tender grilled steak strips with your choice of dressing.

Sweet Potato Fries

Crispy sweet potato fries, lightly seasoned, served with a side of honey mustard dipping sauce.

Local Craft Beer Selection

Rotating selection of the best local and regional craft beers on tap, ask your server for today's choices.

Classic Chili Bowl

Hearty beef chili, slow-cooked with beans and spices, topped with shredded cheddar cheese and sour cream.
