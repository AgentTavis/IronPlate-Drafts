Our Legacy

A Culinary Journey Rooted in Excellence

Welcome to The Guesthouse, an extension of the beloved Choplin's tradition. While Choplin's may be tucked away in a small strip shopping center off Catawba Blvd. in Cornelius, NC, The Guesthouse brings the same dedication to quality and service to Huntersville.

Our chef/owner, Chef West, along with our experienced manager, brings extensive culinary expertise honed over decades. We believe in letting the quality of our ingredients speak for themselves, crafting each dish with precision and passion.

It's more than just a meal; it's an experience built on a foundation of local connection, fresh ingredients, and a commitment to making every visit memorable. Come discover why we're quickly becoming a Huntersville favorite.

"The Guesthouse truly lives up to its name – it feels like being welcomed into a home where every dish is prepared with love and expertise. The atmosphere is just as perfect as their steak!" — Emily R., Regular Guest
