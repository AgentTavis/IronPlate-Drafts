Our Story

More Than Just a Cafe, It's a Community Hub.

Nestled in the heart of Carolina Beach, Crush and Grind began with a simple dream: to create a welcoming space where locals and visitors alike could gather, relax, and savor life's simple pleasures. From the first pour of our craft coffee in the early morning to the last clink of glasses filled with local brews and expertly mixed cocktails, we're dedicated to serving our community with warmth and a smile.

We believe in quality ingredients, a cozy atmosphere, and the kind of attentive service that makes you feel right at home. We're proud to be a place where conversations flow as freely as our coffee, and every visit feels like coming home. Come on in, pull up a chair, and let us make your day a little brighter.

"Crush and Grind truly embodies the spirit of Carolina Beach. Every time I visit, I'm met with friendly faces and fantastic service. It's my go-to spot for everything from morning coffee to an afternoon brew."

— A Dedicated Regular
