Our Heritage

A Taste of Southern Hospitality Since [Year, if known - Using "Generations" for now]

Nestled in the heart of Pilot Mountain, NC, Cousin Gary's Family Restaurant has been a beloved fixture, serving up comforting Southern hospitality and unforgettable meals for generations.

Our reputation for outstanding ground steak sandwiches precedes us — a dish so celebrated, it's been featured in state magazines as a must-try culinary experience unique to Surry County.

We pride ourselves on creating a warm, inviting atmosphere where every guest feels like family. From our friendly team, like Lindsey who goes the extra mile to make your visit special, to our daily bountiful buffet brimming with home-cooked classics, we ensure there's always something to delight every palate. At Cousin Gary's, it's more than just a meal; it's a tradition, a taste of home, and a place where cherished memories are made.
