OUR ROOTS

Where Community Gathers, One Flavorful Moment at a Time

At Mariposa, we believe in the magic of simple pleasures and shared experiences. Nestled in the heart of Oriental, NC, we’re more than just a bakery and wine bar – we're a vibrant community gathering place where the aroma of freshly baked goods mingles with the clinking of glasses and the buzz of friendly conversation.

Inspired by the graceful Mariposa (butterfly), our journey began with a vision to create a space that offers delightful transformation: from morning coffee and artisan pastries to evening grilled specialties and a thoughtfully curated wine selection. We're proud to bring you handcrafted quality, warm hospitality, and a taste of local charm that keeps you coming back.
