Our Journey

More Than Just Meals: A Family's Passion.

At Los Toritos, we believe that great food brings people together. As a family-owned restaurant in the heart of Advance, NC, we've dedicated ourselves to bringing the vibrant, authentic flavors of Mexico right to your table. Every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and a touch of homemade love, passed down through generations.

From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a customer; you're family. We strive to create a warm, inviting atmosphere where memories are made over plates of delicious tacos, sizzling fajitas, and our celebrated appetizers. Join us and taste the tradition!
