The Story Behind the Smoke

While Salisbury boasts a vibrant culinary scene, with established favorites like The Smoke Pit, Sweet Meadow Cafe, and Mambo Grill, EverythingEats24/7 is here to redefine your BBQ experience. We're proud to bring a fresh, authentic taste to the heart of North Carolina, committed to earning our place among the most beloved restaurants in Salisbury.

Our journey began with a simple passion: crafting barbecue that truly stands out. Every cut of meat is slow-smoked to perfection, infused with deep, rich flavors that tell a story. It's not just food; it's a labor of love, a tribute to true Southern tradition, and a promise of an unforgettable meal.
