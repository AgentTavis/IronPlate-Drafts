Our Story

Generations of Warmth, Flavor, and Friendship.

For generations, Berry Patch Market & Grill has been more than just a convenience store and grill; it's a cherished landmark in Lawsonville, NC. We're a place where neighbors gather, travelers refuel, and everyone feels like family.

Whether you're making your weekly trip to Performance Feed and need a hearty breakfast to start your day – like our famous tenderloin, cheese, and egg on a biscuit – or dropping by for a delicious lunch, we pride ourselves on friendly service and fresh, satisfying food. We even have a cozy dining space where you can relax and enjoy your meal, just as many have done since 7:30 AM.

At Berry Patch, every visit is a taste of home. We're committed to serving our community with quality products and a welcoming smile, making sure you leave happier and fuller than when you arrived.

"Berry Patch is more than a stop, it's a destination. Their tenderloin, cheese, and egg biscuit is legendary, and the friendly faces always make my day."

— A Lawsonville Local
