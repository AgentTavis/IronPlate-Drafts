Our Story

More Than Just a Coffee Shop, It's Home.

At Carolina Coffee House, we believe in the simple pleasures: a perfectly brewed cup of coffee, a delightful boba tea, and a freshly prepared snack, all served with genuine Southern hospitality. We're proud to be Waynesville's go-to homestyle spot, where every visit feels like coming home. Our dedication to quick, professional service ensures you get your favorites made with care, just the way you like them.

Since opening our doors, our mission has been to create a welcoming atmosphere where locals and visitors alike can relax, connect, and enjoy quality food and beverages. We're a small, family-focused diner committed to serving our community with a smile, one cup at a time.
