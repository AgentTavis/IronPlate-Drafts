Our Heritage

A Taste of Thailand, Crafted with Love in Elkin

At Prime Thai, we believe in bringing the true essence of Thai cuisine to the heart of Elkin, NC. Our journey began in 2009, born from a passion for authentic flavors and a desire to share our family's culinary traditions. Every dish is a testament to the rich heritage of Thailand, prepared with fresh, high-quality ingredients and time-honored techniques.

We are a family-owned establishment, and when you dine with us, you become part of our extended family. From the moment you step through our doors, you're not just a customer, but a guest invited to experience the warmth and hospitality that define Thai culture. Our commitment to authentic taste and genuine service has made us a beloved fixture in North Carolina's wine country.

"Prime Thai offers truly authentic Thai cuisine, serving a variety of dishes that will transport your taste buds to the streets of Thailand."
