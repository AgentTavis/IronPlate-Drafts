Our Story

A Taste of Thailand, Crafted with Love in Elkin.

At Prime Thai, we believe in sharing the rich culinary heritage of Thailand with our beloved community in Elkin, NC. Since opening our doors in 2009, our family-owned restaurant has been a labor of love, dedicated to offering truly authentic Thai cuisine.

Nestled in the heart of North Carolina's picturesque Wine Country, we pride ourselves on dishes that transport your taste buds directly to the bustling streets and serene landscapes of Thailand. Each recipe is passed down through generations, ensuring every bite carries the essence of tradition and the warmth of home.

We source the freshest ingredients, combining aromatic herbs and spices with the finest local produce to create a dining experience that is both sophisticated and deeply comforting. Come join our family at Prime Thai and discover why we've been Elkin's favorite for over a decade.
