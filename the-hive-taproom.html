Our Story

More Than Just a Drink, It's a Lifestyle.

So… what does Swaggle mean? It’s the rhythm of life, the swing in your step, that laugh that bubbles up when you’re surrounded by good friends. It’s about celebrating life's simple pleasures, those moments of genuine connection and joy.

And yes—it’s also inspired by the honeybee’s waggle dance, the intricate move they bust out to share where the sweetest nectar can be found. If bees dance for food and drink, we figured we should Swaggle to celebrate the best drink. Our session mead is crafted with this spirit in mind, bringing you a drink that's as natural and vibrant as life itself, made with 100% raw honey and clean ingredients, because you deserve to feel the difference.

"The Hive Taproom isn't just a place for great mead; it's a hub for good vibes and even better company. You truly feel the passion behind their craft!"
— A Happy Local from East Troy, NC
