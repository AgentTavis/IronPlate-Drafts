Our Story

From Our Family Kitchen to Your Table: A Taste of Mexico

Taqueria La Monarca is more than just a restaurant; it's a celebration of authentic Mexican flavors, right here in the heart of Boone, NC. We specialize in serving traditional dishes, crafted with love and the freshest ingredients, just like abuela used to make.

Our passion is to bring you an unforgettable culinary journey, featuring vibrant spices, tender meats, and the true essence of Mexican street food and family recipes. Every bite tells a story of heritage and passion.
