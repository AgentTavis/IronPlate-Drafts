Our Story

Taste the Heart of Rockingham

Located in the heart of Rockingham, NC, Number 1 Diner serves up a delightful array of freshly cooked country entrees alongside an enticing selection of classic American diner fare. From our hearty breakfast items to our renowned burger menu, every dish is prepared with care, ensuring a memorable and satisfying experience.

We're more than just a diner; we're a community gathering place where friends and family come together to enjoy good food and friendly faces. We believe in comfort food made with honest ingredients and a dash of Southern hospitality. Come taste why we're Rockingham's favorite local spot for a truly authentic dining experience.
