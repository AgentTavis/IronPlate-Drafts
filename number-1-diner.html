Our Story

A Taste of Home in Rockingham

Welcome to Number 1 Diner – Rockingham's Beloved Spot for Heartfelt Meals.

Located in the heart of Rockingham, NC, #1 Diner serves up a delightful array of freshly cooked country entrees alongside an enticing selection of classic American diner fare. From our hearty breakfast items to our mouth-watering burgers and sandwiches, every dish is crafted with care and a touch of Southern hospitality.

We're more than just a place to eat; we're a community hub where friends and family gather for good food and great company. Our commitment to fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes ensures a memorable dining experience every time you walk through our doors.
