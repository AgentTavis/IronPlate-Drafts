Our Story

Where Every Meal Feels Like Coming Home.

Located in the heart of Aulander, NC, Breaking Bread Family Kitchen is a cozy establishment that prides itself on delivering delicious, home-cooked food. We're a small, family-run business, deeply rooted in the community, and dedicated to serving meals made with love and exceptional quality.

From our kitchen to your table, we pour our heart into every dish, ensuring a warm, welcoming experience that feels just like gathering around your own family table. Join us and become part of our story.
