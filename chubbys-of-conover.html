Taste the Tradition

At Chubby's of Conover, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a local landmark bringing the community together with hearty meals. For years, we've been crafting delicious BBQ, subs, and 'such' with recipes passed down through generations, ensuring every bite is packed with the comfort and flavor you crave.

We pride ourselves on serving fresh, made-to-order meals and a friendly atmosphere where everyone feels like family. From our slow-smoked BBQ to our generously loaded subs, we're dedicated to quality and consistency that keeps our customers coming back week after week.

Savor the Flavors: Our Menu Highlights

Smoked Pulled Pork BBQ

Tender, slow-smoked pork shoulder, hand-pulled and seasoned to perfection. A true Carolina classic.

Classic Chubby's Sub

Our signature sub, loaded with fresh deli meats, crisp veggies, and our secret blend of oil and vinegar.

BBQ Chicken Pizza

A unique combination of tangy BBQ sauce, grilled chicken, red onion, and mozzarella on a crispy crust.

Philly Cheesesteak Sub

Thinly sliced steak, sautéed onions, peppers, and melted provolone cheese, served on a toasted roll.

Grilled Chicken Sandwich

Homestyle Sides
