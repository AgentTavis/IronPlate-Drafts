Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Hollar's Experience.

Nestled in the heart of Taylorsville, Hollar's Backstreet Grill is your go-to spot for delicious, no-frills comfort food and a warm, welcoming atmosphere. We're proud to be a small-town grill with a big heart, serving up classic American favorites that hit just right every time.

From the biggest and best burgers around to our tender BBQ, every dish is prepared with care, using quality ingredients to bring you that homemade taste you crave. We believe in good food, good company, and making every visitor feel like family. Come on down and experience the local charm that keeps our neighbors coming back for more.
