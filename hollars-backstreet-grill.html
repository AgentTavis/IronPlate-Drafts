Our Journey

More Than Just Food, It's Family.

Nestled on Linneys Mountain Road, Hollar's Backstreet Grill began with a simple dream: to create a welcoming spot where friends and family could gather over delicious, hearty comfort food. We're proud to be a small-town grill with a big heart, serving up Taylorsville's "biggest and best burger joint" experience for years.

Our commitment is simple: good food, friendly faces, and a place that feels like home. From our sizzling burgers to our slow-cooked BBQ, every dish is prepared with care, ensuring a taste of tradition in every bite. Come on down and become part of our story.
