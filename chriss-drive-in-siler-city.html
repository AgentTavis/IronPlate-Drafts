Our Story

More Than Just a Meal: It's Siler City Tradition.

Welcome to Chris's Drive In, where Siler City flavor meets genuine Southern hospitality! For years, we've been the go-to spot for locals and travelers alike, serving up dishes that are truly unbeatable.

Our dedication to classic comfort food, combined with a five-star service experience, creates an old-school charm ambiance that's well worth any drive. Whether you're craving our legendary blended pork and beef dog or looking to upgrade to a 'Rollie' for that extra satisfaction, we promise a meal that's bigger on flavor and even bigger on heart.

Come on down and taste the tradition! We're proud to be a cornerstone of the Siler City community, offering delicious food and a welcoming atmosphere that feels just like home.

Our Offerings

Flavorful Feasts, Siler City Style.

Chris's Signature Cheeseburger

Juicy, flame-grilled patty with fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, and melted cheese.

Crispy Chicken Sandwich

Golden-fried chicken breast, crisp pickles, and our secret sauce on a toasted bun.

Loaded Southern Fries

Our crispy fries piled high with cheddar, bacon, and a drizzle of ranch. Pure comfort!

'Rollie' Nachos

Sweet Tea

Onion Ring Tower
