Our Roots, Our Passion

Cooking with Love, Straight from the Heart of Kriti Kitchen

Welcome to Kriti Kitchen, your neighborhood Greek cafe nestled in the vibrant heart of West University, Houston, NC. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a family-run, woman-owned business with a simple, profound love for cooking for people.

Our journey began with a passion for homestyle Greek cuisine, passed down through generations. Every dish we serve is crafted with local, seasonal, and thoughtfully sourced ingredients, ensuring freshness and flavor in every bite. Whether you're joining us for a leisurely brunch, a hearty lunch, or a cozy dinner, you'll feel the warmth and care that goes into everything we create.

We also understand the demands of modern life, which is why we offer a daily selection of heat & eat meals, perfect for families large or small. Simply pick up or have them delivered right to your door. At Kriti Kitchen, we believe good food brings people together, and we can't wait to share our table with you.
