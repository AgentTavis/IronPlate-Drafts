Our Roots

More Than Just a Meal, It's Home.

Edgewood Grill, nestled in the heart of Bessemer City, NC, at 621 Athenia Pl, is more than just a diner – it's a beloved local institution. For years, we've been serving up heartwarming meals and genuine Southern hospitality that keeps our community coming back. We believe in great food, friendly faces, and a clean, comfortable spot where everyone feels like family.

From hearty breakfasts to satisfying lunches, and our well-known dinners, we're dedicated to making every visit special. Our commitment to quality and a welcoming atmosphere is what makes Edgewood Grill a true local favorite.
