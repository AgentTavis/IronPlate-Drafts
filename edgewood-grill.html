Our Journey

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Community Gathering

Edgewood Grill, located at 621 Athenia Pl, Bessemer City, NC 28016, is a charming diner that has won the hearts of locals and visitors alike. Whether you're craving a hearty breakfast to kickstart your morning, a satisfying lunch with friends, or a delightful dinner after a long day, Edgewood Grill offers a truly authentic down home country feel.

Our very friendly staff is dedicated to providing exceptional service in a clean and welcoming environment. We believe in good food, good company, and making every guest feel like family. Come experience the warmth and flavors that make us a beloved cornerstone of the Bessemer City community.
