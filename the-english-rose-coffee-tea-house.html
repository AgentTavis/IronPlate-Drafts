Our Heritage

Where Every Cup Tells a Story, and Every Bite Feels Like Home.

Nestled in the heart of Pleasant Garden, The English Rose Coffee & Tea House is more than just a diner—it's a warm, welcoming retreat. We believe in the simple joys: a perfectly brewed coffee, a freshly baked scone, and the comfort of good company.

Our journey began with a vision to create a cosy sanctuary where families and friends could gather. We've infused every corner with a sprinkle of British charm, offering a delightful escape from the everyday hustle. Whether you're here for a quick morning coffee, a leisurely lunch, or to find a special treat in our bake shop, you're always part of our family.

We pride ourselves on serving coffee and smiles, ensuring that every visit leaves you feeling refreshed and cherished. Come experience the warmth, the aroma, and the genuine hospitality that makes The English Rose a beloved local treasure.
