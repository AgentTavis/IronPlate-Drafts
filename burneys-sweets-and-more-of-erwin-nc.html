Our Journey

Baked with Love, Served with a Smile

Welcome to Burney's Sweets & More, a beloved local diner nestled in the heart of Erwin, NC. For years, we've been dedicated to crafting delicious bakery treats and serving them up with a warm, friendly touch that feels just like home.

Our passion lies in creating those perfect moments of joy, whether it's the first bite of a flaky glazed croissant, the comforting warmth of a fresh cup of coffee, or the sweet satisfaction of a pecan pie brownie. We believe in freshness, quality, and community, baking everything daily to ensure every customer leaves with a smile.

We pride ourselves on providing not just amazing sweets, but also a pristine, welcoming environment and 5-star service. Stop by, relax, and let us sweeten your day!
