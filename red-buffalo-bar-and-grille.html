Our Roots

Crafting Local Flavor and Community Spirit

Welcome to Red Buffalo Bar and Grille, your small, welcoming spot in Statesville, NC. What started as a vision to create a unique local hangout has grown into a cherished bar and grill that also proudly brews all its own beer on-site.

Our intimate space offers relaxed service where everyone feels like family. We've infused our passion for great food and exceptional craft beer with a distinctive Buffalo Bills theme, giving Red Buffalo a unique identity that stands out. Whether you're here for a hearty meal, a fresh pint, or to catch a game, you'll find a friendly atmosphere and a taste of true Statesville hospitality.

"Red Buffalo is more than just a bar; it's a piece of home. The beer is fantastic, and the atmosphere is always lively. A true Statesville gem!"
- Sarah L., Loyal Patron

Our Promise

Experience the Red Buffalo Difference

At Red Buffalo Bar and Grille, we're dedicated to more than just great food and drinks. We offer a truly unique atmosphere that keeps our patrons coming back.

Brewed On-Site, Poured with Passion

Unlike any other bar and grill, we proudly brew all our beer right here. This means every pint is fresh, crafted with care, and unique to Red Buffalo. Experience flavors you won't find anywhere else!

The Ultimate Buffalo Bills Hangout

Immerse yourself in our one-of-a-kind Buffalo Bills themed environment. It's more than just decor; it's a community for fans, a place to celebrate victories and share camaraderie. Feel the spirit of the Bills in Statesville!

Intimate Service, Relaxed Vibes

We pride ourselves on offering an intimate space with genuinely relaxed service. Our team is here to ensure you have a comfortable and enjoyable visit, whether you're dining alone or with a group of friends.
