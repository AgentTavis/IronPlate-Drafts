Our Journey

Rooted in Statesville, Crafted with Passion

Nestled in the heart of Statesville, NC, Red Buffalo Bar and Grille isn't just a place to eat and drink—it's a vibrant community hub. We're a small, welcoming brewery where every beer is lovingly brewed right on site, ensuring the freshest, most unique flavors for your palate.

Our space is intimate, designed for relaxed conversations and genuine connections. The service is always laid-back and friendly, making you feel right at home from the moment you step through our doors. What truly sets us apart is our unique Buffalo Bills theme, creating an identity that's both fun and memorable.

Whether you're here to cheer on your favorite team, discover a new craft brew, or simply enjoy a delicious bite from the OneBite DoneRight Food Truck, Red Buffalo Bar and Grille offers an experience that's authentically Statesville.

"Absolutely love the vibe at Red Buffalo! It's so refreshing to find a place that brews its own beer right here in town, and the Buffalo Bills theme adds such a cool touch. Always a great time."

— A Happy Local
