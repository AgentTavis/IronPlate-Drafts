OUR STORY

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Tradition.

Discover why Greenebow's has been West End's cherished diner for generations.

Welcome to Greenebow's, a beloved West End tradition serving up hearty American classics and Southern comfort food since 1998. For over two decades, we've been the go-to spot for locals seeking a warm welcome, friendly service, and delicious meals that taste like home.

From our famous breakfast platters to our satisfying lunch specials, every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and a sprinkle of Southern hospitality. Gather your family, bring your friends, and make some memories around our table. At Greenebow's, good food and great company are always on the menu.

Southern Style Biscuits & Gravy

Flaky buttermilk biscuits smothered in our rich, savory sausage gravy, a true Southern delight.

Signature Omelette

Hearty Lunch Burger
