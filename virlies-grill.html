Our Story

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Pittsboro Tradition.

Nestled in the heart of Pittsboro, Virlie's Grill has been a beloved local institution, serving up delicious food and warm hospitality. We're proud to be a place where friends and families gather, where every meal feels like coming home.
