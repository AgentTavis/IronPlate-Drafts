Our Heritage

Rooted in Tradition, Crafted for Today

At CASK Southern Kitchen & Bar, we invite you to experience a vibrant new take on traditional southern foods and handcrafted cocktails. Our culinary journey is deeply inspired by Kentucky’s rich bourbon history, though we proudly call Louisville, NC home.

We've meticulously curated a menu that celebrates classic Southern comfort with an innovative twist, designed to perfectly complement our extensive selection of Kentucky's greatest bourbons and carefully chosen local beers. From our kitchen to your table, every dish and drink is a testament to our passion for flavor, quality, and hospitality.

Join us for an unforgettable dining experience where warm Southern charm meets contemporary culinary excellence. Whether you're a bourbon connoisseur, a craft beer enthusiast, or simply seeking delicious food in a welcoming atmosphere, CASK is your destination.
