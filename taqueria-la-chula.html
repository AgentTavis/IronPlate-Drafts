Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings to Your Table

Nestled in the heart of Old Fort, Taqueria La Chula began as a dream to bring authentic, homemade flavors to our beloved community. What started as a vision to serve solid tacos for a little town has blossomed into a permanent culinary destination.

We pride ourselves on crafting every tortilla by hand, ensuring that each bite of our asada, chorizo, and pastor tacos delivers an unmatched freshness and taste. Our menu is a vibrant tapestry, weaving together the rich culinary traditions of Colombia, Mexico, and Honduras, along with delightful antojitos and desserts that truly hit the spot.

Whether you're looking for a quick, delicious lunch or a satisfying dinner after a day of exploring Old Fort, we're the perfect spot. Come join us and discover why Taqueria La Chula is quickly becoming a local favorite – a true taste of tradition and passion in every dish.
