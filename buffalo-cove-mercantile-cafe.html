Our Heritage

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Memory.

Nestled in the heart of Lenoir, NC, Buffalo Cove Mercantile & Cafe isn't just a place to eat—it's a step back in time. We've lovingly preserved the charm of an old country store, blending it with the warmth and flavors of a classic American diner. We believe in good, honest food, served with genuine hospitality.

From the moment you walk through our doors, you'll feel like family. We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming atmosphere where friends gather, stories are shared, and every dish is crafted with care. We gladly accept major credit cards like American Express and Mastercard, offer free Wifi, and are proud to be a dog-friendly establishment with highchairs available for our smallest guests. We're a non-smoking restaurant dedicated to providing a comfortable experience for everyone.

Our Flavors

Classic Diner Fare, Done Right.

Experience the comfort of homestyle cooking with a touch of Lenoir's charm. While our daily specials rotate, you'll always find familiar favorites and delightful surprises.

The Big Country Breakfast

Start your day the Buffalo Cove way with hearty portions of eggs, bacon, sausage, and our signature biscuits & gravy.

Homestyle Meatloaf Dinner

A true classic, slow-cooked and served with mashed potatoes and your choice of vegetable. Just like Grandma used to make.

Signature Cheeseburger

Juicy, grilled-to-perfection patty with fresh toppings, served on a toasted bun with a side of crispy fries.

Southern Fried Chicken

Crispy, golden, and packed with flavor. Served with two traditional country sides.

Daily Soup & Sandwich Combo

Ask about today's freshly made soup paired with a delightful deli sandwich.

Fresh Baked Pies & Desserts

End your meal with a slice of our homemade pie or another delicious dessert, baked fresh daily.

Our Promise

Why Locals Love Buffalo Cove.

Authentic Homestyle Flavors
