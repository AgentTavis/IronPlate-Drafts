Our Journey

Where Good Food & Good Company Meet

Welcome to Mood Swings Restaurant, Creswell's cherished Bar & Grill located right on US-64. We're more than just a place to eat; we're a community hub where delicious food, refreshing drinks, and friendly faces come together. From hearty breakfasts to satisfying evening meals, we're dedicated to serving up comfort and flavor with every visit.

At Mood Swings, we believe in honest hospitality and a straightforward good time. While we focus on timeless classics, we consistently deliver a warm, local experience that keeps our regulars coming back and new friends feeling right at home.
