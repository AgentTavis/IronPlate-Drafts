Our Heritage

Generations of Warmth in Every Cup & Plate

Ol Smoky Coffee Shop and County Store isn't just another café; it's a cherished local institution in the heart of Cherokee, NC. For decades, we've been serving up heartwarming diner classics, freshly brewed coffee, and a curated selection of local goods, embodying the true spirit of mountain hospitality.

We're a place where neighbors gather, travelers find solace, and every dish is made with love, reflecting the rich heritage of the Great Smoky Mountains. Come experience the genuine warmth that makes us a beloved stop for both locals and visitors exploring this beautiful region.

Our Kitchen

Homemade Comforts, Mountain Inspired

Ol' Smoky Classic Breakfast

Farm-fresh eggs cooked to order, crispy bacon or savory sausage, with your choice of grits or hashbrowns, and toasted bread.

Mountain Berry Pancakes

Light and fluffy pancakes griddled to perfection, topped with a luscious compote made from local seasonal berries.

County Store Burger

A juicy, hand-pattied burger made from local beef, served on a toasted bun with all the classic fixings and your choice of side.

Carolina Pulled Pork Sandwich

Slow-smoked pulled pork, drenched in our signature tangy BBQ sauce, piled high on a bun and served with creamy coleslaw.

Fresh Brewed Local Coffee

Our special house blend, locally roasted, ensuring a rich, aromatic cup of coffee every time. The perfect start to your day.

Homemade Apple Pie

A slice of pure Americana! Our grandma's recipe, with a flaky crust and warm, spiced apples. Delicious à la mode.

Biscuits & Gravy

Fluffy buttermilk biscuits smothered in a rich, savory country sausage gravy – a true Southern classic that's a must-try.

Daily Soup & Sandwich Special

Ask your server about today's comforting soup and perfectly paired sandwich, featuring fresh, seasonal ingredients.

Our Promise

More Than Just a Meal: The Ol Smoky Difference

Hearty Diner Classics

From our famous Ol' Smoky Classic Breakfast to the robust County Store Burger, we serve up comforting, delicious meals just like Grandma used to make. Experience flavors that evoke nostalgia and satisfy any craving.

Fresh Local Flavors

As a true County Store, we pride ourselves on sourcing local ingredients whenever possible. Enjoy the authentic taste of the Appalachians in every bite, especially our Mountain Berry Pancakes and Carolina Pulled Pork.

A Warm Welcome Always

Beyond the food, it's the friendly faces, cozy atmosphere, and the feeling of coming home that makes Ol Smoky a cherished stop. We treat every guest like family, ensuring a memorable and welcoming experience.
