Taste The Tradition

Our Signature Dishes

Egg Rolls (Cha Gio)

Crispy, savory fried egg rolls, a perfect start to any meal. Hand-rolled with fresh ingredients and served with our special dipping sauce.

Pho with Medium Rare Beef (Pho Tai)

Our classic beef noodle soup featuring tender slices of medium-rare beef, aromatic broth, rice noodles, and fresh herbs.

Combination Rice

A hearty plate featuring flavorful rice accompanied by a delicious combination of grilled meats and fresh vegetables.

Signature Stir Fry

Wok-tossed perfection with your choice of protein, crisp vegetables, and our house-made savory stir fry sauce.

Creamy Curries

Rich and fragrant curries, slow-cooked to perfection with coconut milk, spices, and your preferred protein. A warm, comforting classic.

Special Fried Rice

A flavorful blend of jasmine rice, fresh vegetables, egg, and your choice of protein, stir-fried to golden perfection.
