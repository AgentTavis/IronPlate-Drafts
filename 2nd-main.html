Our Legacy

Crafting Memories, One Drink at a Time

At 2nd & Main, we pride ourselves on a thoughtfully curated cocktail list designed to elevate your evening. Our signature Smoked Old Fashioned is a testament to our craft – a perfect blend of tradition and innovation, where the rich aroma of hickory smoke meets premium spirits.

We offer a selection of unique whiskeys, including special finds that promise to surprise and delight the discerning palate. Whether you prefer your drink smoked, neat, or expertly mixed, our bartenders are here to guide you to your perfect pour. Every visit is an experience, crafted with care to be 'quite good' and then some, right here in the heart of Lexington.
