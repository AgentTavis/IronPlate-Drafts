About Winners Circle BBQ

BBQ Excellence in Wilkesboro

Winners Circle BBQ isn't just a restaurant—it's a destination for BBQ lovers. Our pit masters smoke every brisket, pulled pork, and rack of ribs low and slow using traditional techniques and premium hardwoods.

Every meal is crafted with passion and served with pride. From family gatherings to corporate events, we bring authentic Carolina BBQ flavor to your table.
