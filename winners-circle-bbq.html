About Winners Circle BBQ

Welcome to Winners Circle BBQ, where tradition meets flavor in every bite. Located in beautiful Wilkesboro, North Carolina, we've been serving the community with authentic, slow-smoked barbecue for years.

Our commitment to quality starts with the finest ingredients and our time-honored smoking techniques. Every brisket, rib, and pulled pork is prepared with passion and perfected over hours of careful attention.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply craving authentic Carolina BBQ, Winners Circle is your destination. We pride ourselves on outstanding food, exceptional service, and a welcoming atmosphere that keeps guests coming back.

Menu Highlights

🍖 Slow-Smoked Brisket

Tender, juicy brisket smoked for 14+ hours over hickory and oak. Served with our signature dry rub and tangy house BBQ sauce.

🍗 Fall-Off-The-Bone Ribs

St. Louis style ribs with a perfect bark. Rubbed with our secret spice blend and smoked to perfection. Available in half or full racks.

🐷 Carolina Pulled Pork

Whole pork shoulders smoked low and slow. Tender, flavorful, and perfect for sandwiches or as a main course with classic sides.

🌮 Smoked Chicken

Half chickens marinated in our herb blend and smoked until golden. Moist on the inside, crispy on the outside. A customer favorite.
