Our Story

Welcome to Winners Circle BBQ, Wilkesboro's premier destination for authentic, slow-smoked barbecue. Since opening our doors, we've been committed to delivering the finest BBQ experience using time-honored techniques and premium cuts of meat.

Every dish is prepared with passion and precision. We smoke our meats low and slow over oak and hickory, ensuring that each bite is packed with flavor. From our signature pulled pork to our tender brisket, we take pride in every plate we serve.

Come join the Winners Circle and experience BBQ done right.
