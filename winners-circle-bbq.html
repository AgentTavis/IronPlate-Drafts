About Winners Circle

Winners Circle BBQ is Wilkesboro's premier destination for authentic, wood-smoked barbecue. With years of pit-mastery and a commitment to quality, we bring you the finest BBQ experience in North Carolina.

Every brisket, rib, and shoulder is smoked for hours using traditional techniques and premium hardwood. We believe that great BBQ isn't rushed—it's crafted with passion and served with pride.

Whether you're celebrating with family, hosting a corporate event, or craving authentic BBQ, Winners Circle delivers excellence on every plate. We're here to win your heart, one smoke ring at a time.
