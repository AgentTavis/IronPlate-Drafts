Our Heritage

More Than Just Breakfast, It's a Tradition.

Welcome to Kate's Pancake House, a beloved local diner nestled in the heart of Carolina Beach since 1998. For over two decades, we've been proudly serving up the freshest breakfast and lunch delights, becoming a cherished gathering spot for families and friends.

Step inside and you'll find a laid-back atmosphere with charming nautical decor that reflects our coastal home. Our specialty pancakes, made with love and our secret family recipes, are the talk of the town, drawing visitors and locals alike who crave that perfect stack.

We believe in good food, great company, and the kind of friendly service that makes you feel right at home. Come on down and experience the warmth and flavor that makes Kate's Pancake House a true Carolina Beach institution.
