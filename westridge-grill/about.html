Our Story

Westridge Grill was built on Southern hospitality, hand-pattied burgers, cold beer, and the kind of trust you only earn by showing up, every week, for almost 30 years.

Meet the Owner

Michael Wise Has Been Running This Place Since 1997.

When Michael opened Westridge Grill in the Westridge Shopping Center on Sunset Ave, George W. Bush was still the governor of Texas. Nearly three decades later, he's still here — still hands-on, still at the door, still remembering regulars by name and knowing which stool at the bar is "theirs." That kind of consistency is rare. It's also the reason this place has 578 Google reviews at a 4.5-star average.

Westridge isn't pretending to be anything it isn't. It's a neighborhood sports bar. Jerseys on the walls above the bar. TVs at every angle. A full ABC license stocked with NC craft beer, a solid wine list, and house specialty cocktails with names like "Sex at Westridge" and "Rockin' Ridge." And outside — under blue Pepsi umbrellas — a pet-friendly patio that fills up fast on sunny Saturdays.

The menu is massive on purpose. Wings with seven sauces including a Carolina Reaper that people drive across Nash County for. Hand-pattied 8 oz chuck burgers. Chicken salad "just like Gramma's." Santa Fe chicken eggrolls, smothered fries, tater skins, fried green tomatoes. Something for everyone who walks through the door — because everyone does.

This is where locals come for half-price apps after work on Tuesday, for trivia at 7:30, for live music on Thursday nights, for Saturday bloody Marys. It's where the Northern Nash, Rocky Mount Academy, Faith Christian and Nash Central communities come to celebrate wins and commiserate losses. It's Rocky Mount's place to be — and has been, for close to 30 years.

What Makes Us Different

It Isn't Just the Wings.

The Carolina Reaper

Our signature wing sauce, made with one of the hottest peppers on the planet. It shows up on salads, wraps and sandwiches too. It's our thing.

Made Here, Every Day

Chicken salad, chili, onion rings, fried green tomatoes, ranch. Homemade is not a marketing word — it's what's happening in our kitchen every morning.

Pet-Friendly Patio

Bring the dog. There's water, there's shade from the blue umbrellas, and there's an industrial fan for the hot Rocky Mount afternoons. Locals bring theirs every week.

A Real Venue

Team trivia every Tuesday at 7:30. Live music every Thursday 7–11 PM. Occasional Saturday nights too. We're not just a restaurant — we're where the week happens.
