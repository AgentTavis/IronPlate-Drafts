Every Week at the Grill

What's Happening

Westridge isn't just a restaurant — it's where the week unfolds. Trivia on Tuesdays, live music Thursdays, bar specials every night, and a patio that's always where the action is on the weekend.

Thursday Nights on the Patio

Weekly Schedule

Mark Your Calendar

Closed Monday & Sunday · everything below runs every week.

Tues & Wed 5:00 – 7:30 PM

Half-Price Appetizers

Every appetizer on the menu at half price. Jumbo wings, Santa Fe eggrolls, shrimp & crab dip, smothered fries, tater skins. Dine-in only. The best happy hour move in Rocky Mount.

Tuesday 7:30 PM · 2 hours

Team Trivia

Grab your crew, grab a table, and win bragging rights. Two hours of bar trivia with draft specials on Budweiser, Bud Light, Michelob Ultra and Michelob Amberbock.

Wednesday All Night

Wednesday Draft Specials

Specials continue on Budweiser, Bud Light, Michelob Ultra and Michelob Amberbock drafts. Quieter than Tuesday — a good night to actually hear your friends.

Thursday 7:00 – 11:00 PM

Live Music Night

Live music on the patio every Thursday, rain or shine (under cover either way). Domestic bottles and white wine glasses — both $1 off. The weekend starts here.

Friday All Night

Mojito Friday

Mojitos $1.50 off. Carolina Brewery draft specials. $1 off red wines. A Friday night where the drinks are cold, the bar is full, and the weekend has officially arrived.

Saturday All Day + Occasional Music

"Best in Town" Bloody Mary Day

Bloody Marys and mimosas — both $1.50 off. Often live music 7–11 PM (call to check). Come for brunch vibes, stay for the patio.
