OUR STORY

A Taste of Thailand, Crafted with Love in Shelby.

At Khop Khun Taste of Thailand, we bring the vibrant and aromatic flavors of traditional Thai cuisine right to the heart of Shelby, NC. Our mission is to offer a culinary journey that transports you to the bustling streets of Bangkok and the serene beaches of Phuket, all from our cozy bar and grill.

Every dish is prepared with authentic ingredients and time-honored recipes, ensuring a truly unforgettable dining experience that reflects our passion for genuine Thai hospitality and exquisite taste.
