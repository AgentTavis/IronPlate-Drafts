Our Story

Bringing the True Taste of Thailand to Shelby

At Khop Khun Taste of Thailand, we believe in bringing authentic, heart-warming Thai cuisine right to your table here in Shelby, NC. Our journey began with a passion for traditional Thai recipes, passed down through generations, and a desire to share those vibrant flavors with our community.

Every dish is crafted with fresh, quality ingredients and a commitment to genuine Thai culinary artistry. From the fragrant spices to the perfect balance of sweet, sour, salty, and spicy, we invite you to embark on a culinary adventure that transports you straight to the bustling streets of Bangkok and the serene beaches of Phuket.

We're more than just a restaurant; we're a place where friends and family gather, where laughter fills the air, and where every meal is an experience to savor. Thank you for making us a part of your Shelby story. We look forward to serving you.

Please note: We occasionally have special hours or closures for family events or holidays. For instance, we were closed for lunch on Friday, May 3rd from 11 AM to 4 PM, reopening for dinner from 4 PM to 8 PM. We always strive to minimize inconvenience and keep our community updated.
