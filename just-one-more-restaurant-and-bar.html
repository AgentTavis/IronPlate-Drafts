Our Journey

The Story Behind Just One More

Welcome to Just One More Restaurant and Bar, your friendly neighborhood spot in Kings Mountain, NC. We set out to create a place where friends and family could gather, relax, and enjoy great food and refreshing beverages in a truly casual and welcoming atmosphere.

We believe in simple pleasures: delicious, honest food made with care, served alongside a fantastic selection of drinks to suit every taste. Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunch, unwinding after work, or looking for a lively evening out, Just One More is designed to be your home away from home.

Our commitment is to provide a consistent, enjoyable experience every time you walk through our doors. We're proud to be a part of the Kings Mountain community and look forward to serving you with a smile.
