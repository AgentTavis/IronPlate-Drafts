Our Heritage

Serving Up Good Times in Kings Mountain

What's Cooking?

Flavorful Bites & Refreshing Sips

From classic comfort food to your favorite cold beverages, our menu is crafted to satisfy every craving. Please call for current availability and to-go orders!

The Classic Cheeseburger

Our juicy, perfectly seasoned patty, melted cheddar, fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles on a toasted brioche bun.

Crispy Chicken Wings

Tossed in your choice of classic buffalo, tangy BBQ, or zesty lemon pepper. Served with ranch or blue cheese.

Loaded Nachos Supreme

Crispy tortilla chips piled high with seasoned beef, melted cheese, jalapeños, pico de gallo, sour cream, and guacamole.

Signature Craft Brews

A rotating selection of local and national craft beers, hand-picked to complement your meal.

Refreshing Cocktails

From classic margaritas to our unique house specials, crafted with premium spirits and fresh ingredients.

Fresh Garden Salad

Crisp mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and your choice of dressing. Add grilled chicken for protein.

Sweet Potato Fries

A delicious alternative to classic fries, served hot and crispy with a side of our signature dipping sauce.

Spicy Chicken Sandwich

Crispy fried chicken breast tossed in hot sauce, topped with coleslaw and pickles, served on a toasted bun.
