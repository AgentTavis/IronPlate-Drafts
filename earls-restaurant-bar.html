Our Story

Where Music Meets Flavor

Nestled in the heart of Downtown Winston-Salem, Earl's Restaurant & Bar is more than just a place to eat and drink—it's an experience. We've cultivated a lively atmosphere and a vibrant vibe that draws you in from the moment you step through our doors. Whether you're here for an intimate evening or a night out with friends, you'll find our cute place and especially our inviting patio outdoor seating perfect for any occasion.

We're proud to be Winston-Salem's go-to spot for live country and rock music, serving up toe-tapping tunes alongside our well-stocked whiskey bar. Our kitchen is dedicated to hearty country-kitchen dishes and satisfying late-night bites, ensuring you're fueled up for the fun. Come discover why Earl's is the heart of Winston-Salem's energetic dining and entertainment scene.
