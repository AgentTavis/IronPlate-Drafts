Our Story

More Than Just a Bar: It's Your OBX Home Away From Home.

Nestled in the heart of Kitty Hawk, Tap That OBX is an awesome spot for drinks, games, and premium cigars. We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels like family. From the moment you step in, you'll feel the genuine, hometown bar vibe that sets us apart.

We're constantly buzzing with events—music bingo, trivia nights, and live music fill our calendar, ensuring there's always something exciting happening. Whether you're a local or just visiting, Tap That OBX is the place where good times and great company come together.
