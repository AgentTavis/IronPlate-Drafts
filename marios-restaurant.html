Our Story

Generations of Flavor, Rooted in Forest City.

At Mario's Restaurant, we're more than just a place to eat; we're a cornerstone of the Forest City community, serving up delicious bar and grill favorites with a personal touch. For years, we've poured our heart into every dish, creating a welcoming atmosphere where friends and family gather to share laughter and great food.

From sizzling grills to refreshing drinks, our passion for good old-fashioned comfort food shines through. We believe in honest flavors, generous portions, and the kind of friendly service that makes you feel right at home. Come on in and experience the Mario's difference – where every meal is a celebration of our heritage and your satisfaction.
