Our Story

Bringing Hearty Flavors to Cleveland, NC

Welcome to Mario's Restaurant! We are a proud Bar & Grill, deeply rooted in the Cleveland, NC community. Our journey has always been about creating a welcoming space where friends and family can gather to enjoy delicious, satisfying meals.

Since opening our doors, we've focused on quality ingredients and a friendly atmosphere. We're thrilled with our new location, which our customers like Jennylyn M. describe as "so much cleaner than the old one," praising our "pleasant and helpful staff" who genuinely "like their job." We believe great food starts with a happy team and a comfortable environment.

Under the guidance of owner Julio B., Mario's isn't just a restaurant; it's a place where every dish is prepared with passion and served with a smile. From hearty mains to our delightful dessert station, we strive to make every visit a memorable one.
