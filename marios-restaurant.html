Our Heritage

Taste the Tradition of Mario's

At Mario's Restaurant, we believe in crafting experiences as much as we craft meals. Nestled in the heart of Forest City, we've been a beloved bar and grill for locals and visitors alike. Our passion for good food and community is at the core of everything we do, from our welcoming atmosphere to our carefully curated menu.

While online ratings can vary, our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction remains constant. We're proud to have garnered 4.5 stars from over 500 reviews, reflecting the dedication we pour into every plate and every smile. Come and discover why Mario's is a cherished spot in Forest City!
