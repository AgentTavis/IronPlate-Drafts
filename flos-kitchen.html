Our Heritage

The Heart of Flo's Kitchen: Quality, Speed & Tradition.

At Flo's Kitchen, we believe the best things in life come wrapped in a simple white paper bag. For years, we've perfected the art of serving authentic Eastern NC BBQ with unmatched speed and a smile.

While this might be your first visit, you'll quickly discover why our drive-thru service is "down to a science." Every item on our menu is lovingly made from scratch, ensuring a taste of tradition in every bite.

Don't forget to swing by on Wednesdays for our famous sweet potato biscuits – a true local favorite that embodies the dedication and care we put into all our cooking. Experience the flavor of Flo's, where quality meets convenience every day.
