Our Heritage

Generations of Flavor, Made with Love

More Than Just a Meal, It's a Tradition.

At Flo's Kitchen, we've been serving up hearty, homemade BBQ and breakfast classics in Rocky Mount, NC, for years. We believe in comfort food that reminds you of home, prepared with care and fresh ingredients.

Our loyal customers often tell us how much they love our biscuits – fluffy, warm, and the perfect start to any day. While we take pride in all our dishes, from the beloved bacon egg and cheese to the impressive Cat Head biscuit, it's the simple joy of a good breakfast that truly defines us.

We invite you to experience the warmth and flavor that makes Flo's Kitchen a local favorite. Whether you're heading out of town or just starting your day, come on down for a taste of genuine Southern hospitality.
