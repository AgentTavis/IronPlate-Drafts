Our Story

Where History Meets Hearty Fare: A Spruce Pine Tradition Since 1950.

For over seven decades, City Drive-In has been the beloved culinary cornerstone of Spruce Pine. What started as a classic drive-in experience in 1950 has evolved, now proudly encompassing '670 on Oak' – your destination for a full bar experience, complete with beer, wine, spirits, and vibrant live music on weekends. We're more than just a diner; we're a community gathering spot where cherished memories are made over satisfying meals.

We're dedicated to serving great food in a casual, welcoming atmosphere. From our timeless diner classics to the exciting new offerings at 670 on Oak, we pride ourselves on providing a variety of dishes to satisfy every craving. Join us and become part of our ongoing story!
